# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of a procurement statement of work (SOW)?

## Options

- **A**: To list every vendor that may receive the bid package.
- **B**: To describe the item being procured in enough detail for sellers to determine whether they can supply it and at what price.
- **C**: To define how the buyer will manage the procurement process internally.
- **D**: To document the legal terms and conditions of the resulting contract.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The procurement SOW describes the work, deliverables, and quality requirements so sellers can evaluate whether to bid and how to price. (A) describes the qualified seller list. (C) describes the procurement management plan. (D) describes the contract itself, of which the SOW is one input.
