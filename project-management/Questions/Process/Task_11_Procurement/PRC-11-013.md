# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of a procurement statement of work (SOW)?

## Options

- **A**: To define how the procurement process will be managed
- **B**: To describe the procurement item in sufficient detail for prospective sellers to determine if they can provide it
- **C**: To list all potential vendors who might bid
- **D**: To document the contract terms and conditions

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. The procurement SOW describes what is to be procured in enough detail that sellers can determine whether they can supply it and at what price. It defines deliverables, quality requirements, quantities, performance specifications, and timelines.

Option A describes the procurement management plan.

Option C describes the qualified seller list.

Option D describes the contract itself.
