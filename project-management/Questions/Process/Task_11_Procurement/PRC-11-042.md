# Question

A qualified sellers list is:

## Options

- **A**: The list of vendors who submitted proposals
- **B**: A pre-approved list of vendors who meet certain criteria and can be invited to bid
- **C**: The list of vendors selected for contract award
- **D**: A list of all possible vendors in the market

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. A qualified sellers list (also called approved vendor list or preferred supplier list) contains vendors who have been pre-evaluated and meet organisation criteria. Using this list can streamline procurement by starting with vendors already known to meet minimum qualifications.

Option A describes bidders, not necessarily pre-qualified sellers.

Option C describes the award outcome.

Option D is broader than a qualified list, which includes only those meeting specific criteria.
