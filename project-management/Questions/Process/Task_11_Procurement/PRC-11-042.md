# Question

What is a qualified sellers list?

## Options

- **A**: A list of every vendor in the relevant market segment that the buyer organisation is aware of.
- **B**: A list of vendors who have submitted proposals on the current procurement opportunity under evaluation.
- **C**: A list of vendors pre-evaluated against organisational criteria who are eligible to be invited to bid.
- **D**: The list of vendors selected to receive the final contract awards on the procurement.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

A qualified sellers list (or approved vendor list) contains vendors that have already passed a baseline evaluation, allowing buyers to streamline procurements by inviting them directly. (A) is the universe of suppliers, not a qualification list. (B) describes bidders. (D) describes award outcomes, not the eligibility list.
