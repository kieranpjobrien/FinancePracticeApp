# Question

Procurement closure includes which activities?

## Options

- **A**: Selecting the vendor and signing the contract
- **B**: Verifying deliverables, settling claims, updating records, and archiving documentation
- **C**: Monitoring vendor performance during execution
- **D**: Defining what needs to be procured

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Procurement closure occurs after work is complete and includes formal acceptance of deliverables, settlement of any outstanding claims or payments, updating procurement records, documenting lessons learned, and archiving procurement documentation for future reference.

Option A describes contract award at the beginning of the procurement.

Option C describes contract administration during execution.

Option D describes procurement planning.
