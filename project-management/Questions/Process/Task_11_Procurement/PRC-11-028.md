# Question

Which set of activities best describes procurement closure?

## Options

- **A**: Selecting the vendor, awarding the contract, and signing legal documents.
- **B**: Defining what to procure and writing the procurement statement of work.
- **C**: Monitoring vendor performance and processing routine invoices during execution.
- **D**: Verifying final deliverables, settling outstanding claims, updating records, and archiving procurement documentation.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Procurement closure occurs at the end of the contract: confirm acceptance, settle claims and payments, capture lessons learned, and archive. (A) describes contract award, the start of administration. (B) describes planning. (C) describes contract administration during execution.
