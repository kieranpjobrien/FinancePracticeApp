# Question

Contract administration includes which of the following activities?

## Options

- **A**: Selecting the vendor
- **B**: Monitoring contract performance and managing contract changes
- **C**: Writing the statement of work
- **D**: Conducting the make-or-buy analysis

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Contract administration occurs after the contract is signed and involves managing the contract relationship, monitoring seller performance, managing changes and claims, processing payments, and ensuring both parties fulfil their obligations.

Option A occurs during the procurement conduct phase, before the contract is signed.

Option C occurs during procurement planning.

Option D occurs during planning to determine whether to procure at all.
