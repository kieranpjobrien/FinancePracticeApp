# Question

Fixed Price with Economic Price Adjustment (FP-EPA) contracts are appropriate when:

## Options

- **A**: The scope is uncertain and may change
- **B**: The contract period is long and prices may change due to inflation or market conditions
- **C**: The buyer wants to incentivise cost savings
- **D**: The work is high-risk with uncertain outcomes

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. FP-EPA contracts include provisions for price adjustments based on predetermined economic conditions (inflation indices, commodity prices, labour rates). This protects both parties when contracts span extended periods where economic conditions may change beyond either party's control.

Option A suggests T&M or cost-reimbursable contracts.

Option C suggests incentive fee contracts.

Option D suggests cost-reimbursable contracts where buyer accepts risk.
