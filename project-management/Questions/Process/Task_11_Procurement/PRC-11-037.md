# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of a bidder conference?

## Options

- **A**: To negotiate prices with potential vendors
- **B**: To give all prospective sellers equal access to information and answers to questions
- **C**: To select the winning vendor
- **D**: To finalise contract terms

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. A bidder conference (also called contractor conference, vendor conference, or pre-bid conference) allows all prospective sellers to ask questions and receive answers. All attendees hear the same information, ensuring fair and equal access. This improves proposal quality and procurement fairness.

Option A occurs after selection, during negotiations.

Option C occurs after proposals are evaluated.

Option D occurs during contract negotiations after selection.
