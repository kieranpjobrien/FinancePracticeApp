# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of a bidder conference?

## Options

- **A**: To give every prospective seller equal access to questions and answers about the procurement.
- **B**: To negotiate prices with the lowest bidders before the final selection decision is made.
- **C**: To identify and short-list which vendors should receive the full RFP and bid package.
- **D**: To finalise contractual terms and conditions with the chosen seller after award.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

A bidder conference (or pre-proposal conference) ensures all sellers hear the same questions and answers, supporting fairness and improving proposal quality. (B) describes negotiation, which happens after evaluation. (C) describes prequalification, which happens earlier. (D) describes contract negotiation after award decision.
