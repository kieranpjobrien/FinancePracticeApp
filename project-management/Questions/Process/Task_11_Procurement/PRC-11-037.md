# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of a bidder conference?

## Options

- **A**: To give every prospective seller equal access to information and to questions and answers about the procurement.
- **B**: To negotiate prices with the lowest bidders before final selection.
- **C**: To identify and short-list which vendors should receive the RFP.
- **D**: To finalise contractual terms with the chosen seller.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

A bidder conference (or pre-proposal conference) ensures all sellers hear the same questions and answers, supporting fairness and improving proposal quality. (B) describes negotiation, which happens after evaluation. (C) describes prequalification, which happens earlier. (D) describes contract negotiation after award decision.
