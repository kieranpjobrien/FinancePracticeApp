# Question

A project requires specialised software development that the organisation has never done before. The team has no internal expertise. What make-or-buy decision factor most strongly suggests buying (outsourcing)?

## Options

- **A**: The work is core to the organisation's competitive advantage
- **B**: The organisation lacks the skills and would need significant time to develop them
- **C**: The organisation has excess capacity available
- **D**: The project has a flexible timeline

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Lack of internal expertise is a strong indicator for buying. Developing new skills takes time and may not be cost-effective for one-time needs. Outsourcing provides immediate access to expertise without the investment in building capabilities that may not be needed again.

Option A suggests making (keeping in-house) since competitive advantages shouldn't be outsourced.

Options C and D suggest making might be viable since resources and time are available.
