# Question

A contracted vendor's key team member who was named in the proposal has left the vendor's company. The vendor assigns a replacement with similar qualifications. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Accept the replacement since they have similar qualifications
- **B**: Terminate the contract for non-compliance
- **C**: Review contract terms regarding key personnel changes and ensure proper notification and approval processes are followed
- **D**: Renegotiate the contract price since the original team is no longer available

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Many contracts include provisions about key personnel, requiring notification and approval of changes. The PM should verify whether proper procedures were followed, assess whether the replacement truly meets requirements, and document the change. This isn't automatic acceptance or rejection.

Option A accepts without verification or proper process.

Option B overreacts if the contract allows substitutions with approval.

Option D may not be justified if the replacement is equally qualified.
