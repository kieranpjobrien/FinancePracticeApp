# Question

What is the purpose of source selection criteria?

## Options

- **A**: To list which vendors are pre-qualified to bid on the procurement.
- **B**: To document the qualifications of the vendor that wins the award.
- **C**: To define how proposals will be evaluated and weighted before bids are received.
- **D**: To describe the goods or services the buyer wishes to procure.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Source selection criteria are the evaluation factors and weights agreed before bids open, so the selection is objective and defensible. (A) describes the qualified seller list. (B) is post-award documentation. (D) describes the procurement statement of work, not the selection criteria.
