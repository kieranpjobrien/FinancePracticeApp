# Question

Which contract type places the MOST cost risk on the seller?

## Options

- **A**: Firm Fixed Price (FFP)
- **B**: Cost Plus Incentive Fee (CPIF)
- **C**: Cost Plus Fixed Fee (CPFF)
- **D**: Time and Materials (T&M)

## Answer

A

## Explanation

In an FFP, the seller commits to deliver the defined scope for a set price; cost overruns reduce the seller's margin or create losses. (B) and (C) reimburse cost and pay a fee, putting cost risk on the buyer. (D) shares risk depending on structure but generally keeps cost risk with the buyer because labour hours are reimbursed at agreed rates.
