# Question

Which contract type places the MOST cost risk on the buyer?

## Options

- **A**: Firm Fixed Price (FFP)
- **B**: Cost Plus Percentage of Cost (CPPC)
- **C**: Time and Materials (T&M)
- **D**: Cost Plus Fixed Fee (CPFF)

## Answer

B

## Explanation

CPPC reimburses the seller for all costs plus a percentage of those costs, creating a perverse incentive to inflate cost; the buyer carries unbounded cost risk. (A) FFP shifts cost risk to the seller. (C) T&M places moderate buyer risk but uses fixed labour rates and is typically capped. (D) CPFF reimburses costs but caps the fee; the seller has no incentive to drive costs up.
