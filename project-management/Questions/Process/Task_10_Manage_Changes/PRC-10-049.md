# Question

An approved change on a predictive construction project requires rework to a completed foundation. The project quality plan requires re-inspection and re-certification. The contractor has quietly begun rework without updating the configuration records. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Let the contractor continue and update configuration records after the rework is complete.
- **B**: Stop rework only for the most safety-critical portions and allow other rework to continue as planned.
- **C**: Wait until re-inspection to audit configuration and assess the damage to configuration records.
- **D**: Stop the rework, update configuration records per the approved change, and proceed under quality controls.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Configuration management discipline must travel with approved changes, especially where quality re-inspection is contractually required. (A) retrofits records, which fails the audit trail that inspection depends on. (B) applies the rule selectively and undermines configuration integrity. (C) compounds the problem rather than stopping and correcting it now.
