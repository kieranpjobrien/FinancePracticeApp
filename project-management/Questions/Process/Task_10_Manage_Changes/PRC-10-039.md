# Question

A change would clearly help Department A but inconveniences Department B. The CCB is split. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Recommend the CCB approve the change because the net benefit is positive.
- **B**: Recommend the CCB reject the change because it harms a stakeholder group.
- **C**: Facilitate a working session with both departments to explore alternatives that could meet Department A's need with less impact on Department B, then bring options back to the CCB.
- **D**: Ask the two departments to negotiate directly and bring an agreed position to the CCB without PM involvement.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The PM's role is to facilitate creative problem-solving before forcing the CCB into a win-lose vote. Surfacing alternatives often reveals solutions that satisfy both groups. (A) and (B) accept the trade-off as binary without exploring better options. (D) abdicates facilitation; departments often need a neutral PM to broker a workable design.
