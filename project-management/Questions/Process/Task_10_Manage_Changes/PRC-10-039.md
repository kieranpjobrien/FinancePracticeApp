# Question

A change request would benefit Department A significantly but would slightly inconvenience Department B. Both departments are stakeholders. The CCB is split. How should the PM facilitate resolution?

## Options

- **A**: Support the change since the net benefit is positive
- **B**: Reject the change since it harms one stakeholder
- **C**: Facilitate analysis of alternatives that might benefit both departments
- **D**: Let the departments negotiate directly and accept their agreement

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Before accepting a win-lose outcome, explore whether win-win alternatives exist. Perhaps the change can be modified to reduce Department B's inconvenience, or Department B can be compensated in some way. The PM should facilitate creative problem-solving before accepting trade-offs.

Options A and B accept trade-offs without exploring alternatives.

Option D abdicates the PM's facilitation role.
