# Question

Which document is MOST likely to be updated as a direct result of an approved scope change?

## Options

- **A**: Project charter
- **B**: Original business case
- **C**: Stakeholder register
- **D**: Project management plan and the affected baseline

## Answer

D

## Explanation

When a scope change is approved, the PMP and at least the scope baseline (and often schedule and cost baselines) must be updated to reflect the new agreed-upon plan. (A) the charter is rarely modified after initiation; it captures high-level authority. (B) the business case is updated for material strategic shifts, not routine approved scope changes. (C) the stakeholder register tracks people, not plan content.
