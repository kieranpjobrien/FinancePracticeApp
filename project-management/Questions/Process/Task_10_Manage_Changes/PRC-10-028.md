# Question

A project team working in a regulated industry must maintain an audit trail of all changes. The current change log is maintained in a spreadsheet that multiple people can edit. What risk does this create?

## Options

- **A**: The spreadsheet may become too large to manage
- **B**: Multiple people editing may create versioning and integrity issues
- **C**: Stakeholders may not have access to the spreadsheet
- **D**: The format may not be acceptable to auditors

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Audit trails require integrity - certainty that records haven't been altered. Spreadsheets edited by multiple people can have overwritten data, conflicting versions, and no clear record of who changed what when. This undermines the audit trail's reliability and may not meet regulatory requirements.

Option A is an administrative concern, not an audit risk.

Option C is an access issue, not an integrity issue.

Option D may or may not be true; the bigger issue is data integrity.
