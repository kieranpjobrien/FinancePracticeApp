# Question

Halfway through a hybrid analytics programme, a new data source becomes available that could significantly increase programme benefits if integrated now. It was not in the original scope. The PM has capacity to assess the opportunity this week. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Reject the opportunity because integrating it would introduce scope changes that the baseline did not contemplate.
- **B**: Raise a change request, complete impact and benefit analysis, and let the CCB decide based on evidence.
- **C**: Add the new data source to the solution quietly during an existing sprint to avoid slowing down delivery.
- **D**: Ask the sponsor informally whether to pursue the opportunity and then proceed without further documentation.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Opportunities are handled through the same integrated change control path as threats; the CCB decides with impact and benefit analysis. (A) rejects value on the grounds of process inconvenience. (C) absorbs scope silently, bypassing change control. (D) relies on informal direction and skips the documentation that governance requires.
