# Question

What does the change log capture?

## Options

- **A**: All change requests submitted to the project, regardless of outcome, with their current status.
- **B**: Only changes that have been approved by the change control board.
- **C**: Only changes that affect the cost, schedule, or scope baselines.
- **D**: Only changes that have been rejected or deferred during review.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The change log records every change request raised on the project together with its disposition (submitted, under review, approved, rejected, deferred, implemented). (B) and (D) capture only one slice and lose traceability of the rest. (C) misses changes that affect quality, risk, or other baselines and changes that turn out not to need a baseline update.
