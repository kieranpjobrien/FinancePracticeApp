# Question

Which combination of contents is MOST appropriate for a complete change request?

## Options

- **A**: A description of the change and the requester's name.
- **B**: Description, business justification, integrated impact analysis, and proposed implementation approach.
- **C**: Detailed technical design and acceptance criteria for the change.
- **D**: A list of stakeholders affected and their preferred resolution.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A change request should give the CCB everything needed to make an informed decision: what is being changed, why, the impact across scope/schedule/cost/quality/risk, and how it would be delivered. (A) gives administrative metadata only and lacks decision content. (C) is implementation detail that may follow approval but is insufficient on its own. (D) records stakeholder views but does not analyse impact, which is the heart of the assessment.
