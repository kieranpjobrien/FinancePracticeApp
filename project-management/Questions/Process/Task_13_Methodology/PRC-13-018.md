# Question

What is the PRIMARY advantage of iterative delivery?

## Options

- **A**: It reduces the total documentation a project must produce.
- **B**: It enables early and continuous feedback that guides course correction.
- **C**: It guarantees a project will be delivered faster than a predictive plan.
- **D**: It requires smaller teams than equivalent predictive projects.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The defining benefit of iteration is the feedback loop — stakeholders see working product early and the team can adjust. (A) is a myth; documentation depends on context, not approach. (C) is not guaranteed and is not the primary advantage. (D) confuses team size with delivery approach.
