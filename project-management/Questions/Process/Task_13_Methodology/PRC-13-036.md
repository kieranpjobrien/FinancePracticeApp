# Question

A 1,200-person organisation rolled out SAFe nine months ago. Teams report excessive ceremonies and feel less productive than before. Middle management is bypassed. Senior leaders rarely attend the events SAFe expects of them. What should the transformation lead do?

## Options

- **A**: Run a SAFe refresher across the whole organisation to fix the practice gaps.
- **B**: Enforce stricter compliance with the SAFe playbook through audit checks each PI.
- **C**: Walk away from agile and reinstate the previous predictive PMO model.
- **D**: Reassess fit between SAFe and the organisation's culture and readiness; consider stepping back to team-level agility before re-attempting scaled coordination.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The symptoms point to a framework-fit and leadership-engagement problem, not a training one. (D) takes that seriously and considers right-sizing. (A) treats it as practitioner ignorance when leadership is part of the problem. (B) escalates compliance against a framework that may not fit. (C) over-corrects and discards what is working at team level.
