# Question

An agile team's velocity has doubled over six months but the product owner's NPS scores are dropping and the sponsor says "we are getting features but not value." What should the project manager focus on?

## Options

- **A**: Treat velocity as the leading indicator and continue the current cadence.
- **B**: Strengthen stakeholder collaboration and outcome-based feedback so the team is delivering the right things, not just more things.
- **C**: Reintroduce a predictive plan so the sponsor sees committed scope.
- **D**: Add a quality engineer to the team to lift the standard of each feature.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PMI emphasises outcomes over outputs and customer collaboration as a core agile value. (B) names the actual gap — high output, low value. (A) doubles down on the wrong metric. (C) hides the problem under a plan rather than fixing the value disconnect. (D) tackles quality, which is a different issue from value.
