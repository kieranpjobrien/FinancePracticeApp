# Question

A Scrum team wants to adopt test-driven development and continuous integration to address quality issues. What should the project manager recommend?

## Options

- **A**: Replace Scrum with full XP so the engineering practices and the framework reinforce each other.
- **B**: Create a custom blended framework that mixes selected ceremonies from Scrum and XP.
- **C**: Defer the engineering practices until the team's Scrum maturity is higher.
- **D**: Keep Scrum as the framework and adopt the XP engineering practices alongside it.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Scrum manages flow and product; XP gives engineering disciplines that bake quality in. The two are complementary. (D) keeps the working framework and adds proven practices to it. (A) is overkill and discards a framework the team already uses. (B) reinvents what already exists in the literature. (C) defers exactly the practices that would address the team's quality problem.
