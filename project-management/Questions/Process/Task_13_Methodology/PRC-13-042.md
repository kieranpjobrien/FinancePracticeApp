# Question

Which Agile Manifesto value emphasizes the importance of being able to change direction?

## Options

- **A**: Individuals and interactions over processes and tools
- **B**: Working software over comprehensive documentation
- **C**: Customer collaboration over contract negotiation
- **D**: Responding to change over following a plan

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The correct answer is D. "Responding to change over following a plan" directly addresses the importance of adaptability. While there is value in having a plan, agile approaches recognize that change is often necessary and should be embraced rather than resisted.

Options A, B, and C are also Agile Manifesto values, but they address interactions, deliverables, and collaboration respectively, not specifically the ability to change direction.
