# Question

Which project characteristic MOST favours an agile approach?

## Options

- **A**: Strict regulatory sequencing of activities.
- **B**: Mature, well-understood technology with proven patterns.
- **C**: Requirements that are expected to evolve with stakeholder feedback.
- **D**: A fully defined scope with little expected change.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Agile's strength is in handling uncertainty and learning through frequent stakeholder feedback. (C) describes those exact conditions. (A) demands sequencing that an iterative approach struggles to honour. (B) does not add value through iteration when there is little to learn. (D) describes the predictive sweet spot, not agile.
