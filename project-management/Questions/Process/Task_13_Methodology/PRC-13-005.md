# Question

What is the MOST important factor in selecting a delivery approach for a project?

## Options

- **A**: The project's characteristics, including uncertainty, regulation, and stakeholder needs.
- **B**: The methodology the project manager has the most experience with.
- **C**: The framework currently used by the majority of projects in the organisation.
- **D**: The approach that minimises documentation overhead for the team.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PMI's tailoring principle says fitness for purpose drives methodology choice. (A) names the right inputs. (B) prioritises convenience over project success. (C) is inertia, not analysis — what worked before may not fit this project. (D) optimises for the wrong dimension; some projects need more documentation, not less.
