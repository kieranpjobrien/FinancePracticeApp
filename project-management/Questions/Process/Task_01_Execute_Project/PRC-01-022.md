# Question

A critical path task is delayed by two weeks. The project deadline is fixed due to a market launch event. What should the PM do first?

## Options

- **A**: Accept the project will be late
- **B**: Analyse options to recover the schedule: fast-tracking, crashing, or scope reduction, and present alternatives to stakeholders
- **C**: Immediately add more resources without analysis
- **D**: Hide the delay from stakeholders

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. When critical path is delayed but deadline is fixed, analyse recovery options. Fast-tracking: parallel activities where possible. Crashing: add resources to critical tasks (cost vs time trade-off). Scope reduction: defer non-essential features. Present options with trade-offs to stakeholders for decision.

Option A doesn't explore recovery options.

Option C may not be effective and adds cost without analysis.

Option D is unethical and will eventually be discovered.
