# Question

What is the primary purpose of value-driven delivery in project management?

## Options

- **A**: To deliver features alphabetically
- **B**: To prioritise and deliver the highest value items first to maximise return on investment
- **C**: To deliver the easiest items first
- **D**: To deliver items in the order they were requested

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Value-driven delivery focuses on prioritising work by business value so that the most important features are delivered first. This maximises ROI, provides early feedback on critical functionality, and ensures that even if the project is cut short, the most valuable capabilities have been delivered.

Option A, C, and D ignore business value as the prioritisation criterion.
