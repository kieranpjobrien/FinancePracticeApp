# Question

The team is working on a complex integration with an external system. The external team is responsive but works on a different cadence - monthly releases versus your two-week sprints. How should you manage this dependency?

## Options

- **A**: Demand the external team match your sprint cadence
- **B**: Plan integration points around the external team's release schedule, mock interfaces for internal development, and coordinate closely on timing
- **C**: Ignore the external dependency in planning
- **D**: Do all integration at the end of the project

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Respect external constraints while managing the dependency. Plan integration points: identify when external releases occur and plan your integration work accordingly. Use mocks/stubs: develop against expected interfaces while waiting for real integration. Coordinate early and often: ensure both teams have visibility into timing and dependencies.

Option A may not be feasible and damages the relationship.

Option C ignores a real constraint that will affect delivery.

Option D accumulates risk by deferring integration.
