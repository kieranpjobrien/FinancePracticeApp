# Question

What is the purpose of a sprint review in Scrum?

## Options

- **A**: To review individual team member performance
- **B**: To inspect the increment, gather feedback from stakeholders, and adapt the product backlog based on what was learned
- **C**: To plan the next sprint
- **D**: To identify who is at fault for any problems

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. The sprint review inspects the increment (completed work) and gathers stakeholder feedback. It's a collaborative session where the team demonstrates what was built, stakeholders provide input, and the product backlog is adapted based on learning. It's about the product, not team performance.

Option A is addressed in performance reviews, not sprint reviews.

Option C describes sprint planning.

Option D misuses the ceremony for blame rather than feedback.
