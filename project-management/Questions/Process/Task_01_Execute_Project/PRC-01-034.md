# Question

A vendor deliverable that's on the critical path is running late. The vendor promises daily that they're almost done. The project deadline is at risk. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Continue trusting the vendor's promises
- **B**: Implement more frequent monitoring, request specific evidence of progress, develop contingency plans, and escalate within both organisations if needed
- **C**: Immediately cancel the vendor contract
- **D**: Wait until the deadline passes to take action

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Vague promises require verification. Increase monitoring: daily standups, specific evidence of progress (not just status updates). Develop contingencies: is there an alternative vendor? Can scope be reduced? Internal workaround? Escalate appropriately to ensure both organisations prioritise resolution. Act now while options still exist.

Option A continues hoping without managing the risk.

Option C may be premature without exploring recovery options.

Option D waits until it's too late to recover.
