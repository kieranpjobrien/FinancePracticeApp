# Question

What is the purpose of a product backlog in agile projects?

## Options

- **A**: To document completed work
- **B**: To maintain a prioritised list of requirements and features that represent the work needed to deliver value
- **C**: To track team attendance
- **D**: To store project documents

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. The product backlog is a prioritised list of everything needed in the product - features, requirements, enhancements, fixes. It's ordered by value, risk, and dependencies. Items at the top are refined and ready for upcoming sprints; lower items are less defined. It evolves throughout the project.

Option A describes a different type of record.

Option C and D are unrelated to backlog purpose.
