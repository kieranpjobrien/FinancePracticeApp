# Question

An activity has an optimistic duration of 4 days, a most likely duration of 6 days, and a pessimistic duration of 14 days. Using the PERT (Beta) distribution, what is the expected duration?

## Options

- **A**: 7 days
- **B**: 6 days
- **C**: 8 days
- **D**: 9 days

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PERT expected duration = (O + 4M + P) / 6 = (4 + 4(6) + 14) / 6 = 42 / 6 = 7 days. (A) is correct. (B) is just the most likely value with no PERT weighting. (C) is the simple three-point average (O + M + P) / 3 = 8. (D) does not match any standard formula and is a plausible-looking distractor.
