# Question

An activity has an optimistic duration of 4 days, most likely duration of 6 days, and pessimistic duration of 14 days. Using PERT, what is the expected duration?

## Options

- **A**: 6 days
- **B**: 7 days
- **C**: 8 days
- **D**: 9 days

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. PERT expected duration = (Optimistic + 4×Most Likely + Pessimistic) / 6

= (4 + 4×6 + 14) / 6
= (4 + 24 + 14) / 6
= 42 / 6
= 7 days

Option A is just the most likely estimate without PERT weighting.

Options C and D result from incorrect formula application.
