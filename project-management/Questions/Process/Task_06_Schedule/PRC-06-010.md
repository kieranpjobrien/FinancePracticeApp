# Question

Using PERT estimation, an activity has optimistic time = 4 days, most likely time = 6 days, and pessimistic time = 14 days. What is the expected duration?

## Options

- **A**: 6 days
- **B**: 7 days
- **C**: 8 days
- **D**: 10 days

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. PERT uses a weighted average formula:

**Expected Duration = (O + 4M + P) / 6**

Where:
- O = Optimistic (4 days)
- M = Most Likely (6 days)
- P = Pessimistic (14 days)

**Calculation:**
Expected = (4 + 4(6) + 14) / 6
Expected = (4 + 24 + 14) / 6
Expected = 42 / 6
Expected = **7 days**

The formula weights the most likely estimate 4x because it's the most probable outcome.
