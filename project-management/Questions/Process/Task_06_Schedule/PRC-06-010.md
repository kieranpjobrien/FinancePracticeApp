# Question

Using PERT (three-point) estimating, the standard deviation of an activity is calculated as (Pessimistic - Optimistic) / 6. If an activity has Optimistic = 4 days, Most Likely = 6 days, and Pessimistic = 14 days, what is the standard deviation?

## Options

- **A**: 1 day
- **B**: 1.33 days
- **C**: 1.67 days
- **D**: 2 days

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Standard deviation = (P - O) / 6 = (14 - 4) / 6 = 10 / 6 = 1.67 days. (C) is correct. (A) would correspond to (P - O) / 10. (B) would correspond to (P - M) / 6. (D) would correspond to (P - O) / 5. None of those formulas matches PMI's PERT standard deviation.
