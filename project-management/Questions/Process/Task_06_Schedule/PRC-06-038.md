# Question

The team completed sprint planning and committed to 8 user stories. After 3 days, they realize one story is much larger than estimated and they likely can't complete all 8. What should happen?

## Options

- **A**: Work overtime to complete all 8
- **B**: Discuss with the product owner - either split the large story and carry remainder to backlog, or remove a lower-priority story to maintain sustainable pace and sprint integrity
- **C**: Complete the large story and ignore the others
- **D**: Blame whoever estimated the story

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Agile allows adaptation during the sprint. PO discussion: what's the priority? Options: split the large story (complete part now), or swap out a lower-priority story. Sustainable pace: don't sacrifice it for one sprint's commitment. Sprint integrity: completing a realistic commitment is better than partially completing an unrealistic one.

Option A is unsustainable.

Option C may not deliver highest value.

Option D doesn't solve the problem.
