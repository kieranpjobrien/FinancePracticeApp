# Question

Activity A (duration 5 days) must finish before Activity B (duration 3 days) can start. There's a 2-day lag between them. If A starts on Day 1, when does B finish?

## Options

- **A**: Day 8
- **B**: Day 10
- **C**: Day 5
- **D**: Day 7

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. A starts Day 1, duration 5 days, finishes end of Day 5. Lag of 2 days means B can start Day 8 (after Days 6-7 lag). B duration 3 days: Days 8, 9, 10. B finishes end of Day 10.

Option A forgets the lag.

Option C is when A finishes.

Option D miscalculates.
