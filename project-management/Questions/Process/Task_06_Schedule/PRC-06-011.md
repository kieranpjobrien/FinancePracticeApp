# Question

Which statement BEST describes total float on an activity?

## Options

- **A**: The amount of time the activity can be delayed without delaying the early start of any successor activity.
- **B**: The amount of time the activity can be delayed without delaying the project's overall finish date.
- **C**: The amount of contingency reserve allocated to the activity for unforeseen risks.
- **D**: The number of days between the activity's start date and the next milestone in the schedule.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

(B) is the PMI definition of total float — slack against the project finish date. (A) describes free float, the related but stricter measure tied to the next successor. (C) describes contingency reserve, a risk concept, not a schedule one. (D) is a distance-to-milestone metric that has no PMI name and is not float.
