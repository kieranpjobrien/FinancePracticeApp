# Question

What is schedule compression?

## Options

- **A**: Making the schedule document smaller
- **B**: Techniques to shorten the project duration without reducing scope, such as crashing or fast-tracking
- **C**: Compressing files for storage
- **D**: Reducing the number of team meetings

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Schedule compression shortens project duration while maintaining scope. Main techniques: crashing (adding resources to critical path activities, typically at added cost) and fast-tracking (performing activities in parallel that were planned sequentially, typically with added risk).

Options A, C, and D misunderstand the scheduling concept.
