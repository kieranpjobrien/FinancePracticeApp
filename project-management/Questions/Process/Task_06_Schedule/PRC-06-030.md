# Question

During execution, a team member completes their task early. They have free float available. Should this early completion benefit the project?

## Options

- **A**: No - they should use all allocated time
- **B**: Yes - pass the early completion forward. Notify successor activities so they can potentially start early, capturing the schedule benefit.
- **C**: Add more work to fill their time
- **D**: Early completion indicates they had too much time allocated

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Early completions are opportunities. If passed forward, successors might start early, potentially benefiting the project (especially if they're on or near critical path). Communicate: let successors know. Capture: adjust the schedule to reflect reality. This proactive approach maximizes schedule benefits.

Option A wastes schedule opportunity.

Option C may not add value.

Option D isn't necessarily true - they may have been efficient.
