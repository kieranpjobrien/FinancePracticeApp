# Question

Using PERT estimation, an activity has optimistic = 4 days, most likely = 6 days, and pessimistic = 14 days. What is the expected duration?

## Options

- **A**: 6 days
- **B**: 7 days
- **C**: 8 days
- **D**: 10 days

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. PERT expected duration = (O + 4M + P) / 6 = (4 + 4(6) + 14) / 6 = (4 + 24 + 14) / 6 = 42 / 6 = 7 days. This weighted average gives more weight to the most likely estimate while accounting for optimistic and pessimistic scenarios.

Option A is just the most likely, ignoring the formula.

Options C and D use incorrect calculations.
