# Question

A schedule activity has been estimated using three points: Optimistic = 4 days, Most Likely = 6 days, Pessimistic = 14 days. Using the simple three-point (triangular) average rather than PERT, what is the expected duration?

## Options

- **A**: 8 days
- **B**: 7 days
- **C**: 6 days
- **D**: 9 days

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The triangular three-point estimate is (O + M + P) / 3 = (4 + 6 + 14) / 3 = 24 / 3 = 8 days. (A) is correct. (B) is the PERT (Beta) estimate, which weights M four times: (O + 4M + P) / 6 = 7. (C) is just the most likely value. (D) does not match either standard formula.
