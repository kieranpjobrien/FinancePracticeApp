# Question

When should closure planning begin?

## Options

- **A**: Only after all deliverables are complete
- **B**: Early in the project, with detailed planning as closure approaches
- **C**: When the sponsor asks for it
- **D**: Closure doesn't require planning

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Closure planning should begin early - understanding what closure will require helps plan the project. Detailed planning happens as closure approaches when specifics are known. Early consideration prevents last-minute scrambles and ensures resources are available for closure activities.

Option A leaves insufficient time for proper closure planning.

Option C is reactive rather than proactive.

Option D ignores that closure involves many activities that benefit from planning.
