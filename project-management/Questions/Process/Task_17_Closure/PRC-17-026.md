# Question

What financial activity is typically performed at project closure?

## Options

- **A**: Requesting additional budget
- **B**: Finalizing project accounts, releasing unused funds, and documenting final project costs
- **C**: Deferring financial reconciliation to the next fiscal year
- **D**: Allocating costs to other projects

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Financial closure includes finalizing accounts (all charges properly allocated), releasing unused contingency or reserve funds back to the organization, and documenting final actual costs for comparison to budget. This provides accurate financial records and frees up resources.

Option A is requesting resources, not closing them.

Option C delays proper closure.

Option D is improper financial management.
