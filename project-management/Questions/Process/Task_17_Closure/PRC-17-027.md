# Question

A project manager is releasing team members back to their functional departments at project closure. What is important to communicate to functional managers about these team members?

## Options

- **A**: Only negative feedback so managers know what to watch for
- **B**: Nothing - let team members speak for themselves
- **C**: Balanced feedback on performance, skills demonstrated or developed, and recognition for contributions - this supports team members' careers and provides functional managers useful information
- **D**: Detailed daily time logs

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Releasing team members includes providing feedback to their functional managers. This should be balanced (strengths and development areas), note skills gained, and recognize contributions. This supports team members' careers, helps functional managers make future assignments, and acknowledges good work.

Option A is biased and unhelpful to team members.

Option B misses an opportunity to support team members.

Option D provides excessive detail without meaningful feedback.
