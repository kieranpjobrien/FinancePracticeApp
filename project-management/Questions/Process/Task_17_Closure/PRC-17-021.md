# Question

During closure of a hardware testing project, the PM has 22 laptops and a rack of testing devices that the team no longer needs. Several still have meaningful residual value. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Allocate the equipment to team members as recognition for their work on the project.
- **B**: Store the equipment in a project locker in case a follow-on project needs it within the year.
- **C**: Dispose of the equipment through the simplest channel available so the project can close on time.
- **D**: Follow the organisation's asset disposition process to return, transfer, or formally dispose of each item according to accounting and asset policies.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Project equipment is an organisational asset and disposition is governed by finance and asset management policies, not the PM's discretion. (D) routes the equipment through the established process, which captures value and meets accounting requirements. (A) gives away organisational assets without authority and creates audit issues. (B) hoards assets that could be redeployed and ignores the asset register. (C) optimises the project schedule at the expense of organisational value and may breach disposal policy.
