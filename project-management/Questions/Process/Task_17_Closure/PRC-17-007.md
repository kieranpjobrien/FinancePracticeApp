# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of obtaining formal acceptance at project closure?

## Options

- **A**: To start the warranty period
- **B**: To confirm that the customer or sponsor agrees the project deliverables meet the acceptance criteria, officially completing the project obligations
- **C**: To begin the payment process
- **D**: To release team members

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Formal acceptance is the customer's or sponsor's confirmation that deliverables meet agreed-upon acceptance criteria. This officially marks the completion of project obligations and creates a clear end point. Without formal acceptance, the project remains open indefinitely.

Option A may be a consequence but isn't the primary purpose.

Option C may be related to acceptance but isn't its core purpose.

Option D is a closure activity but not the purpose of acceptance itself.
