# Question

What is the primary purpose of releasing project resources at closure?

## Options

- **A**: To reduce the project's reported labour cost on the final closure report.
- **B**: To make people, equipment, and funds available for other organisational priorities once they are no longer needed.
- **C**: To remove the team's ability to continue making changes to the delivered product.
- **D**: To trigger the start of the warranty period during which defects can be raised.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Resources are released so the organisation can redeploy them to other work; holding them on a closed project denies that value to the rest of the portfolio. (B) captures this purpose. (A) is an accounting side-effect rather than the purpose. (C) misframes resource release as a control mechanism rather than as resource stewardship. (D) confuses warranty initiation with resource release; warranty is governed by the contract or product, not by whether the team has been released.
