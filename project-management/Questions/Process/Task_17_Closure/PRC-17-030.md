# Question

What is the purpose of releasing project resources at closure?

## Options

- **A**: To punish team members
- **B**: To make resources available for other organizational needs - new projects, operations, or other work
- **C**: To reduce project costs
- **D**: To prevent team members from making changes

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Releasing resources makes them available for other organizational needs. People can move to new projects, return to operational roles, or take on other work. Keeping resources tied to a closed project prevents the organization from using them elsewhere.

Option A is not the purpose of resource release.

Option C - while there may be cost implications, availability for other work is the primary purpose.

Option D is not the purpose of resource release.
