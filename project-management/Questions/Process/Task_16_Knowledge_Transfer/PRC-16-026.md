# Question

Why should lessons learned include both successes and failures?

## Options

- **A**: To balance positive and negative feedback
- **B**: Successes provide templates to replicate; failures provide warnings to avoid - both types improve future performance
- **C**: Auditors require both types
- **D**: It is more fair to the team

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Both types of lessons have distinct value. Successes show what works and can be replicated or adapted. Failures show what to avoid and what went wrong. Together, they provide comprehensive guidance for future projects - repeat the good, avoid the bad.

Option A frames it as balance rather than distinct types of value.

Option C focuses on compliance rather than learning value.

Option D frames it as fairness rather than knowledge value.
