# Question

What is the primary purpose of a lessons learned register?

## Options

- **A**: To capture knowledge from the project's experiences so that current and future projects can benefit from what worked and what did not.
- **B**: To document team-member performance and inform performance reviews after the project closes.
- **C**: To record the project's financial transactions for audit and reconciliation purposes.
- **D**: To list all project deliverables and their final acceptance status.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

(A) is the textbook definition of the lessons learned register. (B) confuses it with performance management; lessons learned focus on practices, not individuals. (C) is the role of project accounting records. (D) is the deliverables register or scope baseline, not lessons learned.
