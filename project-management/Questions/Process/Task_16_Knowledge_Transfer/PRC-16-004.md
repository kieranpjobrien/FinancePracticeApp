# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of a lessons learned register?

## Options

- **A**: To document team member performance for HR purposes
- **B**: To capture knowledge from project experiences that can improve future projects
- **C**: To record project financials for audit purposes
- **D**: To list all project deliverables and their status

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. The lessons learned register captures what worked well, what didn't, and recommendations for future projects. It's a knowledge management tool that helps the organisation learn from experience and avoid repeating mistakes.

Option A (HR performance) is not the purpose. Lessons learned focus on processes and practices, not individual performance evaluation.

Option C (financial audit) is handled by project accounting records, not lessons learned.

Option D (deliverable status) is tracked in the deliverables register or WBS, not lessons learned.
