# Question

What is a community of practice?

## Options

- **A**: A formal training program
- **B**: A group of people who share a concern or passion for something they do, and learn how to do it better through regular interaction
- **C**: A project team
- **D**: An organizational hierarchy

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. A community of practice brings together people with shared interests or expertise for knowledge sharing and mutual learning. Unlike project teams (temporary, specific deliverable) or formal training (structured curriculum), communities of practice enable ongoing, organic knowledge exchange among practitioners.

Option A describes structured training, not community-based learning.

Option C describes a work unit, not a knowledge-sharing community.

Option D describes organizational structure, not knowledge community.
