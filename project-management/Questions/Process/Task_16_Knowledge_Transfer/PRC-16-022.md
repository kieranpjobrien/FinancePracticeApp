# Question

Which best describes a community of practice in the context of project knowledge management?

## Options

- **A**: A required training programme that staff must complete on a defined schedule to maintain their professional accreditation.
- **B**: A formal organisational unit reporting to the PMO that owns overall knowledge management policy across the enterprise.
- **C**: A group who share an interest or domain and learn from each other through ongoing voluntary interaction.
- **D**: A temporary project team formed specifically to deliver a knowledge management deliverable as part of a wider initiative.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

(C) is the standard PMI/Wenger definition of a community of practice. (A) describes formal training. (B) describes a function or department. (D) describes a project team rather than a community.
