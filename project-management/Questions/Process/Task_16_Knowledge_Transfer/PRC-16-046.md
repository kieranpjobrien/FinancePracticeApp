# Question

A hybrid cloud migration is entering closure. Operations will run the new platform. The PM is deciding what must move across beyond the written runbooks and configuration exports. What should the PM prioritise transferring?

## Options

- **A**: The rationale behind design decisions, known failure modes with prior workarounds, and the contextual knowledge operations need to judge future changes safely.
- **B**: A complete historical archive of all meeting minutes, design whiteboards, and chat threads captured over the full life of the migration programme.
- **C**: The budget and schedule variance reports so operations understand how the project tracked against baselines during the design and build phases.
- **D**: The full list of every tool, vendor, and license used during the project so operations can continue the same contractual arrangements.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PMI emphasises transferring both explicit artefacts and the tacit rationale that lets operations make safe decisions. (A) captures the why and the failure context that protects future judgement. (B) dumps raw history without curation or usable signal. (C) is project performance data irrelevant to running the platform. (D) is procurement information, not operational knowledge.
