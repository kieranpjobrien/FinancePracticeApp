# Question

A new team member is not receiving important communications because they weren't added to the right distribution lists and channels. They're falling behind as a result. What should the PM ensure happens?

## Options

- **A**: Let them catch up on their own
- **B**: Immediately add them to relevant communication channels, provide access to key documents, brief them on recent decisions, and establish a buddy to help them get oriented
- **C**: Wait until the next formal communication cycle
- **D**: Forward all past emails individually

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. New team members need rapid integration into communication flows. Add to channels: all relevant distribution lists, chat channels, meetings. Document access: ensure they can find project information. Brief: catch them up on context they missed. Buddy: someone to answer questions and help navigate. This accelerates their ability to contribute.

Option A leaves them struggling unnecessarily.

Option C delays their effectiveness.

Option D is inefficient and doesn't address ongoing access.
