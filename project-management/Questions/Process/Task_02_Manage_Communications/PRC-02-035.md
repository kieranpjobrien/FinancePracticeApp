# Question

A new team member started two weeks ago and has missed several important decisions because they weren't added to the right channels and distribution lists. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Send the new team member a forwarded copy of every relevant email and chat from the last two weeks so they can self-orient.
- **B**: Schedule a recurring weekly catch-up with the PM until the new member feels integrated.
- **C**: Ask the team member to introduce themselves to other stakeholders so they get added organically.
- **D**: Add them to all relevant channels and distribution lists immediately, brief them on key decisions to date, and pair them with a buddy for context.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The PMI onboarding response is direct: fix access, transfer decision context, and assign a peer guide — fast. (D) does all three. (A) overwhelms the new member and lacks context. (B) is helpful but doesn't address the immediate access gap. (C) puts the burden on the newcomer rather than fixing the structural issue.
