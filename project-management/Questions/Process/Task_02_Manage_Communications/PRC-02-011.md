# Question

A senior stakeholder tells the PM they "never know what's happening with the project," even though they are on the weekly status report distribution list. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Send the status report twice a week to give the stakeholder more visibility.
- **B**: Add the stakeholder to all project working group calls so they have direct exposure.
- **C**: Forward the most recent three status reports with a personal note highlighting the key items.
- **D**: Meet with the stakeholder briefly to learn what information they actually need, in what format, and how often, then update the comms plan.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The stakeholder is signalling that the current channel and content don't fit their needs — more of the same won't fix that. (D) diagnoses the gap directly. (A) increases volume without changing fit. (B) overwhelms them with operational detail they probably don't want. (C) is a one-off correction that doesn't address the structural mismatch.
