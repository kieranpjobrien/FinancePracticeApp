# Question

What is a RACI matrix used for in project communication?

## Options

- **A**: To rank team members by performance
- **B**: To clarify roles and communication responsibilities: who is Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, and Informed for each deliverable or decision
- **C**: To schedule meetings
- **D**: To track project budget

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. A RACI matrix maps deliverables or decisions against people, showing: who is Responsible (does the work), Accountable (ultimately answerable), Consulted (provides input), and Informed (kept updated). This clarifies communication paths and ensures appropriate involvement for each item.

Option A relates to performance management, not RACI.

Option C relates to scheduling.

Option D relates to financial management.
