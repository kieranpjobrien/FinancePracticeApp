# Question

Which formula gives the number of potential communication channels among n stakeholders?

## Options

- **A**: n(n-1)/2
- **B**: n(n+1)/2
- **C**: n^2
- **D**: 2n - 1

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The communication channels formula is n(n-1)/2 — every pair of stakeholders represents one channel, with the divide-by-two avoiding double counting. (A) is correct. (B) is the formula for the sum of integers 1 through n, not channel count. (C) counts ordered pairs including self-pairs. (D) is unrelated to the channel calculation.
