# Question

A team member posted an internal architecture diagram that contains commercially sensitive vendor pricing into a public Slack channel that includes contractors. Several team members have been similarly loose with confidential material. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Remove the post, speak privately with the team member, and define information-classification guidelines for the team.
- **B**: Issue a formal warning to the team member and copy their line manager so the disciplinary process is properly recorded.
- **C**: Lock down the public Slack channel and restrict membership to permanent staff only before any further incidents.
- **D**: Issue a team-wide reminder about confidentiality and assume it will resolve the broader behavioural pattern.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The immediate post needs cleaning up, the individual needs a respectful conversation, and the underlying gap (no classification or training) needs closing. (A) sequences all three. (B) escalates to discipline before coaching, which is disproportionate. (C) restricts a useful collaboration channel without addressing why information is being mishandled. (D) is the lightest possible response and won't change behaviour.
