# Question

A vendor is not responding to communications in a timely manner, causing project delays. Contract requirements specify 48-hour response times that aren't being met. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Continue waiting patiently
- **B**: Document the communication failures, escalate through agreed contract channels, and work with procurement/legal to enforce contract terms while seeking to understand and resolve underlying issues
- **C**: Stop working with the vendor entirely
- **D**: Work around the vendor without their involvement

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Contract non-compliance requires formal response. Document: keep records of communication attempts and response times. Escalate: use contract-defined escalation paths. Enforce: work with procurement/legal to ensure contract terms are met. Understand: is there a resolvable issue causing delays? This addresses the problem formally while leaving room for resolution.

Option A allows continued contract violation.

Option C may not be feasible and doesn't attempt resolution.

Option D creates scope/quality risks by bypassing vendor.
