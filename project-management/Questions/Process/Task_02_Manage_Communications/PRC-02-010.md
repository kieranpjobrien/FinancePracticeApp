# Question

What is the primary purpose of a communications management plan?

## Options

- **A**: To list all project documents
- **B**: To define how project information will be created, distributed, stored, and managed throughout the project
- **C**: To track meeting attendance
- **D**: To manage the project's email server

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. The communications management plan describes the project's communication requirements including: what information is needed, who needs it, when they need it, how it will be delivered, and who is responsible. It ensures stakeholders receive relevant information at the right time through appropriate channels.

Option A describes a document repository, not a communications plan.

Options C and D are specific activities, not the plan's purpose.
