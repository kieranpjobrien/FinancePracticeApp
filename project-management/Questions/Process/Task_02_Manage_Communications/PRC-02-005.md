# Question

A project has 10 stakeholders who all need to communicate with each other. How many potential communication channels exist?

## Options

- **A**: 10
- **B**: 20
- **C**: 45
- **D**: 100

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. The formula for communication channels is n(n-1)/2, where n is the number of stakeholders.

**Calculation:** 10 × (10-1) / 2 = 10 × 9 / 2 = 90 / 2 = **45 channels**

This formula helps project managers understand communication complexity. As team size grows, communication channels grow exponentially, which is why large projects need structured communication approaches.

Option A (10) counts only the stakeholders, not the connections between them.

Option B (20) might be a guess based on 10 × 2.

Option D (100) would be 10 × 10, not accounting for the formula.
