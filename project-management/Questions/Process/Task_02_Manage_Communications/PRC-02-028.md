# Question

What is "active listening" in communication?

## Options

- **A**: Listening while doing other tasks
- **B**: Fully concentrating on the speaker, understanding their message, responding thoughtfully, and confirming understanding
- **C**: Hearing words without processing meaning
- **D**: Waiting for your turn to speak

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Active listening involves: full attention to the speaker (not multitasking), seeking to understand their message and perspective, asking clarifying questions, reflecting back what you heard, and confirming understanding. It's a crucial skill for effective communication, especially in conflict resolution and requirements gathering.

Option A is distracted listening, not active.

Option C is passive hearing.

Option D focuses on your response rather than understanding.
