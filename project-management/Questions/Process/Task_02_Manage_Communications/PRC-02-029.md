# Question

A virtual team's chat exchanges keep being misread as hostile, even when intended neutrally. Two recent thread arguments have escalated and required the PM to mediate. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Move all team interaction to email so messages are more considered and less reactive in tone.
- **B**: Establish norms to use video for sensitive topics and agree a shared "assume positive intent" principle.
- **C**: Require all messages to be reviewed by the PM before being sent in shared channels.
- **D**: Schedule weekly team-building sessions to build relationships outside of regular work conversations.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Text loses tone; the fix is matching channel richness to the nature of the discussion and setting team norms. (B) does both and addresses the root cause. (A) is impractical and slow. (C) creates an absurd bottleneck and signals distrust. (D) helps over time but doesn't fix the immediate communication mode problem.
