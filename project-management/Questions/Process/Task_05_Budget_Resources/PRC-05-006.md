# Question

Two resources have been assigned to a critical task at 100% each, but the functional manager has just informed the PM that one will be pulled to another priority for the next two weeks. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Escalate to the sponsor to retain the resource.
- **B**: Assess the task impact, negotiate with the functional manager on timing or partial availability, and update the resource management plan.
- **C**: Reassign the task to the remaining resource at 200% capacity.
- **D**: Raise a change request to extend the project timeline immediately.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The PM's first move in a resource contention is to understand the real impact and negotiate at peer level. (B) follows that process. (A) escalating before negotiating wastes sponsor time and damages the relationship with the functional manager. (C) 200% capacity is unrealistic and unethical to assign. (D) a change request without first exploring alternatives is premature.
