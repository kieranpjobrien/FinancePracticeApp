# Question

The project requires expensive equipment that will only be used for two months. Purchasing costs $100,000; renting costs $20,000 per month. Which option is more cost-effective for this project, and what factors should be considered?

## Options

- **A**: Always purchase to own the asset
- **B**: Rent for $40,000 total, as it's less expensive for the usage period - though also consider if the equipment might be needed for future projects or resale value
- **C**: Always rent to avoid capital expenditure
- **D**: Cost doesn't matter for equipment decisions

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. For this usage period, renting ($40,000) is cheaper than purchasing ($100,000). However, decision factors include: will future projects need this equipment? Can purchased equipment be resold? Are there tax/depreciation benefits? What are maintenance costs? The basic financial comparison favours renting, but context matters.

Options A and C are absolute rules that don't consider specific situations.

Option D ignores relevant cost considerations.
