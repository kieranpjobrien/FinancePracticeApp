# Question

What does a RACI chart identify for project activities?

## Options

- **A**: The sequence of activities and their dependencies.
- **B**: The cost and duration estimates for each activity.
- **C**: The risk level and priority of each activity.
- **D**: Who is Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, and Informed for each activity.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

RACI is a responsibility assignment matrix — it maps activities to people by role: Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed. (A) describes a network diagram. (B) describes a cost-duration estimate, not RACI. (C) describes a risk register attribute.
