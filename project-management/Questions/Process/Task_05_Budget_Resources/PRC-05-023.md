# Question

The organisation uses a matrix structure. A functional manager is charging their staff's time to your project at higher rates than quoted, claiming the resources are more senior than originally assigned. The budget is being consumed faster than planned. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Accept the charges since the functional manager has authority
- **B**: Review the original resource agreements, document the discrepancy, discuss with the functional manager to understand the change, and escalate to the sponsor if the issue can't be resolved - this affects the project's approved budget
- **C**: Reduce the resources' hours to compensate
- **D**: Pay from personal funds

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Unexpected cost increases require investigation and resolution. Review: what was agreed? Document: what's happening differently? Discuss: understand the functional manager's perspective - was there a legitimate change? Escalate: if resolution isn't possible and it impacts approved budget, this is a governance issue. Resources should be provided at agreed rates.

Option A accepts a change without proper governance.

Option C may impact project delivery.

Option D is absurd and inappropriate.
