# Question

A project's BAC = $500,000, EV = $200,000, AC = $250,000. What is the Variance at Completion (VAC) assuming current CPI continues?

## Options

- **A**: -$50,000
- **B**: -$100,000
- **C**: $125,000
- **D**: -$125,000

## Answer

D

## Explanation

CPI = 200/250 = 0.80. EAC = BAC / CPI = 500,000 / 0.80 = $625,000. VAC = BAC - EAC = 500,000 - 625,000 = -$125,000 (over budget). (A) -$50,000 is cost variance at the status date (EV - AC). (B) -$100,000 doesn't match a standard formula. (C) $125,000 has the sign wrong; VAC here is negative.
