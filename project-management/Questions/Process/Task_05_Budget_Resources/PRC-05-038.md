# Question

A team member informs the PM they need to take extended personal leave during a critical project phase. They're highly skilled and difficult to replace. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Deny the leave request
- **B**: Work with the team member to understand timing and duration, develop coverage plans (cross-training, temporary replacement, workload redistribution), adjust schedule if necessary, and support their need while managing project impact
- **C**: Accept the project will fail
- **D**: Proceed without any contingency planning

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Personal circumstances happen. The PM doesn't typically have authority to deny personal leave. Work constructively: understand the situation, find solutions. Coverage: can others be trained? Can work be rescheduled? Temporary help? Support: show care for the team member while managing project needs. This balances human needs with project responsibilities.

Option A may not be appropriate or within PM authority.

Option C is defeatist without exploring options.

Option D ignores a known resource gap.
