# Question

The project uses contractors alongside employees. The contractors are more expensive per hour but don't require benefits, training investment, or ongoing commitment. How should the PM think about this resource mix?

## Options

- **A**: Always use the cheapest hourly rate
- **B**: Consider total cost and strategic factors: contractors for temporary peaks, specialised skills, or flexibility; employees for core capabilities, knowledge retention, and long-term efficiency
- **C**: Always use contractors to avoid commitment
- **D**: Always use employees for loyalty

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Resource mix decisions consider multiple factors. Contractors: good for variable demand, specialised skills, defined scope work. Employees: good for core capabilities, knowledge retention, ongoing work. Total cost: contractor rate may be higher but no benefits, training, or commitment. Strategic: what capabilities should be internal? Consider the full picture.

Options A, C, and D apply single rules to nuanced decisions.
