# Question

The project requires input from subject matter experts (SMEs) who report to other managers. Their managers are reluctant to release them for project work. How should the PM engage these stakeholders?

## Options

- **A**: Escalate to force manager compliance
- **B**: Engage the functional managers as stakeholders - understand their constraints, demonstrate project value, negotiate reasonable time commitments, and work with the sponsor to gain organisational support if needed
- **C**: Work without the SMEs
- **D**: Hire external consultants instead

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Functional managers are stakeholders too. Understand constraints: why are they reluctant? Resource pressure? Different priorities? Demonstrate value: help them see why the project matters to the organisation. Negotiate: find workable arrangements (part-time, specific phases). Sponsor support: if needed, get organisational backing for resource commitment.

Option A starts adversarially before building relationship.

Option C lacks critical expertise.

Option D may be expensive and still misses internal knowledge.
