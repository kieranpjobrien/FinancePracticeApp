# Question

What does the salience model add to stakeholder analysis that the power/interest grid does not?

## Options

- **A**: A financial weighting of each stakeholder's influence.
- **B**: A third dimension — urgency — in addition to power and legitimacy.
- **C**: A ranking of stakeholders by project lifecycle phase.
- **D**: A classification of stakeholders as internal or external.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The salience model evaluates stakeholders on power, legitimacy, and urgency — the third dimension being the key addition over a 2D power/interest grid. (A) there is no financial weighting dimension. (C) phase-based ranking is a different tool. (D) internal/external is a classification attribute, not a dimension of the salience model.
