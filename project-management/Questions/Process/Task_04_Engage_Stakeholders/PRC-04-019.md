# Question

Who is typically responsible for engaging with external stakeholders such as regulatory bodies and government agencies?

## Options

- **A**: Only the most junior team member
- **B**: The PM should understand these relationships but may involve appropriate organisational roles (legal, compliance, government relations) for formal engagement
- **C**: External stakeholders should never be engaged
- **D**: External stakeholders will engage themselves

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. External stakeholder engagement often requires specific expertise and authority. The PM should understand these relationships and their project impact, but formal engagement may involve legal (for regulatory matters), compliance (for regulatory bodies), or government relations (for government agencies). The PM coordinates but may not directly handle all external engagement.

Option A doesn't match the importance of these relationships.

Option C ignores important stakeholders.

Option D is passive about critical relationships.
