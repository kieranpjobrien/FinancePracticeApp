# Question

A stakeholder raises a concern in a status meeting. The PM doesn't have the information to respond immediately. What is the BEST action?

## Options

- **A**: Acknowledge the concern, log it, and commit to a specific follow-up date with the stakeholder.
- **B**: Provide a best-guess answer on the spot to avoid looking uninformed in front of the group.
- **C**: Defer the discussion to the next status meeting without making a specific commitment to respond.
- **D**: Suggest the stakeholder raise the concern with a different team member who may know more about it.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Honest acknowledgement plus a committed follow-up is the professional response. (A) respects the stakeholder and builds credibility. (B) guessing damages credibility when the guess turns out wrong. (C) passive deferral signals the concern isn't important. (D) redirecting shifts responsibility away from the PM, who should track and close issues raised at their forums.
