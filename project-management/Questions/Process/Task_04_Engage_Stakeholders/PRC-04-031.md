# Question

What does "keep satisfied" mean as a stakeholder engagement strategy?

## Options

- **A**: Provide them with snacks and beverages
- **B**: For high-power, low-interest stakeholders, ensure their needs are met and they remain supportive without overwhelming them with information they don't want
- **C**: Satisfy all their demands regardless of impact
- **D**: Keep them away from the project

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. "Keep satisfied" is the strategy for high-power, low-interest stakeholders. They can significantly impact the project but don't want to be deeply involved. Ensure their key needs are met, don't create problems that draw their attention, and provide appropriate (not overwhelming) information. The goal is maintaining their support without demanding their time.

Option A is literal, not strategic.

Option C doesn't consider project constraints.

Option D ignores important stakeholders.
