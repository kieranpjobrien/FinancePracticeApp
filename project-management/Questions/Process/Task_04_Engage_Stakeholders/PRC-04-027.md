# Question

What is a stakeholder register?

## Options

- **A**: A cash register for stakeholder payments
- **B**: A document that lists stakeholders, their interests, involvement level, and potential impact on project success
- **C**: A sign-in sheet for meetings
- **D**: A list of stakeholder complaints

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. The stakeholder register documents identified stakeholders and relevant information about them: contact details, role, interest in the project, assessment of their power/interest/attitude, potential impact on the project, and engagement strategies. It's a key tool for managing stakeholder relationships throughout the project.

Options A, C, and D misunderstand the concept.
