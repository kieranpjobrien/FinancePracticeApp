# Question

A project manager identifies that a stakeholder has high power but low interest in the project. According to stakeholder analysis, what engagement strategy is MOST appropriate?

## Options

- **A**: Manage closely with frequent detailed updates
- **B**: Keep satisfied with periodic updates on matters that affect them
- **C**: Monitor with minimal effort
- **D**: Keep informed with regular general updates

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. The power/interest grid suggests "keep satisfied" for high-power, low-interest stakeholders. They don't want detailed involvement but have authority that could impact the project. Periodic updates on relevant matters maintain goodwill without overwhelming them with information they don't want.

Option A (manage closely) is for high-power, high-interest stakeholders who want active involvement.

Option C (monitor) is for low-power, low-interest stakeholders who neither need nor want engagement.

Option D (keep informed) is for low-power, high-interest stakeholders who want information but lack authority.
