# Question

During project initiation, the PM is building the stakeholder register. Which source provides the MOST comprehensive starting input?

## Options

- **A**: Project charter, business documents, agreements, and interviews with the sponsor and project initiators.
- **B**: Prior project documentation from the same department.
- **C**: Organisational chart of the sponsoring business unit.
- **D**: The functional manager's list of available resources.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Stakeholder identification draws on multiple inputs — the charter names key parties, business documents frame who benefits or is affected, agreements identify external parties, and interviews surface less obvious stakeholders. (B) prior project documents capture one narrow slice and may miss stakeholders specific to this project. (C) an org chart shows internal people but misses external stakeholders, regulators, end users, and suppliers. (D) functional manager resource lists only cover internal contributors, not broader affected parties.
