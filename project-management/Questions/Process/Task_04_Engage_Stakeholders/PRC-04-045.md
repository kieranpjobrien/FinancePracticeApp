# Question

The PM is facilitating an online survey of 200 stakeholders to assess engagement. Response rate has been 12%. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Accept the 12% as representative and report the results.
- **B**: Extend the survey deadline indefinitely until response rate improves.
- **C**: Send repeated reminder emails to the 88% who haven't responded.
- **D**: Investigate why response is low (length, timing, relevance), adjust the instrument or approach, and re-launch with targeted outreach to key groups.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A 12% response rate is unreliable. (D) diagnoses and fixes rather than forcing the broken instrument. (A) reporting on a 12% sample misrepresents the stakeholder population. (B) indefinite extension won't improve quality without addressing root causes. (C) repeated reminders annoy stakeholders and often reduce future response rates without solving the real problem.
