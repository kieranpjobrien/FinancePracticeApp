# Question

What is stakeholder salience?

## Options

- **A**: How salty stakeholders are about the project
- **B**: The degree to which stakeholders demand attention based on their power, legitimacy, and urgency
- **C**: Stakeholder salary information
- **D**: The distance stakeholders are from the project

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Stakeholder salience theory identifies three attributes that determine how much attention a stakeholder demands: Power (ability to influence), Legitimacy (appropriateness of their involvement), and Urgency (time-sensitivity of their claims). Stakeholders with all three attributes are most salient and require immediate attention.

Options A, C, and D misunderstand the term.
