# Question

A PM is about to kick off a new project. Who should be invited to the kickoff meeting?

## Options

- **A**: Only the core delivery team to keep the meeting focused.
- **B**: The full organisation so everyone is aware of the project.
- **C**: Core team, sponsor, key stakeholders identified in the register, and representatives of major affected groups.
- **D**: Executives only, with the team onboarded separately afterward.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Kickoff aligns everyone with a legitimate interest in the project — team, sponsor, key stakeholders, and major affected groups. (A) omitting sponsor and stakeholders produces a team meeting, not a kickoff. (B) inviting the full organisation is wasteful and dilutes the event. (D) executives-only kickoff disconnects them from the team that will deliver and signals hierarchy over collaboration.
