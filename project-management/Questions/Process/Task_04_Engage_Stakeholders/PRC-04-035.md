# Question

Why is stakeholder identification an ongoing process throughout the project?

## Options

- **A**: It's not - stakeholders are only identified at the start
- **B**: New stakeholders may emerge as the project progresses, existing stakeholder characteristics may change, and organisational changes may bring new people into relevant roles
- **C**: To create more paperwork
- **D**: To give the PM something to do

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Stakeholder landscapes change. New stakeholders: as the project progresses, new groups may become affected or interested. Changes: existing stakeholders may shift power, interest, or attitude. Organisational changes: new hires, reorganisations, and departures change who needs to be engaged. Regular review ensures the stakeholder register stays current.

Option A ignores dynamic nature of stakeholder environments.

Options C and D misunderstand the purpose.
