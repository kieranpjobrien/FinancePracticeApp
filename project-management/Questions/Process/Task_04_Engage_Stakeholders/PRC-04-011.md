# Question

In the power/interest grid, how should stakeholders with high power and high interest be managed?

## Options

- **A**: Keep satisfied.
- **B**: Manage closely.
- **C**: Keep informed.
- **D**: Monitor.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

High power, high interest stakeholders are managed closely — they have authority and care about outcomes, so active engagement is warranted. (A) keep satisfied applies to high power, low interest. (C) keep informed applies to low power, high interest. (D) monitor applies to low power, low interest. Mislabelling this quadrant risks both under-engagement and loss of influence.
