# Question

What is a stakeholder engagement assessment matrix?

## Options

- **A**: A tool to evaluate stakeholder job performance
- **B**: A tool that compares each stakeholder's current engagement level to their desired engagement level for project success
- **C**: A financial analysis of stakeholder investments
- **D**: A seating chart for meetings

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. The stakeholder engagement assessment matrix plots stakeholders' current engagement level (Unaware, Resistant, Neutral, Supportive, Leading) against the desired engagement level. Gaps between current and desired states inform engagement strategies - who needs to be moved from resistant to supportive, for example.

Options A, C, and D describe unrelated tools.
