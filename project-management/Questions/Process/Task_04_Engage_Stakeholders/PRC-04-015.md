# Question

What is the power/interest grid used for in stakeholder management?

## Options

- **A**: To assign salaries to stakeholders
- **B**: To classify stakeholders by their level of authority (power) and concern about the project (interest) to determine engagement strategies
- **C**: To generate electricity for the project
- **D**: To determine meeting schedules

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. The power/interest grid categorises stakeholders in a 2x2 matrix: power (ability to influence the project) on one axis, interest (concern about project outcomes) on the other. This classification helps determine appropriate engagement strategies for different stakeholder groups.

Options A, C, and D are unrelated to stakeholder classification.
