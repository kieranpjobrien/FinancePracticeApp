# Question

The resource plan shows a key SME engaged from weeks 8-12. The procurement plan shows that SME's contract only covers weeks 1-10. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Resolve the misalignment between the resource and procurement plans before either is finalised, by analysing options (extend contract, reschedule the work, or substitute resource) and updating both plans consistently.
- **B**: Initiate a contract extension and update the resource plan to match.
- **C**: Compress the work into weeks 8-10 to fit within the existing contract.
- **D**: Log the gap as a risk and proceed with both plans as drafted.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The integration discipline is to surface the conflict, analyse the levers, and reconcile the affected plans before baselining. (B) commits to one solution without comparing. (C) commits to another without comparing and likely degrades the work. (D) baselines a known-broken plan.
