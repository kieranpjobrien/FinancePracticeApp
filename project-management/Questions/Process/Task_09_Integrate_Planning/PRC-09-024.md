# Question

The resource management plan shows a key specialist needed from weeks 8-12. The procurement plan shows their contract only covers weeks 1-10. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Extend the contract through procurement procedures
- **B**: Compress the work to fit weeks 8-10
- **C**: Resolve the misalignment before finalising either plan
- **D**: Flag as a risk and proceed with current plans

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. This is an integration gap - two plans don't align. Before finalising, the PM should resolve the conflict. Options might include extending the contract, adjusting the schedule, finding alternative resources, or accepting the risk. But the plans shouldn't be baselined with a known conflict.

Option A is one possible solution but assumes it's the right one without analysis.

Option B is another possible solution but may not be feasible.

Option D accepts misalignment rather than resolving it. Plans should be internally consistent.
