# Question

A new team member asks the PM whether release planning, sprint planning, or daily planning is the most important agile planning event. What is the best answer?

## Options

- **A**: They form an integrated planning system at three horizons — strategic (release), tactical (sprint), and operational (daily) — and each depends on the others.
- **B**: Release planning, because it sets the strategic direction the rest must follow.
- **C**: Sprint planning, because it determines what is actually committed and built.
- **D**: Daily planning, because the day's coordination is what unblocks the next deliverable.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Agile planning is multi-horizon by design; treating any one level as supreme distorts the others. (B), (C), and (D) each elevate one event over the integrated system. The PMI/agile answer recognises that strategy, tactics, and execution all depend on each other.
