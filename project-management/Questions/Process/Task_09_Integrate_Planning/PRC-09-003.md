# Question

In drafting the project management plan, the PM notices the schedule baseline runs to month 10 but the resource plan only secures the team through month 8. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Submit the plan as drafted and resolve the resource gap during execution when constraints become concrete.
- **B**: Extend the resource allocation through month 10 without adjusting the cost baseline.
- **C**: Cut scope to fit within the eight-month resource window.
- **D**: Reconcile the schedule and resource plans, then update the cost baseline so the plan is internally consistent before approval.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Integrated planning requires consistency across subsidiary plans before baselining; a known two-month resource gap is an unsigned-off problem the PM must resolve up front. (A) baselines a known conflict. (B) extends resources without funding them, embedding a cost overrun on day one. (C) jumps to one solution without analysing alternatives. Only (D) reflects integration discipline.
