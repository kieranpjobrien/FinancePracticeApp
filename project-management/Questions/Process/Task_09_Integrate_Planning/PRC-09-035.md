# Question

During planning, the quality team requests additional testing environments that weren't in the original budget. The schedule depends on testing starting on time. How should the PM handle this integration challenge?

## Options

- **A**: Approve the additional budget since quality is critical
- **B**: Delay testing until budget is resolved through change control
- **C**: Evaluate trade-offs: budget increase, schedule extension, scope reduction, or risk acceptance
- **D**: Have the team work around the constraint with existing environments

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. This is a classic integration challenge involving cost, schedule, quality, and risk. The PM should analyse options: approve the cost increase, accept schedule delay, reduce scope requiring less testing, or accept quality risk with fewer environments. The decision should be made with full understanding of trade-offs.

Option A makes a budget decision without proper analysis or authority.

Option B accepts delay without exploring alternatives.

Option D may compromise quality without explicit trade-off discussion.
