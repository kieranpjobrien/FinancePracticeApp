# Question

During planning, the QA lead requests two additional test environments not in the budget; without them the test schedule slips. How should the project manager handle this integration issue?

## Options

- **A**: Approve the additional budget unilaterally because quality is non-negotiable.
- **B**: Defer the test start until budget is increased through change control.
- **C**: Direct the team to make do with the existing environments and absorb the schedule risk.
- **D**: Lay out the trade-offs — budget increase, schedule extension, scope reduction, or accepted quality/risk trade-off — and route the chosen option through change control for sponsor and CCB decision.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

This is a textbook integration problem touching cost, schedule, quality, and risk; no single dimension can be unilaterally optimised. The PM should make the trade-offs visible and let governance decide. (A) bypasses authority. (B) commits to one option without analysis. (C) silently accepts quality risk and breaks the team's ability to escalate.
