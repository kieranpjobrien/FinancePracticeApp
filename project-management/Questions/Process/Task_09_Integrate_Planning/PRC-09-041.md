# Question

A project involves multiple teams in different time zones. The communications plan specifies synchronous daily meetings, but several team members can't attend due to time zone conflicts. What integration issue is present?

## Options

- **A**: The communications plan wasn't aligned with the stakeholder engagement plan
- **B**: The communications plan wasn't aligned with the resource management plan
- **C**: The meetings should be mandatory regardless of time zones
- **D**: Time zone differences should have been noted as a project risk

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. The communications plan defined meetings without considering the resource constraints (team member locations and availability). Integration means ensuring plans work together. A communications approach that doesn't account for where resources are located is poorly integrated.

Option A might also apply but B is more direct - the communication approach conflicts with the actual resources.

Option C ignores legitimate constraints. Mandatory meetings at bad times harm team wellbeing and performance.

Option D notes the symptom but doesn't identify it as a planning integration issue.
