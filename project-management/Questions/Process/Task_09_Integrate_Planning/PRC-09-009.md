# Question

What is the primary purpose of the project management plan?

## Options

- **A**: To define how the project is executed, monitored, controlled, and closed across all subsidiary plans.
- **B**: To document the business case and the original justification for undertaking the project.
- **C**: To list all project deliverables together with their formal acceptance criteria for sign-off.
- **D**: To capture lessons learned for use on future similar projects across the organisation.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The project management plan is the integrated master plan; its purpose is to govern execution and control. (B) is the role of the business case. (C) is the role of the scope statement. (D) is the role of the lessons learned register.
