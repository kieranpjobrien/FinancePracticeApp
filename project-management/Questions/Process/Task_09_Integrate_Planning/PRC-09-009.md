# Question

What is the primary purpose of the project management plan?

## Options

- **A**: To define how the project will be executed, monitored, controlled, and closed by integrating all subsidiary plans and baselines.
- **B**: To document the business case and the original justification for the project.
- **C**: To list all project deliverables together with their formal acceptance criteria.
- **D**: To capture lessons learned for use on future similar projects.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The project management plan is the integrated master plan; its purpose is to govern execution and control. (B) is the role of the business case. (C) is the role of the scope statement. (D) is the role of the lessons learned register.
