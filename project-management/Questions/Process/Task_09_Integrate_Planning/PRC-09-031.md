# Question

Which planning activity typically occurs LAST when developing the project management plan?

## Options

- **A**: Identifying stakeholders during the initiation phase.
- **B**: Defining the project scope statement during planning.
- **C**: Integrating subsidiary plans and baselines into a cohesive plan.
- **D**: Creating the work breakdown structure from the scope.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Integration is the final assembly step; it depends on having the subsidiary plans (scope, schedule, cost, quality, risk, etc.) already drafted. (A) is initiation, the earliest. (B) and (D) are mid-planning steps that feed integration.
