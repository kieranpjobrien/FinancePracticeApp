# Question

Which statement best describes tailoring in PMI project management?

## Options

- **A**: Adapting processes, tools, and life cycle to fit the project's size, complexity, and context.
- **B**: Reducing documentation across the project to save time and administrative overhead.
- **C**: Choosing exclusively predictive or exclusively agile delivery across the entire project.
- **D**: Producing simplified plans whenever the project budget is considered too small to justify.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Tailoring is broad: it spans process, lifecycle, governance, artefacts, and tools, and the trigger is project context, not just size. (B) is one possible outcome of tailoring, not the concept. (C) treats methodology as binary; tailoring often produces hybrid approaches. (D) reduces tailoring to budget alone.
