# Question

Why is the integration knowledge area considered uniquely the project manager's responsibility?

## Options

- **A**: It requires the most technical expertise
- **B**: It requires seeing the project as a whole and balancing competing demands
- **C**: It involves the most stakeholder communication
- **D**: It has the most processes of any knowledge area

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Integration requires a holistic view that sees how all parts fit together. While functional specialists own their domains (cost analysts own cost, engineers own technical work), only the PM is positioned to balance competing demands across all areas and ensure the project works as a coherent whole.

Option A is incorrect. Integration is more about coordination than technical expertise.

Option C describes communications management, not specifically integration.

Option D isn't accurate, and process count doesn't explain why it's uniquely the PM's responsibility.
