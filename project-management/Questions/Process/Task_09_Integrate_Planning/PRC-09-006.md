# Question

The communications plan specifies a weekly stakeholder meeting requiring two hours of preparation per week, but the resource and schedule plans contain no allocation for that preparation. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Cancel the weekly stakeholder meeting to free the time.
- **B**: Update the schedule and resource plan to include the preparation effort, and reissue the project management plan for re-approval.
- **C**: Ask team members to prepare on personal time to absorb the gap.
- **D**: Proceed without preparation and review whether stakeholders raise concerns.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Integrated plans must be internally consistent. The right move is to update the affected plans and baselines so the work appears where it is owned. (A) solves the inconsistency by removing a planned communication channel, which has its own consequences. (C) treats team time as free and is unsustainable. (D) consciously delivers low-quality stakeholder engagement.
