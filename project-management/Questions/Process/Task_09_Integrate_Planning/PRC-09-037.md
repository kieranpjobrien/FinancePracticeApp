# Question

A government project requires compliance with multiple regulatory frameworks that have overlapping but slightly different requirements. Some team members interpret requirements differently. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Choose the strictest interpretation to ensure compliance
- **B**: Let each team member follow their own interpretation
- **C**: Develop a compliance matrix that clarifies how the project will meet all requirements consistently
- **D**: Hire a compliance specialist to make all regulatory decisions

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. A compliance matrix maps requirements to project approaches, ensuring nothing is missed and interpretations are consistent. Where frameworks overlap, the matrix shows how one approach satisfies multiple requirements. This creates shared understanding and auditable compliance.

Option A may be overly burdensome and still leave ambiguity about specific interpretations.

Option B creates inconsistency and potential gaps or conflicts in compliance approaches.

Option D may be part of the solution but doesn't address the integration need for a consistent project-wide approach.
