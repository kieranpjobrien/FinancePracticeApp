# Question

A project has dependencies on another project managed by a different PM. The other project's schedule is slipping, threatening this project's timeline. What integration activity is needed?

## Options

- **A**: Update this project's schedule to reflect the dependency delay
- **B**: Escalate to portfolio management for cross-project coordination
- **C**: Remove the dependency by finding alternative approaches
- **D**: Wait for the other project to provide updated timelines

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Cross-project dependencies require portfolio-level integration. Neither PM has authority over the other's project. Portfolio management can assess priorities, reallocate resources, or make trade-offs across projects that individual PMs cannot.

Option A accepts the delay without exploring alternatives or escalating appropriately.

Option C may be technically impossible or more expensive than other options. It should be considered but isn't automatically the answer.

Option D is passive. The PM should proactively manage the dependency, not wait.
