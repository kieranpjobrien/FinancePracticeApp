# Question

Customers are complaining about a defect in production. Engineering says a proper fix will take two weeks. Customer support is asking for relief sooner. What should the project manager facilitate?

## Options

- **A**: Tell engineering to compress the work into a single sprint to meet customer support's expectations, accepting any quality risk.
- **B**: Explore a partial mitigation for the most critical customer impact within days while engineering completes the full fix.
- **C**: Ask customer support to manage the customer relationship and wait the full two weeks for the engineering fix to land.
- **D**: Escalate to the sponsor and ask them to choose between speed and quality before engineering does any further work.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A staged response is usually available: a fast partial mitigation now and a proper fix soon. (B) gets that conversation going. (A) compresses engineering work without considering quality risk. (C) leaves customers unprotected for two weeks. (D) escalates a decision the team can usually solve themselves.
