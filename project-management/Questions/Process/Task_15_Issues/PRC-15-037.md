# Question

A customer-facing issue is causing complaints. The technical team says it will take two weeks to fix properly. Customer support is asking for something faster. What approach should the project manager facilitate?

## Options

- **A**: Tell customer support to wait two weeks
- **B**: Tell the technical team to fix it faster regardless of quality
- **C**: Explore whether a partial or temporary fix could address the most critical customer impacts quickly, while the full fix is developed over two weeks
- **D**: Escalate to let someone else decide

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. This is a common situation where a quick partial solution and a thorough complete solution aren't mutually exclusive. Can the worst customer impacts be mitigated quickly while the full solution is developed? This satisfies customer needs while maintaining technical quality. The PM facilitates this creative problem-solving.

Option A doesn't explore intermediate options.

Option B may compromise quality and create new problems.

Option D doesn't try to find a solution before escalating.
