# Question

The team raises the same retro item every sprint, nothing changes, and people are starting to roll their eyes during retrospectives. What should the Scrum Master do?

## Options

- **A**: Quietly remove the recurring item from the next retro template so the team can focus on fresh issues.
- **B**: Cancel retrospectives for a few sprints since they have stopped producing value.
- **C**: Pick the persistent item, commit a concrete action with an owner and deadline at the next retro, and ensure the action is completed and reported back so the team sees that retros lead to change.
- **D**: Escalate the team's cynicism to engineering leadership and request coaching for the team.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Retros only work when they produce visible action. (C) breaks the cynicism cycle by making one promise and keeping it. (A) hides the issue. (B) abandons the practice rather than fixing it. (D) treats a process failure as a people problem and skips the cheaper fix.
