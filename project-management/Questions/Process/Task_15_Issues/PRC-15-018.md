# Question

What is the purpose of assigning an owner to each issue?

## Options

- **A**: To have someone to blame if the issue isn't resolved
- **B**: To establish clear accountability for driving the issue to resolution
- **C**: To limit who can work on the issue
- **D**: To reduce the project manager's workload

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Assigning an owner creates clear accountability: one person is responsible for ensuring the issue is addressed, even if others help with resolution. Without ownership, issues can fall through the cracks with everyone assuming someone else is handling it.

Option A frames ownership as blame assignment rather than accountability.

Option C misunderstands ownership - the owner drives resolution but others can contribute.

Option D is a side benefit but not the purpose.
