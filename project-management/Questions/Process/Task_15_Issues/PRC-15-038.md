# Question

Why does PMI recommend categorising issues in the issue log?

## Options

- **A**: Because it is required for SOX and ISO 9001 audit compliance.
- **B**: Because it slows the rate at which new issues can be added, encouraging higher-quality entries.
- **C**: Because it enables pattern analysis, appropriate routing, and targeted process improvements.
- **D**: Because it produces a longer log that is more useful in lessons-learned reviews.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Categorisation supports operational management and learning. (C) is the standard reason: spot patterns, route to the right specialists, and feed process improvement. (A) overstates audit drivers. (B) treats friction as a feature. (D) confuses log volume with log usefulness.
