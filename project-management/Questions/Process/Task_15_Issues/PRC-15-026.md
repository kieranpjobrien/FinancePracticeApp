# Question

When is it appropriate to close an entry in the issue log?

## Options

- **A**: At the end of each phase as part of the regular phase-gate housekeeping.
- **B**: Once 30 calendar days have passed without further activity on the entry.
- **C**: When the underlying problem has been resolved and verified, or when stakeholders have formally accepted the residual impact.
- **D**: When the assigned owner judges that the issue is no longer worth tracking.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

(C) is PMI's standard: close on verified resolution or on documented acceptance of the impact. (A) closes by calendar rather than by status. (B) lets ageing rather than resolution drive closure. (D) leaves closure to a single individual's judgement without the verification or acceptance step.
