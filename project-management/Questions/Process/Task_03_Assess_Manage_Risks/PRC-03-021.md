# Question

What is a risk trigger?

## Options

- **A**: The person who identified the risk
- **B**: An indicator or warning sign that a risk is about to occur or has occurred
- **C**: The button that activates risk management
- **D**: The first risk on the register

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. A risk trigger is a symptom or warning sign indicating that a risk may be about to occur or has begun to materialise. Identifying triggers helps with early detection and timely response. For example, a vendor missing milestone payments might trigger concerns about their financial stability.

Options A, C, and D misunderstand the concept of risk triggers.
