# Question

Which risk response strategy is appropriate when a project manager purchases insurance to cover potential damage from a severe weather risk on a construction project?

## Options

- **A**: Avoid
- **B**: Mitigate
- **C**: Accept
- **D**: Transfer

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Insurance shifts the financial impact of a risk to a third party, which is the definition of transfer. (A) avoidance would eliminate the risk by changing scope or approach. (B) mitigation reduces probability or impact directly (e.g., reinforced structures), not via a third party. (C) acceptance takes no proactive action and retains the consequences; buying insurance is an active response.
