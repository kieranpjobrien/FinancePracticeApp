# Question

What is a risk owner?

## Options

- **A**: The person who created the risk
- **B**: A person assigned responsibility for monitoring a risk and implementing its response if needed
- **C**: The project sponsor
- **D**: The risk management software vendor

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. A risk owner is assigned responsibility for a specific risk. They monitor the risk for trigger conditions, implement response plans if the risk materialises, and report on risk status. Assigning owners ensures accountability - someone is specifically watching each significant risk.

Option A confuses risk source with ownership.

Option C may own some risks but not all.

Option D misunderstands "owner."
