# Question

A Scrum team holds a retrospective and identifies three impediments related to recurring risks. The Scrum Master wants to add them to the risk register but the PM on the same hybrid project says they belong in the impediment log. What is the BEST approach?

## Options

- **A**: Log current impediments in the impediment log and log recurring patterns as risks in the register.
- **B**: Keep only the impediment log and abandon the risk register to avoid duplication.
- **C**: Keep only the risk register and abandon the impediment log since risks include impediments.
- **D**: Let each role maintain their own log independently to preserve autonomy.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Hybrid projects rightly use both tools: the impediment log handles current blockers at sprint granularity; the risk register tracks potential future events and patterns. (A) distinguishes the two by time horizon and purpose. (B) and (C) each suppress a legitimate tool — PMI expects tailoring, not elimination. (D) independent parallel logs cause drift and confusion about what's authoritative.
