# Question

What does "exploit" mean as a strategy for positive risks (opportunities)?

## Options

- **A**: Taking unfair advantage of others
- **B**: Taking action to ensure the opportunity definitely occurs and the project benefits from it
- **C**: Ignoring the opportunity
- **D**: Sharing the opportunity with competitors

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. "Exploit" in risk management means taking action to ensure an opportunity is captured. If there's a chance to finish early, assign the best resources to make it happen. If there's a potential cost saving, lock it in. The goal is to convert the opportunity from possible to certain.

Option A misuses the word in a negative sense.

Option C is accepting, not exploiting.

Option D is sharing, a different strategy.
