# Question

Which document captures the organisation's approach, tolerances, roles, and categories for managing risks on a project?

## Options

- **A**: Risk register.
- **B**: Risk management plan.
- **C**: Risk breakdown structure.
- **D**: Risk report.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The risk management plan defines how risks will be managed — tolerances, roles, categories, approach, cadence. (A) the risk register lists identified risks with probability, impact, and response. (C) the RBS is the hierarchical categorisation of risk sources, which the plan references but is narrower in scope. (D) the risk report summarises overall project risk exposure at a point in time.
