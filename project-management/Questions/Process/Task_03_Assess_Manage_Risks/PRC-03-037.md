# Question

What is residual risk?

## Options

- **A**: Risk that exists before any response
- **B**: Risk that remains after risk responses have been implemented
- **C**: Risk from previous projects
- **D**: The most recent risk identified

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Residual risk is what remains after mitigation actions have been taken. Risk responses reduce but rarely eliminate risk entirely. Understanding residual risk helps determine if additional response is needed or if the remaining risk level is acceptable.

Option A describes inherent or gross risk.

Option C relates to historical risks.

Option D relates to timing of identification.
