# Question

In an agile project, which artifact provides transparency into the work remaining in the sprint?

## Options

- **A**: Product backlog
- **B**: Sprint backlog
- **C**: Sprint burndown chart
- **D**: Release plan

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. The sprint burndown chart shows work remaining over time within a sprint, providing daily visibility into whether the team is on track to complete sprint commitments. It's a key transparency and tracking artifact.

Option A (product backlog) shows all potential work for the product, not sprint-specific remaining work.

Option B (sprint backlog) shows what's in the sprint but doesn't visualise progress over time as clearly as the burndown.

Option D (release plan) shows longer-term release scope and timing, not sprint-level remaining work.
