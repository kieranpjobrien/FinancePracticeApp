# Question

In Scrum, which artifact provides daily transparency into the work remaining to meet the sprint goal?

## Options

- **A**: The overall product backlog of work
- **B**: The multi-sprint release plan schedule
- **C**: The sprint backlog with a sprint burndown
- **D**: The delivered product increment itself

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The sprint backlog combined with a sprint burndown chart shows the work remaining within the current sprint, updated daily so the team can see whether they are on track. (A) shows all known work for the product, not sprint progress. (B) shows scope and timing for upcoming releases, not daily sprint status. (D) is the working product output of the sprint, not a tracking artifact.
