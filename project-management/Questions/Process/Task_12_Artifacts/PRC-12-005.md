# Question

The PM spends four hours every Friday assembling a PowerPoint executive status report by manually copying numbers and charts out of Jira. The executives value the format and the cadence. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Configure Jira dashboards and an export template that feed the recurring sections of the executive report automatically, leaving only the narrative for the PM to write.
- **B**: Tell the executives the report will be discontinued and provide them with direct Jira access instead.
- **C**: Delegate the report assembly to a junior team member to free up the PM's time.
- **D**: Reduce the report cadence from weekly to monthly to recover three hours per week.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The right move is to remove the repetitive effort while preserving the format and cadence stakeholders value; tooling and templates can produce the recurring data sections automatically. (B) imposes the team's tools on executives and abandons effective communication. (C) shifts effort instead of removing it. (D) sacrifices stakeholder visibility to save PM time.
