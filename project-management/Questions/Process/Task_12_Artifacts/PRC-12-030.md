# Question

What type of project artifact tracks risks throughout the project lifecycle?

## Options

- **A**: Issue log
- **B**: Change log
- **C**: Risk register
- **D**: Lessons learned register

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. The risk register documents identified risks, their probability and impact, planned responses, risk owners, and current status. It is actively maintained throughout the project to track risks from identification through closure or occurrence.

Option A tracks issues (problems that have occurred), not risks (uncertain events that may occur).

Option B tracks change requests and their status.

Option D captures insights from project experiences for future reference.
