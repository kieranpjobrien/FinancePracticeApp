# Question

Which artifact tracks identified risks, their analysis, owners, and planned responses throughout the project lifecycle?

## Options

- **A**: Risk register
- **B**: Issue log
- **C**: Change log
- **D**: Assumption log

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The risk register is the living record of identified risks with probability, impact, category, owner, and response. (B) tracks issues that have already occurred, not uncertain future events. (C) tracks change requests and their status. (D) records assumptions and constraints underpinning the plan; those may turn into risks, but they are not the risk register itself.
