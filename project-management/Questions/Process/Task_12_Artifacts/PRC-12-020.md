# Question

Which artifact captures every change request raised on a project along with its status?

## Options

- **A**: Risk register
- **B**: Change log
- **C**: Issue log
- **D**: Project charter

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The change log records every change request — submitted, approved, rejected, or deferred — providing a single source of truth for change control. (A) tracks identified threats and opportunities, not changes. (C) tracks issues that require resolution, which may or may not become change requests. (D) authorises the project and documents initial scope but does not track ongoing changes.
