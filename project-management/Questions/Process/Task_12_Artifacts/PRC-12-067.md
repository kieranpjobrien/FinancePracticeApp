# Question

The comms plan written at initiation has the PM emailing a weekly status to all 40 stakeholders. Engagement data shows only seven open it and two ever respond. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Continue sending since the comms plan is approved and stakeholders can choose for themselves whether to read it.
- **B**: Stop the email and rely on stakeholders to seek out project status from the repository when they need to.
- **C**: Segment stakeholders by role and information need, choose the right channel for each segment, and revise the plan.
- **D**: Add tracking pixels and follow up with non-openers individually to understand why they are not currently engaging properly.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

PMI expects the comms plan to be a living artifact tailored to actual stakeholder needs and channels. (C) redesigns around evidence of real engagement. (A) continues a channel the data shows is not working. (B) removes a channel without designing a replacement. (D) chases engagement without questioning whether the channel itself is right.
