# Question

What is the primary purpose of an assumption log?

## Options

- **A**: To list constraints that limit project options.
- **B**: To track stakeholder requirements approved during planning.
- **C**: To capture risks identified during planning.
- **D**: To document conditions assumed to be true and track whether they remain valid.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The assumption log records what you took to be true when planning and is monitored across execution because invalid assumptions often become risks or change drivers. (A) confuses assumptions with constraints — related but distinct artifacts. (B) describes the requirements documentation. (C) describes the risk register.
