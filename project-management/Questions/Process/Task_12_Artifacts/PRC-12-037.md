# Question

What is the primary purpose of maintaining an assumption log throughout the project?

## Options

- **A**: To record project risks
- **B**: To document the conditions believed to be true that form the basis for project planning, and to track their validity
- **C**: To list project constraints
- **D**: To capture stakeholder requirements

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. The assumption log documents assumptions made during planning (conditions believed to be true without proof) and tracks whether they prove valid during execution. Invalid assumptions may require plan adjustments, making this an important monitoring artifact.

Option A describes the risk register's purpose.

Option C describes constraint documentation, which may be in the assumption log but isn't its primary purpose.

Option D describes requirements documentation.
