# Question

Which artifact would BEST help a project manager track all project deliverables and their completion status?

## Options

- **A**: Risk register
- **B**: Stakeholder register
- **C**: Deliverables log or requirements traceability matrix
- **D**: Issue log

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. A deliverables log tracks all project deliverables and their status. A requirements traceability matrix links requirements to deliverables and tracks completion. Both are designed specifically to ensure all deliverables are identified, tracked, and completed.

Option A tracks risks, not deliverables.

Option B tracks stakeholders and their engagement needs.

Option D tracks issues that need resolution, not deliverable completion status.
