# Question

The "100% rule" of WBS construction states that:

## Options

- **A**: All team members must contribute equally to building the WBS.
- **B**: The WBS must include 100% of the work defined by the project scope and no more.
- **C**: Each work package must be 100% complete before the next can start.
- **D**: WBS elements should be decomposed until they are 100% understood by every stakeholder.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The 100% rule says the WBS captures all of the in-scope work and only the in-scope work, with each level summing to its parent. (A) describes participation, not WBS content. (C) is sequencing, a schedule concern. (D) is a different (and unrealistic) clarity standard.
