# Question

In requirements workshops for a new CRM rollout, Marketing demands a campaign automation feature; Operations demands a service ticketing feature. Budget and timeline only allow one. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Build whatever the more senior business sponsor prefers.
- **B**: Approve both and request additional budget from the steering committee.
- **C**: Run a facilitated workshop to surface the underlying business outcomes each group needs and look for an option that serves both.
- **D**: Defer the decision to the PMO scope governance board.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Conflicting requirements often reflect different goals stated as solutions. PMI's stakeholder engagement principle calls for the PM to facilitate understanding before forcing a choice; sometimes a third option emerges, sometimes the trade-off becomes clearer. (A) mistakes hierarchy for analysis. (B) assumes money will appear and skips the work of clarifying value. (D) escalates a decision the PM can facilitate at their level.
