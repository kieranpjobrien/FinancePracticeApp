# Question

Which requirements elicitation technique brings together prequalified stakeholders and a trained moderator to discuss expectations?

## Options

- **A**: Interviews
- **B**: Focus groups
- **C**: Observations
- **D**: Questionnaires

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Focus groups gather prequalified stakeholders (people with relevant knowledge or stake in the outcome) with a trained moderator to facilitate discussion. The group dynamic can surface requirements that individual techniques might miss through interaction and idea building.

Option A (interviews) involves one-on-one or small group conversations, not moderated group discussions.

Option C (observations) involves watching users perform work, not facilitated discussions.

Option D (questionnaires) collect written responses, not interactive group dialogue.
