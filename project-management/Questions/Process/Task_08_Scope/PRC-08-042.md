# Question

Which requirements elicitation technique brings together prequalified stakeholders with a trained moderator to discuss expectations interactively?

## Options

- **A**: Focus groups.
- **B**: One-on-one interviews.
- **C**: Direct observation of users at work.
- **D**: Structured questionnaires distributed to a sample.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

A focus group is, by definition, a moderated group session with screened participants. (B) is interactive but individual, not group. (C) involves no facilitated discussion. (D) collects responses asynchronously without dialogue.
