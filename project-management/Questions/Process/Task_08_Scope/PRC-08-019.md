# Question

What is the primary purpose of the WBS dictionary?

## Options

- **A**: To define abbreviations and terms used throughout the WBS.
- **B**: To list all WBS elements alphabetically for ease of lookup.
- **C**: To map WBS elements to the corresponding schedule activities.
- **D**: To capture detailed information for each work package, including scope, deliverables, acceptance criteria, and the responsible party.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The WBS dictionary is the supporting reference behind every box on the WBS — what the work means, who owns it, what makes it acceptable. (A) is a glossary, not a dictionary in this sense. (B) is a sort order, not a content type. (C) is a separate schedule mapping artefact.
