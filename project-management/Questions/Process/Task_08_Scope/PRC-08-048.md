# Question

On a Scrum team building a healthcare portal, the product owner marks stories accepted even when the team has not completed all Definition of Done items. Technical debt is growing and the team is quietly frustrated. What should the PM raise with the Scrum Master and product owner?

## Options

- **A**: Accept the current practice because the product owner has the final say on story acceptance in Scrum.
- **B**: Ask the team to stop raising it in retrospectives because it is damaging team and product owner trust.
- **C**: Reinforce that Definition of Done is a team standard and acceptance must meet it before stories close.
- **D**: Propose splitting stories into smaller slices so the Definition of Done is easier for the team to meet.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Definition of Done is a team-owned quality standard; stories accepted outside it accumulate hidden defects and distort velocity. (A) treats product owner authority as absolute and ignores DoD. (B) suppresses a legitimate team concern and damages psychological safety. (D) changes story size without addressing the acceptance discipline gap.
