# Question

A product owner prioritises the backlog based on business value, but the development team argues that technical debt items should be prioritised higher to maintain velocity. How should the PM facilitate resolution?

## Options

- **A**: Support the product owner since they own backlog prioritisation
- **B**: Support the team since they understand technical implications
- **C**: Facilitate a discussion where the team explains technical debt impact in business terms the product owner can evaluate
- **D**: Create a separate technical debt backlog managed by the development team

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. The product owner needs to understand how technical debt affects future velocity, quality, and cost to make informed prioritisation decisions. The team should explain debt impacts in business terms (slower delivery, increased defects, higher costs). This enables value-based decisions that include technical considerations.

Option A ignores legitimate technical input that affects business outcomes.

Option B undermines the product owner's role without enabling informed decisions.

Option D separates concerns that should be integrated. All work affecting the product should be visible and prioritised together.
