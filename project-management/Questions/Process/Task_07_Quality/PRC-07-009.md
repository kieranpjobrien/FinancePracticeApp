# Question

What is the primary purpose of a Pareto chart in quality management?

## Options

- **A**: To display the sequence of steps in a process from start to finish.
- **B**: To show whether a process is in statistical control over time.
- **C**: To show the correlation between two variables in a data set.
- **D**: To identify the vital few categories of causes that account for the majority of observed problems.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A Pareto chart sorts categories of causes by frequency and overlays a cumulative line to highlight the vital few — typically the top 20% of causes accounting for 80% of effects. (D) captures this. (A) describes a flowchart. (B) describes a control chart. (C) describes a scatter diagram.
