# Question

A project's Cost of Quality breakdown is: Prevention = $15,000, Appraisal = $10,000, Internal failure = $8,000, External failure = $12,000. What is the Cost of Poor Quality (COPQ)?

## Options

- **A**: $20,000
- **B**: $25,000
- **C**: $35,000
- **D**: $45,000

## Answer

A

## Explanation

COPQ is the sum of failure costs only — internal plus external: $8,000 + $12,000 = $20,000. (A) is correct. (B) sums appraisal and failure, but appraisal is a conformance cost. (C) excludes prevention but adds appraisal incorrectly. (D) is the total Cost of Quality (all four buckets), not COPQ.
