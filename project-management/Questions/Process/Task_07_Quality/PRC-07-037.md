# Question

Which of the following is an example of appraisal cost in the cost of quality model?

## Options

- **A**: Training team members on quality standards
- **B**: Reworking a deliverable that failed inspection
- **C**: Testing and inspecting deliverables
- **D**: Handling customer complaints about defects

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Appraisal costs are costs incurred to evaluate products to ensure they meet requirements. Testing, inspection, reviews, and audits are appraisal activities - they detect defects before delivery.

Option A is a prevention cost. Training helps prevent defects from being created in the first place.

Option B is an internal failure cost. Rework occurs when defects are found before delivery to the customer.

Option D is an external failure cost. Customer complaints arise from defects found after delivery.
