# Question

A fishbone diagram is also known by which of the following names?

## Options

- **A**: Pareto diagram
- **B**: Run chart
- **C**: Control chart
- **D**: Ishikawa or cause-and-effect diagram

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The fishbone diagram is also called the Ishikawa diagram (after Kaoru Ishikawa) or the cause-and-effect diagram. (D) is correct. (A) describes a frequency-ranking bar chart. (B) plots data points over time without statistical control limits. (C) plots data over time with upper and lower control limits derived statistically.
