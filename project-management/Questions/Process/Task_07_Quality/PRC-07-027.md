# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of a quality management plan?

## Options

- **A**: To list all the tests that will be performed on the product
- **B**: To describe how quality policies will be implemented and how quality requirements will be met
- **C**: To document the quality certifications required for project team members
- **D**: To track defects found during project execution

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. The quality management plan describes how the organisation's quality policies will be applied to the project. It defines quality standards, quality objectives, roles and responsibilities, deliverables requiring quality review, and the quality activities to be performed.

Option A describes test plans, which are components of quality control but not the purpose of the quality management plan.

Option C may be addressed in the plan but isn't its primary purpose. Team qualifications might be covered in the resource management plan.

Option D describes a defect log or quality control measurements, which are outputs of quality control activities, not the purpose of the plan.
