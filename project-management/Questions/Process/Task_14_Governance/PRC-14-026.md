# Question

What is a key characteristic of effective project governance?

## Options

- **A**: Maximum control over all project decisions
- **B**: Clear definition of roles, responsibilities, and decision-making authority at appropriate levels
- **C**: Avoiding all documentation
- **D**: Letting the team decide everything without oversight

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Effective governance clearly defines who makes what decisions, ensuring authority is at appropriate levels. This enables efficient decision-making (not everything escalates) while maintaining oversight of significant matters. Clarity prevents confusion and delays.

Option A creates bottlenecks and doesn't trust appropriate decision-makers.

Option C makes it difficult to understand or verify governance is working.

Option D lacks oversight that organizations need.
