# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of a project steering committee?

## Options

- **A**: To provide strategic direction and decide on matters that exceed the project manager's delegated authority.
- **B**: To assign daily tasks to team members across the participating departments.
- **C**: To run weekly status checks and update the project schedule with reported progress.
- **D**: To perform quality reviews of project deliverables before they are released.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

A steering committee owns strategic oversight and decisions outside the PM's authority. (A) captures that. (B) is operational and belongs to the PM and team leads. (C) is status reporting, run by the PM. (D) is quality assurance, performed by QA or SMEs.
