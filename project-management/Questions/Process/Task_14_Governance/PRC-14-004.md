# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of a project steering committee?

## Options

- **A**: To perform day-to-day project management activities
- **B**: To provide strategic direction and make key decisions that exceed the project manager's authority
- **C**: To assign tasks to team members
- **D**: To conduct quality reviews of deliverables

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. A steering committee provides oversight, strategic guidance, and decision-making authority for matters beyond the PM's delegated authority - such as major scope changes, budget increases, or resolving stakeholder conflicts. They represent organisational leadership.

Option A (day-to-day management) is the project manager's role, not the steering committee's.

Option C (assign tasks) is operational management, typically done by the PM or team leads.

Option D (quality reviews) may be performed by various parties (QA team, SMEs) but isn't the steering committee's primary purpose.
