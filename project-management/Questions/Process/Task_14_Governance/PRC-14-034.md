# Question

In a projectized organization, who typically has the MOST authority over project resources?

## Options

- **A**: Functional managers
- **B**: Project managers
- **C**: Human resources department
- **D**: Team members themselves

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. In a projectized organization, project managers have significant authority over resources, including team assignments, performance evaluation, and work direction. Resources are primarily assigned to projects rather than functional departments, giving PMs direct authority.

Option A describes functional organizations where functional managers have more authority.

Option C provides HR services but doesn't direct project resources.

Option D describes self-organizing teams but PMs still have organizational authority in projectized structures.
