# Question

A strategic vendor delivering half the project is underperforming against SLAs but the PM has no direct vendor governance forum; all issues go through procurement weeks after the fact. What should the PM establish?

## Options

- **A**: Keep routing vendor issues through procurement and insist procurement respond faster to reported SLA breaches from now on.
- **B**: Bypass procurement entirely and raise SLA breaches directly with the vendor's project director each week going forward.
- **C**: Ask the sponsor to renegotiate the entire contract with tougher SLAs since the current ones are clearly not being met.
- **D**: Set up a joint vendor governance forum with procurement, the vendor lead, and the sponsor at an appropriate regular cadence.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PMI's procurement governance principle creates a working forum that includes procurement, sponsor, and vendor for ongoing oversight. (D) establishes the right structure. (A) accepts a broken loop. (B) undermines the procurement relationship and contract authority. (C) renegotiates a contract to solve a governance gap that is organisational, not contractual.
