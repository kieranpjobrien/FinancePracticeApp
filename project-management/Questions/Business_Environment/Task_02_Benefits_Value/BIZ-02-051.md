# Question

Several benefits are intangible (improved morale, brand perception). The CFO wants every benefit monetised. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Force-monetise each benefit using rough conversion factors.
- **B**: Drop the intangible benefits from the business case entirely.
- **C**: Monetise where sensible, retain qualitative narrative for the rest, and explain the methodology.
- **D**: Tell the CFO that intangible benefits cannot be monetised.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

A mature business case mixes quantitative and qualitative evidence with a transparent method (C). (A) creates false precision. (B) understates the project's value. (D) is a flat refusal that ignores reasonable monetisation methods.
