# Question

A change request would add £400k to project cost and £600k of net present value over five years. The sponsor is hesitant because of the upfront cost. What should the PM present?

## Options

- **A**: A revised cash flow analysis showing the net positive value, payback period, and the assumptions behind the £600k.
- **B**: A summary of qualitative benefits and stakeholder support for the change.
- **C**: A comparison to other change requests with similar cost profiles.
- **D**: A recommendation to defer the decision until the next steering committee.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The decision needs the financial picture and the assumptions; the sponsor can then make a defensible call (A). (B) substitutes sentiment for analysis. (C) is benchmarking that does not address this decision. (D) delays without providing the basis the sponsor needs.
