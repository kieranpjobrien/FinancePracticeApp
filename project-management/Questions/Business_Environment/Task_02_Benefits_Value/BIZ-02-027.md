# Question

What is a benefits dependency network (sometimes called a benefits map)?

## Options

- **A**: A schedule of when each benefit will be measured and reported.
- **B**: A diagram linking project outputs to outcomes and benefits, including the enabling changes required.
- **C**: A list of stakeholders and the benefits each will receive.
- **D**: A financial calculation of return on investment over the benefit period.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A benefits dependency network shows the chain from outputs through outcomes to benefits, plus the enabling changes (B). (A) is a measurement schedule. (C) is a stakeholder mapping. (D) is one financial metric, not a causal map.
