# Question

What is a benefits map or benefits dependency network?

## Options

- **A**: A schedule showing when benefits will be achieved
- **B**: A diagram showing how project outputs lead to outcomes and ultimately to benefits, including required enabling changes
- **C**: A list of stakeholders who will receive benefits
- **D**: A financial calculation of return on investment

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. A benefits map shows the causal chain from project deliverables through intermediate outcomes to final benefits. It typically also shows enabling changes (like training or process changes) required for benefits to materialize. This helps identify what must happen for benefits to be realized.

Option A describes timing but not the causal relationships.

Option C identifies beneficiaries but not the mechanism of benefit delivery.

Option D is a financial metric, not a mapping of how benefits are achieved.
