# Question

A project's benefits will be realised over three years after go-live by the operations team. Who should own each benefit in the benefits register?

## Options

- **A**: The project sponsor, who approved the business case.
- **B**: The project manager, who will track outcomes after closure.
- **C**: The PMO, which consolidates value tracking across projects.
- **D**: The accountable business leader whose function will deliver the benefit.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Benefits are owned by the function that earns them in operations (D). (A) the sponsor approves but does not run the receiving function. (B) the PM hands over at closure and is not accountable for years of operations. (C) the PMO supports tracking but does not own delivery.
