# Question

A Scrum team is debating which features to include in the next quarterly release. Three candidates are technically equivalent in effort. How should the PM advise the product owner to choose?

## Options

- **A**: Pick the feature most requested in customer feedback regardless of measured value.
- **B**: Sequence by expected business value and observed user demand, deferring lower-value features.
- **C**: Include all three features and split them across two releases.
- **D**: Defer the decision to the next sprint planning to gather more input.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The product owner sequences by value, balancing measured benefit and demand (B). (A) treats popularity as value, which it is not. (C) reduces focus and dilutes value delivery. (D) postpones a decision the team is positioned to make now.
