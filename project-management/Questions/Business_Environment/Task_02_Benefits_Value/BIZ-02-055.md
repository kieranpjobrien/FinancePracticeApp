# Question

How often should benefits realisation be reviewed with the sponsor during execution of a 12-month project?

## Options

- **A**: Only at major delivery milestones so the sponsor is not distracted from execution.
- **B**: Continuously, with no formal cadence, through ad hoc emails and corridor conversations.
- **C**: Once at the midpoint of the project and once again at closure.
- **D**: At the agreed governance cadence, with benefits status as a standing agenda item.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Benefits become invisible without a recurring agenda slot, so they are tracked at every governance cycle (D). (A) treats benefits as secondary to delivery. (B) lacks discipline and discoverability. (C) is too sparse to course-correct.
