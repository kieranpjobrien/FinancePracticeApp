# Question

Return on investment (ROI) is best described as:

## Options

- **A**: The total revenue generated by a product over its lifetime.
- **B**: The discounted future cash flows expressed in present-day money.
- **C**: The net benefit divided by the cost, often expressed as a percentage.
- **D**: The expected payback period in months from go-live.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

ROI compares net benefit to cost as a ratio (C). (A) is gross revenue, not return. (B) is net present value (NPV). (D) is payback period.
