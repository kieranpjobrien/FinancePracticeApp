# Question

Which document principally defines the expected benefits and value a project should deliver?

## Options

- **A**: The project schedule.
- **B**: The risk register.
- **C**: The business case.
- **D**: The stakeholder register.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The business case answers "why are we doing this?" by tying expected benefits to the cost of the investment (C). (A) defines when work happens. (B) tracks threats and opportunities. (D) identifies stakeholders, not benefits.
