# Question

Your project will deliver an enabling capability (a data platform) on which three downstream projects depend for their benefits. How should the project's value be expressed?

## Options

- **A**: As the sum of the three downstream projects' benefits.
- **B**: As zero, since the platform itself does not produce business value.
- **C**: As the enabling value plus the contribution to the downstream benefits, with attribution agreed across projects.
- **D**: As the cost saved by not having to build the platform later.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Enablers are valued for their direct contribution and a shared, agreed portion of downstream benefits (C). (A) double-counts and inflates. (B) understates by ignoring the enabling effect. (D) is an opportunity cost framing that misses the actual contribution.
