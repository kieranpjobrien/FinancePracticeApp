# Question

When should benefits tracking typically begin on a project?

## Options

- **A**: Right after go-live, once benefits start to materialise.
- **B**: At initiation, so baselines can be captured before anything changes.
- **C**: At the start of the last phase or final sprint before release.
- **D**: When a stakeholder formally asks for a benefits report.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

You cannot prove an uplift without a baseline, and the baseline must be taken before the change (B). (A), (C), and (D) all miss the baseline window and reduce benefits tracking to after-the-fact storytelling.
