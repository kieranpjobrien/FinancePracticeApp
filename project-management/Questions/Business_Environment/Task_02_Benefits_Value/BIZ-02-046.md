# Question

The benefit "increased customer retention" will only be measurable 12 months post-launch. The sponsor wants earlier signals. What should the PM propose?

## Options

- **A**: Wait for the 12-month measure since the benefit is defined that way.
- **B**: Substitute the 12-month measure with monthly revenue as a proxy.
- **C**: Track leading indicators such as net promoter score and engagement that predict retention, alongside the 12-month measure.
- **D**: Remove the benefit from the register and replace it with something measurable sooner.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Leading indicators give earlier signal without abandoning the lagging measure (C). (A) leaves the sponsor blind for a year. (B) is a poor proxy and may mislead. (D) discards the actual benefit because it is hard to measure.
