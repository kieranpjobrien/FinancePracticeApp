# Question

A hybrid project has fixed regulatory deliverables and a discretionary feature backlog. Capacity is tight. Which approach best protects value?

## Options

- **A**: Treat the regulatory deliverables and the backlog as equal priority.
- **B**: Defer regulatory work to give the backlog a chance to deliver early benefits.
- **C**: Protect the regulatory deliverables and apply value-based prioritisation to the discretionary backlog.
- **D**: Cut the discretionary backlog entirely to focus on regulatory work.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Regulatory work is non-negotiable, but the discretionary backlog still benefits from explicit value ranking (C). (A) treats compliance and discretion as equivalent. (B) violates compliance. (D) sacrifices all value to compliance even when there is room for both.
