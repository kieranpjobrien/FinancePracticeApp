# Question

A new self-service portal will save call-centre time but will increase IT support load due to access issues during early adoption. How should the PM treat the increased IT load?

## Options

- **A**: Exclude it from the business case because it is a temporary effect.
- **B**: Include it as a dis-benefit and net it against expected savings in the business case.
- **C**: Report it to IT separately so it does not affect the business case.
- **D**: Avoid the additional load by delaying the portal rollout.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Dis-benefits are real and must be netted in the business case (B). (A) hides cost. (C) shifts the cost out of view but it still occurs. (D) avoids the dis-benefit at the cost of all the benefits.
