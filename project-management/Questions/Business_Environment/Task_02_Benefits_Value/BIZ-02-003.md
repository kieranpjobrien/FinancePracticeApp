# Question

When should benefits realisation be measured for a project?

## Options

- **A**: Continuously from initiation through post-implementation, until benefits are fully realised.
- **B**: At project closure, as part of the final report to the sponsor.
- **C**: During execution only, while the team is still actively delivering.
- **D**: Whenever the sponsor specifically asks for an update on benefits.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Benefits accrue over time, often well after the project closes; tracking must run from baseline through realisation (A). (B) misses ongoing accrual after closure. (C) ignores baseline and post-go-live data. (D) makes measurement reactive rather than disciplined.
