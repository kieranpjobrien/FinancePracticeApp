# Question

The project will close next month and benefits will accrue over two years through operations. What is the PM's most important closure action regarding benefits?

## Options

- **A**: Archive all project documents in the records system.
- **B**: Hold a closure celebration with the team to mark delivery.
- **C**: Hand over the benefits register, owners, and tracking cadence to the receiving function.
- **D**: Update the sponsor on actual versus forecast cost variance.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Without an explicit handover of benefits ownership, value tracking dies at closure (C). (A), (B), and (D) are valid closure activities but do not protect the benefits horizon.
