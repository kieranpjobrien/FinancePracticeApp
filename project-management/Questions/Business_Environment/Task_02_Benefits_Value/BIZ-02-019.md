# Question

What is benefits realization management?

## Options

- **A**: A process for tracking project costs
- **B**: The systematic process of ensuring that projects deliver their intended benefits and value to the organization
- **C**: A method for calculating return on investment
- **D**: The project closure process

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Benefits realization management encompasses identifying benefits, planning how they'll be achieved and measured, tracking progress toward benefit achievement, and taking action to maximize actual benefits. It ensures projects deliver value, not just outputs.

Option A describes cost management, not benefits realization.

Option C is one component of benefits analysis but not the full management process.

Option D is related (closure often involves benefits verification) but benefits realization extends before and after closure.
