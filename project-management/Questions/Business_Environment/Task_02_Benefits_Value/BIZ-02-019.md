# Question

What is benefits realisation management?

## Options

- **A**: A formal cost-tracking discipline applied during project execution.
- **B**: The systematic process of ensuring projects deliver their intended benefits and value.
- **C**: A method for calculating ROI as part of the business case.
- **D**: The set of activities performed during project closure.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Benefits realisation management spans identification, measurement, tracking, and intervention to deliver value (B). (A) is cost management. (C) is one calculation that feeds the business case. (D) is too narrow — benefits realisation extends well past closure.
