# Question

A project is 18 months in and £4M spent. New analysis shows expected remaining benefits are £1M against £2M of remaining cost. What should the PM recommend to the steering committee?

## Options

- **A**: Continue the project to recover the sunk cost.
- **B**: Recommend cancellation or scope reduction; the forward economics no longer support continuation as planned.
- **C**: Continue with the original plan because the business case was approved.
- **D**: Pause the project for a year and reassess later.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Decisions look forward; £1M of value for £2M of cost is a clear case for cancellation or rescoping (B). (A) and (C) commit to value-destroying work because of money already spent. (D) parks the issue without resolving it and continues to incur indirect cost.
