# Question

Who is typically accountable for ensuring project benefits are realised after the project closes?

## Options

- **A**: The project manager, even after rolling onto another assignment.
- **B**: The delivery team, who built the solution and know it best.
- **C**: A nominated benefits owner from the business, supported by operations.
- **D**: The PMO, as part of portfolio benefits tracking.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Benefits accrue in operations after the project ends, so a benefits owner from the business carries that accountability (C). (A) and (B) are usually no longer in place. (D) tracks rollups across projects but does not own a single project's benefits.
