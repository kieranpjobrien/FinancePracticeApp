# Question

Go-live has occurred and initial benefits are tracking to plan. Operations is asking to disband the benefits review forum now that the project is closed. Benefits are measured across a 24-month horizon. What should the PM advise?

## Options

- **A**: Agree to disband the forum now because delivery is complete and realisation is routine operational reporting.
- **B**: Retain benefits governance with operations and the benefits owner for the full 24-month realisation horizon.
- **C**: Hand benefits reporting to finance entirely so operations can focus on running the new service day-to-day.
- **D**: Keep the forum running but reduce its cadence to annual reviews so operations is not unduly burdened.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Benefits governance runs for the full realisation horizon, not the delivery horizon (B). (A) ends oversight at closure and risks silent drift. (C) separates benefits from the operation that shapes them. (D) dilutes cadence to the point where correction becomes slow.
