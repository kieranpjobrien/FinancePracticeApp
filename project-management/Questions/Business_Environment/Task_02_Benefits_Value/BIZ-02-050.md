# Question

A backlog contains 30 features. The product owner wants the team to deliver the highest-value items first. What is the PM's most useful contribution?

## Options

- **A**: Suggest the team estimate effort first and choose by lowest effort.
- **B**: Help the product owner score items on value and effort and order by value-per-effort.
- **C**: Recommend that the team work alphabetically through the backlog.
- **D**: Advise the product owner that the team should decide which items go first.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Value-per-effort is a defensible prioritisation method the PM can facilitate (B). (A) ignores value entirely. (C) ignores both value and effort. (D) abdicates a decision the product owner is accountable for.
