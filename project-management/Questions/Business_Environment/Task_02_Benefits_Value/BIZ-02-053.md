# Question

A "benefits owner" is best described as:

## Options

- **A**: The accountable individual who realises the benefit in operations after handover.
- **B**: The PM who signs off the benefits register at closure.
- **C**: The finance partner who reconciles benefits to the general ledger.
- **D**: The sponsor who funded the project.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Benefits owners are operations leaders accountable for the outcome that delivers value (A). (B), (C), and (D) play roles around benefits but are not the accountable owner.
