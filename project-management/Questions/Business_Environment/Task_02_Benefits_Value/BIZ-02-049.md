# Question

Which of the following is typically NOT part of a project business case?

## Options

- **A**: Detailed work breakdown structure for the project's delivery teams.
- **B**: Strategic alignment and expected benefits.
- **C**: Cost estimate and financial appraisal.
- **D**: Risks, assumptions, and constraints.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The WBS belongs to delivery planning, not the business case (A). (B), (C), and (D) are standard business case components.
