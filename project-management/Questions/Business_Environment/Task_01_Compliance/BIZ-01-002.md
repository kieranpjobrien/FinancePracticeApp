# Question

Which of the following best describes a project compliance requirement?

## Options

- **A**: A stakeholder preference for a particular reporting format or dashboard layout.
- **B**: A team agreement on coding conventions captured in the development handbook.
- **C**: A mandatory rule imposed by law, regulation, or organisational policy that the project must satisfy.
- **D**: A market expectation that the product should match competitor features at launch.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

A compliance requirement is a non-negotiable rule the project must meet, typically rooted in law (GDPR, HIPAA, SOX), industry standards (PCI-DSS), or internal policy. (A) is a preference, not a mandate. (B) is a team norm, useful but not externally imposed. (D) is a competitive consideration relevant to scope decisions, not a compliance obligation.
