# Question

Which of the following is an example of a compliance requirement that may affect project planning?

## Options

- **A**: Stakeholder preferences for user interface design
- **B**: Industry regulations such as GDPR, HIPAA, or financial reporting standards
- **C**: Team preferences for development methodology
- **D**: Market competition considerations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Compliance requirements are mandatory rules that must be followed - typically legal regulations, industry standards, or organizational policies. Examples include GDPR (data privacy), HIPAA (healthcare data), SOX (financial reporting), and industry-specific safety standards. These requirements constrain project options and must be addressed in planning.

Option A describes preferences, not mandatory compliance requirements.

Option C describes methodology preferences, which are choices, not compliance mandates.

Option D describes business strategy considerations, not compliance requirements.
