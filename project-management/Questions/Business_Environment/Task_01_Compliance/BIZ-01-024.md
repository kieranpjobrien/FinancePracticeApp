# Question

A project manager is preparing for a regulatory inspection. The inspector will review project documentation and interview team members. What should the project manager do to prepare?

## Options

- **A**: Coach team members on what answers to give during interviews
- **B**: Ensure documentation is organized and complete, brief team members on the inspection process, and identify any gaps that need addressing before the inspection
- **C**: Minimize the inspection scope by limiting access to documentation
- **D**: Delay the inspection until all project work is complete

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Proper inspection preparation involves ensuring documentation is accessible and organized, helping team members understand what to expect, and proactively identifying and addressing gaps. Transparency and preparation demonstrate compliance maturity.

Option A suggests coaching answers, which borders on deception and undermines inspection integrity. Team members should answer honestly.

Option C attempts to hide information from regulators, which is inappropriate and often illegal.

Option D may not be possible and delays don't improve compliance status. Better to address gaps than avoid inspection.
