# Question

A regulator has scheduled an on-site inspection of your project in three weeks. The inspector will review documentation and interview team members. What should the project manager do to prepare?

## Options

- **A**: Coach team members on the answers most likely to satisfy the inspector.
- **B**: Restrict the documentation set the inspector can access to reduce questions.
- **C**: Organise documentation, brief the team on the inspection process, and address any visible gaps in advance.
- **D**: Request that the inspection be deferred until the project is fully complete.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Inspection readiness is about transparency and preparation (C): make evidence easy to find, ensure people know what to expect, and close obvious gaps. (A) and (B) verge on obstruction and corrode trust if discovered. (D) avoids rather than addresses the issue and is unlikely to be granted.
