# Question

The sponsor wants to accept the risk of a known compliance gap in exchange for an earlier launch, citing the small probability of audit. What is the PM's best response?

## Options

- **A**: Document the sponsor's risk acceptance and proceed with launch.
- **B**: Refuse to launch and escalate to the sponsor's manager.
- **C**: Present the impact, including penalty exposure and reputational risk, and require formal acceptance from the appropriate compliance authority.
- **D**: Quietly add additional controls to close the gap without delaying launch.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Compliance risks cannot be accepted by a sponsor alone; the appropriate compliance or legal authority must accept the risk in writing after seeing full impact (C). (A) treats the sponsor as the sole authority on a regulatory matter. (B) skips presenting the analysis and the right authority. (D) is unilateral scope change without governance.
