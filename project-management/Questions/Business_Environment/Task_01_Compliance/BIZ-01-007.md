# Question

What is the primary purpose of compliance management on a project?

## Options

- **A**: To produce evidence packages that internal and external auditors can review on request.
- **B**: To slow execution with formal review gates that prevent the team from cutting corners.
- **C**: To protect the organisation by ensuring deliverables meet legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations.
- **D**: To give the legal department final approval over project scope and design decisions.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Compliance exists to keep the organisation out of legal, financial, and reputational trouble (C). (A) describes audit support, which is one output of compliance, not its purpose. (B) confuses the means with the end. (D) confuses advisory input with decision authority; the PM still owns project decisions within compliance constraints.
