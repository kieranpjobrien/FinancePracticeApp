# Question

When should compliance requirements be identified for a project?

## Options

- **A**: During execution, once the design is concrete enough to map controls to it.
- **B**: Only when an external auditor formally requests evidence of conformance.
- **C**: At the closing phase, alongside lessons learned and audit preparation.
- **D**: At project initiation and refined throughout the lifecycle as the scope is detailed.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Compliance is identified early to shape scope, design, and budget, then refined as detail emerges (D). (A) treats compliance as an afterthought and risks expensive rework. (C) is far too late to influence anything. (B) is reactive and indicates a project without a compliance plan.
