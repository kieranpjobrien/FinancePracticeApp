# Question

A customer asks for evidence that your project follows ISO/IEC 27001 information security practices. Your organisation is not certified to ISO 27001 but has implemented controls that map to it. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Decline to share information until the organisation pursues full ISO 27001 certification.
- **B**: Tell the customer the organisation is "ISO 27001 aligned" without further detail.
- **C**: Provide the mapped controls and clearly state the organisation is not certified.
- **D**: Send the certification of a partner organisation as a substitute.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The honest, useful response is to share what the organisation actually does and clarify the certification status (C). (A) refuses information that the customer would benefit from. (B) is intentionally vague and risks misleading. (D) misrepresents another organisation's certificate as evidence for yours.
