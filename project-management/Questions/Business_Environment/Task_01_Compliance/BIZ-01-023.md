# Question

Which stakeholder group typically has primary responsibility for defining compliance requirements for a project?

## Options

- **A**: The project team
- **B**: Legal, compliance, and regulatory affairs departments, along with relevant subject matter experts
- **C**: The project sponsor
- **D**: External customers

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Legal, compliance, and regulatory affairs specialists have expertise in applicable laws, regulations, and standards. They define what compliance means for the organization and project. Subject matter experts in specific domains (security, privacy, safety) contribute technical compliance requirements.

Option A has the team implementing compliance, not defining it. Teams typically lack regulatory expertise.

Option C has the sponsor accountable for compliance but not typically defining detailed requirements.

Option D may influence requirements through contracts but doesn't typically define regulatory compliance requirements.
