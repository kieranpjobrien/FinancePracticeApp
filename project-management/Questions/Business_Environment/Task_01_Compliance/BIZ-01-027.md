# Question

What is the consequence of delivering a project that doesn't meet compliance requirements?

## Options

- **A**: Minor inconvenience that can be addressed post-delivery
- **B**: Potential legal penalties, inability to use the deliverable, reputational damage, and required rework
- **C**: No impact if the functional requirements are met
- **D**: Only impacts the project manager's performance review

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Non-compliance can have severe consequences: regulatory fines and penalties, legal liability, inability to deploy or sell the product, damage to organizational reputation, and costly remediation work. In some industries, non-compliance can result in criminal charges.

Option A severely underestimates compliance consequences. Non-compliance is often not fixable after delivery.

Option C separates functionality from compliance incorrectly. A non-compliant product may be legally unusable regardless of functionality.

Option D ignores organizational and legal consequences that extend far beyond the PM's performance.
