# Question

What does project compliance management ensure?

## Options

- **A**: That the project finishes on time
- **B**: That the project and its deliverables meet applicable laws, regulations, standards, and organisational policies
- **C**: That all stakeholders are satisfied
- **D**: That the project stays within budget

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Compliance management ensures the project meets external requirements (laws, regulations, industry standards) and internal requirements (organisational policies, governance frameworks). This includes things like data privacy, safety standards, accessibility, environmental regulations, and corporate policies.

Option A (on time) is schedule management, not compliance.

Option C (stakeholder satisfaction) is stakeholder management, not compliance.

Option D (within budget) is cost management, not compliance.
