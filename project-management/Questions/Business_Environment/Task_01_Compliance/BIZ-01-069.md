# Question

At project closeout, you discover the vendor wants to delete the shared collaboration workspace next week to free licences. The workspace holds regulated records under a 7-year retention obligation. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Instruct the vendor to halt deletion and arrange transfer of the regulated records to compliant organisational storage.
- **B**: Approve the deletion because the project is closed and the vendor's workspace is no longer project-funded.
- **C**: Ask legal to negotiate an extended vendor contract so the workspace can remain live for seven years.
- **D**: Export the records to a local hard drive held by the project manager for the retention period.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Regulated records must be retained in sanctioned storage; the PM stops deletion and arranges a proper transfer (A). (B) destroys records under a legal hold. (C) keeps an unnecessary licence live instead of doing the right transfer. (D) places regulated records outside sanctioned, auditable storage.
