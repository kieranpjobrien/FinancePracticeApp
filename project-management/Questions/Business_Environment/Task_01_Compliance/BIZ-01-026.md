# Question

Your master services agreement requires all merged code to carry two independent reviewer approvals. Under deadline pressure, the team has been merging with a single reviewer for the last month. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Keep the current practice because single review has not caused incidents.
- **B**: Enforce the two-reviewer requirement now and assess whether recent merges need re-review.
- **C**: Open contract negotiations to relax the review requirement to match actual practice.
- **D**: Note the deviation in the lessons-learned log and inform the customer at closure.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A contractual control that is being bypassed must be restored immediately and the backlog of work assessed for risk (B). (A) is a quiet decision to breach. (C) is a legitimate later conversation but does not cover the current breach. (D) withholds known non-compliance, which typically itself breaches notification clauses.
