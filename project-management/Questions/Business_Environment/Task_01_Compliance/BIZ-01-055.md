# Question

A team member shares a spreadsheet containing confidential client information through a personal cloud drive to "make collaboration easier." What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Direct the team member to delete the file from the personal drive and relocate it to the approved system.
- **B**: Report the team member to HR for a confidentiality breach.
- **C**: Block all cloud storage tools at the network level to prevent recurrence.
- **D**: Update the project communication plan to remind everyone about data handling.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The immediate exposure must be removed and the data returned to a sanctioned location (A). (B) jumps to discipline before resolving the exposure or understanding intent. (C) is an enterprise-wide control change that the PM does not own. (D) is policy reinforcement that does not address the live exposure.
