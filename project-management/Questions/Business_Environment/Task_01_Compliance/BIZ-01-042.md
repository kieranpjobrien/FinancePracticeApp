# Question

Your organisation's internal policy requires code reviews before any production deployment. To meet a deadline, the lead engineer has been merging without review and committing to "fix it later." What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Stop deployments until reviews resume and discuss the pattern with the engineer privately.
- **B**: Report the engineer to the engineering manager for a performance discussion.
- **C**: Adjust the deadline so reviews can fit and notify the sponsor of the schedule slip.
- **D**: Document the bypass as a known risk and continue to deliver.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The immediate compliance breach must stop, and the PM should address the engineer directly before escalating (A). (B) bypasses the direct conversation and frames a process failure as an HR matter. (C) treats the symptom without addressing the policy violation. (D) accepts ongoing non-compliance to a mandated control.
