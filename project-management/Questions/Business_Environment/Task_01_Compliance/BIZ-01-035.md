# Question

The project sponsor instructs the PM to skip a mandated security control review on a regulated workload, saying the review will delay a launch tied to the sponsor's bonus. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Comply, document the sponsor's instruction in the project log, and continue with launch.
- **B**: Refuse, explain the regulatory and ethical reasons, and propose options to launch with the review completed.
- **C**: Escalate immediately to the sponsor's manager without further discussion with the sponsor.
- **D**: Run the review informally and skip the formal sign-off to satisfy both parties.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Compliance and the PMI Code of Ethics are non-negotiable; the PM must refuse and offer constructive alternatives (B). (A) provides a paper trail for non-compliance but is still complicit. (C) skips a direct, professional conversation that may resolve the issue. (D) is a workaround that creates the appearance of compliance without the substance, exposing the organisation to regulatory action.
