# Question

Which of the following is an example of a compliance requirement for a project?

## Options

- **A**: The sponsor wants the project completed by year-end
- **B**: The team prefers to use agile methodology
- **C**: Financial data must be retained for seven years per regulatory requirements
- **D**: Stakeholders requested weekly status reports

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Regulatory data retention requirements are compliance requirements - they are externally mandated by law or regulation and must be met regardless of project preferences or constraints.

Option A is a business constraint or deadline, not a compliance requirement. Missing a desired date has consequences but isn't illegal.

Option B is a methodology preference, not a compliance requirement.

Option D is a stakeholder communication preference, not a legal or regulatory mandate.
