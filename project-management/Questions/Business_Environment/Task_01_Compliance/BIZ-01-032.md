# Question

An external auditor requests evidence that user access reviews have been performed quarterly on the project's production environment. The PM knows reviews have happened but cannot locate the documentation. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Ask the audit lead for a one-week extension and gather the evidence from the operations team.
- **B**: Tell the auditor that reviews have been performed and offer a verbal walkthrough instead.
- **C**: Reconstruct the reviews now and present them as the original quarterly evidence.
- **D**: Escalate to the sponsor that the project may fail the audit due to missing records.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The honest, professional path is to request reasonable time and produce the actual evidence (A). (B) substitutes assertion for evidence, which auditors will not accept. (C) is fabrication and an ethics violation regardless of intent. (D) escalates before the PM has tried to resolve it; the gap is recoverable if the work was genuinely done.
