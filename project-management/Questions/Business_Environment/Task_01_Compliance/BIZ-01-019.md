# Question

How do compliance requirements relate to project scope?

## Options

- **A**: They sit outside scope and are managed entirely by the compliance function.
- **B**: They form part of scope and must be planned, resourced, and verified like any other requirement.
- **C**: They are scope items that can be traded off if the schedule comes under pressure.
- **D**: They affect testing only and do not influence design or build activities.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Compliance obligations are requirements the deliverable must meet, so they belong in scope and the WBS (B). (A) creates an invisible workstream that nothing tracks. (C) misrepresents compliance as optional. (D) understates the impact — compliance shapes design, build, test, and operations.
