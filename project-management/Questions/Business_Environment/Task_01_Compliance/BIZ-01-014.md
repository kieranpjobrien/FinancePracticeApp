# Question

A critical vendor on your project has just failed its SOC 2 recertification audit. The vendor expects to be recertified in eight weeks. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Assess the compliance impact, identify backup vendors, and build a contingency plan.
- **B**: Continue using the vendor based on their recertification timeline commitment.
- **C**: Terminate the vendor contract immediately and source a replacement.
- **D**: Inform internal compliance only after the vendor's recertification result is known.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

A vendor compliance gap is a live risk that must be actively managed (A): assess impact on the project's compliance posture, line up alternatives, and plan contingencies while monitoring the recertification. (B) is hopeful but not a plan. (C) is premature without an alternative or impact assessment. (D) withholds information from compliance, breaching transparency obligations.
