# Question

The compliance officer has identified five new obligations that apply to your project. What should the PM do to integrate these into project management?

## Options

- **A**: Add each obligation to the compliance register and update scope, schedule, and risk artefacts accordingly.
- **B**: Treat each obligation as a project risk and add it to the risk register only.
- **C**: Wait for the next baseline review to incorporate the obligations into project documents.
- **D**: Create a separate compliance project plan that runs alongside the main project plan.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Compliance obligations belong in the compliance register and ripple into other artefacts that they affect (A). (B) misclassifies obligations as risks; an obligation is a requirement, while the risk is failure to meet it. (C) delays integration and lets work proceed without the new requirements. (D) creates a parallel plan that disconnects compliance from project execution.
