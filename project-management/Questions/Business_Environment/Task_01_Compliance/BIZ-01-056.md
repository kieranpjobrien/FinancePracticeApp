# Question

How should a project manager keep up with regulatory changes that may affect a long-running project?

## Options

- **A**: Wait for the compliance officer to escalate any relevant changes.
- **B**: Audit the project's compliance position once a quarter to detect drift.
- **C**: Subscribe to regulator alerts and review changes with the compliance officer at a regular cadence.
- **D**: Rely on the legal team's annual contract review to catch new requirements.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Active monitoring with the compliance officer keeps the project current and decisions timely (C). (A) is passive and may miss items the compliance officer does not connect to your project. (B) detects drift but later than a real-time signal would. (D) is an annual cadence that is far too slow for regulatory change.
