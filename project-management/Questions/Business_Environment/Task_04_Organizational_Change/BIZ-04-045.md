# Question

The PM is preparing a change impact assessment. Which combination is most useful to capture for each affected group?

## Options

- **A**: Their headcount, manager name, and current performance ratings.
- **B**: Their location, age range, and tenure with the organisation.
- **C**: How their work changes, the magnitude of change, current sentiment, and required support.
- **D**: Their salary band, training budget, and contract type.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

A change impact assessment focuses on how the change lands and what is needed to support it (C). (A), (B), and (D) capture demographic or HR data that does not describe the change.
