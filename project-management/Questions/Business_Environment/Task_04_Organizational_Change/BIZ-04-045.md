# Question

The PM is preparing a change impact assessment. Which combination is most useful to capture for each affected group?

## Options

- **A**: Their headcount, manager name, current performance ratings, and historical attrition.
- **B**: Their location, age range, tenure with the organisation, and reporting line.
- **C**: How their work changes, the magnitude of change, sentiment, and required support.
- **D**: Their salary band, training budget, contract type, and annual leave balance.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

A change impact assessment focuses on how the change lands and what is needed to support it (C). (A), (B), and (D) capture demographic or HR data that does not describe the change.
