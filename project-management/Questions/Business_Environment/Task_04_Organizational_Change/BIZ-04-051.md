# Question

The change involves an organisational restructure that will result in layoffs. Affected staff have not been told yet, but the news will leak in days. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Push HR and leadership to bring the formal communication forward with appropriate support.
- **B**: Warn affected staff informally so they hear it from a friendly source before the leak spreads.
- **C**: Continue with the original communication plan and timing regardless of the leak risk.
- **D**: Deny the layoffs publicly until the formal announcement can be made on the planned date.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

When a leak is imminent, the responsible action is to bring the formal, supportive communication forward (A). (B) creates inconsistent, unauthorised messaging. (C) lets people learn through rumour. (D) is dishonest and erodes trust.
