# Question

What is a "change champion" in organisational change management?

## Options

- **A**: The project sponsor who funds and formally authorises the change effort.
- **B**: A peer within an affected group who promotes the change to colleagues.
- **C**: The project manager who leads the delivery team through implementation.
- **D**: A senior stakeholder who openly opposes the change being introduced.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Champions are credible peers inside the affected community (B). (A) is a sponsor. (C) is the PM. (D) is a resistor — the opposite of a champion.
