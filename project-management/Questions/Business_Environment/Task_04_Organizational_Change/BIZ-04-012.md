# Question

What is a change champion in organizational change management?

## Options

- **A**: The project sponsor
- **B**: An individual who advocates for the change within their peer group and helps drive adoption
- **C**: The project manager
- **D**: A person who resists change

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Change champions are individuals throughout the organization who believe in the change and actively promote it among their peers. They provide peer-level influence, answer questions, model new behaviors, and help their colleagues navigate the transition.

Option A is a leadership role; champions are typically at various levels.

Option C manages the project; champions are typically from the affected user community.

Option D describes a resistor, the opposite of a champion.
