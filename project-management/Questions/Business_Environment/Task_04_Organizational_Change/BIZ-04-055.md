# Question

The change sponsor leaves the organisation midway through a major transformation. A new executive has been appointed but is still learning the project. What is the PM's first priority?

## Options

- **A**: Pause the change activities until the new sponsor is fully briefed.
- **B**: Continue change activities at the current pace and let the new sponsor catch up.
- **C**: Retain the previous sponsor as an unofficial advisor.
- **D**: Brief the new sponsor on commitments, current state, and what visible support is needed, then sustain visible sponsorship as quickly as possible.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A sponsor vacuum quickly erodes adoption; the PM closes the gap by briefing and re-establishing visible sponsorship (D). (A) pauses momentum. (B) ignores the sponsor's role in change. (C) creates ambiguous accountability.
