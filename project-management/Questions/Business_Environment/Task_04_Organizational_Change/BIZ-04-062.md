# Question

Three months after launch, usage metrics for the new system are declining as old workarounds return. Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, and Ability were all signalled green at go-live. What should the PM address?

## Options

- **A**: Relaunch awareness campaigns because the decline suggests employees have forgotten why the change was introduced.
- **B**: Reinforce the change with recognition, metrics, coaching, and leader modelling to sustain new behaviours.
- **C**: Replace the system with an older familiar platform because adoption clearly failed despite strong initial signals.
- **D**: Escalate the adoption decline to HR to apply performance consequences for employees who keep using workarounds.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

This is a classic Reinforcement failure; the final ADKAR stage sustains behaviour against the pull of old habits (B). (A) restarts at the top of the model when the gap is at the bottom. (C) abandons the change rather than sustaining it. (D) leaps to punitive enforcement before reinforcement levers are tried.
