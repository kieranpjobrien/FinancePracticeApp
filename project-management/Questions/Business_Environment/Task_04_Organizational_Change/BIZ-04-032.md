# Question

A team manager affected by your project repeatedly cancels meetings, missed two training sessions, and has been overheard expressing scepticism. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Escalate the manager's behaviour to their leader.
- **B**: Replace the manager's role on the project with a more cooperative substitute.
- **C**: Send a formal note reiterating the importance of engagement.
- **D**: Meet with the manager privately to understand their concerns and what would help.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Resistance has a cause; a private conversation surfaces it and opens the door to action (D). (A) escalates without trying. (B) removes the symptom but not the underlying objection. (C) is one-way communication that does not engage the manager.
