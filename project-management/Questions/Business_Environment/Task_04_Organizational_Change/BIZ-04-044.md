# Question

Within an organisational change effort, the change sponsor is best described as:

## Options

- **A**: The PM who runs the day-to-day change activities.
- **B**: The vendor providing the new system.
- **C**: The trainer responsible for upskilling staff.
- **D**: The senior leader who authorises the change and visibly supports it.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A change sponsor is a senior leader who authorises and champions the change (D). (A), (B), and (C) play roles within the effort but are not the sponsor.
