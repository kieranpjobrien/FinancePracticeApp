# Question

The project's executive sponsor agreed to be an active change champion but has been absent from town halls and visible communications. Adoption is starting to lag. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Take over the change champion role personally.
- **B**: Brief the sponsor with specific, low-effort opportunities to be visible and explain the adoption impact of their absence.
- **C**: Find a different executive willing to be visible.
- **D**: Send the sponsor's name on communications without their direct involvement.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The PM coaches the sponsor with concrete, easy actions and the data showing why they matter (B). (A) is below the PM's authority and lacks the executive signal users need. (C) replaces the sponsor without trying to enlist them. (D) misrepresents the sponsor's involvement.
