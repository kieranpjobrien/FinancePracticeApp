# Question

Your change champion network is active but champions are mostly senior, well-paid managers. Adoption in frontline cohorts is lagging behind back-office cohorts. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Recruit additional champions from frontline roles and resource them with time, tools, and visible leader support.
- **B**: Replace the senior champions entirely with frontline champions so the network reflects the adoption gap accurately.
- **C**: Increase the frequency of senior champion appearances on the frontline floor to push adoption by greater exposure.
- **D**: Accept the lag because frontline cohorts typically adopt more slowly and will likely catch up over time.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Champions influence most effectively among peers; the PM widens the network to reflect the affected cohorts (A). (B) discards senior support unnecessarily. (C) tries to push adoption by presence rather than peer influence. (D) normalises a gap that targeted action could close.
