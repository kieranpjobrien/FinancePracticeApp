# Question

What is the role of a change sponsor in organizational change?

## Options

- **A**: To manage the day-to-day project activities
- **B**: To provide leadership, resources, and organizational authority to support the change and remove barriers
- **C**: To train users on new systems
- **D**: To document change requests

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Change sponsors are typically senior leaders who provide visible leadership for the change, secure necessary resources, remove organizational barriers, communicate the importance of the change, and hold people accountable. Their authority and commitment signal organizational seriousness.

Option A is the project manager's role.

Option C is a training team function.

Option D is a project administrative function.
