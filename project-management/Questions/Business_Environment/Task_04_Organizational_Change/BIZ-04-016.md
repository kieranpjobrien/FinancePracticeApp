# Question

What does the ADKAR model describe in change management?

## Options

- **A**: A scheduling technique for sequencing change activities across phases.
- **B**: The five building blocks of individual change: Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, Ability, Reinforcement.
- **C**: A quality framework for measuring change initiative outcomes.
- **D**: A risk-assessment methodology applied to organisational transformation.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

ADKAR (B) is Prosci's five-stage model for individual change. (A), (C), and (D) describe other disciplines and are not what ADKAR stands for.
