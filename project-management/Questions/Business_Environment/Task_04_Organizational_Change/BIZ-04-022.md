# Question

You need to communicate a change that some stakeholders will find unwelcome. What approach should the project manager take?

## Options

- **A**: Communicate openly and early, explain the rationale, acknowledge impacts, and outline the support being put in place.
- **B**: Wait until the last possible moment so people have less time to be anxious.
- **C**: Allow informal channels to surface the news first to soften the blow.
- **D**: Emphasise only the positive impacts to keep morale high.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Honesty plus empathy plus support builds trust even when the news is hard (A). (B) breeds anxiety and surprise. (C) lets misinformation set the narrative. (D) is spin and tends to be discovered.
