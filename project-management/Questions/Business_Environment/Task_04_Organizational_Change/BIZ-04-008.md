# Question

How is "organisational change management" best described in a project context?

## Options

- **A**: A structured approach to moving individuals, teams, and the organisation from a current state to a desired future state.
- **B**: The process of approving scope changes through formal change control.
- **C**: Reorganising the project team's reporting lines for delivery efficiency.
- **D**: Updating the company's organisation chart following a restructure.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

OCM is the structured discipline of leading people through change (A). (B) is project change control. (C) is internal team restructuring. (D) is an HR administrative task.
