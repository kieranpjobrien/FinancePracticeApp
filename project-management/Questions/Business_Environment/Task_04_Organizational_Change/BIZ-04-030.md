# Question

A new system has launched with strong initial adoption and the project is moving to closure. You are concerned that, without ongoing attention, users will drift back to old habits. What should the project manager do before closing?

## Options

- **A**: Document the project as successful and close on the strength of initial adoption metrics.
- **B**: Set up a transition to operations covering adoption monitoring, reinforcement, support, and clear ownership for sustaining the change.
- **C**: Extend the project indefinitely so the team can keep monitoring adoption.
- **D**: Trust strong initial adoption to carry through and close as planned.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Sustaining change requires ownership, monitoring, and reinforcement after the project ends (B). (A) closes against a snapshot that research shows decays. (C) keeps the project open instead of properly transitioning to BAU. (D) is hope, not a plan.
