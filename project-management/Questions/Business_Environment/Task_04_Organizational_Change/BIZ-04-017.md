# Question

A project manager notices that employees understand what they need to do differently (they attended training) but aren't doing it. Using the ADKAR model, which element is likely the barrier?

## Options

- **A**: Awareness - they don't understand why change is needed
- **B**: Knowledge - they don't know how to change
- **C**: Desire - they don't want to change
- **D**: Reinforcement - there's no incentive to maintain the change

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. If employees understand how to change (Knowledge) but aren't changing, the barrier is likely Desire - the motivation to participate in the change. They may not see personal benefit, may be comfortable with the current state, or may have concerns that haven't been addressed.

Option A would mean they don't understand why change is happening, but the scenario shows they completed training.

Option B would mean they don't know how, but they attended training.

Option D applies after the change is made; the scenario describes failure to make the initial change.
