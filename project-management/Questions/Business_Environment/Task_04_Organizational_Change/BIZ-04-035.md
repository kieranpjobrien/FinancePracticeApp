# Question

A new system is launching in three weeks. Training has been delivered, but a recent readiness check shows users still feel unprepared. What is the PM's best response?

## Options

- **A**: Proceed with launch on the agreed date and address any readiness gaps after go-live.
- **B**: Cancel the launch entirely and restart the change activities from the beginning.
- **C**: Identify the specific gaps, target support there, and decide readiness with the sponsor.
- **D**: Push the launch out by three months to give everyone more preparation time.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Targeted support and an informed go/no-go decision protect both adoption and the schedule (C). (A) launches into known under-readiness. (B) discards the work already done. (D) is a blanket delay without diagnosis.
