# Question

What does change readiness assessment measure?

## Options

- **A**: The technical readiness of the system to be deployed.
- **B**: The organisation's capacity and willingness to absorb the change.
- **C**: The completeness of project documentation at go-live.
- **D**: The training budget needed for the change rollout.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Change readiness is about the people and the organisation, not the technology (B). (A) is technical readiness. (C) is documentation completeness. (D) is a budget input.
