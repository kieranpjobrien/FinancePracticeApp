# Question

What role do "change champions" play in organisational change management?

## Options

- **A**: External consultants brought in to drive change from outside the organisation.
- **B**: Executives who hold formal authority to approve change initiatives.
- **C**: Peer-level advocates within affected groups who promote the change to colleagues.
- **D**: Project team members responsible for implementing the technical solution.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Change champions are credible peers who model and promote the change inside affected groups (C). (A) is consultancy. (B) describes sponsors. (D) describes the delivery team. Peer credibility is what makes champions effective.
