# Question

After a major communication push, the PM realises feedback channels were never set up and there is no way to know how the message was received. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Continue with one-way communication; questions from employees will surface on their own over time.
- **B**: Add feedback mechanisms — surveys, listening sessions, anonymised channels — and act on what is heard.
- **C**: Schedule a single Q&A webinar after the project has formally closed and transitioned to operations.
- **D**: Wait for middle managers to relay employee feedback informally through their usual reporting lines.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Effective change communication is two-way; the PM creates routes for feedback and acts on it (B). (A) is broadcast without listening. (C) is too late and infrequent. (D) is unreliable and slow.
