# Question

A new policy will affect 1,200 staff across four offices. The PM is drafting the communication approach. What is the most effective starting point?

## Options

- **A**: Send a single all-staff email when the policy goes live.
- **B**: Identify the audiences, their concerns, and the channels each prefers, then plan messages tailored accordingly.
- **C**: Hold a series of town halls with the same content for every audience.
- **D**: Publish the policy on the intranet and let people find it.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Effective change communication is segmented and intentional (B). (A) is one-shot and undifferentiated. (C) is uniform when audiences differ. (D) is passive publication, not engagement.
