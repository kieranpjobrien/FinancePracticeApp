# Question

What is change readiness assessment?

## Options

- **A**: Testing whether the technical system is ready for deployment
- **B**: Evaluating whether the organization and its people are prepared to adopt and sustain a change
- **C**: Assessing whether the project budget is adequate
- **D**: Reviewing the project schedule for delays

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Change readiness assessment evaluates organizational and individual preparedness for change. It examines factors like leadership alignment, stakeholder support, cultural compatibility, resource availability, training completion, and communication effectiveness to determine whether conditions support successful adoption.

Option A is technical readiness, not organizational change readiness.

Option C is budget review, not change readiness.

Option D is schedule review, not change readiness.
