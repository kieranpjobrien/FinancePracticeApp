# Question

Why is organisational change management important to projects?

## Options

- **A**: It produces the documentation auditors require for change initiatives.
- **B**: It ensures deliverables are adopted and used so benefits can be realised.
- **C**: It records the change history of project deliverables for traceability.
- **D**: It strengthens the financial case used to justify the project budget.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Without adoption, deliverables produce no value (B). (A) reduces OCM to compliance. (C) confuses OCM with configuration management. (D) confuses OCM with business case development.
