# Question

A change-readiness survey returns mixed results: one team is ready, two are partially ready, and one is unprepared. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Delay launch for everyone until the unprepared team catches up.
- **B**: Launch for everyone on schedule.
- **C**: Launch for the ready team and target additional preparation for the others before they go live.
- **D**: Launch only for the team that is fully ready and cancel for the rest.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

A staged rollout matched to readiness protects adoption while keeping momentum (C). (A) penalises ready teams. (B) ignores the readiness data. (D) cancels rather than addressing the gap.
