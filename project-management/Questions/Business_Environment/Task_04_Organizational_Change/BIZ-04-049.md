# Question

A predictive infrastructure project will overlap with an agile product transformation in the same business unit. Affected staff will be hit by both changes. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Coordinate with the other project's PM and the business unit on a combined change roadmap and capacity for absorption.
- **B**: Run the project independently and let staff adapt to both.
- **C**: Delay the infrastructure project until the transformation finishes.
- **D**: Ask the transformation project to slow down.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Change saturation needs cross-project coordination focused on the receivers of change (A). (B) ignores cumulative impact. (C) and (D) prioritise one project over the other without weighing value or capacity.
