# Question

In the change curve (Kübler-Ross-derived), which phase typically follows initial denial?

## Options

- **A**: Acceptance.
- **B**: Integration.
- **C**: Resistance or anger.
- **D**: Commitment.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The classical curve moves from denial to resistance/anger before exploration, acceptance, and commitment (C). (A), (B), and (D) come later in the curve.
