# Question

Training attendance is high, but follow-up assessments show low retention of key concepts. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Schedule another round of identical training to reinforce the content.
- **B**: Replace the training vendor.
- **C**: Investigate whether the training format, content, or context is the issue and adjust accordingly.
- **D**: Accept that some retention loss is inevitable.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The PM diagnoses the cause before re-running or replacing (C). (A) repeats a method that has not worked. (B) blames the vendor without evidence. (D) accepts a known gap.
