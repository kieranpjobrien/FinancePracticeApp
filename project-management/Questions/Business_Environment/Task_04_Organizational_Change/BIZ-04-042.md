# Question

Six months after launch, adoption metrics have dropped from 85% to 60%. Original users have started reverting to workarounds. What is the most likely root cause to investigate?

## Options

- **A**: Insufficient sustainment activity such as reinforcement, refresher training, and visible leadership support.
- **B**: A bug in the system that nobody has reported.
- **C**: An external regulatory change.
- **D**: Lack of enforcement and penalties for non-compliance.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Adoption fade after a strong launch usually points to weak sustainment — reinforcement, communication, and leadership presence (A). (B) is possible but speculative. (C) is rarely the cause of internal-tool reversion. (D) treats adoption as a discipline problem rather than a sustainment problem.
