# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of change management activities in a project?

## Options

- **A**: To control changes to the project scope and documents
- **B**: To help individuals and the organization transition to and adopt new ways of working
- **C**: To manage the technical implementation of systems
- **D**: To ensure project milestones are met

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Organizational change management focuses on the people side of change - helping individuals understand why change is happening, preparing them for new ways of working, supporting them through transition, and reinforcing adoption. This is distinct from project change control.

Option A describes project change control (managing scope/document changes), which is different from organizational change management.

Option C describes technical project management, not change management.

Option D describes schedule management, not change management.
