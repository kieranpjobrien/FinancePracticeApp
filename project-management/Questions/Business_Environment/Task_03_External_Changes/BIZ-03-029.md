# Question

Government has cut renewable-energy subsidies by 40%. Your in-flight wind project's business case assumed the previous level. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Re-calculate the business case under the new subsidy and present continue/modify/pause/cancel options.
- **B**: Continue construction while industry groups lobby to restore the subsidy.
- **C**: Halt construction until the policy environment stabilises.
- **D**: Accept the reduced returns and finish the project as planned.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

A 40% subsidy change is a material economics shift; the right move is to re-do the business case and let stakeholders decide (A). (B) keeps spending on hope. (C) stops without checking viability. (D) absorbs a major hit without authority.
