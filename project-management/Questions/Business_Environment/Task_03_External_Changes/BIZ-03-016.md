# Question

An industry publication reports that several of your software's target customer segment are about to be acquired by larger players. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Add features tailored to the likely acquirers to remain attractive to the larger entities.
- **B**: Pivot the project to a different industry vertical as a precaution.
- **C**: Continue development on the assumption that the underlying need persists.
- **D**: Assess the impact on the addressable market and competitive landscape with stakeholders.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Consolidation can change the buyer, the requirements, and the route to market — analysis comes first (D). (A) and (B) commit to direction without evidence. (C) ignores a meaningful market signal.
