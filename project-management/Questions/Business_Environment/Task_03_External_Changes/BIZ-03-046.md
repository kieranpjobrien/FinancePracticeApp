# Question

A draft regulation has been published for 90-day consultation. It would significantly change reporting requirements for your product if enacted. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Wait until the regulation is enacted before doing anything.
- **B**: Add the draft to the risk register and assess the conditional scope impact now.
- **C**: Implement the proposed changes immediately to be ready.
- **D**: Lobby against the regulation through industry channels.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A draft regulation is a known risk with measurable conditional impact; the PM logs it and analyses the impact in advance (B). (A) is reactive and may leave too little time. (C) commits scope to a regulation that may change in consultation. (D) is outside the PM's role and does not change the project's exposure.
