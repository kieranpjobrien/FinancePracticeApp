# Question

Brexit has created regulatory divergence between UK and EU rules in your product domain. Your product was scoped as a single SKU for all of Europe. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Assess the divergence, model the cost of one version vs. two, and present options to stakeholders.
- **B**: Build separate UK and EU products from day one to avoid surprises later.
- **C**: Drop the UK market and focus only on the larger EU opportunity.
- **D**: Continue with a single SKU and hope the regulations re-converge over time.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

This is a classic make-or-break trade-off that needs analysis, not a default (A). (B) commits to complexity before knowing if it's needed. (C) abandons a market without testing the alternatives. (D) bets on regulatory drift in the wrong direction.
