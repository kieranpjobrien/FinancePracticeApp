# Question

A project is developing an e-commerce platform. A major technology company announces a similar platform with significant marketing investment planned. The sponsor is concerned about competing with a well-resourced rival. What should the project manager facilitate?

## Options

- **A**: Continue as planned and differentiate through execution quality
- **B**: Cancel the project to avoid competing with a technology giant
- **C**: An analysis session with stakeholders to assess competitive positioning, differentiation opportunities, target segments, and strategic options
- **D**: Accelerate delivery to beat the competitor to market

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. New competitive threats require strategic analysis. Consider: What segments is the competitor targeting? Where can we differentiate (price, features, service, niche focus)? Is head-to-head competition necessary or can we coexist? This analysis informs whether to continue, pivot, or exit.

Option A ignores the competitive dynamic without strategic analysis.

Option B surrenders without exploring whether the project can still succeed.

Option D assumes speed is the answer when strategic differentiation might be more important.
