# Question

A large technology vendor has announced a competing e-commerce platform with heavy marketing investment. Your sponsor is anxious about competing head-on. What should the project manager facilitate?

## Options

- **A**: An analysis session with stakeholders covering positioning, segments, differentiation, and strategic options.
- **B**: An immediate decision to cancel the project to avoid competing with a giant.
- **C**: An accelerated launch plan to beat the competitor to market.
- **D**: A continuation of the current plan with a focus on superior delivery quality.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

A new competitor of that scale demands strategic analysis, not a reflex (A). (B) surrenders without testing whether differentiation is feasible. (C) treats speed as strategy. (D) ignores the competitive dynamic and hopes execution alone wins.
