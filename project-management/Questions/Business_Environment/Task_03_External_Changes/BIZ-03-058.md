# Question

Negative media coverage about your industry has emerged, prompting questions about your project from internal staff and external partners. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Refer all questions to corporate affairs and decline to comment.
- **B**: Coordinate with corporate affairs on a consistent response and communicate proactively to project stakeholders.
- **C**: Reassure stakeholders with the PM's personal view of the coverage.
- **D**: Wait for the coverage to fade before saying anything.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The PM partners with corporate affairs on messaging and is proactive with project stakeholders (B). (A) abandons the project's stakeholders. (C) creates inconsistent messaging. (D) leaves a vacuum that rumours will fill.
