# Question

How should the PM build awareness of external changes that may affect a 24-month project?

## Options

- **A**: Rely on the sponsor to flag changes during steering committee meetings.
- **B**: Monitor news headlines daily and act on anything related.
- **C**: Subscribe to industry feeds and review them once at the project midpoint.
- **D**: Establish a regular environmental scan with relevant SMEs as part of risk reviews.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A regular structured scan with the right SMEs catches signals consistently (D). (A) is passive and depends on the sponsor's bandwidth. (B) is unfiltered and reactive. (C) is too infrequent for a long project.
