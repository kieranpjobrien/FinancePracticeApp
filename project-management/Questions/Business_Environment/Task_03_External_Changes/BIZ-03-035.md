# Question

Which of the following is an example of an Enterprise Environmental Factor (EEF) external to the organisation?

## Options

- **A**: The organisation's PMO methodology.
- **B**: The organisation's standard project templates.
- **C**: Government regulations affecting the industry.
- **D**: The organisation's risk tolerance.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

External EEFs include regulations, market conditions, and industry standards (C). (A), (B), and (D) are organisational assets or organisational EEFs internal to the organisation.
