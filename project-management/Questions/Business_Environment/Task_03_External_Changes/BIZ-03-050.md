# Question

A supplier providing critical software components has filed for insolvency. They continue to operate under administration. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Activate the contingency plan immediately and seek alternative suppliers for the components.
- **B**: Stop payments to the supplier immediately while the insolvency process plays out.
- **C**: Continue as if nothing has happened until something materially does affect delivery.
- **D**: Engage procurement and legal to clarify contract status and continuity options.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Insolvency triggers contractual and continuity questions that procurement and legal must analyse first (D). (A) activates contingency before the contract status is known. (B) may breach the contract and accelerate worse outcomes. (C) is wilful blindness.
