# Question

Which of the following is an example of an external business environment change?

## Options

- **A**: A new government regulation introduced in the project's industry.
- **B**: A team member resigning unexpectedly mid-sprint.
- **C**: An internal approval delay caused by a finance team backlog.
- **D**: A scope change requested by the project's product owner.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

External changes originate outside the organisation (A): regulation, markets, economy, competitors, technology, geopolitics. (B), (C), and (D) all happen inside the organisation or the project itself.
