# Question

Which of the following is an example of an external business environment change that could affect a project?

## Options

- **A**: A team member leaving the project
- **B**: New government regulations in the project's industry
- **C**: A delay in an internal approval process
- **D**: A change in the project scope

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. External business environment changes come from outside the organisation - regulatory changes, market shifts, economic conditions, competitor actions, technology disruptions, political events. Government regulations are external factors that can significantly impact projects, potentially requiring scope changes or creating compliance requirements.

Option A (team member leaving) is an internal resource issue.

Option C (internal approval delay) is an internal process issue.

Option D (scope change) is a project-level change, not an external environment change.
