# Question

The sole manufacturer of a critical IoT component has announced end-of-life. Suitable alternatives exist but require redesign. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Place a one-off bulk order to stockpile the original component for the foreseeable future.
- **B**: Stand up a parallel manufacturing capability to keep producing the original component in-house.
- **C**: Begin redesigning around the leading alternative right away.
- **D**: Quantify the EOL timeline, assess alternatives and redesign effort, and bring options to stakeholders.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

EOL on a critical part is a strategic supply risk that calls for an options analysis (D). (A) buys time but does not solve it. (B) is rarely viable economically. (C) commits to redesign before evaluating other paths.
