# Question

How often should a project manager monitor the external business environment?

## Options

- **A**: Only at the start of the project
- **B**: When a significant change is reported in the news
- **C**: Continuously throughout the project lifecycle
- **D**: During annual planning cycles

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. External environments change continuously. Effective project managers maintain ongoing awareness of market conditions, competitive dynamics, regulatory developments, and other factors. This enables early detection and proactive response rather than reactive crisis management.

Option A misses changes that occur after initiation.

Option B is reactive rather than proactive - by the time news reports changes, competitors may already be responding.

Option D is too infrequent for dynamic environments.
