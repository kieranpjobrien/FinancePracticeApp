# Question

A project is developing training content. Generative AI tools have recently become capable of producing similar content much faster and cheaper. This technology wasn't available when the project started. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Continue with the original approach since it was approved
- **B**: Immediately switch to AI-generated content
- **C**: Assess how the new technology could enhance or threaten the project, evaluate quality and cost implications, and discuss with stakeholders whether to adapt the approach
- **D**: Add AI tools to the project without changing scope or budget

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Disruptive technology changes require evaluation. Consider: Can AI produce adequate quality? What are the cost savings? Are there hybrid approaches? What are the risks (quality, intellectual property, differentiation)? Stakeholders should decide whether and how to incorporate the new capability.

Option A ignores a potentially significant opportunity or threat.

Option B adopts new technology without evaluating quality, risks, or stakeholder input.

Option D assumes AI tools can be added without budget or scope implications, which is unrealistic.
