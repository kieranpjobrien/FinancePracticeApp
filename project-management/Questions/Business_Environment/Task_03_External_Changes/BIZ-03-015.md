# Question

What is the purpose of environmental scanning in project management?

## Options

- **A**: To assess the physical environment for construction projects
- **B**: To monitor external factors that could affect the project's viability or approach
- **C**: To evaluate the environmental impact of project deliverables
- **D**: To review team member performance

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Environmental scanning is the continuous monitoring of external factors - market trends, competitive moves, regulatory changes, technological developments, economic conditions - that could affect the project. Early awareness enables proactive response rather than reactive crisis management.

Option A refers to site assessment, not business environmental scanning.

Option C refers to environmental impact assessment, a different concept.

Option D is internal performance management, not external environmental monitoring.
