# Question

What is environmental scanning in a project context?

## Options

- **A**: Auditing a construction site for environmental hazards.
- **B**: Monitoring resource utilisation across the project portfolio.
- **C**: Inspecting the carbon footprint of the project's deliverables.
- **D**: Systematically watching for external signals that could affect the project.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Environmental scanning is the disciplined monitoring of external factors that may impact the project (D). (A) is a physical environmental audit, a different activity. (B) is resource management. (C) is an ESG measure, not scanning.
