# Question

A critical niche vendor on your project quietly enters voluntary administration. They represent 18% of remaining effort with specialised knowledge. You hear about it from an industry contact, not officially. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Immediately terminate the vendor contract before their administrator becomes a legal counterparty on the project.
- **B**: Wait for formal notification from the vendor before changing anything to avoid acting on unverified information hastily.
- **C**: Verify the status through procurement and legal, assess continuity risk, and activate escrow or knowledge capture.
- **D**: Offer to pay the vendor in advance for remaining work to keep them operational through the administration process.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Unverified market signals are verified through proper channels, then continuity levers are activated (C). (A) terminates on rumour and abandons the knowledge. (B) waits when a rumour itself is cause to verify. (D) prepays an insolvent entity, which legally is likely a recoverable transaction to the administrator.
