# Question

A 5-year payback infrastructure project has been approved. Before significant spending begins, economic forecasts now indicate a recession within two years. The CFO asks the PM to reassess. What should the PM provide?

## Options

- **A**: Confirmation that the original business case remains valid because it was recently approved.
- **B**: Updated financial scenarios reflecting the recession, with cash flow analysis and options such as deferral or phasing.
- **C**: A counter-argument that economic forecasts are too unreliable to act upon.
- **D**: A recommendation to cancel the project immediately to preserve cash.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The CFO needs decision-grade analysis under the new assumptions (B): updated returns, cash-flow profile, and structural options. (A) ignores the change. (C) refuses to engage with the question asked. (D) makes a leadership decision without first showing the work.
