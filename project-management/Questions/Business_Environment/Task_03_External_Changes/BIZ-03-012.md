# Question

You are building an EV charging network on the open standard. Reliable industry sources indicate a major automaker is about to announce a competing proprietary standard. What should the project manager do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Begin work immediately to support both the open and proprietary standards in parallel.
- **B**: Wait until the announcement is formally made before doing anything about the rumour.
- **C**: Continue with the planned open standard because it was approved in the business case.
- **D**: Gather intelligence, assess market impact, and present analysis to stakeholders.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A potentially material market move warrants analysis and a stakeholder conversation before any redesign (D). (A) commits to a costly path before the news is even confirmed. (B) is passive when proactive analysis would inform a better decision. (C) ignores potentially significant market signals.
