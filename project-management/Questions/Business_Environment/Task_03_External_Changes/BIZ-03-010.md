# Question

A new tariff regime emerges mid-construction of a manufacturing facility. The cost of imported equipment now pushes the budget 25% over the approved baseline. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Continue construction and address the funding overrun later through the change control process.
- **B**: Halt construction until the trade situation resolves and import tariff rates normalise.
- **C**: Cut scope aggressively to bring the project budget back in line with the baseline.
- **D**: Analyse the cost impact, generate alternatives, and present options to stakeholders.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A 25% budget overrun from an external shock is a stakeholder decision after a proper options analysis (D). (A) keeps spending without authority. (B) is sometimes right but is a decision for stakeholders, not a PM unilateral move. (C) reaches for one lever without checking it preserves value.
