# Question

A geopolitical event has caused public uncertainty about your project's region. Stakeholders are asking pointed questions. What is the most appropriate PM response?

## Options

- **A**: Send a single all-stakeholder email summarising what is currently known about the situation.
- **B**: Wait until the next scheduled status report to address the issue with stakeholders.
- **C**: Provide regular updates tailored to each group, sharing knowns, unknowns, and actions.
- **D**: Refer all questions to corporate communications and let them handle the messaging.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Stakeholder communication during disruption is regular, tailored, and honest about uncertainty (C). (A) is one-time and undifferentiated. (B) lets concern grow. (D) absolves the PM of stakeholder engagement responsibility.
