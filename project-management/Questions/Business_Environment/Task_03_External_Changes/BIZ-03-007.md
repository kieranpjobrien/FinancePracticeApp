# Question

What is an external business environment change in the context of project management?

## Options

- **A**: A change requested by the project sponsor
- **B**: A change in scope approved through change control
- **C**: A change in factors outside the project and organization that affects the project's viability, approach, or value proposition
- **D**: A change in the project team composition

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. External business environment changes include market shifts, competitive moves, regulatory changes, economic conditions, technological disruptions, and other factors outside the project's control that may affect whether and how the project should proceed.

Option A is an internal stakeholder request, not an external environmental factor.

Option B is an internal project change, not an external environmental factor.

Option D is an internal team change, not an external environmental factor.
