# Question

What does "external business environment change" mean in PMI terminology?

## Options

- **A**: A change in factors outside the project and organisation that affects viability, approach, or value.
- **B**: A change requested by the project sponsor through the change control process.
- **C**: A scope change approved by the change control board.
- **D**: A change in the composition of the project team.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

External changes come from outside the organisation: regulators, markets, technology, geopolitics (A). (B), (C), and (D) are all internal changes managed through normal project processes.
