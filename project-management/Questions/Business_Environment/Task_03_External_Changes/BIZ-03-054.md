# Question

A new tariff will add 8% to the imported equipment your project requires. The increase pushes the project over its approved budget. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Approach procurement about alternate sources or pre-tariff inventory and assess net impact.
- **B**: Submit a budget increase request immediately.
- **C**: Cancel the equipment purchase and replace it with cheaper alternatives.
- **D**: Wait to see whether the tariff is reversed.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The PM looks for ways to absorb or reduce the change before requesting more money (A). (B) jumps to a budget increase without exploring options. (C) substitutes scope without analysis. (D) is passive and may incur the tariff anyway.
