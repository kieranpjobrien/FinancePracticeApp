# Question

A competitor has acquired your largest implementation partner. Your delivery model relies on that partner for half of the implementation work. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Continue with the partner because the contract is unchanged.
- **B**: Terminate the partner contract immediately and procure a replacement.
- **C**: Convene legal, procurement, and the partner to clarify implications and agree continuity terms before deciding next steps.
- **D**: Inform customers that delivery may be at risk.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The change requires multi-party clarification before any decision (C); the partner may continue to deliver, conditions may change, or termination may be appropriate. (A) ignores a strategic risk. (B) reacts before facts are known. (D) is premature external communication.
