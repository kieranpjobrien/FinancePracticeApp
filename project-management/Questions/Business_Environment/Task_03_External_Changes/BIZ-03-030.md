# Question

The SaaS vendor underpinning your in-flight implementation has been acquired and the new owner will sunset the product in two years. You are six months from go-live. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Push the vendor for a written guarantee of continued support beyond the sunset date.
- **B**: Confirm the sunset timeline, evaluate migration paths and total cost of ownership, and present options.
- **C**: Cancel the project immediately to avoid throwing more money at a doomed product.
- **D**: Continue the implementation because two years of use will justify the spend.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The right step is fact-finding plus a TCO model that includes the inevitable migration (B). (A) is unlikely to succeed and only delays the decision. (C) abandons a possibly worthwhile short-term value. (D) accepts a two-year lifespan without doing the maths.
