# Question

Six months into your project, a major cloud provider releases a managed service that replaces 30% of the custom code your team is building. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Continue with the custom build because the team is already familiar with it.
- **B**: Switch immediately to the managed service to reduce future maintenance.
- **C**: Evaluate the trade-offs of adopting the managed service and present options to the sponsor.
- **D**: Defer the decision until after launch.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

A meaningful technology shift deserves analysis of cost, time, lock-in, and value (C); the sponsor decides on the trade-offs. (A) ignores a possibly material improvement. (B) reacts without analysis. (D) misses the chance to course-correct before launch.
