# Question

The project budget is in US dollars but a major supplier prices in euros. The euro has strengthened by 12% against the dollar over the past quarter. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Reforecast the budget reflecting the new exchange rate and engage finance about hedging or contingency.
- **B**: Continue at the original budget and absorb the variance in operations.
- **C**: Renegotiate with the supplier to price in dollars.
- **D**: Cancel the supplier engagement to remove FX exposure.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The PM updates the forecast and partners with finance on appropriate FX management (A). (B) ignores a real change and shifts the consequence to others. (C) is one possible outcome of negotiation but not the first action. (D) discards the supplier rather than managing the cost change.
