# Question

The macro environment is unusually unstable; multiple external risks could materialise. The sponsor wants the project plan to "be ready for anything." What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Build scenario plans for two or three plausible futures, identifying triggers and pre-agreed responses.
- **B**: Build a single contingency plan for the most likely scenario.
- **C**: Add 30% contingency to the budget and schedule and hope for the best.
- **D**: Pause the project until the macro environment stabilises.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Scenario planning with triggers makes the project genuinely ready for multiple futures (A). (B) plans for one and ignores the others. (C) buys cushion without intent. (D) avoids the problem.
