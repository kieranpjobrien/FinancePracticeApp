# Question

Your project uses AI components and a draft regulation requiring algorithmic explainability is moving through the legislature. Likely effective date is 12 to 18 months after your launch in three months. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Proceed as planned because the draft regulation is not yet in force.
- **B**: Wait for the regulation to formally pass before considering any design change.
- **C**: Redesign immediately to add full algorithmic explainability to the launch release.
- **D**: Estimate likelihood and effort, then bring "build now" versus "adapt later" options.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The right call depends on probability, cost-of-delay, and cost-of-rework — exactly the trade-off stakeholders should weigh (D). (A) ignores a foreseeable obligation. (B) loses the chance to prepare. (C) over-invests against an uncertain rule.
