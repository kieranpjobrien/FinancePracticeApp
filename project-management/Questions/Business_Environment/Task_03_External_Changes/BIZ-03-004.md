# Question

A project to expand into a new market is 6 months underway when a major competitor announces they're entering the same market with a similar product launching in 3 months. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Continue with the original plan; ignore competitor actions
- **B**: Analyse the competitive threat, assess options (accelerate, differentiate, or pivot), and present to stakeholders
- **C**: Immediately cancel the project since the competitor will win
- **D**: Copy the competitor's approach to ensure parity

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. External business environment changes require analysis and informed response. The PM should assess the threat (is the competitor's product really comparable?), evaluate options (can we launch faster? can we differentiate?), and present options to stakeholders for strategic decision. The response should be deliberate, not reactive.

Option A (ignore) misses a significant change that affects project value.

Option C (cancel immediately) is premature without analysis - there may still be viable paths.

Option D (copy) cedes strategic initiative and may not be appropriate for the organisation's strategy.
