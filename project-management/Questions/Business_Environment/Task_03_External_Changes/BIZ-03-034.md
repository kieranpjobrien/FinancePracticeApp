# Question

A project relies on contractors based in a country experiencing growing geopolitical instability. The team is concerned but no incident has occurred. What is the PM's most appropriate action?

## Options

- **A**: Replace all contractors immediately to remove the risk.
- **B**: Take no action because no incident has occurred yet.
- **C**: Develop a contingency plan with the contractor and procurement covering escalation triggers and continuity options.
- **D**: Add the situation to the issue log and review monthly.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Contingency planning addresses an emerging risk before an incident (C); triggers turn the plan into action when needed. (A) is overreaction without an event. (B) is passive complacency. (D) wrongly classes a future risk as a current issue and lacks a response plan.
