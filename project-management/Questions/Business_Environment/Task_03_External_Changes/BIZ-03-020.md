# Question

A project is developing a consumer product. Social media trends indicate growing consumer concern about data privacy. The product's business model relies on collecting user data for personalized advertising. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Continue as planned since the business model was approved
- **B**: Monitor the trend but take no immediate action
- **C**: Assess how privacy concerns might affect adoption and long-term viability, explore alternative business models, and discuss strategic options with stakeholders
- **D**: Add privacy features without changing the core business model

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Shifting social attitudes toward privacy could significantly affect product adoption and the regulatory environment. The PM should help stakeholders understand the potential impact and explore whether the business model should evolve, or what privacy-preserving alternatives might exist.

Option A ignores changing social dynamics that could affect product success.

Option B is passive when proactive analysis could inform better decisions.

Option D adds features without addressing the fundamental business model question.
