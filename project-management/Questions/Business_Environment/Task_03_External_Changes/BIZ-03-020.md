# Question

Your consumer product's business model relies on collecting user data for personalised advertising. Public sentiment about data privacy is shifting sharply against ad-tracking. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Add a few cosmetic privacy features without meaningfully changing the underlying data model.
- **B**: Continue as planned because the advertising-based business model has already been approved.
- **C**: Monitor the trend passively but defer any action until adoption suffers in a measurable way.
- **D**: Assess how privacy attitudes affect adoption and explore alternative models with stakeholders.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A change in social attitudes can erode adoption and trigger regulation, so it is a strategic risk worth proper analysis (D). (A) is window-dressing. (B) ignores a relevant signal. (C) waits for damage before responding.
