# Question

What does a SWOT analysis evaluate?

## Options

- **A**: Strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats relevant to the project or organisation.
- **B**: Schedule, work, organisation, and team factors of the project.
- **C**: Stakeholders, work products, options, and tasks within the WBS.
- **D**: Scope, workload, ownership, and timeline of the project.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

SWOT examines internal strengths and weaknesses alongside external opportunities and threats (A). (B), (C), and (D) are made-up expansions.
