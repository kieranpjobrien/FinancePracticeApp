# Question

An impediment log shows that similar blockers keep recurring across different teams in the organisation. The PM's individual project has resolved these issues each time. What broader action should the PM consider?

## Options

- **A**: Focus only on their own project's impediments
- **B**: Let other PMs handle their own recurring issues
- **C**: Raise the pattern at a cross-project or PMO level to address systemic root causes that would benefit multiple teams
- **D**: Document the pattern but take no action

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Patterns of recurring impediments across teams indicate systemic issues. Individual projects solving the same problems repeatedly is wasteful. Raising patterns at the organisational level (PMO, community of practice, leadership) can drive systemic improvements that benefit everyone. This is servant leadership beyond the immediate team.

Option A misses an opportunity to help the organisation.

Option B duplicates problem-solving effort.

Option D documents without improving.
