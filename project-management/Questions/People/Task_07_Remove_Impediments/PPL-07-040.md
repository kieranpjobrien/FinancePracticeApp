# Question

What is the first step in addressing an identified impediment?

## Options

- **A**: Escalate to management
- **B**: Understand the impediment clearly—what exactly is blocked, what's the impact, what's the root cause
- **C**: Assign someone to fix it immediately
- **D**: Add it to the retrospective agenda

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Before taking action, ensure clear understanding. "I'm blocked" is insufficient—what specific work is blocked? What's the actual barrier? What's the impact (time, cost, risk)? Understanding root cause prevents addressing symptoms while the real problem persists. This analysis informs the appropriate response level and approach.

Option A may be premature without understanding.

Option C may address the wrong problem.

Option D delays action on current blockers.
