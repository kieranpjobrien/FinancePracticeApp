# Question

During a sprint, a team member raises that unclear requirements are blocking their work. The Product Owner is unavailable until tomorrow. What immediate actions should the Scrum Master take?

## Options

- **A**: Wait for the Product Owner to return
- **B**: Have the developer make their best guess and continue
- **C**: Document the specific questions, check if other team members or stakeholders can clarify, identify any assumptions that could enable progress, and arrange for the PO to address remaining questions as soon as possible
- **D**: Remove the story from the sprint

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Minimise blocking time through multiple approaches. Documenting specific questions (not vague "unclear requirements") enables efficient PO time. Other team members or stakeholders may have answers. Some assumptions may be safe to make (with documentation). When the PO is available, focused questions get fast answers.

Option A wastes time unnecessarily.

Option B risks building the wrong thing.

Option D may be premature if clarification is straightforward.
