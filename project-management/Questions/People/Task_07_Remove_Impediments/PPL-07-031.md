# Question

When should impediments typically be raised within a Scrum cadence?

## Options

- **A**: Only at the sprint retrospective, where the whole team can discuss them in context.
- **B**: Only during sprint planning, before commitment is made.
- **C**: Only when they have escalated into critical blockers affecting the sprint goal.
- **D**: As soon as they are identified, with the daily standup as a regular forum for visibility.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The Scrum Guide and PMI both recommend raising impediments as soon as they appear, with the daily standup serving as the predictable surface point. (D) reflects this. (A) waits too long. (B) limits visibility to the start of the sprint. (C) lets small impediments grow before action is taken.
