# Question

When should impediments typically be raised in Scrum?

## Options

- **A**: Only during sprint retrospective
- **B**: Only during sprint planning
- **C**: As soon as they're identified, with daily standup as a regular forum for visibility
- **D**: Only when they become critical blockers

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Impediments should be raised immediately when discovered—waiting loses valuable time. The daily standup provides a regular checkpoint for visibility ("I'm blocked by..."). This allows quick response and keeps the team informed. Waiting until retrospective or until issues become critical delays resolution unnecessarily.

Option A waits too long.

Option B is only at the sprint start.

Option D allows small issues to grow into larger problems.
