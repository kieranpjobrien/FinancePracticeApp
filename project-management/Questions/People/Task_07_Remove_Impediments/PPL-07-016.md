# Question

The team's biggest impediment is organisational - layers of approval required by company policy slow everything down. The PM has escalated repeatedly without success. What else can the PM try?

## Options

- **A**: Give up and accept the impediment as unchangeable
- **B**: Build a coalition of PMs facing similar issues to advocate for policy change, while also finding ways to work within the system more efficiently
- **C**: Violate the policies to get things done
- **D**: Continuously complain about the organisation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Two-pronged approach: 1) Build coalition for systemic change - other PMs facing similar issues create collective voice with more influence than individual complaints. 2) Optimise within constraints - prepare excellent submissions that pass approval quickly, build relationships with approvers, understand what causes delays and address proactively.

Option A accepts avoidable waste.

Option C creates risk and potential consequences.

Option D is ineffective and damages the PM's credibility.
