# Question

Your project has been blocked repeatedly by the organisation's slow capital approval process. Three escalations have produced no change. What should the project manager do as a longer-term response?

## Options

- **A**: Add buffer to all future estimates to absorb the typical capital approval delays.
- **B**: Bypass the approval process for low-value items so the team can keep moving forward.
- **C**: Vent to the team about organisational dysfunction so they understand the real constraint.
- **D**: Document the impact across recent decisions, then work with the PMO to propose a process improvement.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PMI prefers data-driven advocacy through the right governance channels for systemic problems. (D) addresses the cause and benefits future projects too. (A) institutionalises the waste rather than fixing it. (B) breaches governance and creates audit risk. (C) is unprofessional and does not produce change.
