# Question

An impediment has been escalated several times and your line manager has been unable to get traction with the responsible department. The issue is now affecting the schedule. What should the project manager do NEXT?

## Options

- **A**: Continue to escalate up the same management line until someone finally forces a resolution to it.
- **B**: Halt the affected workstream entirely until the impediment is fully resolved by the other department.
- **C**: Accept the impediment as part of the operating environment and replan the project's schedule around it.
- **D**: Document the impact, prepare options, and engage the sponsor to decide on intervention or scope change.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PMI's escalation pattern is to work the issue at the right level with evidence and options, and to involve the sponsor when normal channels are exhausted. (D) does both. (A) repeats a path that has not worked. (B) is disproportionate and damages delivery. (C) accepts a known and managed-around loss without giving the sponsor a choice.
