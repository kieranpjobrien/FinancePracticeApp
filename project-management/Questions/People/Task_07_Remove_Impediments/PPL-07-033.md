# Question

Your team is repeatedly blocked because the shared test environment is double-booked by other teams. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Direct the team to design around test environment unavailability and run tests opportunistically.
- **B**: Demand exclusive test-environment access for your team's critical windows.
- **C**: Convene the affected teams and the environment owner to agree a booking system, usage rules, and explore options like ephemeral or containerised environments.
- **D**: Pause testing on the affected workstream until the booking conflicts naturally subside.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

PMI treats shared-resource conflicts as collaborative problems, often with technical solutions that reduce contention. (C) addresses cause and impact across the affected teams. (A) accepts the loss and reduces test discipline. (B) treats other teams as obstacles and is likely to escalate the conflict. (D) is a delay strategy that does not fix the underlying issue.
