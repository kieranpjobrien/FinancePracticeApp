# Question

A team is blocked because a shared test environment is frequently unavailable due to conflicts with other teams. What should the PM do to address this systemic impediment?

## Options

- **A**: Have the team work around test environment issues
- **B**: Demand exclusive access to the test environment
- **C**: Work with other teams and environment owners to establish booking systems, usage agreements, or explore options like containerised environments that reduce shared resource conflicts
- **D**: Delay testing until the environment is reliably available

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Shared resource conflicts require collaborative solutions. Booking systems create predictability. Usage agreements establish norms. Technical solutions like containerisation or environment virtualisation may eliminate the conflict entirely. Working with other affected teams builds coalition for better tooling or processes.

Option A doesn't solve the problem.

Option B creates conflict with other teams who have equal needs.

Option D delays delivery without addressing root cause.
