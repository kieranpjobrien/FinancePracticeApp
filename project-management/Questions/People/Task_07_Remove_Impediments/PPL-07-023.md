# Question

In Scrum, who is primarily responsible for removing impediments that the team cannot resolve themselves?

## Options

- **A**: Product Owner
- **B**: Development Team
- **C**: Scrum Master
- **D**: Stakeholders

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. The Scrum Master serves the team by removing impediments that block progress. While the team handles internal issues, the Scrum Master addresses organisational barriers, facilitates cross-team coordination, and escalates when necessary. This is a core servant leadership responsibility.

Option A focuses on product value and backlog.

Option B handles technical work within their capacity.

Option D are external to the team's functioning.
