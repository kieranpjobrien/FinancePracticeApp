# Question

In Scrum, who is primarily responsible for removing impediments that block the team?

## Options

- **A**: The Product Owner
- **B**: The Development Team
- **C**: The Scrum Master
- **D**: The Project Sponsor

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. The Scrum Master's role includes serving the team by removing impediments that block progress. When the team identifies blockers they can't resolve themselves, the Scrum Master works to eliminate them - whether through direct action, escalation, or facilitating resolution.

Option A - The Product Owner manages the backlog and maximises value, not impediment removal.

Option B - The team can resolve some impediments but not those requiring external action.

Option D - The sponsor provides resources and support but isn't part of the Scrum team.
