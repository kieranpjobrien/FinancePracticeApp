# Question

The team's designated code reviewer is consistently overloaded, and code reviews have become a recurring blocker on multiple PRs each sprint. What should the Scrum Master do?

## Options

- **A**: Ask the reviewer to prioritise reviews above their other work for the rest of the release.
- **B**: Distribute review responsibility across more team members, batch reviews at agreed times, and consider pairing on the highest-risk changes to reduce separate review load.
- **C**: Escalate to the reviewer's line manager to free up more of their time.
- **D**: Drop the review step temporarily to clear the backlog of waiting PRs.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PMI prefers systemic solutions to chronic team-level bottlenecks over pressuring individuals or skipping quality. (B) addresses the cause at the team's own level. (A) loads the same person more heavily and does not solve the structural issue. (C) escalates before resolution at the team level. (D) trades quality for speed and removes a critical safeguard.
