# Question

The Scrum Master logged an impediment three days ago about a blocked test environment, but has taken no further action and the developer is still blocked. What should happen NEXT?

## Options

- **A**: Continue to log the impediment and wait for the situation to resolve naturally.
- **B**: The Scrum Master should now actively work the impediment: identify who can resolve it, push it through, escalate where needed, and report progress to the team daily.
- **C**: The developer should resolve the access issue themselves since they are best placed to know what they need.
- **D**: Move the developer onto unrelated backlog items for the rest of the sprint and revisit later.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A Scrum Master is accountable for impediments being addressed, not just recorded. (B) is the active role the Scrum Guide describes. (A) is passive and unacceptable after three days. (C) abdicates the SM's responsibility and burdens the blocked individual. (D) is a workaround that leaves the cause untouched.
