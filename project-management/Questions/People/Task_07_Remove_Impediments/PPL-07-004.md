# Question

Your team repeatedly raises a regulated approval gate as their biggest impediment. Compliance has confirmed the gate is mandatory and cannot be removed. What should the Scrum Master do?

## Options

- **A**: Accept the impediment as immovable and stop investing time in it.
- **B**: Continue to escalate the impediment to senior leadership until the process is changed.
- **C**: Help the team anticipate approval needs early in each sprint, parallelise work that does not depend on the gate, and pre-stage submissions to minimise blocking time.
- **D**: Document the impediment in detail and present it at the quarterly business review.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

PMI expects the SM to reduce the impact of constraints that cannot be removed by changing how the team interacts with them. (C) is practical mitigation. (A) gives up without exploring options. (B) wastes effort on an unwinnable change. (D) documents but does not help the team in the current sprint.
