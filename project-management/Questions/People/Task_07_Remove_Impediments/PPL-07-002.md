# Question

In agile usage, what is the PRIMARY difference between a blocker and an impediment?

## Options

- **A**: A blocker completely halts work on an item, while an impediment slows progress but lets work continue.
- **B**: A blocker is internal to the team, while an impediment is external.
- **C**: Blockers are tracked formally, while impediments are tracked informally.
- **D**: The two terms are interchangeable in everyday agile practice.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The standard distinction is whether work can continue at all. (A) reflects how teams use the terms in practice and how prioritisation typically follows. (B) and (C) are commonly held but incorrect. (D) overstates the synonymy; even though usage varies, the impact distinction is meaningful.
