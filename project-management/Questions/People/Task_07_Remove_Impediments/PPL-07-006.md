# Question

In daily standup, a developer says he has been blocked for two days waiting for access credentials from another department. What should the Scrum Master do?

## Options

- **A**: Engage the other department directly and work the access request to resolution today.
- **B**: Add the blocker to the impediments log and discuss it at the next retrospective.
- **C**: Suggest the developer be more persistent in following up the original credentials request.
- **D**: Reassign the developer to a different ticket and treat access as a future item.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

A two-day block is well past the point where the SM should be acting; servant leadership means clearing the path now. (A) addresses the cause directly. (B) treats an active blocker as a process improvement input. (C) puts the burden on the blocked individual. (D) is a workaround that leaves the impediment untouched.
