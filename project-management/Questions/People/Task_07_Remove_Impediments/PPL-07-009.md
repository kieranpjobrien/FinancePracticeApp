# Question

A team is blocked waiting for a decision from a committee that meets only monthly. The next meeting is 3 weeks away, and the delay will impact the sprint goal. What should the Scrum Master do?

## Options

- **A**: Wait for the committee meeting as that's the established process
- **B**: Find alternative paths - emergency escalation, interim approval authority, or adjusting scope to work around the dependency
- **C**: Have the team guess what the committee will decide and proceed
- **D**: Cancel the sprint until the decision is made

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. A 3-week block requires creative problem-solving. The Scrum Master should explore alternatives: Can an emergency decision be obtained? Can someone provide interim approval? Can the team work on other items while waiting? Can scope be adjusted to remove the dependency? Accepting the delay as unchangeable fails the team.

Option A accepts an avoidable delay.

Option C risks significant rework if the guess is wrong.

Option D is drastic when other options exist.
