# Question

The team is blocked on a decision that the architecture review board only takes up at its monthly meeting, which is three weeks away. The sprint goal will miss without an earlier resolution. What should the Scrum Master do?

## Options

- **A**: Wait for the scheduled monthly review and accept that the sprint goal will slip.
- **B**: Have the team take the architectural decision themselves so the sprint can still complete.
- **C**: Cancel the current sprint and start a new one once the review decision finally arrives.
- **D**: Pursue alternatives such as an out-of-cycle review, a delegated interim approver, or scope changes.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PMI expects the SM to actively pursue paths around an unacceptable wait, especially when the sprint goal is at stake. (D) explores realistic options rather than accepting the delay. (A) gives up too easily. (B) bypasses governance. (C) is a heavy intervention that does not address the underlying block.
