# Question

A vendor is two weeks late delivering a critical component and has stopped responding to the project manager's emails. The team is now blocked on the integration work. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Issue a contract termination notice to compel a response.
- **B**: Have the team build a temporary version of the component internally to keep going.
- **C**: Wait another week before taking further action since the relationship has been good historically.
- **D**: Use the contractual escalation path, involve your procurement and legal contacts, document each contact, and explore mitigation options in parallel.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PMI expects PMs to use contractual mechanisms, document interactions, and pursue mitigation in parallel rather than jumping to either inaction or termination. (D) is the disciplined response. (A) is premature and will likely damage future supply. (B) may breach the contract or create unsupported work. (C) lets the schedule slip further without action.
