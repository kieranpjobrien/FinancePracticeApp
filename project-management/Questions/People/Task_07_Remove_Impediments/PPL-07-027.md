# Question

What tool is commonly used to track and visualise impediments in agile projects?

## Options

- **A**: Gantt chart
- **B**: Impediment board or impediment backlog
- **C**: Work breakdown structure
- **D**: PERT chart

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. An impediment board (physical or digital) makes blockers visible and trackable. It typically shows impediments raised, who is working on them, and their status. Some teams use an impediment backlog prioritised by impact. Visibility drives accountability and helps prioritise resolution efforts.

Option A is for schedule visualisation.

Option C is for decomposing work scope.

Option D is for network schedule analysis.
