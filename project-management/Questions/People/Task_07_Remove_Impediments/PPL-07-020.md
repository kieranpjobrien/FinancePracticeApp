# Question

A team member is blocked because a colleague hasn't completed a dependency. When asked, the colleague says they're too busy with other priorities. What should the PM do first?

## Options

- **A**: Reassign the blocked team member to other work permanently
- **B**: Escalate immediately to the colleague's manager
- **C**: Understand the colleague's situation, clarify the impact of the dependency delay, and work together to find a solution (reprioritisation, assistance, deadline negotiation)
- **D**: Tell the blocked team member to wait

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Before escalating, understand the situation. The colleague may have legitimate competing priorities unknown to the PM. Clarifying impact ("this blocks three people and affects our sprint goal") helps prioritisation. Collaborative problem-solving (can we help? can we adjust scope? can we negotiate?) often resolves issues faster than escalation.

Option A doesn't remove the impediment for future work.

Option B may be premature and damages relationships.

Option D accepts unnecessary delay.
