# Question

A team member is blocked because a colleague in another team has not finished a dependency. The colleague says she is overloaded with other priorities. What should the project manager do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Reassign the blocked team member to other work for the remainder of the sprint.
- **B**: Talk directly with the colleague about her priorities and jointly explore options.
- **C**: Escalate the issue immediately to the colleague's line manager for directive resolution.
- **D**: Ask the team member to wait for the dependency to clear without raising it further.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PMI expects PMs to attempt direct, collaborative resolution before escalating, and to address the underlying issue rather than working around it. (B) does that. (A) is a workaround that leaves the dependency unresolved. (C) escalates prematurely and damages the relationship. (D) accepts an avoidable delay.
