# Question

Five teams in your programme each separately report the shared test environment is unstable and frequently down. Each has escalated alone with no improvement. What should the project managers and Scrum Masters do?

## Options

- **A**: Continue escalating individually so leadership receives consistent signal from each team.
- **B**: Have each team build its own dedicated test environment to remove the dependency.
- **C**: Aggregate the impact across teams, present a unified business case to infrastructure leadership, and request prioritisation.
- **D**: Accept the environment as a constraint and adjust each team's plans accordingly.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

PMI treats systemic, multi-team impediments as candidates for coordinated advocacy with consolidated data. (C) does that. (A) creates noise and fatigue without changing the picture. (B) duplicates infrastructure and may not be feasible. (D) accepts a known and avoidable productivity loss.
