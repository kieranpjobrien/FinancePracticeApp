# Question

Multiple teams identify the same impediment - a shared testing environment that's frequently unavailable. Individual team escalations haven't resolved it. What should the PM/Scrum Masters do?

## Options

- **A**: Each team should continue escalating independently
- **B**: Coordinate across teams to present a unified business case to infrastructure management, quantifying the total impact
- **C**: Each team should build their own testing environment
- **D**: Accept environment limitations as unchangeable

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Systemic impediments affecting multiple teams require coordinated response. Aggregating impact across teams creates a stronger business case than individual complaints. A unified request from multiple project managers/Scrum Masters demonstrates the issue's scope and priority.

Option A dilutes the message and may create escalation fatigue.

Option C creates redundancy and may not be feasible.

Option D accepts preventable productivity loss.
