# Question

A project manager is planning the first meeting for a newly formed virtual team. Some members have worked together before while others are completely new to the organisation. What should be the PM's FIRST priority?

## Options

- **A**: Review the project timeline and deliverables in detail
- **B**: Establish working agreements and allow team members to get to know each other
- **C**: Assign roles and responsibilities immediately
- **D**: Review the technical architecture

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. For a newly formed team, relationship building and shared expectations come before detailed project work. Team members need to know who they're working with and how they'll work together before diving into what they'll work on. This foundation supports all future collaboration.

Option A jumps into project details before the team is formed as a unit.

Option C assigns responsibilities without understanding team dynamics and capabilities.

Option D focuses on technical content before human connection.
