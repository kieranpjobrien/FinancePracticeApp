# Question

What is the PRIMARY advantage of asynchronous communication for a virtual team that spans multiple time zones?

## Options

- **A**: It guarantees that decisions are reached more quickly than synchronous discussion would allow.
- **B**: It removes the need for video conferencing as a communication channel for the team.
- **C**: It reduces the volume of project documentation the team needs to maintain over time.
- **D**: It lets team members contribute substantively during their own working hours instead of attending meetings outside them.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The headline benefit of async work is inclusion across time zones — anyone can contribute meaningfully without anyone having to take calls at midnight. (A) is the opposite: async generally slows decisions; the trade is inclusion for speed. (B) overstates the case; video remains valuable for relationship-building and complex topics. (C) is also backwards — async work usually requires more documentation, not less, because context has to be carried in writing.
