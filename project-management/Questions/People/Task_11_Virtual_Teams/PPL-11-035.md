# Question

When scheduling meetings for a virtual team spanning multiple time zones, what principle should guide the approach?

## Options

- **A**: Always schedule during the project manager's business hours
- **B**: Rotate meeting times to share inconvenience fairly across the team
- **C**: Schedule all meetings for the time zone with the most team members
- **D**: Only hold asynchronous meetings to avoid time zone issues

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Fairness requires distributing the burden of inconvenient meeting times across all team members rather than consistently disadvantaging those in certain time zones. Rotation shows respect for all team members and prevents burnout for those always attending outside business hours.

Option A consistently disadvantages team members in other time zones.

Option C still disadvantages the minority consistently.

Option D eliminates valuable synchronous communication entirely; the goal is balance, not avoidance.
