# Question

A virtual team is experiencing significant conflict via email, with messages becoming increasingly hostile. The conflict started over a technical disagreement but has become personal. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Continue email communication to have a written record
- **B**: Move the discussion to video call to humanise the interaction, facilitate the conversation, and address both the technical issue and the relationship
- **C**: Let them work it out via email
- **D**: Ban email communication for the team

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Email escalates conflict because it lacks human cues and allows time to craft hostile responses. Video brings back facial expressions, tone, and real-time interaction that humanise the other person. The PM should facilitate the call, address the technical disagreement, and then address the interpersonal damage.

Option A allows continued escalation.

Option C abdicates responsibility for team health.

Option D is an overreaction that removes a useful communication tool.
