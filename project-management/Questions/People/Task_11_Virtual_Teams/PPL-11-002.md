# Question

Which communication medium is MOST effective for conveying complex or sensitive information to a virtual team?

## Options

- **A**: Email with detailed written explanation
- **B**: Video conference allowing for visual cues and real-time dialogue
- **C**: Instant messaging for quick back-and-forth
- **D**: Shared document with comments enabled

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Video conferencing provides the richest communication medium for virtual teams - participants can see facial expressions and body language, ask clarifying questions in real-time, and address misunderstandings immediately. For complex or sensitive topics, this richness reduces misinterpretation.

Option A (email) lacks visual cues and real-time interaction, making it harder to convey nuance or gauge understanding. Good for documentation but not for initial discussion of complex topics.

Option C (instant messaging) is too informal and brief for complex topics. Good for quick questions, not detailed discussions.

Option D (shared documents) is good for collaborative writing but lacks the interactive, real-time dialogue needed for complex or sensitive conversations.
