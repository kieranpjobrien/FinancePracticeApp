# Question

A virtual team uses email, chat, video calls, and a project management platform. Team members complain that they can't find information and frequently miss important updates. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Consolidate the team onto a single communication platform to remove the confusion of multiple channels.
- **B**: Establish clear guidelines for which channel is used for what — decisions, quick questions, complex discussions, persistent reference — and document them.
- **C**: Send a daily digest email summarising key updates from across all channels.
- **D**: Designate one person to monitor all channels and ensure nothing is missed.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The problem isn't the number of tools but the lack of an agreement about how to use each one; communication management plans live in this space. (B) addresses the cause. (A) discards channels that each have a legitimate purpose and forces all communication into one inappropriate format. (C) adds another summary layer on top of the existing noise. (D) creates a single point of failure and asks one person to compensate for an absent norm.
