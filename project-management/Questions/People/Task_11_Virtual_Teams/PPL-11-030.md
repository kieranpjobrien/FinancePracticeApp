# Question

A virtual team member recently returned from medical leave and seems hesitant to participate fully in meetings. The PM wants to support their reintegration without making assumptions. What is the BEST approach?

## Options

- **A**: Treat them exactly as before to avoid drawing attention to the leave
- **B**: Reduce their workload without discussing it with them
- **C**: Have a private conversation to understand how they're feeling and what support they need
- **D**: Ask other team members to be patient with them

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. A private conversation respects the individual's privacy while showing care. It avoids assumptions about what they need - some people want to dive back in, others need gradual reintegration. Asking "How can I support you?" empowers them to direct their own return.

Option A ignores that they may have different needs now.

Option B makes assumptions and removes agency from the team member.

Option D treats them as a problem rather than addressing their needs directly.
