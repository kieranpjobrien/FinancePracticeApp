# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of a team charter for virtual teams?

## Options

- **A**: To satisfy organisational compliance requirements
- **B**: To document technical specifications
- **C**: To establish shared expectations for communication, collaboration, and working together
- **D**: To assign individual responsibilities for project deliverables

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. A team charter establishes how the team will work together - communication preferences, meeting norms, response time expectations, decision-making approaches, and conflict resolution methods. For virtual teams, this explicit agreement is especially important since implicit norms don't develop naturally.

Option A - while charters may fulfil compliance, that's not their primary purpose.

Option B - technical specifications belong in other documents.

Option D - responsibility assignment is part of project planning, not the team charter's primary function.
