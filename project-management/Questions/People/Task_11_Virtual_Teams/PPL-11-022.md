# Question

A virtual team member is consistently unresponsive for periods during their stated working hours. Their deliverables are meeting expectations, but other team members are frustrated by the delayed responses. How should the PM address this?

## Options

- **A**: Document the behaviour as a performance issue
- **B**: Require the team member to be online and responsive during all working hours
- **C**: Discuss expectations with the individual and help the team establish clear response time norms
- **D**: Tell other team members to be more patient

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. The situation requires understanding context (the person may have caregiving responsibilities, deep work needs, or other legitimate reasons) while also addressing team needs. Establishing clear norms (e.g., "respond within 4 hours during working hours unless in deep work mode") balances flexibility with predictability.

Option A treats output-meeting behaviour as a problem without understanding context.

Option B removes the flexibility that often makes virtual work valuable and may not be necessary.

Option D dismisses legitimate team concerns about collaboration.
