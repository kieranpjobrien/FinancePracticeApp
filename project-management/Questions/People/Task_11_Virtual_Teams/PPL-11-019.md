# Question

Which virtual team practice MOST directly supports building trust among team members who have never met in person?

## Options

- **A**: Limiting communication to project updates so the team stays focused on outcomes.
- **B**: Documenting every conversation in writing so there is always a clear record.
- **C**: Working through individual tasks to minimise the dependencies that could create friction.
- **D**: Holding regular video calls that include time for informal, non-work conversation.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Trust forms through repeated positive interactions that include some personal connection; video plus informal moments is the most direct simulation of how trust forms in person. (A) reduces the surface area for relationship-building. (B) helps with clarity but written records on their own don't build interpersonal trust. (C) avoids the collaboration that grows trust through shared work — a workaround, not a solution.
