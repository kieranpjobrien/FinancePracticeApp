# Question

A virtual team member in a different time zone is consistently left out of important decisions because discussions happen during synchronous meetings they can't attend. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Accept that some team members will miss discussions due to time zones
- **B**: Rotate meeting times and ensure key decisions include asynchronous input from those who can't attend synchronously
- **C**: Require the team member to attend meetings regardless of their local time
- **D**: Exclude the team member from the project

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Inclusive virtual teams require intentional accommodation. Rotate meeting times so no one always bears the burden. For critical decisions, gather input asynchronously before meetings, share meeting notes promptly, and allow async follow-up. This ensures everyone can contribute regardless of time zone.

Option A accepts preventable exclusion.

Option C is unreasonable and unsustainable.

Option D loses a team member due to a process failure.
