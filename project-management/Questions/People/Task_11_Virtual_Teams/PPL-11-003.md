# Question

A virtual team member seems disengaged - camera always off, minimal participation, and delayed responses. Their work quality remains acceptable. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Mandate cameras be on for all meetings
- **B**: Have a private conversation to understand their situation and explore what support they might need
- **C**: Accept their working style since work quality is acceptable
- **D**: Assign them to more visible tasks to increase engagement

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Changed behavior (assuming this is different from their norm) often indicates something worth understanding - it could be personal circumstances, work environment issues, feeling disconnected, or burnout. A caring, private conversation allows them to share if they wish and opens dialogue about support needs.

Option A uses mandates rather than understanding. Forced camera use may worsen whatever's underlying the disengagement.

Option C accepts potential warning signs without checking in. Acceptable work quality doesn't mean the person is okay.

Option D increases visibility pressure without understanding the situation, which may add stress.
