# Question

A virtual team member consistently misses deadlines and is unresponsive during core hours. Other team members are frustrated. The PM has had informal conversations but behaviour hasn't changed. What should the PM do next?

## Options

- **A**: Remove the team member from the project immediately
- **B**: Have a formal conversation documenting expectations, consequences, and a specific improvement plan
- **C**: Redistribute their work to other team members
- **D**: Accept performance variations as normal for virtual teams

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. After informal conversations fail, formal performance management is appropriate. A documented conversation should clarify specific expectations (availability, responsiveness, deadlines), consequences of continued issues, and a concrete improvement plan. This is fair to both the individual and the team.

Option A (remove immediately) may be premature if formal feedback hasn't been given.

Option C (redistribute work) rewards poor performance and burns out other team members.

Option D (accept it) is unfair to other team members and enables continued dysfunction.
