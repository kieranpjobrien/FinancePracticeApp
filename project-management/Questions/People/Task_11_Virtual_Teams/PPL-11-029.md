# Question

A project manager's virtual team has grown from 5 to 15 members. The informal communication practices that worked with the smaller team are now causing information gaps and duplication of effort. What should the PM implement?

## Options

- **A**: More frequent all-hands video meetings
- **B**: A structured communication plan with defined channels, frequencies, and documentation standards
- **C**: A team chat platform for real-time communication
- **D**: Designate one person as the communication coordinator

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. As teams grow, informal communication doesn't scale. A structured communication plan defines what information goes where (channels), how often updates happen (frequencies), and what gets recorded (documentation). This reduces both information overload and information gaps.

Option A doesn't scale well and may not address the need for structured information flow.

Option C is a tool, not a strategy; without structure, it may just create more noise.

Option D creates a bottleneck and single point of failure.
