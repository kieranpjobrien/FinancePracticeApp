# Question

What is a key challenge that virtual teams face compared to co-located teams?

## Options

- **A**: Virtual teams typically cannot adopt agile or hybrid delivery methods.
- **B**: Virtual teams have reduced opportunities for spontaneous communication and informal relationship-building.
- **C**: Virtual teams generally have higher direct project costs than co-located teams.
- **D**: Virtual teams cannot effectively use modern project management software.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The defining challenge of virtual work is the loss of unplanned contact — hallway moments, lunches, overheard discussions — and the relationship and information benefits that flow from them. (A) is incorrect; agile and hybrid both work well in virtual settings with adaptation. (C) often gets the economics backwards; virtual teams typically reduce travel and facility costs. (D) is also wrong; virtual teams generally make heavier use of collaboration software, not less.
