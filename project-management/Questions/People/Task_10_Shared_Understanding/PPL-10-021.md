# Question

Which agile practice is specifically designed to build shared understanding of user stories?

## Options

- **A**: Daily standup
- **B**: Sprint retrospective
- **C**: Backlog refinement (grooming)
- **D**: Release planning

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Backlog refinement sessions are specifically designed for the team to develop shared understanding of upcoming work. Stories are discussed, clarified, and estimated collaboratively. Questions surface assumptions and ambiguities. By the time work enters a sprint, the team should have shared understanding of what "done" looks like.

Option A focuses on daily coordination.

Option B focuses on process improvement.

Option D focuses on release-level planning.
