# Question

Which Scrum event is most directly designed to build shared understanding of upcoming user stories before they enter a sprint?

## Options

- **A**: Daily Scrum
- **B**: Backlog refinement
- **C**: Sprint retrospective
- **D**: Sprint review

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Backlog refinement is the explicit forum for the team and product owner to discuss, clarify, and size upcoming stories so they are ready for selection into a sprint. (A) Daily Scrum coordinates work in flight, not refinement of upcoming work. (C) Sprint retrospective inspects the team's process from the previous sprint, not the backlog. (D) Sprint review inspects the increment delivered in the just-finished sprint with stakeholders; it works backward from completed work, not forward to upcoming stories.
