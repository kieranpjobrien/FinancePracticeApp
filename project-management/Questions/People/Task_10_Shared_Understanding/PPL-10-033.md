# Question

What is a "Three Amigos" session in agile development?

## Options

- **A**: A meeting of three project managers
- **B**: A collaborative discussion among a developer, tester, and business representative to build shared understanding of a requirement
- **C**: A three-person estimation technique
- **D**: A three-day sprint planning event

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Three Amigos brings together three perspectives—development (how to build it), testing (how to verify it), and business (what value it provides)—to discuss requirements before implementation. Each perspective surfaces different questions and assumptions, building comprehensive shared understanding.

Option A misidentifies the participants.

Option C confuses with estimation techniques.

Option D confuses with sprint events.
