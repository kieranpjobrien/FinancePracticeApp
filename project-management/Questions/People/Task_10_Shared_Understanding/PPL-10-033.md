# Question

What is a "Three Amigos" session in agile development?

## Options

- **A**: A facilitated estimation technique where three team members independently estimate a story and reconcile differences.
- **B**: A coordination meeting between the Scrum Master, product owner, and a stakeholder representative.
- **C**: A short collaborative discussion between a developer, a tester, and a business representative to build shared understanding of a story.
- **D**: A backlog ranking exercise that prioritises stories using three criteria: value, risk, and effort.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The Three Amigos pattern brings the build perspective (developer), the verify perspective (tester), and the business perspective (product owner or BA) together briefly to inspect a story before development begins, surfacing different interpretations. (A) describes a flavour of estimation, not Three Amigos. (B) describes a stakeholder coordination meeting and does not include the testing perspective that defines the pattern. (D) describes a prioritisation exercise.
