# Question

In agile, what is the purpose of a sprint goal?

## Options

- **A**: To list all user stories in the sprint
- **B**: To provide a shared objective that gives the team flexibility in how to achieve it while ensuring alignment
- **C**: To calculate team velocity
- **D**: To assign individual tasks to team members

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. The sprint goal is a single objective for the sprint that creates shared understanding of purpose. It gives the team flexibility - if circumstances change, they can negotiate scope while still achieving the goal. It aligns effort by answering "what are we trying to accomplish this sprint?"

Option A describes the sprint backlog, not the sprint goal.

Option C - velocity is a metric, not the sprint goal.

Option D describes task assignment, not sprint goal.
