# Question

Which visual artefact most directly builds shared understanding of how a process works — including the sequence of activities, decision points, and handoffs between roles?

## Options

- **A**: Gantt chart
- **B**: Stakeholder engagement matrix
- **C**: Risk register
- **D**: Process flowchart

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A process flowchart (or swim-lane diagram) shows activities, branches, and handoffs in sequence, which is exactly what a team needs to align on how work flows. (A) Gantt charts show schedule and dependencies, not process logic. (B) stakeholder engagement matrices map stakeholders against engagement levels, not process steps. (C) risk registers list and assess risks; they say nothing about process flow.
