# Question

Which visual tool helps build shared understanding of how a process works by showing sequence of activities, decision points, and handoffs?

## Options

- **A**: Gantt chart
- **B**: Process flowchart or workflow diagram
- **C**: Risk register
- **D**: Stakeholder matrix

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Process flowcharts visually represent how work flows through a system—activities, decisions, branches, handoffs between roles. This visual representation helps teams develop shared understanding of how things work, identify gaps or inefficiencies, and ensure everyone sees the same process.

Option A shows schedule, not process flow.

Option C shows risks, not process.

Option D shows stakeholder relationships, not process.
