# Question

What is self-regulation in emotional intelligence?

## Options

- **A**: Controlling other people's emotions
- **B**: Managing your own emotional responses appropriately
- **C**: Avoiding all emotional situations
- **D**: Suppressing all emotions at work

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Self-regulation means managing your own emotional responses - not eliminating emotions, but choosing how to express and act on them appropriately. This includes managing impulsive reactions, thinking before acting, and responding rather than reacting.

Option A is about influencing others, not self-regulation.

Option C is avoidance, not regulation.

Option D is suppression, which is unhealthy. Self-regulation is about appropriate expression, not elimination.
