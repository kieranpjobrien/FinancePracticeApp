# Question

What is a "trigger" in the context of emotional intelligence?

## Options

- **A**: A project milestone that starts the next phase
- **B**: A stimulus that provokes a strong emotional reaction, often disproportionate to the situation
- **C**: A technique for motivating team members
- **D**: A meeting facilitation tool

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. An emotional trigger is something that provokes a strong reaction - often because it connects to past experiences, values, or sensitivities. Understanding your triggers (self-awareness) helps you manage your responses (self-regulation) when they're activated.

Options A, C, and D are unrelated to emotional intelligence triggers.
