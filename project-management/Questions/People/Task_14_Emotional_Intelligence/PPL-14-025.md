# Question

Why is empathy important for project managers?

## Options

- **A**: It allows the PM to agree with everyone
- **B**: It helps the PM understand stakeholder perspectives and respond appropriately
- **C**: It means the PM should prioritise relationships over project goals
- **D**: It eliminates the need for formal communication

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Empathy - understanding how others feel and why - helps PMs anticipate concerns, communicate effectively, and build relationships. It doesn't mean agreeing with everyone, but understanding perspectives to work effectively with diverse stakeholders.

Option A confuses empathy with agreement.

Option C creates a false dichotomy - empathy supports achieving project goals.

Option D - empathy enhances but doesn't replace formal communication.
