# Question

A PM notices that stress is affecting their leadership - they're becoming shorter with team members and less patient. They recognise this but struggle to change in the moment. What should they do?

## Options

- **A**: Take time off until stress reduces
- **B**: Apologise to the team and promise to do better
- **C**: Develop strategies for recognising and managing stress before it affects behaviour
- **D**: Delegate leadership responsibilities during stressful periods

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Self-awareness of the pattern is the first step. Developing strategies (recognising early warning signs, stress management techniques, building in recovery time) addresses the root cause. This might include physical activity, mindfulness, better boundaries, or workload management.

Option A may not be possible and doesn't build capability.

Option B acknowledges but doesn't address the underlying issue.

Option D avoids rather than develops leadership capability.
