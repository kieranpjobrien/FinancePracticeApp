# Question

A PM notices they feel frustrated whenever a particular stakeholder sends long, detailed emails. This frustration affects how they respond. What emotional intelligence skill should the PM develop?

## Options

- **A**: Empathy - understanding the stakeholder's perspective
- **B**: Self-awareness - recognising their emotional trigger
- **C**: Social skills - changing the stakeholder's behaviour
- **D**: Motivation - working harder to respond

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. The first step is self-awareness: recognising the emotional trigger and how it affects behaviour. Once the PM is aware that detailed emails trigger frustration and poor responses, they can work on self-regulation (managing the reaction) and then explore empathy (why does the stakeholder communicate this way?).

Option A is important but comes after recognising your own reaction.

Option C focuses on changing others rather than managing yourself.

Option D doesn't address the emotional dynamic.
