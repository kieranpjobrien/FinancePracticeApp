# Question

A project manager notices they feel frustrated whenever a particular stakeholder sends long, detailed emails, and the frustration is leaking into their reply tone. Which emotional intelligence skill should they develop FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Empathy, so they can better understand why the stakeholder communicates this way.
- **B**: Social skills, so they can shape the stakeholder's communication style over time.
- **C**: Motivation, so they push themselves to respond more carefully despite the irritation.
- **D**: Self-awareness, so they recognise the trigger and the way it is shaping their behaviour.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Emotional intelligence starts with self-awareness; without naming the trigger, the other components have nothing to work with. (D) is correct. (A) becomes useful once the trigger is acknowledged. (B) tries to change the stakeholder rather than the project manager. (C) tries to power through with willpower alone, which rarely changes behaviour.
