# Question

A PM receives an email from a stakeholder that feels critical and unfair. The PM's first instinct is to respond defensively. What is the emotionally intelligent approach?

## Options

- **A**: Respond immediately while the points are fresh
- **B**: Forward the email to others to validate that it was unfair
- **C**: Wait before responding, consider the stakeholder's perspective, and craft a measured response
- **D**: Ignore the email

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. The urge to respond defensively is a signal to pause. Waiting allows emotional regulation. Considering the stakeholder's perspective (empathy) may reveal legitimate concerns beneath the tone. A measured response addresses the substance without escalating the emotional content.

Option A likely sends a defensive response that may escalate conflict.

Option B seeks validation rather than resolution.

Option D ignores a communication that requires response.
