# Question

How can a project manager develop their emotional intelligence?

## Options

- **A**: Emotional intelligence is fixed and cannot be developed
- **B**: Through self-reflection, feedback, practice, and intentional development
- **C**: By reading about emotional intelligence
- **D**: By avoiding emotional situations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Emotional intelligence can be developed through multiple approaches: self-reflection (understanding your patterns), feedback (learning how others perceive you), practice (trying new approaches), and intentional development (coaching, training, deliberate practice).

Option A is incorrect - research shows EI can be developed.

Option C is insufficient - knowledge must be applied through practice.

Option D avoids rather than develops.
