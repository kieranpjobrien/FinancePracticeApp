# Question

A PM is about to deliver difficult feedback to a team member about their performance. The PM feels anxious about the conversation and has been putting it off. What should the PM do to prepare emotionally?

## Options

- **A**: Script exactly what they will say and stick to it rigidly
- **B**: Have the conversation immediately before anxiety builds further
- **C**: Acknowledge their own anxiety, prepare thoughtfully, and focus on the developmental purpose of the conversation
- **D**: Ask someone else to deliver the feedback

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Self-awareness includes recognising your own anxiety. Preparation helps manage it. Focusing on developmental purpose (helping the person improve) rather than the discomfort of criticism shifts mindset. This emotional preparation enables a more effective conversation.

Option A may feel mechanical and prevent adaptation to the person's reactions.

Option B doesn't allow for thoughtful preparation.

Option D avoids the PM's development and responsibility.
