# Question

In a team meeting, a quieter team member's proposal is brushed aside without discussion. They visibly withdraw and stop participating. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Bring the proposal back to the table for proper consideration and check in with the team member privately later.
- **B**: Continue with the agenda to keep the meeting on schedule and revisit ideas at the end.
- **C**: Immediately call out the dismissive behaviour so the team understands the standard.
- **D**: End the meeting and reconvene later when the dynamic has reset.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The emotionally intelligent response addresses both the immediate signal (a dismissed idea) and the affected person (the withdrawn team member). (A) does both. (B) lets the dynamic harden into a norm. (C) is public correction that may embarrass and worsen the situation. (D) is overcorrection that disrupts the work without addressing the cause.
