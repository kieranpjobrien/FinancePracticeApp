# Question

What are the five components of emotional intelligence as commonly defined?

## Options

- **A**: Planning, Organising, Leading, Controlling, Evaluating
- **B**: Self-awareness, Self-regulation, Motivation, Empathy, Social skills
- **C**: Listening, Speaking, Writing, Reading, Presenting
- **D**: Technical, Conceptual, Human, Political, Symbolic

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Daniel Goleman's widely-used emotional intelligence framework includes: Self-awareness (knowing your emotions), Self-regulation (managing your emotions), Motivation (internal drive), Empathy (understanding others' emotions), and Social skills (managing relationships).

Option A describes management functions.

Option C describes communication modes.

Option D describes different types of management skills.
