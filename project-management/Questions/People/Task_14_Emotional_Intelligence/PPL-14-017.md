# Question

What are the five components of emotional intelligence in Goleman's commonly used framework?

## Options

- **A**: Listening, Speaking, Writing, Reading, Presenting.
- **B**: Self-awareness, Self-regulation, Motivation, Empathy, Social skills.
- **C**: Planning, Organising, Leading, Controlling, Evaluating.
- **D**: Technical skill, Conceptual skill, Human skill, Political skill, Symbolic skill.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Goleman's five components are widely cited in PMI's leadership materials. (B) is the standard list. (A) is a model of communication modes. (C) is the classic functions of management. (D) is one taxonomy of management skill, not emotional intelligence.
