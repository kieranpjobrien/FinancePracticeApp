# Question

A team member's performance has declined after a death in their family. They haven't shared this with the team, but the PM knows about it. How should the PM handle this with emotional intelligence?

## Options

- **A**: Share the information with the team so they understand
- **B**: Respect privacy while offering private support and flexibility; adjust expectations quietly without broadcasting reasons
- **C**: Treat them exactly the same as before to be fair
- **D**: Immediately refer them to HR

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Emotional intelligence includes empathy (understanding their situation) and social skills (handling it appropriately). Respect their privacy - they chose not to share widely. Offer private support ("I'm here if you need anything"). Provide flexibility where possible. Adjust expectations without public explanation. This balances compassion with privacy.

Option A violates their privacy and trust.

Option C ignores human circumstances in a way that isn't supportive or ultimately effective.

Option D may be appropriate later but isn't the first response.
