# Question

A team is celebrating a successful delivery, but one team member is sitting apart from the group. The project manager knows this person did substantial work that has gone largely unrecognised. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Approach them quietly to acknowledge their specific contribution and check how they are feeling.
- **B**: Bring the team's attention to them publicly so their work is recognised in the moment.
- **C**: Allow them their space; some people prefer not to participate in celebrations.
- **D**: Send them a written note of appreciation later that day.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The withdrawal is a signal worth reading. (A) names the specific work, validates the person, and opens space for what is actually going on. (B) puts a person who is clearly avoiding attention on the spot. (C) misreads the cue as preference rather than hurt. (D) is well-meaning but delays the human moment that matters now.
