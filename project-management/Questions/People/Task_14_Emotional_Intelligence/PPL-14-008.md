# Question

Which component of Goleman's emotional intelligence model focuses on understanding the emotions and perspectives of others?

## Options

- **A**: Empathy
- **B**: Self-awareness
- **C**: Self-regulation
- **D**: Social skills

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Empathy is the component that perceives and understands what others feel and why. (A) is correct. (B) is awareness of one's own emotions. (C) is the management of one's own emotional reactions. (D) is the use of relationships to influence and collaborate, which builds on empathy but is distinct from it.
