# Question

Which component of emotional intelligence involves understanding other people's emotions and perspectives?

## Options

- **A**: Self-awareness
- **B**: Self-regulation
- **C**: Empathy
- **D**: Social skills

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Empathy is the ability to understand and share the feelings of others - to see situations from their perspective and recognise their emotional state. It's fundamental to effective leadership, collaboration, and conflict resolution.

The five components of emotional intelligence (Goleman model):
- **Self-awareness**: Understanding your own emotions
- **Self-regulation**: Managing your emotional responses
- **Motivation**: Internal drive to achieve
- **Empathy**: Understanding others' emotions
- **Social skills**: Managing relationships effectively
