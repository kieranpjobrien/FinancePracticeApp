# Question

A project is behind schedule and the sponsor is visibly angry during a status meeting. The PM feels defensive and wants to explain all the external factors that caused the delay. What is the emotionally intelligent response?

## Options

- **A**: Defend the team by explaining all the factors outside their control
- **B**: Acknowledge the sponsor's frustration, take responsibility, and focus on the path forward
- **C**: Wait silently until the sponsor calms down
- **D**: Redirect blame to specific team members who caused delays

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Emotional intelligence means managing your own defensive reaction (self-regulation) while acknowledging the other person's emotions (empathy). Taking responsibility (even if factors were external) and focusing on solutions demonstrates leadership and helps the sponsor move from anger to problem-solving.

Option A may come across as excuse-making when the sponsor wants solutions.

Option C is passive and may frustrate the sponsor further.

Option D damages trust and team morale.
