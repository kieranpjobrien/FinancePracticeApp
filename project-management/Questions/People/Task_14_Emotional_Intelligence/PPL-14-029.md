# Question

What is active listening in the context of emotional intelligence?

## Options

- **A**: Listening while doing other tasks
- **B**: Fully focusing on the speaker, understanding their message, and responding thoughtfully
- **C**: Listening only for facts and data
- **D**: Preparing your response while the other person is speaking

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Active listening means giving full attention, understanding both content and emotion, using non-verbal cues to show engagement, and responding in a way that demonstrates understanding. It builds trust and ensures clear communication.

Option A is distracted, not active, listening.

Option C ignores the emotional content that's important for understanding.

Option D means you're not fully listening - you're preparing to speak.
