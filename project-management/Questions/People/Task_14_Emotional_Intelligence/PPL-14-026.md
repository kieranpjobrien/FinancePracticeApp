# Question

A team member consistently volunteers for extra work but then complains about being overloaded. The PM suspects they have difficulty saying no. How should the PM address this using emotional intelligence?

## Options

- **A**: Stop giving them extra work
- **B**: Tell them to stop volunteering if they can't handle it
- **C**: Have a supportive conversation exploring their pattern and helping them develop boundaries
- **D**: Praise their work ethic and encourage them to continue

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. This requires empathy (understanding why they volunteer and complain) and development (helping them build healthy boundaries). A supportive conversation explores the pattern without judgment and helps them develop self-management skills for sustainable performance.

Option A addresses symptoms without developing capability.

Option B is judgmental and doesn't help them understand or change the pattern.

Option D reinforces an unsustainable pattern.
