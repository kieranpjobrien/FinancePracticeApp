# Question

A team member says they're "fine" when asked how they're doing, but their tone and body language suggest otherwise. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Accept their answer and move on
- **B**: Tell them they don't seem fine
- **C**: Create space for them to share if they choose, without pressure
- **D**: Inform HR that something may be wrong

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Emotional intelligence means reading beyond words while respecting boundaries. Creating space ("I'm here if you want to talk" or "How are things really going?") shows you noticed and care without forcing disclosure. People share when they're ready and trust the listener.

Option A ignores visible signs of distress.

Option B may feel confrontational and pressure them.

Option D is premature and may feel like a violation of trust.
