# Question

Which component of emotional intelligence involves the ability to understand the emotions of others?

## Options

- **A**: Self-awareness
- **B**: Self-regulation
- **C**: Empathy
- **D**: Motivation

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Empathy is the component of emotional intelligence focused on understanding and sharing the feelings of others. It allows leaders to perceive what team members are experiencing and respond appropriately, which is crucial for effective leadership and team management.

Option A (self-awareness) is understanding your own emotions, not others'.

Option B (self-regulation) is managing your own emotional responses.

Option D (motivation) relates to internal drive and persistence, not understanding others' emotions.
