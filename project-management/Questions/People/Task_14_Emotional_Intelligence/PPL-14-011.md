# Question

In Goleman's model, what is self-awareness?

## Options

- **A**: Recognising and understanding your own emotions, strengths, weaknesses, and how they affect others.
- **B**: Knowing how others perceive you in the workplace.
- **C**: Being confident in your decisions across most situations.
- **D**: Having a thorough command of the facts you need to do your job.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Self-awareness is the foundation of emotional intelligence: knowing what you feel, why, and how it shows up in your behaviour. (A) is the standard definition. (B) is reputation, which is related but external. (C) is confidence. (D) is knowledge, which has nothing to do with emotional awareness.
