# Question

A team has just successfully completed a major milestone. The PM notices the team seems flat rather than celebratory. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Move directly to planning the next phase
- **B**: Tell the team they should be celebrating
- **C**: Acknowledge the achievement and create space for the team to share how they're feeling
- **D**: Plan a celebration event and mandate attendance

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. The flat response may indicate exhaustion, concerns about the next phase, or a feeling that their effort wasn't valued. Creating space for sharing ("We achieved something significant - how is everyone feeling about it?") allows genuine responses and shows emotional attunement.

Option A misses an important moment for team acknowledgment.

Option B tells people how to feel rather than understanding how they do feel.

Option D mandates celebration, which doesn't feel genuine.
