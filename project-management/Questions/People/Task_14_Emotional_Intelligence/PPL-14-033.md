# Question

How does emotional intelligence relate to servant leadership?

## Options

- **A**: They are unrelated concepts
- **B**: Emotional intelligence enables servant leaders to understand and respond to team needs effectively
- **C**: Servant leadership replaces the need for emotional intelligence
- **D**: Emotional intelligence is only relevant for transactional leaders

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Servant leadership focuses on serving the team's needs, which requires understanding those needs - a core emotional intelligence capability. Empathy helps identify needs, self-awareness prevents the leader's needs from dominating, and social skills enable effective support.

Option A ignores the strong connection between the concepts.

Option C misunderstands that EI is foundational to servant leadership.

Option D misunderstands that EI is valuable for all leadership styles.
