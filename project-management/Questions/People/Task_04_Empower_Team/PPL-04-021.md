# Question

A PM has just inherited a team that is used to having every decision approved by the previous PM. The team is hesitant to take ownership when offered. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Start by delegating a small, low-risk decision area with clear boundaries and explicit support, then expand as confidence grows.
- **B**: Send an all-team note announcing that decisions are now the team's to make.
- **C**: Stop replying to approval requests so the team has to decide on its own.
- **D**: Bring in an agile coach to retrain the team on self-management.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Empowerment is built incrementally with safety, which is exactly what (A) does. (B) sets expectation without changing the underlying dynamic and can create anxiety. (C) is a sink-or-swim approach that erodes trust. (D) is a useful add-on later but does not start the change at the level the PM controls today.
