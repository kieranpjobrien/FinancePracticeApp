# Question

A senior business stakeholder wants to be more involved in project decisions but does not have the technical depth to engage meaningfully in design discussions. How should the PM empower his contribution?

## Options

- **A**: Brief him in detail on every technical decision so he can keep up.
- **B**: Limit his involvement to monthly steering committees where business decisions are made.
- **C**: Define decisions where his business judgement adds clear value and bring him into those, while keeping technical decisions with the team.
- **D**: Pair him with the lead engineer so he can shadow the technical work for a sprint.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Empowerment means matching involvement to where the stakeholder genuinely adds value, which is what (C) does. (A) overwhelms him with detail he cannot use and slows the team. (B) under-uses his business judgement and leaves him feeling distant from the project. (D) is a development activity and not a way to channel his contribution to decisions.
