# Question

You've empowered a team member to lead the daily standup. They're struggling with facilitation - meetings run long and dominant voices take over. What should you do?

## Options

- **A**: Take back facilitation of standups
- **B**: Let them continue struggling to learn from experience
- **C**: Provide coaching on facilitation techniques, observe and give feedback, and support them in improving while maintaining their leadership role
- **D**: Replace them with a more skilled facilitator

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Empowerment includes development support. Provide specific coaching: timeboxing techniques, how to redirect dominant speakers, how to draw out quiet members. Observe and give constructive feedback. Let them continue leading while building skills. This develops their capability rather than removing the growth opportunity.

Option A removes the empowerment and learning opportunity.

Option B provides insufficient support for skill development.

Option D doesn't develop the current team member and may limit future empowerment.
