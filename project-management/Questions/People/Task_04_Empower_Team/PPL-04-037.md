# Question

A developer has been empowered to facilitate the daily stand-up but the meeting routinely runs over time and one or two voices dominate. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Take back facilitation of the stand-up until the team's facilitator has more experience.
- **B**: Coach the facilitator on time-boxing and inviting quieter voices, sit in to give feedback, and let her keep facilitating.
- **C**: Move stand-ups to a written async format so facilitation is no longer required.
- **D**: Replace her with a more experienced facilitator from another team.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The right move is to develop the facilitator while she keeps the role, which is what (B) does. (A) revokes empowerment over a coachable issue. (C) changes the meeting format to avoid the development opportunity. (D) substitutes someone external instead of growing the person who currently owns the role.
