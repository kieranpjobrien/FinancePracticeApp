# Question

What is the PRIMARY benefit of empowering team members?

## Options

- **A**: It reduces the project manager's workload
- **B**: It creates ownership, engagement, and faster decision-making by those closest to the work
- **C**: It eliminates the need for project management
- **D**: It guarantees better outcomes

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Empowerment creates ownership - people who make decisions are invested in outcomes. It improves engagement because people feel valued and trusted. It enables faster decisions because those closest to the work can act without waiting for approval. These combine to improve team performance.

Option A (reduces PM workload) may be a side effect but isn't the primary purpose.

Option C (eliminates PM need) is incorrect - empowerment requires leadership to set boundaries and support.

Option D (guarantees outcomes) is too strong - empowerment enables better outcomes but doesn't guarantee them.
