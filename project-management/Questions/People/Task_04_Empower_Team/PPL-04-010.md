# Question

What is the PRIMARY benefit of empowering team members?

## Options

- **A**: It removes the need for a project manager on day-to-day decisions.
- **B**: It guarantees better technical decisions because they are made closer to the work.
- **C**: It improves engagement and decision speed by giving authority to the people closest to the work.
- **D**: It reduces the number of decisions the PM has to document.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Empowerment's main benefit is faster, better-informed decisions and stronger ownership from the people doing the work, which is what (C) describes. (A) overstates the effect; the PM still leads, sets boundaries, and integrates across the system. (B) is too strong, because empowerment improves likelihood, not certainty. (D) is a minor administrative side effect, not the primary benefit.
