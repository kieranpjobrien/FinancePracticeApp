# Question

A product owner has been empowered to prioritise the backlog, but stakeholders are constantly pressuring them to change priorities. The PO is struggling under conflicting demands. How should you support them?

## Options

- **A**: Take over prioritisation to relieve the pressure
- **B**: Help the PO develop stakeholder management skills, facilitate alignment discussions, and provide backing when they make difficult priority decisions
- **C**: Tell stakeholders to stop contacting the PO
- **D**: Let the PO handle it alone since they were empowered

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Empowerment includes ongoing support. Help the PO build capability: coach them on stakeholder management, conflict resolution, and saying no constructively. Facilitate: bring stakeholders together to align on priorities. Back them up: when they make difficult decisions, support them publicly. Empowerment doesn't mean abandonment.

Option A removes their empowerment rather than supporting it.

Option C is unrealistic and doesn't build the PO's capability.

Option D confuses empowerment with isolation.
