# Question

The product owner has been empowered to own backlog prioritisation but is buckling under conflicting pressure from three business stakeholders. He has started asking the PM to make the priority calls. How should the PM respond?

## Options

- **A**: Make the priority calls on behalf of the PO for now and transition them back to him later.
- **B**: Bring the stakeholders together to align on value and coach the PO on facilitating the conflict.
- **C**: Ask the sponsor to arbitrate between the three stakeholders so the pressure on the PO is removed.
- **D**: Let the PO work through the pressure alone, since empowerment fundamentally means ownership of such calls.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The PM should support the PO's authority by removing the dysfunction driving the pressure and building his capability to handle it, which is what (B) does. (A) quietly strips empowerment under the banner of helping. (C) escalates before the PM has worked the issue and signals the PO cannot handle his role. (D) confuses empowerment with abandonment; supported empowerment is what develops capability.
