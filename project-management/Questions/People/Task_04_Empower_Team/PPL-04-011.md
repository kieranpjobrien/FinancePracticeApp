# Question

A project manager wants to empower the team to make more decisions independently. Currently, all decisions flow through the PM. What is the BEST first step?

## Options

- **A**: Immediately delegate all decisions to the team
- **B**: Define clear decision boundaries - what decisions the team can make independently versus what requires PM involvement
- **C**: Continue making all decisions to maintain control
- **D**: Let the team decide which decisions they want to own

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Effective empowerment requires clear boundaries. The PM should define what types of decisions the team can make independently, what requires consultation, and what needs PM approval. This gives the team clear authority while managing risk. Decision boundaries can expand over time as trust builds.

Option A creates confusion and risk - teams need clarity on authority limits.

Option C maintains the status quo without progress toward empowerment.

Option D puts the team in an awkward position without guidance on appropriate scope.
