# Question

A team member wants to try a new technology that could benefit the project but carries some risk. The technology is not standard in your organisation. How should you handle this empowerment opportunity?

## Options

- **A**: Reject the idea since it's non-standard
- **B**: Let them use it without any oversight
- **C**: Discuss the risks and benefits together, agree on a bounded experiment with clear success criteria, and support them in learning and evaluating the technology
- **D**: Escalate to architecture review board and wait for their decision

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Innovation requires empowerment with appropriate risk management. Discuss openly: understand their reasoning, explore risks together. Create a bounded experiment: try it in a limited scope with clear success criteria. Support the learning: even if it fails, the knowledge gained has value. This encourages innovation while managing risk appropriately.

Option A stifles innovation and doesn't trust the team member's judgment.

Option B is empowerment without appropriate risk consideration.

Option D may be appropriate for significant decisions but is excessive for bounded experiments.
