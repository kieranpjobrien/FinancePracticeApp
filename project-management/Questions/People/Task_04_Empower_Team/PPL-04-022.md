# Question

What does delegation of authority to team members require from the project manager?

## Options

- **A**: Complete removal of oversight
- **B**: Clear boundaries, appropriate support, and accountability while trusting team members to perform
- **C**: Detailed instruction for every task
- **D**: Regular check-ins every hour

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Effective delegation requires defining what decisions team members can make autonomously (boundaries), providing resources and guidance they need (support), maintaining appropriate accountability mechanisms, and trusting them to perform within those parameters. It's neither abandonment nor micromanagement.

Option A removes necessary accountability and support.

Option C is micromanagement, the opposite of empowerment.

Option D suggests excessive oversight that undermines trust.
