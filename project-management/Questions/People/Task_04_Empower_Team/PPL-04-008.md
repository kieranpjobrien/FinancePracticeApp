# Question

A junior developer pitches an unconventional approach to a feature. The PM is unsure it will work but the developer is enthusiastic and the feature is not on the critical path. What action BEST demonstrates empowerment?

## Options

- **A**: Decline the proposal and ask her to use the standard approach so risk stays contained.
- **B**: Take over the design with her to make sure the approach actually delivers.
- **C**: Agree a time-boxed prototype with explicit success criteria and let her run it.
- **D**: Defer the proposal to the technical lead to evaluate before any work begins.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Empowerment with guardrails means letting people try ideas inside a defined risk envelope, which is what (C) does. (A) shuts down initiative without testing the idea. (B) co-opts ownership and removes the development opportunity. (D) routes the decision around the developer and treats her authority as conditional on someone else's sign-off.
