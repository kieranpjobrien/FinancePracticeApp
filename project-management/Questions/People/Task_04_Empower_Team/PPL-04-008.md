# Question

A team member proposes an innovative solution that differs from the standard approach. The PM is uncertain if it will work but the team member is enthusiastic. What approach best demonstrates empowerment?

## Options

- **A**: Reject the proposal to avoid risk
- **B**: Take over the solution design to ensure it works
- **C**: Allow the team member to prototype and test the idea within defined parameters
- **D**: Escalate to management for decision

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Empowerment means giving people authority to make decisions and take action within boundaries. Allowing a prototype with defined parameters (time, resources, success criteria) lets the team member explore their idea while managing risk. This builds ownership, develops skills, and may yield innovation.

Option A (reject) discourages initiative and may lose a valuable idea.

Option B (take over) undermines the team member's ownership and development.

Option D (escalate) delays unnecessarily and doesn't develop the team member's judgment.
