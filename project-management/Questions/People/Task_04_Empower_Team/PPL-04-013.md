# Question

What is the relationship between empowerment and accountability on a project team?

## Options

- **A**: Authority to decide must be paired with responsibility for the resulting outcomes.
- **B**: Empowerment removes accountability because team members are now self-directing.
- **C**: Accountability sits with the project manager; empowerment is purely about decision speed.
- **D**: They are independent: a team can be empowered without being accountable.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Real empowerment couples authority and accountability so that decisions are taken seriously and learning is connected to ownership. (B) removes the consequence side and produces careless decisions. (C) keeps accountability centralised, which contradicts empowerment in practice. (D) describes a model that produces low-quality decisions because the deciders are insulated from the outcomes.
