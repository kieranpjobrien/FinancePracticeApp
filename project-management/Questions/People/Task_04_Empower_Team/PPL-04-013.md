# Question

What is the relationship between empowerment and accountability?

## Options

- **A**: Empowerment removes accountability
- **B**: Empowerment and accountability are unrelated concepts
- **C**: Empowerment must be paired with accountability - authority to decide comes with responsibility for outcomes
- **D**: Accountability should precede empowerment by several months

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. True empowerment includes both authority and accountability. When team members have decision-making power, they should also own the outcomes of those decisions. This creates a healthy dynamic where people take decisions seriously because they're responsible for results. Empowerment without accountability leads to careless decisions.

Option A creates authority without responsibility - a recipe for poor decisions.

Option B misses the essential connection between these concepts.

Option D creates an artificial delay without clear rationale.
