# Question

A business stakeholder routinely emails the project manager with small requests that are clearly within the team's day-to-day authority. The PM could answer most of them in two minutes. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Answer each email to keep the stakeholder happy and avoid friction.
- **B**: Forward the emails to the team without comment so they pick them up.
- **C**: Set up an auto-reply pointing the stakeholder to the team's backlog tool.
- **D**: Reply to introduce the team member who owns the area, copy the team, and ask the stakeholder to use that channel going forward.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Empowerment includes giving stakeholders direct routes to the people who own the work, and (D) does that while preserving the relationship. (A) cements the PM as the only contact and undermines the team's standing. (B) confuses the team about whether to act and leaves the stakeholder uncertain who owns the response. (C) deflects without modelling the new working pattern and reads as impersonal.
