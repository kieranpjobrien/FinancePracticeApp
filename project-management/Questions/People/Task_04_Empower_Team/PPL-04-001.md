# Question

A developer asks the project manager to approve a small refactor inside her own component that has no impact on other teams. What response BEST demonstrates empowerment?

## Options

- **A**: Approve the refactor on the spot so she is unblocked.
- **B**: Schedule a team discussion at the next planning session to agree the refactor.
- **C**: Ask her to raise a change request so it is recorded in the change log.
- **D**: Confirm she has authority to make this kind of decision herself and ask her to share the change in the next stand-up.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Empowerment is about clarifying the authority the team member already has, not about the PM granting fast approvals, and (D) does that. (A) is faster than process but still positions the PM as gatekeeper. (B) creates unnecessary group decision-making for a contained change and slows initiative. (C) drags a low-impact within-component decision into formal change control, which is reserved for changes affecting baselines.
