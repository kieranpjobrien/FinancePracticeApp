# Question

What is the primary goal of empowering team members?

## Options

- **A**: To reduce the project manager's workload
- **B**: To enable team members to take ownership, make decisions, and contribute their best work
- **C**: To shift blame away from leadership when things go wrong
- **D**: To avoid having to manage people

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Empowerment enables team members to take ownership of their work, make appropriate decisions independently, and fully contribute their skills and creativity. This increases engagement, speeds decision-making, and produces better outcomes than centralised control.

Option A is a side effect, not the primary goal.

Option C misunderstands empowerment as abdication of responsibility.

Option D confuses empowerment with abandonment of leadership duties.
