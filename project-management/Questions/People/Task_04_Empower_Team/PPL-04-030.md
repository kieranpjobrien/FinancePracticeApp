# Question

What is the key difference between delegation and empowerment?

## Options

- **A**: Delegation hands off a task; empowerment hands off authority to decide how the work is done within agreed boundaries.
- **B**: Delegation is permanent; empowerment is temporary.
- **C**: Delegation is for managers; empowerment is for executive leaders.
- **D**: They are the same concept and the terms are interchangeable.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Delegation is about handing off a task or responsibility; empowerment goes further by handing off the authority to decide how to achieve it, which is what (A) captures. (B) reverses typical practice and is incorrect. (C) ties the concepts to organisational level rather than to authority. (D) collapses an important distinction.
