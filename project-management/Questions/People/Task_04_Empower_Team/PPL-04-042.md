# Question

What is the relationship between transparency and empowerment?

## Options

- **A**: Transparency undermines empowerment by exposing mistakes
- **B**: Transparency enables empowerment by ensuring people have the information they need to make good decisions
- **C**: Transparency and empowerment are unrelated concepts
- **D**: Transparency should be limited to protect empowered decision-makers

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Empowerment requires information. People cannot make good decisions without visibility into relevant context: project status, constraints, stakeholder needs, strategic direction. Transparency provides this information, enabling empowered decision-making. Without transparency, empowerment becomes guessing in the dark.

Option A views transparency negatively when it's actually enabling.

Option C misses the enabling relationship between the concepts.

Option D limits the information needed for good empowered decisions.
