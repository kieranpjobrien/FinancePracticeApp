# Question

An empowered team makes a decision that leads to a public-facing bug affecting customers. Stakeholders are asking who is responsible. How should the PM handle accountability?

## Options

- **A**: Blame the team member who made the decision
- **B**: Accept accountability publicly while privately reviewing what happened with the team to improve future decisions
- **C**: Claim the PM made the decision to protect the team
- **D**: Point out that the team was empowered and it's their problem

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. The PM remains accountable for project outcomes regardless of delegation. Accept public accountability ("This happened on my watch"). Privately, conduct a blameless review with the team - what led to the decision? What can be improved? This maintains trust and empowerment while ensuring learning. Blaming the team undermines empowerment.

Option A destroys psychological safety and empowerment.

Option C is dishonest.

Option D abandons accountability that comes with leadership.
