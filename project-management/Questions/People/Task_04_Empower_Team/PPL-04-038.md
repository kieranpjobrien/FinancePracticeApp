# Question

What is a key barrier to empowerment that project managers must overcome in themselves?

## Options

- **A**: Lack of budget
- **B**: The desire to maintain control and the discomfort of letting others make decisions
- **C**: Team members who don't want empowerment
- **D**: Organisational regulations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. A primary barrier to empowerment is the PM's own discomfort with giving up control. It can feel risky to let others make decisions, especially when you're accountable for outcomes. Overcoming this requires trust in the team, acceptance that others may do things differently, and focus on outcomes rather than methods.

Option A is an external constraint, not a personal barrier.

Option C exists but isn't the barrier within the PM themselves.

Option D is an external constraint that may require navigation but isn't about the PM's mindset.
