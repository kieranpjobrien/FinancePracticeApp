# Question

A team has clear authority to make day-to-day decisions but keeps escalating routine choices to the PM. The PM believes the issue is confidence rather than authority. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Make the decisions quickly so the team is not blocked.
- **B**: Reduce the team's empowerment until it matches their current comfort level.
- **C**: Remove escalation as an option so the team has to decide on its own.
- **D**: Push the choices back with coaching questions such as "what would you recommend and why?"

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Building decision confidence requires practice with support, which is what coaching questions provide, and (D) does that. (A) reinforces the dependency and signals that escalation is the easy path. (B) shrinks empowerment to fit the symptom rather than developing the underlying capability. (C) removes the safety net abruptly and can damage trust.
