# Question

A junior analyst has identified a process improvement worth presenting to senior stakeholders but is nervous about presenting at that level. How should the PM empower her contribution?

## Options

- **A**: Present the idea on her behalf and credit her contribution.
- **B**: Help her prepare and rehearse, attend the meeting in support, and let her present and own the idea.
- **C**: Suggest she pitch it to her line manager first and let it filter up through that channel.
- **D**: Add her idea to the next steering committee pre-read so she does not have to present at all.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Empowerment with support means helping her stretch into the room while she keeps ownership, which is what (B) does. (A) takes away her growth opportunity and her visibility. (C) routes the idea around her and dilutes her authorship. (D) removes the stretch entirely and the development moment with it.
