# Question

A junior team member has an innovative idea but is hesitant to propose it to senior stakeholders. How can you empower them to contribute?

## Options

- **A**: Present the idea yourself and give them credit
- **B**: Tell them to present it without any support
- **C**: Help them prepare the presentation, offer to be there for support, and ensure they get appropriate visibility for their contribution
- **D**: Discourage them since junior members shouldn't propose ideas to seniors

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Empowerment includes helping team members stretch into new situations. Help them prepare: refine the idea, anticipate questions, practice the presentation. Offer support: your presence can provide confidence without taking over. Ensure visibility: they should present and receive credit. This builds their capability and confidence for future contributions.

Option A empowers them passively but doesn't develop their skills.

Option B sets them up to struggle unnecessarily.

Option D suppresses valuable contributions based on hierarchy.
