# Question

The PM has delegated authority for a workstream to a senior analyst. The analyst delivers well but pings the PM on Slack before almost every decision asking "are you ok with this?" How should the PM respond?

## Options

- **A**: Reply quickly each time so the analyst is unblocked.
- **B**: Have a one-on-one to surface what is driving the check-ins and explicitly reaffirm her authority within the workstream.
- **C**: Reduce the scope of the delegation since she is not yet operating independently.
- **D**: Tell her to stop checking in and only escalate true blockers.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Pre-decision check-ins usually signal a confidence or boundary issue, and (B) addresses both by exploring the cause and reinforcing authority. (A) confirms the dependency and the pattern continues. (C) treats a coaching opportunity as a delegation failure and shrinks her remit. (D) is correct in spirit but a blunt instruction without addressing the underlying cause and may push real concerns underground.
