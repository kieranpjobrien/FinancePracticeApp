# Question

Which of the following BEST describes effective mentoring?

## Options

- **A**: Telling the mentee what decision to make so they avoid mistakes.
- **B**: Restricting feedback to constructive criticism so the mentee improves.
- **C**: Sharing experience, asking guiding questions, and supporting the mentee's own decisions.
- **D**: Operating only within a formal mentoring program approved by the organisation.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Mentoring develops the mentee's own judgement; the mentor is a guide, not a substitute decision-maker. (C) captures this. (A) creates dependency rather than capability. (B) ignores the role of positive reinforcement and recognition in growth. (D) is wrong because effective mentoring is often informal and emergent.
