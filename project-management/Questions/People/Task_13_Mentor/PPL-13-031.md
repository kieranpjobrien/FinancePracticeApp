# Question

What is a benefit of reverse mentoring?

## Options

- **A**: It's faster than traditional mentoring
- **B**: It allows junior team members to share fresh perspectives and skills with senior staff
- **C**: It eliminates the need for traditional mentoring
- **D**: It reduces the workload for senior staff

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Reverse mentoring pairs junior and senior people with the junior person as mentor in specific areas. This is valuable for skills where younger generations may have more current knowledge (technology, social media, new methodologies) and creates mutual learning and connection across levels.

Option A - timing isn't the distinguishing feature.

Option C - reverse mentoring complements, not replaces, traditional mentoring.

Option D - it's about learning, not workload.
