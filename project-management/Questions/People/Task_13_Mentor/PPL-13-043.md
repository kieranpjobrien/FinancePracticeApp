# Question

What role does feedback play in an effective mentoring relationship?

## Options

- **A**: Feedback should flow only from the mentor to the mentee.
- **B**: Feedback should be predominantly positive to keep the relationship strong.
- **C**: Feedback should be avoided to keep the relationship safe and supportive.
- **D**: Constructive feedback should flow in both directions to improve the relationship and the work.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Bidirectional, constructive feedback is the engine of a healthy mentoring relationship. (D) captures this. (A) ignores that the mentor needs information to be useful too. (B) reduces feedback to flattery. (C) eliminates the very mechanism through which growth happens.
