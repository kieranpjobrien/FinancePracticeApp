# Question

What role does feedback play in effective mentoring?

## Options

- **A**: Feedback should only flow from mentor to mentee
- **B**: Feedback should be primarily positive to maintain the relationship
- **C**: Constructive feedback should flow both ways to support development
- **D**: Feedback should be avoided to maintain a supportive relationship

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Bidirectional feedback enhances the mentoring relationship. The mentor provides feedback to support the mentee's development. The mentee provides feedback to help the mentor be more effective. Both giving and receiving feedback constructively is part of professional development.

Option A misses the value of mentee feedback on the mentoring approach.

Option B avoids developmental feedback that is essential for growth.

Option D prevents the learning that feedback enables.
