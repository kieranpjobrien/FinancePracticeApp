# Question

What should a mentor focus on to help a mentee develop project management skills?

## Options

- **A**: Providing a complete library of templates for every project situation.
- **B**: Sharing experience, offering guidance, and creating opportunities to learn through practice.
- **C**: Regularly testing the mentee's knowledge of PMI processes and terminology.
- **D**: Directing the mentee's career path decisions on their behalf.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Skill is built through a combination of context, frameworks, and supported practice. (B) captures all three. (A) provides artefacts without understanding. (C) tests rather than develops. (D) confuses mentorship with control over the mentee's career; the mentee owns those choices.
