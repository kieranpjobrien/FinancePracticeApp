# Question

What should a mentor focus on to help a mentee develop project management skills?

## Options

- **A**: Providing detailed templates for all situations
- **B**: Sharing experiences, providing guidance, and creating opportunities to learn through practice
- **C**: Testing their knowledge regularly
- **D**: Directing their career path decisions

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Effective development combines experience sharing (lessons learned, how to think about situations), guidance (frameworks, principles), and practice opportunities (stretch assignments, supported autonomy). This develops both knowledge and capability.

Option A provides tools without understanding.

Option C tests but doesn't develop.

Option D oversteps - the mentee should direct their own career with the mentor's input.
