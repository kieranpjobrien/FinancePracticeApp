# Question

A project manager will hand over their project to a successor in three months and has been asked to mentor that successor. What should the focus of the mentoring be?

## Options

- **A**: Produce thorough handover documentation the successor can refer to from day one.
- **B**: Have the successor shadow meetings to understand how the project currently runs.
- **C**: Combine knowledge transfer with progressively giving the successor activities to lead with the project manager's support.
- **D**: Save the bulk of the handover for the final two weeks before transition.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Succession requires both knowledge and confidence, and confidence only comes from doing. (C) gives the incoming PM a runway of supported reps. (A) provides reference but no practice. (B) is too passive to build the skill of leading the project. (D) leaves no time for the inevitable questions that arise after the successor starts trying.
