# Question

A project manager is asked to mentor a stakeholder who will be taking over the PM role when the current PM moves to a new project. The transition is in three months. What should be the PM's mentoring focus?

## Options

- **A**: Document everything so the new PM can reference it
- **B**: Let the incoming PM shadow meetings to observe how things work
- **C**: Combine knowledge transfer with progressive responsibility, allowing the incoming PM to lead activities with support
- **D**: Wait until the last week for a comprehensive handover

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Effective succession mentoring combines knowledge transfer (context, relationships, history) with experiential learning (progressively taking on responsibilities). Leading activities with support builds confidence and capability before the full handover. Three months allows meaningful development.

Option A provides information but not experience.

Option B is passive and may not build confidence to lead.

Option D doesn't allow time for learning and adjustment.
