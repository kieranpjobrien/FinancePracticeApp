# Question

A project manager is asked to mentor a marketing stakeholder on project management basics so they can collaborate better with delivery teams. The stakeholder has limited time. What approach is BEST?

## Options

- **A**: Run a condensed end-to-end overview of the PMBOK process groups for them.
- **B**: Focus on the project management aspects most relevant to their role with practical, immediately applicable content.
- **C**: Recommend they enrol in a CAPM course as a more rigorous foundation.
- **D**: Decline because cross-functional mentoring sits outside the project manager role.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Effective mentoring meets people where they are and pays back quickly. (B) tailors content to the stakeholder's actual job (writing requirements, reading status reports, working to milestones) and respects their time. (A) overloads them with material they will not use. (C) is more rigorous but slower and may never happen. (D) declines a useful cross-functional opportunity.
