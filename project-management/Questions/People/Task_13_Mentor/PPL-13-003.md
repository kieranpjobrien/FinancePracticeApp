# Question

A project sponsor new to agile keeps asking for detailed Gantt charts and firm dates for work months ahead. How should the project manager mentor them?

## Options

- **A**: Produce the Gantt charts they request to keep the sponsor satisfied.
- **B**: Decline the request and explain that agile teams do not produce that artefact.
- **C**: Recommend the sponsor enrol in an agile certification course before continuing the project.
- **D**: Show them how the roadmap and release plan deliver the same visibility their underlying need requires.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The sponsor's underlying need (visibility and forecast) is legitimate; mentoring means meeting that need in an agile-appropriate way. (D) connects the sponsor's question to the artefacts agile actually uses. (A) creates false precision and reinforces the misframe. (B) is confrontational and does not help the sponsor adapt. (C) outsources the project manager's stakeholder development responsibility.
