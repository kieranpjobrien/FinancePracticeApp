# Question

An agile coach mentoring several Scrum Masters is asked for advice about a team conflict by one Scrum Master. The Scrum Master clearly already has a good idea of what to do; they want validation. What is the BEST response?

## Options

- **A**: Use questions to help them articulate their own approach and build confidence in it.
- **B**: Give them the validation they are asking for so they can move on.
- **C**: Suggest a different approach to demonstrate the coach's perspective.
- **D**: Note that they do not need mentoring on this question and conclude the session.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

When a mentee already has the answer, the mentor's value is helping them trust their own judgement, not adding new content. (A) does that. (B) provides external approval that does not build internal confidence. (C) undermines a sound idea unnecessarily. (D) dismisses the coaching moment that is actually present.
