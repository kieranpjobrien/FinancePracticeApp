# Question

A new product owner is overwhelmed by stakeholder demands and struggles to push back on requests. The Scrum Master is mentoring them. What approach would be MOST effective?

## Options

- **A**: Coach them through a prioritisation framework and rehearse difficult stakeholder conversations.
- **B**: Field stakeholder conversations on their behalf until they feel ready to take them on.
- **C**: Suggest they push every difficult request up to the steering committee for resolution.
- **D**: Recommend a course on stakeholder management to address the root capability gap.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The product owner needs both a tool (frameworks for saying no with reasoning) and a skill (handling pushback in real time). (A) builds both through guided practice. (B) creates dependence and delays the real learning. (C) erodes the product owner's authority. (D) is a slower, more abstract route while the immediate problem is happening now.
