# Question

A Scrum Master is mentoring a new product owner on backlog management. The product owner is overwhelmed by stakeholder demands and struggles to say no. What mentoring approach would be MOST effective?

## Options

- **A**: Tell the product owner which requests to decline
- **B**: Handle stakeholder conversations for them until they're more confident
- **C**: Help them develop frameworks for prioritisation and practice having difficult stakeholder conversations
- **D**: Suggest they escalate all difficult decisions to management

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Building capability means giving tools (prioritisation frameworks) and skills (stakeholder conversations). Practice - perhaps role-playing difficult conversations or working through prioritisation decisions together - builds confidence and capability for independent action.

Option A makes the decision for them rather than building their capability.

Option B doesn't build their skills.

Option D avoids the development opportunity entirely.
