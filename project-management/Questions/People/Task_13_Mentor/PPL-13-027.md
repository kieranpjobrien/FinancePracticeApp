# Question

In a project context, who can appropriately benefit from mentoring by the project manager?

## Options

- **A**: Junior team members only, since they need development the most.
- **B**: Only people who report directly to the project manager.
- **C**: Only individuals with a formal mentoring agreement in place.
- **D**: Team members, stakeholders, and even peer project managers, depending on the need.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Mentoring can serve anyone with a development need, not only direct reports or junior staff. (D) reflects the PMI view that the project manager's mentoring contribution can extend across the team and stakeholder community. (A) is unduly narrow. (B) excludes the many useful mentoring relationships that cross reporting lines. (C) ignores the value of informal mentoring.
