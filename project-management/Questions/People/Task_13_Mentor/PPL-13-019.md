# Question

Why is mentoring a meaningful responsibility for project managers in PMI's view?

## Options

- **A**: It is required for renewing the PMP credential.
- **B**: It develops team capability, supports succession, and transfers knowledge.
- **C**: It reduces the project manager's workload over the project's life.
- **D**: It is the primary mechanism for resolving interpersonal conflict on a team.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PMI views mentoring as a leadership behaviour that strengthens both individuals and the organisation. (B) captures the developmental, succession, and knowledge-transfer benefits. (A) is incorrect; PDU rules apply, but mentoring itself is not a PMP renewal requirement. (C) confuses long-term capability building with short-term workload relief. (D) conflates mentoring with conflict management, which is a separate competency.
