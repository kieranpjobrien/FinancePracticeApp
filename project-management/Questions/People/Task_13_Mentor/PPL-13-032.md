# Question

A mentee received critical feedback in a project review and is now discouraged and questioning their abilities. The project manager is mentoring them. What is the BEST response?

## Options

- **A**: Reassure them the feedback was overstated and they should not take it to heart.
- **B**: Walk through the feedback together, separate the actionable points, and reaffirm the mentee's strengths.
- **C**: Encourage them to put the review behind them quickly and focus on the next sprint.
- **D**: Help them draft a response challenging the most damaging items in the review.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Setbacks are mentoring opportunities. (B) helps the mentee process the feedback objectively, identify what is genuinely actionable, and rebuild confidence on a realistic foundation. (A) softens the truth and prevents learning. (C) suppresses an emotional reaction without working through it. (D) channels the energy into defensiveness instead of growth.
