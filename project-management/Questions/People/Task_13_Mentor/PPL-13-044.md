# Question

A mentee asks the project manager for advice on an ethical dilemma: they have observed a colleague falsifying time sheets and are unsure what to do. What is the BEST mentoring response?

## Options

- **A**: Tell the mentee exactly what action to take in this situation.
- **B**: Stay neutral; the decision is the mentee's to make alone.
- **C**: Help the mentee work through the facts, options, consequences, and their own values.
- **D**: Report the conduct to HR yourself, since you now have knowledge of it.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Ethical dilemmas are mentoring gold; they grow professional judgement. (C) walks the mentee through structured reasoning rather than handing them an answer. (A) bypasses development. (B) leaves them without the support they sought. (D) jumps over the mentee, removes their agency, and may also be premature without their account of what they saw.
