# Question

An experienced project manager wants to start mentoring others but has never done so formally. What is the BEST first step?

## Options

- **A**: Approach one or two people who would clearly benefit and offer regular conversations.
- **B**: Wait until someone explicitly asks for mentoring so the relationship is mentee-driven.
- **C**: Send a broad note offering availability and accept all responses that come back.
- **D**: Complete a mentoring certification before taking on any mentees.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The best way to learn to mentor is to start small with explicit intent. (A) provides a safe scope to develop the practice while delivering value. (B) is reactive and may miss willing mentees. (C) risks attracting more demand than the project manager can sustain. (D) treats certification as a prerequisite for an act of service that requires only experience and commitment.
