# Question

A stakeholder has been raising project concerns directly with the executive sponsor without first surfacing them with the PM. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Reach out to the stakeholder, listen, address what is addressable, and rebuild the relationship.
- **B**: Confront the stakeholder directly about going around the PM and breaching the reporting line.
- **C**: Ignore it; stakeholders are free to escalate concerns to the sponsor as they see fit.
- **D**: Brief the sponsor and request that the sponsor redirect the stakeholder back to the PM.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The pattern signals a relationship and trust gap that the PM needs to close directly; PMI expects the PM to engage at the source first. (B) is confrontational and tends to reinforce the avoidance behaviour. (C) ignores a clear warning signal about stakeholder relations. (D) escalates to the sponsor without the PM having spoken to the stakeholder, and asks the sponsor to do the PM's relationship work.
