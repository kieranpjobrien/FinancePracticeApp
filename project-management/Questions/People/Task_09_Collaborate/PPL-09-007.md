# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of a stakeholder collaboration approach?

## Options

- **A**: To document who is responsible for what tasks
- **B**: To create shared ownership and commitment by involving stakeholders appropriately in project activities
- **C**: To reduce the number of stakeholders involved in the project
- **D**: To assign blame when things go wrong

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Collaboration creates shared ownership - stakeholders who participate in decisions and activities feel invested in outcomes. This leads to better decisions (diverse input), stronger commitment (people support what they help create), and smoother implementation (early buy-in reduces resistance).

Option A describes RACI or responsibility assignment, not collaboration approach.

Option C contradicts collaboration - the goal is appropriate involvement, not exclusion.

Option D is antithetical to collaboration, which builds shared accountability rather than blame.
