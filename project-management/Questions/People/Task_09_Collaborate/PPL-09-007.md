# Question

What is the primary purpose of a stakeholder collaboration approach?

## Options

- **A**: To document which stakeholder is responsible for which project deliverables.
- **B**: To reduce the number of stakeholders who have to be kept informed of progress.
- **C**: To create shared ownership and commitment through appropriate stakeholder involvement.
- **D**: To provide audit evidence that every stakeholder was formally consulted.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Collaboration is fundamentally about shared ownership — decisions stakeholders help shape they are more likely to commit to and defend. (A) describes responsibility assignment (RACI), which is a related but distinct tool. (B) contradicts the idea of appropriate engagement. (D) describes compliance documentation, which may be a by-product but is not the purpose of the approach.
