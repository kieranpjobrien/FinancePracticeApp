# Question

During a sprint review, stakeholders provide extensive feedback that would require significant backlog changes. The Product Owner seems overwhelmed. How should the Scrum Master support effective stakeholder collaboration?

## Options

- **A**: Limit stakeholder feedback to protect the PO
- **B**: Tell stakeholders their feedback will be ignored
- **C**: Help capture and organise feedback, facilitate prioritisation discussion, and support the PO in processing input into actionable backlog items
- **D**: Implement all feedback immediately

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Stakeholder feedback is valuable—the goal is managing it effectively, not suppressing it. Helping capture feedback ensures nothing is lost. Facilitating prioritisation helps stakeholders understand trade-offs. Supporting the PO in translating feedback to backlog items maintains the proper product ownership role while ensuring input is processed.

Option A loses valuable stakeholder input.

Option B damages stakeholder relationships.

Option D bypasses proper backlog management.
