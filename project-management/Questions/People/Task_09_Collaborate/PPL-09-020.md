# Question

The project's stakeholders span four time zones and no single time slot works for all of them. How should the PM structure stakeholder collaboration?

## Options

- **A**: Always schedule the meeting at the most senior stakeholder's local working hours.
- **B**: Move all stakeholder communication to email-only to remove the scheduling problem.
- **C**: Combine asynchronous channels (recorded updates, shared documents) with rotating synchronous meeting times so the inconvenience is shared and decisions are documented.
- **D**: Hold one meeting time and require attendance regardless of local hour, citing project priority.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

PMI distributed-team guidance is to balance async and sync deliberately and to share the burden of inconvenient hours rather than concentrate it. (A) systematically disadvantages stakeholders in other regions. (B) loses real-time decision making and slows everything down. (D) imposes a single hour on people who will quietly disengage from a project that does not respect them.
