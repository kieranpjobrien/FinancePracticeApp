# Question

What is a key principle of effective stakeholder collaboration?

## Options

- **A**: Treat all stakeholders the same regardless of their needs
- **B**: Limit information sharing to protect the project
- **C**: Engage stakeholders early and often, seeking their input and keeping them informed
- **D**: Only involve stakeholders when problems arise

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Effective collaboration requires ongoing engagement. Early involvement allows stakeholders to shape requirements and feel ownership. Regular communication keeps them informed and prevents surprises. Seeking input values their expertise and builds commitment. This is proactive relationship management.

Option A ignores that different stakeholders have different needs and interests.

Option B limits information sharing, which reduces trust and creates surprises.

Option D is reactive rather than proactive collaboration.
