# Question

Which of the following best describes a core principle of effective stakeholder collaboration?

## Options

- **A**: Engage stakeholders early and regularly, seek their input, and keep them informed.
- **B**: Communicate with stakeholders only when a decision requires their approval.
- **C**: Route all stakeholder communication through the sponsor to stay consistent.
- **D**: Give every stakeholder the same level of engagement to be fair.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PMI's stakeholder engagement guidance is proactive: engage early, communicate regularly, and invite input so commitment is built before it is needed. (B) is reactive and surfaces issues late. (C) is a single-channel funnel that slows information and creates bottlenecks. (D) treats engagement as equality rather than tailoring, which wastes attention on low-influence stakeholders and starves high-influence ones.
