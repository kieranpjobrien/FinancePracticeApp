# Question

A project requires input from a stakeholder who is extremely busy and difficult to schedule. Previous attempts to get their input have failed, causing project delays. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Proceed without their input
- **B**: Escalate to their manager to demand their time
- **C**: Adapt to their constraints - offer async options, very short focused sessions, or delegation to someone they trust
- **D**: Wait indefinitely for their availability

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Collaboration requires meeting stakeholders where they are. Adapt to their constraints: Can they review and comment on a document instead of attending a meeting? Can a 15-minute focused call replace an hour-long session? Can they delegate to someone who has their authority? Flexibility improves collaboration.

Option A loses important input and may cause problems later.

Option B damages relationships and may not succeed.

Option D accepts indefinite delays.
