# Question

The PM has discovered that a stakeholder forwarded a confidential project document to colleagues outside the project's authorised distribution list. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Restrict the stakeholder's access to all project information immediately as a containment measure.
- **B**: Raise the breach in the next stakeholder forum so the rest of the group sees it being addressed.
- **C**: Note the breach and continue with the existing communication arrangements.
- **D**: Speak with the stakeholder privately to understand whether the disclosure was inadvertent or intentional, reinforce the confidentiality requirement, and escalate only if the situation warrants it.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PMI's first move on a confidentiality issue is a private, fact-finding conversation that preserves dignity, surfaces intent, and reinforces expectations — escalation follows if needed. (A) is disproportionate for what may be an inadvertent breach and damages a working relationship. (B) is a public callout that embarrasses without producing better outcomes. (C) ignores a legitimate governance concern.
