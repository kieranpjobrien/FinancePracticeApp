# Question

What characterises a "leading" stakeholder engagement level?

## Options

- **A**: The stakeholder is aware of the project but not involved
- **B**: The stakeholder is resistant to the project
- **C**: The stakeholder actively works to advance the project and its success
- **D**: The stakeholder is neutral about the project

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. The stakeholder engagement assessment matrix includes levels: Unaware, Resistant, Neutral, Supportive, and Leading. "Leading" stakeholders are actively engaged in ensuring project success—they advocate for the project, remove barriers, and champion the initiative. This is the highest positive engagement level.

Option A describes "Aware" level.

Option B describes "Resistant" level.

Option D describes "Neutral" level.
