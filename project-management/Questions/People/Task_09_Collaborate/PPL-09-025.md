# Question

In the stakeholder engagement assessment matrix, which level describes a stakeholder who actively works to advance the project's success?

## Options

- **A**: Aware
- **B**: Supportive
- **C**: Neutral
- **D**: Leading

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PMI's engagement levels are Unaware, Resistant, Neutral, Supportive, and Leading; Leading describes a stakeholder who is actively championing the project. (A) Aware means they know the project exists but are not engaged. (B) Supportive means they back the project but are not actively pushing it forward. (C) Neutral means neither for nor against.
