# Question

A new executive has joined the organisation and will become a key stakeholder for an ongoing project. The executive is unfamiliar with the project history. How should the PM facilitate their engagement?

## Options

- **A**: Wait for them to request information
- **B**: Send all project documentation for them to review
- **C**: Request a meeting to provide a concise project overview, understand their priorities and concerns, and establish an appropriate ongoing communication approach
- **D**: Continue current communication patterns

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. New executive stakeholders need efficient onboarding. A concise overview respects their time while providing necessary context. Understanding their priorities enables tailored engagement. Establishing communication expectations early prevents misalignment. Proactive engagement demonstrates professionalism and builds the relationship from the start.

Option A misses the opportunity to shape first impressions.

Option B overwhelms without highlighting what matters.

Option D doesn't account for their newness to the organisation and project.
