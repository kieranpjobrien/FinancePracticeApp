# Question

A new executive has just joined the organisation and inherits a key sponsor role on the PM's in-flight project. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Continue the existing communication cadence and cover the project history at the next quarterly review.
- **B**: Forward the full project document library so the executive can self-onboard.
- **C**: Request a short meeting to give a focused project overview, learn the executive's priorities, and agree the ongoing engagement approach.
- **D**: Wait for the executive to reach out once she has settled into the new role.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

PMI's stakeholder onboarding for a new executive sponsor is a tailored, time-respecting briefing that exchanges context for priorities and sets engagement expectations. (A) misses the chance to shape the executive's first impression. (B) overwhelms with detail and abdicates filtering. (D) is passive and forfeits the opportunity to set the relationship up well.
