# Question

In the power/interest grid for stakeholder analysis, how should you manage stakeholders with high power but low interest?

## Options

- **A**: Ignore them
- **B**: Keep them satisfied—monitor their concerns and keep them informed without overwhelming them
- **C**: Manage them closely with frequent engagement
- **D**: Try to increase their interest level immediately

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. High-power, low-interest stakeholders can significantly impact the project if dissatisfied but don't want heavy involvement. Keep them satisfied with appropriate updates, address their concerns promptly, and don't burden them with excessive detail. They may become more interested during critical phases, so maintain the relationship.

Option A risks negative intervention if they become concerned.

Option C may annoy them with unwanted attention.

Option D may not align with their preferences or capacity.
