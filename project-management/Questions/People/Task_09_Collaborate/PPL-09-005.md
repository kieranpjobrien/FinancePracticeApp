# Question

The PM needs input from a subject matter expert who is known across the organisation for being difficult to engage and whose delays have affected other projects. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Understand the SME's workload and priorities, then agree a working rhythm that fits her constraints.
- **B**: Ask the SME's line manager to direct her to make time for the project immediately.
- **C**: Find a second-tier expert who can provide close-enough input without the usual engagement risk.
- **D**: Send a detailed written brief and a firm deadline with a clear escalation path if missed.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Servant leadership starts with understanding, not pressure; SMEs who are "difficult" often have unmanaged demand from many directions and respond well to being engaged on their own terms. (B) escalates before the PM has had the conversation. (C) accepts a lower-quality input and avoids building the relationship the project needs. (D) leads with a deadline and escalation threat, which hardens resistance.
