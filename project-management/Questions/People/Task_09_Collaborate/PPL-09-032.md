# Question

A project will affect five business units whose interests partially conflict. What is the most effective collaboration approach for the PM?

## Options

- **A**: Establish a shared outcome, create cross-unit forums, and surface trade-offs transparently.
- **B**: Negotiate separate side agreements with each business unit so they do not have to interact with each other.
- **C**: Default to the position of the unit that contributes the largest share of project funding and governance.
- **D**: Let the business units sort out their conflicts directly without PM facilitation or structured dialogue.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PMI's stakeholder model says align on shared outcomes first, build forums for joint dialogue, and use transparent trade-offs to address remaining conflicts before escalating. (B) prevents integration and creates inconsistent commitments. (C) introduces an arbitrary tiebreaker that has nothing to do with project value or merit. (D) abdicates the PM's facilitation role and lets conflicts harden.
