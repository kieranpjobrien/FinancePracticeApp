# Question

In an agile project, stakeholders are bypassing the Product Owner to ask developers directly for features, and the PO is starting to lose control of the backlog. What should the Scrum Master do?

## Options

- **A**: Forbid stakeholders from any direct contact with the development team.
- **B**: Reaffirm the PO as the single backlog authority, coach stakeholders to route requests through the PO, and support the PO with techniques for managing inbound demand.
- **C**: Allow the developers to triage requests themselves and forward only the larger ones to the PO.
- **D**: Bring in a second PO to share the load.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The Scrum Master's role is to protect the PO's product authority and coach the system back into a healthy state without isolating the team. (A) is too blunt and cuts off legitimate informal collaboration. (C) fragments backlog accountability and undermines the PO. (D) splits an explicitly singular role and creates new conflicts.
