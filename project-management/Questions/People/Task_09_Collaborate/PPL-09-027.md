# Question

A project requires input from subject matter experts (SMEs) who report to different managers and have competing priorities. Getting their time is challenging. How should the PM facilitate SME collaboration?

## Options

- **A**: Demand SME time from their managers
- **B**: Do without SME input
- **C**: Work with SME managers to schedule dedicated time, respect SME constraints, make sessions efficient, and demonstrate value from their contributions
- **D**: Hire external consultants instead

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. SME collaboration requires respecting their constraints while securing necessary input. Working with managers creates formal allocation. Efficient sessions respect their time. Demonstrating value (showing how their input improved outcomes) builds willingness for future collaboration. Treating SMEs as valued partners rather than resources to be demanded yields better results.

Option A creates conflict without solving the underlying issue.

Option B compromises project quality.

Option D may lack specific organisational knowledge and adds cost.
