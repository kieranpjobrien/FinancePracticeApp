# Question

What is the primary purpose of a stakeholder register?

## Options

- **A**: To track stakeholder complaints
- **B**: To document stakeholder identification, interests, influence, and engagement approach
- **C**: To rank stakeholders by importance for resource allocation
- **D**: To record stakeholder contact information only

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. A stakeholder register documents who stakeholders are, their interests in the project, their level of influence/power, their current and desired engagement level, and strategies for effective engagement. This enables tailored communication and proactive relationship management throughout the project.

Option A is too narrow—it's about complaints only.

Option C is one element but not the primary purpose.

Option D captures only basic contact details.
