# Question

A project has many stakeholders but limited PM time for engagement. How should the PM prioritise stakeholder collaboration efforts?

## Options

- **A**: Treat all stakeholders equally regardless of impact
- **B**: Focus only on the most senior stakeholders
- **C**: Use stakeholder analysis (power, interest, urgency) to prioritise engagement, tailor communication frequency and depth to stakeholder needs, and leverage technology for efficient broader communication
- **D**: Delegate all stakeholder communication to others

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Limited time requires strategic prioritisation. Stakeholder analysis identifies who needs most attention. Tailored communication ensures high-impact stakeholders get appropriate engagement while others receive efficient communication. Technology (dashboards, automated updates, collaboration platforms) extends reach without consuming PM time for every interaction.

Option A spreads effort too thin.

Option B may miss important influencers who aren't senior.

Option D removes the PM from critical relationships.
