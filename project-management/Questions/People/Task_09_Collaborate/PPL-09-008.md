# Question

A stakeholder keeps requesting changes that the team believes are low value. The team is frustrated by the constant disruption. How should the PM improve collaboration?

## Options

- **A**: Implement a change freeze to stop all requests
- **B**: Reject all requests from this stakeholder
- **C**: Facilitate a conversation between the team and stakeholder to understand the stakeholder's goals and educate on project constraints
- **D**: Accept all requests to keep the stakeholder happy

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. The underlying issue is misalignment - the stakeholder has needs that aren't being met, and the team doesn't understand the value behind requests. Bringing them together creates mutual understanding. The stakeholder learns about constraints; the team learns about business needs. This leads to better requests and better prioritisation.

Option A blocks legitimate changes without addressing root cause.

Option B damages the stakeholder relationship.

Option D creates unlimited scope creep.
