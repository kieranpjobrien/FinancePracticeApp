# Question

The salience model classifies stakeholders based on which three attributes?

## Options

- **A**: Power, urgency, and legitimacy
- **B**: Power, interest, and influence
- **C**: Authority, availability, and expertise
- **D**: Influence, sentiment, and proximity

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The Mitchell, Agle and Wood salience model classifies stakeholders by power (ability to influence), urgency (claim requires immediate attention), and legitimacy (claim is appropriate). (B) names attributes from the power/interest grid (Mendelow), a different model. (C) and (D) list plausible-sounding attributes that are not the salience model's three.
