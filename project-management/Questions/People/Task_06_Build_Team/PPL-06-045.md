# Question

You are setting up a new team and need to decide between full co-location, fully remote, or hybrid working. What should drive the decision?

## Options

- **A**: Default to fully remote because it expands the candidate pool and is cheaper.
- **B**: Default to full co-location because it is known to maximise collaboration.
- **C**: Let each team member choose individually based on personal preference.
- **D**: Assess project complexity, the team's experience working together, the nature of collaboration required, and the available tooling, then choose the arrangement that best fits those factors.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PMI's tailoring principle treats working arrangements as a choice that should follow project and team context, not a default. (D) explicitly weighs the factors. (A) and (B) are blanket positions that ignore context. (C) optimises for individual preference at the expense of coherent collaboration.
