# Question

You are setting up a new team and need to decide between full co-location, fully remote, or hybrid working. What should drive the decision?

## Options

- **A**: Default to fully remote because it significantly expands the candidate pool and is cheaper on facilities.
- **B**: Default to full co-location because research shows co-location is known to maximise team collaboration outcomes.
- **C**: Let each individual team member choose their working arrangement based purely on their own personal preference.
- **D**: Assess project complexity, team experience, collaboration needs, and tooling, then pick the best-fitting arrangement.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PMI's tailoring principle treats working arrangements as a choice that should follow project and team context, not a default. (D) explicitly weighs the factors. (A) and (B) are blanket positions that ignore context. (C) optimises for individual preference at the expense of coherent collaboration.
