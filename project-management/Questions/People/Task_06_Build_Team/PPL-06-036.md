# Question

What is the recommended size for a Scrum team according to the Scrum Guide?

## Options

- **A**: Exactly 7 people
- **B**: 10 or fewer people total
- **C**: 15-20 people
- **D**: No size limit

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. The Scrum Guide recommends teams of 10 or fewer people total (including Scrum Master and Product Owner). Smaller teams communicate better and are more productive. Larger teams should be split into multiple Scrum teams.

Option A is too prescriptive; the guide provides a range, not an exact number.

Option C is too large for effective Scrum.

Option D ignores research showing communication overhead increases exponentially with team size.
