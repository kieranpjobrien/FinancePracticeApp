# Question

What is a RACI matrix used for in team building?

## Options

- **A**: Measuring team performance
- **B**: Clarifying roles and responsibilities by defining who is Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, and Informed for each task or decision
- **C**: Scheduling team meetings
- **D**: Calculating team velocity

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. RACI (Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed) clarifies who does the work (R), who owns the outcome (A), who provides input (C), and who needs to know (I). This reduces confusion, prevents gaps, and avoids duplication of effort. It's particularly valuable for cross-functional teams.

Option A relates to performance metrics.

Option C relates to calendar management.

Option D relates to agile estimation.
