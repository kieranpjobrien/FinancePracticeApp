# Question

In Tuckman's model, what characterises the Performing stage?

## Options

- **A**: Team members are testing boundaries and being cautious
- **B**: Conflict and power struggles are common
- **C**: The team works effectively with high trust and autonomy, focusing on achieving goals
- **D**: The team is establishing norms and finding its rhythm

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Performing is the highest-functioning stage. The team has developed trust, established effective working patterns, and can focus energy on achieving goals rather than internal dynamics. Team members work autonomously and interdependently, supporting each other effectively.

Option A describes Forming.

Option B describes Storming.

Option D describes Norming.
