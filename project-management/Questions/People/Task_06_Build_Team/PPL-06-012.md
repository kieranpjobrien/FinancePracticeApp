# Question

A project team includes both full-time members and part-time contributors who split time across multiple projects. The part-time members seem less engaged and are often out of sync with team progress. How should the PM build better team cohesion?

## Options

- **A**: Exclude part-time members from team activities
- **B**: Demand full-time allocation from all members
- **C**: Create structured touchpoints, ensure part-time members receive context updates, and find ways to include them in team activities
- **D**: Accept that part-time members will never be fully integrated

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Part-time team members face integration challenges due to limited contact time. The PM should create structures that help them stay connected: regular sync points, async updates on progress, inclusion in key team activities. Making explicit effort to include them builds cohesion despite limited time.

Option A worsens the disconnection.

Option B may not be possible given resource constraints.

Option D gives up on achievable integration.
