# Question

A senior architect has just announced she is leaving in four weeks. She owns several critical design areas. What should the project manager prioritise FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Reassign her in-flight architectural work to other team members immediately to start absorbing the load.
- **B**: Wait for the replacement architect to be hired before changing anything so the team is not disrupted twice.
- **C**: Plan structured knowledge transfer with the architect, involve the team in the transition, and support them.
- **D**: Focus exclusively on accelerating the external recruitment of a replacement architect for the team.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

PMI's approach to a key departure is to capture knowledge, manage the team impact, and then handle replacement, in that order. (C) addresses all three. (A) front-loads disruption without preserving knowledge. (B) leaves a known knowledge risk unmanaged. (D) prioritises the replacement and ignores the knowledge and team impact.
