# Question

A team has been working together effectively for several months when a key member announces they are leaving. What should the PM prioritise to maintain team effectiveness?

## Options

- **A**: Immediately redistribute the leaving member's work to remaining team members
- **B**: Wait until the replacement arrives before making any changes
- **C**: Facilitate knowledge transfer from the departing member, involve the team in transition planning, and support the remaining team through the change
- **D**: Focus solely on finding a replacement

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Departure of a key member affects team dynamics and knowledge. Structured knowledge transfer preserves critical information. Involving the team acknowledges the emotional impact and helps them adapt. The remaining team members need support to handle increased workload and the loss of a colleague.

Option A ignores knowledge transfer and team impact.

Option B may leave critical work uncovered.

Option D neglects the immediate team needs during transition.
