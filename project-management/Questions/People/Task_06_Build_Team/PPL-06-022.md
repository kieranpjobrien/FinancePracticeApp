# Question

An agile team's velocity has plateaued despite no obvious problems. Team members get along well but rarely challenge each other's ideas. What team dynamic might be causing this?

## Options

- **A**: The team is performing optimally
- **B**: The team may be experiencing groupthink due to excessive cohesion without constructive conflict
- **C**: The team needs more technical training
- **D**: The team is too large

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. High cohesion without healthy conflict can lead to groupthink, where teams converge on comfortable solutions rather than optimal ones. Teams need psychological safety to disagree constructively. When everyone agrees too easily, innovation and improvement stall.

Option A ignores warning signs of complacency.

Option C assumes a technical problem without evidence.

Option D doesn't address the underlying dynamic of avoiding conflict.
