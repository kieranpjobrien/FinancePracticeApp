# Question

A newly formed team is polite but guarded. Members rarely challenge ideas, ask few questions, and avoid disagreement in meetings. According to Tuckman's model, what stage is the team in and what should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Forming; facilitate trust-building and create psychological safety so members are willing to engage openly.
- **B**: Storming; let conflict emerge naturally and stay out of the way.
- **C**: Norming; reinforce the working agreements that are already in place.
- **D**: Performing; protect the existing dynamic and avoid disturbing it.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The behaviours described are characteristic of Forming, when members are still testing boundaries and politeness masks low trust. (A) addresses the actual stage. (B) misreads quietness as productive conflict. (C) assumes norms exist when they have not yet been established. (D) treats early-stage caution as high performance.
