# Question

A project team includes contractors who are technically proficient but not integrating socially with full-time employees. This is affecting collaboration. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Accept that contractors don't need to integrate socially
- **B**: Explicitly include contractors in team-building activities and create collaborative working structures
- **C**: Have contractors work separately on isolated tasks
- **D**: Replace contractors with full-time employees

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Team cohesion requires intentional inclusion of all members, regardless of employment status. Include contractors in team events, social activities, and team communications. Pair contractors with employees on shared work. Treat them as team members rather than external resources. This improves collaboration and quality.

Option A accepts suboptimal collaboration.

Option C isolates them further.

Option D may not be feasible and loses their expertise.
