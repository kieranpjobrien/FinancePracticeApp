# Question

A team has completed a successful project and will be disbanded. Several team members express sadness about the team ending. According to team development theory, what stage is this?

## Options

- **A**: Storming
- **B**: Norming
- **C**: Performing
- **D**: Adjourning

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The correct answer is D. Adjourning (sometimes called Mourning) is the final stage of Tuckman's model when teams disband. It's characterised by mixed emotions—satisfaction with accomplishments but sadness about ending relationships. Effective PMs acknowledge this transition and may facilitate closure activities.

Option A involves conflict and competition.

Option B involves establishing norms.

Option C involves high productivity and collaboration.
