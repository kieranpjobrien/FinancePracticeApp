# Question

A successful project is wrapping up and the team will disband next month. Members are reflective, somewhat sad, and have been reminiscing about the work. Which Tuckman stage describes the team?

## Options

- **A**: Storming.
- **B**: Norming.
- **C**: Performing.
- **D**: Adjourning.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The Adjourning (sometimes Mourning) stage is when teams wind down and process the end of the working relationship. (D) fits the description. (A) is open conflict. (B) is settling into norms. (C) is the high-output phase before the team begins winding down.
