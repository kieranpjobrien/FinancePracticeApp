# Question

According to Tuckman's model, which stage comes immediately after Storming?

## Options

- **A**: Performing
- **B**: Norming
- **C**: Forming
- **D**: Adjourning

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Tuckman's model describes team development as: Forming → Storming → Norming → Performing → Adjourning. After the conflict and competition of Storming, teams move into Norming where they establish working agreements, develop trust, and begin collaborating more effectively.

Option A (Performing) comes after Norming.

Option C (Forming) is the first stage.

Option D (Adjourning) is the final stage when the team disbands.
