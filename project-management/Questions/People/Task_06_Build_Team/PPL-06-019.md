# Question

Which Tuckman stage comes immediately after Storming?

## Options

- **A**: Adjourning.
- **B**: Norming.
- **C**: Performing.
- **D**: Forming.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The Tuckman sequence is Forming, Storming, Norming, Performing, Adjourning. (B) is the correct successor to Storming. (A) is the final stage. (C) is the stage after Norming. (D) is the first stage.
