# Question

What are the five stages of team development described by Tuckman?

## Options

- **A**: Initiating, Planning, Executing, Monitoring & Controlling, Closing.
- **B**: Defining, Designing, Developing, Deploying, Decommissioning.
- **C**: Selecting, Onboarding, Maturing, Optimising, Releasing.
- **D**: Forming, Storming, Norming, Performing, Adjourning.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Tuckman's stages are a foundational model PMP candidates are expected to recall. (D) is the standard sequence. (A) is the PMI process group structure, not a team development model. (B) is a generic delivery lifecycle. (C) is a plausible-sounding HR sequence, not Tuckman.
