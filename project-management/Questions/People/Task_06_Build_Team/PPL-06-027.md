# Question

Which of the following best describes a cross-functional team?

## Options

- **A**: A team where every member is qualified across all functional areas equally.
- **B**: A team made up of members from different functional disciplines with the combined skills needed to deliver the work end to end.
- **C**: A team that supports several projects in parallel using the same members.
- **D**: A team whose members each report to more than one functional manager.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PMI defines cross-functional in terms of the team's combined skills, not interchangeability or reporting structure. (B) captures it. (A) describes a fully generalist team, which is uncommon. (C) describes shared resourcing. (D) describes matrix reporting, not team composition.
