# Question

Which of the following best describes a cross-functional team?

## Options

- **A**: A team where all members have the same skill set
- **B**: A team composed of members from different functional areas or disciplines with varied expertise
- **C**: A team that works across multiple projects simultaneously
- **D**: A team that reports to multiple managers

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Cross-functional teams bring together people with different specialisations (development, testing, design, business analysis, etc.) to work toward a common goal. This structure reduces handoffs, improves communication, and enables faster delivery.

Option A describes a functional or specialist team.

Option C describes resource sharing, not cross-functionality.

Option D describes matrix reporting, not team composition.
