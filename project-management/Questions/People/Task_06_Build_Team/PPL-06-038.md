# Question

A team is newly formed and members are being overly polite, avoiding any disagreement, and deferring to the PM on all decisions. What team development stage does this indicate?

## Options

- **A**: Performing
- **B**: Storming
- **C**: Forming
- **D**: Norming

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Forming is characterised by politeness, uncertainty, and dependence on the leader. Team members are cautious as they learn about each other and the project. They avoid conflict and look to the PM for direction. This is natural but the team needs to progress through subsequent stages to become effective.

Option A (Performing) shows autonomous, high-functioning behaviour.

Option B (Storming) involves conflict and challenging authority.

Option D (Norming) shows established relationships and working agreements.
