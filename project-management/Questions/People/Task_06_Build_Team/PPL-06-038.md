# Question

At the first two workshops of a new project, the team is noticeably polite, avoids disagreement, and pushes decisions to the project manager. According to Tuckman's model, what stage is the team in?

## Options

- **A**: Performing.
- **B**: Norming.
- **C**: Forming.
- **D**: Storming.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Forming is marked by politeness, caution, uncertainty, and dependence on the leader. (C) is the textbook match. (A) is high-performance and autonomous. (B) is settled norms. (D) is open conflict, which is not yet present.
