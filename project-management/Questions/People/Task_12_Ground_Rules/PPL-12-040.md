# Question

The team has a "cameras on" ground rule for video calls. A team member privately tells the project manager they need to keep their camera off for the next week because of a family emergency. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Maintain the rule consistently because granting exceptions undermines the agreement.
- **B**: Quietly approve the exception without telling the team about the change.
- **C**: Suggest the team member take personal leave so they do not have to attend meetings at all.
- **D**: Approve the exception, briefly inform the team without sharing private details, and offer support.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Servant leadership balances care for the individual with transparency to the team. (D) honours the team member's privacy while preventing other members from feeling the rule is being unevenly applied. (A) is rigid and lacks compassion. (B) creates the appearance of inconsistent enforcement. (C) jumps to leave when the team member has signalled they want to keep contributing.
