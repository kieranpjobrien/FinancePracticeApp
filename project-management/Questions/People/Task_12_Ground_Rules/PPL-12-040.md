# Question

A team's ground rules include "Cameras on during video calls." A team member has experienced a family emergency and asks to have their camera off for the next week. How should the PM respond?

## Options

- **A**: Enforce the rule consistently - exceptions undermine rules
- **B**: Grant the exception privately without telling the team
- **C**: Grant the exception, inform the team appropriately (respecting privacy), and offer support
- **D**: Suggest the team member take leave instead

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Ground rules should allow for human compassion. Granting the exception shows care for the individual. Informing the team (without sharing private details) maintains transparency and may prevent others from feeling the rule is being unfairly ignored. Offering support shows servant leadership.

Option A applies rules rigidly without humanity.

Option B may cause confusion if others notice the camera-off behaviour.

Option D doesn't accommodate the team member's preference to continue contributing.
