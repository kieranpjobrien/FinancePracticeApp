# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of establishing team ground rules?

## Options

- **A**: To document policies for HR compliance
- **B**: To create shared expectations for how team members will work together
- **C**: To limit individual autonomy and enforce discipline
- **D**: To satisfy project governance requirements

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Ground rules establish shared expectations for team behaviour, communication, decision-making, and collaboration. They create a common understanding of how the team will operate, which reduces conflict and increases efficiency.

Option A - while ground rules may support compliance, that's not their primary purpose.

Option C - ground rules should enable collaboration, not restrict autonomy; they're agreed by the team, not imposed on them.

Option D - ground rules benefit the team directly rather than existing primarily to satisfy governance.
