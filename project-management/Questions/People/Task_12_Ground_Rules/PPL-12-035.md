# Question

Who should be responsible for enforcing team ground rules?

## Options

- **A**: Only the project manager
- **B**: Only HR
- **C**: All team members share responsibility for upholding the rules
- **D**: Only the most senior team member

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. When all team members share responsibility for upholding ground rules, enforcement becomes peer accountability rather than hierarchical control. This creates stronger compliance and reduces the burden on any single person. Team ownership of rules includes ownership of enforcement.

Option A creates a policing dynamic and is unsustainable.

Option B removes team ownership and involves external parties unnecessarily.

Option D places unfair burden on one person and may affect relationships.
