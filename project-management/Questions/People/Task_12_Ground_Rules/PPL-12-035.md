# Question

Who is primarily responsible for upholding team ground rules?

## Options

- **A**: All team members share responsibility for honouring and reinforcing the rules.
- **B**: The project manager, as the team's accountable leader.
- **C**: The most senior team member, who sets the example others follow.
- **D**: Human Resources, as the function responsible for workplace conduct.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Team ownership means team responsibility. (A) reflects the PMI view that ground rules work best when peer accountability supports them. (B) creates a policing dynamic and overloads the project manager. (C) confuses seniority with responsibility and leaves enforcement uneven. (D) is incorrect because ground rules are working agreements, not HR policy.
