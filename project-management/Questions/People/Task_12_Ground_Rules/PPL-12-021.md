# Question

A new team member joins mid-project. The existing team has well-established ground rules that they follow naturally. How should the PM handle the new member's integration regarding ground rules?

## Options

- **A**: Assume the new member will pick up the norms by observing the team
- **B**: Explicitly share the ground rules and invite the new member to ask questions or suggest modifications
- **C**: Tell the new member to follow whatever the team does
- **D**: Wait for the new member to violate a rule before explaining it

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Explicit sharing ensures the new member understands expectations from the start rather than learning through mistakes. Inviting questions shows respect and helps them understand the reasoning. Inviting modifications acknowledges they may bring valuable perspectives and ensures continued buy-in from all team members.

Option A risks confusion and unintentional violations that could affect relationships.

Option C doesn't provide clarity or context for the rules.

Option D creates an uncomfortable situation and may damage the new member's integration.
