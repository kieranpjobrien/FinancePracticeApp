# Question

Two team members have a conflict about whether working from home should be allowed. One wants flexibility; the other feels remote work hurts collaboration. The team ground rules don't address this. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Make a unilateral decision about work location
- **B**: Refer to company policy and close the discussion
- **C**: Facilitate a discussion to create a team agreement that balances flexibility with collaboration needs
- **D**: Let each person do whatever they want

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. This is an opportunity to create a new ground rule that addresses a team need. Facilitate discussion about the underlying concerns (flexibility vs. collaboration), explore options that might satisfy both, and reach a team agreement. This addresses the immediate conflict while creating a lasting team norm.

Option A doesn't involve the team in decisions that affect them.

Option B defers to policy without exploring team-level agreements within policy bounds.

Option D doesn't resolve the underlying tension.
