# Question

What should ground rules focus on?

## Options

- **A**: Technical specifications and quality standards
- **B**: Behaviours and interactions between team members
- **C**: Project scope and deliverables
- **D**: Budget and resource allocation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Ground rules define how team members will behave and interact with each other. They cover communication, collaboration, decision-making, conflict resolution, and working norms. This focus on behaviour creates the environment for effective work.

Option A - technical standards are important but are separate documentation (Definition of Done, coding standards).

Option C - scope belongs in project documentation.

Option D - budget and resources are managed through project controls, not ground rules.
