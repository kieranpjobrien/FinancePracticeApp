# Question

Which of the following is an example of an effective team ground rule?

## Options

- **A**: "Be nice to each other"
- **B**: "Respond to team messages within 4 hours during working hours"
- **C**: "Don't make mistakes"
- **D**: "Always agree with the PM"

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Effective ground rules are specific, measurable, and behavioural. "Respond to messages within 4 hours during working hours" is clear - you can tell if it's being followed. It's a reasonable expectation that creates predictable communication.

Option A is vague - what does "nice" mean?

Option C is unrealistic - mistakes are inevitable.

Option D is authoritarian and stifles healthy debate.
