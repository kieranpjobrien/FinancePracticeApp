# Question

Which of the following is MOST important when establishing effective ground rules?

## Options

- **A**: Having comprehensive rules that cover every possible situation
- **B**: Ensuring senior management approves the rules
- **C**: Having the team participate in creating and agreeing to the rules
- **D**: Keeping the rules confidential within the team

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Team participation creates ownership and commitment. When team members help create the rules, they understand the reasoning, feel invested in success, and are more likely to hold themselves and each other accountable.

Option A - too many rules become burdensome and hard to remember; focus on the most important norms.

Option B - ground rules are for the team's benefit; management approval isn't the key factor.

Option D - ground rules can often be shared with stakeholders to set expectations.
