# Question

Which of the following is MOST important when establishing effective team ground rules?

## Options

- **A**: The team participates in creating and agreeing to the rules.
- **B**: The rules cover every foreseeable situation in detail.
- **C**: The project sponsor formally signs off on the rule set.
- **D**: The rules are kept confidential within the immediate team.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Buy-in is the difference between rules that are followed and rules that are ignored. (A) is the foundation of ownership and accountability. (B) tends to produce a rule list nobody can remember. (C) is unnecessary; sponsors don't typically govern team norms. (D) is incorrect; ground rules are often shared with stakeholders to set expectations.
