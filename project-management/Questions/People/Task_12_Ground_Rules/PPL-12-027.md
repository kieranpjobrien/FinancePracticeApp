# Question

Which of the following is an example of an effective ground rule?

## Options

- **A**: "Always do your best work"
- **B**: "Acknowledge messages within 4 business hours and respond within 24 hours"
- **C**: "Be a team player"
- **D**: "Maintain high quality"

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Effective ground rules are specific and measurable. "Acknowledge within 4 hours, respond within 24 hours" is clear - everyone knows exactly what's expected and can tell if the rule is being followed.

Options A, C, and D are too vague. "Best work," "team player," and "high quality" mean different things to different people. Without specificity, these rules can't be consistently applied or enforced.
