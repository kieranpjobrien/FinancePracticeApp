# Question

A team establishes a ground rule that all team members must be available during core hours of 10am-3pm. One team member has a recurring medical appointment during this time that they haven't disclosed. They frequently miss meetings scheduled during core hours. What is the BEST approach?

## Options

- **A**: Enforce the rule strictly - everyone agreed to it
- **B**: Remind the team member of the ground rule in front of the team
- **C**: Have a private conversation to understand the situation and work out an accommodation
- **D**: Eliminate core hours to avoid such conflicts

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Ground rules should have room for individual circumstances. A private conversation respects the team member's privacy while addressing the issue. Understanding the situation allows for accommodation (adjusting meeting times, different core hours for this person) that respects both the rule's intent and individual needs.

Option A applies rules rigidly without considering legitimate individual needs.

Option B violates privacy and may embarrass the team member.

Option D abandons a useful rule rather than making reasonable accommodations.
