# Question

A team established ground rules at project start, but one team member consistently violates the rule about responding to messages within four hours. Other team members are frustrated. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Revise the ground rule since it's clearly not working
- **B**: Have a private conversation with the team member to understand the situation and reinforce the expectation
- **C**: Call out the behaviour in the next team meeting
- **D**: Document the violations for their performance review

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Before escalating, understand why the rule is being violated. There may be legitimate reasons (workload, unclear expectations, technical issues) or a misunderstanding. A private conversation maintains dignity while addressing the issue. Reinforcing the expectation clarifies that the rule matters.

Option A abandons a valid rule without addressing the real issue.

Option C may embarrass the team member and damage trust.

Option D is a delayed response that doesn't address the immediate problem.
