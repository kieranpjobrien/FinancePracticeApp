# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of team ground rules?

## Options

- **A**: To establish shared expectations for behaviour and interaction that support effective teamwork.
- **B**: To document who is accountable for which deliverables across the project.
- **C**: To provide a basis for disciplinary action against team members who behave inappropriately.
- **D**: To demonstrate to the organisation that the project is operating in a controlled manner.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Ground rules exist to make the team's behavioural norms explicit so collaboration runs more smoothly and conflict is avoided where possible. (B) describes responsibility assignment (a RACI or similar), which is a separate artefact. (C) reframes ground rules as disciplinary tools, which they aren't. (D) treats them as a control or governance signal, which is incidental at best.
