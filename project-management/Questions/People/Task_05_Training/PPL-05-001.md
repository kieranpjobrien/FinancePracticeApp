# Question

A project requires the team to use a new cloud platform that no team member has experience with. The timeline is aggressive and stakeholders are anxious about ramp-up time. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Build a training plan into the schedule that combines short formal sessions with paired learning, and reset stakeholder expectations on ramp-up impact.
- **B**: Replace the team with contractors who already have cloud platform experience.
- **C**: Allow the team to learn on the job during the first sprints and absorb the slowdown later.
- **D**: Reduce the project's use of the new platform so less learning is needed.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The PM owns both the skill gap and the stakeholder expectation, and (A) addresses both directly without sacrificing the project's value or the team. (B) is fast but loses institutional knowledge and is rarely the right default for a single new technology. (C) hides the cost of learning and delivers it later as missed dates. (D) reshapes the project to avoid the training need rather than addressing it.
