# Question

A team has completed two days of agile training but continues to behave like a waterfall team: detailed up-front design, sequential phases, and resistance to mid-sprint change. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Schedule a refresher of the agile training so the concepts are reinforced.
- **B**: Mandate strict adherence to Scrum events to break the old pattern.
- **C**: Stop the agile transition and return to waterfall, since the team is not adopting it.
- **D**: Engage an agile coach to work with the team in real sprints, and use retrospectives to address the transition explicitly.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Behaviour change requires coaching during real work, not more classroom time, which is what (D) provides. (A) re-presents knowledge that is not the gap. (B) forces compliance without changing mindset and usually generates surface adoption. (C) abandons a deliberate change because it is harder than expected.
