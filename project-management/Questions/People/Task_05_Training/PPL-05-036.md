# Question

Your delivery team is split across Sydney, Bangalore, and London. You need to roll out training on a new test automation framework. What approach should the project manager take?

## Options

- **A**: Use a blended approach with recorded modules for self-paced learning and rotating live sessions for Q&A and practice.
- **B**: Schedule one live session at a time that suits the largest office and record it for the others to watch.
- **C**: Deliver the same live session three times, once in each region, ignoring overlap of content.
- **D**: Require everyone to attend a single live workshop in the timezone of the framework vendor.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PMI recommends tailoring delivery to a distributed team's reality and combining methods to support different learning styles. (A) gives every region equitable access and uses live time for the highest-value activities. (B) systematically disadvantages two regions. (C) is workable but inefficient and easy to deprioritise; it also misses the chance to use async content well. (D) ignores work-life balance and reduces learning effectiveness.
