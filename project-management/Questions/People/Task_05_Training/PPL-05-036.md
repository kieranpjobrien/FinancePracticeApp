# Question

Your team operates across multiple time zones. You need to provide training that works for everyone. What approach would be most effective?

## Options

- **A**: Hold live training at a time that works for headquarters
- **B**: Rotate training times so the inconvenience is shared
- **C**: Use a blended approach with recorded content for asynchronous learning, supplemented by time-zone-friendly live sessions for discussion and questions
- **D**: Require everyone to attend regardless of their local time

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Distributed teams need flexible training approaches. Recorded content allows learning at convenient times. Supplement with live sessions that rotate to share the burden of inconvenient times, or hold multiple sessions to cover time zones. Discussion forums allow async interaction. This respects distributed realities while ensuring everyone has access to training and support.

Option A disadvantages those outside headquarters time zone.

Option B shares burden but may not be effective for learning.

Option D disrespects work-life balance and may reduce learning effectiveness.
