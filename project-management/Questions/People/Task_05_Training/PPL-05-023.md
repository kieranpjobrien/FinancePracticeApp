# Question

What is a skills matrix used for in project management?

## Options

- **A**: To rank team members from best to worst
- **B**: To document team member competencies across relevant skills, identifying strengths and gaps for resource planning and development
- **C**: To determine team member salaries
- **D**: To assign blame when things go wrong

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. A skills matrix maps team members against required skills, showing proficiency levels. This helps with: identifying skill gaps requiring training, assigning work to people with relevant expertise, planning knowledge transfer, and understanding team capability for project planning. It's a planning tool, not a ranking or evaluation tool.

Option A misuses the matrix for ranking rather than planning.

Option C relates to compensation decisions, not skill planning.

Option D misuses information that should support development.
