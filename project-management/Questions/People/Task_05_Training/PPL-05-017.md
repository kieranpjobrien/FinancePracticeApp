# Question

What is the FIRST step in developing a project training plan?

## Options

- **A**: Select training providers that match the project's budget.
- **B**: Schedule training sessions in the project schedule before sprints begin.
- **C**: Allocate a training budget based on benchmarks from previous projects.
- **D**: Assess current team capabilities against the capabilities the project requires.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A training plan starts with the gap, because everything else (vendors, schedule, budget) depends on what is actually needed, which is what (D) does. (A), (B), and (C) all jump to delivery decisions before the gap has been understood and risk solving the wrong problem.
