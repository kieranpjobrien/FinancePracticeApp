# Question

What is the first step in developing a training plan for a project team?

## Options

- **A**: Select training vendors
- **B**: Assess current competencies against project requirements to identify skill gaps
- **C**: Schedule training sessions
- **D**: Allocate the training budget

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Before planning any training, you must understand what training is needed. This requires assessing current team competencies against what the project requires. The gap between current and required skills informs what training is necessary, for whom, and at what priority level.

Option A presumes you know what training is needed before assessing gaps.

Option C schedules training without knowing what's required.

Option D allocates budget without understanding training needs to inform that allocation.
