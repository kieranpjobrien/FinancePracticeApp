# Question

Training was provided, but you observe that team members are not applying what they learned and have reverted to old habits. What should you do?

## Options

- **A**: Repeat the same training
- **B**: Accept that training didn't work and stop investing in it
- **C**: Reinforce the training through coaching, create accountability for applying new skills, remove barriers to adoption, and understand why reversion occurred
- **D**: Punish team members for not applying training

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Training alone often doesn't change behaviour. Reinforce through coaching: provide support during application. Create accountability: set expectations for using new skills. Investigate barriers: why did they revert? Is the new approach harder? Unclear? Not supported by tools? Addressing root causes enables lasting behaviour change.

Option A assumes more content will work when the issue is application.

Option B gives up without understanding why transfer failed.

Option D punishes without understanding or addressing barriers.
