# Question

A finance transformation will change how 1,500 employees across 14 countries process invoices. Individual classroom training is not feasible within the timeline, and self-service videos alone produced poor adoption on a similar past project. What approach should the project manager recommend?

## Options

- **A**: Run a single global webinar that all affected employees are required to attend live.
- **B**: Implement a train-the-trainer model with regional champions, supported by self-service resources and post-launch coaching.
- **C**: Push the new process out with detailed written procedures and rely on managers to enforce compliance.
- **D**: Deliver country-by-country instructor-led training and stage the rollout over 18 months.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PMI favours scalable, locally tailored delivery that combines methods, especially when prior data shows a single channel underperforms. (B) cascades expertise through champions who can adapt to local context, and reinforces with self-service and coaching to support transfer. (A) ignores time-zone and language reality and treats live attendance as evidence of learning. (C) repeats the failure mode of relying on documentation alone. (D) is unlikely to fit the timeline and delays benefits.
