# Question

When are training needs BEST identified for a project?

## Options

- **A**: After a team member misses a deliverable due to a skill gap.
- **B**: At the kickoff workshop, when the team first comes together.
- **C**: During planning, alongside resource planning and skill-gap assessment.
- **D**: At project closure, as part of lessons learned for future projects.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Training needs sit with resource planning and should be identified during planning so they can be funded and scheduled, which is what (C) describes. (A) is reactive and pays for the gap in missed work. (B) is too late to influence schedule and budget. (D) helps future projects but not the current one.
