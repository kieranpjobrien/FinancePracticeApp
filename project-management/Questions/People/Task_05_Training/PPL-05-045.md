# Question

Your organisation has limited training budget, but you've identified multiple training needs across the team. How should you prioritise training investments?

## Options

- **A**: First come, first served for training requests
- **B**: Give training to the most senior team members first
- **C**: Prioritise based on criticality to project success, impact of skill gaps, risk if not addressed, and potential for knowledge multiplication through train-the-trainer approaches
- **D**: Don't provide any training since you can't afford everything

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. With limited budget, prioritise strategically. Criticality: which skills are essential for project delivery? Impact: where would training produce the biggest improvement? Risk: which gaps pose the biggest threats if unaddressed? Multiplication: can trained individuals share knowledge with others? This ensures maximum value from limited training investment.

Option A doesn't optimise for impact.

Option B allocates based on seniority rather than need or impact.

Option D gives up rather than optimising available resources.
