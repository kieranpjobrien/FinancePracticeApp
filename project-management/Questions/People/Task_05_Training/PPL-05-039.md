# Question

Why is it important to evaluate training effectiveness?

## Options

- **A**: To justify the training budget to management
- **B**: To ensure training achieves its intended outcomes - improved skills and performance - and to identify improvements for future training
- **C**: To rank participants
- **D**: To prove the training vendor delivered what was contracted

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Training evaluation ensures the investment achieves its purpose. Did participants learn the intended skills? Can they apply them? Has performance improved? Evaluation identifies what worked for future training and what needs reinforcement. This closes the loop between training delivery and business outcomes.

Option A is a secondary benefit, not the primary purpose.

Option C misuses evaluation for ranking rather than effectiveness assessment.

Option D is about contract compliance, not learning effectiveness.
