# Question

A team member has just returned from a week of agile training and is enthusiastic about new practices. The rest of the team is sceptical. How should the PM handle the knowledge transfer?

## Options

- **A**: Direct the team to adopt the practices the trained member learned.
- **B**: Have the trained member share what they learned and facilitate a discussion on which practices the team will trial.
- **C**: Let the trained member apply the practices to her own work and let others observe.
- **D**: Send the rest of the team on the same training course.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The right move is structured knowledge transfer that lets the team evaluate fit and choose what to trial, which is what (B) does. (A) bypasses team buy-in and usually produces compliance rather than adoption. (C) creates a private practice that does not transfer to the team. (D) is expensive and unnecessary if knowledge can be transferred from the person who already attended.
