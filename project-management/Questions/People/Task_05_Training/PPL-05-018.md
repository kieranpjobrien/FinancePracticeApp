# Question

The project will use a technology that most team members are unfamiliar with. The timeline is tight. How should you address the training need?

## Options

- **A**: Delay the project until everyone is fully trained
- **B**: Proceed without training and let people learn on the job
- **C**: Provide focused just-in-time training on essential skills, supplement with documentation, and pair less experienced members with those who have relevant skills
- **D**: Replace all team members with experienced specialists

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Balanced approach: provide essential training focused on what's needed immediately (just-in-time learning). Create supporting materials for reference. Use pairing to spread knowledge while doing productive work. This addresses the skill gap pragmatically within timeline constraints without delaying the project or proceeding without any support.

Option A may not be feasible given timeline constraints.

Option B sets the team up to struggle and make avoidable mistakes.

Option D loses institutional knowledge and may not be possible.
