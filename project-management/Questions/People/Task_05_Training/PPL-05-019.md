# Question

A senior business stakeholder will sign off on deliverables but has no experience in the product domain. What training should the PM arrange?

## Options

- **A**: Provide the same technical onboarding the development team received so the stakeholder has equivalent depth.
- **B**: Skip formal training because acceptance is mainly a sign-off activity.
- **C**: Replace him with a stakeholder who already has the domain background.
- **D**: Run a focused orientation covering the domain concepts, the acceptance criteria, and how to spot issues during demonstrations.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Stakeholder training should match the role they need to perform, and acceptance review needs domain orientation rather than developer-level depth, which is what (D) provides. (A) over-trains and wastes time. (B) leaves the stakeholder unable to perform the role meaningfully. (C) is rarely possible and avoids the underlying need to prepare the assigned stakeholder.
