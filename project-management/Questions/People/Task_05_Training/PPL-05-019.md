# Question

A key stakeholder will be responsible for accepting project deliverables but has no experience with the product domain. What training approach should you recommend?

## Options

- **A**: No training needed - they just need to sign off
- **B**: Provide comprehensive technical training equivalent to the development team
- **C**: Offer domain orientation training focused on what they need to evaluate deliverables effectively - key concepts, acceptance criteria, and how to identify issues
- **D**: Replace them with a more qualified stakeholder

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Stakeholder training should match their role. For acceptance responsibility, they need to understand: key domain concepts, what "good" looks like (acceptance criteria), how to evaluate quality, and what issues to watch for. They don't need technical depth - just enough to perform their role effectively.

Option A leaves them unable to meaningfully evaluate deliverables.

Option B provides more depth than needed and wastes resources.

Option D may not be possible and doesn't address the underlying need for stakeholder preparation.
