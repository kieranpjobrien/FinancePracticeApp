# Question

Which negotiation style seeks outcomes that satisfy the core interests of all parties at the table?

## Options

- **A**: Distributive negotiation
- **B**: Positional bargaining
- **C**: Integrative (win-win) negotiation
- **D**: Hardball negotiation

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Integrative negotiation expands the available value by understanding interests and inventing options before dividing — that is the structural difference from zero-sum styles. (A) divides a fixed pie without expanding it. (B) trades concessions on stated positions and tends toward stalemate or split-the-difference. (D) maximises unilateral pressure and is generally counterproductive on relationships the PM has to live with.
