# Question

What negotiation approach seeks outcomes that satisfy all parties' core interests?

## Options

- **A**: Distributive negotiation
- **B**: Win-lose negotiation
- **C**: Integrative (win-win) negotiation
- **D**: Competitive negotiation

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Integrative or win-win negotiation focuses on expanding value for all parties by understanding underlying interests, creating options, and finding mutually beneficial outcomes. This approach builds relationships and creates sustainable agreements. It contrasts with distributive (win-lose) negotiation where one party's gain is another's loss.

Option A and B describe zero-sum approaches.

Option D focuses on beating the other party rather than mutual gain.
