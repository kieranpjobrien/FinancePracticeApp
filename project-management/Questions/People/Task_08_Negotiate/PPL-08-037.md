# Question

A contract negotiation has stalled on the liability cap, with both sides holding firm. What technique is most likely to break the impasse?

## Options

- **A**: Drop the liability cap issue and proceed with the contract without a defined cap.
- **B**: Explore creative options such as tiered liability, third-party insurance, or trades on other clauses.
- **C**: Apply schedule pressure to force the counterparty to concede on the liability cap.
- **D**: Hand the issue to in-house counsel to resolve without further commercial PM input.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PMI's negotiation guidance is to expand the pie when a single issue is stuck — tiering, insurance, and cross-issue trades create new value the simple cap argument cannot. (A) leaves a major risk undefined. (C) damages the relationship and rarely produces durable concessions. (D) loses the commercial judgement only the PM can supply on what trade-offs are acceptable.
