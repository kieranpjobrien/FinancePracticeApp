# Question

What does BATNA stand for in negotiation?

## Options

- **A**: Best Approach To Negotiated Agreements
- **B**: Best Alternative To a Negotiated Agreement
- **C**: Basic Agreement Terms for New Arrangements
- **D**: Bilateral Agreement Tactics for Negotiation Advantage

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. BATNA (Best Alternative To a Negotiated Agreement) represents what you'll do if current negotiations fail. Understanding your BATNA determines your negotiating power—if your alternative is strong, you can walk away confidently. If your BATNA is weak, you may need to make more concessions. BATNA analysis is fundamental to principled negotiation.

Options A, C, and D are fabricated terms that don't reflect the established concept.
