# Question

The PM learns through a side conversation that the vendor has an undisclosed quarter-end deadline that creates real pressure to close the deal this week. What is the most ethical and effective response?

## Options

- **A**: Use the information to understand the vendor's interests and propose a fair, sustainable deal.
- **B**: Demand significantly improved terms and explicitly cite the quarter-end deadline as leverage.
- **C**: Tell the vendor's senior leadership that their negotiator's confidential deadline has been disclosed.
- **D**: Disregard the information entirely and continue negotiating as if the PM did not know about it.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PMI's ethics standard requires fair dealing while still permitting the PM to use information to design a sustainable agreement; understanding pressure helps shape options without exploitation. (B) is overt exploitation that produces a deal the vendor will resent and potentially under-deliver on. (C) is a breach of confidence that helps no one. (D) discards relevant context that could legitimately improve the agreement design for both sides.
