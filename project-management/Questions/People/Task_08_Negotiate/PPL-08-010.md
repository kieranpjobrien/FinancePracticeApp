# Question

What does BATNA stand for in negotiation theory?

## Options

- **A**: Budget Authority To Negotiate Agreements
- **B**: Best Alternative To a Negotiated Agreement
- **C**: Baseline Acceptance Threshold for New Agreements
- **D**: Bilateral Agreement Terms and Negotiation Approach

## Answer

B

## Explanation

BATNA is the best course of action a party has if the current negotiation fails; it is the benchmark against which any proposed deal should be judged and the source of real walk-away power. (A), (C) and (D) are plausible-sounding distractor expansions but none of them is the actual term used in negotiation literature.
