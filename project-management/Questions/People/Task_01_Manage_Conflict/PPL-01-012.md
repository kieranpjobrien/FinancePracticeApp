# Question

Which conflict resolution approach is characterized by one party giving in to the other party's position?

## Options

- **A**: Collaborating
- **B**: Compromising
- **C**: Accommodating/Smoothing
- **D**: Avoiding

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Accommodating (also called Smoothing) is when one party yields to the other's position, often to preserve harmony or because the issue matters more to the other party. The accommodating party gives up their position to satisfy the other.

Option A (Collaborating) seeks win-win solutions where both parties' needs are met.

Option B (Compromising) involves both parties giving up something - mutual concessions, not one-sided yielding.

Option D (Avoiding) is withdrawing from the conflict, not yielding to the other position.
