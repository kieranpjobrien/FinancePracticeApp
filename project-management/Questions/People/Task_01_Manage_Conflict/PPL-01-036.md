# Question

A cross-functional team has marketing pushing features that will delay engineering, and engineering wanting to cut features marketing considers essential. What approach should the PM take?

## Options

- **A**: Decide which department's priorities matter more.
- **B**: Escalate to both department heads to resolve.
- **C**: Facilitate a discussion grounded in project objectives and customer value to find solutions that address each side's core concern.
- **D**: Implement everything requested and extend the timeline.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

(C) reframes a positional dispute around shared outcomes — customer value and the project's objectives — which often surfaces creative options like phased delivery or MVP scope. (A) picks a winner and damages the relationship. (B) escalates rather than facilitating at the PM level. (D) rewards every request and ignores baseline integrity.
