# Question

A vendor has been delivering late, causing friction with the internal team who are working overtime to recover. The vendor blames unclear requirements; the team blames vendor incompetence. How should the PM address this?

## Options

- **A**: Terminate the vendor contract immediately to remove the friction.
- **B**: Bring both parties together to review the requirements process, identify gaps, agree communication protocols and set measurable expectations.
- **C**: Accept the vendor's account and tell the team to plan for ongoing overtime.
- **D**: Determine who is at fault before deciding what to do next.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

(B) is collaborating on a systemic problem: shared review surfaces process gaps, the new protocols prevent recurrence, and measurable expectations create accountability without blame. (A) is drastic and ignores possible internal contribution to the problem. (C) institutionalises the dysfunction. (D) anchors the discussion on blame rather than improvement.
