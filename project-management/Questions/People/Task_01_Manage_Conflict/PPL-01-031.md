# Question

Which conflict resolution approach is most appropriate when the issue is trivial or when more important issues are pressing?

## Options

- **A**: Collaborating
- **B**: Forcing
- **C**: Avoiding/Withdrawing
- **D**: Compromising

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Avoiding or withdrawing is appropriate when the issue is trivial, when there's no chance of winning, when the potential damage outweighs benefits, or when people need time to cool down. Not every conflict requires active resolution.

Option A (Collaborating) requires significant time investment not warranted for trivial issues.

Option B (Forcing) uses power unnecessarily on minor matters.

Option D (Compromising) still requires negotiation effort that may not be worthwhile.
