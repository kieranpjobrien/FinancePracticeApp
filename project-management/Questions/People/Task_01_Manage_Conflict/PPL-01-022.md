# Question

A team is in the Storming stage of development, with frequent disagreements about processes and approaches. The PM is tempted to step in and resolve each conflict quickly. What is the BEST approach?

## Options

- **A**: Resolve conflicts quickly to maintain productivity
- **B**: Allow constructive conflict while facilitating resolution, recognising this is a normal developmental stage
- **C**: Avoid all conflict by making decisions for the team
- **D**: Disband the team and form a new one

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Storming is a normal team development stage. Constructive conflict helps teams work through differences and establish norms. The PM should facilitate (not suppress) healthy conflict, helping the team develop its own conflict resolution capabilities. Resolving every conflict for them prevents this growth.

Option A prevents team development and creates dependency on the PM.

Option C avoids necessary team development.

Option D abandons a team at a critical growth stage.
