# Question

What does "smoothing" or "accommodating" as a conflict resolution technique involve?

## Options

- **A**: Assertively pushing for your preferred solution
- **B**: Emphasising areas of agreement and downplaying differences to maintain harmony
- **C**: Finding a middle ground where each party gives up something
- **D**: Working together to find a solution that fully satisfies everyone

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Smoothing/accommodating focuses on maintaining relationships by emphasising common ground and minimising the significance of differences. It's useful when preserving the relationship is more important than the issue at hand, or when you recognise you're wrong.

Option A describes forcing/competing.

Option C describes compromising.

Option D describes collaborating.
