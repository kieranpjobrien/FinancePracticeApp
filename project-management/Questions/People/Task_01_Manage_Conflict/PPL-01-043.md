# Question

Which of the following best describes "smoothing" or "accommodating" as a conflict-handling technique?

## Options

- **A**: Emphasising areas of agreement and downplaying differences to maintain harmony.
- **B**: Pushing assertively for your preferred solution.
- **C**: Finding a middle ground where each side gives up something.
- **D**: Working together to find a solution that fully satisfies everyone.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Smoothing/accommodating preserves the relationship by emphasising commonalities and minimising the issue — useful when the relationship matters more than the outcome, or when you realise you are wrong. (B) describes forcing/competing. (C) describes compromising. (D) describes collaborating.
