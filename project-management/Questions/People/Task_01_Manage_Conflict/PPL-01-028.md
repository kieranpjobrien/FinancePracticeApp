# Question

According to the Thomas-Kilmann Conflict Model, which approach combines high assertiveness with high cooperativeness?

## Options

- **A**: Competing
- **B**: Avoiding
- **C**: Collaborating
- **D**: Accommodating

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Collaborating is characterised by high assertiveness (pursuing your own concerns) and high cooperativeness (seeking to satisfy others' concerns). This approach aims for a win-win solution where all parties' needs are fully met.

Option A (Competing) is high assertiveness but low cooperativeness.

Option B (Avoiding) is low on both dimensions.

Option D (Accommodating) is high cooperativeness but low assertiveness.
