# Question

In the Thomas-Kilmann conflict model, which approach is high in assertiveness but low in cooperativeness?

## Options

- **A**: Avoiding
- **B**: Competing (forcing)
- **C**: Collaborating
- **D**: Accommodating

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Competing — also called forcing — is high assertiveness, low cooperativeness: one party imposes its solution. (A) Avoiding is low on both. (C) Collaborating is high on both. (D) Accommodating is low assertiveness, high cooperativeness.
