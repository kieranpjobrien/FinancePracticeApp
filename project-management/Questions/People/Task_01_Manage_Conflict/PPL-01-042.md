# Question

A team is in conflict over working hours. Some prefer early starts; others prefer late nights; remote members in different time zones add complexity. Core collaboration is suffering. How should the PM address this?

## Options

- **A**: Mandate standard 9-to-5 hours for everyone on the team regardless of their home time zone.
- **B**: Facilitate agreement on core collaboration hours that cover all zones, with flexibility outside them.
- **C**: Let everyone work whatever hours they prefer with no coordination across the distributed team.
- **D**: Move the team to fully asynchronous communication only and remove any scheduled synchronous meetings.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

(B) balances individual flexibility with the team's need for synchronous overlap and shares the inconvenience fairly across time zones — the PMI tailoring approach for distributed teams. (A) imposes one zone's preference on the rest. (C) sacrifices necessary collaboration for individual convenience. (D) avoids the scheduling discussion and may not suit the work.
