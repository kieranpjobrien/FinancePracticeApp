# Question

A team is experiencing conflict about working hours and availability. Some members prefer early starts, others prefer late nights. Remote team members in different time zones add complexity. Core collaboration is suffering. How should the PM address this?

## Options

- **A**: Mandate standard 9-5 hours for everyone
- **B**: Let everyone work whenever they want with no coordination
- **C**: Facilitate agreement on core collaboration hours while allowing flexibility outside those windows, considering all time zones
- **D**: Move to fully asynchronous communication only

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Balance individual flexibility with team needs. Facilitate discussion to identify minimum core hours when synchronous collaboration is essential. Allow flexibility outside these windows. For distributed teams, find overlap hours that work across time zones, possibly rotating to share inconvenience fairly. Document agreements clearly.

Option A ignores legitimate preferences and time zone realities.

Option B sacrifices necessary collaboration for individual convenience.

Option D may not suit all work types and avoids the scheduling conversation.
