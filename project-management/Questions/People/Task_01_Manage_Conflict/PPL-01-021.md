# Question

Which conflict-handling technique is BEST when the relationship matters and there is time to find a solution that meets everyone's interests?

## Options

- **A**: Forcing
- **B**: Collaborating
- **C**: Avoiding
- **D**: Accommodating

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Collaborating (problem-solving) is the win-win approach and is PMI's preferred technique whenever time and trust permit. (A) Forcing wins the argument but damages the relationship. (C) Avoiding leaves the issue unresolved. (D) Accommodating sacrifices one party's interests, which is unsustainable over time.
