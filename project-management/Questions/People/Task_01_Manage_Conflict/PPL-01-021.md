# Question

What is the BEST long-term conflict resolution approach when the relationship is important and there's time to find a solution?

## Options

- **A**: Forcing
- **B**: Avoiding
- **C**: Collaborating
- **D**: Accommodating

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Collaborating (also called problem-solving or confronting) seeks a win-win solution that fully satisfies both parties' needs. It requires time and effort but produces the best outcomes when relationships matter and creative solutions are possible. Both parties work together to find a solution that addresses underlying interests.

Option A (Forcing) damages relationships by creating winners and losers.

Option B (Avoiding) doesn't resolve anything.

Option D (Accommodating) sacrifices your needs for others', which isn't sustainable.
