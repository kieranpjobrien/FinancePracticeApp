# Question

Which conflict resolution technique involves finding a solution that partially satisfies all parties but may not fully address anyone's concerns?

## Options

- **A**: Forcing
- **B**: Compromising
- **C**: Collaborating
- **D**: Avoiding

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Compromising involves each party giving up something to reach a middle ground. While it can resolve conflicts quickly, it often results in a lose-lose outcome where no party gets exactly what they wanted.

Option A (Forcing) is a win-lose approach where one party imposes their solution.

Option C (Collaborating) seeks a win-win solution that fully addresses all concerns.

Option D (Avoiding) withdraws from the conflict without resolution.
