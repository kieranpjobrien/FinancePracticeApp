# Question

Which conflict-handling technique produces a partial solution where each party gives something up and no one is fully satisfied?

## Options

- **A**: Forcing
- **B**: Collaborating
- **C**: Avoiding
- **D**: Compromising

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Compromising is "splitting the difference" — both sides concede something. (A) Forcing imposes one party's view (win-lose). (B) Collaborating produces a win-win that meets both interests. (C) Avoiding sidesteps the conflict entirely.
