# Question

Two project managers compete for the same shared resource. Both projects are high priority and the resource cannot meet both demands. The functional manager has asked the PMs to work it out. What is the BEST approach?

## Options

- **A**: Escalate to the PMO to make the call.
- **B**: Claim the resource first and let the other PM find an alternative.
- **C**: Split the resource's time 50/50 regardless of actual demand.
- **D**: Meet the other PM, share timelines and constraints, and jointly propose a plan optimised for organisational priorities.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

(D) is collaborating between peers, exactly what the functional manager has asked for, and likely surfaces flexibility neither PM saw alone. (A) escalates a peer-level negotiation. (B) creates an adversarial relationship and may not align with organisational priority. (C) is artificial fairness that ignores the actual work.
