# Question

In sprint planning, a developer publicly tells the product owner that the backlog order "makes no business sense". The product owner becomes defensive. What should the Scrum Master do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Side with the product owner since they own backlog ordering.
- **B**: Side with the developer since they may have a valid point.
- **C**: Adjourn the meeting and reschedule once both have cooled off.
- **D**: De-escalate, acknowledge both perspectives and refocus the conversation on the prioritisation criteria the team uses.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

(D) is collaborating: defuse the personal exchange and turn the discussion to the objective criteria behind backlog order, where both parties can contribute. (A) and (B) take sides and create winners and losers. (C) is avoidance — postponing prevents resolution and disrupts the sprint cadence.
