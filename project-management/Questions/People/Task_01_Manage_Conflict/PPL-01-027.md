# Question

A stakeholder sends an angry email to the PM criticising the team's work quality, copying senior executives. The PM knows the criticism is based on a misunderstanding of scope. What is the best response?

## Options

- **A**: Reply-all with a defensive rebuttal pointing out the stakeholder's error
- **B**: Schedule a private call with the stakeholder to understand their concerns, clarify the scope, then follow up with a summary email to all parties
- **C**: Ignore the email and hope it blows over
- **D**: Escalate to your own manager to handle the conflict

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Address the conflict directly but professionally. A private call allows for two-way dialogue to uncover the real issue and correct misunderstandings without public confrontation. Following up with a summary email documents the resolution and informs all parties appropriately.

Option A escalates conflict publicly and damages relationships.

Option C allows the misunderstanding to persist and may damage the team's reputation.

Option D abdicates responsibility for stakeholder management.
