# Question

You observe directly that one team member has been taking credit for another team member's work. The victim has not complained. How should you lead through this?

## Options

- **A**: Wait for the victim to raise it before acting.
- **B**: Publicly call out the credit-taker to make an example.
- **C**: Address the behaviour privately with the credit-taker, correct attribution where appropriate, and monitor for recurrence.
- **D**: Treat it as office politics and stay out of it.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

(C) is proactive ethical leadership — address it directly, correct the record without humiliation, and watch for recurrence. PMI's code of ethics treats fairness as non-negotiable. (A) lets unfairness continue. (B) is unnecessarily humiliating and likely backfires. (D) normalises unethical behaviour.
