# Question

According to situational leadership theory, what determines the most effective leadership style for a given task?

## Options

- **A**: The leader's natural personality and preferences.
- **B**: The organisation's culture alone.
- **C**: The team member's competence and commitment for that specific task.
- **D**: The project's budget and timeline.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Hersey and Blanchard's situational leadership matches style to the follower's readiness — competence (ability) and commitment (willingness) — for the task at hand. The same person may need different styles for different tasks. (A) makes the leader, not the follower, the variable. (B) and (D) are contextual factors but not the model's basis.
