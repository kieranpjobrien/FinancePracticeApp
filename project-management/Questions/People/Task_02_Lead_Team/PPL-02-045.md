# Question

The organisation announces a new strategic direction that will significantly impact your project. The team is confused about what it means for their work. What leadership action should you take FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Gather information, share what you know and don't know, and plan to adapt together.
- **B**: Wait for official guidance from leadership before communicating anything to the team.
- **C**: Reassure the team that nothing about their current work and priorities will change.
- **D**: Start making project changes immediately based on your own interpretation of the announcement.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

(A) is leadership during ambiguity — proactive, transparent, and explicit about uncertainty. (B) leaves the team in an information vacuum. (C) makes promises you can't keep. (D) risks major rework if your interpretation turns out wrong.
