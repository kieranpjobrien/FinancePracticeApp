# Question

A team member asks to be excused from a mandatory team event due to a religious observance. Some other members have said that everyone should attend. How should you handle this?

## Options

- **A**: Tell them the event is mandatory for everyone regardless of personal reasons.
- **B**: Ask them to demonstrate the sincerity of their religious beliefs before deciding.
- **C**: Grant the accommodation and make sure they receive any critical information afterward.
- **D**: Postpone the event to a later date to accommodate everyone's schedule.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

(C) is the legally and ethically sound response — religious accommodation is standard in most jurisdictions and no proof of sincerity is appropriate. (A) is unnecessarily rigid and likely violates accommodation requirements. (B) is inappropriate. (D) is often impractical and not necessary when an individual accommodation covers it.
