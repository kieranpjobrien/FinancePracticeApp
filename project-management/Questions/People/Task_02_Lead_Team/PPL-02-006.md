# Question

In Tuckman's model of team development, during which stage does the team begin working together effectively, with shared norms and growing mutual trust starting to take hold?

## Options

- **A**: Forming
- **B**: Norming
- **C**: Storming
- **D**: Performing

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Norming is when the team has resolved early conflicts and starts establishing shared norms and trust. (A) Forming is polite but guarded, with reliance on the leader. (C) Storming features the conflict that precedes norming. (D) Performing is the peak stage — the question asks when these qualities BEGIN to emerge, which is norming.
