# Question

According to Tuckman's model, during which stage does a team experience the most conflict as members push against boundaries and each other?

## Options

- **A**: Forming
- **B**: Storming
- **C**: Norming
- **D**: Performing

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Storming is characterised by conflict as team members push against established boundaries, challenge leadership, and compete for position. Differences in working styles and opinions surface. This conflict, while uncomfortable, is necessary for the team to progress to more effective stages.

Option A (Forming) is polite orientation with minimal conflict.

Option C (Norming) shows conflict resolution and agreement on ways of working.

Option D (Performing) features high-functioning collaboration with minimal dysfunctional conflict.
