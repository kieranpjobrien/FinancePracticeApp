# Question

Your team includes both highly experienced members and newcomers. The experienced members sometimes dismiss the newcomers' ideas without full consideration. How should you lead to address this dynamic?

## Options

- **A**: Let seniority determine whose ideas are heard
- **B**: Model inclusive behaviour, explicitly invite newcomer contributions, and coach experienced members on the value of diverse perspectives
- **C**: Stop taking input from experienced members to balance power
- **D**: Create separate forums for each experience level

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Inclusive leadership values all perspectives. Model the behaviour you want: explicitly ask for newcomer input, thank them for fresh perspectives, build on their ideas. Privately coach experienced members: newcomers see things differently and often identify issues experts overlook. Create meeting structures that ensure all voices are heard.

Option A loses valuable fresh perspectives and demotivates newcomers.

Option C creates reverse unfairness and loses valuable experience.

Option D prevents the knowledge sharing that benefits everyone.
