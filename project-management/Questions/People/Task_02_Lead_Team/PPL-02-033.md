# Question

Your team mixes highly experienced members and newcomers. The seniors sometimes dismiss the newer members' ideas without fully considering them. How should you lead to address this?

## Options

- **A**: Let seniority decide whose ideas are heard in team discussions and decisions.
- **B**: Model inclusive behaviour yourself and coach seniors on the value of different perspectives.
- **C**: Stop taking input from the senior members entirely in order to rebalance the power dynamic.
- **D**: Run separate idea-generation forums for each experience level of the team.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

(B) is inclusive leadership: model the behaviour, structure meetings to surface all voices, and coach privately. (A) loses valuable fresh perspectives and demotivates newcomers. (C) is reverse unfairness and loses experience. (D) creates silos and blocks knowledge transfer.
