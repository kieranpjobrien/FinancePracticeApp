# Question

A team member approaches you confidentially about personal problems affecting their work. They're worried about being seen as weak or unreliable. How should you respond?

## Options

- **A**: Tell them to keep personal issues out of work
- **B**: Listen supportively, explore what accommodation might help, maintain confidentiality, and follow up appropriately
- **C**: Report the conversation to HR immediately
- **D**: Share with the team so they can be supportive

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. When team members trust you with personal information, honour that trust. Listen without judgment. Explore what might help (flexible hours, temporary workload adjustment, EAP resources). Maintain strict confidentiality. Follow up to show you care and to ensure any agreed accommodations are working. This builds deep trust.

Option A dismisses their trust and their humanity.

Option C may be appropriate for serious issues (safety concerns) but isn't the default response.

Option D violates confidentiality and the trust they placed in you.
