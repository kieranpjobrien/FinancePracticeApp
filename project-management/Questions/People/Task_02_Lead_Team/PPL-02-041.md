# Question

A team member confides in you about personal problems affecting their work. They are anxious about being seen as weak or unreliable. How should you respond?

## Options

- **A**: Tell them to keep personal issues strictly out of the workplace.
- **B**: Report the conversation to HR straight away as a formal matter.
- **C**: Share the situation with the team so they can support the colleague.
- **D**: Listen supportively, explore what accommodations might help, and maintain confidentiality.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

(D) honours the trust placed in you and uses the levers you actually have — flexible hours, temporary load adjustment, EAP referral. (A) dismisses the human element. (B) escalates without justification (HR is appropriate for safety or policy issues, not as the default). (C) breaks the confidence.
