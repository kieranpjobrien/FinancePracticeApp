# Question

One team member consistently outperforms the rest, creating resentment. The high performer is becoming isolated and other members are demotivated by comparison. How should the PM address this?

## Options

- **A**: Channel the high performer into mentoring, and recognise individual growth rather than output.
- **B**: Ask the high performer to throttle their output down to match the rest of the team's pace.
- **C**: Praise the high performer publicly in team forums to motivate the others to match their output.
- **D**: Ignore the team dynamic entirely and focus only on overall output at the collective team level.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

(A) leverages the high performer for team uplift and removes the unhealthy comparison by changing what gets recognised. (B) penalises excellence and wastes talent. (C) deepens resentment. (D) lets the dysfunction continue unchecked.
