# Question

One team member consistently outperforms the rest, creating resentment. The high performer is becoming isolated and other members are demotivated by comparison. How should the PM address this?

## Options

- **A**: Channel the high performer into mentoring others and shift recognition toward individual growth and contribution rather than absolute output.
- **B**: Ask the high performer to throttle their output to match the team.
- **C**: Praise the high performer publicly to motivate others to match them.
- **D**: Ignore the team dynamic and focus only on overall output.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

(A) leverages the high performer for team uplift and removes the unhealthy comparison by changing what gets recognised. (B) penalises excellence and wastes talent. (C) deepens resentment. (D) lets the dysfunction continue unchecked.
