# Question

A PM leads a team where one member consistently outperforms others, creating resentment. The high performer is becoming isolated, and other team members are demotivated by comparison. How should the PM address this?

## Options

- **A**: Ask the high performer to reduce their output to match the team
- **B**: Use the high performer to mentor others while ensuring recognition is based on individual growth, not just absolute output
- **C**: Publicly praise the high performer to motivate others to match their performance
- **D**: Ignore the dynamics and focus only on overall team output

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Channel the high performer's abilities to lift the team through mentoring. Shift recognition from absolute output to growth and contribution - celebrate improvement, not just top scores. This reduces unhealthy competition while leveraging the high performer's skills to develop others.

Option A penalises excellence and wastes talent.

Option C increases resentment and unhealthy comparison.

Option D allows team dysfunction to continue unchecked.
