# Question

A high-performing team member is showing signs of burnout — long hours, increasing cynicism, falling enthusiasm. Output quality is still acceptable. How should the PM lead in this situation?

## Options

- **A**: Leave it alone since work quality is still acceptable.
- **B**: Assign more work to keep them engaged.
- **C**: Caution them that the negative attitude is affecting the team.
- **D**: Have a supportive conversation to understand the cause, agree workload adjustments together, and ensure they take time off.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

(D) is the PMI servant-leadership response: burnout worsens silently and the cure is a real conversation, sustainable workload, and recovery. (A) lets the burnout collapse into resignation or quality failure. (B) accelerates the burnout. (C) attacks a symptom and misses the cause.
