# Question

What best describes servant leadership?

## Options

- **A**: A style where the leader makes all decisions for the team to ensure consistency.
- **B**: A style applied only in not-for-profit organisations.
- **C**: A style focused on serving the team's needs, removing impediments and enabling success.
- **D**: A style where the leader steps back and avoids all responsibility.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Servant leadership puts the team's growth and effectiveness first — removing obstacles, providing resources and creating the conditions for success. (A) describes directive leadership. (B) wrongly limits servant leadership to a sector. (D) confuses serving the team with abdicating leadership.
