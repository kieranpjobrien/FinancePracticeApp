# Question

A new team member has strong technical skills but is struggling to integrate socially — eats lunch alone and rarely contributes in team discussions. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Insist they participate in all team social activities.
- **B**: Leave it alone as long as their work is good.
- **C**: Consider removing them for not being a culture fit.
- **D**: Create low-pressure inclusion opportunities, pair them with welcoming colleagues on work, and have a private conversation to understand their preferences.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

(D) respects individual differences while supporting cohesion — some people are introverted or simply take time to warm up, and the PM should adapt their approach to the person. (A) is forcing and likely creates anxiety. (B) ignores that integration affects collaboration and belonging. (C) punishes personality differences rather than leading.
