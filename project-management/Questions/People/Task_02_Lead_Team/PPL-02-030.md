# Question

A new team member has great technical skills but is struggling to integrate socially with the team. They eat lunch alone and rarely participate in team discussions. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Force them to participate in all social activities
- **B**: Ignore it as long as their work is good
- **C**: Create natural opportunities for inclusion, pair them with welcoming team members, and have a private conversation to understand their comfort level and preferences
- **D**: Consider removing them for not being a culture fit

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Leadership includes fostering team cohesion while respecting individual differences. Some people are introverted or take time to warm up. Create low-pressure opportunities for connection. Pair them with welcoming colleagues on work tasks. Have a private conversation - they may be struggling, or they may simply prefer less social interaction. Adapt your approach to their needs.

Option A disrespects individual differences and may cause anxiety.

Option B ignores that social integration affects collaboration and belonging.

Option D punishes personality differences rather than adapting leadership.
