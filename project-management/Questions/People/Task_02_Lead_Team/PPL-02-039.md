# Question

You inherit a team with a toxic culture - cliques, gossip, blame-shifting, and little collaboration. The previous leader ignored these behaviours. What leadership approach should you take to transform the culture?

## Options

- **A**: Replace the entire team to start fresh
- **B**: Accept the culture as unchangeable and work around it
- **C**: Set clear expectations for professional behaviour, model the desired culture, consistently address violations, and recognise positive changes over time
- **D**: Implement strict rules and heavy penalties immediately

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. Culture change requires sustained, consistent leadership effort. Set clear expectations: define acceptable behaviour explicitly. Model it yourself consistently. Address violations promptly and fairly (first privately, then escalate if needed). Recognise and reward behaviour that aligns with desired culture. This is gradual work, but most people will adapt when expectations are clear and consistent.

Option A loses institutional knowledge and may not be feasible.

Option B accepts dysfunction that will undermine project success.

Option D relies on punishment rather than positive transformation and may increase resistance.
