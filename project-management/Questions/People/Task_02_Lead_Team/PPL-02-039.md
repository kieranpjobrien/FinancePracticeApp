# Question

You inherit a team with a toxic culture — cliques, gossip, blame-shifting and little collaboration. The previous leader ignored this. What leadership approach should you take?

## Options

- **A**: Replace the entire team to start fresh.
- **B**: Set and model clear expectations for professional behaviour, consistently address violations, and recognise positive changes over time.
- **C**: Accept the culture as it is and work around it.
- **D**: Impose strict rules with heavy penalties from day one.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

(B) is sustained, consistent leadership — the only reliable path to culture change. Set the norms, live them, act on violations, and reinforce the positives. (A) destroys institutional knowledge and isn't usually feasible. (C) lets dysfunction derail project success. (D) relies on punishment, which produces compliance and resentment rather than transformation.
