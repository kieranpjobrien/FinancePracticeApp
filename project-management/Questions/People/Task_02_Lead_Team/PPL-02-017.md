# Question

A new team member is struggling. They have the technical skills but seem unclear on priorities and how their work fits the bigger picture. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Provide clear direction on priorities and project context, with regular check-ins to ensure alignment.
- **B**: Give them more time to figure it out independently.
- **C**: Restrict them to simple tasks until they improve.
- **D**: Begin documenting performance for a possible separation conversation.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

(A) is the directing style under situational leadership for someone new to the situation — high commitment, low context. (B) leaves them to flounder. (C) underuses their skills and may demotivate. (D) is premature; the issue is onboarding support, not performance.
