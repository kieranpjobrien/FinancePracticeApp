# Question

What is the primary purpose of establishing a team vision?

## Options

- **A**: To satisfy documentation requirements
- **B**: To provide shared direction and purpose that motivates and aligns team efforts
- **C**: To give the PM something to present to stakeholders
- **D**: To benchmark against other teams

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. A team vision provides shared purpose and direction. It helps team members understand why their work matters, how it fits into larger goals, and what success looks like. A compelling vision motivates effort, guides decisions, and aligns individual actions toward common objectives.

Option A treats vision as bureaucratic requirement rather than leadership tool.

Option C focuses on external presentation rather than team benefit.

Option D is about comparison rather than intrinsic purpose.
