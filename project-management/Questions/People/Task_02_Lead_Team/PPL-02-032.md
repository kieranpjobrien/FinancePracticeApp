# Question

What is the primary purpose of establishing a team vision?

## Options

- **A**: To meet a documentation requirement.
- **B**: To give the PM something to present to stakeholders.
- **C**: To benchmark the team against others.
- **D**: To provide shared direction and purpose that motivates and aligns team effort.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A team vision sets shared purpose, helps members see why their work matters and aligns decisions toward common goals. (A) treats vision as paperwork. (B) treats it as a stakeholder artefact rather than a team tool. (C) makes it about comparison rather than intrinsic direction.
