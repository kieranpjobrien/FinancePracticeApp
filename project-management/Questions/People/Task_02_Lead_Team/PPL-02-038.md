# Question

Your project sponsor frequently changes direction, causing rework and team frustration. Morale is suffering from the perceived chaos. What is the best leadership approach?

## Options

- **A**: Criticise the sponsor to the team to show you understand their frustration.
- **B**: Shield the team from churn while working with the sponsor on change-management checkpoints.
- **C**: Tell the team that change is part of the job and to get on with it.
- **D**: Escalate the sponsor's behaviour to the sponsor's own manager immediately.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

(B) handles both the symptom (team morale) and the cause (sponsor decision-making). Filtering reversible changes protects the team while the change-management conversation prevents recurrence. (A) undermines the sponsor and makes a partisan of the PM. (C) dismisses legitimate frustration. (D) escalates before direct resolution.
