# Question

Your project sponsor frequently changes direction, causing rework and frustration for the team. Team morale is suffering due to the perceived chaos. What is the best leadership approach?

## Options

- **A**: Criticise the sponsor to the team to show you understand their frustration
- **B**: Shield the team from unnecessary changes while working with the sponsor to establish better change management processes
- **C**: Tell the team to accept that change is part of the job
- **D**: Escalate to the sponsor's manager immediately

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Leadership includes protecting the team while addressing systemic issues. Shield the team: filter out changes that reverse themselves, batch communications about stable decisions. Work with the sponsor: understand drivers for changes, propose better decision-making checkpoints, establish change thresholds. This addresses both team morale and root cause.

Option A undermines the sponsor relationship and doesn't solve the problem.

Option C dismisses legitimate frustration about inefficiency.

Option D escalates before attempting direct resolution.
