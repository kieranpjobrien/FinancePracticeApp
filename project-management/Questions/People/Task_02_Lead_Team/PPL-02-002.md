# Question

What is the PRIMARY role of a servant leader on an agile project?

## Options

- **A**: To make decisions for the team to maximize efficiency
- **B**: To remove obstacles and support the team in achieving their goals
- **C**: To report team performance to senior management
- **D**: To ensure all team members follow standard processes

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Servant leadership focuses on serving the team by removing impediments, providing resources, and creating an environment where team members can do their best work. The servant leader prioritizes the team's needs and growth over their own authority or control.

Option A contradicts servant leadership principles. In agile environments, teams should be self-organizing and make their own decisions where possible. The leader facilitates rather than directs.

Option C (reporting to management) is a necessary activity but not the PRIMARY role. Servant leaders focus on serving the team, not primarily on upward management.

Option D (enforcing processes) is more characteristic of command-and-control management. While processes matter, servant leaders focus on outcomes and support, adapting processes to serve the team rather than enforcing compliance.
