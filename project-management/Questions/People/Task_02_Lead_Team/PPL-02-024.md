# Question

What is the primary characteristic of servant leadership?

## Options

- **A**: The leader makes all decisions and team members simply execute the directives.
- **B**: The leader focuses on supporting team members to do their best work.
- **C**: The leader delegates all responsibility to the team and stays out of the way.
- **D**: The leader prioritises their own career advancement above team needs and goals.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Servant leadership inverts the hierarchy — the leader serves by removing obstacles, providing resources, coaching and creating conditions for team success. (A) is command-and-control. (C) is abdication, not service. (D) is the opposite of servant leadership.
