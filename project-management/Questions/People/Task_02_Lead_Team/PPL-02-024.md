# Question

What is the primary characteristic of servant leadership?

## Options

- **A**: The leader makes all decisions and team members execute
- **B**: The leader focuses on supporting and enabling team members to do their best work
- **C**: The leader delegates all responsibility to the team
- **D**: The leader prioritises their own career advancement

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Servant leadership inverts the traditional hierarchy - the leader's primary role is to serve the team by removing obstacles, providing resources, coaching, and creating an environment where team members can succeed. The focus is on team needs rather than leader authority.

Option A describes command-and-control leadership.

Option C is abdication of leadership responsibility, not servant leadership.

Option D is self-serving, the opposite of servant leadership.
