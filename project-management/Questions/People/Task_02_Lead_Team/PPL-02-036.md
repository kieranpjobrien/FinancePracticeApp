# Question

What is "psychological safety" in a team context?

## Options

- **A**: Physical security measures in the workplace.
- **B**: Insurance coverage for mental-health treatment.
- **C**: A guarantee that no one will be made redundant.
- **D**: A shared belief that the team is safe for taking interpersonal risks — speaking up, disagreeing, admitting mistakes — without fear of punishment.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Edmondson's definition — the shared belief that interpersonal risk-taking is safe — is the foundation for learning, innovation and collaboration. (A) and (B) are different forms of safety. (C) is job security, which is not the same construct.
