# Question

What is the primary purpose of a team's Definition of Done?

## Options

- **A**: List the acceptance criteria the product owner uses to accept individual stories.
- **B**: Provide the criteria a sprint must meet for the sprint goal to be considered achieved.
- **C**: Describe the closing activities required to formally close the project.
- **D**: Establish a shared, team-wide standard for when a piece of work is complete and releasable.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The Definition of Done is the team's shared agreement on what "complete" means and what quality gates apply to every increment. (A) describes story-level acceptance criteria, which vary by story and are owned by the product owner. (B) describes the sprint goal itself, not the DoD. (C) describes project closure, not the DoD.
