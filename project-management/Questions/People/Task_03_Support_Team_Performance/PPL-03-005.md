# Question

Daily standups have drifted into status reports for management rather than team coordination — members read prepared updates instead of collaborating. What change would BEST restore the standup's intended purpose?

## Options

- **A**: Invite management to observe but not participate.
- **B**: Take management out of the standup and refocus on team coordination, impediments and collaboration needs; cover management's information needs through other channels.
- **C**: Shorten the standup to five minutes to prevent long status reads.
- **D**: Replace the verbal standup with a written status board that management can view.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

(B) attacks the root cause: management presence has changed the meeting's nature, so removing them restores safety for honest blocker discussion while still serving their needs another way. (A) is observed presence — same effect on behaviour. (C) compresses the status read but keeps the wrong dynamic. (D) gives management the data but eliminates the team coordination value of the standup.
