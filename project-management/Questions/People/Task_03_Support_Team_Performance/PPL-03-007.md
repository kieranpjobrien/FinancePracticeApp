# Question

A Scrum team has been using story points for several months. Management now wants to convert points to hours for resource planning. The team is concerned this will undermine relative estimation. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Build the conversion formula since management needs the data for planning.
- **B**: Explain why story points should not be converted to hours, coach management on using velocity for capacity planning, and help them get the planning information they need through appropriate metrics.
- **C**: Switch the team to hour-based estimation to satisfy management's request.
- **D**: Refuse the request and escalate the disagreement to the project sponsor.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

(B) addresses the legitimate planning need (velocity for capacity) without corrupting the estimation method (story points measure complexity, not time). PMI rewards coaching and tailoring over compliance. (A) provides false precision. (C) abandons an effective practice based on a misunderstanding. (D) escalates before education is even attempted.
