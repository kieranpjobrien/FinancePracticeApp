# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of a sprint retrospective?

## Options

- **A**: To plan the next sprint's work
- **B**: To demonstrate completed work to stakeholders
- **C**: To inspect how the team worked and identify improvements for the next sprint
- **D**: To update the project status report

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The correct answer is C. The sprint retrospective is dedicated to continuous improvement - examining how the team worked together, what went well, what didn't, and what can be improved. It's a key mechanism for supporting team performance through regular reflection and adaptation.

Option A describes sprint planning.

Option B describes the sprint review (demo).

Option D is a reporting activity, not a Scrum ceremony.
