# Question

What is the primary purpose of the daily scrum?

## Options

- **A**: Allow the Scrum Master to assign the day's work to each developer.
- **B**: Provide a status report to managers and the product owner.
- **C**: Let the developers inspect progress toward the sprint goal and adapt the plan for the day.
- **D**: Resolve technical questions and design issues that have come up overnight.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The daily scrum is a 15-minute event for the developers to inspect progress against the sprint goal and adapt their plan for the next 24 hours. (A) misrepresents both the Scrum Master role and team self-management. (B) treats the event as upward reporting, which is not its purpose. (D) is something teams sometimes drift into, but technical problem-solving belongs after the daily scrum so the timebox is preserved.
