# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of conducting regular retrospectives in agile projects?

## Options

- **A**: To assign blame for sprint failures and missed commitments.
- **B**: To document lessons learned for the organisational knowledge archive.
- **C**: To report team performance metrics to external stakeholders and sponsors.
- **D**: To enable continuous improvement of the team's processes and effectiveness.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The retrospective is "inspect and adapt" applied to the team's own process — its purpose is continuous improvement. (A) destroys safety and contradicts the practice. (B) is a possible by-product but not the purpose. (C) confuses retrospectives with status reporting; they are internal team events.
