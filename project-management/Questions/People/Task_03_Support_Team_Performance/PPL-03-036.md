# Question

A senior developer delivers her own work to a high standard but declines to attend retrospectives, skips pairing, and ignores teammates' requests for help, saying her own deliverables come first. How should the project manager respond?

## Options

- **A**: Lower her performance rating at the next review cycle to reflect her lack of teamwork.
- **B**: Reassign collaborative work to other developers so the team can move forward without her.
- **C**: Have a direct conversation about team-level expectations and agree specific, observable contributions she will make.
- **D**: Raise the behaviour with her line manager so it can be addressed through her management chain.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The PM owns expectation-setting on the project and should address team behaviour directly with the person before involving others, which is what (C) does. (A) substitutes a delayed formal consequence for a conversation she has not yet had. (B) accommodates the behaviour and silently rewrites the team's working agreement. (D) escalates an issue the PM has the standing and responsibility to handle first.
