# Question

An agile team's velocity has declined for three sprints in a row despite no change in team composition. Members report feeling overwhelmed and meetings have become tense. What should the Scrum Master do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Reduce the sprint commitment to a more achievable level.
- **B**: Report the decline to management and request additional resources.
- **C**: Run a focused retrospective to surface the root causes of the decline.
- **D**: Introduce overtime to catch up on missed commitments.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

(C) follows PMI's "diagnose before you treat" principle — the team owns the inspection of what changed (technical debt, unclear requirements, interruptions, dynamics). (A) is a symptom fix that may extend the pain. (B) escalates without diagnosis and gives management nothing actionable. (D) compounds an overload problem and degrades quality.
