# Question

What is psychological safety in the context of team performance?

## Options

- **A**: Physical security measures for the workspace.
- **B**: A shared belief that the team is safe for taking interpersonal risks — speaking up, disagreeing, admitting mistakes — without fear of punishment.
- **C**: Mental-health support services provided by the organisation.
- **D**: Protection of team members' personal information.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Edmondson's definition: a shared belief that interpersonal risk-taking is safe. It enables learning, candour and innovation. (A) refers to physical safety. (C) is a benefit. (D) is data privacy — none of these is the team-dynamic concept.
