# Question

The team is struggling to complete stories within sprints due to dependencies on another team that frequently delivers late. What should the PM do to support performance?

## Options

- **A**: Accept late delivery as outside your control
- **B**: Work to establish better coordination with the dependent team, consider adjusting sprint boundaries, and buffer appropriately for dependency risk
- **C**: Blame the other team in status reports
- **D**: Stop working with the dependent team entirely

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correct answer is B. Dependencies are often the biggest source of delivery variability. Improve coordination: establish regular sync points, share sprint plans, align on interfaces earlier. Adjust your approach: consider longer sprints to absorb variability, build buffers, or resequence work to reduce blocking. Escalate systemic dependency issues to leadership if needed.

Option A accepts poor performance without trying to address causes.

Option C damages relationships without solving the problem.

Option D may not be feasible and doesn't address the coordination challenge.
