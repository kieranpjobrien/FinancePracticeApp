# Question

A Scrum team is establishing how it will use velocity. What is the PRIMARY purpose of tracking velocity from sprint to sprint?

## Options

- **A**: Provide management with a productivity metric for performance reviews.
- **B**: Demonstrate continuous improvement by showing velocity increases over time.
- **C**: Compare this team's output against other Scrum teams in the organisation.
- **D**: Help the team forecast how much work it can realistically commit to in upcoming sprints.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Velocity is an internal planning tool. It exists so the team can use historical throughput to forecast realistic sprint commitments and protect sustainable pace. (A) misuses velocity as an individual or team performance score, which encourages gaming and inflates estimates. (B) treats rising velocity as an improvement signal, but increasing velocity is often just point inflation and is not a goal of agile teams. (C) is invalid because story points are relative to a single team's context and cannot be compared across teams.
