# CFA Study Resources

## Practical Skills Modules (PSMs)

CFA Institute requires one PSM per level — you must complete it before results release or your result is **voided**. They're included in the exam registration fee (no extra cost). Access via your candidate account under "My PSMs" in the Learning Ecosystem. Each takes 10-20 hours.

### Available Modules by Level

| Module | L1 | L2 | L3 |
|--------|:--:|:--:|:--:|
| Financial Modeling | Yes | — | Yes |
| Python Programming Fundamentals | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Analyst Skills | — | Yes | Yes |
| Python, Data Science & AI | — | Yes | Yes |
| Portfolio Development & Construction | — | — | Yes |

**Note:** You cannot reuse the same module across levels. Plan ahead — if you do Python Fundamentals at L1, you'll need a different one at L2.

### Recommended Path

- **L1:** Python Programming Fundamentals (builds foundation for later levels, complements the quant methods curriculum)
- **L2:** Analyst Skills or Python, Data Science & AI
- **L3:** Portfolio Development & Construction

## Primary Study Materials

### Already Have

### Worth Considering

**Mark Meldrum (markmeldrum.com)**
- Best-in-class video lectures, widely regarded as the top third-party resource
- Self-Study package: **US$419** (L1, 2026)
- Self-Study + Applied Analysis: US$709
- Instructor-Led Online: US$1,259
- Section access available if you only need specific topics
- **Verdict:** If you want video instruction, this is the one. The free YouTube samples give a good feel for his teaching style before committing.

### Udemy — Not Recommended as Primary

Unlike PMP, Udemy courses don't cut it for CFA. The curriculum is too broad and deep. The consensus on 300hours.com and CFA forums is clear: Udemy is supplementary at best.

**If you want extra practice questions:**
- [CFA Level 1: 1500 MCQs with Solutions](https://www.udemy.com/course/cfa-level-1-2026-1500-mcqs-with-solutions/) — decent for drilling, but the practice app already covers this
- [365 Careers Bootcamp](https://www.udemy.com/course/level-1-cfa-exam-prep-bootcamp/) — reasonable overview, split into two paid parts

**Better alternatives for practice questions:**
- **UWorld (formerly Wiley)** — 2,400+ questions, exam-realistic interface, 7-day free trial
- **CFAI Learning Ecosystem** — included with registration, practice questions from the source

## Study Time

Standard guidance is ~300 hours per level, typically starting 6 months before the exam.
