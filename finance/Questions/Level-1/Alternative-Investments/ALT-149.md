# Question

Cryptocurrency yield generation through DeFi protocols involves:

## Options

- **A**: No risk to principal investment
- **B**: Providing liquidity or staking tokens in exchange for protocol rewards
- **C**: Guaranteed returns from government backing

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Crypto yield generation:

**Methods:**
- Staking (proof-of-stake networks)
- Liquidity provision
- Lending protocols
- Yield farming

**Staking:**
- Lock tokens to validate
- Earn network rewards
- Slashing risk for misbehavior

**Liquidity provision:**
- Supply tokens to DEX pools
- Earn trading fees
- Impermanent loss risk

**Lending:**
- Deposit tokens
- Borrowers pay interest
- Smart contract risk

**Risks:**
- Smart contract exploits
- Impermanent loss
- Protocol failure
- Token price decline
- No deposit insurance

**Typical yields:**
- Vary widely (2-20%+)
- Higher yield = higher risk
- Unsustainable rates red flag

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Significant risks exist
- Option C: No government backing
