# Question

Recallable distributions in private equity allow the GP to:

## Options

- **A**: Permanently keep all distributed capital
- **B**: Request previously distributed capital back from LPs for follow-on investments
- **C**: Increase the total commitment amount

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Recallable distributions:

**Purpose:**
- Fund follow-on investments
- Protect existing investments
- Avoid separate capital calls
- Capital efficiency

**How it works:**
- Fund distributes capital
- LP receives distribution
- GP can recall portion
- For approved purposes

**Common limits:**
- Typically up to commitment
- Time-limited window
- Specific use restrictions
- Subject to LPA terms

**When used:**
- Add-on acquisitions
- Portfolio company support
- Dividend recaps reversed
- Growth capital needs

**LP planning:**
- Cannot assume finality
- Reserve requirements
- Cash flow uncertainty
- Track recallable amounts

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: May be recalled
- Option C: Total commitment unchanged
