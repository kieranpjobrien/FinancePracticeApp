# Question

Alternative investments may provide diversification benefits because:

## Options

- **A**: They always have negative correlation with stocks
- **B**: Their returns may have low correlation with traditional asset classes
- **C**: They eliminate all portfolio risk

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Diversification from alternatives:

**Correlation characteristics:**
- Often low correlation with stocks/bonds
- Different return drivers
- Exposure to unique risk factors
- May dampen portfolio volatility

**Why low correlation:**
- Private markets: Different pricing dynamics
- Real assets: Inflation sensitivity
- Hedge funds: Market-neutral strategies
- Commodities: Supply/demand fundamentals

**Caution needed:**
- Correlations can increase in crises
- Smoothed returns may understate correlation
- Strategy-dependent variation
- Not guaranteed diversification

**Portfolio impact:**
- Potential risk reduction
- Improved risk-adjusted returns
- Access to different return streams

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Correlation isn't always negative
- Option C: No investment eliminates all risk
