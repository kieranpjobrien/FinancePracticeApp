# Question

Due diligence for alternative investments is particularly important because:

## Options

- **A**: Regulatory oversight is typically stronger than for traditional investments
- **B**: Performance dispersion between top and bottom managers is much wider
- **C**: All alternative investments have the same risk-return profiles

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Why due diligence matters in alternatives:

**Manager dispersion:**
- Top quartile vs bottom quartile: 20%+ difference
- Much wider than traditional asset managers
- Manager skill matters enormously
- Unlike index-tracking strategies

**Limited transparency:**
- Less regulatory disclosure
- Complex strategies hard to evaluate
- Proprietary information protected
- Verification more difficult

**Key due diligence areas:**
- Track record verification
- Investment process understanding
- Operational infrastructure
- Key person risk
- Fee structure analysis

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Less regulatory oversight, not more
- Option C: Wide variation in risk-return profiles
