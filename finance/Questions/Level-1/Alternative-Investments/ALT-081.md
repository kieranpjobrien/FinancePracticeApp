# Question

Hedge funds are best characterized as:

## Options

- **A**: Mutual funds available to all retail investors
- **B**: Pooled investment vehicles with flexible strategies and limited regulation
- **C**: Government-guaranteed investment products

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Hedge fund characteristics:

**Key features:**
- Pooled investment vehicle
- Flexible investment mandate
- Limited regulatory oversight
- Accredited investors only

**Strategy flexibility:**
- Long and short positions
- Leverage permitted
- Derivatives usage
- Concentrated positions

**Structure:**
- Limited partnership typical
- Offshore and onshore
- Lock-up periods
- Performance-based fees

**What they can do:**
- Short selling
- Use leverage
- Trade any asset class
- Employ complex strategies

**Regulatory status:**
- Exempt from '40 Act (US)
- Private placement
- Limited disclosure
- Qualified purchasers

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Not available to retail investors
- Option C: No government guarantee
