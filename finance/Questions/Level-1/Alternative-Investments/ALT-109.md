# Question

Regulatory considerations for digital asset investments include:

## Options

- **A**: Uniform global regulations that apply everywhere identically
- **B**: Varying and evolving regulations across jurisdictions
- **C**: Complete absence of any regulatory oversight

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Digital asset regulation:

**Current landscape:**
- Jurisdiction-specific rules
- Rapidly evolving
- Uncertainty remains
- Enforcement increasing

**Key regulatory areas:**
- Securities classification
- Tax treatment
- AML/KYC requirements
- Exchange licensing
- Custody rules

**Jurisdictional differences:**
- US: SEC/CFTC oversight
- EU: MiCA framework
- Asia: Varied approaches
- Some countries ban crypto

**Investor implications:**
- Compliance requirements
- Tax reporting obligations
- Access restrictions
- Legal uncertainty

**Trends:**
- Increasing regulation
- Institutional frameworks
- Stablecoin focus
- Consumer protection

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Highly fragmented globally
- Option C: Significant and growing regulation
