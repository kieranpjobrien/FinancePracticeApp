# Question

The secondary market for alternative investments allows:

## Options

- **A**: Funds to issue new shares to investors
- **B**: Existing investors to sell their fund interests before maturity
- **C**: Only institutional investors to participate

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Secondary market in alternatives:

**What it provides:**
- Liquidity for existing investors
- Exit before fund termination
- Transfer of LP interests
- Price discovery for private assets

**Common reasons to sell:**
- Portfolio rebalancing needs
- Regulatory capital requirements
- Change in investment strategy
- Liquidity needs

**Pricing:**
- Often at discount to NAV
- Discount varies by asset quality
- Market conditions affect pricing
- Typical discounts: 5-20%

**Market participants:**
- Dedicated secondary funds
- Other institutional investors
- Specialized intermediaries

**Growth trend:**
- Market has grown significantly
- Provides important liquidity function
- Increasing institutional acceptance

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: This describes primary market
- Option C: Accredited individuals can participate
