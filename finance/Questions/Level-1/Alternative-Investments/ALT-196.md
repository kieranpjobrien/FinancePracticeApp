# Question

Base case projections in infrastructure investment analysis typically:

## Options

- **A**: Represent the worst possible outcome
- **B**: Reflect management's most likely expectations based on reasonable assumptions
- **C**: Guarantee investment returns

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Base case modeling:

**Definition:**
- Most likely scenario
- Reasonable assumptions
- Neither optimistic nor pessimistic
- Foundation for valuation

**Key assumptions include:**
- Traffic/demand forecasts
- Inflation rates
- Operating costs
- Capital expenditure timing
- Financing terms

**Scenario analysis:**
- Base case: Expected outcome
- Upside: Optimistic scenario
- Downside: Conservative scenario
- Stress: Severe adverse case

**Why important:**
- Valuation foundation
- Return expectations
- Risk assessment
- Investment committee review

**Common adjustments:**
- Revenue haircuts
- Cost inflation
- Delay factors
- Demand scenarios

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Base is most likely, not worst
- Option C: Projections never guarantee returns
