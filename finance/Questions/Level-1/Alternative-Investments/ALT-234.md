# Question

Blockchain bridges facilitate:

## Options

- **A**: Connecting cryptocurrencies to traditional banking systems only
- **B**: Transfer of assets and data between different blockchain networks
- **C**: Mining of new cryptocurrencies

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Blockchain bridges:

**Purpose:**
- Connect separate blockchains
- Transfer assets cross-chain
- Share data between networks
- Enable interoperability

**How they work:**
- Lock assets on source chain
- Mint equivalent on destination
- Reverse process to return
- Various trust models

**Types:**
- Trusted (centralized)
- Trustless (decentralized)
- Federated (multi-sig)
- Light client

**Use cases:**
- Move tokens between chains
- Access DeFi on other networks
- Escape high fees
- Portfolio diversification

**Risks:**
- Bridge hacks (significant)
- Smart contract vulnerabilities
- Centralization concerns
- Liquidity fragmentation

**Notable exploits:**
- Multiple billion-dollar hacks
- High-value targets
- Security critical

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Cross-blockchain, not just banking
- Option C: Different from mining
