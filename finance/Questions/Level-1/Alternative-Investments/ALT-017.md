# Question

Co-investment opportunities in alternative investments allow LPs to:

## Options

- **A**: Reduce their total exposure to the main fund
- **B**: Invest alongside the fund in specific deals, often with reduced fees
- **C**: Replace the GP in managing certain investments

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Co-investment explained:

**Definition:**
- Direct investment alongside main fund
- In specific portfolio companies
- Offered to existing LPs
- Usually larger deals

**Fee advantages:**
- Reduced or no management fee
- Reduced or no carry
- Lower total cost of investment
- Significant savings on large deals

**Why GPs offer co-invest:**
- Access larger deals
- Strengthen LP relationships
- Reduce concentration risk
- Competitive advantage in fundraising

**LP considerations:**
- Increased exposure to single deals
- Less diversification
- Need deal evaluation capability
- Timing and speed requirements

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Co-invest increases total exposure
- Option C: GP retains full management control
