# Question

Which of the following is NOT typically classified as an alternative investment?

## Options

- **A**: Private equity
- **B**: Investment-grade corporate bonds
- **C**: Hedge funds

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Alternative investment classification:

**Alternative investments include:**
- Private equity and venture capital
- Hedge funds
- Real estate (private)
- Infrastructure
- Natural resources/commodities
- Digital assets

**Traditional investments include:**
- Publicly traded stocks
- Investment-grade bonds
- Government securities
- Money market instruments

**Why bonds are traditional:**
- Publicly traded
- High liquidity
- Transparent pricing
- Standardized structures

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Private equity is alternative
- Option C: Hedge funds are alternative
