# Question

Private equity investors typically seek board representation to:

## Options

- **A**: Reduce their involvement in the company
- **B**: Monitor and influence company strategy and operations
- **C**: Avoid any fiduciary responsibilities

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Board representation:

**Purpose:**
- Strategic oversight
- Monitor management
- Protect investment
- Influence decisions

**PE board involvement:**
- Regular meetings
- Committee participation
- CEO relationship
- Performance monitoring

**Key responsibilities:**
- Strategy approval
- CEO selection/evaluation
- Major transaction approval
- Financial oversight

**Value-add activities:**
- Strategic guidance
- Network connections
- Operational expertise
- M&A support

**Governance structure:**
- Majority control (buyouts)
- Minority board seats (growth)
- Observer rights (some VC)
- Protective provisions

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Active involvement sought
- Option C: Board has fiduciary duties
