# Question

Redemption gates in hedge funds:

## Options

- **A**: Guarantee investors can withdraw capital at any time
- **B**: Limit the amount investors can redeem in a given period
- **C**: Are illegal under securities regulations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Redemption gates explained:

**Purpose:**
- Prevent fire sale of assets
- Protect remaining investors
- Manage liquidity mismatch
- Orderly redemption process

**How gates work:**
- Limit redemptions per period
- Example: 10-25% of NAV per quarter
- Pro-rata across redemption requests
- Activated at manager discretion

**When used:**
- Market stress periods
- Large redemption requests
- Illiquid portfolio positions
- Protect fund integrity

**Investor impact:**
- Cannot exit immediately
- Withdrawal spread over time
- May take multiple quarters
- Uncertainty during crisis

**2008 example:**
- Many funds gated
- Investors trapped
- Highlighted liquidity risk

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Gates specifically limit withdrawal
- Option C: Legal and common provision
