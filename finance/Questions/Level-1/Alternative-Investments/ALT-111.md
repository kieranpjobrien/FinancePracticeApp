# Question

Operational due diligence for alternative investments focuses on:

## Options

- **A**: Only the investment strategy and past returns
- **B**: Business operations, controls, and infrastructure supporting the investment process
- **C**: Marketing materials and brand reputation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Operational due diligence:

**Key areas:**
- Organizational structure
- Compliance and controls
- Technology infrastructure
- Valuation processes
- Trade operations

**Why it matters:**
- Fraud prevention (Madoff lesson)
- Operational failures can devastate
- Separate from investment skill
- Protects investor interests

**Red flags:**
- Weak segregation of duties
- Related party service providers
- Unusual auditor
- Concentrated ownership
- Poor documentation

**Best practices:**
- Independent administrator
- Reputable auditor
- Robust compliance program
- Documented procedures
- Regular testing

**Operational failures examples:**
- Madoff: Complete fraud
- Bayou: Fabricated returns
- Various hedge fund blowups

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Investment DD is separate
- Option C: Marketing isn't operational review
