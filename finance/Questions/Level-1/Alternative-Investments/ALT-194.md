# Question

Drag-along rights in venture capital allow:

## Options

- **A**: Minority shareholders to block any sale
- **B**: Majority shareholders to force minority shareholders to participate in a sale
- **C**: Employees to purchase company shares

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Drag-along rights:

**Definition:**
- Majority can compel minority
- To sell in approved transaction
- Same terms and price
- Facilitates clean exit

**Purpose:**
- Enable company sales
- Prevent minority holdout
- Clean transaction execution
- Buyer certainty

**How it works:**
- Majority approves sale
- All shareholders must sell
- Same price per share
- Binding obligation

**Typical threshold:**
- Majority of preferred
- Sometimes supermajority
- Board approval
- Defined in shareholder agreement

**Contrast with tag-along:**
- Tag-along: Right to join sale
- Drag-along: Obligation to join
- Opposite dynamics

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Drag-along prevents blocking
- Option C: Describes employee options
