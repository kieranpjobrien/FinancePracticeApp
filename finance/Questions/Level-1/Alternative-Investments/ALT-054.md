# Question

A key requirement for REIT (Real Estate Investment Trust) status is:

## Options

- **A**: Investing exclusively in residential properties
- **B**: Distributing at least 90% of taxable income to shareholders
- **C**: Maintaining zero debt on properties

## Answer

B

## Explanation

REIT requirements:

**Distribution requirement:**
- 90%+ of taxable income
- Distributed as dividends
- Avoids entity-level taxation
- Pass-through structure

**Other requirements:**
- 75%+ assets in real estate
- 75%+ income from real estate
- 100+ shareholders
- No concentrated ownership

**Types of REITs:**
- Equity REITs: Own properties
- Mortgage REITs: Own loans
- Hybrid REITs: Both

**Benefits of structure:**
- Tax efficiency
- Liquidity (if public)
- Diversification
- Professional management

**Investor considerations:**
- High dividend income
- Limited internal growth
- Interest rate sensitivity

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: REITs can own any property type
- Option C: REITs often use leverage
