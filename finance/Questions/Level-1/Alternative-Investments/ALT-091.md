# Question

A hedge fund-of-funds provides investors with:

## Options

- **A**: Lower total fees than direct hedge fund investment
- **B**: Diversification across multiple hedge fund managers and strategies
- **C**: Guaranteed positive returns

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Hedge fund-of-funds:

**Benefits:**
- Manager diversification
- Strategy diversification
- Due diligence outsourced
- Lower minimums
- Access to closed funds

**Diversification value:**
- Multiple managers (10-30+)
- Various strategies
- Different styles/approaches
- Risk reduction

**Fee consideration:**
- Additional layer of fees
- FOF fee + underlying fees
- "1 and 10" plus "2 and 20"
- Significant fee drag

**Due diligence:**
- Professional evaluation
- Ongoing monitoring
- Manager access
- Risk management

**Suitable for:**
- Smaller institutions
- New hedge fund investors
- Those lacking internal resources
- Seeking diversified exposure

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Higher total fees, not lower
- Option C: No return guarantees exist
