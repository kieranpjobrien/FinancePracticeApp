# Question

Digital assets as an alternative investment category primarily include:

## Options

- **A**: Traditional bank deposits and CDs
- **B**: Cryptocurrencies, tokens, and blockchain-based assets
- **C**: Digital copies of stock certificates

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Digital assets defined:

**Main categories:**
- Cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum)
- Utility tokens
- Security tokens
- NFTs (non-fungible tokens)
- Stablecoins

**Key characteristics:**
- Blockchain-based
- Cryptographic security
- Decentralized (often)
- Digital native

**Investment vehicles:**
- Direct ownership
- Crypto funds
- ETFs (emerging)
- Public companies with exposure

**Investment thesis:**
- New asset class
- Technological innovation
- Decentralization value
- Store of value (Bitcoin)

**Risks:**
- Extreme volatility
- Regulatory uncertainty
- Technology risks
- Custody challenges

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Traditional banking, not digital assets
- Option C: Digitized securities are different concept
