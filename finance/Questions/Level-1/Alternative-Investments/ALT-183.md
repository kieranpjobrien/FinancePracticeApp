# Question

Investing in natural resource company stocks versus commodity futures differs because:

## Options

- **A**: Company stocks have identical returns to commodity prices
- **B**: Stocks include company-specific risks like management and operations
- **C**: Futures have no connection to commodity prices

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Equities vs commodities:

**Commodity producer equities include:**
- Commodity price exposure
- Operational efficiency
- Management quality
- Reserve quality
- Cost structure
- Balance sheet strength

**Equity-specific factors:**
- Company management
- Production growth
- Exploration success
- Acquisition activity
- Dividend policy

**Correlation differences:**
- Not perfect correlation
- May outperform/underperform commodity
- Leverage to commodity price
- Operating margin matters

**Example - gold miners:**
- Gold price rises 10%
- Miner may rise 15-25%
- Operating leverage effect
- But company issues can hurt

**Futures advantages:**
- Pure commodity exposure
- No company risk
- Easier to trade

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Returns can diverge significantly
- Option C: Futures track commodity prices
