# Question

Hedge fund seeding arrangements typically involve:

## Options

- **A**: Planting agricultural commodities
- **B**: Early capital provided to new managers in exchange for fee discounts or revenue sharing
- **C**: Government funding for hedge funds

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Hedge fund seeding:

**Definition:**
- Initial capital for new managers
- Launch vehicle
- Seed investor gets benefits
- Helps new funds start

**Seeder benefits:**
- Discounted fees
- Revenue sharing
- Capacity rights
- Co-investment opportunities

**Manager benefits:**
- Day-one AUM
- Credibility boost
- Operational support
- Focus on investing

**Typical terms:**
- 2-5 year commitment
- Revenue share 10-25%
- Fee discounts
- First-loss provisions possible

**Seeder types:**
- Dedicated seeding firms
- Family offices
- Institutions
- Accelerator platforms

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Financial seeding, not agriculture
- Option C: Private capital, not government
