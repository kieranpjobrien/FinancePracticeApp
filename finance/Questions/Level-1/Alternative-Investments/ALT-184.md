# Question

Sector-focused hedge funds:

## Options

- **A**: Must invest across all sectors equally
- **B**: Concentrate investments in specific industries like technology or healthcare
- **C**: Only invest in government bonds

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Sector-focused strategies:

**Common sectors:**
- Technology
- Healthcare/biotech
- Financial services
- Energy
- Consumer

**Why sector focus:**
- Deep expertise
- Information advantage
- Industry relationships
- Specialized knowledge

**Strategy approaches:**
- Long/short within sector
- Event-driven sector specific
- Technical trading
- Fundamental research

**Advantages:**
- Expert knowledge
- Better security selection
- Industry network
- Pattern recognition

**Risks:**
- Concentration risk
- Sector downturns
- Correlation with sector
- Less diversification

**Investor consideration:**
- Portfolio allocation
- Sector view alignment
- Manager expertise

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Concentrated, not diversified
- Option C: Equity-focused typically
