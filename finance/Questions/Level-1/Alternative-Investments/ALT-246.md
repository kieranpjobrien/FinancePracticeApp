# Question

OPEC's influence on oil markets comes from:

## Options

- **A**: Setting retail gasoline prices directly
- **B**: Coordinating production levels among member countries to influence supply and prices
- **C**: Controlling all global oil reserves

## Answer

B

## Explanation

OPEC dynamics:

**What OPEC does:**
- Sets production quotas
- Coordinates output
- Manages supply
- Influences prices

**Member countries:**
- Saudi Arabia (largest)
- Iraq, Iran, UAE, Kuwait
- Others in Middle East, Africa, South America
- OPEC+ includes Russia

**Market influence:**
- ~30-40% of global production
- Price signaling
- Supply management
- Spare capacity holder

**Tools:**
- Production cuts
- Production increases
- Quota adjustments
- Emergency meetings

**Limitations:**
- Non-OPEC competition
- Shale production
- Demand fluctuations
- Member compliance

**Investment implications:**
- Oil price volatility
- Geopolitical factor
- Supply forecasting input

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Doesn't set retail prices
- Option C: Controls significant, not all reserves
