# Question

The main commercial real estate property types include:

## Options

- **A**: Only residential apartments
- **B**: Office, retail, industrial, and multifamily
- **C**: Exclusively hotel and hospitality properties

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Commercial real estate sectors:

**Core property types:**
1. Office: Business workspaces
2. Retail: Shopping centers, stores
3. Industrial: Warehouses, logistics
4. Multifamily: Apartment buildings

**Other sectors:**
- Hotels/Hospitality
- Healthcare facilities
- Self-storage
- Data centers
- Student housing

**Sector characteristics differ:**
- Lease lengths
- Tenant quality
- Cyclicality
- Capital requirements

**Recent trends:**
- Industrial growth (e-commerce)
- Office challenges (remote work)
- Retail disruption
- Multifamily stability

**Investment considerations:**
- Sector allocation decisions
- Geographic diversification
- Economic sensitivity
- Supply/demand dynamics

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Apartments are one type, not only
- Option C: Hotels are one sector among many
