# Question

Compared to traditional asset classes, cryptocurrencies are characterized by:

## Options

- **A**: Very low volatility and stable returns
- **B**: Extremely high volatility with potential for large gains and losses
- **C**: Guaranteed positive returns over time

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Cryptocurrency volatility:

**Volatility levels:**
- Bitcoin: 50-100%+ annualized volatility
- Altcoins: Often higher
- Stocks: ~15-20% typically
- Bonds: ~5-10% typically

**Why so volatile:**
- Nascent asset class
- Speculative trading
- Limited liquidity (some)
- Sentiment-driven
- No fundamental anchor

**Historical drawdowns:**
- Multiple 80%+ declines
- 2022: ~75% BTC decline
- Rapid recoveries possible
- Cyclical patterns

**Implications for investors:**
- Position sizing critical
- Stomach for volatility
- Long-term perspective needed
- Diversification important

**Risk management:**
- Small portfolio allocation
- Dollar-cost averaging
- Secure custody
- Exit discipline

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Extremely high volatility
- Option C: No guarantee of positive returns
