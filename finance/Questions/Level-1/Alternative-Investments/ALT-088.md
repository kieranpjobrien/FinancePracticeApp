# Question

Managed futures (CTA) strategies primarily:

## Options

- **A**: Invest only in physical commodities
- **B**: Trade futures contracts across asset classes based on systematic rules
- **C**: Focus exclusively on currency hedging

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Managed futures/CTAs:

**Strategy characteristics:**
- Trade futures/forwards
- Systematic/quantitative
- Multi-asset class
- Often trend-following

**Assets traded:**
- Commodities
- Currencies
- Equity indices
- Interest rates

**Common approaches:**
- Trend following (most common)
- Mean reversion
- Pattern recognition
- Fundamental quantitative

**Trend following:**
- Buy assets in uptrends
- Short assets in downtrends
- Momentum-based signals
- Time series analysis

**Portfolio benefits:**
- Low correlation with stocks
- "Crisis alpha" potential
- Positive in market stress
- Tail risk protection

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Futures, not physical commodities
- Option C: Multi-asset, not just currencies
