# Question

Which characteristic most distinguishes alternative investments from traditional investments?

## Options

- **A**: Higher expected returns
- **B**: Lower liquidity and less transparency
- **C**: Lower risk profiles

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Distinguishing features of alternatives:

**Key differences:**
- Lower liquidity: Harder to buy/sell quickly
- Less transparency: Limited public information
- Complex structures: Often use leverage, derivatives
- Higher fees: Management + performance fees

**Traditional investments:**
- Publicly traded stocks and bonds
- Daily pricing and liquidity
- Regulated disclosure requirements
- Standardized structures

**Why B is correct:**
- Illiquidity is defining feature
- Private markets lack transparency
- These drive illiquidity premiums

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Returns may be higher but not defining
- Option C: Alternatives often have higher risk
