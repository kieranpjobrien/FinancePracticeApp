# Question

Renewable energy infrastructure investments have grown due to:

## Options

- **A**: Declining government support for clean energy
- **B**: Energy transition policies, technology improvements, and ESG considerations
- **C**: Lack of investor interest in sustainable investments

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Renewable energy growth drivers:

**Policy support:**
- Climate commitments
- Renewable mandates
- Carbon pricing
- Subsidies and incentives

**Technology improvements:**
- Solar cost decline (90%+ since 2009)
- Wind efficiency gains
- Battery storage advances
- Grid integration improvements

**ESG considerations:**
- Investor demand for sustainable assets
- Pension fund mandates
- Divestment from fossil fuels
- Impact investing growth

**Investment characteristics:**
- Contracted cash flows (PPAs)
- Long asset lives
- Government support
- Growing demand

**Risks:**
- Subsidy changes
- Technology obsolescence
- Weather/resource variability
- Grid curtailment

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Strong and growing government support
- Option C: High investor interest in renewables
