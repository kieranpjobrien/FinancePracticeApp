# Question

A company pursuing a cost leadership strategy typically:

## Options

- **A**: Charges premium prices for unique products
- **B**: Achieves competitive advantage through operational efficiency and economies of scale
- **C**: Focuses on a narrow market segment

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Cost leadership: compete by being low-cost producer. Requires operational efficiency, scale economies, tight cost control, efficient distribution. Enables profitable pricing at or below competitors. Risk: technology changes or competitors achieving similar costs. Example: Walmart, Southwest Airlines.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes differentiation strategy
- Option C describes focus strategy
