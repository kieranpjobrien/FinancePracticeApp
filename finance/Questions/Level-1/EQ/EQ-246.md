# Question

The EV/Sales ratio is particularly useful for valuing companies that:

## Options

- **A**: Have consistent positive profit margins
- **B**: Are unprofitable or have highly variable margins
- **C**: Pay high dividends

## Answer

B

## Explanation

EV/Sales advantages: always positive (unlike earnings), comparable across firms with different margins, less affected by accounting choices. Useful for: early-stage companies, unprofitable firms, cyclical businesses with volatile margins. Limitation: ignores profitability entirely—use with margin analysis.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—profitable firms can use EBITDA or earnings multiples
- Option C—dividend metrics more relevant for income investors
