# Question

The DAX Index tracks:

## Options

- **A**: US technology stocks
- **B**: 40 largest German blue-chip companies on Frankfurt Stock Exchange
- **C**: Asian emerging market equities

## Answer

B

## Explanation

DAX (Deutscher Aktienindex): Germany's premier stock index. 40 largest liquid companies on Frankfurt Stock Exchange (expanded from 30 in 2021). Performance index including dividends. Major European benchmark. Market-cap weighted with caps on individual weights.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes NASDAQ or similar
- Option C describes Asian indices like MSCI EM Asia
