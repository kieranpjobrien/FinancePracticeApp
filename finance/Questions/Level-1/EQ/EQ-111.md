# Question

The equity risk premium represents:

## Options

- **A**: The guaranteed return from investing in stocks
- **B**: The excess return investors require for bearing equity market risk above the risk-free rate
- **C**: The dividend yield on the market index

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Equity risk premium (ERP): compensation for bearing systematic risk of equity investment. ERP = Expected equity return - Risk-free rate. Estimated historically (realized returns) or implied from current prices and earnings forecasts. Key input for cost of equity (CAPM) and valuation models.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores that equity returns are uncertain
- Option C is one component of return, not the risk premium
