# Question

The key function of secondary markets is to provide:

## Options

- **A**: Capital directly to issuing companies
- **B**: Liquidity for investors to trade existing securities
- **C**: Underwriting services for new issues

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Secondary market functions: liquidity (ability to buy/sell), price discovery, transaction efficiency. Investors can exit positions without waiting for maturity or company buyback. Active secondary markets support primary markets—investors more willing to buy new issues knowing they can later sell.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes primary market function
- Option C describes investment banking/underwriting services
