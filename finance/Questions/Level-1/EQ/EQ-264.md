# Question

Limited liability for equity shareholders means:

## Options

- **A**: Shareholders can never lose money on their investment
- **B**: Shareholders' losses are limited to their invested amount
- **C**: Shareholders are personally liable for company debts

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Limited liability: maximum loss is investment amount. Personal assets protected from corporate obligations. Key feature enabling broad share ownership and risk-taking. Contrasts with partnerships where partners may have unlimited liability. Foundation of modern corporate structure.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—can lose entire investment, just not more
- Option C describes unlimited liability, the opposite
