# Question

Cash burn rate and runway analysis is most critical for:

## Options

- **A**: Mature profitable companies
- **B**: Early-stage or unprofitable companies that consume cash
- **C**: Companies with no operating expenses

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Cash burn: rate of cash consumption for unprofitable companies. Runway: cash / monthly burn = months until cash exhausted. Critical for: startups, biotech, distressed companies. Determines financing needs and survival. Analysts assess burn rate trends and runway to evaluate viability.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—profitable companies generate, not burn cash
- Option C—all operating companies have expenses
