# Question

Analyzing management compensation structures is important because:

## Options

- **A**: Compensation has no effect on management behavior
- **B**: Incentive structures influence what metrics management prioritizes
- **C**: All compensation plans are identical

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Compensation analysis: examines how incentives align with shareholder interests. Stock-based = aligned with price. EPS targets may encourage buybacks. Revenue focus may ignore profitability. Short-term bonuses may conflict with long-term value. Good governance includes well-designed incentives.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores powerful incentive effects
- Option C—compensation structures vary significantly
