# Question

According to Porter's Five Forces, industry profitability is maximized when:

## Options

- **A**: Rivalry is intense, buyers are powerful, and entry barriers are low
- **B**: Entry barriers are high, supplier/buyer power is low, substitutes are weak, and rivalry is moderate
- **C**: Many substitutes exist and switching costs are minimal

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Favorable industry structure: high barriers (protect profits), low supplier power (keep input costs down), low buyer power (maintain pricing), weak substitutes (reduce alternatives), moderate rivalry (avoid destructive competition). All five forces weak = high profit potential for industry participants.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes unfavorable conditions reducing profitability
- Option C describes high substitution threat, reducing industry returns
