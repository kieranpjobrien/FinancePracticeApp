# Question

Strong brand value creates competitive advantage by:

## Options

- **A**: Only affecting marketing expenses
- **B**: Enabling premium pricing, customer loyalty, and reduced customer acquisition costs
- **C**: Eliminating the need for product quality

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Brand advantages: pricing power (consumers pay more for trusted brands), loyalty (repeat purchases, lower churn), lower marketing costs relative to unknown brands, entry barrier for competitors. Brand is intangible asset—may not appear on balance sheet but creates real economic value.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A undersells strategic importance
- Option C—brand must be supported by quality, otherwise erodes
