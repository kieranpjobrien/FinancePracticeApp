# Question

A "buy" recommendation from an analyst typically indicates:

## Options

- **A**: The stock is expected to underperform
- **B**: The stock is expected to appreciate in value and outperform
- **C**: The analyst has sold their personal holdings

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Buy rating: analyst expects stock to outperform—appreciation expected to exceed alternative investments or benchmark. Ratings vary by firm: buy/overweight/outperform vs. hold/neutral vs. sell/underweight/underperform. Based on upside to target price and risk assessment.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes sell recommendation
- Option C relates to analyst trading, not recommendation meaning
