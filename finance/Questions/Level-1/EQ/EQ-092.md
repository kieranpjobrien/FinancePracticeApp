# Question

Buyer bargaining power is high when:

## Options

- **A**: Buyers are fragmented with small individual purchases
- **B**: Buyers are concentrated, purchase large volumes, and can easily switch suppliers
- **C**: The product is highly differentiated with few substitutes

## Answer

B

## Explanation

High buyer power: concentrated buyers, large purchase volumes, low switching costs, credible backward integration threat, undifferentiated products. Powerful buyers can negotiate lower prices, better terms, or higher quality, squeezing supplier margins. Retail consolidation has increased buyer power in many industries.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes weak buyer power
- Option C also describes weak buyer power
