# Question

The MSCI EAFE Index represents:

## Options

- **A**: US large-cap stocks only
- **B**: Developed markets outside North America (Europe, Australasia, Far East)
- **C**: Emerging market equities

## Answer

B

## Explanation

MSCI EAFE: Europe, Australasia, Far East—developed markets excluding US and Canada. Major international equity benchmark. Float-adjusted market-cap weighted. Includes Japan, UK, France, Germany, Australia, etc. Used to benchmark international developed market allocations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes US-focused indices like S&P 500
- Option C describes MSCI EM (Emerging Markets)
