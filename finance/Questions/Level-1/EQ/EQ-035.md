# Question

A company's economic moat refers to:

## Options

- **A**: The physical security around its facilities
- **B**: Sustainable competitive advantages that protect market share and profitability
- **C**: Its cash reserves

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Economic moat: durable competitive advantages protecting earnings from competition. Sources: brand, patents, network effects, cost advantages, switching costs, regulatory licenses. Wide moat companies can sustain high returns on capital. Critical for long-term investment value. Term popularized by Warren Buffett.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A takes the term literally
- Option C describes financial resources, not competitive position
