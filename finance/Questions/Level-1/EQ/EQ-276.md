# Question

Frontier markets differ from emerging markets primarily in that frontier markets have:

## Options

- **A**: More developed financial markets and higher liquidity
- **B**: Less developed capital markets, lower liquidity, and higher political risk
- **C**: Larger economies and more multinational corporations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Frontier markets: even earlier stage than emerging—smaller economies, less liquid markets, limited foreign investor access, higher risks. Examples: Vietnam (until recent EM upgrade), Nigeria, Kenya, Bangladesh. Higher potential returns but significant operational challenges. MSCI Frontier Markets Index tracks this segment.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes developed vs. emerging distinction
- Option C describes developed or large emerging markets
