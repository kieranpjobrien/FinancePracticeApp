# Question

Market capitalization is calculated as:

## Options

- **A**: Total assets minus total liabilities
- **B**: Stock price multiplied by shares outstanding
- **C**: Annual revenue multiplied by profit margin

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Market cap = Stock Price × Shares Outstanding. Represents total market value of equity. Used to classify companies (large-cap, mid-cap, small-cap). Determines weight in market-cap weighted indices. Changes with stock price movements. Different from enterprise value which includes debt.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes book value of equity
- Option C attempts to derive value from income, not market price
