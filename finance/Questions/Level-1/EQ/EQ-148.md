# Question

The Beneish M-Score is a model designed to detect:

## Options

- **A**: Optimal capital structure
- **B**: Potential earnings manipulation
- **C**: Future stock price movements

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Beneish M-Score: statistical model using financial ratios to identify earnings manipulation. Variables include days sales in receivables index, gross margin index, asset quality index, depreciation index, leverage index, accruals to total assets. Score above -2.22 suggests higher probability of manipulation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A relates to financial structure analysis
- Option C describes stock prediction, not fraud detection
