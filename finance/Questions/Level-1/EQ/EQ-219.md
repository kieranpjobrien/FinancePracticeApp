# Question

The build-up method for estimating required return adds risk premiums to the risk-free rate for:

## Options

- **A**: Only systematic market risk
- **B**: Various risk factors including size, industry, and company-specific risks
- **C**: Only the dividend yield

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Build-up method: r = Rf + Equity risk premium + Size premium + Industry premium + Company-specific risk premium. Alternative to CAPM for private company valuation. Adds premiums for factors not captured in public market beta. More subjective but captures risks CAPM may miss.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes CAPM approach (systematic risk only)
- Option C describes dividend component, not required return
