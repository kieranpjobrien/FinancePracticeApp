# Question

Participating preferred stock differs from non-participating preferred in that holders:

## Options

- **A**: Receive only their fixed dividend regardless of company performance
- **B**: May receive additional dividends beyond the stated rate if common dividends exceed a threshold
- **C**: Have voting rights on all corporate matters

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Participating preferred: receives stated dividend plus may participate in additional distributions with common shareholders. Non-participating: receives only stated dividend, no matter how large common dividends. Participation feature provides upside; typically includes caps on additional participation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes non-participating preferred
- Option C describes voting features, separate from participation
