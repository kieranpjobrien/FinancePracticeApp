# Question

EV/EBITDA is preferred over P/E for comparing companies with:

## Options

- **A**: Identical capital structures and tax rates
- **B**: Different capital structures, as it is unaffected by financing decisions
- **C**: No debt and high cash balances

## Answer

B

## Explanation

EV/EBITDA advantages: capital structure neutral (useful for comparing firms with different leverage), less affected by depreciation differences, less likely to be negative than net income. Preferred for capital-intensive industries, M&A analysis, and cross-border comparisons with varying tax rates.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A doesn't explain the comparative advantage—P/E works fine if structures identical
- Option C describes firms where EV and market cap are similar anyway
