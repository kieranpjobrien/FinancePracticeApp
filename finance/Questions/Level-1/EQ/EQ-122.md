# Question

Dark pools are alternative trading systems that:

## Options

- **A**: Provide complete pre-trade transparency of orders
- **B**: Do not display quotes publicly, reducing information leakage for large orders
- **C**: Only allow retail investors to trade

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Dark pools: alternative trading systems where orders are not displayed publicly before execution. Benefits: reduces market impact and information leakage for institutional investors executing large orders. Trades reported post-execution. Concerns include reduced overall market transparency and price discovery.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is opposite—dark pools specifically lack pre-trade transparency
- Option C is wrong—dark pools primarily serve institutional investors
