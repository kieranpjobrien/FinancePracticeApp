# Question

A top-down investment approach begins with:

## Options

- **A**: Analyzing individual company financials
- **B**: Macroeconomic and market analysis before focusing on sectors and companies
- **C**: Technical chart patterns

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Top-down: start with macroeconomy (GDP, interest rates, inflation), then sectors likely to benefit, then best companies within chosen sectors. Ensures portfolio aligned with economic cycle. Contrasts with bottom-up (individual company analysis first, then portfolio construction).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes bottom-up approach
- Option C is a separate analysis methodology
