# Question

A sector index tracks:

## Options

- **A**: The entire market across all industries
- **B**: Companies within a specific industry or economic sector
- **C**: Only international stocks

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Sector indices: measure performance of specific industry groups—technology, healthcare, financials, energy. Enable sector-specific benchmarking, sector rotation strategies. Examples: S&P 500 Information Technology Index, MSCI Health Care Index. Used for sector ETFs and performance attribution.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes broad market indices
- Option C describes international or regional indices
