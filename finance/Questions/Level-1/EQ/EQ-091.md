# Question

In the decline stage of the industry lifecycle:

## Options

- **A**: New competitors are rapidly entering
- **B**: Demand decreases due to substitutes or changing consumer preferences
- **C**: Companies invest heavily in capacity expansion

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Decline stage: demand falls due to technological obsolescence, substitute products, or changed preferences. Companies exit, consolidate, or pivot. Remaining firms may harvest cash flows with minimal reinvestment. Focus shifts to cost control and cash generation rather than growth.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes growth or early maturity
- Option C describes growth stage behavior
