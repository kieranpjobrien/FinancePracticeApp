# Question

The threat of substitute products is HIGH when:

## Options

- **A**: Switching costs are high and substitutes offer inferior value
- **B**: Substitutes offer similar benefits at competitive prices with low switching costs
- **C**: The industry has strong brand loyalty and differentiation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Substitute threat factors: price-performance of alternatives, switching costs, buyer propensity to substitute. High threat when substitutes offer comparable value, switching is easy, and buyers are price-sensitive. Examples: streaming vs. cable TV, plant-based vs. traditional meat products.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes conditions for LOW substitute threat
- Option C describes industry defenses against substitutes
