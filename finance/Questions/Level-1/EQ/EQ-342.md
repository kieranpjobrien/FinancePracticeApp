# Question

Supplier concentration risk exists when:

## Options

- **A**: A company has many alternative suppliers
- **B**: A company depends on few suppliers for critical inputs
- **C**: Suppliers have no bargaining power

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Supplier concentration: dependence on limited suppliers for key inputs. Risks: supply disruption, price increases, quality issues, reduced bargaining power. Single-source suppliers particularly risky. Evaluate supplier relationships, alternative sources, vertical integration options.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes diversified supply chain (low risk)
- Option C contradicts supplier power from concentration
