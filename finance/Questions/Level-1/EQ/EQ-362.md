# Question

Assessing disruption risk in industry analysis involves evaluating:

## Options

- **A**: Only current competitive dynamics
- **B**: Potential for new technologies, business models, or entrants to fundamentally change the industry
- **C**: Historical stock prices

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Disruption analysis: identifies threats from innovation, new business models, regulatory change, or technology shifts that could undermine incumbent positions. Examples: streaming vs. cable, EVs vs. combustion, fintech vs. banks. Forward-looking assessment beyond current competition.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A misses future threats
- Option C is backward-looking, not forward-looking analysis
