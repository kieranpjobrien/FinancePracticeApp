# Question

Total addressable market (TAM) analysis helps investors by:

## Options

- **A**: Calculating exact future revenues
- **B**: Estimating the maximum revenue opportunity for a company's products or services
- **C**: Determining current market share precisely

## Answer

B

## Explanation

TAM: theoretical maximum market size if 100% share achieved. Used to assess growth runway, validate revenue projections, compare opportunity sizes. SAM (serviceable addressable) and SOM (serviceable obtainable) refine realistic targets. Important for growth company valuation but requires careful methodology.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—TAM is potential, not prediction
- Option C—TAM sizes the market, separate from share calculation
