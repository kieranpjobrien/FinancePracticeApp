# Question

Demographic trends affect industry analysis by:

## Options

- **A**: Having no measurable impact on business fundamentals
- **B**: Influencing demand patterns, labor availability, and consumption preferences
- **C**: Only affecting government policy

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Demographics impact: aging populations (healthcare demand, retirement products), urbanization (real estate, infrastructure), population growth (consumer markets), workforce trends (labor costs, skills availability). Long-term, relatively predictable trends. Affects different industries differently—opportunity for some, threat for others.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores significant fundamental impacts
- Option C undersells direct business effects
