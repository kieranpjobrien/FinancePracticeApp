# Question

Index investing (passive investing) aims to:

## Options

- **A**: Beat the market through stock selection
- **B**: Match the returns of a benchmark index at low cost
- **C**: Only invest in small-cap stocks

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Index/passive investing: replicates index returns rather than trying to outperform. Benefits: low costs (no research, low turnover), tax efficiency, transparency, consistent benchmark-matching. Vehicles: index mutual funds, ETFs. Grows from belief that markets are largely efficient.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes active management
- Option C is arbitrary constraint unrelated to index investing
