# Question

Classifying conglomerates into industry sectors can be challenging because:

## Options

- **A**: Conglomerates only operate in one industry
- **B**: Conglomerates operate across multiple unrelated industries
- **C**: Conglomerates are not publicly traded

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Conglomerate classification: diversified companies span multiple sectors (e.g., Berkshire Hathaway—insurance, utilities, manufacturing, retail). Typically classified by largest revenue segment or as "Diversified Industrials." Sum-of-parts valuation needed. Creates benchmark challenges for performance measurement.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—conglomerates by definition are multi-industry
- Option C—many conglomerates are publicly traded
