# Question

Free cash flow to the firm (FCFF) is discounted using:

## Options

- **A**: Cost of equity
- **B**: Weighted average cost of capital (WACC)
- **C**: Risk-free rate

## Answer

B

## Explanation

FCFF: cash available to all capital providers (debt and equity). Discount at WACC to get firm value. Then subtract debt value to get equity value. FCFE (to equity only) is discounted at cost of equity. Matching cash flows to appropriate discount rate is critical.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is for FCFE, not FCFF
- Option C doesn't reflect risk of cash flows
