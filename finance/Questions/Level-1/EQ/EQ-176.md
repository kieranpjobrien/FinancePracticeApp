# Question

A buy limit order at $50 will only execute when:

## Options

- **A**: The market price rises above $50
- **B**: The market price is at $50 or below
- **C**: The market price equals exactly $50

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Buy limit order: sets maximum purchase price. Executes at limit price or better (lower). Won't pay more than $50. Provides price certainty but no execution guarantee—may not fill if price never reaches limit. Opposite of sell limit (minimum acceptable price).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A would result in paying more than limit—not how limits work
- Option C—orders can execute at better prices too
