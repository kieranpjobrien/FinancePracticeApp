# Question

The FTSE 100 Index represents:

## Options

- **A**: The 100 smallest UK companies
- **B**: The 100 largest UK-listed companies by market capitalization
- **C**: German blue-chip stocks

## Answer

B

## Explanation

FTSE 100: "Footsie"—100 largest companies on London Stock Exchange by market cap. UK's primary equity benchmark. Float-adjusted market-cap weighted. Includes multinational companies with significant non-UK revenues. Reviewed quarterly for constituent changes.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes small-cap segment
- Option C describes DAX (Germany)
