# Question

The CAC 40 is:

## Options

- **A**: A UK bond index
- **B**: A French stock market index of 40 leading companies on Euronext Paris
- **C**: A Japanese small-cap index

## Answer

B

## Explanation

CAC 40 (Cotation Assistée en Continu): France's benchmark stock index. 40 largest companies by market cap on Euronext Paris. Float-adjusted, capped to prevent dominance. Major component of European investment strategies. Includes global multinationals headquartered in France.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes UK fixed income
- Option C describes Japanese equities like Russell/Nomura Small Cap
