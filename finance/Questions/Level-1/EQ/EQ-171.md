# Question

The price-to-sales (P/S) ratio is particularly useful for valuing:

## Options

- **A**: Companies with stable, positive earnings
- **B**: Companies with negative earnings or volatile profits
- **C**: Companies with no revenue

## Answer

B

## Explanation

P/S ratio advantages: always positive (unlike earnings), less volatile than earnings, harder to manipulate than earnings. Useful for: unprofitable companies, cyclical firms with volatile earnings, startups with revenue but no profit. Limitation: ignores cost structure and profitability.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—for stable earnings companies, P/E is typically preferred
- Option C—companies need revenue for P/S to be meaningful
