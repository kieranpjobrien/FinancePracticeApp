# Question

Analyzing segment margin trends is important because:

## Options

- **A**: All segments always have identical margins
- **B**: Different business lines may have varying profitability and growth characteristics
- **C**: Segment data is never disclosed to investors

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Segment analysis: reveals hidden dynamics within diversified companies. High-margin segments may subsidize low-margin ones. Growth segment trends differ from mature segments. Sum-of-parts valuation uses segment-specific multiples. Margin trends indicate competitive position changes within each business line.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores segment diversity
- Option C—segments must be disclosed under accounting standards
