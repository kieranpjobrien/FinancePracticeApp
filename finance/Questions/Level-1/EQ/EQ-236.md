# Question

The Dow Jones Industrial Average (DJIA) is:

## Options

- **A**: A market-cap weighted index of 500 stocks
- **B**: A price-weighted index of 30 large US companies
- **C**: An equal-weighted index of technology stocks

## Answer

B

## Explanation

DJIA: oldest US stock market indicator, 30 large blue-chip companies, price-weighted methodology. Higher-priced stocks have more influence regardless of market cap. Divisor adjusted for splits and component changes. Less representative than S&P 500 but highly recognizable benchmark.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes S&P 500
- Option C describes neither DJIA's weighting nor composition
