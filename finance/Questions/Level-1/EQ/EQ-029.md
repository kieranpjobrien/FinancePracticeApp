# Question

Industry classification systems like GICS (Global Industry Classification Standard) help investors by:

## Options

- **A**: Guaranteeing investment returns
- **B**: Grouping companies with similar business activities for comparison and analysis
- **C**: Setting stock prices

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Industry classification: standardized systems categorizing companies by primary business activity. Enables peer comparison, sector analysis, and portfolio construction. GICS has 11 sectors, 25 industry groups, 74 industries, and 163 sub-industries. Used for benchmarking and index construction.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is unrealistic—classification doesn't affect returns
- Option C is not related to classification systems
