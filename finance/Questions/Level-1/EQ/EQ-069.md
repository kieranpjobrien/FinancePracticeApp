# Question

Supplier bargaining power is high when:

## Options

- **A**: Many suppliers compete for business
- **B**: Suppliers are concentrated and their products are differentiated or essential
- **C**: Switching costs to alternative suppliers are low

## Answer

B

## Explanation

High supplier power: few dominant suppliers, unique/differentiated inputs, high switching costs, credible threat of forward integration. Allows suppliers to raise prices or reduce quality, squeezing industry margins. Opposite conditions (many suppliers, commodity inputs, low switching costs) favor buyers.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes weak supplier power
- Option C also describes weak supplier power
