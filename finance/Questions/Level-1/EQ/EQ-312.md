# Question

Activist investors typically seek to:

## Options

- **A**: Passively hold shares without engaging management
- **B**: Influence company strategy, capital allocation, or governance to unlock value
- **C**: Always support current management decisions

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Activist investing: acquiring significant stake then pushing for changes—strategic shifts, cost cuts, M&A, capital returns, board seats, management changes. Aim to close gap between current and potential value. Can be collaborative or confrontational. Disclosure required when ownership exceeds threshold.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes passive investing
- Option C—activists often challenge management
