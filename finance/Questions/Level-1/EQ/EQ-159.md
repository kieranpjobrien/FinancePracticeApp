# Question

Return on equity (ROE) measures:

## Options

- **A**: A company's ability to generate profits from its total assets
- **B**: How effectively a company uses shareholders' equity to generate profits
- **C**: The ratio of dividends paid to shareholders

## Answer

B

## Explanation

ROE = Net Income / Shareholders' Equity. Measures profitability from shareholders' perspective—how much profit generated per dollar of equity invested. Key metric for comparing companies and assessing management effectiveness. Can be decomposed via DuPont analysis into margin, turnover, and leverage.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes ROA (Return on Assets)
- Option C describes dividend payout ratio
