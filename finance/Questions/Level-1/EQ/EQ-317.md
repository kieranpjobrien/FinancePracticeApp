# Question

A discount for lack of marketability (DLOM) is applied in valuations because:

## Options

- **A**: All securities trade at a discount to intrinsic value
- **B**: Illiquid securities cannot be easily sold and require compensation for this constraint
- **C**: Public companies are worth less than private companies

## Answer

B

## Explanation

DLOM: reduction in value for securities that cannot be quickly converted to cash at fair price. Private company shares, restricted stock, thinly-traded securities. Reflects inability to exit position easily, time to find buyer, transaction costs. Typically ranges 15-35% depending on circumstances.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A overgeneralizes about all securities
- Option C reverses typical relationship
