# Question

The limit-up/limit-down (LULD) mechanism applies to:

## Options

- **A**: Market-wide indices only
- **B**: Individual securities, pausing trading when prices move outside specified bands
- **C**: After-hours trading exclusively

## Answer

B

## Explanation

LULD: individual stock circuit breaker. Price bands set around reference price. Trading pauses if price moves to limit, allowing price discovery and liquidity restoration. Different bands for different price levels and market tiers. Prevents errant trades from causing extreme individual stock moves.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes market-wide circuit breakers
- Option C—LULD applies during regular trading hours
