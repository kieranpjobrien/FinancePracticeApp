# Question

Sector indices are useful for:

## Options

- **A**: Only tracking overall market performance
- **B**: Analyzing performance of specific industry groups and sector rotation strategies
- **C**: Valuing individual companies

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Sector indices: track specific industries (technology, healthcare, financials, etc.). Uses: benchmark sector fund performance, analyze sector rotation, identify leading/lagging sectors, tactical allocation decisions. Examples: S&P 500 sector indices, MSCI sector indices. Enable granular market analysis.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes broad market indices
- Option C—indices provide context but don't directly value companies
