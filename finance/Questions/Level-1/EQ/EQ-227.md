# Question

Increasing globalization of an industry typically leads to:

## Options

- **A**: Reduced competition from foreign producers
- **B**: Greater competitive pressure and more diverse sourcing and customer options
- **C**: Higher profit margins for all participants

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Globalization effects: increased competition (foreign entrants), pressure on labor costs, supply chain options, access to new markets, currency exposure, regulatory complexity. Winners and losers—some gain scale economies and market access, others face margin pressure from low-cost competitors.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is opposite—globalization increases competition
- Option C—globalization often pressures margins through competition
