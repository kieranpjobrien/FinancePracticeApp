# Question

A progressive tax system is one where:

## Options

- **A**: Everyone pays the same percentage of income
- **B**: Higher income earners pay a higher percentage of their income in taxes
- **C**: Lower income earners pay a higher percentage of their income

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A progressive tax takes a larger percentage from higher incomes. If someone earning $50,000 pays 15% while someone earning $200,000 pays 30%, the tax is progressive. Progressive taxation is based on the ability-to-pay principle and acts as an automatic stabilizer. A proportional (flat) tax applies the same rate to all; a regressive tax takes a higher percentage from lower incomes.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes a proportional or flat tax
- Option C describes a regressive tax
