# Question

The current account in the balance of payments includes:

## Options

- **A**: Foreign direct investment and portfolio investment flows
- **B**: Trade in goods and services, income, and unilateral transfers
- **C**: Central bank reserve transactions only

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The current account tracks transactions in goods and services (trade balance), primary income (wages, investment income), and secondary income (remittances, foreign aid, and other transfers). The capital and financial account tracks investment flows. A current account deficit must be financed by a capital/financial account surplus (net capital inflows), linking trade flows to investment flows.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes financial account components
- Option C is only a small part of the overall balance of payments
