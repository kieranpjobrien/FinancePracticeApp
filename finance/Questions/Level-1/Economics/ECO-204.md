# Question

Human capital refers to:

## Options

- **A**: The physical equipment and machinery used in production
- **B**: The knowledge, skills, and health of the workforce
- **C**: The population size and demographic structure

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Human capital encompasses the productive capabilities embodied in people through education, training, experience, and health. Like physical capital, human capital requires investment and depreciates without maintenance. Higher human capital increases labor productivity and is crucial for economic development and adopting new technologies.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes physical capital
- Option C describes demographic factors, not human capital quality
