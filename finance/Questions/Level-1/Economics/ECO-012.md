# Question

Automatic fiscal stabilizers include:

## Options

- **A**: Discretionary government infrastructure projects
- **B**: Progressive income taxes and unemployment benefits
- **C**: Central bank interest rate decisions

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Automatic stabilizers are built-in fiscal mechanisms that adjust without explicit government action. Progressive income taxes automatically reduce tax collections during recessions (as incomes fall) and increase collections during expansions. Unemployment benefits automatically rise during downturns and fall during expansions. These countercyclical effects help stabilize aggregate demand without requiring new legislation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes discretionary fiscal policy, not automatic stabilizers
- Option C describes monetary policy, not fiscal policy
