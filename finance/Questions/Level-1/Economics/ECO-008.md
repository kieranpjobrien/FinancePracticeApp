# Question

Stagflation is characterized by:

## Options

- **A**: Rapid economic growth with high inflation
- **B**: Economic stagnation combined with high inflation
- **C**: Deflation with strong economic growth

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Stagflation is the unusual combination of stagnant economic growth (or recession) with high inflation—conditions that typically don't occur together. It often results from adverse supply shocks (like oil price spikes) that simultaneously reduce output and increase prices. Standard monetary policy faces a dilemma: fighting inflation may worsen recession, while stimulating growth may worsen inflation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes overheating, where strong growth drives inflation
- Option C is not a recognized economic phenomenon
