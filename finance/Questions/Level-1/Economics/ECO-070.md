# Question

Central bank independence is considered important because it:

## Options

- **A**: Ensures elected officials control monetary policy
- **B**: Reduces the temptation to use monetary policy for short-term political gains
- **C**: Eliminates all economic fluctuations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Independent central banks can focus on long-term price stability without political pressure to stimulate the economy before elections or to finance government deficits through money printing. Research shows that countries with more independent central banks tend to have lower and more stable inflation. Independence is typically supported by fixed terms for governors and prohibition on direct government financing.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes politically controlled central banks
- Option C overstates what independence can achieve
