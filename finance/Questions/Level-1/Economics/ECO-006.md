# Question

During the expansion phase of a business cycle, which of the following typically occurs?

## Options

- **A**: Rising unemployment and falling output
- **B**: Increasing employment and rising GDP
- **C**: Deflation and declining consumer confidence

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Expansion is characterized by growing economic activity: increasing real GDP, rising employment, higher consumer and business spending, and improved confidence. Businesses hire more workers, invest in capacity, and output increases. The expansion phase follows a trough and continues until the economy reaches a peak.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes contraction or recession
- Option C describes potential characteristics of a recession or depression
