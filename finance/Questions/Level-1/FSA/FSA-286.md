# Question

Full consolidation is required when an investor has:

## Options

- **A**: 20-50% ownership with significant influence
- **B**: Control over the investee
- **C**: A passive minority investment

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Control (typically >50% voting power but can exist with less) requires full consolidation—combining all assets, liabilities, revenues, and expenses. Significant influence (typically 20-50%) uses the equity method. Passive investments (<20%) use fair value or amortized cost methods. Control means power to direct relevant activities.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes equity method threshold
- Option C describes fair value method investments
