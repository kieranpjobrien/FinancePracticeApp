# Question

A company has purchases of $900,000 and average accounts payable of $75,000. Days payables outstanding is closest to:

## Options

- **A**: 30 days
- **B**: 45 days
- **C**: 60 days

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Payables turnover = Purchases / Average A/P = $900,000 / $75,000 = 12 times. Days payables outstanding = 365 / Turnover = 365 / 12 = 30.4 ≈ 30 days. Higher DPO means slower payment to suppliers—good for cash flow but may strain supplier relationships if excessive.

**Common mistakes:**
- Options B and C result from calculation errors
