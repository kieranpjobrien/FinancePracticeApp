# Question

Which pattern most likely indicates potential revenue quality issues?

## Options

- **A**: Revenue growth tracking industry growth
- **B**: Revenue growing faster than receivables
- **C**: Revenue growing significantly faster than cash collections

## Answer

C

## Explanation

When revenue grows faster than cash collections, earnings quality is questionable. This suggests aggressive revenue recognition (booking sales before cash is certain) or collection problems. High-quality revenue closely tracks cash receipts. DSO (days sales outstanding) increasing is a related warning sign.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A suggests normal, sustainable growth
- Option B indicates improving collections, a positive sign
