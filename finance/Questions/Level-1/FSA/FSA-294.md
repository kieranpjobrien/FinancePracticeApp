# Question

Equipment has gross PPE of $1,000,000 and accumulated depreciation of $600,000. Annual depreciation expense is $100,000. The average age of assets is approximately:

## Options

- **A**: 4 years
- **B**: 6 years
- **C**: 10 years

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Average age = Accumulated depreciation / Annual depreciation expense = $600,000 / $100,000 = 6 years. This approximation assumes straight-line depreciation. Total useful life ≈ Gross PPE / Depreciation = 10 years. Remaining useful life ≈ Net PPE / Depreciation = $400,000 / $100,000 = 4 years.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is remaining useful life, not average age
- Option C is total useful life, not average age
