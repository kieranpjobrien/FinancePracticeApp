# Question

Under US GAAP, an uncertain tax position is recognized only if:

## Options

- **A**: It is more likely than not to be sustained upon examination
- **B**: The amount is certain to be allowed by tax authorities
- **C**: The company has received a favorable private letter ruling

## Answer

A

## Explanation

FIN 48 (ASC 740) requires a two-step approach: (1) recognition threshold—the position must be more likely than not (>50%) to be sustained; (2) measurement—recognized at the largest amount with >50% probability. Uncertain positions failing the threshold are not recognized as benefits.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B sets too high a threshold
- Option C is not required for recognition
