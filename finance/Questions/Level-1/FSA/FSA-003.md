# Question

The two major sets of accounting standards used globally are:

## Options

- **A**: FASB and SEC standards
- **B**: IFRS and US GAAP
- **C**: SOX and PCAOB standards

## Answer

B

## Explanation

IFRS (International Financial Reporting Standards) and US GAAP (Generally Accepted Accounting Principles) are the two dominant global accounting frameworks. IFRS, developed by the IASB, is used in over 140 countries. US GAAP, developed by FASB, is required for US public companies. While convergence efforts continue, significant differences remain.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A lists a standard-setter (FASB) and a regulator (SEC), not complete frameworks
- Option C lists regulatory requirements (SOX) and an oversight body (PCAOB)
