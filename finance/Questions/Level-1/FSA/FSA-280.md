# Question

Under Sarbanes-Oxley, management must:

## Options

- **A**: Only design internal controls over financial reporting
- **B**: Assess and report on the effectiveness of internal controls
- **C**: Have the board conduct all internal control evaluations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

SOX Section 404 requires management to assess internal controls over financial reporting and include a statement of responsibility in annual reports. For large companies, auditors must also attest to management's assessment. This increases accountability and transparency regarding control effectiveness. Material weaknesses must be disclosed.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the assessment and reporting requirements
- Option C misassigns responsibility—management, not board, assesses
