# Question

Periodic pension cost for a defined benefit plan includes:

## Options

- **A**: Only employer contributions made during the period
- **B**: Service cost, interest cost, and expected return on plan assets
- **C**: Dividends paid to plan beneficiaries

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Pension expense components: (1) Service cost—present value of benefits earned during the period; (2) Interest cost—increase in PBO due to passage of time; (3) Expected return on plan assets (reduces expense); (4) Amortization of prior service costs and actuarial gains/losses. Cash contributions affect funding, not expense.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses cash contributions with expense
- Option C misunderstands—pensions pay benefits, not dividends
