# Question

The three-component DuPont decomposition of ROE includes:

## Options

- **A**: Net profit margin, asset turnover, and financial leverage
- **B**: Gross margin, operating margin, and net margin
- **C**: Current ratio, quick ratio, and cash ratio

## Answer

A

## Explanation

DuPont analysis: ROE = (Net income/Sales) × (Sales/Assets) × (Assets/Equity) = Net profit margin × Asset turnover × Financial leverage. This decomposition reveals whether ROE is driven by profitability, efficiency, or leverage. The five-component version further breaks down profit margin.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B lists only profitability margins, missing efficiency and leverage
- Option C lists liquidity ratios, unrelated to ROE decomposition
