# Question

Excess tax benefits from stock-based compensation are classified in the cash flow statement as:

## Options

- **A**: Operating activities
- **B**: Financing activities
- **C**: Investing activities

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Under current standards (post-ASU 2016-09), all tax effects from share-based payments are classified as operating activities, simplifying the previous treatment that split benefits between operating and financing. The excess benefit occurs when the tax deduction (based on stock price at exercise) exceeds book compensation expense.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B was required under previous standards for excess benefits
- Option C is not used for tax benefits
