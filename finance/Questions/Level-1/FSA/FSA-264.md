# Question

A company had 100,000 shares outstanding on January 1. On July 1, it issued 20,000 additional shares. The weighted average shares for the year is:

## Options

- **A**: 100,000
- **B**: 110,000
- **C**: 120,000

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Weighted average = (100,000 × 6/12) + (120,000 × 6/12) = 50,000 + 60,000 = 110,000 shares. Shares are weighted by the portion of the year they were outstanding. The July 1 issuance means 120,000 shares were outstanding for the second half. Stock dividends and splits require retroactive adjustment.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A uses beginning shares only
- Option C uses ending shares only without weighting
