# Question

Under IFRS, defined benefit pension expense in profit or loss includes:

## Options

- **A**: Service cost, net interest, and remeasurements
- **B**: Service cost and net interest only
- **C**: Service cost only

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Under IFRS, pension expense in P&L includes service cost (current, past, and settlement) and net interest on the net defined benefit liability/asset. Remeasurements (actuarial gains/losses and return on plan assets excluding interest) go directly to OCI and are never recycled to P&L.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly includes remeasurements in P&L
- Option C omits the interest component
