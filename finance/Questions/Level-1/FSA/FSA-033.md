# Question

A company has net income of $30 million and average total assets of $300 million. Its return on assets (ROA) is:

## Options

- **A**: 10%
- **B**: 15%
- **C**: 30%

## Answer

A

## Explanation

ROA = Net income / Average total assets = $30 million / $300 million = 0.10 = 10%. ROA measures how efficiently a company uses all its assets to generate profit. Unlike ROE, it doesn't depend on capital structure since it uses total assets regardless of funding source.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B may result from calculation error
- Option C incorrectly multiplies instead of divides
