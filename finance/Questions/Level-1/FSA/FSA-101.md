# Question

The fixed charge coverage ratio is more comprehensive than the interest coverage ratio because it includes:

## Options

- **A**: Dividend payments
- **B**: Lease payments
- **C**: Capital expenditures

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Fixed charge coverage = (EBIT + Lease payments) / (Interest + Lease payments). It expands interest coverage by including lease payments (and sometimes preferred dividends), recognizing these as fixed obligations similar to debt interest. This provides a fuller picture of a company's ability to meet all fixed financing costs.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—dividends are typically discretionary, not fixed charges
- Option C—CapEx is discretionary and not a fixed charge
