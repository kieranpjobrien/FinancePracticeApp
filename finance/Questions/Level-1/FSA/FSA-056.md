# Question

Total income tax expense reported on the income statement consists of:

## Options

- **A**: Current tax expense only
- **B**: Current tax expense plus or minus deferred tax expense
- **C**: Cash taxes paid during the period

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Total tax expense = Current tax expense ± Deferred tax expense. Current tax expense is based on taxable income per tax rules. Deferred tax expense reflects changes in deferred tax assets and liabilities from temporary differences. Cash taxes paid may differ from both due to timing of payments.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores deferred taxes
- Option C confuses accrual expense with cash payment
