# Question

Which inventory cost flow method is prohibited under IFRS?

## Options

- **A**: First-in, first-out (FIFO)
- **B**: Last-in, first-out (LIFO)
- **C**: Weighted average cost

## Answer

B

## Explanation

IFRS prohibits LIFO because it often doesn't reflect the actual physical flow of goods and can result in outdated inventory values on the balance sheet. US GAAP permits LIFO, which some companies use for tax benefits during inflationary periods. This is a significant IFRS/GAAP difference affecting comparability.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is permitted under both IFRS and US GAAP
- Option C is permitted under both frameworks
