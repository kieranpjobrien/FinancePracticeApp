# Question

The 10-K annual report filed with the SEC includes:

## Options

- **A**: Quarterly financial statements only
- **B**: Audited annual financial statements, MD&A, and disclosures
- **C**: Only the balance sheet

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The 10-K is the comprehensive annual filing containing audited financial statements, notes, MD&A, risk factors, business description, executive compensation, and other required disclosures. The 10-Q is quarterly (unaudited). The 8-K reports material events. Together, these provide comprehensive company disclosure.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes 10-Q content
- Option C is incomplete—10-K includes all major statements and extensive disclosures
