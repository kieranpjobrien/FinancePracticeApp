# Question

A defined benefit pension plan is underfunded when:

## Options

- **A**: Plan assets exceed the projected benefit obligation
- **B**: The projected benefit obligation exceeds plan assets
- **C**: Current service cost exceeds employer contributions

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Funded status = Fair value of plan assets - Projected benefit obligation (PBO). When PBO > plan assets, the plan is underfunded, and the difference appears as a net pension liability on the balance sheet. Overfunded plans (assets > PBO) show a net pension asset. Funded status must be recognized on the balance sheet.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes an overfunded plan
- Option C relates to cash flow, not funded status
