# Question

Equipment with original cost of $200,000 and accumulated depreciation of $150,000 is sold for $60,000. The gain or loss on disposal is:

## Options

- **A**: $10,000 gain
- **B**: $10,000 loss
- **C**: $60,000 gain

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Book value = Cost - Accumulated depreciation = $200,000 - $150,000 = $50,000. Gain/loss = Proceeds - Book value = $60,000 - $50,000 = $10,000 gain. The gain is typically reported in other income or as part of operating income, depending on the nature of the disposal.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B reverses the gain/loss calculation
- Option C treats proceeds as the entire gain
