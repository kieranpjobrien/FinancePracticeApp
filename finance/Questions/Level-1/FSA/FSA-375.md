# Question

Which combination of factors represents the highest financial reporting risk?

## Options

- **A**: Stable revenues, high CFO, conservative accounting
- **B**: Revenue growing faster than receivables, consistent margins, auditor continuity
- **C**: Aggressive accounting, high executive turnover, complex transactions

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Multiple red flags together signal elevated risk: aggressive accounting choices suggest management incentive to inflate results, high executive/auditor turnover may indicate disagreements or control problems, complex transactions can obscure economic reality. These factors compound to create significant manipulation risk requiring deep scrutiny.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A shows quality characteristics, low risk
- Option B shows healthy patterns, not concerning
