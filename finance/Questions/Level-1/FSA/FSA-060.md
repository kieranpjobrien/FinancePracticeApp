# Question

A company has pretax income of $100 million and income tax expense of $22 million. Its effective tax rate is:

## Options

- **A**: 18%
- **B**: 22%
- **C**: 28%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Effective tax rate = Income tax expense / Pretax income = $22 million / $100 million = 22%. The effective rate may differ from the statutory rate due to permanent differences (tax-exempt income, non-deductible expenses), tax credits, and differences in jurisdictional tax rates for multinational companies.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A may result from using after-tax income in denominator
- Option C may confuse with statutory rate
