# Question

A company has net income of $50 million and average shareholders' equity of $250 million. Its return on equity (ROE) is:

## Options

- **A**: 5%
- **B**: 20%
- **C**: 25%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

ROE = Net income / Average shareholders' equity = $50 million / $250 million = 0.20 = 20%. ROE measures the return generated on shareholders' investment. Using average equity (rather than ending) better matches the income earned during the period with the capital employed.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A divides equity by net income (inverts the ratio)
- Option C may result from arithmetic error
