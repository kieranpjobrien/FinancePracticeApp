# Question

Income smoothing by management:

## Options

- **A**: Always improves earnings quality
- **B**: May mask underlying business volatility
- **C**: Is required by accounting standards

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Income smoothing uses discretion (reserves, timing of gains/losses, estimates) to reduce earnings volatility. While some smoothing reflects natural hedging, excessive smoothing hides real business fluctuations, making trend analysis and risk assessment difficult. Analysts should identify smoothing mechanisms and adjust.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores that smoothing can obscure reality
- Option C is incorrect—smoothing is a management choice, often discouraged
