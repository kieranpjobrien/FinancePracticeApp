# Question

A company has cash of $50,000, marketable securities of $30,000, receivables of $70,000, and daily cash expenditures of $5,000. The defensive interval is:

## Options

- **A**: 10 days
- **B**: 30 days
- **C**: 50 days

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Defensive interval = (Cash + Marketable securities + Receivables) / Daily cash expenditures = ($50,000 + $30,000 + $70,000) / $5,000 = $150,000 / $5,000 = 30 days. This measures how long a company can operate using only liquid assets without additional cash inflows.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A uses only cash
- Option C uses an incorrect calculation
