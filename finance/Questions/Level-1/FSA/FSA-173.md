# Question

Goodwill on the balance sheet arises from:

## Options

- **A**: Internal development of brand value
- **B**: A business combination where purchase price exceeds fair value of net identifiable assets
- **C**: Appreciation of fixed assets above cost

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Goodwill = Purchase price - Fair value of net identifiable assets acquired. It represents the premium paid for synergies, workforce, reputation, and other factors not separately identifiable. Internally generated goodwill cannot be capitalized. Asset appreciation creates revaluation surplus under IFRS, not goodwill.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes internally generated goodwill, which cannot be recognized
- Option C describes revaluation, not goodwill
