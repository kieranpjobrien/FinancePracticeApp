# Question

Under the successful efforts method for oil and gas accounting:

## Options

- **A**: All exploration costs are capitalized
- **B**: Only costs of successful wells are capitalized; dry holes are expensed
- **C**: All costs are expensed as incurred

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Successful efforts capitalizes only costs directly associated with finding and developing proved reserves. Dry hole costs and unsuccessful exploration are expensed. The full cost method capitalizes all costs within large cost centers. Successful efforts is more conservative, showing higher expense volatility but better matching.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes the full cost method
- Option C is overly conservative and not GAAP-compliant
