# Question

Executive compensation details are primarily disclosed in:

## Options

- **A**: The income statement
- **B**: The proxy statement (DEF 14A)
- **C**: The statement of cash flows

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The proxy statement (DEF 14A) filed before shareholder meetings contains detailed executive compensation information: salary, bonus, stock awards, option grants, pension benefits, and perquisites. This enables assessment of pay-performance alignment. Summary compensation tables and pay ratio disclosures are required.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A shows total compensation expense, not executive detail
- Option C doesn't contain compensation details
