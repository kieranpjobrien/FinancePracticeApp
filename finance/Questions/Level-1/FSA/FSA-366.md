# Question

Off-balance-sheet commitments that should be analyzed include:

## Options

- **A**: Only guarantees to third parties
- **B**: Operating leases, purchase commitments, and contingent obligations
- **C**: All expenses recorded on the income statement

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Key off-balance-sheet items include: purchase commitments (contracts to buy), capital commitments, operating lease obligations (pre-IFRS 16), unconditional purchase obligations, and guarantees. These represent potential future cash requirements not fully reflected as liabilities. Note disclosures provide details for analysis.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is too narrow—many other commitments exist
- Option C describes recognized items, not off-balance-sheet
