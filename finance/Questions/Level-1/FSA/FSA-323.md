# Question

Pro forma earnings typically:

## Options

- **A**: Are more conservative than GAAP earnings
- **B**: Exclude items management considers non-recurring
- **C**: Are required under SEC regulations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Pro forma (non-GAAP) earnings exclude items management deems non-recurring or non-core: restructuring, stock compensation, amortization of intangibles, acquisition costs. They often exceed GAAP earnings. While not required, SEC requires reconciliation to GAAP and prohibits giving more prominence to non-GAAP measures.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is typically reversed—pro forma usually exceeds GAAP
- Option C is incorrect—pro forma is voluntary, though regulated
