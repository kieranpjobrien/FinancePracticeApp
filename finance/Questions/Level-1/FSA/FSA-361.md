# Question

A company's presentation currency is:

## Options

- **A**: Always the currency of its home country
- **B**: The currency in which financial statements are presented
- **C**: The currency used for tax reporting

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Presentation currency is the currency chosen for financial statement display. It may differ from functional currency (primary economic environment) or local currency. IFRS allows any presentation currency with appropriate translation. Foreign operations are translated from functional to presentation currency.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores that companies can choose presentation currency
- Option C describes tax filing, not financial reporting
