# Question

Under US GAAP (ASC 842), a lease is classified as a finance lease if it meets criteria such as:

## Options

- **A**: Lease term is less than 25% of the asset's economic life
- **B**: Transfer of ownership, bargain purchase option, or lease term for major part of economic life
- **C**: Monthly payments are made in advance

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Finance lease criteria include: transfer of ownership, bargain purchase option, lease term for major part (typically 75%+) of economic life, present value of payments equals substantially all (typically 90%+) of fair value, or specialized asset with no alternative use. Meeting any criterion results in finance classification.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A suggests operating lease characteristics
- Option C describes payment timing, not classification criteria
