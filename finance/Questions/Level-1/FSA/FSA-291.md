# Question

In a period of falling prices, which inventory method produces the highest gross profit?

## Options

- **A**: FIFO
- **B**: LIFO
- **C**: Weighted average

## Answer

B

## Explanation

In falling prices, LIFO assigns lower recent costs to COGS, producing higher gross profit. FIFO assigns older, higher costs to COGS, producing lower gross profit. This is the opposite of rising price environments. Weighted average falls between FIFO and LIFO in both scenarios.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A produces highest gross profit in rising prices, not falling
- Option C is always between FIFO and LIFO
