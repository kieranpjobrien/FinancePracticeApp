# Question

A company has ROE of 15% and a dividend payout ratio of 40%. The sustainable growth rate is:

## Options

- **A**: 6%
- **B**: 9%
- **C**: 15%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Sustainable growth rate = ROE × Retention ratio = ROE × (1 - Payout ratio) = 15% × (1 - 0.40) = 15% × 0.60 = 9%. This represents the rate at which a company can grow using internally generated funds without changing its capital structure or issuing new equity.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly uses payout ratio instead of retention ratio
- Option C ignores the dividend payout entirely
