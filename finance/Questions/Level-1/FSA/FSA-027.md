# Question

Which of the following is classified as an investing activity?

## Options

- **A**: Repayment of long-term debt
- **B**: Purchase of equipment
- **C**: Dividends paid to shareholders

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Investing activities involve acquiring and disposing of long-term assets and investments. Equipment purchases, building construction, and buying/selling securities (other than trading securities) are investing activities. Debt repayment and dividend payments are financing activities.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is a financing activity
- Option C is a financing activity
