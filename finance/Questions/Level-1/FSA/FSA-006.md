# Question

Faithful representation requires financial information to be:

## Options

- **A**: Relevant, timely, and understandable
- **B**: Complete, neutral, and free from error
- **C**: Comparable, verifiable, and consistent

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Faithful representation means information accurately depicts the economic phenomena it purports to represent. It requires: (1) completeness—all necessary information is included; (2) neutrality—unbiased presentation without manipulation; (3) freedom from error—no errors in process, though estimates may be imprecise. Along with relevance, it's a fundamental qualitative characteristic.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A mixes fundamental (relevance) with enhancing characteristics
- Option C lists only enhancing qualitative characteristics
