# Question

A company's effective tax rate is lower than its statutory rate most likely because of:

## Options

- **A**: Non-deductible expenses such as fines
- **B**: Tax credits or income taxed at lower rates
- **C**: Temporary differences reversing

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Tax credits (R&D, foreign, energy) directly reduce taxes owed, lowering the effective rate. Income taxed at lower rates (foreign jurisdictions, capital gains) also reduces effective rates. Non-deductible expenses increase effective rates. Temporary differences affect timing but not the overall effective rate.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A would increase the effective rate above statutory
- Option C affects deferred taxes, not the current period effective rate
