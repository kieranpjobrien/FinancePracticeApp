# Question

Active fixed-income management seeks to generate alpha through:

## Options

- **A**: Simply matching benchmark duration and sector weights
- **B**: Making tactical bets on rates, curve shape, sectors, and security selection
- **C**: Minimizing all tracking error

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Active management: outperform benchmark through tactical views. Duration bets (rate direction), curve positioning (steepening/flattening), sector rotation (over/underweight sectors), security selection (relative value). Accept tracking error to earn excess returns.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—describes passive/indexing approach
- Option C—active management accepts tracking error for alpha
