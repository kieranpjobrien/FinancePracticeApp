# Question

Value at Risk (VaR) for a bond portfolio estimates:

## Options

- **A**: The expected return over a given period
- **B**: The maximum loss at a given confidence level over a specified time horizon
- **C**: The average daily price volatility

## Answer

B

## Explanation

VaR: maximum expected loss at confidence level (e.g., 95%, 99%) over time period (e.g., 1 day, 10 days). "5% chance of losing more than $X." Doesn't describe magnitude of losses beyond VaR (use CVaR/Expected Shortfall for that).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes expected return, not risk measure
- Option C describes volatility, not tail loss
