# Question

Extension risk for mortgage-backed securities occurs when:

## Options

- **A**: Prepayments accelerate beyond expectations
- **B**: Prepayments slow below expectations, extending average life
- **C**: The MBS defaults on payments

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Extension risk: rising rates cause prepayments to slow. MBS average life extends beyond expected. Investors locked into below-market yields longer. Opposite of prepayment/contraction risk. Both risks make MBS duration uncertain.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes contraction/prepayment risk
- Option C describes credit risk, not extension risk
