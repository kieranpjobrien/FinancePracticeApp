# Question

The "pull to par" effect describes:

## Options

- **A**: Price volatility increasing as maturity approaches
- **B**: Bond price converging toward par value as maturity approaches
- **C**: Yields increasing as credit quality deteriorates

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Pull to par: as bond approaches maturity, price converges to par regardless of coupon vs. yield relationship. Premium bond price declines toward par; discount bond price rises toward par. At maturity, receive par value.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—price volatility typically decreases near maturity
- Option C describes credit spread widening
