# Question

The total return of a fixed-income investment over a holding period includes:

## Options

- **A**: Coupon income only
- **B**: Coupon income, reinvestment income, and price change
- **C**: Price change only

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Total return = Coupon income + Reinvestment income + Price change. Coupon: periodic interest. Reinvestment: return on reinvested coupons. Price change: capital gain/loss at sale. All three components matter. Horizon analysis considers all components.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—ignores reinvestment and capital gains
- Option C—ignores income components
