# Question

All else equal, a bond trading at a higher yield has:

## Options

- **A**: Higher duration
- **B**: Lower duration
- **C**: Same duration regardless of yield level

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Higher yield = lower duration. Higher discount rate reduces present value of distant cash flows more—shifts weight toward nearer payments. Also, higher yields mean lower prices with more convexity benefit. Duration varies with yield level (yield effect).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—reverses the relationship
- Option C—duration depends on yield level
