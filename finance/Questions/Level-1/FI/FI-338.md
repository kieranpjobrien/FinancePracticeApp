# Question

Horizon matching combines:

## Options

- **A**: Cash flow matching for near-term liabilities and duration matching for longer-term liabilities
- **B**: Only duration matching across all time periods
- **C**: Active management without liability considerations

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Horizon matching: hybrid approach. Cash flow match near-term liabilities (years 1-5) for certainty. Duration match longer-term liabilities where exact matching impractical. Balances precision for near term with flexibility for long term. Practical for pension funds.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B—ignores cash flow matching component
- Option C—horizon matching is liability-driven
