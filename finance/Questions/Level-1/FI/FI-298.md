# Question

Yield to call (YTC) assumes:

## Options

- **A**: The bond is held to maturity
- **B**: The bond is called at the first call date at the call price
- **C**: The bond defaults before maturity

## Answer

B

## Explanation

YTC: yield assuming bond called at first call date. For premium callable bonds, YTC may be lower than YTM (more likely to be called). Yield to worst: lowest of YTM, YTC at various dates. Important for callable bond analysis.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—describes yield to maturity
- Option C—describes recovery analysis, not YTC
