# Question

Subordination as a credit enhancement technique involves:

## Options

- **A**: Purchasing insurance from a third party
- **B**: Creating tranches where junior tranches absorb losses before senior tranches
- **C**: Holding extra collateral in reserve

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Subordination (credit tranching): junior tranches absorb first losses, protecting senior tranches. Internal credit enhancement—no external guarantor. Senior tranche rated higher due to subordination cushion. Common in ABS, CMBS, CLO structures.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes external credit enhancement (surety bond)
- Option C describes overcollateralization
