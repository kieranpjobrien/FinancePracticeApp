# Question

Weighted average life (WAL) for an MBS is:

## Options

- **A**: The same as maturity for all bonds
- **B**: The average time to receive principal payments, weighted by amount
- **C**: The duration of the underlying mortgages

## Answer

B

## Explanation

WAL: average time to receive principal repayments. Accounts for scheduled amortization and expected prepayments. Shorter than stated maturity for amortizing/prepaying securities. WAL changes as prepayment assumptions change.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—WAL typically much shorter than maturity for MBS
- Option C—WAL measures principal timing, not price sensitivity
