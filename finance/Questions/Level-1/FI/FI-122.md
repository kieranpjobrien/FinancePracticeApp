# Question

A mortgage pass-through security:

## Options

- **A**: Distributes principal and interest payments proportionally to all investors
- **B**: Prioritizes certain investors over others for principal payments
- **C**: Guarantees fixed monthly payments regardless of prepayments

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Pass-through: pools mortgages, passes through principal and interest (less servicing fee) pro rata to all holders. Simple structure—all investors share prepayment risk equally. CMOs restructure pass-throughs to redirect cash flows.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B describes CMO tranches, not pass-throughs
- Option C—payments vary with prepayments
