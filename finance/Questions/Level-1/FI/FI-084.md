# Question

The 30/360 day count convention:

## Options

- **A**: Assumes 30 days per month and 360 days per year
- **B**: Uses actual days and 360-day year
- **C**: Uses actual days for both month and year

## Answer

A

## Explanation

30/360: assumes every month has 30 days, year has 360 days. Common for corporate and municipal bonds. Simplifies calculations. Actual/actual uses real calendar days—common for Treasuries. Actual/360 common for money market.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B describes actual/360 convention
- Option C describes actual/actual convention
