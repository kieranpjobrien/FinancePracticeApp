# Question

An investor in the 35% tax bracket considers a municipal bond yielding 4%. The taxable-equivalent yield is:

## Options

- **A**: 4.00%
- **B**: 6.15%
- **C**: 2.60%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Taxable-equivalent yield = Tax-exempt yield / (1 - marginal tax rate) = 4% / (1 - 0.35) = 4% / 0.65 = 6.15%. This is the pre-tax yield a taxable bond would need to match the muni's after-tax return.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores tax benefit
- Option C incorrectly applies the tax rate
