# Question

High operating leverage increases credit risk because:

## Options

- **A**: It reduces sensitivity of profits to revenue changes
- **B**: Fixed costs amplify earnings volatility from revenue changes
- **C**: It increases cash reserves

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Operating leverage: high fixed costs mean earnings vary more than revenue. Good in upturns (more profit per incremental sale), bad in downturns (fixed costs still due). Higher earnings volatility = higher default risk. Airlines, utilities have high operating leverage.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—high leverage increases sensitivity
- Option C—operating leverage doesn't directly affect cash
