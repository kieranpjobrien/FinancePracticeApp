# Question

Sector rotation in fixed-income investing involves:

## Options

- **A**: Keeping constant allocations regardless of market conditions
- **B**: Over/underweighting sectors based on relative value and expected performance
- **C**: Only investing in government bonds

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Sector rotation: adjust weights among corporates, MBS, ABS, governments, etc. Based on spread levels, fundamentals, technicals. Overweight sectors expected to outperform (spreads tighten or better carry). Underweight deteriorating sectors. Key active strategy.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—describes passive/indexed approach
- Option C—ignores non-government sectors
