# Question

Affirmative covenants in a bond indenture require the issuer to:

## Options

- **A**: Refrain from certain actions like issuing more debt
- **B**: Take certain actions like maintaining insurance and paying taxes
- **C**: Maintain specific financial ratios at all times

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Affirmative covenants: things issuer must DO—pay interest/principal, maintain insurance, file reports, pay taxes, maintain assets. Negative covenants restrict actions. Financial covenants specify ratio requirements (a subset of negative covenants).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes negative covenants
- Option C describes financial covenants specifically
