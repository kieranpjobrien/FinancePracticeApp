# Question

Agency CMOs differ from non-agency CMOs primarily because:

## Options

- **A**: Agency CMOs have no credit risk due to government or GSE guarantee
- **B**: Agency CMOs have shorter maturities
- **C**: Non-agency CMOs pay higher coupons regardless of credit quality

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Agency CMOs: guaranteed by Ginnie Mae (government), Fannie Mae, or Freddie Mac (GSE). Credit risk minimal. Non-agency (private-label): credit risk based on underlying mortgages. Require credit enhancement. Different credit analysis needed for non-agency.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B—maturity depends on structure, not agency status
- Option C—coupon reflects credit risk, but statement oversimplifies
