# Question

A bond trades at a premium when:

## Options

- **A**: Its coupon rate is lower than the yield to maturity
- **B**: Its coupon rate is higher than the yield to maturity
- **C**: It is trading at par value

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Premium: coupon > YTM, price > par. Investors pay above par for higher coupon. As rates rise, existing high-coupon bonds become more valuable. Premium amortizes toward par over remaining life ("pull to par").

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes discount bond
- Option C describes par bond where coupon = YTM
