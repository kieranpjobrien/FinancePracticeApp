# Question

If the 1-year spot rate is 3% and the 2-year spot rate is 5%, what is the 1-year forward rate starting in 1 year (1y1y)?

## Options

- **A**: 4.00%
- **B**: 7.04%
- **C**: 2.00%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Forward rate: (1+s₂)²/(1+s₁) - 1 = (1.05)²/(1.03) - 1 = 1.1025/1.03 - 1 = 7.04%. The forward rate must be higher than 5% to pull the 2-year average up from 3% to 5%.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A assumes simple average (5%-3%)/2 + 3%
- Option C subtracts rates directly
