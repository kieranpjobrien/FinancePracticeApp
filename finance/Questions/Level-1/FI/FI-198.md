# Question

Green bonds are characterized by:

## Options

- **A**: Higher yields than comparable conventional bonds
- **B**: Proceeds dedicated to environmentally beneficial projects
- **C**: Government guarantee of principal

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Green bonds: proceeds fund environmental projects (renewable energy, efficiency, clean transport). Verified by third parties. Growing market. May trade at slight "greenium" (lower yield) due to investor demand. Credit risk same as issuer's other debt.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—green bonds often trade at lower yields
- Option C—no automatic government guarantee
