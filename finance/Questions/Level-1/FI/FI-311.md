# Question

A duration-matched portfolio requires rebalancing because:

## Options

- **A**: Interest rates never change
- **B**: Duration changes as time passes and rates change, causing mismatch
- **C**: Credit quality always improves over time

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Duration drift: as time passes, duration shortens. When rates change, duration changes (convexity effect). Originally matched portfolio becomes mismatched. Requires periodic rebalancing to maintain target duration. Transaction costs and timing considerations apply.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—rates do change, affecting duration
- Option C—credit changes are separate from duration management
