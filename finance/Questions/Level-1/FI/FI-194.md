# Question

A bond with 5% coupon, $1,000 par value, trading at $950 has a current yield of:

## Options

- **A**: 5.00%
- **B**: 5.26%
- **C**: 4.75%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Current yield = Annual coupon / Price = $50 / $950 = 5.26%. Simple income measure—ignores capital gain/loss and reinvestment. Higher than coupon rate when trading at discount. Lower than YTM for discount bonds.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A uses coupon rate (par assumption)
- Option C multiplies by price/par ratio incorrectly
