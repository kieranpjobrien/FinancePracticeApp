# Question

Arbitrage-free bond valuation ensures:

## Options

- **A**: The bond is fairly priced relative to investor expectations
- **B**: The bond's value equals the sum of values of its individual cash flows priced using the spot curve
- **C**: The bond will outperform the benchmark

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Arbitrage-free: price each cash flow at appropriate spot rate. No arbitrage if bond price equals sum of separately priced cash flows (strips). Prevents riskless profit from buying bond and selling strips (or vice versa).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes fair value, different concept
- Option C describes relative performance, not arbitrage-free pricing
