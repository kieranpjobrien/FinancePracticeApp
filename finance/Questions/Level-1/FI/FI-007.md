# Question

A bond with original maturity of 15 years is classified as:

## Options

- **A**: A short-term bond
- **B**: A medium-term bond
- **C**: A long-term bond

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Bond maturity classifications: short-term (1-5 years), medium-term (5-12 years), long-term (>12 years). 15 years falls in long-term category. Longer maturities have greater interest rate sensitivity. Classification affects portfolio duration and risk characteristics.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—short-term is typically under 5 years
- Option B—medium-term typically 5-12 years
