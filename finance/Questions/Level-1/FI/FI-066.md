# Question

A bond indenture is:

## Options

- **A**: The certificate representing bond ownership
- **B**: The legal contract specifying bond terms, covenants, and rights
- **C**: The prospectus for new bond issues

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Bond indenture (trust deed): legal contract between issuer and bondholder trustee. Specifies: coupon, maturity, covenants, call provisions, default events, remedies. Trustee monitors compliance. Important source of bondholder protections and rights.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes ownership certificate, not contract
- Option C describes disclosure document, separate from indenture
