# Question

In a securitization, the servicer is responsible for:

## Options

- **A**: Providing credit enhancement through subordination
- **B**: Collecting payments from underlying borrowers and distributing to investors
- **C**: Rating the securities issued by the SPV

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Servicer: collects payments from underlying asset pool (mortgages, loans, receivables), manages delinquencies, handles foreclosures if needed, distributes cash flows per waterfall. Often the originator acts as servicer. Critical operational role in securitization.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—credit enhancement comes from structure, not servicer
- Option C—rating agencies rate securities, not servicer
