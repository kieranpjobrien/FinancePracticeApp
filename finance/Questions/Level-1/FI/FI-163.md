# Question

A general obligation (GO) municipal bond is backed by:

## Options

- **A**: Revenue from a specific project like a toll road
- **B**: The issuer's full faith, credit, and taxing power
- **C**: Federal government guarantee

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GO bonds: backed by issuer's taxing authority—property taxes, sales taxes, income taxes. "Full faith and credit" pledge. Generally lower risk than revenue bonds. Voter approval often required. Analyzeissuer's tax base and fiscal health.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes revenue bonds
- Option C—munis not federally guaranteed
