# Question

When analyzing sovereign credit risk, analysts consider:

## Options

- **A**: Only the country's GDP level
- **B**: Political stability, monetary flexibility, fiscal strength, and external position
- **C**: Just the central bank's foreign exchange reserves

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Sovereign credit analysis: multiple factors. Political stability affects policy continuity. Monetary flexibility (own currency, inflation credibility). Fiscal strength (debt/GDP, deficits). External position (current account, reserves). Holistic approach needed.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—GDP alone doesn't capture debt sustainability
- Option C—reserves important but not sole factor
