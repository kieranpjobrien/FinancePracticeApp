# Question

During periods of deflation, TIPS provide:

## Options

- **A**: No protection—principal can fall below original par at maturity
- **B**: A deflation floor—principal cannot fall below original par at maturity
- **C**: Higher returns than during inflation periods

## Answer

B

## Explanation

TIPS deflation floor: at maturity, investor receives greater of inflation-adjusted principal or original par. Protects against cumulative deflation. During holding period, adjusted principal can fall below par, but maturity payment floored.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the deflation floor protection
- Option C—TIPS underperform nominal bonds in deflation
