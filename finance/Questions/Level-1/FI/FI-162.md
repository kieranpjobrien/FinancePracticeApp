# Question

For a callable bond, one-sided durations measure:

## Options

- **A**: Duration for rate increases versus decreases separately
- **B**: Duration to the call date only
- **C**: Duration excluding the embedded option

## Answer

A

## Explanation

One-sided durations: separate duration for rate rises vs. falls. Callable bonds have asymmetric response—when rates fall, call option limits price rise. Up-duration may exceed down-duration. Captures non-linear behavior missed by single duration.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B describes duration to call, different concept
- Option C describes modified duration ignoring options
