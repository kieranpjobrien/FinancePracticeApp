# Question

The servicer in a securitization structure is responsible for:

## Options

- **A**: Purchasing the securitized assets from the originator
- **B**: Collecting payments from underlying borrowers and passing them to the SPV
- **C**: Rating the securitized bonds

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Servicer: collects payments from borrowers (mortgages, loans), handles delinquencies, remits to trustee/SPV. Often the originator continues as servicer. Receives servicing fee. Master servicer may oversee multiple sub-servicers.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes SPV's role
- Option C describes rating agency's role
