# Question

Credit migration risk refers to:

## Options

- **A**: The risk that credit spreads will widen due to economic conditions
- **B**: The risk that a bond's credit rating will change, affecting its value
- **C**: The risk that a bond cannot be sold at fair value

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Credit migration: rating upgrade or downgrade. Even without default, downgrades cause price declines due to wider required spreads. Rating transition matrices show historical probabilities of rating changes. Downgrade risk typically greater than upgrade probability.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes spread risk generally
- Option C describes liquidity risk
