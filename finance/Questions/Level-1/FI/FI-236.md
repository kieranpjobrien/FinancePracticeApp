# Question

T+1 settlement for bonds means:

## Options

- **A**: The bond matures in one day
- **B**: Trade settles one business day after trade date
- **C**: Payment is made one month after trade

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Settlement: exchange of bond for cash. T+1 = trade date plus one business day. US Treasuries typically T+1. Corporate bonds often T+2. Settlement date is when accrued interest calculated and ownership transfers. Different from trade date.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes maturity
- Option C describes much longer settlement than standard
