# Question

A debenture is:

## Options

- **A**: A bond secured by specific collateral
- **B**: An unsecured bond backed only by the issuer's creditworthiness
- **C**: A government-guaranteed bond

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Debenture: unsecured corporate bond. No specific collateral—relies on issuer's general credit. Lower priority than secured bonds in default. Common for investment-grade issuers. May have subordinated debentures (even lower priority).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—describes secured bond/mortgage bond
- Option C—debentures are corporate, not government
