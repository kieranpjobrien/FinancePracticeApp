# Question

A change-of-control covenant protects bondholders from:

## Options

- **A**: Rising interest rates
- **B**: Leveraged buyouts or acquisitions that increase credit risk
- **C**: Currency fluctuations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Change-of-control: if company acquired or control changes, bondholders can put bonds back at par. Protects against LBOs where acquirer loads debt onto target. Event risk covenant. Common in investment-grade bonds. Limits downgrade risk from transactions.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—interest rate risk not addressed by covenants
- Option C—currency risk not addressed by change-of-control
