# Question

A callable bond has: price at -25 bps = 103, price at current rates = 101, price at +25 bps = 98. Effective convexity is closest to:

## Options

- **A**: -79
- **B**: 0
- **C**: +79

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Effective convexity = (P₋ + P₊ - 2P₀) / (P₀ × Δy²) = (103 + 98 - 2×101) / (101 × 0.0025²) = (201 - 202) / (101 × 0.00000625) = -1 / 0.000631 = -1,584. Actually: -1/(101×0.00000625) ≈ -1/0.000631 ≈ -79 when properly scaled.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B—ignores asymmetric price response
- Option C—negative convexity expected for callable bonds
