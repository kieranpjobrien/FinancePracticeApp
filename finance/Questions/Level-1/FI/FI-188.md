# Question

The "waterfall" in a securitization refers to:

## Options

- **A**: The declining credit quality of assets over time
- **B**: The priority of payment distribution among tranches
- **C**: The process of selling assets to the SPV

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Waterfall: rules governing cash flow distribution. Senior tranches paid first (interest then principal), then mezzanine, then equity/residual. Losses absorbed in reverse order. Waterfall defines trigger events that redirect cash flows if performance deteriorates.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes asset performance, not payment structure
- Option C describes securitization setup, not waterfall
