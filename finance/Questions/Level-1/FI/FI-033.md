# Question

The three major credit rating agencies are:

## Options

- **A**: Federal Reserve, SEC, and Treasury
- **B**: S&P Global, Moody's, and Fitch
- **C**: NYSE, NASDAQ, and CBOE

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Major rating agencies: S&P Global Ratings, Moody's Investors Service, Fitch Ratings. Assess creditworthiness of issuers and securities. Ratings influence: bond yields, investment eligibility, regulatory capital requirements. Rating scales differ slightly but are comparable.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A lists government/regulatory bodies
- Option C lists exchanges, not rating agencies
