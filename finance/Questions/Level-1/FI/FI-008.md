# Question

Yield to maturity (YTM) represents:

## Options

- **A**: Only the coupon income from a bond
- **B**: The total return if the bond is held to maturity, assuming reinvestment at YTM
- **C**: The current yield based on coupon and price

## Answer

B

## Explanation

YTM: internal rate of return equating present value of all cash flows to current price. Assumes: hold to maturity, no default, reinvestment of coupons at YTM. Includes coupon income, reinvestment income, and gain/loss from difference between purchase price and par.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores capital gain/loss and reinvestment
- Option C describes current yield (annual coupon / price)
