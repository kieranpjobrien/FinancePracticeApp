# Question

Spread duration measures sensitivity to:

## Options

- **A**: Changes in the risk-free rate
- **B**: Changes in the credit spread
- **C**: Changes in currency exchange rates

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Spread duration: percentage price change for 1% change in credit spread. For bullet bonds, approximately equals modified duration. Critical for credit portfolios—measures sensitivity to spread widening/narrowing. Different from interest rate duration (risk-free sensitivity).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—describes interest rate duration
- Option C—describes currency risk, unrelated to spread duration
