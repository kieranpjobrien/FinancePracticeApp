# Question

A registered bond differs from a bearer bond in that:

## Options

- **A**: Registered bonds pay higher coupons
- **B**: The owner's name is recorded with the issuer or registrar for registered bonds
- **C**: Bearer bonds can only be traded on exchanges

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Registered bonds: issuer/registrar tracks owner; interest paid to registered holder. Bearer bonds: ownership by possession; no name on record; present coupon for payment. Most bonds now registered (tax compliance, anti-laundering). Bearer bonds rare today.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—coupon rate unrelated to registration type
- Option C—bearer bonds traded OTC, not exclusively on exchanges
