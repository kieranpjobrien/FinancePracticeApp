# Question

A mortgage principal-only (PO) strip:

## Options

- **A**: Has negative duration
- **B**: Benefits when interest rates fall and prepayments increase
- **C**: Receives only interest payments

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PO strip: receives only principal payments. Lower rates = faster prepayments = receive principal sooner = higher value (lower discount for shorter time). Very high positive duration. Gains when rates fall. IO and PO durations offset in pass-through.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—POs have high positive duration (IOs have negative)
- Option C describes IO strips
