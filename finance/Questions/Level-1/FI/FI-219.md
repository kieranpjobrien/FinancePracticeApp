# Question

U.S. Treasury bonds and most corporate bonds pay coupons:

## Options

- **A**: Monthly
- **B**: Semiannually
- **C**: Annually

## Answer

B

## Explanation

US convention: semiannual coupon payments. YTM quoted as annual rate (2 × semiannual rate). European bonds often pay annually. Monthly payments common for mortgage-related products. Payment frequency affects yield comparisons.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—monthly typical for mortgages, not most bonds
- Option C—annual typical in Europe, not US
