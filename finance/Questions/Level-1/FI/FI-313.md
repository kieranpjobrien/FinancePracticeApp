# Question

The boundary between investment grade and high yield (junk) bonds is:

## Options

- **A**: AAA/AA
- **B**: BBB-/BB+ (Baa3/Ba1)
- **C**: CCC/D

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Investment grade: BBB- (S&P/Fitch) or Baa3 (Moody's) and above. Below this = high yield/junk. Critical threshold for many institutional investors (insurance, pensions). "Fallen angels": downgraded from IG to HY. Different spread, liquidity, and default characteristics.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—this boundary within investment grade
- Option C—these are deep junk/default ratings
