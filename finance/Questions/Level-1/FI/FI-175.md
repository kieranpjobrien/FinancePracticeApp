# Question

"On-the-run" Treasury securities are:

## Options

- **A**: Treasuries approaching maturity
- **B**: The most recently issued securities at each maturity point
- **C**: Treasury securities held by foreign investors

## Answer

B

## Explanation

On-the-run: most recently auctioned Treasury at each maturity. More liquid, trade at lower yields than off-the-run (older issues). Used as benchmarks. When new security issued, previous becomes off-the-run, typically trades at slight yield premium.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes near-maturity bonds
- Option C describes holder type, not issue status
