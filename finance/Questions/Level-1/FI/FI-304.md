# Question

A bond with a 5% coupon rate trading at $95 has a current yield of:

## Options

- **A**: 5.00%
- **B**: 5.26%
- **C**: 5.50%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Current yield = Annual coupon / Current price = $5 / $95 = 5.26%. Measures current income return only. Ignores capital gain/loss to maturity and reinvestment. Simple measure but incomplete—YTM is more comprehensive.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—confuses coupon rate with current yield
- Option C—calculation error
