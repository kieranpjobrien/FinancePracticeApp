# Question

During a "flight to quality," you would expect:

## Options

- **A**: Treasury yields to rise and credit spreads to narrow
- **B**: Treasury yields to fall and credit spreads to widen
- **C**: All yields to move together

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Flight to quality: investors sell risky assets, buy safe assets. Treasuries rally (yields fall), corporate bonds sell off (spreads widen). Happens during financial stress, recessions, market panics. Opposite of risk-on environment.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A reverses the relationship
- Option C ignores quality differential
