# Question

A bond trading at a premium:

## Options

- **A**: Has a market price below its par value
- **B**: Has a market price above its par value
- **C**: Has a coupon rate equal to market yields

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Premium bond: price > par value. Occurs when coupon rate > current market yield. Investors pay more for above-market coupon. Premium amortizes to par at maturity. Discount bond: price < par when coupon < market yield. Par bond: coupon = yield.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes a discount bond
- Option C describes a par bond
