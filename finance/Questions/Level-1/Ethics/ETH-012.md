# Question

When facing an ethical dilemma, an investment professional should consider which of the following as the most important factor in the decision-making process?

## Options

- **A**: Whether the action is legal in the relevant jurisdiction
- **B**: Whether the action aligns with the interests of the member's employer
- **C**: Whether the action is consistent with the Code and Standards and promotes market integrity

## Answer

C

## Explanation

While legality and employer interests are relevant considerations, professional ethical frameworks prioritize alignment with the Code and Standards and promoting market integrity above other factors. Members are expected to go beyond mere legal compliance to uphold the higher ethical standards of the profession.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is insufficient—many actions may be legal but still violate ethical standards; ethics requires going beyond legal minimums
- Option B may conflict with ethical duties—employer interests should not override professional ethical obligations
