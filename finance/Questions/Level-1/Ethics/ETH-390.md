# Question

An analyst faces a situation not explicitly covered by the Standards. The most appropriate approach is to:

## Options

- **A**: Proceed without concern since no rule is being violated
- **B**: Apply the underlying ethical principles of the Code and Standards to guide the decision
- **C**: Wait for explicit guidance from CFA Institute

## Answer

B

## Explanation

When specific rules do not address a situation, members should apply the fundamental ethical principles underlying the Code and Standards. These principles provide guidance for novel situations where explicit rules may not exist.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores ethical obligations beyond explicit rules
- Option C is impractical and fails to address immediate decision needs
