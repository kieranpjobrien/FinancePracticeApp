# Question

The Code of Ethics emphasizes the importance of:

## Options

- **A**: Maintaining competence only at the time of initial certification
- **B**: Continuous professional development throughout one's career
- **C**: Competence only in portfolio management skills

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The Code of Ethics calls for maintaining and improving professional competence on an ongoing basis. Investment knowledge evolves, and members must continuously develop their skills throughout their careers.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A treats competence as a one-time achievement
- Option C too narrowly defines the scope of required competence
