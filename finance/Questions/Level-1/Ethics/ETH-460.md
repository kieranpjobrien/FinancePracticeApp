# Question

Using secondary research from external providers, members must:

## Options

- **A**: Accept findings without question from reputable sources
- **B**: Evaluate the quality, independence, and methodology of the research
- **C**: Only use research from CFA charterholders

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(A) requires diligent evaluation of all research sources. Members cannot blindly rely on external research but must assess quality, potential biases, and methodological soundness before incorporating into recommendations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A abdicates due diligence responsibility
- Option C imposes an unrealistic credential requirement
