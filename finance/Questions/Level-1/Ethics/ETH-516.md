# Question

An analyst stores research records in a personal cloud account not controlled by the employer. Under Standard V(C):

## Options

- **A**: This is acceptable for backup purposes
- **B**: Records should be maintained in systems controlled by or accessible to the employer
- **C**: Cloud storage is prohibited under the Standard

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(C) contemplates that records belong to the employer and must be accessible. Personal storage outside employer control creates access and retention issues. Records should be in employer-controlled systems.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores employer access requirements
- Option C overly restricts technology choices when properly managed
