# Question

When facing pressure to compromise ethical standards, an effective first step is:

## Options

- **A**: Immediately resign from the position
- **B**: Identify the ethical issue and consider who is affected
- **C**: Ignore the pressure and hope it resolves

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The ethical decision-making framework begins with identifying the issue and affected stakeholders. This systematic approach helps ensure thoughtful responses rather than hasty actions or avoidance.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A may be premature before understanding the situation
- Option C fails to address the ethical challenge
