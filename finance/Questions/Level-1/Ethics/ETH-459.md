# Question

A supervisor delegates compliance oversight to a junior employee without providing adequate training or resources. If violations occur:

## Options

- **A**: Only the junior employee is responsible
- **B**: The supervisor may be liable for failing to establish adequate supervisory systems
- **C**: Delegation fully transfers supervisory responsibility

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(C) holds supervisors responsible for establishing reasonable supervision systems. Delegating to untrained personnel without adequate resources does not meet the reasonable supervision standard. Supervisors cannot escape responsibility through inadequate delegation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the supervisor's responsibility for delegation
- Option C incorrectly transfers full responsibility through delegation
