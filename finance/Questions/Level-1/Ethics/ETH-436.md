# Question

Disclosure under Standard VI(A) should be:

## Options

- **A**: Only in legal documents clients rarely read
- **B**: Prominent and delivered in plain language
- **C**: Optional for sophisticated investors

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(A) requires that disclosures be made prominently and in plain language clients can understand. Buried or technical disclosures fail to achieve the transparency objective of the Standard.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A hides disclosures in ineffective locations
- Option C varies disclosure based on client sophistication
