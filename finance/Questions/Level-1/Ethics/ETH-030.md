# Question

The ethical requirement to "use reasonable care and exercise independent professional judgment" is most closely related to which professional obligation?

## Options

- **A**: Conducting thorough analysis before making investment recommendations
- **B**: Maintaining client confidentiality at all times
- **C**: Reporting suspected violations to regulatory authorities

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Using reasonable care and exercising independent professional judgment directly relates to the analytical process underlying investment recommendations. Professionals must thoroughly research and analyze investments, consider relevant factors, and form independent opinions rather than simply following others' recommendations or relying on superficial analysis.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B relates to confidentiality duties, which is a separate ethical requirement
- Option C relates to reporting obligations, not the analytical judgment process
