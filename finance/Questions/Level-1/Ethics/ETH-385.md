# Question

Standard VII(A) requires CFA candidates to:

## Options

- **A**: Share exam content with other candidates after the exam
- **B**: Not engage in conduct that compromises the integrity of the CFA exam
- **C**: Only follow rules explicitly stated in the exam booklet

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(A) prohibits conduct that compromises the reputation or integrity of the CFA designation or exam. This includes maintaining exam security and not sharing confidential exam content.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A directly violates the Standard
- Option C too narrowly interprets the scope of required conduct
