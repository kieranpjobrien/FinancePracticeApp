# Question

A company CEO tells one analyst about upcoming earnings before public announcement. The analyst:

## Options

- **A**: May trade immediately since she obtained the information legally
- **B**: Has received MNPI and must not trade until it becomes public
- **C**: May share with colleagues at her firm only

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Regardless of how the information was obtained, material nonpublic information cannot be used for trading. The CEO's selective disclosure doesn't make the information public or legal to trade on.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses legal acquisition with legal use
- Option C extends the violation to others at the firm
