# Question

An analyst serves on the board of a company she covers. Under Standard VI(A), she must:

## Options

- **A**: Disclose this relationship in all research reports on the company
- **B**: Keep the board position confidential to avoid confusion
- **C**: Only disclose if she owns stock in the company

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Standard VI(A) requires disclosure of all matters that could reasonably impair objectivity. Board membership creates a significant conflict that must be disclosed to allow clients to evaluate the analyst's independence.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B hides a material conflict
- Option C ignores non-ownership conflicts of interest
