# Question

A portfolio manager's personal brokerage account holds the same securities as client accounts. Under Standard III(B), when executing a block trade:

## Options

- **A**: The manager's personal orders must receive no better execution than client orders
- **B**: The manager's orders should be executed first to reduce personal risk
- **C**: The manager cannot hold the same securities as clients

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Standard III(B) requires that members' personal transactions not disadvantage clients. When participating in block trades, personal accounts should not receive preferential pricing or allocation over client accounts.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B prioritizes the manager's interests over clients, violating fair dealing
- Option C is overly restrictive; holding the same securities is permitted with proper management
