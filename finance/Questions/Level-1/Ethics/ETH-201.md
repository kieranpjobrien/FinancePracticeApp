# Question

Under Standard IV(A), research reports created during employment belong to:

## Options

- **A**: The analyst who created them
- **B**: The employer
- **C**: Jointly to the analyst and employer

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Work product created during employment, including research reports, is generally considered employer property under Standard IV(A). Employees cannot take or copy proprietary materials when leaving the firm.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses authorship with ownership
- Option C misunderstands the employment relationship
