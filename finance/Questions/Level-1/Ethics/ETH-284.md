# Question

CFA candidates owe duties under Standard VII(A) to:

## Options

- **A**: Only CFA Institute as an organization
- **B**: CFA Institute, the CFA Program, and other candidates
- **C**: Only fellow candidates taking the same exam

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(A) protects the integrity of CFA Institute, the CFA Program, and the designation. Candidates owe duties to the institution, the program's integrity, and indirectly to other candidates who deserve a fair testing environment.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores program and candidate interests
- Option C too narrowly defines affected parties
