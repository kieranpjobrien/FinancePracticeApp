# Question

Standard V(A) Diligence and Reasonable Basis requires that investment recommendations be based on:

## Options

- **A**: The most popular consensus view among analysts
- **B**: Thorough research and appropriate investigation
- **C**: Instructions from senior management

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(A) requires members to have a reasonable and adequate basis for investment recommendations, supported by appropriate research and investigation. Recommendations should not simply follow consensus or management preferences without independent analysis.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A substitutes consensus for independent analysis
- Option C prioritizes management direction over analytical rigor
