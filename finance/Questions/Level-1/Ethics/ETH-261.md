# Question

A new portfolio must be included in the appropriate composite:

## Options

- **A**: Immediately upon inception
- **B**: On a timely and consistent basis according to firm policies
- **C**: Only after one year of performance history

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS requires that new portfolios be included in composites on a timely and consistent basis, following documented firm policies. This prevents cherry-picking based on early performance while allowing reasonable time for full investment.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A may not allow time for full investment
- Option C delays inclusion far longer than permitted
