# Question

A portfolio manager posts exaggerated performance claims on personal social media. Under Standard I(C):

## Options

- **A**: Personal social media is not covered by professional standards
- **B**: Misrepresentation on any platform that reaches clients or prospects violates the Standard
- **C**: Only official firm communications are subject to the Standard

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(C) applies to all communications that could be used to solicit or retain clients. Personal social media that discusses professional achievements reaches prospective clients and must be truthful, not exaggerated.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly excludes personal platforms
- Option C too narrowly defines covered communications
