# Question

An analyst discovers that a colleague has been falsifying expense reports. Under Standard I(A), the analyst should:

## Options

- **A**: Report directly to law enforcement immediately
- **B**: Report the suspected violation through appropriate internal channels
- **C**: Ignore it since expense reports are not investment-related

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(A) requires members to report suspected violations through appropriate channels, typically starting with internal compliance or supervisory personnel. External reporting may follow if internal channels are ineffective, but initial reporting should be internal.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A skips required internal reporting steps
- Option C incorrectly limits the scope of reportable misconduct
