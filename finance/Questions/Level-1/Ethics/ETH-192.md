# Question

An analyst develops a valuation model but omits discussion of its limitations in the research report. This most likely violates Standard I(C) because:

## Options

- **A**: All models must be approved by compliance before publication
- **B**: Omitting known limitations misrepresents the reliability of the analysis
- **C**: Models cannot be used in published research

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(C) prohibits misrepresentation, including omissions that create false impressions. Failing to disclose model limitations could mislead readers about the reliability of conclusions, even if the model's outputs are accurately reported.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A addresses a procedural issue not related to misrepresentation
- Option C incorrectly prohibits model use in research
