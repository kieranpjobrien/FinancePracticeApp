# Question

GIPS verification by an independent third party:

## Options

- **A**: Is mandatory for all firms claiming GIPS compliance
- **B**: Is recommended but not required for GIPS compliance
- **C**: Guarantees that all composite performances are calculated correctly

## Answer

B

## Explanation

While verification by an independent party adds credibility to a firm's claim of GIPS compliance, it is not required. Firms can claim compliance without verification. However, verification provides additional assurance to prospective clients that the firm has properly applied GIPS policies and procedures.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly states verification is mandatory
- Option C overstates verification scope; it tests processes and policies, not individual calculations
