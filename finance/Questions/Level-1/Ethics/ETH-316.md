# Question

The primary purpose of the CFA Institute Code of Ethics is to:

## Options

- **A**: Create barriers to entry in the investment profession
- **B**: Establish standards of conduct that protect clients and markets
- **C**: Provide legal defense for members

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The Code of Ethics establishes ethical standards that protect clients and promote market integrity. It sets expectations for professional conduct rather than creating entry barriers or providing legal protection.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A misunderstands the Code's purpose
- Option C confuses ethical standards with legal protections
