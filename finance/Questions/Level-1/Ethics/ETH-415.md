# Question

GIPS-compliant firms must present performance for a minimum of:

## Options

- **A**: One year of history
- **B**: Five years, or since firm inception if less than five years
- **C**: Ten years in all cases

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS requires at least five years of compliant performance history, or since firm or composite inception if shorter. After presenting five years, firms must build toward a minimum of ten years of track record.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A sets the threshold too low
- Option C does not account for newer firms or composites
