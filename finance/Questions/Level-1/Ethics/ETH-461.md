# Question

Standard V(B) requires disclosure of the investment process used because:

## Options

- **A**: It protects the analyst from liability
- **B**: Clients need to understand how decisions are made to evaluate appropriateness
- **C**: Regulators require detailed methodology documentation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(B) emphasizes that clients should understand the investment process to evaluate whether it meets their needs. Transparency about methodology enables informed client decisions rather than simply protecting the analyst or satisfying regulators.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A focuses on analyst protection rather than client benefit
- Option C emphasizes regulatory compliance over client needs
