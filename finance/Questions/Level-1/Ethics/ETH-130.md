# Question

A financial firm maintains an information barrier between its investment banking and research departments. An analyst accidentally receives material nonpublic information about an upcoming acquisition. The analyst should:

## Options

- **A**: Trade on the information before it becomes public
- **B**: Report the breach and refrain from trading or sharing the information
- **C**: Share the information only with the research director

## Answer

B

## Explanation

When information barriers are breached, the recipient must immediately report the breach and refrain from trading on or further disseminating the information. Trading or sharing would violate Standard II(A) regardless of how the information was obtained.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A would constitute insider trading
- Option C would further spread the MNPI in violation of the Standard
