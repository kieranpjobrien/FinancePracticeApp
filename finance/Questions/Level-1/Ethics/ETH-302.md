# Question

A client offers a portfolio manager a personal bonus tied to portfolio performance above benchmark. Under Standard IV(B):

## Options

- **A**: Performance-based bonuses from clients are always prohibited
- **B**: The arrangement must be disclosed to and approved by the employer
- **C**: Only fixed bonuses require disclosure

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(B) requires disclosure and consent for additional compensation from clients. Performance-based arrangements create particular concern as they may incentivize risk-taking, but are permissible with proper approval.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is too restrictive
- Option C incorrectly limits disclosure to fixed amounts
