# Question

A manager receives revenue sharing payments from a fund company based on client assets placed in their funds. Standard VI(C) requires:

## Options

- **A**: Disclosure only at the point of sale
- **B**: Prominent, ongoing disclosure of the revenue sharing arrangement
- **C**: No disclosure since this is standard industry practice

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(C) requires disclosure of referral arrangements including revenue sharing. Ongoing payments create continuous conflicts that warrant ongoing disclosure, not just at initial sale. Industry prevalence does not eliminate disclosure requirements.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A treats disclosure as one-time
- Option C uses industry practice to avoid disclosure
