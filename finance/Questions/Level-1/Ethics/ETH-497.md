# Question

GIPS requires disclosure of total firm assets to:

## Options

- **A**: Demonstrate the firm's profitability
- **B**: Provide context for composite size and the firm's overall investment management activities
- **C**: Satisfy regulatory capital requirements

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Total firm assets disclosure provides context about the firm's scale and the relative significance of each composite. This helps prospects understand how representative composite results are of the firm's activities.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses assets with profitability
- Option C misidentifies the purpose as regulatory
