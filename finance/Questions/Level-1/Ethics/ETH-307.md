# Question

"Full and fair disclosure" of conflicts requires:

## Options

- **A**: Hiding conflicts that might embarrass the firm
- **B**: Complete, understandable disclosure that enables informed decisions
- **C**: Disclosure only of conflicts the member considers significant

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(A) requires disclosure sufficient for affected parties to understand the conflict and make informed decisions. Selective or minimal disclosure fails the "full and fair" requirement.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A prioritizes firm reputation over disclosure
- Option C allows subjective filtering of relevant conflicts
