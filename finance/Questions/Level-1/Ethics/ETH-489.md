# Question

A firm is being acquired and the buyer plans to discard historical research records. A member concerned about Standard V(C) should:

## Options

- **A**: Accept the buyer's decision as beyond their control
- **B**: Advocate for preserving records sufficient to support past recommendations
- **C**: Personally retain copies of all records

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(C) creates obligations to maintain supporting records. Members should advocate for preservation during transitions. However, taking records personally may violate property rights, so advocacy for institutional preservation is appropriate.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A passively accepts inadequate record keeping
- Option C may create property right issues
