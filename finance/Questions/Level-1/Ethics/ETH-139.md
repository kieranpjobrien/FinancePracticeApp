# Question

Standard III(D) Performance Presentation requires that when communicating investment performance:

## Options

- **A**: Only time-weighted returns may be used
- **B**: Information be fair, accurate, and complete
- **C**: Performance be compared to at least three benchmarks

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(D) requires that performance information be presented fairly, accurately, and completely. The specific methodology or number of benchmarks is not mandated, but the information must not mislead clients.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is too restrictive; different return calculations may be appropriate for different purposes
- Option C invents a specific benchmark requirement that doesn't exist in the Standard
