# Question

High ethical standards in the investment profession are important primarily because:

## Options

- **A**: They reduce the need for government regulation and oversight of financial markets
- **B**: They build and maintain trust, which is essential for financial markets to function effectively
- **C**: They ensure that all investment professionals earn similar levels of compensation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Trust is the foundation of financial markets. Investors must trust that markets are fair, that information is accurate, and that investment professionals will act in their interests. Without this trust, investors would be unwilling to participate, capital formation would suffer, and economic growth would be impaired. High ethical standards build and maintain this essential trust.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A reverses the relationship—while ethics may complement regulation, reducing regulation is not the primary purpose of ethical standards; ethics often goes beyond regulatory requirements
- Option C is incorrect—ethical standards are about proper conduct, not compensation levels; pay varies based on skill, experience, and market forces
