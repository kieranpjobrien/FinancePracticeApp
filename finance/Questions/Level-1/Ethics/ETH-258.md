# Question

A CFA candidate copies and sells unauthorized CFA exam preparation materials. Under Standard VII(A):

## Options

- **A**: This violates only copyright law, not the Standards
- **B**: This compromises the integrity of the CFA Program and violates the Standard
- **C**: Candidates are not bound by the Standards until they become charterholders

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(A) applies to candidates and requires conduct that protects the integrity of CFA Institute programs. Selling unauthorized materials compromises program integrity and violates the Standard, in addition to any legal issues.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the ethical dimension under the Standards
- Option C incorrectly suggests candidates aren't bound by the Standards
