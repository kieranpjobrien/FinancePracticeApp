# Question

Under Standard III(A) Loyalty, Prudence, and Care, members must place:

## Options

- **A**: Employer interests first in all circumstances
- **B**: Client interests before their own and their employer's interests
- **C**: Their own interests first when managing personal accounts

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(A) establishes the fundamental duty to place client interests above those of the member and the employer. This fiduciary principle is central to the trust relationship between investment professionals and their clients.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A reverses the proper priority
- Option C directly contradicts the client-first principle
