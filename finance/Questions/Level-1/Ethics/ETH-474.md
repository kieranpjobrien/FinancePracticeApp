# Question

A vendor invites an analyst to a sponsored industry conference at a resort location with paid travel and accommodations. Standard I(B) suggests:

## Options

- **A**: Accepting is fine since the conference has educational value
- **B**: The analyst should pay own travel expenses to maintain independence
- **C**: Acceptance is only prohibited if explicitly tied to trading commitments

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(B) cautions against gifts that may compromise independence. Paying one's own expenses removes the appearance of being beholden to vendors. Educational value does not eliminate independence concerns.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores independence concerns from valuable gifts
- Option C sets too narrow a standard for prohibited inducements
