# Question

When making an ethical decision, identifying stakeholders helps because:

## Options

- **A**: It reduces the number of people affected by the decision
- **B**: It ensures consideration of all parties who might be impacted
- **C**: It eliminates conflicts of interest

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Identifying stakeholders is a key step in ethical decision-making because it ensures the decision-maker considers all parties who might be affected. This comprehensive view helps avoid unintended consequences and ethical blindspots.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A misunderstands the purpose of stakeholder identification
- Option C overstates what stakeholder analysis accomplishes
