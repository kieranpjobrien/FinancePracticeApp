# Question

At a company holiday party, an analyst overhears executives discussing an unannounced merger. The analyst:

## Options

- **A**: May trade since she obtained the information accidentally
- **B**: Must not trade on the information regardless of how it was obtained
- **C**: May trade if she leaves the party immediately

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard II(A) prohibits trading on material nonpublic information regardless of how it was obtained. The prohibition applies to information received accidentally, inadvertently, or through social situations—not just information actively sought.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly exempts accidentally obtained information
- Option C suggests that physical departure changes the ethical obligation
