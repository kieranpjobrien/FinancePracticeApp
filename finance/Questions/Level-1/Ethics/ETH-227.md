# Question

An employee planning to leave may prepare to compete with their employer but may NOT:

## Options

- **A**: Update their resume
- **B**: Solicit the employer's clients while still employed
- **C**: Research potential new employers

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(A) permits reasonable preparation to leave, including resume updates and job searches. However, actively soliciting clients while still employed violates the duty of loyalty to the current employer.

**Common mistakes:**
- Options A and C describe permitted activities
