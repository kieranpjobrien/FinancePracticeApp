# Question

Standard I(B) Independence and Objectivity applies to pressure from:

## Options

- **A**: Only external sources like issuers and clients
- **B**: All sources including internal management, clients, and external parties
- **C**: Only internal management

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(B) requires members to maintain independence from all sources of influence that could compromise objectivity, whether external (clients, issuers) or internal (management, colleagues). The source of pressure is irrelevant; the threat to objectivity is what matters.

**Common mistakes:**
- Options A and C incorrectly limit the sources of prohibited influence
