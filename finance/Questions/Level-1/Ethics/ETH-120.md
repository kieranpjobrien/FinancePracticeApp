# Question

Standard I(A) Knowledge of the Law requires members to understand and comply with:

## Options

- **A**: Only the laws of their home country
- **B**: All applicable laws, rules, regulations, and the Code and Standards
- **C**: Only CFA Institute rules and regulations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(A) requires members to understand and comply with all applicable laws, rules, and regulations of any government, regulatory organization, licensing agency, or professional association governing their professional activities, as well as the Code and Standards.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is too narrow; members must comply with laws wherever they practice
- Option C ignores governmental and regulatory requirements
