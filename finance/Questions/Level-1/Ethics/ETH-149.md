# Question

A quantitative analyst develops a complex trading model but cannot explain its logic to clients. Under Standard V(A), the analyst:

## Options

- **A**: Can use the model as long as it generates positive returns
- **B**: Should ensure she understands the model sufficiently to explain its logic and limitations
- **C**: Should discontinue use of any model she did not personally develop

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(A) requires analysts to understand the tools they use. If a member cannot explain the logic and limitations of a model, she may lack the reasonable basis required for recommendations based on that model. Understanding does not require personal development, but does require comprehension.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A prioritizes returns over analytical understanding
- Option C is too restrictive; members can use models developed by others if they understand them
