# Question

Maintaining professional competence is best achieved through:

## Options

- **A**: Relying on skills and knowledge acquired during initial certification
- **B**: Ongoing education and staying current with industry developments
- **C**: Delegating complex matters to specialists rather than learning new areas

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Investment management is a dynamic field with evolving markets, products, regulations, and best practices. Professionals must continuously update their knowledge and skills to serve clients competently. Initial certification provides a foundation, but ongoing education is essential to maintain competence over a career.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A assumes static knowledge is sufficient in a dynamic industry
- Option C avoids the responsibility to maintain broad competence
