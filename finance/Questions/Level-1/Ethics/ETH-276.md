# Question

"Additional compensation" under Standard IV(B) includes:

## Options

- **A**: Only salary increases from the employer
- **B**: Benefits from parties other than the employer that could create conflicts
- **C**: Only commission-based compensation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(B) addresses benefits received from parties other than the employer that might compete with or create conflicts with the employer's interest. This includes gifts, payments, or arrangements that could influence the employee's conduct.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A addresses internal compensation, not the Standard's focus
- Option C too narrowly defines compensatory benefits
