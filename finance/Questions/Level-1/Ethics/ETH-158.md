# Question

An analyst plans to buy a stock for her personal account and also intends to recommend it for client portfolios. Under Standard VI(B), she should:

## Options

- **A**: Buy personally first to test the investment thesis
- **B**: Execute client transactions first, or simultaneously with her personal trade
- **C**: Wait at least 30 days after the client recommendation before buying personally

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(B) requires that client interests take priority. The analyst should either execute client trades before her personal trade or include her order with the client orders to ensure she doesn't benefit at clients' expense. No specific waiting period is mandated.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A constitutes front-running, which is prohibited
- Option C invents a specific time period not required by the Standard
