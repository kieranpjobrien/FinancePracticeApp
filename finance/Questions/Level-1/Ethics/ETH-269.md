# Question

An analyst's forthcoming research report contains a price target significantly above current market price. This unpublished report is:

## Options

- **A**: Material nonpublic information under Standard II(A)
- **B**: Simply an opinion that cannot be material
- **C**: Only material if the analyst is highly followed

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Unpublished research that could affect security prices can be material nonpublic information. Trading ahead of publication (front-running) violates Standard II(A), regardless of whether the content is "opinion."

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B incorrectly suggests opinions cannot be material
- Option C links materiality to analyst reputation rather than market impact
