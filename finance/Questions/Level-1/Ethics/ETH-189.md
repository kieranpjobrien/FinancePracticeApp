# Question

A CFA charterholder violates the Code of Ethics. CFA Institute's Professional Conduct Program may impose:

## Options

- **A**: Criminal penalties including imprisonment
- **B**: Sanctions ranging from private censure to revocation of the right to use the CFA designation
- **C**: Monetary fines payable to affected clients

## Answer

B

## Explanation

CFA Institute's disciplinary sanctions range from private censure to permanent revocation of membership and the right to use the CFA designation. CFA Institute cannot impose criminal penalties or order direct compensation—those are matters for courts and regulators.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses professional discipline with criminal justice
- Option C confuses professional sanctions with civil remedies
