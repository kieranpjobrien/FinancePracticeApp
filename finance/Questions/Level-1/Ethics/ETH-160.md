# Question

Standard VII(A) Conduct as Participants in CFA Institute Programs prohibits candidates from:

## Options

- **A**: Studying in groups for the CFA exam
- **B**: Revealing or discussing specific exam questions
- **C**: Taking the exam in a different city than originally scheduled

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(A) prohibits disclosing or discussing the specific content of CFA exam questions. This protects exam integrity and ensures fairness among all candidates. Study groups and administrative changes are not prohibited.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is allowed; collaborative study is encouraged
- Option C is an administrative matter, not an ethical violation
