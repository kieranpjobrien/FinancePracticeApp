# Question

A firm requires pre-clearance for all employee trades. An analyst executes a trade without obtaining clearance. This:

## Options

- **A**: Is acceptable if the trade did not conflict with client interests
- **B**: Violates firm policy designed to ensure compliance with Standard VI(B)
- **C**: Is only a problem if the trade was profitable

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Pre-clearance policies support Standard VI(B) compliance. Bypassing the process, regardless of outcome, undermines the system designed to prevent conflicts and ensure client transaction priority.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A uses outcomes to judge procedural compliance
- Option C incorrectly ties violation to profitability
