# Question

Standard V(B) requires investment reports to include:

## Options

- **A**: Only buy or sell recommendations without supporting analysis
- **B**: The basic format and principles of the analysis used
- **C**: A guarantee of expected returns

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(B) requires members to communicate the basic characteristics and principles of their investment analysis. This helps clients understand the basis for recommendations and make informed decisions.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A omits required supporting information
- Option C would violate the prohibition on guaranteeing returns
