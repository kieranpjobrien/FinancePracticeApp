# Question

A compliance officer suspects that a trader is violating trading rules but lacks the authority to investigate or take disciplinary action. Under Standard IV(C), the compliance officer should:

## Options

- **A**: Take no action since she lacks authority
- **B**: Escalate the concerns to someone with appropriate authority
- **C**: Directly confront the trader and demand compliance

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(C) requires members with supervisory responsibility to make reasonable efforts to prevent and detect violations. When a member lacks authority to act directly, she must escalate to those who have such authority. Inaction is not acceptable when violations are suspected.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A fails the duty to make reasonable efforts
- Option C may not be effective and bypasses proper channels
