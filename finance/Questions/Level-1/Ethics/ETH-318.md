# Question

A client pressures an analyst to guarantee that a recommended stock will rise. Under Standard I(B):

## Options

- **A**: Modest guarantees are acceptable to maintain the client relationship
- **B**: The analyst must refuse and explain why guarantees cannot be made
- **C**: Guarantees are only prohibited in writing

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(B) and I(C) prohibit guaranteeing returns. Analysts must maintain objectivity and refuse client pressure to make promises they cannot keep, regardless of relationship considerations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A allows client pressure to override ethical requirements
- Option C incorrectly limits the prohibition to written statements
