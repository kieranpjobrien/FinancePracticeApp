# Question

Compliance with a reasonable non-compete agreement after leaving employment:

## Options

- **A**: Is not required under the Standards
- **B**: May be part of the duty to employers under Standard IV(A)
- **C**: Violates the employee's right to work

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(A) encompasses duties that survive employment, including honoring reasonable contractual obligations like non-compete agreements. Legal enforceability varies by jurisdiction, but ethical obligations may still apply.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores post-employment ethical duties
- Option C assumes all such agreements are unreasonable
