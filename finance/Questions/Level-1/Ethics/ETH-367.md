# Question

An issuer threatens to withhold business unless the analyst maintains a favorable rating. Under Standard I(B):

## Options

- **A**: The analyst should consider the business implications before changing the rating
- **B**: The analyst must maintain objectivity and issue ratings based on merit, regardless of threats
- **C**: The analyst should escalate to management to decide the rating

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(B) requires independence from external pressure. Analysts must issue ratings based on objective analysis, not external threats. Yielding to such pressure compromises the integrity of the research process.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A allows business considerations to influence ratings
- Option C abdicates analytical responsibility
