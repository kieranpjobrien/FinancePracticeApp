# Question

When managing a trust with multiple beneficiaries who have conflicting interests, Standard III(A) requires the manager to:

## Options

- **A**: Favor the beneficiary with the largest stake
- **B**: Act impartially and fairly among all beneficiaries
- **C**: Follow only the instructions of the primary beneficiary

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(A) requires acting for the benefit of clients, which includes all beneficiaries. When managing assets with multiple beneficiaries, the manager must balance interests fairly and act impartially, not favor particular beneficiaries.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A prioritizes by stake size rather than fiduciary duty
- Option C ignores the duty to all beneficiaries
