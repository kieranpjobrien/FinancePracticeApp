# Question

A vendor offers an analyst equity in a startup in exchange for favorable research coverage. Under Standard IV(B):

## Options

- **A**: This may be accepted with employer disclosure
- **B**: This creates an unacceptable conflict that should be declined
- **C**: Equity compensation is not covered by the Standard

## Answer

B

## Explanation

While Standard IV(B) addresses employer disclosure for additional compensation, equity tied to favorable coverage creates an independence conflict under Standard I(B). Such arrangements compromise objectivity and should be declined regardless of disclosure.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the independence violation
- Option C incorrectly excludes equity from compensation
