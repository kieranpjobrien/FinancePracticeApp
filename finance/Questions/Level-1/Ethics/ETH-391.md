# Question

The CFA Institute Code of Ethics requires members to:

## Options

- **A**: Maximize returns regardless of methods used
- **B**: Place the integrity of the investment profession and client interests above personal interests
- **C**: Follow only the rules of their local jurisdiction

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The Code of Ethics establishes that members must prioritize professional integrity and client interests above their own. This foundational principle supports public trust in the investment profession.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores ethical constraints on methods
- Option C limits ethical obligations to local legal requirements
