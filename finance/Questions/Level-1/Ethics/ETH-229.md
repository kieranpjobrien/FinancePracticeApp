# Question

An analyst signs off on a group research report without reviewing one section prepared by a colleague. If that section contains errors, under Standard V(A):

## Options

- **A**: Only the colleague who prepared that section is responsible
- **B**: The analyst may share responsibility for issuing the report without proper review
- **C**: Group reports have lower individual accountability

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(A) requires reasonable basis for recommendations. When signing off on group research, analysts take responsibility for the overall product. Failing to review sections they're approving may constitute inadequate diligence.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores shared responsibility in group work
- Option C incorrectly suggests reduced accountability
