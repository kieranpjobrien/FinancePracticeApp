# Question

A candidate observes another candidate using prohibited materials during the CFA exam. Under Standard VII(A):

## Options

- **A**: The observing candidate should ignore it to avoid conflict
- **B**: The observing candidate should report the incident to proctors or CFA Institute
- **C**: Candidates are only responsible for their own conduct

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(A) requires conduct that protects the integrity of CFA Institute programs. Reporting suspected cheating helps preserve exam integrity. While candidates are primarily responsible for their own conduct, ignoring known violations undermines the program.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A fails to protect program integrity
- Option C too narrowly defines responsibility
