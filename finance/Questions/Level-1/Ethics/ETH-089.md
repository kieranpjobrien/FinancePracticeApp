# Question

The priority of transactions standard requires that:

## Options

- **A**: Client and employer transactions receive priority over personal transactions
- **B**: Personal transactions be completed before market open each day
- **C**: All transactions be executed in chronological order of receipt

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The priority of transactions standard establishes a hierarchy: client transactions come first, followed by employer transactions, with personal transactions last. This ensures that professionals do not benefit at the expense of those they serve and that conflicts between personal and professional interests are resolved in favor of clients.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B addresses timing but not the priority hierarchy
- Option C focuses on order receipt rather than the client-first principle
