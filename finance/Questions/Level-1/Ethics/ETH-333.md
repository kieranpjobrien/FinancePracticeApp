# Question

When aggregating client orders with personal orders, Standard VI(B) requires:

## Options

- **A**: Personal orders receive priority to build employee commitment
- **B**: Fair allocation with no advantage to personal accounts
- **C**: Separation of all personal from client trading

## Answer

B

## Explanation

When personal orders are included in block trades with client orders, allocation must be fair. Personal accounts should not receive better prices or fills than client accounts in the same aggregated transaction.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A reverses the required priority
- Option C may be more conservative but isn't required
