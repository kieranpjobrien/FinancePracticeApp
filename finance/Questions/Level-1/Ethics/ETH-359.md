# Question

Actions that violate Standard VII(A) include:

## Options

- **A**: Only cheating on the exam
- **B**: Any conduct that compromises the reputation or integrity of CFA Institute or the CFA Program
- **C**: Only sharing actual exam questions

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(A) broadly covers conduct that compromises the reputation or integrity of CFA Institute or the CFA Program. This includes but is not limited to cheating and question sharing.

**Common mistakes:**
- Options A and C each identify only specific violations rather than the broader principle
