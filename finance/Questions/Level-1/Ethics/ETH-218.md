# Question

A buy-side analyst receives a draft research report from a sell-side firm before publication for comment. Under Standard I(B):

## Options

- **A**: The analyst must immediately destroy the draft
- **B**: Providing feedback is acceptable, but the analyst should not use MNPI from the draft
- **C**: The analyst may trade based on the draft's conclusions

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Receiving draft research for feedback is a common practice. Standard I(B) requires the analyst to maintain independence and not trade on any material nonpublic information that may be contained in unpublished research.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A overreacts to normal business practice
- Option C ignores potential MNPI concerns
