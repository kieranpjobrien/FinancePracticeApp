# Question

An employee reports her employer's securities violations to regulators after internal reporting failed. Under Standard IV(A):

## Options

- **A**: This violates the duty of loyalty to employers
- **B**: Whistleblowing may be appropriate when internal channels fail to address illegal conduct
- **C**: Employees must never report employers to external authorities

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(A) does not require loyalty to illegal conduct. When internal reporting fails to address violations, whistleblowing to appropriate authorities may be necessary and does not violate the Standards.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A misinterprets loyalty as requiring complicity in wrongdoing
- Option C incorrectly prohibits legitimate whistleblowing
