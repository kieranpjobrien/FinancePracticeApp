# Question

A supervisor relies on the firm's compliance department to monitor employee trading. Under Standard IV(C), this:

## Options

- **A**: Is insufficient; supervisors must personally review all trades
- **B**: May be acceptable if the compliance procedures are reasonable and the supervisor periodically ensures they are followed
- **C**: Completely absolves the supervisor of any monitoring responsibility

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(C) allows supervisors to delegate monitoring to compliance functions if the procedures are reasonable and the supervisor takes steps to ensure they are being followed. Delegation does not eliminate supervisory responsibility but can be part of reasonable supervision.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A sets an unrealistic standard for personal review
- Option C incorrectly suggests delegation eliminates responsibility
