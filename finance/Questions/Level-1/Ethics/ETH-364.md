# Question

Ethical behavior in the investment profession benefits:

## Options

- **A**: Only the individual practitioner
- **B**: Practitioners, clients, and society through better functioning markets
- **C**: Only regulatory compliance officers

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Ethical conduct creates benefits at multiple levels: practitioners build trusted reputations, clients receive fair treatment, and society benefits from efficient capital allocation through well-functioning markets.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores systemic benefits
- Option C misunderstands who benefits from ethical behavior
