# Question

An analyst's firm seeks investment banking business from a company the analyst covers. To maintain independence under Standard I(B):

## Options

- **A**: The analyst should coordinate ratings with investment banking timelines
- **B**: Policies should separate research from investment banking influence
- **C**: Research can favor the company while the banking relationship is pursued

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(B) requires independence from investment banking pressure. Firms should implement information barriers and policies that prevent banking relationships from influencing research. Research must be based on objective analysis regardless of banking aspirations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A explicitly coordinates research with banking interests
- Option C allows banking interests to bias research
