# Question

A portfolio manager relies entirely on a quantitative model developed by another team without understanding its methodology. This most likely violates which aspect of the Code of Ethics?

## Options

- **A**: The requirement to maintain confidentiality
- **B**: The requirement to act with competence and use reasonable care
- **C**: The prohibition on market manipulation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Using tools or models without understanding their methodology violates the Code's requirement to act with competence. Members must have a reasonable understanding of the methods they use to make investment decisions, even if they didn't develop those methods personally.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A relates to protecting client information, not analytical competence
- Option C addresses market manipulation, which is unrelated to analytical understanding
