# Question

A firm systematically routes first fills to its largest accounts. Under Standard III(B):

## Options

- **A**: This is acceptable since larger accounts need more liquidity
- **B**: This creates unfair treatment of smaller clients
- **C**: Trade routing is not covered by the Standards

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(B) requires fair dealing among clients. Systematically favoring larger accounts with better executions disadvantages smaller clients and violates fair dealing, regardless of operational justifications.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A rationalizes discrimination by account size
- Option C incorrectly excludes execution quality from fair dealing
