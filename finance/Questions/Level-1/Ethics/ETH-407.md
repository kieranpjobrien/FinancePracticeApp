# Question

An analyst's report highlights a stock's upside potential but omits discussion of significant downside risks. This:

## Options

- **A**: Is acceptable if the analyst is bullish on the stock
- **B**: Violates Standard V(B) by failing to communicate material risks
- **C**: Is permitted if the overall recommendation is positive

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(B) requires balanced communication including discussion of factors that could affect investment value. Omitting significant risks, regardless of the analyst's view, fails to provide complete information for client decision-making.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A allows conviction to override disclosure requirements
- Option C incorrectly suggests positive recommendations excuse risk omission
