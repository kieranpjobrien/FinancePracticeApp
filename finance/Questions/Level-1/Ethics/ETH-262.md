# Question

Under GIPS, portfolios should be valued using:

## Options

- **A**: Cost basis to ensure consistency
- **B**: Fair value based on current market prices or reasonable estimates
- **C**: The higher of cost or market

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS requires fair value as the basis for valuation. Fair value uses actual market prices where available, or reasonable estimates for illiquid investments. Cost-based or conservative accounting methods don't represent fair value.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores market movements since purchase
- Option C applies an accounting convention inappropriate for GIPS
