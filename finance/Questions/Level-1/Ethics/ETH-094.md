# Question

A member becomes aware that a colleague has violated professional standards by falsifying client performance reports. The member should:

## Options

- **A**: Take no action since it is not her responsibility to police others
- **B**: Consider reporting the conduct to appropriate authorities or the professional body
- **C**: Discuss the violation with the colleague privately to give them a chance to correct it first

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Members have a responsibility to the profession and may need to report observed violations to maintain the integrity of the profession. While the specific actions required may vary, being aware of violations and taking no action can enable continued misconduct that harms clients and the profession. Reporting to appropriate bodies helps maintain professional standards.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores responsibility to the profession and potential ongoing client harm
- Option C may be appropriate as a first step but is insufficient for serious violations involving client harm
