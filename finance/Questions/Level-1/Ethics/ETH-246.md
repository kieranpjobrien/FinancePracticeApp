# Question

A client directs the manager to use a specific broker that charges higher commissions. Under Standard III(A):

## Options

- **A**: The manager must refuse and use lower-cost brokers
- **B**: The manager may comply if the client understands the cost impact
- **C**: Directed brokerage always violates best execution

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Client-directed brokerage is permissible under Standard III(A) if the client understands the impact on costs and execution quality. The manager should document the client's direction and ensure the client is making an informed decision.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores client autonomy
- Option C overstates restrictions on directed brokerage
