# Question

Investment communications should be written:

## Options

- **A**: Using as much technical jargon as possible to demonstrate expertise
- **B**: In a manner appropriate to the intended audience
- **C**: Only in legal language to limit liability

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(B) requires communications appropriate to the audience. Retail clients may need simpler explanations, while institutional clients can handle technical detail. The goal is effective communication, not complexity for its own sake.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A prioritizes appearing knowledgeable over client understanding
- Option C focuses on liability rather than communication effectiveness
