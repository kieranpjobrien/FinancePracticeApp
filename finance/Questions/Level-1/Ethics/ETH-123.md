# Question

An analyst writes research reports that are paid for by the companies being analyzed. To comply with Standard I(B), the analyst must:

## Options

- **A**: Decline all issuer-paid research assignments
- **B**: Disclose the nature of the compensation arrangement in the research reports
- **C**: Ensure the issuer approves all content before publication

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Issuer-paid research is permitted under Standard I(B) provided the analyst discloses the compensation arrangement prominently. This allows readers to understand potential conflicts and evaluate the research accordingly. Allowing issuer approval of content would compromise independence.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is overly restrictive; issuer-paid research is acceptable with disclosure
- Option C would actually violate independence requirements
