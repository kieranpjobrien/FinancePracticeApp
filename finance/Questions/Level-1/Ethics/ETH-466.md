# Question

A candidate is caught using prohibited materials during the CFA exam. This violates:

## Options

- **A**: Only the exam center rules
- **B**: Standard VII(A) regarding conduct that compromises exam integrity
- **C**: Only local academic honesty policies

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(A) prohibits conduct that compromises the reputation or integrity of the CFA designation, including cheating on exams. Such behavior undermines the credibility of the entire CFA program.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A treats cheating as only a procedural matter
- Option C limits the violation to local policies
