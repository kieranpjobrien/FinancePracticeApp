# Question

A member's firm wants to invest in a country subject to international sanctions. Under Standard I(A):

## Options

- **A**: The investment is acceptable if profitable
- **B**: The member must ensure compliance with applicable sanctions laws
- **C**: International sanctions only apply to government investors

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(A) requires compliance with applicable laws including international sanctions. Members must ensure investments comply with all relevant legal restrictions, regardless of potential profitability.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A prioritizes returns over legal compliance
- Option C incorrectly limits sanctions applicability
