# Question

A supervisor notices unusual trading patterns in a subordinate's accounts but takes no action because quarterly compliance reviews are upcoming. This approach:

## Options

- **A**: Is acceptable since scheduled reviews will address any issues
- **B**: May violate Standard IV(C) by failing to respond promptly to potential violations
- **C**: Complies with the Standard if the supervisor documents the observations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(C) requires prompt investigation of potential violations. Supervisors cannot wait for scheduled reviews when red flags appear. Timely response to suspicious activity is essential for effective supervision.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A delays action when immediate investigation is warranted
- Option C substitutes documentation for appropriate action
