# Question

An analyst's parent owns 15% of a company the analyst covers. Under Standard VI(A):

## Options

- **A**: Only direct ownership requires disclosure
- **B**: Beneficial ownership through close family members should be disclosed
- **C**: Family holdings are always confidential

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(A) requires disclosure of matters that could impair objectivity. Significant ownership by close family members creates potential conflicts that should be disclosed, even though ownership is indirect.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A too narrowly defines relevant ownership
- Option C prioritizes privacy over conflict disclosure
