# Question

A department head delegates compliance monitoring to a junior employee without providing training or reviewing the employee's work. If violations occur that the junior employee fails to detect, the department head:

## Options

- **A**: Is not responsible since the task was properly delegated
- **B**: May be held responsible for failure to adequately supervise the delegated function
- **C**: Is only responsible if she had direct knowledge of the violations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Supervisory responsibility cannot be fully discharged through delegation. Supervisors must ensure that delegated functions are performed properly, which includes providing adequate training and oversight. Delegating a task without proper support and monitoring does not relieve the supervisor of responsibility for ensuring compliance.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly suggests delegation eliminates supervisory responsibility
- Option C focuses on knowledge rather than the duty to establish adequate supervisory systems
