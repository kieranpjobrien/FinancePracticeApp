# Question

The level of research required under Standard V(A) depends on:

## Options

- **A**: A fixed standard applicable to all situations
- **B**: The complexity of the investment and resources reasonably available
- **C**: Only the client's sophistication level

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(A) requires "reasonable" diligence, which varies based on investment complexity and available resources. More complex investments warrant deeper analysis, while resource constraints may affect practical scope.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores situational factors
- Option C limits consideration to just one factor
