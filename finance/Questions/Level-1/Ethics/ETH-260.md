# Question

GIPS-compliant presentations are primarily intended for:

## Options

- **A**: Regulatory filings only
- **B**: Prospective and existing clients to evaluate firm performance
- **C**: Internal management reporting

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS are designed to provide prospective and existing clients with standardized, comparable performance presentations. While they may satisfy regulatory needs, their primary purpose is client communication and fair representation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A limits GIPS to regulatory use
- Option C focuses on internal rather than external communication
