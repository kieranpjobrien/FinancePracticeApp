# Question

A firm experiences a data breach exposing client information. Under Standard III(E), the member should:

## Options

- **A**: Keep the breach confidential to avoid reputational damage
- **B**: Ensure appropriate notification to affected clients and take steps to prevent future breaches
- **C**: Only disclose if specifically required by data protection laws

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(E) creates an obligation to protect client confidentiality. When that protection fails through a breach, members should support appropriate client notification and remediation, not conceal the incident from affected parties.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A prioritizes reputation over client interests
- Option C sets minimum legal compliance rather than ethical response
