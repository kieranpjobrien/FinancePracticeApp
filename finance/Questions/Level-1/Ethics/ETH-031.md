# Question

A code of ethics requirement to deal fairly with all clients and prospects most likely means that investment professionals should:

## Options

- **A**: Treat all clients identically regardless of account size or service level
- **B**: Not discriminate based on factors unrelated to investment considerations
- **C**: Charge the same fees to all clients for identical services

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Fair dealing means treating clients equitably without discrimination based on factors unrelated to legitimate investment considerations. It does not require identical treatment—professionals may appropriately differentiate service levels based on account size, fees paid, or service agreements. What matters is that all clients receive fair treatment appropriate to their relationship.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly interprets fair dealing as identical treatment; different service levels for different client tiers is acceptable if disclosed
- Option C addresses fee structure, which may legitimately vary; fair dealing focuses on equitable treatment, not uniform pricing
