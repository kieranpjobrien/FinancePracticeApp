# Question

A firm advertises: "Our investment process is CFA-certified." Under Standard VII(B):

## Options

- **A**: This is accurate if charterholders developed the process
- **B**: This misrepresents the CFA designation, which certifies individuals not processes
- **C**: This is acceptable marketing language

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(B) prohibits misrepresenting the CFA designation. The designation certifies that individuals have met program requirements; it does not certify investment processes, products, or firms.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A extends the designation beyond its meaning
- Option C accepts misleading marketing claims
