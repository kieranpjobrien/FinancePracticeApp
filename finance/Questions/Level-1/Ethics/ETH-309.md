# Question

Two advisors agree to refer clients to each other without any cash payment changing hands. Under Standard VI(C):

## Options

- **A**: No disclosure is needed since no cash is exchanged
- **B**: The mutual referral arrangement should still be disclosed to clients
- **C**: Referral fee rules only apply to cash payments

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(C) covers all compensation for referrals, including non-cash benefits like reciprocal referrals. The potential conflict exists regardless of whether money changes hands and should be disclosed.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly focuses only on cash
- Option C too narrowly defines covered compensation
