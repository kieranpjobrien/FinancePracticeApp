# Question

An employee criticizes their employer's investment process on social media. Under Standard IV(A):

## Options

- **A**: Personal expression is always protected
- **B**: Public criticism may violate the duty of loyalty if it harms the employer
- **C**: Social media activities have no bearing on employment duties

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(A) requires acting in the employer's interest and not causing harm. Public criticism that damages the employer's reputation or business may violate loyalty duties, even on personal social media accounts.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores professional obligations
- Option C incorrectly separates online conduct from professional duties
