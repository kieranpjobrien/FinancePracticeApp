# Question

A portfolio manager's spouse purchases shares of a stock that the manager's firm is recommending. Under Standard VI(B):

## Options

- **A**: This is not covered since the spouse is a separate person
- **B**: This may constitute a violation as beneficial ownership includes family accounts
- **C**: Spousal trades are always exempt from priority rules

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(B) extends to accounts in which the member has beneficial ownership, which typically includes spousal accounts. Family member trades that front-run client orders can violate priority of transactions requirements.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores beneficial ownership concepts
- Option C incorrectly creates a blanket exemption for family trades
