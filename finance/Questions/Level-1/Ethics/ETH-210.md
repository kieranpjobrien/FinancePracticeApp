# Question

CFA charterholders are expected to maintain their competence by:

## Options

- **A**: Retaking the CFA exams every five years
- **B**: Staying current with developments in the investment profession
- **C**: Only reading CFA Institute publications

## Answer

B

## Explanation

While no formal continuing education is mandated, charterholders are expected to maintain and improve their professional competence. This involves staying current with developments in the field through various means, not limited to any single source.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A invents a re-examination requirement that doesn't exist
- Option C incorrectly limits acceptable learning sources
