# Question

Standard VI(B) requires personal trading to be conducted:

## Options

- **A**: Before any client trading to test ideas
- **B**: After clients have been given adequate opportunity to act
- **C**: Only during market hours

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(B) requires that clients have adequate opportunity to act on recommendations before members trade for personal benefit. This prevents members from profiting at client expense through information or timing advantages.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A reverses the required priority
- Option C addresses timing of trading hours, not priority
