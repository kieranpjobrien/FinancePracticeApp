# Question

A candidate who has passed CFA Level II states on his resume: "CFA Level II Passed." Under Standard VII(B):

## Options

- **A**: This is prohibited because only charterholders may reference the CFA Program
- **B**: This is acceptable factual information about exam progress
- **C**: He should instead write "CFA Almost Complete"

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Candidates may accurately state their progress in the CFA Program, including exams passed. "CFA Level II Passed" is a factual statement. However, candidates must not misrepresent their status or imply charterholder status.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly restricts candidates from noting their progress
- Option C uses imprecise language that could mislead about actual status
