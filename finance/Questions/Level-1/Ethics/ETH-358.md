# Question

A firm requires pre-clearance of employee trades. This policy helps implement Standard VI(B) by:

## Options

- **A**: Generating additional fees from employees
- **B**: Ensuring personal trades don't conflict with client trading or firm recommendations
- **C**: Slowing down personal trading to discourage it

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Pre-clearance helps firms identify potential conflicts between personal trades and client interests before they occur. This supports Standard VI(B)'s requirement that personal transactions not disadvantage clients.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A mischaracterizes the policy's purpose
- Option C focuses on discouragement rather than conflict management
