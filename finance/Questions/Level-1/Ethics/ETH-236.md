# Question

Under GIPS, a "firm" is defined as:

## Options

- **A**: Any legal entity registered with securities regulators
- **B**: A distinct business entity held out to clients as the investment manager
- **C**: Only asset management divisions of banks

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS defines a firm as an investment firm, subsidiary, or division held out to clients or prospective clients as a distinct business entity. The definition focuses on how the entity presents itself to the market, not legal structure alone.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A focuses only on regulatory status
- Option C incorrectly limits the definition
