# Question

An analyst learns confidentially that a tender offer for a target company will be announced next week. Standard II(A):

## Options

- **A**: Permits trading if the tender offer premium is already rumored
- **B**: Prohibits trading on this material nonpublic information
- **C**: Allows trading if the analyst limits position size

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Confidential tender offer information is clearly material nonpublic information under Standard II(A). Rumors do not constitute public dissemination, and position size does not affect the prohibition on trading on MNPI.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses rumors with public information
- Option C incorrectly suggests position limits cure the violation
