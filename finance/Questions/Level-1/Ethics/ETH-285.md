# Question

A CFA charterholder may correctly state:

## Options

- **A**: "The CFA designation guarantees superior investment returns"
- **B**: "I have earned the right to use the CFA designation"
- **C**: "As a CFA, I am exempt from securities regulations"

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(B) permits accurate statements about earning the designation. Claiming guaranteed returns or regulatory exemptions would misrepresent what the designation signifies.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A makes prohibited performance guarantees
- Option C falsely claims regulatory exemption
