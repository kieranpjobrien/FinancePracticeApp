# Question

Timely communication of investment recommendations is important because:

## Options

- **A**: It allows preferred clients to trade first
- **B**: Delayed information may no longer be relevant for client decisions
- **C**: Speed of communication is more important than accuracy

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(B) recognizes that investment information has time value. Delayed recommendations may be less useful or even harmful if market conditions have changed. Timeliness should not, however, compromise accuracy.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A violates fair dealing requirements
- Option C incorrectly prioritizes speed over accuracy
