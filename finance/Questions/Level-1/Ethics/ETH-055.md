# Question

An analyst has just completed research suggesting a strong buy rating for a stock. To comply with fair dealing requirements, the analyst should:

## Options

- **A**: Inform the firm's largest clients before disseminating the research more broadly
- **B**: Disseminate the recommendation to all clients at approximately the same time
- **C**: Wait 24 hours before trading for clients to ensure personal trades are not prioritized

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Fair dealing requires that investment recommendations be disseminated to all clients fairly and without discrimination. All clients who could benefit from the recommendation should receive it at approximately the same time, not in a tiered sequence based on account size or importance. This ensures equal treatment and prevents some clients from gaining unfair advantage.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes discriminatory treatment that favors larger clients, violating fair dealing
- Option C conflates timing issues with the requirement for simultaneous dissemination; the 24-hour wait is not a standard requirement
