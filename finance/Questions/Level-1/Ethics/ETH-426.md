# Question

An analyst downgrades a stock from buy to sell. Before the report is published, a large client calls asking for an update. The analyst should:

## Options

- **A**: Provide the downgrade information to maintain the client relationship
- **B**: Indicate a report is forthcoming without revealing the rating change
- **C**: Provide the information since the client specifically asked

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(B) requires fair dealing in disseminating recommendation changes. All clients should receive changes fairly and simultaneously. Revealing the downgrade to one client before publication violates fair dealing requirements.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A prioritizes relationship over fair dealing
- Option C allows client requests to justify preferential treatment
