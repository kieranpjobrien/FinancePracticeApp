# Question

When facing an ethical dilemma, which approach best reflects the CFA Institute's ethical decision-making framework?

## Options

- **A**: Follow the action that maximizes firm profitability while staying within legal limits
- **B**: Identify stakeholders, consider consequences, and act consistently with ethical principles
- **C**: Defer to senior management's judgment on the appropriate course of action

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The CFA Institute's ethical decision-making framework emphasizes identifying all affected stakeholders, considering the consequences of various actions, and making decisions consistent with fundamental ethical principles. This systematic approach helps ensure decisions are made thoughtfully rather than reactively.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A prioritizes profits over ethical considerations
- Option C abdicates personal ethical responsibility to others
