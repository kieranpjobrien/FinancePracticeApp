# Question

A member discovers that a colleague has been front-running client orders. Under Standard I(A), the member's first step should be to:

## Options

- **A**: Immediately report to regulators
- **B**: Report to the supervisor or compliance department
- **C**: Confront the colleague privately first

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(A) recommends reporting violations through internal channels first, typically to a supervisor or compliance. This allows the firm to address the issue while the member fulfills their ethical obligation. Regulatory reporting may follow if internal resolution fails.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A skips appropriate internal reporting channels
- Option C may enable continued violations and lacks appropriate oversight
