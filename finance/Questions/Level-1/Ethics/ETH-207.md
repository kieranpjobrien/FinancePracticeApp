# Question

A research analyst's firm is seeking investment banking business from a company the analyst covers. Under Standard VI(A):

## Options

- **A**: The analyst must stop covering the company
- **B**: The potential conflict must be disclosed in research reports
- **C**: No disclosure is needed if the banking deal hasn't closed

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Potential investment banking relationships create conflicts that must be disclosed under Standard VI(A), even if the business hasn't been awarded. Readers need this information to evaluate potential bias, regardless of deal status.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is overly restrictive; disclosure manages the conflict
- Option C delays disclosure until after the conflict could affect research
