# Question

A company's debt capacity is increased by:

## Options

- **A**: High earnings volatility
- **B**: Stable cash flows and tangible assets
- **C**: Significant intangible assets

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Debt capacity increases with: stable/predictable cash flows (can reliably service debt), tangible assets (collateral value in default), low business risk, strong interest coverage. Volatile earnings, intangibles, and high operating leverage reduce debt capacity—increasing default risk.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A increases bankruptcy probability
- Option C provides poor collateral value
