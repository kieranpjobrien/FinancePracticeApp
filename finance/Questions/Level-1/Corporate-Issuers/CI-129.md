# Question

Share repurchases may be tax-advantaged compared to dividends because:

## Options

- **A**: Repurchases are never taxed
- **B**: Capital gains may be taxed at lower rates and are deferred until realization
- **C**: Dividends are tax-free for all investors

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Tax advantage: dividends are taxed immediately at dividend rates. Repurchases create capital gains taxed at potentially lower rates and only when shares are sold (deferral). Shareholders can also manage timing. However, tax laws vary and have changed over time.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A overstates—capital gains are taxed on sale
- Option C ignores dividend taxation
