# Question

According to static trade-off theory, a company's optimal capital structure:

## Options

- **A**: Contains no debt to minimize risk
- **B**: Balances the tax benefits of debt against bankruptcy and agency costs
- **C**: Maximizes debt to maximize tax shields

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Static trade-off: optimal structure where marginal benefit of debt (tax shields) equals marginal cost (financial distress, agency costs). Too little debt wastes tax benefits; too much increases distress risk. Optimal varies by company based on profitability, asset tangibility, and business risk.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores valuable tax benefits
- Option C ignores increasing distress costs at high leverage
