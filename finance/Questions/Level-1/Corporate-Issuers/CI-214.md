# Question

Estimating cost of capital for private companies is challenging because:

## Options

- **A**: Private companies don't need to consider cost of capital
- **B**: No market prices exist to estimate beta or equity value directly
- **C**: Private companies always have lower risk than public companies

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Private company challenges: no traded stock for beta estimation, no observable market value for weights, limited public information. Approaches: use comparable public company betas (adjusted for leverage differences), build-up method with size and specific risk premiums. Requires more judgment and adjustment.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is incorrect—cost of capital matters for all firms
- Option C is not necessarily true—depends on specific company
