# Question

The accounting break-even point is the sales level at which:

## Options

- **A**: NPV equals zero
- **B**: Net income equals zero
- **C**: Cash flow equals zero

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Accounting break-even: revenues equal total costs (fixed + variable), resulting in zero net income. It ignores time value of money. Cash break-even ignores depreciation. Economic break-even (NPV = 0) is the most comprehensive, considering opportunity cost of capital.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes economic break-even
- Option C describes cash break-even
