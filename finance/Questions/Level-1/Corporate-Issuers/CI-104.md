# Question

The duty of loyalty requires directors to:

## Options

- **A**: Prioritize their personal interests when beneficial
- **B**: Put the company's interests ahead of their personal interests
- **C**: Maintain confidentiality only during board meetings

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Duty of loyalty: directors must act in good faith for the company's benefit, not their own. This includes avoiding self-dealing, disclosing conflicts of interest, not usurping corporate opportunities, and maintaining confidentiality. Breaches can result in personal liability.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A contradicts the duty
- Option C understates the ongoing confidentiality obligation
