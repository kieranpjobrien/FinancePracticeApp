# Question

Unlevering a beta removes the effect of:

## Options

- **A**: Business risk
- **B**: Financial risk from the company's capital structure
- **C**: Systematic risk

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Levered beta includes both business (operating) risk and financial risk from leverage. Unlevering removes the financial risk component, isolating business risk (asset beta). This allows comparison across companies with different leverage or relevering for a different capital structure.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is removed, not kept—it's financial risk that's removed
- Option C remains—unlevered beta still reflects systematic business risk
