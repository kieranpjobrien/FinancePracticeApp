# Question

Industries with high leverage typically have:

## Options

- **A**: High R&D intensity and volatile cash flows
- **B**: Stable cash flows, tangible assets, and low business risk
- **C**: High growth opportunities and intangible assets

## Answer

B

## Explanation

High leverage industries (utilities, real estate, banking): stable, predictable cash flows support fixed debt payments; tangible assets provide collateral; low business risk allows financial risk. Low leverage industries (tech, biotech): volatile cash flows, intangible assets, and high operating risk limit debt capacity.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes low-leverage industry characteristics
- Option C also describes low-leverage characteristics
