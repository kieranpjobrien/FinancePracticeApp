# Question

Using the bond yield plus risk premium approach, cost of equity equals:

## Options

- **A**: Risk-free rate plus equity risk premium
- **B**: Company's bond yield plus an equity risk premium
- **C**: Dividend yield plus growth rate

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Bond yield plus risk premium: cost of equity = Yield on company's long-term debt + Equity risk premium (typically 3-5%). This reflects that equity is riskier than the same company's debt. Simple but subjective—the risk premium isn't precisely determined.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes CAPM structure
- Option C describes the dividend growth model
