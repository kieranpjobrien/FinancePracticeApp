# Question

A company has days of inventory on hand of 45, days of sales outstanding of 30, and days of payables outstanding of 40. The cash conversion cycle is:

## Options

- **A**: 35 days
- **B**: 115 days
- **C**: 25 days

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Cash conversion cycle = Days of inventory + Days of sales outstanding - Days of payables = 45 + 30 - 40 = 35 days. This represents the time between paying suppliers and receiving cash from customers. Lower CCC means less working capital financing needed.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B adds all three components instead of subtracting payables
- Option C may result from calculation errors
