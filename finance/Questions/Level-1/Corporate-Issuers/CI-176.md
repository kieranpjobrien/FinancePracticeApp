# Question

Economic value added (EVA) measures:

## Options

- **A**: Accounting profit only
- **B**: Operating profit minus the cost of all capital employed
- **C**: Revenue growth rate

## Answer

B

## Explanation

EVA = NOPAT - (WACC × Invested capital). Measures whether a company generates returns above its cost of capital. Positive EVA indicates value creation. Focuses on economic rather than accounting profit by charging for equity capital use. Used for performance evaluation and incentive compensation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores capital costs
- Option C measures growth, not value creation
