# Question

A lockbox system for cash collection:

## Options

- **A**: Increases the time funds are in transit
- **B**: Accelerates collection by having banks process payments near customer locations
- **C**: Is only used by retail businesses

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Lockbox: customers mail payments to a local bank PO box. Bank collects, processes, and deposits payments multiple times daily. Reduces mail float and processing float by having regional collection points. Particularly valuable for companies with geographically dispersed customers. Bank charges fees for service.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is opposite—lockboxes reduce transit time
- Option C is incorrect—used across industries
