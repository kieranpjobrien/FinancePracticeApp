# Question

Loosening credit standards is likely to:

## Options

- **A**: Decrease sales and decrease bad debt expenses
- **B**: Increase sales but also increase bad debts and collection costs
- **C**: Have no effect on working capital requirements

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Easier credit terms attract customers who couldn't qualify under stricter standards. This boosts sales but increases receivables investment, bad debt risk, and collection costs. The decision compares incremental profit from higher sales against incremental costs and investment.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A reverses the effects
- Option C ignores increased receivables investment
