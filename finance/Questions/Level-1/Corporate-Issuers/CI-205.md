# Question

Debt covenants are designed to:

## Options

- **A**: Increase borrowing flexibility for the company
- **B**: Protect lender interests by restricting certain borrower actions
- **C**: Guarantee loan repayment

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Covenants: contractual restrictions in loan agreements protecting creditors. Affirmative covenants require actions (maintain insurance, provide financials). Negative covenants restrict actions (limit additional debt, dividends, asset sales). Violations trigger default provisions. Reduce agency costs between shareholders and bondholders.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is opposite—covenants restrict flexibility
- Option C is too strong—covenants mitigate but don't guarantee
