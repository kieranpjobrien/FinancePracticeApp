# Question

A project costs $100,000 and generates $25,000 per year in cash flows. The simple payback period is:

## Options

- **A**: 2.5 years
- **B**: 4.0 years
- **C**: 25 years

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Simple payback period = Initial Investment / Annual Cash Flow

Payback = $100,000 / $25,000 = 4.0 years

The payback period is the time needed to recover the initial investment. Note that simple payback ignores the time value of money.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A divides $100,000 by $40,000 or uses incorrect values
- Option C divides in the wrong direction ($25,000 × 4 = $100,000 is correct, not 100/4)
