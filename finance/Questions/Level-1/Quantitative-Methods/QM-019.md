# Question

The correlation coefficient between two assets can range from:

## Options

- **A**: 0 to +1
- **B**: -1 to +1
- **C**: -∞ to +∞

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The correlation coefficient is a standardized measure of the linear relationship between two variables, bounded between -1 and +1. A correlation of +1 indicates perfect positive linear relationship, -1 indicates perfect negative linear relationship, and 0 indicates no linear relationship.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the possibility of negative correlation
- Option C describes the range of covariance, not correlation (correlation is standardized covariance)
