# Question

An investor deposits $5,000 in an account earning 8% per year compounded annually. The value after 5 years will be closest to:

## Options

- **A**: $7,000
- **B**: $7,347
- **C**: $7,500

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Future Value = Present Value × (1 + r)ⁿ

FV = $5,000 × (1.08)⁵
FV = $5,000 × 1.4693
FV = $7,346.64 ≈ $7,347

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A uses simple interest ($5,000 + 5 × $400 = $7,000)
- Option C incorrectly calculates the compound growth factor
