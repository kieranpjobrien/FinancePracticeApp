# Question

Which of the following is a property of the normal distribution?

## Options

- **A**: It is bounded between 0 and 1
- **B**: Its mean, median, and mode are all equal
- **C**: It has positive skewness

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The normal distribution is symmetric and bell-shaped, with the mean, median, and mode all located at the center. It has zero skewness (symmetric) and kurtosis of 3 (excess kurtosis of 0). The distribution extends from negative infinity to positive infinity, and approximately 68% of observations fall within one standard deviation of the mean, 95% within two, and 99.7% within three.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes a bounded distribution like the uniform distribution between 0 and 1
- Option C is incorrect; the normal distribution has zero skewness (it is symmetric)
