# Question

The total return of a fixed-income investment over a holding period consists of:

## Options

- **A**: Coupon income only
- **B**: Coupon income, reinvestment income, and capital gains or losses
- **C**: Price appreciation only

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Total return for bonds includes three components:
1. Coupon income: Periodic interest payments received
2. Reinvestment income: Interest earned on reinvested coupons
3. Capital gain/loss: Change in bond price over the holding period

The realized return can differ significantly from the yield to maturity if reinvestment rates differ from expectations or if the bond is sold before maturity.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores reinvestment and price change components
- Option C ignores the income component that is often the primary return source for bonds
