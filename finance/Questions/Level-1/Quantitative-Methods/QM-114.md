# Question

In the regression equation Y = a + bX + ε, the variable Y is called the:

## Options

- **A**: Independent variable or predictor
- **B**: Dependent variable or response variable
- **C**: Error term or residual

## Answer

B

## Explanation

In regression, Y is the dependent variable (also called the response, outcome, or explained variable)—it is what we are trying to predict or explain. X is the independent variable (predictor, explanatory variable) that we use to explain Y. The relationship flows from X to Y, with Y "depending on" X in the model specification.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes X, the independent variable
- Option C describes ε (epsilon), the error term
