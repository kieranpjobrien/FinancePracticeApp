# Question

The spot EUR/USD rate is 1.10. The 1-year USD interest rate is 5% and EUR rate is 3%. The 1-year forward EUR/USD rate is closest to:

## Options

- **A**: 1.079
- **B**: 1.100
- **C**: 1.121

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Currency forward calculation:

**Covered Interest Rate Parity:**
F = S × (1 + r_price) / (1 + r_base)

**EUR/USD means:**
- EUR is base currency
- USD is price currency
- Rate quoted as USD per EUR

**Given:**
- Spot = 1.10 USD/EUR
- r_USD = 5%
- r_EUR = 3%

**Calculation:**
F = 1.10 × (1 + 0.05) / (1 + 0.03)
F = 1.10 × 1.05 / 1.03
F = 1.10 × 1.0194
F = 1.121

**Interpretation:**
- EUR trades at forward premium
- USD trades at forward discount
- Higher USD rate means USD depreciates forward

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Inverted the rate formula
- Option B: Assumed no change (ignored interest differential)
