# Question

The duration of a pay-fixed, receive-floating interest rate swap is:

## Options

- **A**: Zero
- **B**: Positive (similar to a long bond position)
- **C**: Negative (similar to a short bond position)

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Swap duration:

**Pay-fixed swap decomposition:**
- Like: Short fixed-rate bond
- Plus: Long floating-rate note
- Net duration = FRN duration - Fixed bond duration

**Fixed leg:**
- Long duration
- Sensitive to rate changes
- Like a bond

**Floating leg:**
- Near-zero duration
- Resets to par at each payment
- Minimal rate sensitivity

**Net effect:**
- Pay-fixed: Negative duration
- Short fixed bond exposure
- Benefits from rising rates

**Receive-fixed swap:**
- Opposite: Positive duration
- Long fixed bond exposure
- Loses when rates rise

**Interpretation:**
- Pay-fixed: Short duration position
- Hedge for assets with positive duration
- Asset-liability matching tool

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Swaps have significant duration
- Option B: This describes receive-fixed swap
