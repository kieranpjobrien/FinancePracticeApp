# Question

Price transparency in derivative markets is generally higher for:

## Options

- **A**: OTC derivatives between two private parties
- **B**: Exchange-traded derivatives with public price quotes
- **C**: Customized derivative contracts

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Price transparency comparison:

**Exchange-traded derivatives:**
- Continuous price quotes
- Publicly available
- Real-time information
- Bid-ask spreads visible

**OTC derivatives:**
- Negotiated privately
- Prices not publicly disclosed
- Less standardized
- Valuation more complex

**Why exchange transparency higher:**
- Centralized trading
- Reporting requirements
- Competition among market makers
- Standardized contracts

**Post-2008 reforms:**
- OTC reporting mandates
- Trade repositories
- Some improved transparency
- Still less than exchange

**Benefits of transparency:**
- Better price discovery
- Reduced information asymmetry
- Fairer markets
- Easier valuation

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: OTC has lower transparency
- Option C: Customization reduces transparency
