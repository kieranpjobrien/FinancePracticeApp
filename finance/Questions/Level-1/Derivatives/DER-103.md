# Question

An investor entered a long forward contract at a price of $100. At expiration, the spot price is $108. The investor's profit is:

## Options

- **A**: $0
- **B**: $8
- **C**: $100

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Long forward payoff:

**Formula:**
Profit = Spot price - Forward price
Profit = ST - F

**Calculation:**
Profit = $108 - $100 = $8

**Interpretation:**
- Agreed to buy at $100
- Asset now worth $108
- Immediate gain of $8

**Long forward outcomes:**
- Profit if Spot > Forward price
- Loss if Spot < Forward price
- Breakeven if Spot = Forward price

**Short forward (opposite):**
- Profit = F - ST
- Gains when price falls

**Zero-sum game:**
- Long's gain = Short's loss
- No net value creation

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Would apply if spot = forward
- Option C: This is the forward price, not profit
