# Question

Derivatives improve market efficiency by:

## Options

- **A**: Reducing the number of investors in the market
- **B**: Allowing faster incorporation of information into prices
- **C**: Eliminating all forms of market risk

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Derivatives and market efficiency:

**Information efficiency:**
- Lower transaction costs than cash markets
- Leverage allows larger positions
- Informed traders attracted
- Prices reflect information quickly

**Mechanisms:**
- Arbitrage links derivative and cash prices
- Price discovery in futures markets
- Options reveal volatility expectations
- Speculators process information

**Examples:**
- Futures prices lead cash prices
- Options predict earnings surprises
- CDS spreads signal credit risk
- Currency forwards reflect rate expectations

**Additional benefits:**
- Hedgers can transfer risk efficiently
- Risk allocation improves
- More complete markets

**Limitations:**
- Can amplify volatility
- May transmit shocks
- Complexity introduces risks

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Derivatives increase participation
- Option C: Risk is transferred, not eliminated
