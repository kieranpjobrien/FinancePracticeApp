# Question

Alternative investments typically differ from traditional investments by having:

## Options

- **A**: Daily liquidity and transparent pricing
- **B**: Lower correlations to traditional assets, less liquidity, and more complex structures
- **C**: Guaranteed positive returns

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Alternative investment characteristics:

**Common features:**
- Lower correlation to stocks/bonds
- Less liquidity
- Complex structures
- Higher fees
- Less regulation
- Limited transparency

**Types of alternatives:**
- Hedge funds
- Private equity
- Real estate
- Commodities
- Infrastructure
- Natural resources

**Benefits:**
- Diversification
- Access to unique strategies
- Potential for higher returns

**Challenges:**
- Illiquidity
- Valuation difficulties
- Higher fees (2 and 20)
- Lock-up periods
- Due diligence requirements

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: These describe traditional investments, not alternatives
- Option C: No guaranteed returns in alternatives
