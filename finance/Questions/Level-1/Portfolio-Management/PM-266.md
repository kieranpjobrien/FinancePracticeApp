# Question

A fund's expense ratio includes:

## Options

- **A**: Only trading commissions
- **B**: Management fees, administrative costs, and other operating expenses
- **C**: Only performance-based fees

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Expense ratio:

**Included:**
- Management/advisory fees
- Administrative costs
- 12b-1 fees (distribution)
- Custodian fees
- Legal and audit
- Other operating expenses

**NOT included:**
- Trading commissions (transaction costs)
- Loads (sales charges)
- Account fees

**Typical ranges:**
- Index funds: 0.03-0.20%
- Active funds: 0.50-1.50%
- Hedge funds: 2%+ management

**Impact:**
- Directly reduces returns
- Compounds over time
- Lower is generally better

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Trading costs are separate
- Option C: Performance fees may be separate
