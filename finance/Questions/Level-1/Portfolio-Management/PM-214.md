# Question

A concentrated stock position represents:

## Options

- **A**: Optimal diversification
- **B**: Significant unsystematic risk exposure
- **C**: Low portfolio volatility

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Concentrated positions:

**Risks:**
- High unsystematic (firm-specific) risk
- Potential for significant losses
- Single-company exposure

**Common causes:**
- Executive compensation (stock options)
- Company founders
- Inheritance
- Long-term holdings with gains

**Management challenges:**
- Capital gains taxes if sold
- Emotional attachment
- Restrictions (lockups, insider rules)

**Strategies to manage:**
- Gradual diversification
- Exchange funds
- Protective puts
- Prepaid forwards
- Charitable giving

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Concentration is opposite of diversification
- Option C: Single-stock positions have high volatility
