# Question

Political risk in international investing includes:

## Options

- **A**: Only currency exchange rate movements
- **B**: Government policy changes, instability, and potential expropriation
- **C**: Company-specific management changes

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Political risk:

**Types:**
- Policy changes (tax, regulation, trade)
- Government instability
- Expropriation/nationalization
- Civil unrest/war
- Sanctions
- Capital controls

**Examples:**
- Nationalization of oil companies
- Unexpected tax increases
- Trade war tariffs
- Political regime change

**Assessment factors:**
- Political stability
- Rule of law
- Property rights protection
- History of policy changes

**Management:**
- Country analysis
- Diversification across countries
- Political risk insurance
- Investment structuring

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Currency risk is separate from political risk
- Option C: This is company/operational risk
