# Question

Credit risk is best described as the risk that:

## Options

- **A**: Interest rates will rise causing bond prices to fall
- **B**: A borrower will fail to make promised payments
- **C**: Currency exchange rates will move unfavorably

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Credit risk:

**Definition:**
- Risk of default or credit deterioration
- Counterparty fails to meet obligations
- Also called default risk

**Components:**
- Default probability
- Loss given default
- Exposure at default
- Credit migration risk

**Manifestations:**
- Missed interest payments
- Principal default
- Credit rating downgrade
- Wider credit spreads

**Management:**
- Credit analysis
- Diversification
- Collateral requirements
- Credit derivatives (CDS)

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: This is interest rate risk
- Option C: This is currency/exchange rate risk
