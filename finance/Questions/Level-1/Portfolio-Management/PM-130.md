# Question

Which of the following is an example of systematic risk?

## Options

- **A**: A labor strike at a manufacturing company
- **B**: A recession affecting the entire economy
- **C**: A product recall by a consumer goods company

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Systematic vs. unsystematic risk:

**Systematic risk (non-diversifiable):**
- Affects all securities in the market
- Cannot be eliminated through diversification
- Examples:
  - Economic recessions
  - Interest rate changes
  - Inflation
  - Political/geopolitical events
  - Natural disasters affecting economy

**Unsystematic risk (diversifiable):**
- Specific to individual companies/sectors
- Can be eliminated through diversification
- Examples:
  - Labor strikes (company-specific)
  - Product recalls
  - Management changes
  - Lawsuits

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Labor strike is company-specific (unsystematic)
- Option C: Product recall is company-specific (unsystematic)
