# Question

The risk of loss due to failed internal processes, people, or systems is best described as:

## Options

- **A**: Market risk
- **B**: Credit risk
- **C**: Operational risk

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Operational risk:

**Definition:**
- Risk of loss from inadequate or failed internal processes, people, systems, or external events
- Not related to market movements or counterparty default

**Examples:**
- Trading errors or unauthorized trading
- System failures or cyber attacks
- Fraud by employees
- Inadequate internal controls
- Legal and compliance failures
- Natural disasters affecting operations

**Management approaches:**
- Internal controls and procedures
- Business continuity planning
- Insurance
- Staff training and oversight

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A (Market risk): Risk from price movements in markets
- Option B (Credit risk): Risk of counterparty default on obligations
