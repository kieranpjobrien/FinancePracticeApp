# Question

A "2 and 20" fee structure refers to:

## Options

- **A**: 2% front-end load and 20% back-end load
- **B**: 2% management fee and 20% performance fee
- **C**: 20% management fee and 2% performance fee

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Hedge fund fee structure:

**"2 and 20":**
- 2% annual management fee (of AUM)
- 20% performance fee (of profits)
- Traditional hedge fund structure

**Components:**
- Management fee: Paid regardless of performance
- Performance fee: Paid only on gains
- Often with high-water mark

**High-water mark:**
- Must recover losses before earning performance fee
- Protects investors from paying on same gains twice

**Trend:**
- Fees have been declining
- Competition and fee pressure
- "1 and 10" or other structures

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Load fees are for mutual funds
- Option C: Reversed the percentages
