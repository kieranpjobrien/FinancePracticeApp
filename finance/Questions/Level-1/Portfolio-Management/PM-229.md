# Question

Value investing focuses on:

## Options

- **A**: Companies with high P/E ratios and rapid earnings growth
- **B**: Companies trading at low prices relative to fundamentals
- **C**: Only technology sector stocks

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Value vs. growth investing:

**Value investing:**
- Low price relative to fundamentals
- Low P/E, P/B ratios
- High dividend yields
- "Cheap" stocks
- Margin of safety concept

**Growth investing:**
- Companies with high growth potential
- Higher P/E, P/B acceptable
- Rapid earnings/revenue growth
- Premium valuation justified by growth

**Value characteristics:**
- Often mature companies
- May be out of favor
- Potential turnaround situations
- Focus on current fundamentals

**Historical evidence:**
- Value premium documented
- Cycles between value/growth outperformance

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: These describe growth stocks
- Option C: Value applies across sectors
