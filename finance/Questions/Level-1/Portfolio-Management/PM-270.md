# Question

Using peer group comparisons for performance evaluation has limitations because:

## Options

- **A**: All funds in a peer group have identical strategies
- **B**: Peer group composition may change over time and include diverse strategies
- **C**: Peer groups always provide the most accurate benchmark

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Peer group limitations:

**Problems:**
- Survivorship bias (failed funds exit)
- Strategy differences within groups
- Composition changes over time
- Self-reported categories
- Median may shift

**Additional issues:**
- Not investable
- Backward-looking only
- Gaming (funds switching categories)
- May not match specific mandate

**Advantages:**
- Easy to understand
- Shows relative ranking
- Broad competitive comparison

**Best practice:**
- Use with appropriate benchmark
- Understand group composition
- Consider multiple evaluation methods

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Peer groups include diverse strategies
- Option C: Peer groups have significant limitations
