# Question

A portfolio has equal weights in three assets with standard deviations of 20%, 25%, and 30%. If all pairwise correlations are 0.5, the portfolio variance is closest to:

## Options

- **A**: 4.03%
- **B**: 6.25%
- **C**: 8.33%

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Three-asset portfolio variance:

**Given:**
- w₁ = w₂ = w₃ = 1/3
- σ₁ = 20%, σ₂ = 25%, σ₃ = 30%
- All correlations = 0.5

**Formula:**
σ²p = Σwᵢ²σᵢ² + ΣΣwᵢwⱼρᵢⱼσᵢσⱼ (i≠j)

**Variance terms:**
- (1/3)² × 0.20² = 0.0044
- (1/3)² × 0.25² = 0.0069
- (1/3)² × 0.30² = 0.0100
- Sum = 0.0214

**Covariance terms:**
- 2 × (1/3)² × 0.5 × 0.20 × 0.25 = 0.0056
- 2 × (1/3)² × 0.5 × 0.20 × 0.30 = 0.0067
- 2 × (1/3)² × 0.5 × 0.25 × 0.30 = 0.0083
- Sum = 0.0206

**Total variance:**
0.0214 + 0.0189 = 0.0403 = 4.03%

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B: Incorrect formula application
- Option C: Simple average of variances
