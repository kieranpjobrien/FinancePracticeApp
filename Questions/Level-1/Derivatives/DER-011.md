# Question

In a forward contract, both parties:

## Options

- **A**: Have the right but not the obligation to transact
- **B**: Are obligated to transact at the agreed-upon terms
- **C**: Must post margin with an exchange

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Forward contract obligations:

**Binding agreement:**
- Both parties obligated
- Must transact at expiration
- No optionality
- Cannot walk away

**Long position (buyer):**
- Obligated to buy underlying
- At the forward price
- On the settlement date

**Short position (seller):**
- Obligated to sell/deliver underlying
- At the forward price
- On the settlement date

**Settlement types:**
- Physical delivery: Actual asset exchanged
- Cash settlement: Difference paid

**Risk implications:**
- No upfront premium
- Both parties face risk
- Credit/counterparty risk exists

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: This describes options, not forwards
- Option C: Forwards are OTC, no exchange margin (futures have margin)
