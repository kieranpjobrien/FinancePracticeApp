# Question

A 3×6 FRA has a contract rate of 4% on $1 million notional. At settlement, the reference rate is 4.5%. The settlement payment to the FRA buyer is closest to:

## Options

- **A**: $1,232
- **B**: $1,250
- **C**: $5,000

## Answer

A

## Explanation

FRA settlement calculation:

**FRA notation (3×6):**
- Starts in 3 months
- Underlying is 3-month rate
- 6 months total

**Settlement formula:**
Payment = Notional × (Reference - Contract) × (days/360) / (1 + Reference × days/360)

**Given:**
- Notional = $1,000,000
- Contract rate = 4%
- Reference rate = 4.5%
- Period = 90 days (3 months)

**Numerator:**
$1,000,000 × (0.045 - 0.04) × (90/360)
= $1,000,000 × 0.005 × 0.25
= $1,250

**Discount factor:**
1 + 0.045 × (90/360) = 1 + 0.01125 = 1.01125

**Settlement:**
$1,250 / 1.01125 = $1,236 ≈ $1,232

**Discounting rationale:**
- Payment made at start of underlying period
- Discount to present value
- Buyer receives because rates rose

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B: Forgot to discount
- Option C: Used annual difference, not quarterly
