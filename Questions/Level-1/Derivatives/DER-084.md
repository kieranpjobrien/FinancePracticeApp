# Question

To terminate an interest rate swap before maturity, a party can:

## Options

- **A**: Simply stop making payments without consequence
- **B**: Enter an offsetting swap or pay/receive the swap's market value
- **C**: Only wait until the original maturity date

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Swap termination methods:

**Offsetting swap:**
- Enter opposite swap position
- Same notional and maturity
- Net payments approximately zero
- Still have two contracts

**Cash settlement:**
- Pay/receive mark-to-market value
- Terminates original contract
- Single payment required
- Clean exit

**Assignment:**
- Transfer position to third party
- Original counterparty must agree
- Novation of contract

**Mutual termination:**
- Both parties agree to cancel
- Settle at market value
- Common for bilateral swaps

**Why terminate early:**
- Hedge no longer needed
- Credit concerns
- Portfolio restructuring
- Rate views changed

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Obligations remain, default consequences
- Option C: Early termination is possible
