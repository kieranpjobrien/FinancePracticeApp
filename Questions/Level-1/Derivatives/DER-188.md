# Question

Variation margin in futures trading represents:

## Options

- **A**: The initial deposit required to open a position
- **B**: The daily settlement of gains and losses
- **C**: The minimum account balance required

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Variation margin:

**Definition:**
- Daily P&L settlement
- Paid by losing party
- Received by winning party
- Based on price change

**How it works:**
- End of day: Calculate settlement price
- Compare to previous day
- Transfer difference between accounts

**Example:**
- Long futures at $100
- Day 1 settlement: $98
- Variation margin: -$2 × contracts
- Debited from long's account

**Contrast with initial margin:**
- Initial: Deposit to open position
- Variation: Daily gain/loss settlement

**Contrast with maintenance margin:**
- Maintenance: Minimum balance level
- Triggers margin call if breached

**Purpose:**
- Realize profits/losses daily
- Prevent accumulation of large debts
- Reduce counterparty risk

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: This is initial margin
- Option C: This is maintenance margin
