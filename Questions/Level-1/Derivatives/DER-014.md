# Question

The party that agrees to buy the underlying asset in a forward contract is said to be:

## Options

- **A**: Short the forward
- **B**: Long the forward
- **C**: The writer of the forward

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Forward position terminology:

**Long forward (buyer):**
- Agrees to BUY the underlying
- At the forward price
- On the settlement date
- Benefits if price rises

**Short forward (seller):**
- Agrees to SELL the underlying
- At the forward price
- On the settlement date
- Benefits if price falls

**Payoff at expiration:**
- Long: Spot price - Forward price
- Short: Forward price - Spot price
- Zero-sum game

**Example:**
- Forward price = $100
- Spot at expiration = $110
- Long gains $10, Short loses $10

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Short is the seller, not buyer
- Option C: "Writer" applies to options, not forwards
