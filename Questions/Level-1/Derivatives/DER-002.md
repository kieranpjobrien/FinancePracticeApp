# Question

Which of the following is a key difference between forward contracts and futures contracts?

## Options

- **A**: Forwards are standardized while futures are customized
- **B**: Futures are traded on exchanges while forwards trade over-the-counter
- **C**: Forwards require daily settlement while futures settle at expiration

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Key differences between forwards and futures:

| Feature | Forwards | Futures |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Trading venue | Over-the-counter (OTC) | Exchange-traded |
| Standardization | Customized | Standardized |
| Settlement | At expiration | Daily (mark-to-market) |
| Counterparty risk | Higher (direct exposure) | Lower (clearinghouse) |
| Liquidity | Lower | Higher |

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A reverses the relationship (futures are standardized, forwards are customized)
- Option C reverses the settlement feature (futures settle daily, forwards at expiration)
