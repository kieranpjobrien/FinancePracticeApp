# Question

Compared to forward contracts, futures contracts:

## Options

- **A**: Are privately negotiated between two parties
- **B**: Are marked to market daily with gains/losses settled
- **C**: Have more flexible terms and delivery dates

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Futures vs. forwards:

**Futures characteristics:**
- Exchange-traded
- Standardized contracts
- Daily mark-to-market (MTM)
- Margin requirements
- Clearinghouse guarantee
- High liquidity

**Daily settlement:**
- Gains credited, losses debited daily
- Prevents accumulation of large losses
- Margin calls if account falls below maintenance

**Forward characteristics:**
- OTC (over-the-counter)
- Customized terms
- Settlement at expiration only
- Direct counterparty risk
- Less liquid

**Practical impact:**
- Futures: cash flows throughout life
- Forwards: single cash flow at end

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Describes forwards, not futures
- Option C: Forwards have more flexibility, not futures
