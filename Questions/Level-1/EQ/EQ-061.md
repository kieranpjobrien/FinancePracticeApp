# Question

The sustainable growth rate (g) for a company equals:

## Options

- **A**: Dividend growth rate only
- **B**: Retention ratio multiplied by return on equity (b × ROE)
- **C**: Revenue growth rate

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Sustainable growth rate: g = b × ROE, where b = retention ratio (1 - payout ratio). Represents maximum growth without external financing. Higher ROE or higher retention enables faster sustainable growth. If company grows faster, needs external capital; slower growth generates excess cash.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A may equal sustainable growth in steady state, but isn't the formula
- Option C is a different growth measure
