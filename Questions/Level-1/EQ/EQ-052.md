# Question

Strong-form market efficiency implies that:

## Options

- **A**: Only technical analysis is ineffective
- **B**: Even insider information cannot generate abnormal returns
- **C**: Markets are completely unpredictable

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Strong-form efficiency: prices reflect ALL information—public and private. Even insiders cannot earn excess returns. Least supported by evidence (insider trading laws exist because insider trading is profitable). Empirically rejected. Weak and semi-strong forms have more supporting evidence.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is too limited—refers to weak-form efficiency
- Option C overstates—markets still respond to new information
