# Question

A company's pricing power is strongest when it has:

## Options

- **A**: Many close substitutes and price-sensitive customers
- **B**: Differentiated products, loyal customers, and limited competition
- **C**: Commodity products in a highly competitive market

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Pricing power determinants: product differentiation, brand strength, switching costs, limited substitutes, inelastic demand, low buyer concentration. Allows maintaining or raising prices without losing significant volume. Critical for margin sustainability. Opposite conditions (commoditization, competition) erode pricing power.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes conditions for weak pricing power
- Option C describes commodity markets with no pricing power
