# Question

Circuit breakers in securities markets are designed to:

## Options

- **A**: Permanently stop trading in a security
- **B**: Temporarily halt trading during extreme price movements to prevent panic
- **C**: Increase volatility

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Circuit breakers: automatic trading halts triggered by significant price declines or volatility. Purpose: pause trading to allow information dissemination, reduce panic selling, give traders time to assess. May apply market-wide (e.g., 7%, 13%, 20% S&P declines) or to individual securities.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is too permanent—halts are temporary
- Option C is opposite—circuit breakers aim to reduce volatility
