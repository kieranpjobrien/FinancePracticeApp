# Question

EPS growth can be achieved through:

## Options

- **A**: Only revenue growth
- **B**: Revenue growth, margin expansion, share repurchases, or financial leverage changes
- **C**: Exclusively share repurchases

## Answer

B

## Explanation

EPS drivers: revenue growth (top line), margin improvement (more profit per dollar of sales), share reduction (same profit over fewer shares), leverage (amplifies profit growth). Quality EPS growth from operations preferred over buyback-driven or leverage-driven growth. Decompose EPS growth to understand sustainability.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores other drivers
- Option C ignores operational and margin factors
