# Question

During the shakeout stage of the industry lifecycle:

## Options

- **A**: Many new competitors enter the market
- **B**: Growth slows, competition intensifies, and weaker firms exit
- **C**: Industry sales are at their peak growth rate

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Shakeout stage: growth decelerates, industry overcapacity, intense price competition, weaker competitors fail or merge. Follows growth stage. Survivors tend to have scale advantages or differentiated positions. Profitability pressured. Precedes mature stage with stable competition.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes growth stage
- Option C describes growth stage's peak
