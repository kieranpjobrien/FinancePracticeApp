# Question

Asset-light business models typically feature:

## Options

- **A**: Heavy investment in manufacturing facilities
- **B**: Higher ROIC, lower capital requirements, and more scalable operations
- **C**: Very low profit margins

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Asset-light: low fixed asset investment—software, platforms, franchising, outsourced manufacturing. Benefits: higher ROIC (less capital for same profit), scalability, flexibility. Trade-offs: may have less control, dependent on partners. Tech companies often asset-light with high margins.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes capital-intensive model
- Option C—asset-light often has higher margins due to scalability
