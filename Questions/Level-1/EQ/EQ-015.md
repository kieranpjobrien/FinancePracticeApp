# Question

Market anomalies such as the January effect and size effect:

## Options

- **A**: Prove markets are completely inefficient
- **B**: Are patterns that appear to contradict market efficiency but may reflect risk, data mining, or disappear after discovery
- **C**: Guarantee future excess returns

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Anomalies: apparent patterns generating excess returns. Possible explanations: unmeasured risk factors, data mining/statistical artifacts, transaction costs eliminating profits, or patterns disappearing once publicized. Evidence is mixed—some anomalies persist, others weaken. Doesn't necessarily disprove efficiency.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A overstates the implication
- Option C ignores that anomalies often fade
