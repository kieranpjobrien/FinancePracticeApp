# Question

When selecting peer companies for relative valuation, the most important criterion is:

## Options

- **A**: Similar stock price levels
- **B**: Similar business model, risk profile, and growth prospects
- **C**: Same geographic headquarters location

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Peer selection for comparables: focus on economic similarity—same industry, business model, customer base, risk profile, growth characteristics. Financial metrics (size, leverage, profitability) also matter. Stock price level is irrelevant. Geography matters only if it affects business characteristics.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—stock price level has no bearing on comparability
- Option C—geography alone doesn't ensure economic similarity
