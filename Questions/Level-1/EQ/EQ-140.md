# Question

During the shakeout stage of the industry lifecycle:

## Options

- **A**: Demand grows rapidly and new competitors enter easily
- **B**: Industry growth slows, competition intensifies, and weaker firms exit or merge
- **C**: Demand is stable and few firms dominate with high profitability

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Shakeout stage: growth deceleration, overcapacity, intense price competition. Industry consolidation as marginal players exit, merge, or go bankrupt. Survivors typically those with strongest competitive advantages, best cost positions, or deepest resources. Precedes mature stage with fewer, stronger competitors.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes growth stage
- Option C describes mature stage characteristics
