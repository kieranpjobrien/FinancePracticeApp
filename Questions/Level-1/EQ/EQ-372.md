# Question

Regulation FD (Fair Disclosure) in the US requires:

## Options

- **A**: Selective disclosure of material information to favored analysts
- **B**: Simultaneous disclosure of material information to all investors
- **C**: Companies to stop communicating with analysts

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Reg FD: prohibits selective disclosure. If company tells analyst material non-public information, must simultaneously disclose publicly (press release, 8-K). Levels playing field between institutional and retail investors. Changed corporate communication practices significantly.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes what Reg FD prohibits
- Option C is excessive—companies can communicate, just equally
