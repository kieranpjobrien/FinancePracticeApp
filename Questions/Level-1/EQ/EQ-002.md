# Question

The bid-ask spread represents:

## Options

- **A**: The difference between the highest price a buyer will pay and the lowest price a seller will accept
- **B**: The change in stock price over a trading day
- **C**: The commission charged by brokers

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Bid-ask spread: bid is highest price buyers offer; ask (offer) is lowest price sellers accept. Spread is the difference. Represents a transaction cost for investors and compensation for market makers. Narrower spreads indicate higher liquidity; wider spreads suggest lower liquidity or higher risk.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B describes price volatility or daily range
- Option C is a separate cost component
