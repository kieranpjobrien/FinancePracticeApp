# Question

Vertical integration can benefit a company by:

## Options

- **A**: Always reducing costs and complexity
- **B**: Securing supply chains, capturing margins, and reducing dependency on external parties
- **C**: Eliminating all competitive threats

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Vertical integration: owning stages of supply chain (backward to suppliers, forward to customers). Benefits: secure supply, reduce transaction costs, capture margins, quality control. Risks: capital intensity, reduced flexibility, managing unfamiliar businesses. Must weigh make vs. buy decision.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores complexity and capital costs of integration
- Option C overstates competitive benefits
