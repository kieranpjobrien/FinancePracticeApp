# Question

High-quality earnings are characterized by:

## Options

- **A**: Aggressive revenue recognition and one-time gains
- **B**: Sustainable sources, cash backing, and conservative accounting choices
- **C**: Maximum use of accounting flexibility to boost reported figures

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Earnings quality factors: sustainability (recurring vs. one-time), cash flow support (operating cash ≥ net income), conservative accounting (minimal estimates, early loss recognition). High-quality earnings persist into future periods and reflect true economic performance, not accounting manipulation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes low-quality, potentially manipulated earnings
- Option C indicates earnings management, reducing quality
