# Question

Companies may prefer share repurchases over cash dividends because repurchases:

## Options

- **A**: Are required by securities regulations
- **B**: Offer tax advantages to shareholders and greater flexibility for the company
- **C**: Are always cheaper than paying dividends

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Repurchase advantages: tax deferral (capital gains taxed only when sold, often at lower rates), flexibility (no commitment to repeat), signal undervaluation, offset dilution from stock options, increase EPS. Dividends create ongoing expectation. Repurchases are optional, not required.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—repurchases are voluntary, not mandated
- Option C oversimplifies—cost depends on stock price, tax treatment
