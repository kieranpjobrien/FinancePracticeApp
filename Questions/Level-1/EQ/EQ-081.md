# Question

Market makers provide liquidity by:

## Options

- **A**: Only buying securities when prices are rising
- **B**: Continuously quoting bid and ask prices and standing ready to trade
- **C**: Providing investment advice to clients

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Market makers: quote both buy (bid) and sell (ask) prices, willing to trade at those prices. Profit from bid-ask spread. Provide immediacy—investors can trade without waiting for counterparty. Carry inventory risk. Essential for market liquidity. May have obligations to maintain quotes.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is not market making
- Option C describes advisory, not market making
