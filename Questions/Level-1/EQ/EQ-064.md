# Question

A limitation of using relative valuation multiples is that:

## Options

- **A**: They are too complex to calculate
- **B**: If the entire sector is mispriced, comparable valuation will not identify this
- **C**: They never provide useful information

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Relative valuation limitation: compares to peers assuming peers are correctly valued. If entire sector is overvalued (bubble) or undervalued (panic), multiples comparison won't detect it. "Cheap relative to expensive stocks" may still mean overvalued absolutely. Should complement with intrinsic value analysis.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is opposite—multiples are simple to calculate
- Option C is too extreme—multiples are widely used and useful
