# Question

High-quality earnings are characterized by:

## Options

- **A**: Aggressive revenue recognition and significant non-recurring gains
- **B**: Sustainable, cash-backed, recurring earnings from core operations
- **C**: Maximized reported income through accounting flexibility

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Earnings quality: sustainable earnings from ongoing operations, backed by cash flow, not dependent on one-time items or aggressive accounting. High quality: recurring, conservative recognition, strong operating cash flow. Low quality: non-recurring items, aggressive accruals, earnings-cash flow divergence.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes low-quality earnings
- Option C also describes aggressive accounting, not quality
