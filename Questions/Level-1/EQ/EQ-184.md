# Question

Heavy government regulation of an industry most likely:

## Options

- **A**: Has no impact on profitability or competitive dynamics
- **B**: Can create barriers to entry but may also limit pricing power
- **C**: Always increases industry profitability

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Regulatory effects are mixed: can create barriers (licenses, compliance costs disadvantage new entrants), but may limit pricing (utilities), mandate costs (environmental), or restrict activities. Regulated industries often have stable but moderate returns. Industry analysis must consider specific regulatory regime and changes.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores significant regulatory impacts
- Option C oversimplifies—regulation often caps returns
