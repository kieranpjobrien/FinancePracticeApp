# Question

Secular industry trends differ from cyclical trends in that secular trends:

## Options

- **A**: Reverse every few years
- **B**: Represent long-term structural changes that persist across economic cycles
- **C**: Only last for one quarter

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Secular trends: long-term, structural shifts—e-commerce growth, aging demographics, digitalization, energy transition. Persist regardless of economic cycles. Cyclical trends: rise and fall with economy—auto sales, housing, advertising. Industry analysis must distinguish between both for proper forecasting.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes cyclical patterns
- Option C describes very short-term fluctuations
