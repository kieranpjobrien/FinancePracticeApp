# Question

Enterprise value (EV) to EBITDA is preferred over P/E when:

## Options

- **A**: Comparing companies with similar capital structures
- **B**: Comparing companies with different capital structures and depreciation policies
- **C**: Valuing companies with negative EBITDA

## Answer

B

## Explanation

EV/EBITDA: EV = Market cap + Debt - Cash. EBITDA is before interest and depreciation. Allows comparison across capital structures (unlike P/E which is affected by leverage). Also useful when depreciation methods differ. Widely used in M&A and leveraged transactions.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A doesn't require EV—P/E works fine
- Option C makes the ratio meaningless (negative denominator)
