# Question

The ETF creation/redemption mechanism helps maintain:

## Options

- **A**: Maximum deviation between ETF price and NAV
- **B**: Close alignment between ETF market price and underlying NAV
- **C**: Higher expenses than mutual funds

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Creation/redemption: authorized participants can create ETF shares by delivering underlying basket (creation), or redeem ETF shares for underlying (redemption). Arbitrage opportunity if price deviates from NAV. Mechanism keeps price and NAV aligned. In-kind transactions provide tax efficiency.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes the problem mechanism solves
- Option C—mechanism contributes to ETF cost efficiency
