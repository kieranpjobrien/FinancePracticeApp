# Question

Market share is an important competitive metric because it indicates:

## Options

- **A**: Only absolute company size
- **B**: Relative competitive position within an industry
- **C**: Guaranteed future profitability

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Market share: company sales as percentage of total industry sales. Indicates competitive strength, economies of scale potential, brand power. Trends matter—gaining vs. losing share. High share may indicate pricing power but also antitrust scrutiny. Context needed—small share in huge market can be valuable.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the relative/competitive aspect
- Option C—share doesn't guarantee profitability
