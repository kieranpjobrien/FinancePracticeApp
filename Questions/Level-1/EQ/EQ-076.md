# Question

A 2-for-1 stock split results in:

## Options

- **A**: Shareholders receiving twice as many shares at half the price each
- **B**: A doubling of shareholder wealth
- **C**: The company receiving additional capital

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Stock split: increases shares outstanding, proportionally decreases price per share. 2-for-1: each share becomes two shares at half the price. Total value unchanged—cosmetic transaction. Purpose: improve liquidity by lowering share price to more accessible range. No impact on fundamentals.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B is incorrect—total wealth unchanged
- Option C confuses with stock issuance
