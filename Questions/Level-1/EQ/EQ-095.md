# Question

When assessing company management, analysts should consider:

## Options

- **A**: Only the CEO's educational background
- **B**: Track record, capital allocation decisions, incentive alignment, and corporate culture
- **C**: Stock price performance over the past month

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Management assessment: historical performance (revenue growth, margin improvement, returns), capital allocation (acquisitions, investments, returns to shareholders), compensation alignment with long-term value, strategic vision, succession planning, and corporate culture. Qualitative but critical factor.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is too narrow—background is one small factor
- Option C is too short-term and affected by many factors
