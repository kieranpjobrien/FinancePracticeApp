# Question

Trade confirmation provides:

## Options

- **A**: A prediction of future stock prices
- **B**: Details of an executed trade including price, quantity, fees, and settlement date
- **C**: Investment recommendations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Trade confirmation: official record of trade details—security, quantity, price, date/time, commission, fees, settlement date (T+1 or T+2). Allows investor to verify correct execution. Discrepancies should be immediately reported. Regulatory requirement for broker-dealers.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses confirmation with prediction
- Option C describes investment advice, not trade records
