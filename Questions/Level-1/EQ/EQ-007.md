# Question

Dark pools are trading venues that:

## Options

- **A**: Display all orders publicly before execution
- **B**: Do not display orders publicly, reducing market impact for large trades
- **C**: Are illegal in most jurisdictions

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Dark pools: private trading venues where orders are not visible to other market participants. Used primarily by institutional investors to execute large orders without revealing their intentions and moving market prices. Trade-off: less transparency but reduced market impact. Regulated but not public exchanges.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes lit (transparent) exchanges
- Option C is incorrect—dark pools are legal and regulated
