# Question

High-frequency trading (HFT) firms generally:

## Options

- **A**: Hold positions for weeks or months
- **B**: Execute large numbers of trades at very high speeds, often holding positions for seconds
- **C**: Only trade during market opening and closing

## Answer

B

## Explanation

HFT: algorithmic strategies executing thousands of trades per second. Characteristics: co-location near exchanges, low latency infrastructure, short holding periods (seconds to milliseconds), small profit per trade but high volume. Strategies: market making, arbitrage, momentum ignition. Controversial role in market structure.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes traditional investing, not HFT
- Option C is too restrictive—HFT operates throughout trading day
