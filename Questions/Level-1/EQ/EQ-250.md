# Question

Status quo bias causes investors to:

## Options

- **A**: Actively seek new investment opportunities
- **B**: Disproportionately favor current holdings over potentially better alternatives
- **C**: Always rebalance portfolios optimally

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Status quo bias: preference for current state, reluctance to change. In investing: holding inherited stocks, staying in default 401k options, not rebalancing. Combines loss aversion (fear of regret from change) and inertia. Leads to suboptimal portfolios, excessive concentration in familiar holdings.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes active, unbiased behavior
- Option C describes optimal, unbiased rebalancing
