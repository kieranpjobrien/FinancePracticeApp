# Question

A bonus issue (scrip issue) is:

## Options

- **A**: A cash payment to shareholders
- **B**: Free additional shares issued to existing shareholders from retained earnings
- **C**: A payment to employees only

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Bonus issue: free shares to existing shareholders—funded by capitalizing retained earnings or reserves. Similar to stock dividend. Share price adjusts downward proportionally. Total value unchanged. Common in markets where term "stock dividend" less used. No cash changes hands.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes cash dividend
- Option C confuses with employee stock awards
