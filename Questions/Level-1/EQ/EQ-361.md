# Question

Supply chain analysis in equity research helps understand:

## Options

- **A**: Only the company's internal operations
- **B**: Upstream (suppliers) and downstream (customers) relationships affecting competitive position
- **C**: Employee satisfaction levels

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Supply chain analysis: examines supplier power, customer relationships, intermediary margins, competitive dynamics throughout value chain. Helps identify: margin pressure points, dependency risks, vertical integration opportunities, industry bottlenecks. Important for understanding full competitive picture.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores external relationships
- Option C relates to HR, not supply chain
