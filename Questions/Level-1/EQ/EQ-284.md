# Question

Analyst consensus estimates represent:

## Options

- **A**: A single analyst's forecast
- **B**: The average or median of multiple analysts' forecasts
- **C**: Company management's official guidance

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Consensus: aggregation of analyst forecasts, typically mean or median. Reflects market expectations for earnings, revenue, etc. Earnings surprises measured vs. consensus. Data providers (Bloomberg, FactSet, IBES) compile estimates. Consensus can shift as analysts update views.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes individual estimate, not consensus
- Option C describes guidance, which informs but differs from consensus
