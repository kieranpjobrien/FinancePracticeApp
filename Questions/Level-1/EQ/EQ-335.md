# Question

The MSCI World Index covers:

## Options

- **A**: All global markets including emerging markets
- **B**: Developed markets only across Americas, Europe, and Asia-Pacific
- **C**: Only US equities

## Answer

B

## Explanation

MSCI World: developed market equity index covering 23 countries—US, Japan, UK, France, Germany, Canada, Australia, and others. Float-adjusted market-cap weighted. Does NOT include emerging markets (that's MSCI ACWI or MSCI EM). Major benchmark for global developed market allocation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes MSCI ACWI (All Country World Index)
- Option C describes US-only indices like S&P 500
