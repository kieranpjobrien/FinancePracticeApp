# Question

Board independence in corporate governance means:

## Options

- **A**: All directors are company employees
- **B**: A majority of directors have no material relationship with the company beyond board service
- **C**: The board never meets

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Independent directors: no employment, business, or family ties with company. Provide objective oversight of management. Regulatory requirements and best practices specify minimum independence levels. Key committees (audit, compensation, nominating) typically require independent chairs. Protects shareholder interests.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes lack of independence
- Option C is unrelated to independence definition
