# Question

Free cash flow to equity (FCFE) represents:

## Options

- **A**: Cash available to all capital providers
- **B**: Cash available to equity holders after debt payments and reinvestment
- **C**: Operating cash flow before working capital changes

## Answer

B

## Explanation

FCFE = Net income + Depreciation - CapEx - Δ Working capital + Net borrowing. Represents cash available to pay dividends or repurchase shares after all obligations and reinvestment needs. Differs from FCFF (to firm) which is before debt payments and available to all capital providers.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes FCFF, not FCFE
- Option C describes a component, not the complete measure
