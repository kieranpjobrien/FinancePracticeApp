# Question

A company's economic moat refers to:

## Options

- **A**: Its physical security measures
- **B**: Sustainable competitive advantages that protect profits from competition
- **C**: The amount of cash on its balance sheet

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Economic moat (Warren Buffett term): durable competitive advantages creating barriers. Sources: brand, patents, network effects, switching costs, cost advantages, regulatory licenses. Wide moats allow sustained above-average returns. Moat analysis key to long-term value investing.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A takes "moat" literally rather than metaphorically
- Option C describes liquidity, not competitive position
