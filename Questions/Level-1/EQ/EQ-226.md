# Question

Industries most vulnerable to technological disruption typically have:

## Options

- **A**: High regulatory barriers and government protection
- **B**: Products or services that can be digitized or automated
- **C**: Strong natural monopoly characteristics

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Disruption vulnerability: products/services easily replicated digitally (media, retail, financial services), legacy business models, slow incumbent response, low switching costs for customers. Protected industries: physical products, regulatory moats, network effects, high touch services. Digital transformation affects most industries differently.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes protection from disruption
- Option C—natural monopolies may be stable despite technology
