# Question

A stock dividend distributes:

## Options

- **A**: Cash to shareholders
- **B**: Additional shares to shareholders proportionally
- **C**: Shares only to new investors

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Stock dividend: additional shares distributed to existing shareholders proportionally (e.g., 5% stock dividend = 5 new shares per 100 held). No cash involved. Share price adjusts downward proportionally. Retains cash while rewarding shareholders. Similar economic effect to stock split.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes cash dividend
- Option C—stock dividends go to existing shareholders
