# Question

Free cash flow to equity (FCFE) equals:

## Options

- **A**: CFO - Fixed capital investment + Net borrowing
- **B**: CFO - Fixed capital investment - Net borrowing
- **C**: Net income - Dividends

## Answer

A

## Explanation

FCFE = CFO - FCInv + Net borrowing. Cash available to equity holders after operating needs, capital expenditures, and debt transactions. Net borrowing added because it provides cash to equity (debt repayment would be subtracted). Alternative: FCFE = NI - (1-DR)(FCInv - Dep) - (1-DR)(WCInv).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B subtracts borrowing—new debt provides cash, doesn't use it
- Option C ignores capital expenditures and working capital needs
