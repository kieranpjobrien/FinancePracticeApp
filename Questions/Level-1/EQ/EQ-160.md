# Question

If a company has ROA of 8% and financial leverage ratio of 2.0, its ROE is approximately:

## Options

- **A**: 4%
- **B**: 16%
- **C**: 10%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

ROE = ROA × Financial Leverage = 8% × 2.0 = 16%. Financial leverage (Assets/Equity) amplifies ROA into ROE. Higher leverage increases ROE when ROA exceeds cost of debt, but also increases risk. This simplified relationship shows how leverage magnifies shareholder returns.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A divides instead of multiplies
- Option C adds instead of multiplies
