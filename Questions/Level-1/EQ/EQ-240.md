# Question

An initial public offering (IPO) occurs when:

## Options

- **A**: Existing shareholders sell their shares to other investors
- **B**: A private company sells shares to the public for the first time
- **C**: A public company buys back its shares

## Answer

B

## Explanation

IPO: first sale of stock by a private company to public investors. Transforms private company to publicly traded. Proceeds go to company (primary shares) and/or selling shareholders. Process involves underwriters, SEC registration, roadshow, pricing. Post-IPO, shares trade on secondary market.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes secondary market trading
- Option C describes share repurchase
