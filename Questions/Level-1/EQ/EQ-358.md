# Question

Break-even analysis determines:

## Options

- **A**: The maximum profit a company can achieve
- **B**: The sales volume or price at which revenues equal total costs
- **C**: The optimal capital structure

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Break-even point: where total revenue equals total costs (fixed + variable). At break-even, profit = 0. Below break-even, company loses money. Formula: Fixed Costs / (Price - Variable Cost per unit). Important for: startup planning, margin of safety assessment, scenario analysis.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—break-even shows zero profit point, not maximum
- Option C describes capital structure optimization
