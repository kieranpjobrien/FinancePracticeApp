# Question

Extended-hours trading (pre-market and after-hours):

## Options

- **A**: Has higher liquidity than regular trading hours
- **B**: Typically has lower liquidity and wider spreads than regular hours
- **C**: Is not available on major US exchanges

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Extended hours: trading before 9:30am and after 4pm ET. Lower participation means less liquidity, wider spreads, more volatility. Prices may move significantly on news but can be misleading due to thin volume. Important for reacting to earnings releases. Higher execution risk.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A reverses liquidity relationship
- Option C—extended hours trading is widely available
