# Question

On the ex-dividend date:

## Options

- **A**: The stock price typically increases by the dividend amount
- **B**: The stock price typically decreases by approximately the dividend amount
- **C**: Shareholders must sell their shares

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Ex-dividend date: first day stock trades without right to upcoming dividend. Buyers on/after ex-date don't receive dividend. Price adjusts downward by approximately dividend amount to reflect this. Must own shares before ex-date to receive dividend. Record date follows ex-date.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is opposite—price falls, not rises
- Option C—no requirement to sell
