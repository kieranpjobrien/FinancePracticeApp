# Question

A minority discount is applied because:

## Options

- **A**: Minority shareholders have complete control over company decisions
- **B**: Minority positions lack control and cannot influence major corporate decisions
- **C**: All minority stakes are worthless

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Minority discount: reflects lack of control associated with less-than-controlling interest. Minority can't elect directors, change strategy, force dividends, or sell company. Value reflects proportional claim without control benefits. Inverse of control premium. Typically 15-30%.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is opposite—minority lacks control
- Option C overstates—minority still has proportional claim on value
