# Question

Bottom-up analysis focuses primarily on:

## Options

- **A**: Interest rate trends and monetary policy
- **B**: Individual company fundamentals regardless of broader economic conditions
- **C**: Industry sector allocation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Bottom-up: analyzes individual companies—business model, competitive position, financial health, valuation—to find opportunities regardless of macro environment. Builds portfolio from best individual opportunities. May ignore sector or economic considerations. Often associated with value investing.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes macro/top-down factors
- Option C is more top-down oriented
