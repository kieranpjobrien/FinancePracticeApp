# Question

A 2-for-1 stock split results in:

## Options

- **A**: Doubling the share price and halving shares outstanding
- **B**: Halving the share price and doubling shares outstanding
- **C**: No change to either share price or shares outstanding

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Stock split: increases shares outstanding proportionally while reducing share price. 2-for-1 split doubles shares, halves price. Total market cap unchanged. Purposes: increase liquidity, make shares more accessible to retail investors. No fundamental value change—purely cosmetic.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes a reverse split (1-for-2)
- Option C ignores the mechanical effects of splits
