# Question

Enterprise value (EV) is calculated as:

## Options

- **A**: Market cap minus total debt plus cash
- **B**: Market cap plus total debt minus cash and cash equivalents
- **C**: Total assets minus total liabilities

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Enterprise value = Market cap + Total debt + Preferred stock + Minority interest - Cash and equivalents. Represents total firm value to all capital providers. Adding debt accounts for value owed to creditors; subtracting cash shows net acquisition cost. Used in EV/EBITDA and EV/Sales ratios.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A reverses the debt and cash adjustments
- Option C describes book value of equity, not enterprise value
