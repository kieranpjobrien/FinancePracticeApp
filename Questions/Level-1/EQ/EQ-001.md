# Question

The primary market for securities refers to:

## Options

- **A**: The market where previously issued securities are traded between investors
- **B**: The market where new securities are issued and sold for the first time
- **C**: The largest stock exchange in a country

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Primary market: where issuers sell new securities to investors, raising capital. Includes IPOs and secondary offerings. After initial sale, securities trade in secondary markets between investors without issuer involvement. Primary market proceeds go to the issuer; secondary market proceeds go to selling investors.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes the secondary market
- Option C confuses market type with exchange size
