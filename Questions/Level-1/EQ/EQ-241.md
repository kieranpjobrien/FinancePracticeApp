# Question

A seasoned equity offering (SEO) is:

## Options

- **A**: The first time a company's stock is sold to the public
- **B**: An additional issuance of stock by an already publicly traded company
- **C**: A stock split by an established company

## Answer

B

## Explanation

SEO (secondary offering, follow-on offering): public company issues additional shares after IPO. Can be primary (company sells new shares, receives proceeds) or secondary (existing shareholders sell their shares). Dilutes existing shareholders if primary. Used to raise capital for growth, acquisitions, or debt reduction.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes IPO
- Option C is a stock split, not a new issuance
