# Question

Non-cash investing and financing activities are:

## Options

- **A**: Included in operating cash flows
- **B**: Disclosed separately in the notes or supplemental schedule
- **C**: Not required to be reported

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Non-cash transactions (like acquiring assets through stock issuance, converting debt to equity, or obtaining assets through capital leases) don't appear on the cash flow statement but must be disclosed separately. This ensures users understand all significant investing and financing activities regardless of cash involvement.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is incorrect—they don't involve cash so aren't in the statement
- Option C is incorrect—disclosure is required
