# Question

Cash flows from operating activities include:

## Options

- **A**: Proceeds from issuing bonds
- **B**: Cash received from customers
- **C**: Purchase of property and equipment

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Operating activities relate to the company's core business operations—producing and selling goods/services. Cash from customers, payments to suppliers, wages paid, and interest/taxes paid (under US GAAP) are operating activities. Bond proceeds are financing activities; PP&E purchases are investing activities.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is a financing activity
- Option C is an investing activity
