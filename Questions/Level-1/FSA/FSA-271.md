# Question

If ending inventory is understated, current period net income will be:

## Options

- **A**: Overstated
- **B**: Understated
- **C**: Unaffected

## Answer

B

## Explanation

COGS = Beginning inventory + Purchases - Ending inventory. If ending inventory is understated, COGS is overstated, reducing gross profit and net income. The error self-corrects next period: understated ending inventory becomes understated beginning inventory, understating next period's COGS and overstating next period's income.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A reverses the relationship
- Option C ignores the impact on COGS
