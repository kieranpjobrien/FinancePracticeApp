# Question

In the indirect method, an increase in accounts payable is:

## Options

- **A**: Added to net income
- **B**: Subtracted from net income
- **C**: Reported in financing activities

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Increased payables mean expenses exceeded cash paid—the company is using supplier financing. This preserved cash, so it's added when converting to cash basis. Increases in operating liabilities (payables, accrued expenses, deferred revenue) are sources of operating cash; decreases are uses.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B reverses the adjustment direction
- Option C confuses operating and financing classification
