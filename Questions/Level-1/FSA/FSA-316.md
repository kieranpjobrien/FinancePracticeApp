# Question

Intraperiod tax allocation means:

## Options

- **A**: Allocating taxes between quarterly periods within a year
- **B**: Allocating tax expense to different components of comprehensive income
- **C**: Deferring taxes to future periods

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Intraperiod allocation assigns tax expense/benefit to the income statement components that generated it: continuing operations, discontinued operations, other comprehensive income, and prior period adjustments. This ensures each section shows its after-tax impact. Interperiod allocation (deferred taxes) allocates between years.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes interim reporting, not intraperiod allocation
- Option C describes deferred taxes (interperiod allocation)
