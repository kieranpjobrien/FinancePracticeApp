# Question

Equipment has a carrying amount of $80,000 and a tax base of $60,000. This difference will result in:

## Options

- **A**: A deferred tax asset
- **B**: A deferred tax liability
- **C**: No deferred tax effect

## Answer

B

## Explanation

When carrying amount > tax base, future taxable amounts will exceed deductible amounts, creating a taxable temporary difference and DTL. Here, the company has taken more tax depreciation than book depreciation. When the asset is sold or fully depreciated, taxable income will exceed book income, requiring more taxes.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A occurs when tax base exceeds carrying amount
- Option C ignores the temporary difference
