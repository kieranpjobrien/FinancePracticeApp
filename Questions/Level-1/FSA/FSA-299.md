# Question

Off-balance-sheet financing arrangements are concerning because:

## Options

- **A**: They are illegal under all accounting standards
- **B**: They may understate reported liabilities and leverage
- **C**: They always involve fraud

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Off-balance-sheet arrangements (special purpose entities, certain leases, take-or-pay contracts) can hide debt and make leverage appear lower than economic reality. While not necessarily illegal, they require careful disclosure analysis. Post-Enron reforms and IFRS 16 lease standards reduced off-balance-sheet opportunities.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is incorrect—many off-balance-sheet items are legitimate
- Option C overstates—they're concerning, not automatically fraudulent
