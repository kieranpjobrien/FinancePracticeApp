# Question

A company has days sales outstanding of 45, days inventory on hand of 60, and days payables outstanding of 30. The cash conversion cycle is:

## Options

- **A**: 45 days
- **B**: 75 days
- **C**: 135 days

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Cash conversion cycle = DSO + DOH - DPO = 45 + 60 - 30 = 75 days. This measures the time between paying suppliers and collecting from customers. Shorter cycles indicate more efficient working capital management. Negative cycles (common in retail) mean companies receive cash before paying suppliers.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores inventory days
- Option C adds all three instead of subtracting DPO
