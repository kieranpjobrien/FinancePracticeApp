# Question

The five-component extended DuPont analysis breaks down ROE into:

## Options

- **A**: Net profit margin, asset turnover, leverage, interest burden, and tax burden
- **B**: Gross margin, operating margin, net margin, turnover, and leverage
- **C**: Tax burden, interest burden, operating margin, asset turnover, and leverage

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Five-component DuPont: ROE = (NI/EBT) × (EBT/EBIT) × (EBIT/Revenue) × (Revenue/Assets) × (Assets/Equity) = Tax burden × Interest burden × Operating margin × Asset turnover × Leverage. This separates the effects of taxes, financing costs, operations, efficiency, and capital structure.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A mixes components incorrectly
- Option B lists margin types but not the correct decomposition
