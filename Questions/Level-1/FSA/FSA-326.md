# Question

A company has gross accounts receivable of $500,000 and an allowance for doubtful accounts of $35,000. Net accounts receivable is:

## Options

- **A**: $465,000
- **B**: $500,000
- **C**: $535,000

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Net A/R = Gross A/R - Allowance for doubtful accounts = $500,000 - $35,000 = $465,000. The allowance is a contra-asset reflecting estimated uncollectible accounts. Analyzing the allowance as a percentage of gross receivables reveals management's collection expectations and potential earnings manipulation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B ignores the allowance
- Option C adds instead of subtracts the allowance
