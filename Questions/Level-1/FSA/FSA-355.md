# Question

The income tax note typically discloses:

## Options

- **A**: Only total tax expense
- **B**: Current/deferred split, rate reconciliation, and DTA/DTL components
- **C**: Projected future tax payments only

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Tax disclosures include: current vs. deferred tax expense split, effective tax rate reconciliation to statutory rate, components of DTAs and DTLs, valuation allowances, unrecognized tax benefits, and tax positions taken. These help assess tax sustainability, risk, and impact of future rate changes.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A understates disclosure requirements
- Option C is not a required disclosure
