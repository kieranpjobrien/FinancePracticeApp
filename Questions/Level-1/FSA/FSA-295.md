# Question

A multinational company's effective tax rate is affected by:

## Options

- **A**: Only domestic statutory rates
- **B**: The mix of income across different tax jurisdictions
- **C**: Foreign currency translation only

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Multinationals face varying tax rates across jurisdictions. Income earned in low-tax countries reduces the blended effective rate; high-tax countries increase it. Tax planning involves profit shifting to lower-tax locations. The rate reconciliation discloses how foreign rates differ from domestic statutory rates.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores international tax rate differences
- Option C affects financial statement translation, not tax rates
