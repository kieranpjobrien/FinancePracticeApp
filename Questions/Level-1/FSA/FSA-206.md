# Question

Accumulated other comprehensive income (AOCI) includes:

## Options

- **A**: Retained earnings from prior periods
- **B**: Cumulative unrealized gains/losses that bypass net income
- **C**: Treasury stock purchases

## Answer

B

## Explanation

AOCI accumulates OCI items over time: unrealized gains/losses on AFS debt securities, foreign currency translation adjustments, pension remeasurements (actuarial gains/losses), and effective cash flow hedge gains/losses. These bypass the income statement but affect total equity. AOCI is reported in shareholders' equity.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes retained earnings, a separate equity component
- Option C is a contra-equity account, not AOCI
