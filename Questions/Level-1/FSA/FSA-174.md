# Question

Which intangible asset is NOT subject to amortization?

## Options

- **A**: Patents with 17-year legal life
- **B**: Goodwill
- **C**: Customer relationships with 10-year useful life

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Goodwill has an indefinite life and is not amortized. Instead, it is tested annually for impairment. Intangible assets with finite useful lives (patents, customer relationships, software) are amortized over their useful or legal life, whichever is shorter. Impairment testing applies to all intangibles.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A has a finite life and must be amortized
- Option C has a definite useful life requiring amortization
