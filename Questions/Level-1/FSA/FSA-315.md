# Question

Unrecognized tax benefits represent:

## Options

- **A**: Tax refunds expected to be received
- **B**: Tax positions that may not be sustained upon audit
- **C**: Deferred tax assets with full valuation allowances

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Unrecognized tax benefits (UTB) are uncertain tax positions—deductions or credits claimed that may be challenged by tax authorities. Companies must disclose total UTBs and amounts that would affect the effective tax rate if recognized. Large UTBs suggest tax risk. Increases signal aggressive positioning; decreases may indicate settlements.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes tax receivables
- Option C describes DTAs unlikely to be realized, different from UTBs
