# Question

Under the weighted average cost method, the cost assigned to inventory is:

## Options

- **A**: The cost of the most recently purchased units
- **B**: The cost of the oldest units purchased
- **C**: An average of all units available for sale

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Weighted average cost assigns the same cost per unit to both COGS and ending inventory, calculated as total cost of goods available for sale divided by total units available. It smooths out price fluctuations and produces results between FIFO and LIFO. Under a periodic system, one average is calculated; perpetual systems recalculate after each purchase.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes LIFO
- Option B describes FIFO
