# Question

Goodwill arises when:

## Options

- **A**: A company internally develops a valuable brand
- **B**: The purchase price of an acquisition exceeds the fair value of net identifiable assets
- **C**: An asset's market value exceeds its book value

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Goodwill = Purchase price - Fair value of net identifiable assets acquired. It represents the premium paid for synergies, reputation, customer relationships, and other intangibles not separately recognizable. Goodwill is not amortized but tested annually for impairment. Internally generated goodwill cannot be capitalized.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes internally generated goodwill, which cannot be recognized
- Option C describes unrealized appreciation, not goodwill
