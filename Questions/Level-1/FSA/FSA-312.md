# Question

Absorption costing for inventory includes:

## Options

- **A**: Only direct costs (materials and labor)
- **B**: Direct costs plus allocated manufacturing overhead
- **C**: All costs including selling and administrative

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Absorption (full) costing includes all manufacturing costs: direct materials, direct labor, and manufacturing overhead (both variable and fixed). It's required under GAAP for external reporting. Variable costing (excluding fixed overhead) is used internally but not for GAAP. Selling and administrative costs are period expenses, not inventoried.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes prime costs only, ignoring overhead
- Option C includes period costs that shouldn't be capitalized
