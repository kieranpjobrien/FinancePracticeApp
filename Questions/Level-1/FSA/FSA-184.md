# Question

Which item would typically be included in other comprehensive income (OCI)?

## Options

- **A**: Depreciation expense on factory equipment
- **B**: Unrealized gains on available-for-sale debt securities
- **C**: Interest expense on bonds payable

## Answer

B

## Explanation

OCI includes items that bypass the income statement: unrealized gains/losses on AFS debt securities, foreign currency translation adjustments, pension remeasurements, and cash flow hedge gains/losses. Depreciation and interest are income statement items. OCI accumulates in shareholders' equity as AOCI.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is an operating expense on the income statement
- Option C is a financing cost on the income statement
