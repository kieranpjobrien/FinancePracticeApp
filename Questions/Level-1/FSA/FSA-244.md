# Question

A company has operating income of $300,000, interest expense of $40,000, and income tax expense of $65,000. Net income is:

## Options

- **A**: $195,000
- **B**: $235,000
- **C**: $260,000

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Net income = Operating income - Interest expense - Income tax expense = $300,000 - $40,000 - $65,000 = $195,000. This represents earnings available to common shareholders after all expenses, financing costs, and taxes. EBT (earnings before taxes) would be $260,000.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B only deducts income tax, ignoring interest
- Option C is EBT (before tax), not net income
