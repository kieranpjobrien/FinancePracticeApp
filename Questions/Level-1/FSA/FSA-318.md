# Question

Post-employment benefits other than pensions (OPEB) typically include:

## Options

- **A**: Salaries earned during employment
- **B**: Retiree health care and life insurance
- **C**: Stock options vesting during employment

## Answer

B

## Explanation

OPEB includes benefits provided after employment other than pensions: retiree medical insurance, dental, life insurance, and other welfare benefits. Like pensions, these create obligations accrued over the employee's service period. OPEB liabilities can be substantial, particularly for companies with legacy commitments and rising healthcare costs.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes current compensation, not post-employment
- Option C vests during employment, not post-employment benefits
