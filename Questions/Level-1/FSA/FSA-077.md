# Question

The Beneish M-Score is used to:

## Options

- **A**: Predict stock price movements
- **B**: Detect the likelihood of earnings manipulation
- **C**: Calculate intrinsic value of a company

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The Beneish M-Score uses eight financial ratios to identify potential earnings manipulators. Variables include days sales in receivables index, gross margin index, asset quality index, sales growth index, and others. An M-Score above -1.78 suggests a higher probability of manipulation. It's a screening tool, not definitive proof.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes technical analysis
- Option C describes valuation models like DCF
