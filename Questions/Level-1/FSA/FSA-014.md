# Question

A company has net income of $10 million, preferred dividends of $1 million, and 4 million weighted average common shares outstanding. Basic EPS is closest to:

## Options

- **A**: $2.25
- **B**: $2.50
- **C**: $2.75

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Basic EPS = (Net income - Preferred dividends) / Weighted average common shares = ($10 million - $1 million) / 4 million = $9 million / 4 million = $2.25 per share. Preferred dividends are subtracted because EPS measures earnings available to common shareholders.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B ignores preferred dividends: $10M / 4M = $2.50
- Option C adds preferred dividends instead of subtracting
