# Question

A company has days inventory on hand of 45 and days sales outstanding of 35. The operating cycle is:

## Options

- **A**: 10 days
- **B**: 40 days
- **C**: 80 days

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Operating cycle = Days inventory on hand + Days sales outstanding = 45 + 35 = 80 days. This measures the time from purchasing inventory to collecting cash from sales. A shorter cycle indicates more efficient working capital management. The cash conversion cycle further subtracts days payables outstanding.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A subtracts instead of adds
- Option B calculates average instead of sum
