# Question

Under the flow-through method for investment tax credits:

## Options

- **A**: The credit is amortized over the asset's useful life
- **B**: The full credit reduces tax expense in the year of acquisition
- **C**: The credit is reported as deferred revenue

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The flow-through method recognizes the entire investment tax credit benefit in the acquisition year, immediately reducing income tax expense. The deferral method (Option A) spreads the benefit over the asset's life. US GAAP allows either method; IFRS generally requires deferral. The choice affects earnings volatility.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes the deferral method
- Option C is not an accepted treatment for investment tax credits
