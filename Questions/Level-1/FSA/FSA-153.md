# Question

Profitability ratios measure:

## Options

- **A**: The speed of converting assets to cash
- **B**: The ability to generate profit from revenue and assets
- **C**: The probability of default on debt obligations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Profitability ratios assess a company's ability to generate earnings relative to sales, assets, or equity. They include gross margin, operating margin, net profit margin, ROA, and ROE. These ratios help evaluate management effectiveness and compare profitability across companies.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes liquidity/activity ratios
- Option C describes credit risk metrics
