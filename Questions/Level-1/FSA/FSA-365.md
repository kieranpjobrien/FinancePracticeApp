# Question

A company has total equity of $500 million, goodwill of $80 million, and other intangibles of $45 million. Tangible book value is:

## Options

- **A**: $375 million
- **B**: $420 million
- **C**: $455 million

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Tangible book value = Total equity - Goodwill - Other intangibles = $500M - $80M - $45M = $375 million. This conservative measure excludes assets that may not be recoverable in liquidation. It's particularly relevant for financial institutions and companies with significant intangible assets.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B subtracts only goodwill
- Option C subtracts only other intangibles
