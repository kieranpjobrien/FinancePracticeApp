# Question

Free cash flow to equity (FCFE) represents cash available to:

## Options

- **A**: All providers of capital
- **B**: Common shareholders only
- **C**: Bondholders only

## Answer

B

## Explanation

FCFE = CFO - Fixed capital investment + Net borrowing. After servicing debt obligations (interest and principal), FCFE represents cash that could theoretically be distributed to equity holders. FCFF (to firm) represents cash available to all capital providers before debt service.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes FCFF, not FCFE
- Option C is incorrect—bondholders receive interest payments, not free cash flow
