# Question

Which of the following is most likely a red flag for potential earnings manipulation?

## Options

- **A**: Receivables growing in line with sales
- **B**: Receivables growing significantly faster than sales
- **C**: Inventory declining relative to cost of goods sold

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Receivables growing faster than sales may indicate: fictitious revenue recognition, extended payment terms to boost sales, or failure to write off uncollectible accounts. Analysts compare DSO trends and receivables-to-sales ratios over time. Normal operations would show receivables growing roughly in line with sales growth.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A represents normal business growth
- Option C could indicate efficient inventory management
