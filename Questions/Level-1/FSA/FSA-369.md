# Question

Credit analysts focus most on:

## Options

- **A**: Stock price performance
- **B**: Ability to service and repay debt obligations
- **C**: Earnings per share growth

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Credit analysis assesses default risk—whether the borrower can meet interest and principal payments. Key metrics: coverage ratios (interest coverage, debt service), leverage ratios (debt/EBITDA, debt/capital), and cash flow adequacy. Stock price and EPS matter more for equity investors.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is equity investor focused
- Option C is relevant for equity valuation, not credit
