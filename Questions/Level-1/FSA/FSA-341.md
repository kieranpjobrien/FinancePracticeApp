# Question

Comparability as a qualitative characteristic means:

## Options

- **A**: All companies use identical accounting policies
- **B**: Users can identify similarities and differences across entities and periods
- **C**: Financial statements are prepared monthly

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Comparability allows users to identify and understand similarities and differences between entities (cross-sectional) and over time (longitudinal). Consistency (same policies over time) enhances comparability. Companies needn't use identical policies, but differences should be disclosed to enable adjustment.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A overstates the requirement—differences are allowed with disclosure
- Option C describes frequency, not comparability
