# Question

A company has COGS of $600,000, beginning inventory of $50,000, ending inventory of $70,000, beginning payables of $40,000, and ending payables of $35,000. Cash paid to suppliers is:

## Options

- **A**: $605,000
- **B**: $615,000
- **C**: $625,000

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Purchases = COGS + Ending inventory - Beginning inventory = $600,000 + $70,000 - $50,000 = $620,000. Cash paid = Purchases - Decrease in payables = $620,000 + ($40,000 - $35,000) = $620,000 + $5,000 = $625,000. Payables decreased, so more cash was paid than purchases.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A subtracts the payable change
- Option B ignores the payable change
