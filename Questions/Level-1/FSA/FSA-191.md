# Question

A company has beginning inventory of 100 units at $10 each. It purchases 200 units at $12 each and 100 units at $14 each. Under weighted-average cost (periodic), the cost per unit is:

## Options

- **A**: $11.50
- **B**: $12.00
- **C**: $12.50

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Total cost = (100 × $10) + (200 × $12) + (100 × $14) = $1,000 + $2,400 + $1,400 = $4,800. Total units = 100 + 200 + 100 = 400. Weighted average cost = $4,800 / 400 = $12.00 per unit. This method smooths price fluctuations across all available inventory.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is the simple average of prices: ($10+$12+$14)/3 = $12, not weighted
- Option C results from calculation error
