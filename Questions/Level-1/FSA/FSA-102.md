# Question

Debt covenants are most likely included in loan agreements to:

## Options

- **A**: Increase the borrower's flexibility
- **B**: Protect lenders by restricting borrower actions
- **C**: Reduce the interest rate to zero

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Covenants are contractual restrictions designed to protect lenders by limiting borrower actions that could increase credit risk. Affirmative covenants require actions (maintain insurance, provide statements); negative covenants prohibit actions (limit additional debt, restrict dividends). Violations can trigger default or renegotiation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is opposite—covenants restrict flexibility
- Option C misunderstands—covenants don't eliminate interest
