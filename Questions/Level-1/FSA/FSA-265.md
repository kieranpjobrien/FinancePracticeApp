# Question

Non-controlling interest (minority interest) is reported:

## Options

- **A**: As a liability
- **B**: Within shareholders' equity, separately from parent's equity
- **C**: Between liabilities and equity

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Non-controlling interest represents the equity in a consolidated subsidiary not owned by the parent. It's reported as a separate component of equity (not liability) under both IFRS and US GAAP. On the income statement, net income is allocated between parent shareholders and NCI.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A was an older treatment, now incorrect
- Option C (mezzanine) is used for redeemable preferred, not NCI
