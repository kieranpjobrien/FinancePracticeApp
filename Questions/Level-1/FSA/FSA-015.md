# Question

Current assets are those expected to be:

## Options

- **A**: Held for more than one year
- **B**: Converted to cash or used within one year or one operating cycle
- **C**: Used to generate revenue over multiple periods

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Current assets are expected to be converted to cash, sold, or consumed within one year or the operating cycle, whichever is longer. Examples include cash, accounts receivable, inventory, and prepaid expenses. Non-current (long-term) assets, like PP&E and intangibles, provide benefits beyond one year.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes non-current assets
- Option C describes long-lived assets like property and equipment
