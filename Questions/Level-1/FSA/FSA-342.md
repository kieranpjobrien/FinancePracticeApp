# Question

The principle of substance over form means:

## Options

- **A**: Legal form determines accounting treatment
- **B**: Economic reality guides accounting, even if it differs from legal form
- **C**: Oral agreements take precedence over written contracts

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Substance over form requires accounting to reflect economic reality, not just legal structure. Examples: finance leases are treated as purchases despite being legally leases; off-balance-sheet entities may require consolidation based on control. This principle prevents structuring transactions to achieve misleading accounting.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A reverses the principle
- Option C misinterprets the concept entirely
