# Question

The sustainable growth rate is calculated as:

## Options

- **A**: ROE × Dividend payout ratio
- **B**: ROE × Retention ratio
- **C**: ROA × Financial leverage

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Sustainable growth rate = ROE × (1 - Dividend payout ratio) = ROE × Retention ratio. It represents the maximum growth rate achievable without external equity financing or changing capital structure. Higher ROE and higher earnings retention support faster sustainable growth.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A uses payout ratio instead of retention ratio
- Option C describes components of ROE, not sustainable growth
