# Question

Results from discontinued operations are reported:

## Options

- **A**: As part of operating income
- **B**: Separately, after income from continuing operations
- **C**: In other comprehensive income

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Discontinued operations—components sold or held for sale meeting certain criteria—are reported separately, net of tax, below income from continuing operations. This separation helps analysts focus on sustainable ongoing business results. Prior periods are restated to show comparable continuing operations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A would mix non-recurring results with ongoing operations
- Option C is for items that bypass net income entirely
