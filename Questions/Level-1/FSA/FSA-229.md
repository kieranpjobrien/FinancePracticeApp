# Question

A company has net income of $150,000, revenue of $1,500,000, and average total assets of $1,000,000. Using the basic DuPont equation, ROA is:

## Options

- **A**: 10%
- **B**: 15%
- **C**: 22.5%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

ROA = Net profit margin × Asset turnover = (Net income / Revenue) × (Revenue / Average assets) = ($150,000 / $1,500,000) × ($1,500,000 / $1,000,000) = 10% × 1.5 = 15%. Or directly: ROA = Net income / Average assets = $150,000 / $1,000,000 = 15%.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is the net profit margin alone
- Option C results from calculation error
