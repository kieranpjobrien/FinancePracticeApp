# Question

Cannibalization occurs when:

## Options

- **A**: A new project increases sales of existing products
- **B**: A new product takes sales away from existing company products
- **C**: Competition reduces market share

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Cannibalization: a new product's sales come partly at the expense of existing products. The lost sales from existing products are negative externalities that should reduce the new project's incremental cash flows. If a competitor would capture those sales anyway, cannibalization is less relevant.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes positive externalities/synergies
- Option C describes external competition, not internal cannibalization
