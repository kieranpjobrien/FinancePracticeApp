# Question

Commercial paper is:

## Options

- **A**: Long-term secured debt
- **B**: Short-term unsecured promissory notes issued by corporations
- **C**: Government-backed securities

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Commercial paper is short-term (typically 1-270 days) unsecured debt issued by creditworthy corporations. It's sold at a discount and redeemed at face value. Used to finance working capital and short-term needs. Issuers need strong credit ratings; backup credit lines are common.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes bonds, not commercial paper
- Option C describes government securities
