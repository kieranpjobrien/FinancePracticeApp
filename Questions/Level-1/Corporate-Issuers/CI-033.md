# Question

Stock options for executives are intended to:

## Options

- **A**: Guarantee a fixed salary
- **B**: Align management incentives with shareholder interests
- **C**: Reduce executive total compensation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Stock options give executives the right to buy shares at a set price. If the stock price rises, options become valuable—aligning management's wealth with shareholders'. This addresses agency problems by making managers act more like owners. Criticism: may encourage excessive risk-taking or short-term focus.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes base salary, not options
- Option C is often false—options increase total compensation potential
