# Question

Concentration banking involves:

## Options

- **A**: Using multiple banks with no coordination
- **B**: Consolidating cash from regional banks to a central account for better management
- **C**: Keeping all cash in a single local bank

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Concentration banking: funds from multiple collection points are pooled at a central concentration bank. This provides better oversight, investment of larger balances at better rates, and efficient disbursement. Electronic transfers move funds from depository banks to the concentration account.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes decentralized cash management
- Option C may not optimize float or provide centralized control
