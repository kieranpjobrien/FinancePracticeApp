# Question

Sunk costs should be:

## Options

- **A**: Included in project cash flow analysis
- **B**: Excluded from project cash flow analysis
- **C**: Depreciated over the project life

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Sunk costs are past expenditures that cannot be recovered regardless of the decision—they're irrelevant to future decisions. Only incremental (additional) cash flows matter for NPV analysis. Including sunk costs leads to incorrect accept/reject decisions.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A includes irrelevant costs
- Option C treats sunk costs as capital expenditure
