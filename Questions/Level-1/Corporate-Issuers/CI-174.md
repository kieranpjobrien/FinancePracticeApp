# Question

Clawback provisions in executive compensation:

## Options

- **A**: Guarantee bonuses regardless of future performance
- **B**: Allow companies to recover compensation if earnings are later restated or misconduct discovered
- **C**: Accelerate vesting of stock options

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Clawback provisions: contractual right to reclaim paid compensation if triggering events occur (accounting restatements, fraud, misconduct). Discourage manipulation and align incentives with long-term value. Increasingly required by regulations and considered good governance practice.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is opposite—clawbacks can remove guarantees
- Option C describes acceleration provisions, not clawbacks
