# Question

A board risk committee's primary responsibility is to:

## Options

- **A**: Manage day-to-day operational risks
- **B**: Oversee the company's risk management framework and risk appetite
- **C**: Approve all business transactions

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Risk committee: board-level oversight of risk management. Reviews risk appetite, monitors major risks, evaluates risk management effectiveness, ensures appropriate controls. Does not manage daily risks (management's job) but provides oversight and strategic direction. Increasingly important post-financial crisis.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is management's operational responsibility
- Option C exceeds risk committee scope
