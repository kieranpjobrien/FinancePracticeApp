# Question

Monte Carlo simulation in capital budgeting:

## Options

- **A**: Guarantees project success
- **B**: Generates probability distributions of outcomes by running many iterations with random variable inputs
- **C**: Replaces the need for NPV analysis

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Monte Carlo: assigns probability distributions to uncertain inputs, runs thousands of iterations with random draws, generates distribution of possible NPVs. Shows probability of positive NPV, expected value, and risk profile. More comprehensive than single-point estimates or limited scenarios.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A overstates—simulation analyzes risk, doesn't eliminate it
- Option C is incorrect—simulation enhances NPV analysis
