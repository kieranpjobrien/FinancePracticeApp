# Question

Extending credit terms to customers will likely:

## Options

- **A**: Decrease sales and receivables
- **B**: Increase sales but also increase receivables and bad debt risk
- **C**: Have no effect on working capital

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Looser credit terms (longer payment periods, easier qualifications) attract customers and boost sales but increase receivables investment, collection period, and bad debt risk. The decision involves trade-offs between sales growth and the costs of carrying/collecting receivables.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A reverses the sales effect
- Option C ignores that receivables are working capital
