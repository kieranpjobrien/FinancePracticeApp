# Question

Real options in capital budgeting refer to:

## Options

- **A**: Stock options granted to project managers
- **B**: Managerial flexibility to modify projects based on future developments
- **C**: Options traded on real estate

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Real options recognize managerial flexibility has value: options to expand, abandon, delay, or modify projects based on new information. Standard NPV ignores this flexibility. Real options analysis quantifies the value of waiting, staging investments, or switching strategies.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes employee compensation
- Option C misinterprets "real" as real estate
