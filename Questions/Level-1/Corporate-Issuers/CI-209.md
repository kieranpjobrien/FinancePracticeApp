# Question

The duty of care requires directors to:

## Options

- **A**: Always agree with management recommendations
- **B**: Make informed decisions with due diligence and reasonable inquiry
- **C**: Guarantee positive financial results

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Duty of care: directors must act with diligence, making informed decisions after reasonable investigation. Attend meetings, review materials, ask questions, seek expert advice when needed. Standard: what a reasonably prudent person would do in similar circumstances. Protected by business judgment rule if exercised properly.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A violates independent oversight responsibility
- Option C imposes impossible standard—results not guaranteed
