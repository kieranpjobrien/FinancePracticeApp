# Question

"Say on pay" votes allow shareholders to:

## Options

- **A**: Set executive compensation levels directly
- **B**: Express approval or disapproval of executive compensation packages
- **C**: Elect the compensation committee members

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Say on pay: advisory shareholder vote on executive compensation. Non-binding in most jurisdictions but significant negative vote creates pressure for changes. Enhances accountability and transparency. Required annually or periodically in many countries. Separate from binding votes on director elections.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A overstates the power—votes are typically advisory
- Option C describes board committee elections, not compensation votes
