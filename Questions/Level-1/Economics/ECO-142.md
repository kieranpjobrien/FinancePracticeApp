# Question

Official dollarization occurs when a country:

## Options

- **A**: Uses the US dollar alongside its domestic currency
- **B**: Adopts a foreign currency (typically USD) as its sole legal tender
- **C**: Pegs its currency to the dollar at a flexible rate

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Official dollarization means abandoning the domestic currency entirely and adopting a foreign currency (usually the US dollar) as legal tender. Countries like Ecuador and El Salvador have officially dollarized. This eliminates currency risk and inflation credibility problems but sacrifices monetary policy independence and seigniorage revenue.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes partial dollarization where both currencies circulate
- Option C describes a fixed exchange rate, not full dollarization
