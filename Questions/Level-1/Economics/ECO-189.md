# Question

At the trough of a business cycle, which condition is most likely present?

## Options

- **A**: Accelerating inflation and tight labor markets
- **B**: Significant excess capacity and high unemployment
- **C**: Strong GDP growth and rising corporate profits

## Answer

B

## Explanation

At the business cycle trough, economic activity has bottomed out. Unemployment is high, factories have excess capacity, consumer and business confidence is low, and inventory levels have been depleted. However, the economy is poised to begin recovery. Interest rates are typically low as central banks have eased policy.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes conditions at the peak
- Option C describes conditions during an expansion
