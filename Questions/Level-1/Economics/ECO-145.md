# Question

In the currency pair GBP/USD = 1.30, the base currency is:

## Options

- **A**: USD
- **B**: GBP
- **C**: Neither

## Answer

B

## Explanation

In forex quotations, the first currency (left of the slash) is the base currency, and the second is the quote currency. GBP/USD = 1.30 means 1 British pound equals 1.30 US dollars. The base currency is what you're buying or selling, and the quote currency tells you the price. GBP is the base; USD is the quote currency.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses base and quote currencies
- Option C misunderstands standard forex notation
