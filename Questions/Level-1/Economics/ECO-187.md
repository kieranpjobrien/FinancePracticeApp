# Question

Which of the following is most likely a lagging economic indicator?

## Options

- **A**: Average duration of unemployment
- **B**: New manufacturing orders
- **C**: Consumer confidence index

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Lagging indicators change after the economy has already turned, confirming trends rather than predicting them. Average unemployment duration is a classic lagging indicator—it takes time for layoffs to accumulate during recessions and for hiring to reduce duration during recoveries. Other lagging indicators include inflation, unit labor costs, and business loan volumes.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B (new orders) is a leading indicator
- Option C (consumer confidence) is a leading indicator
