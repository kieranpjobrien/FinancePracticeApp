# Question

China's Belt and Road Initiative primarily involves:

## Options

- **A**: Reducing Chinese exports to partner countries
- **B**: Large-scale infrastructure investment and trade route development
- **C**: Adopting Western financial system standards

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The Belt and Road Initiative (BRI) is China's massive infrastructure and development program connecting Asia, Europe, and Africa through roads, railways, ports, and pipelines. It aims to expand Chinese trade and influence while providing development finance to participating countries. The initiative has geopolitical implications, affecting trade patterns, debt relationships, and global economic governance.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is contrary to the trade-expanding purpose of BRI
- Option C contradicts China's approach of offering alternative development finance
