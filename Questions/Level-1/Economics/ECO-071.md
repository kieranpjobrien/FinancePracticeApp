# Question

A trade war is best described as:

## Options

- **A**: Military conflict over trade routes
- **B**: Escalating tariffs and trade restrictions between countries
- **C**: Competition for new export markets

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A trade war occurs when countries engage in escalating trade restrictions against each other, typically through tariffs, quotas, or other barriers. Country A imposes tariffs, Country B retaliates, and both escalate restrictions. Trade wars raise prices for consumers, disrupt supply chains, reduce trade volumes, and can harm economic growth in all countries involved. Recent examples include US-China trade tensions.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is a literal interpretation that doesn't match economic usage
- Option C describes normal trade competition, not a trade war
