# Question

A bank quotes USD/EUR as 1.0980/1.1020. The bid-ask spread is:

## Options

- **A**: 0.0040 or 40 pips
- **B**: 0.0020 or 20 pips
- **C**: 1.1000

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The bid price (1.0980) is what the bank will pay for euros, and the ask price (1.1020) is what the bank will charge for euros. The spread is:

Spread = Ask - Bid = 1.1020 - 1.0980 = 0.0040 = 40 pips

The spread represents the bank's profit margin and a transaction cost for clients.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B miscalculates the difference
- Option C confuses the midpoint with the spread
