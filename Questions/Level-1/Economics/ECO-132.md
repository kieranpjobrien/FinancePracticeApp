# Question

Supply-side economics emphasizes:

## Options

- **A**: Increasing aggregate demand through government spending
- **B**: Using tax cuts and deregulation to increase production incentives
- **C**: Expanding the money supply to boost economic growth

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Supply-side economics focuses on policies to increase aggregate supply—the economy's productive capacity. Key policies include reducing marginal tax rates (to increase work and investment incentives), deregulation (to reduce business costs), and policies promoting capital formation. This approach contrasts with demand-side Keynesian economics that emphasizes managing aggregate demand.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes Keynesian demand management
- Option C describes expansionary monetary policy
