# Question

If the marginal propensity to consume is 0.8, the spending multiplier is closest to:

## Options

- **A**: 0.8
- **B**: 1.25
- **C**: 5.0

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The spending multiplier = 1 / (1 - MPC) = 1 / (1 - 0.8) = 1 / 0.2 = 5.0. This means a $1 increase in autonomous spending increases GDP by $5. The multiplier works through successive rounds of spending: $1 spent becomes $0.80 of income, which becomes $0.64 of new spending, and so on.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses the MPC with the multiplier
- Option B uses 1/MPC instead of 1/(1-MPC)
