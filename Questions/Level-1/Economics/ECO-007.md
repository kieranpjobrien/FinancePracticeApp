# Question

Which of the following is most likely a leading economic indicator?

## Options

- **A**: Real GDP
- **B**: Building permits for new housing
- **C**: Average duration of unemployment

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Leading indicators change before the overall economy, helping predict future economic activity. Building permits signal future construction spending and are issued before homes are built. Other leading indicators include stock prices, money supply, and consumer expectations. Leading indicators help forecast turning points in the business cycle.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is a coincident indicator that moves with the economy
- Option C is a lagging indicator that changes after the economy has already shifted
