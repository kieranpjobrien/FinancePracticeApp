# Question

Operational risk includes losses from:

## Options

- **A**: Market price movements
- **B**: Failed internal processes, people, or systems
- **C**: Changes in interest rates

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Operational risk:

**Definition:**
- Risk from inadequate processes
- Human error
- System failures
- External events

**Categories:**
- Internal fraud
- External fraud
- Employment practices
- Clients and products
- Physical assets
- Business disruption
- Execution and delivery

**Examples:**
- Trading errors
- IT system failures
- Unauthorized trading
- Compliance violations
- Natural disasters

**Management:**
- Internal controls
- Backup systems
- Employee training
- Business continuity plans

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: This is market risk
- Option C: This is interest rate risk
