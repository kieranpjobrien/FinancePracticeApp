# Question

Investors receive compensation for bearing:

## Options

- **A**: Only unsystematic risk
- **B**: Only systematic risk
- **C**: Both systematic and unsystematic risk equally

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Compensated risk:

**Systematic (market) risk:**
- Cannot be diversified
- Affects all securities
- Compensated with risk premium
- Measured by beta

**Unsystematic (specific) risk:**
- Can be diversified away
- Specific to individual securities
- No compensation (no premium)
- "Free lunch" to eliminate

**Logic:**
- No one pays for avoidable risk
- Rational investors diversify
- Only non-diversifiable risk priced

**Implications:**
- Diversify to eliminate unsystematic
- Earn premium only for systematic
- Total risk irrelevant if diversified

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Unsystematic is diversifiable, not compensated
- Option C: Only systematic compensated
