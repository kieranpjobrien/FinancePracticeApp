# Question

According to CAPM, the market portfolio:

## Options

- **A**: Contains only risk-free assets
- **B**: Includes all risky assets weighted by their market values
- **C**: Is composed of equal weights in all available securities

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The market portfolio in CAPM:

- Contains ALL risky assets in the economy
- Each asset weighted by its market capitalization
- Represents the tangency portfolio on the efficient frontier
- Has a beta of exactly 1.0
- Lies on both the CML and SML

Theoretical composition:
- Stocks, bonds, real estate, commodities, human capital
- Global assets, not just domestic

In practice, broad market indices (S&P 500, MSCI World) serve as proxies.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Market portfolio contains risky assets only; risk-free assets are separate
- Option C: Equal weighting is a different strategy; market portfolio uses market-cap weights
