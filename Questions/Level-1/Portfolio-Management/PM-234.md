# Question

Tax-loss harvesting involves:

## Options

- **A**: Holding all investments indefinitely to avoid taxes
- **B**: Selling losing positions to realize losses that offset gains
- **C**: Only investing in tax-exempt securities

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Tax-loss harvesting:

**Strategy:**
- Sell positions with unrealized losses
- Realize losses for tax purposes
- Offset capital gains (reduce tax liability)
- Maintain market exposure

**Implementation:**
- Sell losing position
- Buy similar (not identical) security
- Avoid wash sale rule (30 days)
- Net losses against gains

**Benefits:**
- Reduce current year taxes
- Carry forward excess losses
- Tax-free rebalancing opportunity

**Considerations:**
- Transaction costs
- Tracking cost basis
- Wash sale rule compliance
- May not be beneficial in all situations

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: This defers taxes but doesn't harvest losses
- Option C: Not related to loss harvesting
