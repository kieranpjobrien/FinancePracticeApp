# Question

A blended benchmark combining 60% equity index and 40% bond index is most appropriate for:

## Options

- **A**: A pure equity manager
- **B**: A balanced fund targeting 60/40 allocation
- **C**: A short-term money market fund

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Blended benchmarks:

**Purpose:**
- Match multi-asset portfolios
- Reflect strategic allocation
- Appropriate for balanced strategies

**Construction:**
- Combine indices in target weights
- Rebalance periodically
- Match investment policy

**Example:**
- 60% S&P 500 + 40% Bloomberg Agg
- Matches 60/40 balanced fund
- Appropriate comparison

**Advantages:**
- Reflects actual mandate
- Fair evaluation
- Captures allocation decisions

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Pure equity needs equity-only benchmark
- Option C: Money market needs short-term benchmark
