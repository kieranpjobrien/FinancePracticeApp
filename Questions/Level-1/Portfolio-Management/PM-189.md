# Question

International diversification benefits are reduced when:

## Options

- **A**: Countries have low economic correlation
- **B**: Global financial market integration increases correlations
- **C**: Exchange rates are stable

## Answer

B

## Explanation

International diversification dynamics:

**Traditional benefits:**
- Low correlations between countries
- Different economic cycles
- Sector diversification
- Currency diversification

**Factors reducing benefits:**
- **Globalization:** Increased trade links economies
- **Financial integration:** Capital flows freely
- **Contagion:** Crises spread quickly
- **Correlation convergence:** Markets move together

**Crisis behavior:**
- Correlations spike during market stress
- "All correlations go to 1" phenomenon
- Diversification fails when needed most

**Still valuable:**
- Long-term benefits remain
- Sector/style exposure differs by country
- Emerging markets less correlated
- Currency adds dimension

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Low correlation increases benefits
- Option C: Stable exchange rates are neutral for diversification
