# Question

A wrap account bundles investment services for:

## Options

- **A**: A single all-inclusive fee
- **B**: Commission-based trading only
- **C**: Free services with no charges

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Wrap accounts:

**Definition:**
- Bundled investment services
- Single comprehensive fee
- Usually percentage of AUM
- "Wrap" all services together

**Included services:**
- Investment management
- Trading execution
- Custody
- Administrative services
- Reporting

**Fee structure:**
- Typically 1-3% of assets
- Annual fee, often quarterly billing
- No separate commissions
- Flat rate regardless of trading

**Advantages:**
- Fee transparency
- Aligned interests (no churning incentive)
- Simplified billing
- Comprehensive service

**Considerations:**
- May be expensive for low turnover
- Compare to unbundled options
- Understand what's included

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B: Replaces commissions with flat fee
- Option C: Wrap accounts do charge fees
