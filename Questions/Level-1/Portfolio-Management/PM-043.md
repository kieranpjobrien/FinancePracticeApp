# Question

A stock with a beta of 0.7 is best described as:

## Options

- **A**: An aggressive stock that amplifies market movements
- **B**: A defensive stock that dampens market movements
- **C**: A stock with no systematic risk

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Beta interpretation:
- β > 1.0: Aggressive - amplifies market movements
- β = 1.0: Moves in line with market
- 0 < β < 1.0: Defensive - dampens market movements
- β = 0: No systematic risk (uncorrelated with market)
- β < 0: Moves opposite to market (rare)

A beta of 0.7 means:
- If market rises 10%, stock expected to rise 7%
- If market falls 10%, stock expected to fall 7%
- Less volatile than the market
- Typical of utilities, consumer staples, healthcare

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Aggressive stocks have beta > 1.0
- Option C: A beta of 0 would indicate no systematic risk; 0.7 indicates some systematic risk
