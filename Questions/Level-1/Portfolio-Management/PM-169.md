# Question

Portfolio construction should be primarily driven by:

## Options

- **A**: Current market trends and recent performance
- **B**: The investor's objectives and constraints as stated in the IPS
- **C**: The portfolio manager's personal investment philosophy

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Portfolio construction drivers:

**Primary guide: The IPS**
- Return objectives
- Risk tolerance
- Time horizon
- Liquidity needs
- Tax situation
- Legal constraints
- Unique circumstances

**Why IPS-driven:**
- Tailored to specific investor
- Long-term perspective
- Clear benchmarks for evaluation
- Reduces emotional decision-making

**Secondary considerations:**
- Capital market expectations
- Manager skill/expertise
- Market conditions (within IPS bounds)

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Chasing trends ignores long-term objectives
- Option C: Manager philosophy must align with client IPS
