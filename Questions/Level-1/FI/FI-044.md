# Question

Bootstrapping is used to:

## Options

- **A**: Calculate bond credit ratings
- **B**: Derive spot rates from prices of coupon-paying bonds
- **C**: Determine optimal portfolio allocation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Bootstrapping: iteratively derives spot rates from observed bond prices. Start with shortest maturity, work forward solving for each spot rate. Uses known prices and previously derived rates. Creates spot curve from par curve. Essential for arbitrage-free valuation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A relates to credit analysis, not curve construction
- Option C describes portfolio management, not rate derivation
