# Question

If 1-year spot rate is 3% and 2-year spot rate is 4%, a 2-year 5% annual coupon bond (par $1,000) should be priced at approximately:

## Options

- **A**: $1,000.00
- **B**: $1,018.86
- **C**: $1,025.00

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Price using spot rates: PV = 50/(1.03) + 1050/(1.04)² = 48.54 + 970.32 = $1,018.86. Each cash flow discounted at appropriate spot rate. Arbitrage-free pricing ensures no riskless profit from buying/selling bonds vs. strips.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A assumes par pricing
- Option C uses simple averaging of rates rather than proper discounting
