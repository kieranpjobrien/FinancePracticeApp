# Question

Event risk for bondholders includes:

## Options

- **A**: Normal business cycle fluctuations
- **B**: Unexpected events like leveraged buyouts, mergers, or natural disasters
- **C**: Gradual changes in interest rates

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Event risk: sudden, unexpected changes affecting credit quality. LBOs add massive debt, hurting existing bondholders. Mergers may weaken combined credit. Natural disasters, regulatory changes, fraud. Change of control puts help mitigate some event risk.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes cyclical business risk
- Option C describes interest rate risk
