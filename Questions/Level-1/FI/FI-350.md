# Question

The "carry" on a bond position refers to:

## Options

- **A**: The physical cost of storing bond certificates
- **B**: The net income from holding the bond (coupon minus financing cost)
- **C**: The commission paid to trade the bond

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Carry = Coupon income - Financing cost. Positive carry when coupon exceeds funding rate. Combined with "roll" (curve rolldown benefit), determines total expected return assuming unchanged curve. Important for leveraged positions (repo-financed).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—bonds are dematerialized, no storage
- Option C—commissions are transaction costs, not carry
