# Question

Auto loan ABS typically feature:

## Options

- **A**: A revolving period where principal is reinvested
- **B**: Fully amortizing loans with declining pool balance over time
- **C**: Interest-only payments throughout the life

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Auto loans: fully amortizing, fixed payments. Pool balance declines as loans pay down. Non-revolving structure (unlike credit cards). Prepayment risk exists but less variable than mortgages. Shorter maturities than MBS. Credit enhancement through subordination.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—describes credit card ABS structure
- Option C—auto loans have principal + interest payments
