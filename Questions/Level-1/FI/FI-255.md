# Question

Currency risk for a US investor holding a Euro-denominated bond arises from:

## Options

- **A**: Changes in US interest rates
- **B**: Changes in the EUR/USD exchange rate
- **C**: Changes in European equity markets

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Currency risk: bond's value in investor's home currency varies with exchange rate. Euro strengthens vs. USD = higher USD value. Euro weakens = lower USD value. Can hedge with currency forwards/options. Adds volatility to returns.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes interest rate risk
- Option C unrelated to bond currency exposure
