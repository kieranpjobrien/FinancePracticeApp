# Question

Cash flow matching (dedication) strategy:

## Options

- **A**: Matches portfolio duration to liability duration
- **B**: Matches portfolio cash flows exactly to each liability payment
- **C**: Invests only in floating-rate securities

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Cash flow matching: structure portfolio so bond cash flows (coupons + principal) equal liability payments in each period. No reinvestment assumption needed. More conservative than immunization but may be more costly. Works for multiple liabilities. Minimal ongoing management.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—describes duration matching/immunization
- Option C—unrelated strategy
