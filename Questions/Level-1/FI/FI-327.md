# Question

The current ratio measures:

## Options

- **A**: Ability to meet long-term debt obligations
- **B**: Ability to meet short-term obligations with current assets
- **C**: Profitability relative to sales

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Current ratio = Current assets / Current liabilities. Measures short-term liquidity—ability to pay near-term obligations. Higher is better for creditors. Quick ratio (excludes inventory) is more conservative. Important for credit analysis of working capital needs.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—describes leverage or coverage ratios
- Option C—describes profit margin
