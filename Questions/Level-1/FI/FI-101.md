# Question

A "fallen angel" bond is one that:

## Options

- **A**: Was issued as investment grade but has been downgraded to high yield
- **B**: Was issued as high yield but has been upgraded to investment grade
- **C**: Has defaulted on interest payments

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Fallen angel: originally investment-grade, now high-yield due to downgrade. Often caused by deteriorating financials or industry changes. "Rising star": opposite—upgraded from high yield to investment grade. Fallen angels may trade differently than original-issue high yield.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B describes rising star
- Option C describes defaulted bonds, not just downgraded
