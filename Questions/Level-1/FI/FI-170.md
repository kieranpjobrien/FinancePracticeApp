# Question

Total return analysis for bonds considers:

## Options

- **A**: Only the yield to maturity at purchase
- **B**: Coupon income, reinvestment income, and projected sale price
- **C**: Only the coupon rate

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Total return: projects actual return over investment horizon. Components: coupons received, reinvestment income (at assumed rate), capital gain/loss at sale (at projected yield). YTM assumes hold to maturity and reinvest at YTM—often unrealistic.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores reinvestment and horizon effects
- Option C ignores price changes and reinvestment
