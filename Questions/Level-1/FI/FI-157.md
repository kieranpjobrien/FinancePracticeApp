# Question

Industry risk in credit analysis considers:

## Options

- **A**: Only the specific company's market share
- **B**: Cyclicality, competition, regulation, and growth prospects of the industry
- **C**: The company's internal controls

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Industry analysis: cyclicality (earnings volatility), competitive dynamics (pricing power), barriers to entry, regulatory environment, technology disruption, growth/decline trends. Industry risk affects all companies in sector. Top-down credit analysis component.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is company-specific, not industry analysis
- Option C describes operational/governance risk
