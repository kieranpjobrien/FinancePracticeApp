# Question

In a securitization payment waterfall, interest payments typically:

## Options

- **A**: Are distributed equally to all tranches
- **B**: Flow sequentially from senior to junior tranches
- **C**: Are paid to all tranches pro-rata based on outstanding principal

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Interest waterfall: senior tranches paid first. After senior interest covered, subordinate tranches receive interest. If pool cash flow insufficient, junior tranches may not receive full interest (or any). Principal waterfall may differ (sequential vs pro-rata).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—tranches have priority, not equal treatment
- Option C—describes some principal distributions, not typical interest
