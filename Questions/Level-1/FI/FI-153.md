# Question

The interest coverage ratio measures:

## Options

- **A**: Total debt relative to equity
- **B**: Ability to pay interest from operating earnings
- **C**: Proportion of floating-rate debt

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Interest coverage = EBIT / Interest expense. Higher ratio = more cushion to pay interest. Key credit metric. Variants: EBITDA coverage, fixed charge coverage (includes leases). Lower coverage = higher default risk.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes debt-to-equity ratio
- Option C describes interest rate structure, not coverage
