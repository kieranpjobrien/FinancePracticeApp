# Question

Arbitrage-free bond valuation:

## Options

- **A**: Uses a single discount rate for all cash flows
- **B**: Discounts each cash flow at the appropriate spot rate for its maturity
- **C**: Ignores the time value of money

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Arbitrage-free valuation: discount each cash flow at spot rate matching its timing. Prevents arbitrage between coupon bonds and zero-coupon bonds. More precise than YTM approach. Basis for pricing structured products and derivatives.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes traditional YTM approach
- Option C contradicts fundamental valuation principle
