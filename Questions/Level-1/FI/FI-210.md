# Question

The Fisher equation relates nominal interest rates to:

## Options

- **A**: Real interest rates and inflation expectations
- **B**: Credit spreads and liquidity premiums
- **C**: Short-term and long-term rates

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Fisher equation: Nominal rate ≈ Real rate + Expected inflation. More precisely: (1 + nominal) = (1 + real)(1 + inflation). TIPS yield approximates real rate; nominal Treasury yield includes inflation expectations. Breakeven inflation derived from this relationship.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B describes credit/liquidity factors
- Option C describes term structure shape
