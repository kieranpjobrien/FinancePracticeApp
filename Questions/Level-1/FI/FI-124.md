# Question

A planned amortization class (PAC) tranche in a CMO:

## Options

- **A**: Has the highest prepayment risk of all tranches
- **B**: Has more predictable cash flows within a specified prepayment range
- **C**: Receives no principal until all other tranches are retired

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PAC tranche: scheduled principal payments within PSA collar (e.g., 100-300 PSA). Support/companion tranches absorb prepayment variability outside collar. PAC has lower prepayment uncertainty but not zero. If prepayments break collar, PAC protection erodes.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes support/companion tranches
- Option C describes Z-tranche or last sequential tranche
