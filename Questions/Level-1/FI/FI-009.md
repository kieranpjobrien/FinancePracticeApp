# Question

A bond with a 6% coupon trading at $950 has a current yield of approximately:

## Options

- **A**: 6.00%
- **B**: 6.32%
- **C**: 5.70%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Current yield = Annual coupon / Current price = $60 / $950 = 6.32%. Simple measure of income return relative to price paid. Does not account for capital gain/loss at maturity or reinvestment. Higher than coupon rate when bond trades at discount.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is the coupon rate, not current yield
- Option C incorrectly calculates the relationship
