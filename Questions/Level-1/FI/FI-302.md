# Question

In a dealer market for bonds:

## Options

- **A**: Buyers and sellers trade directly with each other
- **B**: Dealers quote bid and ask prices, earning the spread
- **C**: All trades occur on a centralized exchange

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Dealer market: dealers make markets, quoting bid (buy from customer) and ask (sell to customer). Profit from spread. Principal trading—dealers own inventory. Unlike exchange markets (central order book). OTC bond trading relies on dealer liquidity provision.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—describes pure brokered market
- Option C—most bonds trade OTC, not on exchanges
