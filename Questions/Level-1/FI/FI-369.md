# Question

Bonds are typically quoted on a "clean" (flat) price basis because:

## Options

- **A**: Clean prices include all accrued interest
- **B**: Clean prices exclude accrued interest, allowing easier comparison of yields across bonds
- **C**: Clean prices are always higher than dirty prices

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Clean price quotes: exclude accrued interest. Allows comparison across bonds regardless of where they are in coupon cycle. Dirty (full) price = clean + accrued. Settlement occurs at dirty price. Standard market convention for quoting.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—clean EXcludes accrued interest
- Option C—dirty price higher except right after coupon
