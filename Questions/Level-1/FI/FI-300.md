# Question

The swap spread equals:

## Options

- **A**: The difference between the swap rate and the government bond yield of the same maturity
- **B**: The bid-ask spread in the swap market
- **C**: The difference between fixed and floating rates in a swap

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Swap spread = Swap rate - Treasury yield (same maturity). Reflects bank credit risk (LIBOR/SOFR-based) versus sovereign risk. Widens during stress as interbank credit risk rises. Used as alternative benchmark to Treasury spreads. Liquidity differences also affect spread.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B—describes market-making spread
- Option C—swap rate already incorporates rate differential
