# Question

The primary bond market is where:

## Options

- **A**: Investors trade existing bonds among themselves
- **B**: Issuers sell newly issued bonds to investors
- **C**: Bonds are redeemed at maturity

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Primary market: new issuance. Issuer sells bonds to initial investors, raises capital. Involves underwriters, syndication. Secondary market: trading of already-issued bonds. Primary market transaction creates the security; secondary provides liquidity.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—describes secondary market
- Option C—describes maturity, not a market type
