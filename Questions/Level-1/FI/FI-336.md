# Question

A collateralized loan obligation (CLO) is backed by:

## Options

- **A**: Residential mortgages
- **B**: Leveraged loans to corporations
- **C**: Credit card receivables

## Answer

B

## Explanation

CLO: securitizes pool of leveraged loans (bank loans to below-investment-grade companies). Tranched structure provides credit enhancement. Actively managed—manager can trade loans. Floating-rate assets match floating-rate liabilities. Major institutional asset class.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—describes RMBS/CMO
- Option C—describes credit card ABS
