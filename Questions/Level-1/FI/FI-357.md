# Question

The attachment point of a CDO tranche represents:

## Options

- **A**: The coupon rate on the tranche
- **B**: The level of portfolio losses at which the tranche begins to suffer losses
- **C**: The price at which the tranche trades

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Attachment point: loss threshold where tranche starts absorbing losses. Detachment point: where tranche is wiped out. Example: 3%-7% tranche loses nothing until 3% portfolio loss, then absorbs losses, fully gone at 7%. Width = 4% of portfolio losses absorbed by tranche.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—coupon is separate from structure
- Option C—trading price is market-determined
