# Question

A bond indenture is:

## Options

- **A**: The physical bond certificate
- **B**: The legal contract specifying bond terms, covenants, and bondholder rights
- **C**: The prospectus for marketing the bond

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Indenture: legal document defining bond terms. Includes coupon, maturity, call provisions, covenants (affirmative/negative), events of default, trustee duties. Trustee (usually a bank) monitors compliance on behalf of bondholders. Foundation of bondholder protection.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—physical certificate is separate from indenture
- Option C—prospectus is marketing/disclosure document
