# Question

A bond's contribution to portfolio duration equals:

## Options

- **A**: The bond's duration multiplied by its portfolio weight
- **B**: The bond's duration divided by the number of holdings
- **C**: The bond's duration plus the portfolio duration

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Contribution to duration = Weight × Duration. Measures how much each position contributes to total portfolio duration. Sum of contributions = portfolio duration. Useful for risk attribution and rebalancing decisions.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B describes equal weighting approach
- Option C adds rather than weights
