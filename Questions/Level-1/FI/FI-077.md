# Question

Commercial paper is:

## Options

- **A**: Short-term unsecured promissory notes issued by corporations
- **B**: Long-term secured bonds issued by banks
- **C**: Government-guaranteed money market instruments

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Commercial paper: short-term unsecured corporate debt, typically 1-270 days. Issued at discount by creditworthy corporations (usually investment grade). Used for working capital. Lower cost than bank loans for high-quality issuers.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B describes different instrument (long-term, secured)
- Option C confuses with government securities
