# Question

A Treasury note quoted at "99-16" means a price of:

## Options

- **A**: $99.16 per $100 face
- **B**: $99.50 per $100 face (99 and 16/32nds)
- **C**: $99.016 per $100 face

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Treasury prices quoted in 32nds. "99-16" = 99 + 16/32 = 99 + 0.50 = 99.50. Sometimes quoted in 64ths (99-16+ = 99-33/64). Corporate bonds quoted in decimals. Know convention differences.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A treats 16 as decimals
- Option C misplaces decimal
