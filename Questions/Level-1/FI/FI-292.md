# Question

Reinvestment risk is highest for:

## Options

- **A**: Zero-coupon bonds
- **B**: High-coupon bonds with long maturities
- **C**: Treasury bills

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Reinvestment risk: uncertainty about rate at which coupons can be reinvested. Higher for: large coupons (more cash to reinvest) and longer maturities (more time for rates to change). Zero-coupon has no reinvestment risk—no interim payments.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—zero-coupon has no reinvestment risk
- Option C—T-bills mature quickly with no coupons
