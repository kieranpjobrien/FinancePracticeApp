# Question

Excess spread in a securitization refers to:

## Options

- **A**: The difference between asset yields and liability costs
- **B**: The difference between senior and subordinate tranche yields
- **C**: The spread over Treasury rates

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Excess spread: interest collected on assets minus interest paid to investors minus servicing fees. First line of defense against losses. Trapped in reserve if performance triggers hit. Excess may be released to equity/residual holder if pool performs well.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B describes yield differential between tranches
- Option C describes credit spread to benchmarks
