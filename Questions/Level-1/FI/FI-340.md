# Question

A company has EBIT of $80 million and interest expense of $20 million. Its interest coverage ratio is:

## Options

- **A**: 0.25x
- **B**: 4.0x
- **C**: 25%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Interest coverage = EBIT / Interest expense = $80M / $20M = 4.0x. Company earns 4 times its interest obligations. Higher coverage = more margin of safety for bondholders. Key credit metric. EBITDA coverage often used as alternative.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—inverts the ratio
- Option C—expresses as percentage instead of multiple
