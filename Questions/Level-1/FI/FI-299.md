# Question

Excess spread in a securitization refers to:

## Options

- **A**: The difference between the bid and ask prices of ABS
- **B**: The difference between interest collected from the asset pool and interest paid to investors plus servicing fees
- **C**: The amount by which liabilities exceed assets

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Excess spread = Interest from pool - (Interest to investors + Servicing fees + Other expenses). First line of defense against losses. Absorbs minor losses before affecting subordinate tranches. Can be trapped in reserve account for additional protection. Form of credit enhancement.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—describes trading spread, not structural feature
- Option C—describes negative equity, opposite of excess spread
