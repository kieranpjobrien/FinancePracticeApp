# Question

A portfolio has 60% in bonds with duration 4 and 40% in bonds with duration 8. The portfolio duration is:

## Options

- **A**: 5.6
- **B**: 6.0
- **C**: 6.4

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Portfolio duration = weighted average = (0.60 × 4) + (0.40 × 8) = 2.4 + 3.2 = 5.6. Weights by market value. Portfolio gains/loses ~5.6% for 100bp rate change. Simple approximation—assumes parallel shift.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B uses simple average (4+8)/2
- Option C may weight incorrectly
