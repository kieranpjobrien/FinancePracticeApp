# Question

Bottom-up credit analysis focuses on:

## Options

- **A**: Macroeconomic trends and interest rate forecasts
- **B**: Individual issuer fundamentals, financial health, and security-specific factors
- **C**: Sector allocation decisions only

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Bottom-up: analyze individual companies. Financial statements, cash flows, capital structure, management, industry position. Security selection based on relative value of specific bonds. Complements top-down (macro/sector) analysis. Both needed for comprehensive approach.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—describes top-down analysis
- Option C—sector allocation is top-down
