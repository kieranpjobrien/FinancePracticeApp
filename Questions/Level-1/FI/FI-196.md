# Question

A bond with semiannual YTM of 3% per period has an effective annual yield of:

## Options

- **A**: 6.00%
- **B**: 6.09%
- **C**: 5.91%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Effective annual yield = (1 + periodic rate)^periods - 1 = (1.03)² - 1 = 1.0609 - 1 = 6.09%. Accounts for compounding. Higher than simple doubling (6%) due to interest-on-interest. Important for comparing bonds with different payment frequencies.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A simply doubles the periodic rate
- Option C may subtract compounding benefit
