# Question

The I-spread (interpolated spread) is the spread over:

## Options

- **A**: The Treasury yield curve
- **B**: The swap rate curve
- **C**: The corporate bond index

## Answer

B

## Explanation

I-spread: yield spread over swap curve (interpolated to match maturity). Swap curve often used as benchmark for credit (reflects bank credit). I-spread may differ from G-spread as swap-Treasury spread varies. Common in Europe and Asia.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes G-spread
- Option C describes relative value to peers
