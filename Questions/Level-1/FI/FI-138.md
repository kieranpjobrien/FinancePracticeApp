# Question

A barbell portfolio structure consists of:

## Options

- **A**: Bonds concentrated at intermediate maturities
- **B**: Bonds concentrated at short and long maturities with few intermediate
- **C**: Bonds evenly distributed across all maturities

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Barbell: weights at extremes (short and long maturities). Contrasts with bullet (concentrated at target maturity) and ladder (evenly distributed). Barbell has higher convexity than bullet of same duration—benefits from large rate moves either direction.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes bullet portfolio
- Option C describes ladder portfolio
