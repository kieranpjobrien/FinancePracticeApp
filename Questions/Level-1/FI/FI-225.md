# Question

A debt service coverage ratio (DSCR) of 1.5x indicates:

## Options

- **A**: Debt is 1.5 times equity
- **B**: Operating income is 1.5 times debt service (principal + interest)
- **C**: Interest expense is 1.5 times operating income

## Answer

B

## Explanation

DSCR = Net Operating Income / Debt Service. 1.5x means income covers debt payments with 50% cushion. Common in project finance, real estate. DSCR <1x means income insufficient to cover debt payments—distressed situation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes debt-to-equity ratio
- Option C inverts the ratio
