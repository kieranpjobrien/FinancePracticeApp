# Question

A Eurobond is:

## Options

- **A**: A bond denominated in euros
- **B**: A bond issued outside the jurisdiction of any single country in a currency different from the issuer's home currency
- **C**: A bond issued by European Union member countries

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Eurobond: issued in international market, outside jurisdiction of any single country. Currency differs from issuer's home country. Example: US company issuing yen bonds in London. Less regulation, global investor base. "Euro" refers to external market, not euro currency.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses Euro (currency) with Eurobond (market)
- Option C describes EU sovereign bonds
