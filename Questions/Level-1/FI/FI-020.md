# Question

Investment-grade bonds are rated:

## Options

- **A**: BB+ or lower by S&P
- **B**: BBB- or higher by S&P (Baa3 or higher by Moody's)
- **C**: Only AAA

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Investment-grade: BBB-/Baa3 or higher. Considered lower default risk, eligible for many institutional portfolios. High-yield (junk): BB+/Ba1 or lower. Higher default risk, higher yields. "Fallen angels" are downgraded from IG to HY. Rating agencies: S&P, Moody's, Fitch.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes high-yield threshold
- Option C is too restrictive—IG includes AAA through BBB-
