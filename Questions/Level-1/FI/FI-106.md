# Question

Supranational bonds are issued by:

## Options

- **A**: Large multinational corporations
- **B**: International organizations like the World Bank or IMF
- **C**: Federal government agencies

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Supranational: issued by international institutions (World Bank, European Investment Bank, Asian Development Bank). Often AAA-rated due to multiple sovereign backing. Used to fund development projects. Distinct from sovereign (single country) or agency (government-sponsored).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes multinational corporate bonds
- Option C describes agency bonds (government-sponsored enterprises)
