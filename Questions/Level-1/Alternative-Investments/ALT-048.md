# Question

Private real estate investments differ from public REITs primarily in:

## Options

- **A**: The type of properties owned
- **B**: Liquidity and trading characteristics
- **C**: The need for property management

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Private vs public real estate:

**Private real estate:**
- Direct property ownership
- Limited partnership structures
- Illiquid, long holding periods
- Infrequent valuations

**Public REITs:**
- Traded on stock exchanges
- Daily liquidity
- Market-based pricing
- Regulated structures

**Key differences:**
- Liquidity: Private illiquid, REITs liquid
- Pricing: Private appraisal, REITs market
- Correlation: REITs correlate with stocks
- Access: REITs have lower minimums

**Similar aspects:**
- Same underlying properties
- Both need management
- Income generation
- Property type exposure

**Investment considerations:**
- Private: Illiquidity premium, smoothed returns
- REITs: Liquidity, stock market correlation

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Both can own similar property types
- Option C: Both require property management
