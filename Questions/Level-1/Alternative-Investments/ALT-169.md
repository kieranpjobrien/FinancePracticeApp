# Question

A decline curve in oil and gas production:

## Options

- **A**: Shows increasing production over time
- **B**: Models the expected decrease in well production over its life
- **C**: Measures oil price changes

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Decline curve analysis:

**What it shows:**
- Production rate over time
- Natural depletion pattern
- Well productivity forecast
- Reserve estimation tool

**Typical pattern:**
- High initial production
- Rapid early decline
- Slower later decline
- Eventually uneconomic

**Decline types:**
- Exponential: Constant percentage decline
- Hyperbolic: Declining percentage
- Harmonic: Special case

**Uses:**
- Reserve estimation
- Cash flow forecasting
- Well economics
- Acquisition valuation

**Factors affecting decline:**
- Reservoir characteristics
- Well type
- Production method
- Reservoir pressure

**Investment implications:**
- Front-loaded cash flows
- Reinvestment needs
- Reserve replacement
- Asset life planning

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Production decreases, not increases
- Option C: Production volumes, not prices
