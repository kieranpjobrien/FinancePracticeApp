# Question

A continuation fund or GP-led secondary allows:

## Options

- **A**: Forcing all LPs to remain invested indefinitely
- **B**: GPs to roll selected assets into a new vehicle while offering liquidity to existing LPs
- **C**: LPs to invest in an entirely different manager

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Continuation funds:

**Structure:**
- GP forms new vehicle
- Transfers select portfolio companies
- Existing LPs choose to roll or sell
- New investors may join

**GP motivation:**
- More time for value creation
- Retain best assets
- Crystallize carry
- Reset economics

**LP options:**
- Roll into new vehicle
- Sell to new investors
- Partial cash-out possible
- Decision required

**Why growing:**
- Flexible exit timing
- Portfolio optimization
- Mature fund solutions
- Liquidity for LPs

**Considerations:**
- Fairness of pricing
- Conflict of interest
- GP incentives
- New investor terms

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: LPs have choice to exit
- Option C: Same GP continues managing
