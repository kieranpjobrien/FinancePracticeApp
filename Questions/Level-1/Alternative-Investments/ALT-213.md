# Question

Tokenization of real-world assets (RWA) aims to:

## Options

- **A**: Replace all physical assets with purely digital versions
- **B**: Create blockchain-based representations of traditional assets for improved accessibility and efficiency
- **C**: Eliminate the underlying asset value entirely

## Answer

B

## Explanation

RWA tokenization:

**Concept:**
- Digital representation on blockchain
- Of real-world assets
- Fractional ownership possible
- Programmable features

**Assets being tokenized:**
- Real estate
- Art and collectibles
- Private equity
- Bonds and treasuries
- Commodities

**Benefits:**
- Fractional ownership
- 24/7 trading potential
- Reduced intermediaries
- Global accessibility
- Programmable compliance

**Challenges:**
- Regulatory clarity needed
- Legal enforceability
- Custody solutions
- Adoption barriers

**Use cases:**
- Real estate fractionalization
- Private credit on-chain
- Treasury bonds (T-bills)
- Invoice financing

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Represents, not replaces assets
- Option C: Underlying asset value preserved
