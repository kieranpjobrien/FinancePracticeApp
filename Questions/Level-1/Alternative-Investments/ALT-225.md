# Question

Energy transition as an investment theme in natural resources focuses on:

## Options

- **A**: Only investing in fossil fuel companies
- **B**: The shift from carbon-intensive to cleaner energy sources
- **C**: Avoiding all natural resource investments

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Energy transition investing:

**Core concept:**
- Moving to cleaner energy
- Reducing carbon intensity
- Climate change mitigation
- New technology adoption

**Investment opportunities:**
- Renewable energy
- Battery storage
- Electric vehicles
- Hydrogen technology
- Grid modernization

**Natural resource implications:**
- Declining fossil fuel demand
- Critical minerals demand (lithium, cobalt)
- Stranded asset risk
- New commodity exposure

**Metals for transition:**
- Copper (electrification)
- Lithium (batteries)
- Nickel (batteries)
- Rare earths (magnets)

**Balanced approach:**
- Transition takes decades
- Traditional energy still needed
- Investment in both possible
- Risk management

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Focus on clean energy growth
- Option C: Natural resources central to transition
