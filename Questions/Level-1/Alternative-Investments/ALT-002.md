# Question

Compared to direct real estate investment, investing in REITs offers:

## Options

- **A**: Greater control over property management decisions
- **B**: Higher liquidity and lower minimum investment
- **C**: Better tax benefits through depreciation deductions

## Answer

B

## Explanation

REITs (Real Estate Investment Trusts) offer several advantages over direct real estate:

| Feature | REITs | Direct Real Estate |
|---------|-------|-------------------|
| Liquidity | High (traded on exchanges) | Low (illiquid) |
| Minimum investment | Low (buy shares) | High (property purchase) |
| Diversification | Easy (one REIT = many properties) | Difficult |
| Management | Professional, passive | Active, hands-on |
| Control | None | Full control |

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is incorrect; REITs offer no control over property decisions (that's a disadvantage)
- Option C is incorrect; direct ownership typically offers better tax benefits through depreciation pass-through
