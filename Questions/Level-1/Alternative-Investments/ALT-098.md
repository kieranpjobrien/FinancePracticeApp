# Question

Prime brokers provide hedge funds with:

## Options

- **A**: Investment recommendations and stock picks
- **B**: Securities lending, margin financing, and trade execution services
- **C**: Guaranteed investment returns

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Prime broker services:

**Core services:**
- Securities lending (for shorts)
- Margin financing (leverage)
- Trade execution
- Clearing and settlement
- Custody of assets

**Additional services:**
- Capital introduction
- Risk analytics
- Technology platforms
- Operational support

**Why funds need prime brokers:**
- Access to leverage
- Borrow securities for shorting
- Consolidated reporting
- Operational efficiency

**Major prime brokers:**
- Goldman Sachs
- Morgan Stanley
- JP Morgan
- Credit Suisse (historic)

**Counterparty considerations:**
- Concentration risk
- Credit quality of PB
- Multiple PB relationships
- 2008 lessons learned

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: PBs don't give investment advice
- Option C: No return guarantees
