# Question

Vacancy risk in real estate investing refers to:

## Options

- **A**: The risk that property values will decline
- **B**: The risk that space will remain unrented, reducing income
- **C**: The risk of construction delays

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Vacancy risk explained:

**Definition:**
- Risk of unleased space
- Loss of rental income
- Common in all property types
- Must be modeled in analysis

**Factors affecting vacancy:**
- Economic conditions
- Local market supply/demand
- Property quality
- Location desirability
- Lease expirations

**Vacancy allowance:**
- Typical 5-10% of gross income
- Market-specific estimates
- Historical analysis
- Conservative underwriting

**Management strategies:**
- Tenant retention focus
- Competitive pricing
- Property improvements
- Marketing efforts

**High vacancy impact:**
- Reduced NOI
- Lower property value
- Debt service challenges
- Cash flow shortfalls

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: This is market/price risk
- Option C: This is development risk
