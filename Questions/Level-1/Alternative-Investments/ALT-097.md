# Question

Multi-strategy hedge funds:

## Options

- **A**: Focus exclusively on one investment strategy
- **B**: Combine multiple strategies within a single fund structure
- **C**: Only invest in other hedge funds

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Multi-strategy funds:

**Structure:**
- Multiple strategy teams
- Single fund vehicle
- Dynamic allocation
- Centralized risk management

**Common strategies combined:**
- Long/short equity
- Credit
- Event-driven
- Quantitative
- Macro

**Advantages:**
- Built-in diversification
- Flexible capital allocation
- Shift to best opportunities
- Single fee structure
- Netting of positions

**For investors:**
- One relationship
- Diversified exposure
- Professional allocation
- Simpler administration

**Compared to fund-of-funds:**
- Lower total fees
- Better netting
- More integrated
- Less transparency by strategy

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Multi means multiple strategies
- Option C: This describes fund-of-funds
