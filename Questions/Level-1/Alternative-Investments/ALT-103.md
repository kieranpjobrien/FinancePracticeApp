# Question

Ethereum differs from Bitcoin primarily by:

## Options

- **A**: Having a larger maximum supply cap
- **B**: Enabling programmable smart contracts and decentralized applications
- **C**: Being controlled by a central bank

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Ethereum characteristics:

**Smart contracts:**
- Self-executing code
- Automated agreements
- No intermediaries
- Programmable money

**Platform functionality:**
- Build applications on Ethereum
- DeFi (decentralized finance)
- NFTs
- DAOs (decentralized autonomous organizations)

**Key differences from Bitcoin:**
- Programmable vs simple transfers
- Unlimited supply (but controlled issuance)
- Faster block times
- Different consensus mechanism (PoS)

**Ethereum use cases:**
- Decentralized exchanges
- Lending/borrowing protocols
- Stablecoins
- Gaming/metaverse

**Investment thesis:**
- "World computer"
- Network effects
- Developer ecosystem
- Gas fees as revenue

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Both have different supply mechanics
- Option C: Both are decentralized
