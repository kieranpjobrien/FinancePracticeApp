# Question

A term sheet in venture capital:

## Options

- **A**: Is the final binding legal agreement
- **B**: Outlines key terms of a proposed investment before definitive documents
- **C**: Is only used for debt transactions

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Term sheet purpose:

**What it is:**
- Summary of key terms
- Non-binding (mostly)
- Negotiation framework
- Precedes definitive docs

**Key terms covered:**
- Valuation
- Investment amount
- Board composition
- Liquidation preference
- Voting rights

**Process:**
- Investor issues term sheet
- Negotiation occurs
- Agreement in principle
- Detailed docs follow

**Binding provisions:**
- Exclusivity (often)
- Confidentiality
- Expense provisions
- Everything else non-binding

**After term sheet:**
- Due diligence
- Definitive documentation
- Closing
- Funding

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Non-binding, not final
- Option C: Used for equity too
