# Question

Subscription documents for alternative investments typically include:

## Options

- **A**: Only the investor's name and investment amount
- **B**: Investor qualifications, fund terms, and detailed risk disclosures
- **C**: Guaranteed return projections

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Subscription documents:

**Key components:**
- Subscription agreement
- Limited Partnership Agreement (LPA)
- Private Placement Memorandum (PPM)
- Side letters (if applicable)

**Investor information:**
- Accredited investor verification
- Qualified purchaser status
- Beneficial ownership
- Tax information

**Fund terms:**
- Fee structures
- Lock-up periods
- Redemption provisions
- Governance rights

**Risk disclosures:**
- Investment risks
- Liquidity risks
- Manager risks
- Conflicts of interest

**Legal provisions:**
- Indemnification
- Confidentiality
- Dispute resolution
- Representations

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Extensive documentation required
- Option C: Returns never guaranteed in legitimate funds
