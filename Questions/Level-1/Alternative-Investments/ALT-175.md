# Question

Shadow tolls in infrastructure financing:

## Options

- **A**: Are paid directly by vehicle users
- **B**: Are payments from government based on traffic without direct user charges
- **C**: Only apply to rail projects

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Shadow toll mechanism:

**How it works:**
- Government pays operator
- Based on traffic volumes
- No direct user charge
- Free to drivers

**Payment structure:**
- Per vehicle payment
- Banded by vehicle type
- May have caps/floors
- Traffic-dependent

**Advantages:**
- Free public access
- Revenue proportional to use
- Government controls policy
- Investment incentive

**Comparison to real tolls:**
- User pays: Direct tolls
- Shadow toll: Government pays
- Availability: No volume link
- Different risk profiles

**Where used:**
- UK historically (PFI roads)
- Some European countries
- Political preference for free roads

**Investor considerations:**
- Traffic forecasting critical
- Government credit risk
- Contract terms

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Users don't pay directly
- Option C: Common for roads
