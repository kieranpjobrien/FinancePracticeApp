# Question

The Cayman Islands is a popular hedge fund domicile because:

## Options

- **A**: It has the strictest regulatory requirements globally
- **B**: It offers tax neutrality, flexible regulation, and is recognized by institutional investors
- **C**: All hedge funds are required to be domiciled there

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Cayman domicile advantages:

**Tax neutrality:**
- No income tax
- No capital gains tax
- Pass-through to investors
- Avoids double taxation

**Regulatory framework:**
- Established fund regime
- CIMA oversight
- Familiar to investors
- Balanced approach

**Investor acceptance:**
- Long track record
- Institutional comfort
- Standard structure
- Service provider ecosystem

**Infrastructure:**
- Legal expertise
- Administrators
- Prime brokers
- Professional services

**Considerations:**
- FATCA/CRS compliance
- Substance requirements
- Economic presence
- Regulatory evolution

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Flexible, not strictest
- Option C: Choice, not requirement
