# Question

A portfolio company in private equity refers to:

## Options

- **A**: A company that invests in multiple PE funds
- **B**: A company that a private equity fund has invested in
- **C**: The administrative company managing the PE fund

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Portfolio company defined:

**Definition:**
- Company owned by PE fund
- Part of fund's investment portfolio
- Operating business
- Subject to value creation efforts

**Relationship:**
- PE fund is investor/owner
- Portfolio company is investment
- Fund may own 100% or significant stake
- Multiple portfolio companies per fund

**Value creation at portfolio company:**
- Operational improvements
- Revenue growth
- Cost optimization
- Strategic repositioning
- Add-on acquisitions

**Monitoring:**
- Board representation
- Performance tracking
- Strategic guidance
- Exit planning

**Typical fund has:**
- 10-20 portfolio companies
- Diversified across sectors
- Different stages of value creation

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: This would be an LP investor
- Option C: This is the GP or management company
