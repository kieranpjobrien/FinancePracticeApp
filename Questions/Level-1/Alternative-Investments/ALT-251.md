# Question

A loan workout in distressed real estate involves:

## Options

- **A**: Only foreclosure and property sale
- **B**: Negotiated modifications to loan terms to avoid foreclosure
- **C**: Immediate full repayment by the borrower

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Workout process:

**Definition:**
- Loan restructuring
- Alternative to foreclosure
- Negotiated solutions
- Value preservation

**Common modifications:**
- Interest rate reduction
- Term extension
- Principal forbearance
- Payment deferrals
- Covenant relief

**Why lenders workout:**
- Avoid foreclosure costs
- Preserve relationships
- Minimize losses
- Time value of money

**Why borrowers seek:**
- Avoid default
- Preserve equity
- Continue operations
- Time for recovery

**Typical triggers:**
- Covenant violations
- Payment defaults
- Property distress
- Market downturns

**Outcomes:**
- Modified loan
- Deed-in-lieu
- Short sale
- Foreclosure (last resort)

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Workout avoids foreclosure
- Option C: Borrower lacks funds for this
