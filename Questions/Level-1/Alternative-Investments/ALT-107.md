# Question

Custody solutions for digital assets are important because:

## Options

- **A**: Cryptocurrencies cannot be stolen or lost
- **B**: Private keys must be securely stored to prevent loss or theft
- **C**: All digital assets are held by central banks

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Digital asset custody:

**The challenge:**
- Private keys control assets
- Lost keys = lost assets
- Theft if keys compromised
- Irreversible transactions

**Custody options:**
- Self-custody (hardware wallets)
- Exchange custody
- Institutional custodians
- Multi-signature solutions

**Institutional requirements:**
- Regulated custodians
- Insurance coverage
- Audit capabilities
- Operational controls

**Key considerations:**
- Security vs. accessibility
- Hot vs. cold storage
- Insurance coverage
- Regulatory compliance

**Risks addressed:**
- Hacking prevention
- Key management
- Disaster recovery
- Employee fraud

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Significant theft/loss risk exists
- Option C: Decentralized, no central bank involvement
