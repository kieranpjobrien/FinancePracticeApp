# Question

Concession extensions in infrastructure can create value by:

## Options

- **A**: Shortening the investment period
- **B**: Extending the period of cash flow generation and deferring asset transfer
- **C**: Eliminating all revenue during the extension period

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Concession extension value:

**How extensions create value:**
- More years of cash flows
- Delay asset reversion
- Additional investment time
- NPV increase

**Extension mechanics:**
- Negotiated with authority
- May require conditions
- Additional investment
- Performance requirements

**Why governments extend:**
- Additional infrastructure investment
- Upgrade requirements
- Political considerations
- Avoid re-tender process

**Valuation impact:**
- Higher terminal value
- Extended income stream
- Reduced reversion risk
- Improved returns

**Conditions often required:**
- Capex commitments
- Rate concessions
- Service improvements
- Environmental upgrades

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Extends, not shortens
- Option C: Revenue continues during extension
