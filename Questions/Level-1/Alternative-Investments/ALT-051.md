# Question

Net Operating Income (NOI) for a property is calculated as:

## Options

- **A**: Gross revenue minus all expenses including debt service
- **B**: Gross revenue minus operating expenses, excluding debt service
- **C**: Total property value minus outstanding mortgage

## Answer

B

## Explanation

NOI calculation:

**Formula:**
NOI = Gross Revenue - Operating Expenses

**Included in revenue:**
- Rental income
- Parking income
- Other fees
- Less: Vacancy allowance

**Operating expenses:**
- Property taxes
- Insurance
- Utilities (if owner pays)
- Maintenance
- Property management
- Marketing/leasing costs

**Excluded from NOI:**
- Debt service (interest, principal)
- Capital expenditures
- Income taxes
- Depreciation

**Why exclude debt:**
- Allows property comparison
- Unlevered basis
- Financing decisions separate

**Importance:**
- Primary valuation metric
- Used in cap rate calculation
- Measures property performance

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Debt service excluded from NOI
- Option C: This describes equity, not NOI
