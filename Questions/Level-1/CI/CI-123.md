# Question

The option to delay a project is valuable when:

## Options

- **A**: The project has irreversible costs and uncertain future conditions
- **B**: Market conditions are completely predictable
- **C**: The project must be implemented immediately or not at all

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Delay options: waiting may reveal information that improves decisions. With irreversible investment and uncertainty, waiting has value—proceed when conditions are favorable, avoid when unfavorable. If conditions are certain or the opportunity expires, waiting has no value.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B makes waiting valueless—no new information
- Option C eliminates the delay option
