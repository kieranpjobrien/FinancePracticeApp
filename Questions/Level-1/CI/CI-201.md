# Question

The marginal cost of capital is:

## Options

- **A**: The average cost of all previously raised capital
- **B**: The cost of raising one additional dollar of new capital
- **C**: Always lower than the weighted average cost of capital

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Marginal cost of capital: cost of the next dollar of new financing. Relevant for investment decisions—projects should earn returns exceeding the cost of funds used to finance them. May increase as company raises more capital (target structure changes, credit deteriorates, markets saturate).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes historical or average cost
- Option C is not always true—marginal cost can exceed WACC
