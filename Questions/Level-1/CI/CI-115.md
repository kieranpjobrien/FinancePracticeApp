# Question

A company has ROE of 18% and a dividend payout ratio of 40%. The sustainable growth rate is:

## Options

- **A**: 7.2%
- **B**: 10.8%
- **C**: 18%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Sustainable growth rate = ROE × Retention ratio = ROE × (1 - Payout ratio) = 18% × (1 - 0.40) = 18% × 0.60 = 10.8%. This is the rate a company can grow without external equity financing, maintaining current leverage and payout.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A uses payout ratio instead of retention ratio
- Option C ignores the retention ratio
