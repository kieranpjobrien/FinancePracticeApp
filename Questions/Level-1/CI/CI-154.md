# Question

Just-in-time (JIT) inventory management aims to:

## Options

- **A**: Maximize safety stock levels
- **B**: Minimize inventory holding costs by receiving goods only when needed for production
- **C**: Increase days of inventory on hand

## Answer

B

## Explanation

JIT: coordinate production with suppliers to minimize inventory holdings. Benefits: reduced carrying costs, less warehouse space, lower obsolescence risk. Requires: reliable suppliers, accurate demand forecasting, and strong supplier relationships. Risk: supply disruption can halt production.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is opposite—JIT minimizes safety stock
- Option C would increase costs, contrary to JIT goals
