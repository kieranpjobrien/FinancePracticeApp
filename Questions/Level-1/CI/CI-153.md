# Question

Commercial paper as a source of short-term financing:

## Options

- **A**: Is typically secured by specific assets
- **B**: Is available only to firms with strong credit ratings and is usually unsecured
- **C**: Has longer maturities than corporate bonds

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Commercial paper: short-term unsecured promissory notes issued by large, creditworthy corporations. Typical maturities under 270 days (to avoid SEC registration). Lower cost than bank loans but requires strong credit rating. Investors include money market funds and corporations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is incorrect—CP is typically unsecured
- Option C is backwards—CP has very short maturities
