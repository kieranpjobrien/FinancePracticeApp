# Question

The duty of care requires directors to:

## Options

- **A**: Always maximize short-term profits
- **B**: Make informed decisions using reasonable diligence
- **C**: Personally guarantee company debts

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Duty of care: directors must act with the care a reasonably prudent person would use in similar circumstances. This includes staying informed, attending meetings, reviewing materials, and making thoughtful decisions. The business judgment rule protects good-faith decisions even if outcomes are poor.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A prioritizes short-term over prudent decision-making
- Option C describes personal liability beyond fiduciary duty
