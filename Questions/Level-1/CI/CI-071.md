# Question

Under a residual dividend policy:

## Options

- **A**: Dividends are paid first, then remaining funds are invested
- **B**: Dividends are paid only after funding all positive NPV projects
- **C**: Dividends are constant regardless of earnings

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Residual dividend: earnings fund positive NPV investments first (maintaining target capital structure); dividends equal remaining ("residual") earnings. This prioritizes value creation but results in volatile dividends. In practice, companies often smooth dividends to avoid signaling concerns.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A prioritizes dividends over investment
- Option C describes a stable dividend policy
