# Question

Board diversity is considered important for governance because:

## Options

- **A**: It satisfies regulatory requirements only
- **B**: Diverse perspectives may improve decision-making and reduce groupthink
- **C**: It eliminates all conflicts of interest

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Board diversity (gender, ethnicity, skills, backgrounds): brings varied perspectives, challenges groupthink, improves problem-solving, and better represents stakeholder interests. Research suggests diverse boards may have better governance outcomes. Many investors and regulators now emphasize board diversity.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A understates the substantive benefits
- Option C overstates diversity's effect on conflicts
