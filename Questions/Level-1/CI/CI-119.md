# Question

Collection float consists of:

## Options

- **A**: Time between invoice creation and customer payment
- **B**: Mail float, processing float, and availability float
- **C**: The interest earned on cash balances

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Collection float: delay between customer payment and available funds. Mail float (payment in transit), processing float (company processes check), and availability float (bank clears funds). Companies try to reduce collection float and extend disbursement float to maximize usable funds.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes the entire receivables period
- Option C describes investment returns
