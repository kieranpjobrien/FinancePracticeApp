# Question

Market value added (MVA) is calculated as:

## Options

- **A**: Annual operating profit minus capital charge
- **B**: Market value of the company minus total capital invested
- **C**: Net income divided by shareholders' equity

## Answer

B

## Explanation

MVA = Market value of company - Total capital invested (book value). Measures cumulative value creation since inception. Positive MVA indicates market values company above invested capital. Related to EVA: MVA approximates present value of expected future EVA. Reflects market's assessment of value creation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes EVA, not MVA
- Option C describes return on equity
