# Question

A company's operating cycle is:

## Options

- **A**: The time from paying suppliers to receiving cash from customers
- **B**: The time from purchasing inventory to collecting cash from sales
- **C**: Always shorter than the cash conversion cycle

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Operating cycle = Days of inventory + Days of sales outstanding. Measures time from inventory acquisition to cash collection. Cash conversion cycle subtracts days payables (operating cycle - payables deferral). Operating cycle is always longer than or equal to cash conversion cycle.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes the cash conversion cycle
- Option C is backwards—operating cycle is longer or equal
