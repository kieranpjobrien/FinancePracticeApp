# Question

Post-audit of capital projects helps companies by:

## Options

- **A**: Eliminating all future project risk
- **B**: Identifying forecasting errors and improving future capital budgeting
- **C**: Guaranteeing positive NPV for future projects

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Post-audits compare actual project results to forecasts. This identifies systematic forecasting biases (over-optimism on sales, under-estimation of costs), improves future projections, and creates accountability. Learning from past projects enhances decision-making quality.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A overstates benefits—risk cannot be eliminated
- Option C is impossible—audits improve, don't guarantee, outcomes
