# Question

Short-term investment policies for excess cash should prioritize:

## Options

- **A**: Maximum yield regardless of risk
- **B**: Safety and liquidity over yield
- **C**: Long-term capital gains

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Short-term cash investments: primary goals are principal preservation and liquidity. Cash needed for operations should not be at risk. Typical instruments: Treasury bills, commercial paper, money market funds. Yield is secondary—accept lower returns for safety and immediate availability.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A takes inappropriate risk with operating cash
- Option C inappropriate for short-term cash needs
