# Question

Precautionary cash balances are held to:

## Options

- **A**: Earn the highest possible investment returns
- **B**: Provide a buffer against unexpected cash needs
- **C**: Meet regular, predictable payment obligations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Precautionary balances: safety buffer for unexpected needs—equipment breakdowns, sudden opportunities, economic downturns. Companies with more volatile cash flows hold more precautionary balances. Transaction balances handle regular needs; speculative balances fund potential investments.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes investment objectives
- Option C describes transaction balances
