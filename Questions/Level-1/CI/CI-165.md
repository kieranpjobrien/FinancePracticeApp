# Question

Degree of total leverage (DTL) measures the sensitivity of:

## Options

- **A**: EBIT to changes in sales
- **B**: EPS to changes in sales
- **C**: Sales to changes in economic conditions

## Answer

B

## Explanation

DTL = DOL × DFL = (% change in EPS) / (% change in sales). Combines operating leverage (fixed costs) and financial leverage (fixed financial costs). High DTL means EPS is highly sensitive to sales changes—amplifies both positive and negative impacts on shareholder returns.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes degree of operating leverage only
- Option C reverses the relationship
