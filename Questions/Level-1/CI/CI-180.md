# Question

The build-up method for estimating cost of equity is most appropriate for:

## Options

- **A**: Large public companies with actively traded stocks
- **B**: Small private companies without reliable beta estimates
- **C**: Companies in highly efficient markets

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Build-up method: alternative when CAPM inputs unavailable. Cost of equity = Risk-free rate + Equity risk premium + Size premium + Company-specific risk premium. Used for private companies, small caps, or when beta unreliable. Adds premiums for various risk factors not captured by market beta.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A can use CAPM with reliable betas
- Option C suggests beta estimates would be reliable
