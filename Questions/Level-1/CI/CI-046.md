# Question

Direct costs of financial distress include:

## Options

- **A**: Loss of customers due to concerns about company survival
- **B**: Legal and administrative costs of bankruptcy
- **C**: Increased cost of equity

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Direct costs: legal fees, accounting fees, court costs, investment banker fees during bankruptcy proceedings. Indirect costs (Option A) include lost sales, customer flight, supplier reluctance, management distraction, and employee turnover. Option C is the increased required return from leverage risk.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes indirect costs
- Option C describes cost of capital effects, not distress costs
