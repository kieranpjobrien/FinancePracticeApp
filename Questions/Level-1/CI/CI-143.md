# Question

Net working capital equals:

## Options

- **A**: Total assets minus total liabilities
- **B**: Current assets minus current liabilities
- **C**: Cash plus accounts receivable

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Net working capital = Current assets - Current liabilities. It represents the liquid resources available for operations after meeting short-term obligations. Positive NWC indicates liquidity; negative NWC may signal financial stress (though some businesses operate successfully with negative NWC).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes net worth/equity
- Option C describes only part of current assets
