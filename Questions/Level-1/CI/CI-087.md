# Question

Asset substitution (risk shifting) occurs when:

## Options

- **A**: Shareholders replace risky assets with safe assets
- **B**: Shareholders substitute riskier projects after debt is issued
- **C**: Bondholders take over asset management

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Asset substitution: after borrowing, shareholders may prefer riskier projects than promised to bondholders. If risky projects succeed, shareholders gain; if they fail, bondholders bear losses. This wealth transfer from bondholders to shareholders is an agency cost of debt, addressed through covenants.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes the opposite behavior
- Option C misidentifies who controls asset decisions
