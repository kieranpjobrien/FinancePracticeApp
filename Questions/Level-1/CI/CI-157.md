# Question

The equivalent annual annuity (EAA) method is most appropriate when comparing:

## Options

- **A**: Projects with equal lives and different risk levels
- **B**: Mutually exclusive projects with different useful lives that can be repeated
- **C**: Independent projects with the same useful life

## Answer

B

## Explanation

EAA: converts NPV into an annual equivalent amount, allowing comparison of projects with different lives. Assumption: projects can be replicated at constant scale. For projects that cannot be repeated, NPV with explicit reinvestment assumptions is more appropriate.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A requires risk adjustment, not EAA
- Option C can be compared directly using NPV
