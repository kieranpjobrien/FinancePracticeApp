# Question

Under static trade-off theory, the optimal capital structure:

## Options

- **A**: Includes 100% equity to minimize bankruptcy risk
- **B**: Balances tax benefits and financial distress costs
- **C**: Always includes maximum possible debt for tax benefits

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Static trade-off: optimal leverage is where marginal tax benefit equals marginal distress cost. Too little debt forgoes tax shields; too much debt increases bankruptcy probability. The optimal point maximizes firm value—neither corner solution (all equity or maximum debt) is optimal.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores tax benefits of debt
- Option C ignores distress costs at high leverage
