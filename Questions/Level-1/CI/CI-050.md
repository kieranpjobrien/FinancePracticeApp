# Question

A company has days inventory of 40, days receivables of 35, and days payables of 25. The cash conversion cycle is:

## Options

- **A**: 50 days
- **B**: 75 days
- **C**: 100 days

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Cash conversion cycle = Days inventory + Days receivables - Days payables = 40 + 35 - 25 = 50 days. This measures the time between paying suppliers and collecting from customers. Shorter cycles require less working capital financing.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B adds days payables instead of subtracting
- Option C adds all three components
