# Question

Stretching payables beyond credit terms:

## Options

- **A**: Always improves supplier relationships
- **B**: May damage supplier relationships and credit standing
- **C**: Is required by accounting standards

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Stretching payables: delaying payment beyond agreed terms. Short-term cash benefit but risks: damaged supplier relationships, lost discounts, credit rating impact, potential supply disruptions. May signal financial distress. Sustainable working capital management maintains good supplier relationships.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is opposite—it damages relationships
- Option C is not an accounting requirement
