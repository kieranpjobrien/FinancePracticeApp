# Question

An investor deposits $10,000 in an account earning 5% annual interest, compounded annually. The value of the account after 3 years is closest to:

## Options

- **A**: $11,500
- **B**: $11,576
- **C**: $11,625

## Answer

B

## Explanation

FV = PV × (1 + r)^n
FV = $10,000 × (1.05)^3
FV = $10,000 × 1.157625
FV = $11,576.25

The future value reflects compound interest over three years.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A uses simple interest: $10,000 × (1 + 0.05 × 3) = $11,500
- Option C may result from a calculation error or using a different rate
