# Question

A sample contains the values: 4, 6, 8, 10, 12. The sample mean is:

## Options

- **A**: 6
- **B**: 8
- **C**: 10

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Sample mean = Sum of values / Number of values

Mean = (4 + 6 + 8 + 10 + 12) / 5
Mean = 40 / 5
Mean = 8

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is incorrect; perhaps only summing first three values
- Option C might result from calculation errors
