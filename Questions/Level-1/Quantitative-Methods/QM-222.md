# Question

The discount factor for 2 years at a 5% annual discount rate is:

## Options

- **A**: 0.907
- **B**: 0.952
- **C**: 1.103

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Discount factor = 1 / (1 + r)^n

DF = 1 / (1.05)²
DF = 1 / 1.1025
DF = 0.907

The discount factor converts future values to present values by multiplying: PV = FV × DF

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B is the one-year discount factor: 1/1.05 = 0.952
- Option C is the future value factor (1.05)², not the discount factor
