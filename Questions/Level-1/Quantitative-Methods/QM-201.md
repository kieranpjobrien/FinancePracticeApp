# Question

In the Capital Asset Pricing Model (CAPM), the market portfolio:

## Options

- **A**: Contains only risk-free assets
- **B**: Contains all risky assets in proportion to their market values
- **C**: Is the portfolio with the highest expected return

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The market portfolio in CAPM theory includes all risky assets (stocks, bonds, real estate, etc.) held in proportion to their market capitalizations. It represents the tangency portfolio on the efficient frontier when combined with the risk-free asset. In practice, a broad market index is often used as a proxy.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is incorrect; the market portfolio contains risky assets, not risk-free
- Option C ignores that risk must be considered; higher expected return comes with higher risk
