# Question

Standard IV(A) Loyalty to employers requires members to:

## Options

- **A**: Never disagree with employer decisions
- **B**: Act for the benefit of the employer and avoid activities detrimental to employer interests
- **C**: Prioritize employer interests above all other obligations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(A) requires acting in the employer's interest and avoiding harm to those interests. This does not mean blind agreement or prioritizing employer over client interests, but rather faithful service within ethical bounds.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A equates loyalty with unquestioning obedience
- Option C incorrectly places employer above client interests
