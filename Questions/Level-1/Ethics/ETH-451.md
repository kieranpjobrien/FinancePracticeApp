# Question

The key element distinguishing market manipulation from legitimate trading is:

## Options

- **A**: The size of the trades
- **B**: The intent to deceive or artificially affect prices
- **C**: Whether the trades are profitable

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard II(B) focuses on the intent behind trading activity. Manipulation involves deliberately misleading market participants or artificially affecting prices. Trade size and profitability do not determine whether activity is manipulative.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A focuses on quantity rather than intent
- Option C uses outcomes rather than purpose to judge conduct
