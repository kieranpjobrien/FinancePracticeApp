# Question

A wealth manager receives ongoing referral fees from a fund company for assets placed in their funds. This arrangement:

## Options

- **A**: Only needs to be disclosed at account opening
- **B**: Must be disclosed prominently and updated if terms change
- **C**: Need not be disclosed if the fees are industry standard

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(C) requires disclosure of referral fee arrangements. Ongoing fees require continued disclosure, and any material changes to terms must be communicated. "Industry standard" does not eliminate disclosure obligations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A treats disclosure as a one-time event
- Option C incorrectly uses industry practice to avoid disclosure
