# Question

An analyst maintains research records in electronic format only. Regulatory guidance recommends paper backups. Under Standard V(C):

## Options

- **A**: Electronic records alone are always insufficient
- **B**: The format should comply with applicable laws and reasonably support the investment basis
- **C**: Only paper records are acceptable under the Standards

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(C) requires records that support investment recommendations and actions, in compliance with applicable requirements. The specific format—electronic or paper—depends on regulatory requirements and firm policies, not a universal mandate.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores that electronic records may be adequate
- Option C incorrectly mandates paper format
