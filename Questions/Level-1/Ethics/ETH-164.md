# Question

The primary purpose of the Global Investment Performance Standards (GIPS) is to:

## Options

- **A**: Guarantee investment returns for clients
- **B**: Ensure fair representation and full disclosure of investment performance
- **C**: Replace local regulatory requirements

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS are designed to ensure that investment performance is presented fairly and with full disclosure, enabling meaningful comparison across firms globally. GIPS complement, rather than replace, local regulations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses presentation standards with return guarantees
- Option C misunderstands the relationship between GIPS and local regulations
