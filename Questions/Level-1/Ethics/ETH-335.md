# Question

Sharing CFA exam question content after the exam:

## Options

- **A**: Is acceptable since the exam is completed
- **B**: Violates Standard VII(A) because it compromises exam integrity
- **C**: Is prohibited only during the testing window

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(A) prohibits sharing exam content at any time, not just during testing. Sharing content after completion can help future candidates unfairly and compromises the validity of the testing program.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores ongoing confidentiality requirements
- Option C too narrowly defines the prohibition period
