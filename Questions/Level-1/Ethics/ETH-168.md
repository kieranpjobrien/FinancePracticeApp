# Question

A strong ethical culture within an investment firm is primarily characterized by:

## Options

- **A**: Strict punishment for any compliance violations
- **B**: Leadership that models ethical behavior and clear communication of values
- **C**: Extensive written policies without active reinforcement

## Answer

B

## Explanation

An ethical culture requires leadership commitment ("tone at the top"), clear communication of values, and consistent modeling of expected behavior. Written policies alone are insufficient without active reinforcement and leadership example.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A focuses on punishment rather than culture development
- Option C ignores the need for active implementation of policies
