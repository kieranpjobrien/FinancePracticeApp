# Question

Under Standard I(A), participating in or assisting violations:

## Options

- **A**: Is only a violation if the member profits
- **B**: Violates the Standard regardless of personal benefit
- **C**: Is acceptable if ordered by a supervisor

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(A) prohibits participation in or assistance with violations, regardless of whether the member personally benefits. The prohibition focuses on conduct, not profit, and supervisor orders don't excuse violations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly ties violation to profit
- Option C incorrectly permits violations when ordered
