# Question

A professional Code of Ethics for investment professionals requires members to place the integrity of the investment profession and the interests of clients above:

## Options

- **A**: Their own personal interests
- **B**: The interests of their employer
- **C**: Both their own personal interests and the interests of their employer

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Professional codes of ethics typically state that members must "Place the integrity of the investment profession and the interests of clients above their own personal interests." While members should not harm their employer's interests, such codes specifically address placing client and profession interests above personal interests.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B is not explicitly stated in typical codes of ethics
- Option C overstates the requirement—codes specifically mention personal interests, not employer interests
