# Question

A candidate who passed Level II states "CFA Level II" on their resume. Under Standard VII(B):

## Options

- **A**: This accurately reflects their progress
- **B**: This is improper; the correct statement is "Passed CFA Level II exam"
- **C**: Any reference to CFA is prohibited until chartered

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(B) requires accurate representation of CFA program status. "CFA Level II" implies a designation level. Correct usage states passing specific exams or being a current candidate, not implying partial designation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A accepts confusing terminology
- Option C prohibits legitimate status references
