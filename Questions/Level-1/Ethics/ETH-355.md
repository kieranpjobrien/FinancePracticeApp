# Question

An analyst tweets brief investment opinions to followers. Under Standard V(B):

## Options

- **A**: Social media posts are informal and not subject to the Standards
- **B**: Even brief communications should distinguish fact from opinion
- **C**: Only long-form reports require compliance with communication standards

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(B) applies to all forms of communication, including social media. Even in brief formats, members should distinguish between facts and opinions and avoid misleading statements.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly exempts social media
- Option C creates an unjustified format distinction
