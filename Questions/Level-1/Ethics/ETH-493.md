# Question

CFA candidates may share:

## Options

- **A**: Actual exam questions with future candidates
- **B**: Personal study strategies and approved preparation materials
- **C**: Content from the exam through anonymous online posts

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(A) protects exam confidentiality. Candidates may share study approaches and discuss approved materials but cannot disclose actual exam content regardless of forum or anonymity.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A directly violates confidentiality
- Option C incorrectly assumes anonymity permits sharing
