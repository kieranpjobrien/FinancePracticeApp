# Question

Exaggerating the services a firm can provide to prospective clients violates:

## Options

- **A**: Only marketing regulations
- **B**: Standard I(C) Misrepresentation
- **C**: Nothing if the services could potentially be added later

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(C) prohibits misrepresentation of services the firm can provide. Overstating capabilities creates false expectations and misleads clients about what they will receive.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores ethical obligations beyond regulations
- Option C allows current misrepresentation based on future possibilities
