# Question

When CFA Institute conducts an investigation, members and candidates must:

## Options

- **A**: Only cooperate if legally compelled
- **B**: Cooperate with the investigation as required by the Professional Conduct Program
- **C**: Refuse until represented by legal counsel

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(A) requires cooperation with CFA Institute investigations. This is a condition of membership and candidacy, not dependent on legal compulsion or counsel representation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A treats cooperation as optional
- Option C creates barriers to required cooperation
