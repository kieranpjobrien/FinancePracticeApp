# Question

Research reports that comply with Standard I(B) should be based on:

## Options

- **A**: The desires of investment banking clients
- **B**: Thorough independent analysis of relevant data
- **C**: Popular market sentiment

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(B) requires independence and objectivity. Research should be based on thorough, independent analysis of relevant data, not influenced by banking relationships or simply following market consensus.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A compromises independence for commercial interests
- Option C substitutes market opinion for independent analysis
