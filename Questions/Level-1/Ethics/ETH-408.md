# Question

An analyst's firm is acquired and records from the old firm are being destroyed. Under Standard V(C), the analyst should:

## Options

- **A**: Allow destruction since it is the new firm's decision
- **B**: Attempt to preserve records supporting past recommendations for the required retention period
- **C**: Only preserve records of currently active recommendations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(C) requires maintaining records that support past analyses and recommendations. Members should advocate for preservation of these records during transitions. The retention obligation covers historical work, not just current recommendations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the member's personal responsibility for record retention
- Option C too narrowly limits retention to active recommendations
