# Question

An analyst incorporates ESG factors into her analysis. Under Standard V(A):

## Options

- **A**: ESG analysis is not appropriate for investment decisions
- **B**: ESG factors may be considered if the analyst has reasonable basis for their relevance
- **C**: ESG must always be the primary consideration

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(A) requires reasonable basis for recommendations. ESG factors may appropriately be considered as part of analysis if the analyst has reasonable basis for believing they affect investment value or client objectives.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly excludes ESG considerations
- Option C mandates a specific analytical priority
