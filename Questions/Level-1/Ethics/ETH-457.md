# Question

Reporting illegal employer activities to regulators:

## Options

- **A**: Always violates the duty of loyalty to employers
- **B**: May be appropriate after internal reporting fails or is not feasible
- **C**: Is never permitted under any circumstances

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(A) loyalty does not require concealing illegal activity. When internal channels fail or are inappropriate, external reporting may be necessary. Whistleblower protections often exist for good-faith reporting of violations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A misinterprets loyalty as covering up wrongdoing
- Option C ignores the ethical obligation to report serious violations
