# Question

For institutional clients with investment expertise, suitability requirements:

## Options

- **A**: Do not apply
- **B**: Still apply but the analysis may recognize the client's sophistication
- **C**: Are more stringent than for retail clients

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(C) suitability applies to all clients. However, for sophisticated institutional clients, the analysis can recognize their expertise and potentially more complex needs while still ensuring recommendations are appropriate.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly exempts institutional clients
- Option C reverses the appropriate consideration
