# Question

When using third-party research under Standard V(A), members must:

## Options

- **A**: Accept it at face value from reputable sources
- **B**: Exercise diligence and reasonable judgment in evaluating the research
- **C**: Only use research from CFA charterholders

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(A) requires diligent evaluation of third-party research. Members cannot blindly rely on external sources regardless of reputation. They must apply reasonable judgment to assess quality and appropriateness.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A abdicates the member's responsibility for due diligence
- Option C imposes an unrealistic and non-existent requirement
