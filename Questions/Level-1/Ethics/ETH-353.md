# Question

A supervisor's responsibilities under Standard IV(C) include:

## Options

- **A**: Only addressing violations after they occur
- **B**: Both preventing and detecting violations through reasonable procedures
- **C**: Guaranteeing that no violations occur

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(C) requires both prevention through training and procedures, and detection through monitoring. Supervisors must make reasonable efforts on both fronts, though they cannot guarantee zero violations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores prevention responsibility
- Option C sets an impossible standard
