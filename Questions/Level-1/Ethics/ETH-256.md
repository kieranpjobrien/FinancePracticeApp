# Question

An analyst plans to purchase shares in a company after issuing a buy recommendation. Standard VI(A) requires:

## Options

- **A**: The analyst never purchase shares of covered companies
- **B**: Disclosure of the intent to purchase, allowing clients to act first
- **C**: Immediate purchase to align interests with clients

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(A) and VI(B) require disclosure of ownership interests and giving clients priority. The analyst should disclose the intent to purchase and ensure clients have opportunity to act before the personal transaction.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is too restrictive; ownership is permitted with disclosure
- Option C ignores transaction priority requirements
