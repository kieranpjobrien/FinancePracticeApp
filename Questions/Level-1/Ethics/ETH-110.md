# Question

Which characteristic most distinguishes a profession from other occupations?

## Options

- **A**: Higher average compensation
- **B**: Specialized knowledge combined with a duty to serve clients ethically
- **C**: Government licensing requirements

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A profession is distinguished by the combination of specialized knowledge and skills along with an ethical duty to serve clients' interests. While licensing and compensation may be features of some professions, the defining characteristic is the commitment to ethical service alongside expertise.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses economic outcomes with professional characteristics
- Option C describes a regulatory feature that applies to many occupations, not just professions
