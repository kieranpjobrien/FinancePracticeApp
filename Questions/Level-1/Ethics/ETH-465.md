# Question

A firm pays solicitors to bring in new clients. The solicitor fee arrangement:

## Options

- **A**: Need only be disclosed to the solicitors, not clients
- **B**: Must be disclosed to prospective clients before they become clients
- **C**: Is only disclosed after the client relationship is established

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(C) requires advance disclosure of referral fee arrangements. Prospective clients must understand the solicitor's financial interest before deciding to engage. Post-engagement disclosure is too late for informed decision-making.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A limits disclosure to the wrong party
- Option C delays disclosure past the decision point
