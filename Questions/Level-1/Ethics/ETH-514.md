# Question

Standard V(A) requires that investment recommendations have:

## Options

- **A**: Unanimous agreement among analysts
- **B**: A reasonable and adequate basis supported by appropriate research
- **C**: Guaranteed positive outcomes

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(A) requires recommendations to be supported by reasonable and adequate basis, meaning sufficient research and analysis. Unanimous agreement is not required, and outcomes cannot be guaranteed.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A imposes an unrealistic consensus requirement
- Option C confuses thorough analysis with guaranteed results
