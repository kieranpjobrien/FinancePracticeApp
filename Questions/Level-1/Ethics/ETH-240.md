# Question

The Code of Ethics applies to CFA Institute members:

## Options

- **A**: Only when working in their primary professional capacity
- **B**: In all professional activities, regardless of jurisdiction or role
- **C**: Only in countries where CFA Institute has an official presence

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The Code of Ethics applies to all professional activities of members and candidates, regardless of where they work, what role they hold, or whether CFA Institute has local operations. The ethical obligations are personal and travel with the individual.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A too narrowly limits application
- Option C incorrectly ties obligations to institutional presence
