# Question

A firm stores all investment research records in cloud-based systems. Under Standard V(C):

## Options

- **A**: Only physical records satisfy the Standard
- **B**: Cloud storage is acceptable if records are secure, accessible, and meet retention requirements
- **C**: Cloud storage requires regulatory pre-approval

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(C) focuses on maintaining accessible, complete records supporting investment decisions. The storage medium—physical or digital—is less important than ensuring records are secure, accessible, and retained appropriately.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly requires physical format
- Option C invents a pre-approval requirement
