# Question

A member has a significant gambling problem that leads to financial distress. Standard I(D):

## Options

- **A**: Only applies if the gambling affects work directly
- **B**: May be triggered if the behavior leads to dishonesty or affects professional judgment
- **C**: Does not cover personal lifestyle choices

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(D) covers conduct reflecting adversely on professional integrity. While personal choices are generally private, gambling problems that result in financial pressure, dishonesty, or impaired judgment can trigger professional misconduct concerns.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A too narrowly defines relevant conduct
- Option C ignores personal behavior that affects professional integrity
