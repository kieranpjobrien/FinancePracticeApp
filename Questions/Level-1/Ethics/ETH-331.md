# Question

A firm receives notice of pending litigation and is subject to a document hold. Under Standard V(C):

## Options

- **A**: Normal retention policies continue to apply
- **B**: The firm must preserve all potentially relevant records regardless of normal retention periods
- **C**: Only documents specifically requested must be preserved

## Answer

B

## Explanation

When litigation is pending or reasonably anticipated, legal hold requirements typically override normal retention schedules. Standard V(C)'s record-keeping obligations include compliance with legal preservation requirements.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores litigation hold obligations
- Option C too narrowly defines preservation scope
