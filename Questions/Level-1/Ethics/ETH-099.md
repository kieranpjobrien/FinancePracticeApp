# Question

The primary purpose of the Global Investment Performance Standards (GIPS) is to:

## Options

- **A**: Require all investment firms to achieve minimum performance levels
- **B**: Standardize the calculation and presentation of investment performance
- **C**: Mandate specific investment strategies for institutional portfolios

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS provides a standardized framework for calculating and presenting investment performance, enabling fair comparison across firms and time periods. The standards focus on how performance is calculated and reported, not on the level of performance achieved or investment strategies employed.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses performance standards with performance requirements
- Option C addresses investment process rather than performance reporting
