# Question

An analyst wants to provide investment consulting services to friends on weekends for a fee, separate from her regular employment. Under Standard IV(A):

## Options

- **A**: This is prohibited as it competes with her employer
- **B**: She must obtain written consent from her employer before beginning
- **C**: Personal business activities outside work hours require no disclosure

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(A) requires members to obtain consent before engaging in independent practice that could compete with their employer. Weekend consulting for compensation could create conflicts and must be disclosed to and approved by the employer.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is too restrictive; such activities are permitted with consent
- Option C ignores the duty to disclose potentially competing activities
