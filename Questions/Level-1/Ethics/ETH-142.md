# Question

Standard IV(A) Loyalty requires members to act for the benefit of their employer and:

## Options

- **A**: Never disagree with management decisions
- **B**: Not cause harm to the employer through actions that are illegal or unethical
- **C**: Place employer interests above all client interests

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(A) requires members to act in their employer's interest and not engage in conduct that harms the employer. However, this does not require unquestioning obedience or placing employer interests above clients.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A misinterprets loyalty as requiring agreement with all decisions
- Option C reverses the proper priority; client interests come before employer interests
