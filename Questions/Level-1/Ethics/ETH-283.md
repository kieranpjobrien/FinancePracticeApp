# Question

A financial planner receives ongoing payments for clients referred to a mutual fund company. Under Standard VI(C):

## Options

- **A**: One-time disclosure at the beginning of the relationship is sufficient
- **B**: Ongoing arrangements should be disclosed and may require periodic reminder
- **C**: Only the initial referral fee needs disclosure

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(C) requires disclosure of referral fee arrangements. Ongoing payments create continuing conflicts that may warrant periodic disclosure to ensure clients remain aware of the arrangement's influence on recommendations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A may become stale as time passes
- Option C ignores the ongoing nature of the conflict
