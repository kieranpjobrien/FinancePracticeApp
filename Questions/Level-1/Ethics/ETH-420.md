# Question

An analyst writes research reports for companies that pay for coverage. To comply with Standard I(B):

## Options

- **A**: This practice is prohibited regardless of disclosure
- **B**: The analyst must disclose the payment and take steps to maintain objectivity
- **C**: Disclosure is only required for negative ratings

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Issuer-paid research is permitted under Standard I(B) but requires prominent disclosure of the payment arrangement. Analysts must also implement procedures to protect objectivity, such as flat fees regardless of rating outcome.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly prohibits a permissible practice
- Option C applies disclosure selectively rather than consistently
