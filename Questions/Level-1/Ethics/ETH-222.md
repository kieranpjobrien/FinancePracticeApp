# Question

A large pension fund rebalances its portfolio, causing significant market impact. Under Standard II(B):

## Options

- **A**: The fund has manipulated the market by moving prices
- **B**: Legitimate rebalancing is not manipulation even if it affects prices
- **C**: The fund should avoid rebalancing to prevent price impact

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard II(B) prohibits trading intended to mislead or manipulate. Legitimate portfolio management activities, including rebalancing, are not manipulation even if they have price impact. Intent to deceive, not market impact, determines manipulation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses price impact with manipulative intent
- Option C would prevent legitimate portfolio management
