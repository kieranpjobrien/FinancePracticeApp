# Question

Compensation structures that heavily emphasize short-term performance may:

## Options

- **A**: Always improve ethical behavior
- **B**: Create incentives for excessive risk-taking or unethical shortcuts
- **C**: Have no effect on employee behavior

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Compensation tied primarily to short-term results can create pressure to take excessive risks or cut ethical corners. Understanding this risk helps firms design incentive structures that balance performance with ethical conduct.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the potential for perverse incentives
- Option C underestimates how compensation influences behavior
