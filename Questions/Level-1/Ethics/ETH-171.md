# Question

An analyst's resume states she holds an MBA degree when she only completed coursework without graduating. This violates Standard I(C) because:

## Options

- **A**: Only investment-related credentials matter
- **B**: It misrepresents professional qualifications
- **C**: Resume errors are not covered by the Standards

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(C) prohibits misrepresentation of qualifications, including academic credentials. Claiming a degree not earned is a clear misrepresentation that could mislead employers and clients about the analyst's qualifications.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly limits the scope of misrepresentation
- Option C ignores that resumes used professionally are subject to the Standards
