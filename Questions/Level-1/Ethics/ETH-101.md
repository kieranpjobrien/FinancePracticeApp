# Question

GIPS compliance must be applied on a:

## Options

- **A**: Portfolio-by-portfolio basis, allowing firms to choose which accounts comply
- **B**: Firm-wide basis, with consistent standards across all discretionary portfolios
- **C**: Composite-by-composite basis, with different standards for different strategies

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS requires firm-wide compliance—a firm cannot claim partial compliance or apply the standards selectively to only certain portfolios or composites. All discretionary, fee-paying portfolios must be included in at least one composite, and consistent policies must apply across the firm.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A allows cherry-picking of compliant accounts, which GIPS prohibits
- Option C permits inconsistent application of standards across the firm
