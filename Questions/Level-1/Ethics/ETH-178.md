# Question

Under Standard IV(B), which gift from a client requires employer notification?

## Options

- **A**: A thank-you card
- **B**: A $2,000 bonus for exceptional portfolio performance
- **C**: A coffee mug with the client's company logo

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(B) requires disclosure of additional compensation that could create conflicts. A $2,000 bonus is substantial compensation that could influence behavior and requires employer consent. Token gifts like cards or promotional items typically don't require disclosure.

**Common mistakes:**
- Options A and C are de minimis items that don't create meaningful conflicts
