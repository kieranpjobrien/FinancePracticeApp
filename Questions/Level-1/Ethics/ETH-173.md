# Question

"Wash trading" refers to:

## Options

- **A**: Executing legitimate hedging transactions
- **B**: Simultaneously buying and selling a security to create misleading appearance of activity
- **C**: Trading based on technical analysis signals

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Wash trading involves buying and selling the same security simultaneously to create an artificial appearance of trading activity. This is a form of market manipulation prohibited by Standard II(B) because it misleads other market participants about actual supply and demand.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes legitimate risk management
- Option C describes a valid trading methodology
