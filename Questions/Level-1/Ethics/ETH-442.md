# Question

A firm states it is "in partial compliance with GIPS." This statement:

## Options

- **A**: Is acceptable as it indicates progress toward full compliance
- **B**: Is prohibited because GIPS compliance is all-or-nothing
- **C**: Is required during the transition period

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS prohibits claims of partial compliance. Firms either fully comply with all requirements or they do not comply. Partial compliance claims are misleading and not permitted under GIPS.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A accepts a prohibited claim
- Option C incorrectly suggests partial compliance during transition
