# Question

A portfolio manager is offered a bonus directly from a grateful client, outside of the firm's normal compensation structure. Under Standard IV(B), the manager must:

## Options

- **A**: Refuse any additional compensation under all circumstances
- **B**: Obtain written consent from the employer before accepting
- **C**: Accept but not disclose to avoid complications

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(B) requires members to obtain written consent from all parties involved before accepting additional compensation that might create a conflict with the employer's interests. This ensures transparency and allows the employer to assess potential conflicts.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is too restrictive; additional compensation is permitted with consent
- Option C violates disclosure requirements and could create conflicts
