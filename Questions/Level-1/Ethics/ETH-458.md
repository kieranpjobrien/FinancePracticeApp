# Question

A client offers a portfolio manager a significant bonus if the portfolio outperforms by year-end. Under Standard IV(B):

## Options

- **A**: The bonus may be accepted if disclosed to and approved by the employer
- **B**: Direct client bonuses are always prohibited
- **C**: Disclosure is only required if the bonus exceeds a certain threshold

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Standard IV(B) permits additional compensation with employer disclosure and consent. The potential bonus should be disclosed so the employer can evaluate conflicts, such as incentives for excessive risk-taking.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B prohibits a permissible arrangement with proper disclosure
- Option C creates an undefined threshold
