# Question

An analyst's firm has no specific record retention policy. Under Standard V(C), the analyst should:

## Options

- **A**: Keep records only as long as personally convenient
- **B**: Maintain records for at least seven years in accordance with recommended guidance
- **C**: Destroy records immediately after completing each analysis

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(C) recommends maintaining records for at least seven years when no firm or regulatory policy exists. This supports the ability to demonstrate the basis for recommendations and protects against future disputes.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A fails to establish minimum retention periods
- Option C destroys evidence needed to support analytical conclusions
