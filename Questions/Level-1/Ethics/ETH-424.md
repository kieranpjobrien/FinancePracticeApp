# Question

Transaction-based manipulation under Standard II(B) includes:

## Options

- **A**: Legitimate hedging strategies
- **B**: Trades designed to artificially affect price or volume
- **C**: Normal portfolio rebalancing

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard II(B) prohibits transactions designed to mislead market participants by artificially affecting prices or volume. Legitimate investment activities like hedging and rebalancing are not manipulation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses legitimate strategies with manipulation
- Option C mischaracterizes normal portfolio management
