# Question

An employee's personal business activities:

## Options

- **A**: Never affect employment duties
- **B**: May create conflicts that require disclosure to the employer
- **C**: Are completely private and need not be disclosed

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(A) recognizes that personal business activities can create conflicts with employment duties. Activities that might compete with or affect employment should be disclosed for employer evaluation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores potential conflicts
- Option C treats all personal activities as private regardless of impact
