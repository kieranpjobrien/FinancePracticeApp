# Question

A candidate who has passed all three levels of the CFA exam but has not yet received the charter writes "CFA (Pending)" on her business card. This:

## Options

- **A**: Is acceptable because she has completed the exams
- **B**: Violates Standard VII(B) because the designation cannot be used until awarded
- **C**: Is required to inform clients of her qualifications

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(B) prohibits misrepresentation of CFA status. Until the charter is officially awarded, candidates cannot imply they hold the designation. "CFA (Pending)" falsely suggests imminent charterholder status.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores that charter requirements include more than passing exams
- Option C misunderstands disclosure obligations
