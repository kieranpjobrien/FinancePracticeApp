# Question

A financial advisor receives a fee for referring clients to a mortgage broker. Under Standard VI(C) Referral Fees:

## Options

- **A**: Such arrangements are prohibited
- **B**: The advisor must disclose the arrangement to clients before making referrals
- **C**: Disclosure is only required if the fee exceeds $500

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(C) requires disclosure of any compensation received for referrals. Clients must be informed of the arrangement before the referral so they can evaluate whether the recommendation is based on their interest or the advisor's financial incentive.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is too restrictive; referral fees are permitted with disclosure
- Option C invents a materiality threshold that doesn't exist in the Standard
