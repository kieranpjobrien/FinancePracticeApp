# Question

Effective supervision under Standard IV(C) includes:

## Options

- **A**: Only reviewing completed transactions
- **B**: Training employees and implementing preventive compliance systems
- **C**: Relying entirely on employee certifications

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(C) requires reasonable efforts to prevent and detect violations. This includes proactive measures like training, establishing compliance systems, and ongoing monitoring—not just reactive review.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is only reactive, not preventive
- Option C provides insufficient oversight
