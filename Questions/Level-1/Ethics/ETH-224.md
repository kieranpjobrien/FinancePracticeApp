# Question

Standard III(B) Fair Dealing:

## Options

- **A**: Prohibits different service levels for different clients
- **B**: Requires that no client be disadvantaged by the actions of the member
- **C**: Mandates identical treatment of all clients

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Fair dealing means clients should not be disadvantaged by a member's actions. Different service levels are acceptable (premium clients may receive more services), but dissemination of recommendations and investment actions must be fair.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A prohibits acceptable service tiering
- Option C requires identical rather than fair treatment
