# Question

Before making investment recommendations, Standard III(C) requires members to understand the client's:

## Options

- **A**: Only their stated investment goals
- **B**: Financial situation, investment experience, and objectives
- **C**: Only their risk tolerance

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(C) requires comprehensive understanding of the client's complete profile, including financial circumstances, investment experience, risk tolerance, and objectives. This enables appropriate suitability determinations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores financial situation and experience
- Option C focuses too narrowly on one aspect of the profile
