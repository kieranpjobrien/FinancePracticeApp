# Question

Standard VI(B) Priority of Transactions establishes that:

## Options

- **A**: Personal trades should be executed before client trades
- **B**: Client and employer transactions take priority over personal transactions
- **C**: All transactions should be executed simultaneously

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(B) requires that client and employer transactions receive priority over personal trades. This prevents front-running and ensures members do not benefit personally at the expense of those they serve.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A reverses the required priority
- Option C ignores the hierarchical nature of transaction priority
