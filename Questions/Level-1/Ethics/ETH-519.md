# Question

A member receives referral fees for recommending third-party services. Beyond disclosure, Standard VI(C) suggests:

## Options

- **A**: No further obligation once disclosure is made
- **B**: Ensuring referred services are appropriate and suitable for client needs
- **C**: Declining all referral arrangements

## Answer

B

## Explanation

While Standard VI(C) focuses on disclosure, broader fiduciary duties require that referred services be appropriate. Members should not recommend unsuitable services merely because referral fees are disclosed.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A treats disclosure as the only obligation
- Option C prohibits a permissible practice
