# Question

An analyst is offered a paid board seat at a company she might recommend. Under Standard IV(B):

## Options

- **A**: Board compensation is not additional compensation requiring disclosure
- **B**: The arrangement must be disclosed to and approved by her employer
- **C**: She can accept without disclosure if she recuses from coverage

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Board compensation is additional compensation under Standard IV(B) and must be disclosed to the employer. The conflict exists regardless of recusal plans, so employer knowledge and consent are required.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly excludes board fees
- Option C fails to require employer disclosure even with recusal
