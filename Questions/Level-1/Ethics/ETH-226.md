# Question

A marketing presentation includes backtested performance results. Under Standard III(D):

## Options

- **A**: Backtested results cannot be presented to clients
- **B**: Backtested results must be clearly identified as such and distinguished from actual performance
- **C**: Backtested results may be presented without any special disclosure

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(D) requires fair, accurate, and complete performance presentation. Backtested or simulated results must be clearly labeled as such so clients understand they do not represent actual trading results.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is too restrictive; backtested results can be useful if disclosed
- Option C fails to require essential disclosure
