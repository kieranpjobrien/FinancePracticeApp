# Question

Before accepting compensation from a third party for services rendered, a member must:

## Options

- **A**: Only disclose if the amount is significant
- **B**: Obtain written consent from the employer after full disclosure
- **C**: Accept it privately if it does not affect work quality

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(B) requires disclosure and written employer consent before accepting outside compensation. This applies regardless of amount or perceived impact on work quality, as the employer needs to assess potential conflicts.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A creates an undefined materiality threshold
- Option C ignores the disclosure requirement regardless of impact
