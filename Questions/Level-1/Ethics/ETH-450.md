# Question

An analyst pays an expert network for access to industry consultants. During a call, a consultant reveals a company will announce a major contract next week. The analyst should:

## Options

- **A**: Trade immediately since the information was obtained through legitimate research
- **B**: Not trade or share the information as it constitutes material nonpublic information
- **C**: Trade only in small amounts to avoid detection

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard II(A) prohibits trading on MNPI regardless of how it was obtained. Information about an unannounced major contract is material and nonpublic. The expert network channel does not legitimize trading on inside information.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly legitimizes the source
- Option C compounds the violation while attempting to evade detection
