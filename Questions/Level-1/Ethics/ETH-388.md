# Question

GIPS verification:

## Options

- **A**: Is required for all firms claiming compliance
- **B**: Is recommended but not required, and applies to the firm as a whole
- **C**: Can be performed by internal staff

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS verification is voluntary but recommended. When performed, it must be conducted by an independent third party and applies to the entire firm, not individual composites. Firms cannot verify themselves.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly states verification is mandatory
- Option C ignores the independence requirement
