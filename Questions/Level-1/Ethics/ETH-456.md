# Question

A private wealth advisor works at a firm that also provides investment banking services. Under Standard III(E):

## Options

- **A**: The advisor may freely share client information within the firm
- **B**: Information barriers should protect client confidentiality across divisions
- **C**: Client information may be shared if it benefits the banking relationship

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(E) requires protecting client confidentiality. In multi-service firms, information barriers should prevent inappropriate sharing across divisions. Client information should not be shared merely because it could benefit other firm activities.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores confidentiality obligations within the firm
- Option C prioritizes firm revenue over client privacy
