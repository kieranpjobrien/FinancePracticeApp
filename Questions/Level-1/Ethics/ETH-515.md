# Question

An analyst posts investment opinions on personal social media accounts. Standard V(B):

## Options

- **A**: Does not apply to personal social media
- **B**: Applies to all investment communications regardless of platform
- **C**: Only applies if the employer's name is mentioned

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(B) communication requirements apply to all investment recommendations and opinions, regardless of platform. Personal social media posts about investments must still meet professional communication standards.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly exempts personal platforms
- Option C creates an artificial distinction based on employer mention
