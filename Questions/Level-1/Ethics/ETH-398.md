# Question

A manager uses client brokerage commissions (soft dollars) to pay for office furniture. This practice:

## Options

- **A**: Is acceptable if disclosed to clients
- **B**: Violates Standard III(A) because soft dollars must benefit clients, not the manager
- **C**: Is permitted if the furniture is used in the investment process

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(A) requires that soft dollar arrangements benefit clients. Office furniture primarily benefits the manager's operations, not the investment process or client interests. Soft dollars should fund research or services that improve investment decisions.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly suggests disclosure cures misuse
- Option C stretches the definition of investment process benefit
