# Question

A supervisor can satisfy supervisory responsibilities under professional standards by:

## Options

- **A**: Hiring only experienced professionals who should know the rules
- **B**: Implementing reasonable procedures to detect and prevent violations
- **C**: Requiring employees to sign acknowledgments that they understand all regulations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Supervisors must establish and implement reasonable procedures designed to detect and prevent violations. This includes training, monitoring, and enforcement systems appropriate to the firm's size and activities. Mere reliance on employee experience or acknowledgments is insufficient without actual supervisory systems.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A relies on employee qualifications rather than active supervision
- Option C focuses on documentation rather than substantive compliance procedures
