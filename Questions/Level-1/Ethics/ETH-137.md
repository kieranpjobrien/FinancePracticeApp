# Question

Before making an investment recommendation, Standard III(C) Suitability requires members to:

## Options

- **A**: Ensure the investment will generate positive returns
- **B**: Make reasonable inquiry into the client's financial situation, investment experience, and objectives
- **C**: Obtain written approval from the client for each transaction

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(C) requires members to understand their clients' investment profile—including financial situation, risk tolerance, investment experience, and objectives—before making recommendations. This forms the basis for determining suitability.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is impossible to guarantee; suitability is about appropriateness, not guaranteed returns
- Option C may be required by some regulations but is not the core suitability requirement
