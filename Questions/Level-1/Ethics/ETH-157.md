# Question

Standard VI(B) Priority of Transactions establishes that investment transactions for clients should take priority over transactions for:

## Options

- **A**: Other clients with smaller accounts
- **B**: The member's own account and the employer's account
- **C**: All parties equally without priority

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(B) establishes a priority hierarchy: client transactions take priority over employer transactions, which take priority over member personal transactions. This prevents front-running and other self-dealing at client expense.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A creates improper distinctions among clients
- Option C ignores the clear priority established by the Standard
