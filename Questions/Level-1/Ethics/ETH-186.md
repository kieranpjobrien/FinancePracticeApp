# Question

A portfolio manager joins a new firm and wants to bring her prior performance record. Under GIPS portability requirements:

## Options

- **A**: Prior performance can always be used if the manager was the primary decision-maker
- **B**: Prior performance may be portable if substantially all the decision-making personnel transferred and other requirements are met
- **C**: Performance history cannot be transferred between firms

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS allows performance portability under specific conditions: substantially all decision-making personnel must transfer, the new firm must have records to support the history, and other requirements must be satisfied. Simply being the primary decision-maker is insufficient.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A oversimplifies the portability requirements
- Option C is too restrictive; portability is permitted under specific conditions
