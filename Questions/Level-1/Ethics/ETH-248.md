# Question

An Investment Policy Statement (IPS) should be:

## Options

- **A**: Created once and never modified
- **B**: Reviewed and updated periodically to reflect changes in client circumstances
- **C**: Kept confidential from the client

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(C) requires ongoing suitability assessment. The IPS should be reviewed regularly and updated when client circumstances, objectives, or constraints change. A static IPS may become inappropriate over time.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the need for ongoing review
- Option C contradicts client transparency requirements
