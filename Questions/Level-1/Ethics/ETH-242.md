# Question

An analyst's spouse works for a company the analyst covers. Under Standard I(B):

## Options

- **A**: The analyst must stop covering the company immediately
- **B**: The analyst should disclose the relationship and manage the conflict appropriately
- **C**: Personal relationships never affect professional objectivity

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A spouse's employment creates a potential conflict that must be disclosed under Standard I(B). Complete withdrawal from coverage may not be required if the conflict can be managed through disclosure and appropriate safeguards.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A may be appropriate but isn't always required
- Option C ignores obvious potential for bias
