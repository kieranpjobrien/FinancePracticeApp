# Question

What is the primary difference between ethical conduct and legal compliance?

## Options

- **A**: Ethical conduct only applies to personal behavior, while legal compliance applies to professional behavior
- **B**: Ethical conduct may require standards beyond what the law mandates
- **C**: Legal compliance is voluntary while ethical conduct is mandatory

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Ethical conduct often requires adhering to standards that go beyond minimum legal requirements. While legal compliance sets the floor for acceptable behavior, ethics represents a higher standard that considers what is right, not just what is legally permitted.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly separates ethical and legal domains; both apply professionally
- Option C reverses the relationship; legal compliance is mandatory while ethical standards may exceed requirements
