# Question

Self-regulation in the investment profession:

## Options

- **A**: Replaces the need for government oversight
- **B**: Complements regulatory oversight by promoting higher standards
- **C**: Only benefits practitioners, not clients

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Self-regulation through codes of ethics and professional standards complements government regulation. It often sets higher standards than legal minimums and promotes a culture of ethical conduct that benefits all stakeholders.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A suggests self-regulation eliminates regulatory need
- Option C ignores client protection benefits
