# Question

A candidate observes another candidate using unauthorized materials during a professional certification examination. The observing candidate should:

## Options

- **A**: Focus on her own exam and not concern herself with others' conduct
- **B**: Report the suspected violation to exam administrators or the professional body
- **C**: Confront the other candidate directly to stop the cheating

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Maintaining the integrity of professional examinations is important for the value of the credential. Candidates have a responsibility to report suspected violations to appropriate authorities. Cheating undermines the certification's value and is unfair to candidates who follow the rules. Proper reporting channels should be used rather than direct confrontation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the responsibility to maintain exam integrity
- Option C may be disruptive and is not the proper channel for addressing violations
