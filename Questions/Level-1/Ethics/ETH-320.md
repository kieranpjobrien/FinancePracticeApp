# Question

An analyst's independent research leads to the same conclusion as material nonpublic information she inadvertently received. She may:

## Options

- **A**: Trade based on her independent research, documenting it thoroughly
- **B**: Never trade once she has received any MNPI
- **C**: Trade only if the MNPI confirms her prior conclusion

## Answer

A

## Explanation

If an analyst has an independent, documented basis for her conclusion that predates receipt of MNPI, she may be able to trade on that independent research. Thorough documentation is essential to demonstrate the independent basis.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B is too restrictive if independent basis exists
- Option C suggests MNPI can validate rather than contaminate analysis
