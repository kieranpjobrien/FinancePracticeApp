# Question

A regulatory investigation requests records that the member destroyed after holding them for three years. Regulatory requirements specify five-year retention. Under Standard V(C):

## Options

- **A**: The member complied with Standard V(C) as long as firm policy was followed
- **B**: The member failed to meet the Standard's requirement to comply with applicable regulations
- **C**: Three years is generally sufficient for all purposes

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(C) requires compliance with applicable regulatory record retention requirements. If regulations require five-year retention, destroying records after three years violates both the regulations and the Standard.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly prioritizes firm policy over regulations
- Option C invents a universal standard that doesn't exist
