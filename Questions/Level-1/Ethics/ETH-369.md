# Question

An analyst combines public financial statements, industry reports, and her own channel checks with retailers to form a unique investment conclusion. This approach is:

## Options

- **A**: A violation because the conclusion differs from public consensus
- **B**: Consistent with mosaic theory and permissible
- **C**: Only allowed if disclosed to clients

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Mosaic theory allows analysts to combine public and non-material nonpublic information to reach unique conclusions. The analyst's process of synthesizing public data with her own research (channel checks) represents legitimate analysis.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A misunderstands that unique conclusions from public data are acceptable
- Option C incorrectly requires disclosure of the analytical process
