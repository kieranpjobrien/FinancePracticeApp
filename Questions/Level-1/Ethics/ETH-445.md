# Question

The primary purpose of ethical standards in investment management is to:

## Options

- **A**: Protect investment professionals from liability
- **B**: Promote trust and integrity in financial markets
- **C**: Create barriers to entry for new professionals

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Ethical standards exist to promote trust and integrity in financial markets. When investors trust the markets and their advisors, capital allocation becomes more efficient, benefiting the entire economy.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A focuses on professional protection rather than market integrity
- Option C misunderstands the purpose as exclusionary
