# Question

A GIPS-compliant presentation shows gross returns of 12% and net returns of 10.5%. The difference reflects:

## Options

- **A**: Trading costs only
- **B**: Investment management fees and potentially other fees
- **C**: Benchmark outperformance

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The difference between gross and net returns under GIPS primarily reflects investment management fees. Gross returns are calculated before deduction of management fees, while net returns are after. The 1.5% difference indicates fees deducted.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes trading costs, typically deducted from both
- Option C confuses the fee difference with relative performance
