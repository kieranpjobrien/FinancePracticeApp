# Question

A firm's marketing states "Our CFA team manages your money." Standard VII(B) requires:

## Options

- **A**: This is acceptable as general marketing
- **B**: The firm must clarify which team members hold the CFA designation
- **C**: The statement must include each charterholder's CFA number

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(B) prohibits misleading implications about CFA designation. "CFA team" could imply all team members are charterholders. Firms should clarify which individuals actually hold the designation to avoid misrepresentation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A accepts potentially misleading language
- Option C imposes a requirement that does not exist
