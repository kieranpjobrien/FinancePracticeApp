# Question

To maintain the right to use the CFA designation, charterholders must:

## Options

- **A**: Work in a specific type of financial role
- **B**: Maintain their membership in CFA Institute and submit to the Professional Conduct Program
- **C**: Complete a minimum number of transactions annually

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Maintaining the CFA designation requires ongoing CFA Institute membership and agreement to abide by the Code and Standards. The designation is not tied to specific job functions or transaction volumes.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly limits who can hold the designation
- Option C invents an activity requirement that doesn't exist
