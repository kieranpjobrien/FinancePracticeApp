# Question

A supervisor implements a compliance system but fails to periodically review its effectiveness. Violations occur that would have been detected by updated procedures. Under Standard IV(C):

## Options

- **A**: The supervisor complied by implementing a system
- **B**: The supervisor may have failed by not ensuring the system remained adequate
- **C**: Periodic review is only required by regulatory mandate

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(C) requires reasonable efforts to prevent and detect violations, which includes maintaining effective compliance systems. Implementing a system once without periodic review falls short of ongoing supervisory responsibility.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A treats initial implementation as sufficient
- Option C incorrectly ties requirement to external mandates
