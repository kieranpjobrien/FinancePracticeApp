# Question

Using another analyst's research without attribution constitutes:

## Options

- **A**: Efficient use of industry resources
- **B**: Plagiarism and a violation of Standard I(C)
- **C**: Only a violation if the research is copyrighted

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(C) requires proper attribution when using others' work. Using research without credit constitutes plagiarism regardless of copyright status. Members must acknowledge sources of ideas, analysis, and recommendations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores attribution requirements
- Option C incorrectly links the ethical requirement to copyright law
