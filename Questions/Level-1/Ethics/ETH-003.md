# Question

Which of the following best describes why ethics is important in the investment profession?

## Options

- **A**: Ethics helps investment professionals avoid legal penalties
- **B**: Ethical behavior builds the trust necessary for financial markets to function efficiently
- **C**: Ethics is primarily important for marketing purposes to attract clients

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Ethics in the investment profession is fundamentally about building and maintaining trust. Financial markets require trust to function efficiently—investors must trust that markets are fair, that professionals are acting in their interests, and that information is reliable. Without this trust, market participation decreases and capital allocation becomes inefficient.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is too narrow—while avoiding legal issues is a benefit, it's not the core purpose of ethical behavior
- Option C misunderstands ethics as a marketing tool rather than a fundamental requirement for market integrity
