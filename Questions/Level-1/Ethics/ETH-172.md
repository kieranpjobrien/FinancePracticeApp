# Question

An analyst learns from a reliable source that a tender offer for a company will be announced tomorrow. Under Standard II(A), the analyst:

## Options

- **A**: May trade on this information if she independently believed the company was undervalued
- **B**: Must not trade or recommend trading until the information becomes public
- **C**: May share the information with select institutional clients

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Information about an upcoming tender offer is material nonpublic information. Standard II(A) prohibits trading on or communicating such information until it becomes public. Independent research conclusions do not justify trading on MNPI.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly allows trading if the analyst had a prior opinion
- Option C violates the prohibition on communicating MNPI
