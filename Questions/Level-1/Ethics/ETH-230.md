# Question

Investment research reports should include disclosure of:

## Options

- **A**: Only positive factors supporting the recommendation
- **B**: Key assumptions and limitations of the analysis
- **C**: Proprietary trading algorithms in full detail

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(B) requires disclosure of assumptions, limitations, and factors that could affect the investment analysis. This enables clients to understand the basis for recommendations and evaluate their applicability.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A presents biased information
- Option C would compromise proprietary methods unnecessarily
