# Question

A firm presents returns from "carved-out" segments of balanced portfolios without allocating cash. Under GIPS:

## Options

- **A**: This is acceptable for any historical periods
- **B**: Cash must be allocated to carve-outs shown for periods after certain dates
- **C**: Carve-outs may never be shown

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS requires that carve-outs include cash allocation to present fair returns. Current GIPS standards require cash allocation for carve-out segments to be included in composites.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores cash allocation requirements
- Option C is too restrictive; carve-outs can be shown with proper treatment
