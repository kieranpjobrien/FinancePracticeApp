# Question

When facing a potential legal or ethical issue, members should:

## Options

- **A**: Act quickly to resolve before anyone notices
- **B**: Document the situation and their analysis of the proper course of action
- **C**: Avoid creating any written record that could be subpoenaed

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(A) supports thorough documentation when facing legal or ethical issues. Documentation demonstrates the member's good faith effort to comply and provides evidence of thoughtful analysis. Avoiding documentation suggests attempts to hide misconduct.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A prioritizes speed over proper analysis
- Option C treats documentation as a liability rather than protection
