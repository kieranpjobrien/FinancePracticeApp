# Question

A client approaching retirement continues to hold an aggressive growth portfolio. Under Standard III(C):

## Options

- **A**: The portfolio remains suitable if it was appropriate when established
- **B**: The advisor should proactively discuss whether the portfolio still meets the client's needs
- **C**: Changes can only be made at the client's initiative

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(C) requires ongoing suitability assessment. As clients approach retirement, their circumstances typically change, requiring proactive discussion about whether current investments remain appropriate.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A treats initial suitability as permanent
- Option C abdicates the advisor's responsibility for ongoing assessment
