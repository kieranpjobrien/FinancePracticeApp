# Question

What is the PRIMARY role of a servant leader on an agile project?

## Options

- **A**: Make decisions for the team so it can move quickly.
- **B**: Report team performance to senior management.
- **C**: Remove impediments and create the conditions for the team to succeed.
- **D**: Enforce process compliance across the team.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Servant leadership puts the team's needs first by removing blockers, providing what is needed and creating space for the team to do its best work. (A) is command-and-control, the opposite of servant leadership. (B) is a real but secondary activity, not the primary role. (D) treats process compliance as an end in itself rather than a means.
