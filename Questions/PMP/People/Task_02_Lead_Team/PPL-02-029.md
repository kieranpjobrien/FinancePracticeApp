# Question

A project has an extremely tight deadline. The team is working hard but progress is slower than required. What leadership approach should the PM take?

## Options

- **A**: Add more people to the team to speed delivery and meet the deadline.
- **B**: Demand longer hours and weekend work from the team until the deadline passes.
- **C**: Work with the team to remove obstacles, ruthlessly prioritise, and cut non-essential scope.
- **D**: Accept that the project will fail on the deadline and stop pushing the team.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

(C) is the leadership pattern PMI prefers under crunch: enable the team rather than load them. (A) often slows things further (Brooks's Law). (B) burns the team out and degrades quality. (D) is premature defeatism.
