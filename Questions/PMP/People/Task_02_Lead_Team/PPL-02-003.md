# Question

A high-performing team member privately tells the PM they are bored, feel underutilised on routine tasks, and are considering leaving the project. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Remind them of their commitment to the project and the importance of finishing assigned work.
- **B**: Offer them a salary increase if they stay through to delivery.
- **C**: Explore stretch opportunities — challenging assignments, mentoring, leading an improvement initiative.
- **D**: Begin recruiting their replacement while looking for new work for them.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

(C) addresses Herzberg's motivators — growth, meaningful work, autonomy — which are what's actually driving the disengagement. (A) appeals to obligation, which accelerates departure. (B) is a hygiene factor; it removes dissatisfaction but rarely creates motivation. (D) plans for failure before attempting retention.
