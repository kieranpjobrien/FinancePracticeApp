# Question

A new PM inherits a team accustomed to authoritarian leadership — they wait to be told exactly what to do. The PM wants to develop autonomous behaviour. What approach should they use?

## Options

- **A**: Transition gradually from explaining, to asking for input, to delegating decisions.
- **B**: Stop giving directions immediately and expect the team to self-organise on their own.
- **C**: Maintain the authoritarian style since it is what the team already knows and expects.
- **D**: Replace team members with people already accustomed to working autonomously.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

(A) is developmental: build understanding, then participation, then ownership — a graduated movement that matches situational leadership principles. (B) creates anxiety and stalls work. (C) entrenches dependency. (D) loses team knowledge and is rarely practical.
