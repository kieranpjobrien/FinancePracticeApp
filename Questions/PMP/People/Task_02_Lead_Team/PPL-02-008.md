# Question

An experienced team is approaching a critical milestone when a key member announces unexpected medical leave for several weeks. The team is concerned about workload impact. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Request a replacement resource from management straight away.
- **B**: Facilitate a team session to assess impact, identify options and commit to a plan together.
- **C**: Spread the absent member's work across the team and extend the milestone date.
- **D**: Personally take on the absent member's responsibilities to limit disruption.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

(B) involves the team in the problem-solving, leveraging their domain knowledge and creating ownership of whatever plan emerges — exactly what PMI servant leadership prescribes. (A) assumes a replacement is needed and cannot ramp up in time. (C) makes the call for the team and concedes scope before exploring options. (D) creates a single point of failure and signals distrust in the team.
