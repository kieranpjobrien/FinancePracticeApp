# Question

In situational leadership, which style is MOST appropriate when team members are highly skilled AND highly motivated?

## Options

- **A**: Delegating
- **B**: Directing
- **C**: Coaching
- **D**: Supporting

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Delegating fits high competence and high commitment — the leader empowers and steps back. (B) Directing suits low competence and low commitment. (C) Coaching suits motivated but still-developing people. (D) Supporting suits skilled but less-motivated members who need encouragement.
