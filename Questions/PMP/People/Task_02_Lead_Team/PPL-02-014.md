# Question

A new PM inherits a team of highly skilled experts who resent being micromanaged. The previous PM gave detailed instructions for every task, and the team is disengaged. What leadership style should the new PM adopt?

## Options

- **A**: Continue detailed instructions to keep things consistent.
- **B**: Provide no direction at all and let the team figure everything out.
- **C**: Adopt a servant-leadership stance — clarify outcomes, remove blockers, empower the team to self-organise.
- **D**: Limit yourself to administrative work and stay out of the team's way.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

(C) is the servant-leader response: experts respond best to clear outcomes, removed obstacles and decision authority within their expertise. (A) perpetuates the disengagement. (B) abandons the team — even experts need direction-setting and coaching. (D) abdicates leadership and produces drift.
