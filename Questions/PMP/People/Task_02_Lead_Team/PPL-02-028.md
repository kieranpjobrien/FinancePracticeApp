# Question

In Tuckman's model, during which stage does the team experience the most conflict as members push against boundaries and each other?

## Options

- **A**: Forming
- **B**: Norming
- **C**: Performing
- **D**: Storming

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Storming is when members challenge leadership, compete for position and surface differing styles — uncomfortable but necessary for the team to progress. (A) Forming is polite orientation. (B) Norming is when conflict gives way to shared norms. (C) Performing is high-functioning collaboration with minimal dysfunctional conflict.
