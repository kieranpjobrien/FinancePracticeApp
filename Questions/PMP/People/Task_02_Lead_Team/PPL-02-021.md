# Question

A PM notices that decisions made in team meetings are not being followed through — members agree in the room and then carry on with their own approach. What leadership action should the PM take?

## Options

- **A**: Make all decisions unilaterally to ensure compliance going forward.
- **B**: Stop holding team meetings since they aren't producing follow-through.
- **C**: Publicly call out non-compliance in the next meeting to enforce accountability.
- **D**: Investigate the root cause of the pattern and address it directly with the team.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

(D) is the diagnostic, root-cause response PMI prefers. The pattern is symptomatic; identify the cause before changing process. (A) buys compliance and loses engagement. (B) abandons a useful forum without fixing it. (C) creates fear without addressing why decisions aren't sticking.
