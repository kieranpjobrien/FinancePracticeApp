# Question

An influential stakeholder wants three additional features added to the project with no change to schedule or budget. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Accept the request to preserve the relationship, and quietly re-sequence the team's work.
- **B**: Decline the request and cite the approved project baseline as the reason.
- **C**: Agree now and plan to quietly deprioritise the new features as delivery pressure builds.
- **D**: Show the quantified impact, offer options, and ask the stakeholder to choose among them.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The PM's responsibility is informed decision-making by the person with authority; an honest trade-off presentation respects both the baseline and the stakeholder's prerogative. (A) accepts uncompensated scope growth and sets the team up to miss commitments. (B) refuses without offering a path, which damages the relationship and forecloses a workable answer. (C) is a dishonest commitment that breaches the PMI Code of Ethics.
