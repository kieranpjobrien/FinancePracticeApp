# Question

Which communication technique involves restating what a speaker said in your own words to confirm you have understood them correctly?

## Options

- **A**: Active listening
- **B**: Clarifying questions
- **C**: Summarising
- **D**: Paraphrasing

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Paraphrasing — "so what I hear you saying is..." — restates the speaker's message in the listener's words so the speaker can confirm or correct the interpretation. (A) active listening is broader: it includes attention, body language, and silence as well as paraphrasing. (B) clarifying questions seek new information rather than reflect what was already said. (C) summarising condenses several points into a recap, usually at the end of a discussion, rather than confirming a single message in the moment.
