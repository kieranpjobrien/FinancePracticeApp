# Question

A conflict between two team members has escalated to the point where they refuse to work together. Other members are taking sides and productivity is dropping. Mediation attempts by the PM have failed. What is the BEST next step?

## Options

- **A**: Permanently separate them onto different workstreams with no contact.
- **B**: Engage HR and the relevant functional managers, providing documented impact on the project.
- **C**: Recommend that one of them be removed from the project to restore team cohesion.
- **D**: Allow more time and hope it resolves on its own.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

(B) is the appropriate escalation now that team-level resolution has failed: HR and line managers have the authority and tools the PM lacks, and the documented impact gives them a basis to act. (A) treats the symptom but the conflict will resurface. (C) is a drastic action the PM does not own and removes potentially valuable contributors. (D) allows continued deterioration and is not leadership.
