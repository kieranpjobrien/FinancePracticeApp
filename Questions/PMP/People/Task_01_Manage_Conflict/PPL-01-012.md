# Question

Which conflict-handling technique is characterised by one party giving in to the other's position, often to preserve harmony?

## Options

- **A**: Collaborating
- **B**: Compromising
- **C**: Forcing
- **D**: Accommodating (smoothing)

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Accommodating, also called smoothing, is when one party yields entirely. (A) Collaborating seeks a win-win that meets both parties' needs. (B) Compromising means both sides give something up, not just one. (C) Forcing imposes one party's view on the other.
