# Question

Which conflict-handling approach is most appropriate when the issue is trivial or when more important matters need attention?

## Options

- **A**: Avoiding (withdrawing)
- **B**: Forcing
- **C**: Collaborating
- **D**: Compromising

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Avoiding is appropriate for trivial issues, when emotions need time to cool, or when the cost of engagement outweighs the benefit. (B) Forcing burns relationship capital on minor matters. (C) Collaborating costs more time than the issue is worth. (D) Compromising still requires negotiation effort that the issue does not justify.
