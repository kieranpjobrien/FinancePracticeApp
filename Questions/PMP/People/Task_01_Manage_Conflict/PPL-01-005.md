# Question

Which of the following is most commonly a source of conflict on projects?

## Options

- **A**: A clear, agreed work breakdown structure
- **B**: Detailed, approved project baselines
- **C**: Scarce resources and competing priorities
- **D**: Active, engaged executive sponsorship

## Answer

C

## Explanation

PMI lists scarce resources, scheduling priorities, and technical opinions among the top sources of conflict. (A) and (B) are preventive — clarity and baselines reduce ambiguity, which reduces conflict. (D) an engaged sponsor typically resolves rather than creates conflict; weak sponsorship is the risk factor.
