# Question

Which conflict-handling technique results in a partial solution where neither party fully gets what they wanted?

## Options

- **A**: Collaborating
- **B**: Compromising
- **C**: Forcing
- **D**: Avoiding

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Compromising splits the difference — both sides give up something and neither is fully satisfied. (A) Collaborating produces a win-win that meets both parties' needs. (C) Forcing is a win-lose. (D) Avoiding leaves the conflict unresolved entirely.
