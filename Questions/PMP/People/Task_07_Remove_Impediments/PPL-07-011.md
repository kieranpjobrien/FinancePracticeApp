# Question

In Scrum, who is primarily accountable for ensuring impediments to the Scrum team are addressed?

## Options

- **A**: The Product Owner.
- **B**: The development team members themselves.
- **C**: The Scrum Master.
- **D**: The project sponsor.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The Scrum Guide assigns the Scrum Master accountability for the team's effectiveness, including the removal of impediments that the team cannot resolve on its own. (C) is correct. (A) owns value and the backlog. (B) handles many impediments themselves but the accountability for ensuring they are addressed sits with the SM. (D) is outside the Scrum team.
