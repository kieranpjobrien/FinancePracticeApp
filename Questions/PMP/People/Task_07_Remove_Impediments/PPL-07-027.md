# Question

Which tool is most commonly used by agile teams to make impediments visible and trackable?

## Options

- **A**: An impediment board or impediment backlog showing each impediment, owner, and status.
- **B**: A Gantt chart showing planned versus actual durations for each task.
- **C**: A work breakdown structure decomposing the project's deliverables.
- **D**: A PERT chart showing optimistic, pessimistic, and most likely durations.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Agile teams typically use a visible impediment board or backlog so blockers cannot be forgotten and ownership is clear. (A) is the standard. (B), (C) and (D) are project planning tools and not designed for impediment tracking.
