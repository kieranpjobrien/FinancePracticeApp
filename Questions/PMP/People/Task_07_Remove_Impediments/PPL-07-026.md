# Question

The team raises slow office internet as a recurring impediment. IT has previously declined an upgrade citing budget. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Accept the constraint and reduce sprint commitments going forward to match the productivity loss.
- **B**: Quantify the productivity impact and present a data-backed case to IT leadership with interim mitigations.
- **C**: Direct the whole team to work from home so personal connections become the project's connectivity.
- **D**: Stop raising the issue with IT and absorb the loss as part of project overhead.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PMI prefers data-driven advocacy when initial budget objections block a clear productivity issue, paired with interim mitigation. (B) does both. (A) bakes in the loss. (C) shifts an organisational problem onto individual home setups. (D) abandons the team's stated impediment.
