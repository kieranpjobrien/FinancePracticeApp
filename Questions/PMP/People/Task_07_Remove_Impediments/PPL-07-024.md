# Question

A production outage in the operations team has pulled their support away from your project for the third time this month. Each outage costs your team several days of progress. What should the project manager do BEYOND clearing the immediate blocker?

## Options

- **A**: Escalate each production outage to executive leadership as it occurs during the project.
- **B**: Remove the operations team dependency from the project entirely so it cannot recur.
- **C**: Accept that production outages will always take priority over planned project work.
- **D**: Work with operations leadership on root-cause analysis and agree explicit fall-back arrangements.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PMI treats recurring impediments as candidates for systemic resolution and explicit agreements rather than reactive escalation or avoidance. (D) addresses both cause and impact. (A) creates escalation noise without addressing cause. (B) discards a needed capability. (C) accepts an avoidable repeating loss.
