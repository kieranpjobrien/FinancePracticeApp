# Question

In standup, a developer reports she has been blocked for two days waiting on a privileged-access credential from IT security. Tickets through the standard queue have not been actioned. What should the Scrum Master do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Reassign her to other backlog items so the sprint can continue without further delay.
- **B**: Log the impediment and raise it at the next sprint retrospective for discussion.
- **C**: Escalate to the project sponsor so they can apply pressure on IT security leadership.
- **D**: Contact the IT security team directly and work the request through to resolution.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A Scrum Master acts as a servant leader by removing impediments, especially when the standard path has failed and the team is actively blocked. (D) is direct action at the right level. (A) is a workaround that leaves the impediment in place. (B) treats an active blocker as a process improvement topic. (C) escalates before the SM has tried to resolve at their level.
