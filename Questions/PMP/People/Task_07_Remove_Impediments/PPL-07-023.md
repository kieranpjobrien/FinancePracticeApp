# Question

In Scrum, who is primarily responsible for ensuring that impediments the team cannot resolve themselves are addressed?

## Options

- **A**: The Product Owner.
- **B**: The development team members collectively.
- **C**: External stakeholders who experience the impact.
- **D**: The Scrum Master.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The Scrum Guide assigns the Scrum Master accountability for the team's effectiveness, including impediment removal. (D) is correct. (A) owns value and the backlog. (B) handles many impediments but is not the role accountable for ensuring resolution. (C) experiences impact but is outside the team.
