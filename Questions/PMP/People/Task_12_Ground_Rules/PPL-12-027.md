# Question

Which of the following is the BEST example of an effective ground rule?

## Options

- **A**: Team members will always do their best work.
- **B**: Team members will demonstrate professionalism in all interactions.
- **C**: Team members will be supportive collaborators who put the team first.
- **D**: Team members will acknowledge messages within four hours and respond within one business day.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Effective ground rules are observable and measurable. (D) defines a behaviour anyone can confirm or refute, making it easy to honour and to discuss when missed. (A), (B), and (C) are aspirations rather than rules; they cannot be consistently interpreted, applied, or reinforced.
