# Question

Team ground rules should focus primarily on which of the following?

## Options

- **A**: Coding standards and quality gates that govern technical deliverables.
- **B**: How team members behave and interact with each other.
- **C**: Project scope, deliverables, and acceptance criteria for the work.
- **D**: Budget and resource allocation across the project's phases.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Ground rules govern team behaviour and interaction; that is their distinctive purpose. (B) is correct. (A) belongs in the Definition of Done or coding standards. (C) is captured in the scope baseline and acceptance criteria. (D) is managed through the cost and resource management plans, not team norms.
