# Question

The team's ground rules include core hours of 10am-3pm during which everyone should be available. One member regularly misses meetings during this window. The project manager has not previously discussed it with them. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Reiterate the rule in the next team meeting so all members are reminded.
- **B**: Speak with the team member privately to understand what is happening and look for an accommodation.
- **C**: Adjust the core hours so they better suit the most common availability of the team.
- **D**: Note the absences in the team's working agreement as a known exception.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PMI emphasises understanding before action and respecting individual context. (B) uncovers whether there is a legitimate constraint (medical, caring responsibilities, time-zone issue) and lets the project manager craft a solution that respects both the rule's intent and the team member's situation. (A) calls out a person publicly without facts. (C) changes the rule for the whole team based on one unexplored case. (D) bakes in an exception without ever discussing it with the person involved.
