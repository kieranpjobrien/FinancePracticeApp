# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of establishing team ground rules?

## Options

- **A**: To document team norms for compliance with HR and governance requirements.
- **B**: To create a shared understanding of how the team will work together.
- **C**: To give the project manager a basis for performance discussions later on.
- **D**: To formalise the team's reporting structure and escalation paths.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Ground rules exist to make implicit expectations explicit so the team can collaborate predictably and resolve conflict more easily. (B) captures this. (A) is a possible by-product, not the purpose. (C) frames ground rules as a disciplinary tool, which contradicts the collaborative intent. (D) confuses ground rules with the project's organisational chart and communications plan.
