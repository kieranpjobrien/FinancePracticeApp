# Question

A project manager is helping a newly formed team draft ground rules. Which of the following would be the MOST effective ground rule to include?

## Options

- **A**: Team members will treat each other with mutual respect at all times.
- **B**: Team members will reply to direct messages within four working hours.
- **C**: Team members will be supportive of each other's contributions.
- **D**: Team members will avoid bringing personal issues into the workplace.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Effective ground rules are specific, observable, and measurable so the team can tell whether they are being honoured. (B) defines a concrete behaviour with a measurable threshold. (A) and (C) express values rather than rules; "respect" and "supportive" mean different things to different people and cannot be objectively assessed. (D) is well-intentioned but vague and potentially unrealistic, since personal context inevitably affects work.
