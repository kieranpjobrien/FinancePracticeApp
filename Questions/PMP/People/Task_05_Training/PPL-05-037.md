# Question

An approved scope change adds work using a streaming data technology no one on the team has used. The CCB approved the change with no schedule extension. What should the project manager do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Submit a follow-up change request to extend the schedule on the grounds that the team needs full training before starting.
- **B**: Identify the minimum viable training, bring in an experienced contractor to pair with the team, and document the residual capability risk for stakeholders.
- **C**: Direct the team to start the work immediately and acquire skills as issues arise.
- **D**: Recommend the CCB reverse the change because the team is not currently equipped to deliver it.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PMI expects the PM to deliver against an approved baseline by combining capability-building with risk transparency. (B) blends quick targeted training, expert support, and explicit risk communication. (A) immediately reopens an approved decision before lower-impact options are tried. (C) ignores the capability gap and is likely to produce rework or quality issues. (D) escalates back to the CCB without first attempting to solve the problem.
