# Question

When should training needs be identified for a project?

## Options

- **A**: During planning, when required capabilities are compared against the team's current skills.
- **B**: At the first sprint review, after the team has tried the work and surfaced the gaps.
- **C**: During lessons learned at project closure, so future projects benefit.
- **D**: Whenever a team member fails to deliver against an assigned task.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Training needs are part of resource planning and should be identified during planning, when there is time to act, which is what (A) describes. (B) waits until the gap has already cost time. (C) helps future projects but does not help this one. (D) is reactive and treats training as a personal failure response rather than a planned investment.
