# Question

What does a training needs assessment primarily identify?

## Options

- **A**: The training providers and vendors that should deliver each course.
- **B**: The team members whose roles are at risk if they are not retrained.
- **C**: The cost of internal versus external training delivery options.
- **D**: The gap between current capabilities and the capabilities required for project success.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A needs assessment is fundamentally a gap analysis between current and required capabilities, which is what (D) describes. (A) is a procurement step that follows the assessment. (B) misuses an assessment for risk management of individuals rather than for development planning. (C) is a delivery decision that comes after the gap is understood.
