# Question

Why is it important to evaluate the effectiveness of training delivered to the project team?

## Options

- **A**: To rank participants against each other for performance review purposes.
- **B**: To confirm that intended skills were learned and applied, and to improve future training.
- **C**: To prove that the contracted training vendor delivered the agreed scope.
- **D**: To justify the line item in next year's training budget submission.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PMI views evaluation as part of the plan-do-check-act loop for capability development; the goal is verified outcomes and continuous improvement. (B) covers both the immediate question (did it work?) and the feedback loop. (A) misuses evaluation for ranking. (C) is a contract management activity, not a learning evaluation. (D) is a budgetary by-product, not the purpose.
