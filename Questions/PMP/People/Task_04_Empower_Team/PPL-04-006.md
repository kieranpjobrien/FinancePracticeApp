# Question

An empowered team has chosen a database technology the PM thinks is suboptimal but not wrong. The team has documented the alternatives they considered and the rationale. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Override the choice and direct the team to use the option the PM prefers.
- **B**: Accept the decision and trust the team's judgment within their domain.
- **C**: Ask the team to repeat the analysis with additional criteria the PM specifies.
- **D**: Log the PM's preference as a dissent in the decision record.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Empowerment means letting the team decide even when the PM would have chosen differently, provided the decision is sound, which is what (B) honours. (A) destroys delegated authority and tells the team that empowerment is conditional on agreement. (C) is fake empowerment dressed up as governance and signals the team's analysis is not trusted. (D) is passive-aggressive and sets up an "I told you so" if anything goes wrong, which damages psychological safety.
