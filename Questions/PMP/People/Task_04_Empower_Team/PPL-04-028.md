# Question

The organisation has a strong command-and-control culture in which senior leadership expects to approve material decisions. The PM wants to empower the team. What approach should the PM take?

## Options

- **A**: Wait for an organisation-wide cultural change before introducing any empowerment to the team.
- **B**: Empower the team fully on every decision type and absorb any pushback from senior leadership.
- **C**: Identify decisions that do not need approval, empower the team there, and advocate upward over time.
- **D**: Brief leadership that the team will now make all delivery decisions and ask them to back the change.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The right move is to find empowerment space inside current constraints and build the case for more over time, which is what (C) does. (A) defers change indefinitely and accepts the status quo. (B) creates avoidable career and project risk and will likely cause leadership to clamp down further. (D) frames a change leadership has not agreed to as a fait accompli, which usually backfires.
