# Question

The product owner on an agile programme tells the project manager they feel they have no real say: every backlog priority they propose is overturned by a steering group that meets fortnightly, and developers have learned to wait for the steering group rather than follow the product owner. What should the PM do first?

## Options

- **A**: Facilitate an agreement with the steering group that day-to-day backlog ordering sits with the product owner, and that the group sets strategic direction and constraints rather than re-ordering individual items.
- **B**: Arrange a standing seat for the product owner in the steering group so they are in the room and can speak to each priority when it is discussed.
- **C**: Establish a fast escalation path so that when the steering group wants to change an ordering, it goes to the product owner to action rather than around them to the developers.
- **D**: Propose a time-boxed trial in which the steering group holds its reordering for two sprints to see whether the product owner's backlog delivers acceptable outcomes.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The root cause is a misplaced boundary: a fortnightly governance body is making tactical, item-level ordering calls that belong to the product owner, and the developers have rationally followed the real authority. (A) corrects that boundary directly, leaving strategy and constraints with the steering group and item ordering with the product owner, which is what makes the role real rather than nominal. (B) puts the product owner in the room but, without changing what the group decides, they remain one voice the group can still overrule item by item. (C) improves the routing so changes flow through the product owner, but the group is still the one deciding the ordering, so the authority has not actually moved. (D) tests the product owner's judgement but frames their authority as provisional and reversible after two sprints, leaving the underlying boundary unresolved. (A) is best because it relocates the decision right itself, which is the structural fix the others only soften.
