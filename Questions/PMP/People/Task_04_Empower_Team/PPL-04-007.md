# Question

A team has been empowered to choose its own technology stack and selects an emerging open-source framework. IT governance flags concerns about long-term support and security review. What should the project manager do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Direct the team to choose an approved framework so that all governance concerns are immediately removed.
- **B**: Defend the team's delegated authority and proceed with the chosen framework as originally planned.
- **C**: Bring the team and IT governance together to assess the actual risks and agree mitigations.
- **D**: Escalate the conflict to the project sponsor to decide between empowerment and corporate governance rules.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Empowerment operates inside organisational guardrails, and the PM's role is to broker a workable answer between the team and governance, which is what (C) does. (A) revokes the empowerment without exploring the actual risk. (B) ignores legitimate organisational concerns. (D) escalates a question that should be resolved at the working level first.
