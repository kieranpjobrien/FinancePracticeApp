# Question

A new PM has inherited a team that the previous PM ran in a hands-on way, making most decisions personally. The new PM wants to introduce more empowerment. What is the BEST approach?

## Options

- **A**: Announce in the first team meeting that decision-making now belongs to the team and ask members to escalate only true blockers.
- **B**: Continue the previous approach for the first six months to avoid disrupting delivery.
- **C**: Replace the team members who appear least comfortable with making decisions.
- **D**: Explain the change, agree decision boundaries with the team, start with low-risk decisions, and expand authority as confidence grows.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Cultural change requires explanation, agreed boundaries, and incremental delegation, which is what (D) does. (A) sets expectation without giving the team the structure or safety to act on it. (B) preserves the dependency indefinitely. (C) punishes the team for adapting to the previous regime and breaks team continuity.
