# Question

Which component of emotional intelligence describes the ability to understand the emotions of others?

## Options

- **A**: Self-awareness
- **B**: Self-regulation
- **C**: Empathy
- **D**: Motivation

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Goleman's framework places empathy as the component focused on perceiving and understanding others' emotions. (C) is correct. (A) refers to awareness of one's own emotions. (B) is the ability to manage one's own emotional reactions. (D) is internal drive and persistence; it relates to self, not others.
