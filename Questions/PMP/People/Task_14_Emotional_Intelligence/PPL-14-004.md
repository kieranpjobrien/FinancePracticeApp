# Question

During a project crisis, the project manager notices they are feeling overwhelmed and starting to give snappy answers to team questions. What is the BEST emotionally intelligent response?

## Options

- **A**: Push through; the team needs to see steady leadership during a crisis.
- **B**: Briefly acknowledge their state, take a short reset, and return with better composure.
- **C**: Tell the team they are stressed and ask them to be patient for a while.
- **D**: Hand all team interactions to a deputy until the crisis passes.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Self-awareness without self-regulation is incomplete. (B) does both: notice the state, manage it, and return rather than withdraw. (A) ignores the warning sign and risks doing damage. (C) shifts the burden onto the team to accommodate the project manager. (D) removes leadership presence at exactly the moment the team needs it.
