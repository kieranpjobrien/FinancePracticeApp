# Question

The project is behind schedule and the sponsor is visibly angry in a status meeting. The project manager feels defensive and wants to point to external causes for the delay. What is the emotionally intelligent response?

## Options

- **A**: Lay out the external factors that caused the slip so the sponsor sees the team is not at fault.
- **B**: Stay silent until the sponsor has finished venting, then continue with the status update.
- **C**: Identify the team members responsible for the missed milestones so accountability is clear.
- **D**: Acknowledge the sponsor's frustration, take ownership at the project level, and pivot to the recovery path.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The emotionally intelligent move is to manage the defensive impulse, validate the sponsor's emotion, and pivot from blame to solution. (D) does this. (A) reads as excuse-making in the moment. (B) is passive and may inflame the sponsor's anger. (C) damages team trust and rarely satisfies a sponsor who wants direction, not names.
