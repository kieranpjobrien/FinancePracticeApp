# Question

In a power/interest grid, what is the recommended engagement approach for stakeholders with high power but low interest?

## Options

- **A**: Manage closely with frequent, detailed engagement.
- **B**: Monitor with minimal effort and only respond if they reach out.
- **C**: Ignore until they actively raise an objection.
- **D**: Keep satisfied with concise updates that respect their limited bandwidth.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The classic Mendelow recommendation for high-power/low-interest stakeholders is "keep satisfied" — they can damage the project if dissatisfied but do not want heavy involvement. (A) is the prescription for high-power/high-interest, not this quadrant. (B) is the approach for low-power/low-interest stakeholders. (C) leaves the project exposed to a disruptive intervention if they later become concerned.
