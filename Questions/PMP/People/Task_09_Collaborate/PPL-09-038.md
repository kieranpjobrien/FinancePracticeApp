# Question

Which agile practice exists primarily to give stakeholders regular feedback opportunities on working product?

## Options

- **A**: Sprint planning
- **B**: Daily stand-up
- **C**: Sprint retrospective
- **D**: Sprint review (demo)

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The sprint review is the dedicated event in Scrum where the team shows the increment to stakeholders and collects feedback to inform the next sprint's plan. (A) is for the team to plan the upcoming sprint; stakeholders may attend but it is not feedback-focused. (B) is for the development team's daily synchronisation. (C) is internal to the team and focused on process improvement.
