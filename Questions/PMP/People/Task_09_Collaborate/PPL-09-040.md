# Question

A new project sponsor has replaced the original sponsor mid-project. The new sponsor is challenging several already-approved decisions. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Defend the original decisions and ask the new sponsor to respect the prior governance record.
- **B**: Brief the new sponsor on context, understand her priorities, and route any changes through governance.
- **C**: Re-run all the original decisions to give the new sponsor full ownership of each one going forward.
- **D**: Treat the questioning as inappropriate interference and raise it with the steering committee for a ruling.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PMI handles sponsor transitions by combining context-sharing with respect for the new sponsor's authority and the governance process for any revisions. (A) is positional and dismisses legitimate sponsor authority. (C) creates instability and effectively re-baselines the entire project unnecessarily. (D) frames appropriate sponsor oversight as interference and damages the relationship from day one.
