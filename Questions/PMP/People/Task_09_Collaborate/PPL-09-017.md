# Question

What is the primary purpose of the stakeholder register?

## Options

- **A**: To capture stakeholders' identification, interests, influence, and planned engagement approach.
- **B**: To list every email the PM has exchanged with each stakeholder.
- **C**: To rank stakeholders strictly by seniority for invitation purposes.
- **D**: To record only contact details so that distribution lists can be generated.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PMI defines the stakeholder register as the central record of who stakeholders are, what they care about, what influence they hold, and how the PM intends to engage them — exactly what (A) describes. (B) is a communication log, not the register. (C) reduces a multi-dimensional analysis to seniority alone. (D) describes a contact list, which is a small subset of the register's content.
