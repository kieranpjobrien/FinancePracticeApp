# Question

The project will eliminate roles in two departments. Employees in those departments are project stakeholders whose cooperation is needed during the transition. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Withhold the workforce impact information until after the project goes live to maintain cooperation.
- **B**: Disclose the impact to all affected employees immediately at the next project meeting without HR.
- **C**: Refuse to continue with the project on ethical grounds given the workforce impact.
- **D**: Work with HR and leadership on a coordinated communication plan that handles timing and support.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Workforce-impact communication has legal, HR, and ethical dimensions that must be coordinated; PMI's compliance and ethics principles require the PM to work within that framework, not unilaterally. (A) is deception that breaches the PMI Code of Ethics and shatters trust when discovered. (B) bypasses the legal and HR processes that exist to protect both employees and the organisation. (C) is unrealistic in most organisational settings and ignores that ethical handling is possible.
