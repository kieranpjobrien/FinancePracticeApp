# Question

Which communication principle is MOST important for managing a virtual team effectively?

## Options

- **A**: Restricting written communication to formal channels so messages are clearly attributed.
- **B**: Minimising scheduled meetings so people protect their focus time and respect each other's calendars.
- **C**: Deliberately over-communicating compared to a co-located team to compensate for the absence of informal contact.
- **D**: Standardising on the most advanced collaboration platform available so the team has the best tooling.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Virtual teams lose the informal contact that quietly carries information in a shared office, so deliberate over-communication — frequent updates, repeating context, sharing rationale — keeps everyone aligned. (A) starves the channels that carry tacit information. (B) reduces a key vehicle for connection and makes information gaps worse. (D) treats tooling as the strategy; the best platform poorly used still produces poor communication.
