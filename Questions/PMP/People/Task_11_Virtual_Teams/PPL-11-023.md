# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of a team charter for a virtual team?

## Options

- **A**: To capture the project's business case and the value the team is expected to deliver.
- **B**: To establish shared expectations for communication, collaboration, and decision-making.
- **C**: To document the technical architecture the team will use during the project.
- **D**: To define the individual roles and reporting lines within the project organisation.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A team charter codifies how the team will work together — meeting cadence, response times, decision rules, conflict-handling — and is especially important for virtual teams where these norms don't form by osmosis. (A) describes the project charter / business case, a different artefact at the project level. (C) describes a technical architecture document. (D) describes a RACI or organisation chart; team charters are about behaviour and norms rather than structure.
