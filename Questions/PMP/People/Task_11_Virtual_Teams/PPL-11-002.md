# Question

Which communication medium is generally MOST effective for conveying complex or sensitive information to a virtual team?

## Options

- **A**: A detailed email that gives recipients time to absorb the content at their own pace.
- **B**: A live video call that allows tone, facial cues, and immediate clarification.
- **C**: An instant-message thread that supports rapid back-and-forth across time zones.
- **D**: A shared document with comments that lets each member contribute asynchronously.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Communication richness theory — and PMI's virtual-team guidance — places live video at the top for sensitive or complex topics because it carries tone, body language, and immediate clarification. (A) email lacks tone and feedback loops; misinterpretations compound silently. (C) IM is too low-bandwidth for nuanced topics and tends to produce fragmented, easily misread exchanges. (D) shared documents are great for collaborative writing but the dialogue is async and slower than the topic warrants.
