# Question

What is the MOST important factor for successful collaboration in a virtual team?

## Options

- **A**: Trust between team members and clear, agreed communication norms.
- **B**: A short list of overlapping working hours across the team's time zones.
- **C**: Adoption of the latest collaboration platform and conferencing technology.
- **D**: A comprehensive set of project documentation maintained in a single repository.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Trust is the substrate that lets virtual collaboration work, and shared norms convert that trust into predictable behaviour. (B) is helpful but many successful global teams have minimal overlap and still collaborate well. (C) is enabling, not causal — strong tools without trust still produce dysfunction. (D) is supporting infrastructure; documentation cannot substitute for the relationships and norms that drive day-to-day collaboration.
