# Question

The project manager is mentoring a product owner who keeps asking, "What should I prioritise?" instead of working through it. The product owner has the information needed to make the call. What is the BEST mentoring response?

## Options

- **A**: Provide the answer so the team is not blocked while the product owner learns.
- **B**: Take over prioritisation for the next sprint as a worked example for the product owner.
- **C**: Decline to answer until the product owner produces their own draft prioritisation.
- **D**: Use guiding questions to help them apply a prioritisation framework themselves.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Mentoring builds the mentee's own decision-making, not their reliance on the mentor. (D) coaches through Socratic questions ("What is the value? What is the cost of delay? What is the dependency?") and reinforces a reusable framework. (A) creates dependence. (B) shows process but does not transfer the decision-making. (C) is unnecessarily withholding and can damage trust.
