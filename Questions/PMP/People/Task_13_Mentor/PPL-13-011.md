# Question

A team member asks the project manager to mentor them on their career. The project manager has relevant experience but limited time. What is the BEST response?

## Options

- **A**: Decline the request, explaining that current project demands prevent taking on a mentee.
- **B**: Suggest a colleague with similar experience who has more bandwidth right now for mentoring.
- **C**: Agree to bounded sessions, such as a monthly 30-minute conversation, and be explicit about it.
- **D**: Agree to be available whenever the team member wants to talk about their career.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Mentoring quality is more about consistency than volume. (C) commits to a sustainable cadence that respects both parties. (A) misses an opportunity that a small commitment could fulfil. (B) is reasonable but unnecessarily redirects when the project manager has the experience and could give limited time. (D) sets up false expectations and is unsustainable.
