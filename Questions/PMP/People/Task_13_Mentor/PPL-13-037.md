# Question

A mentee tells the project manager they are frustrated that peers are advancing faster than they are despite their best efforts. What is the BEST mentoring response?

## Options

- **A**: Validate that promotions in the organisation are often unfair so they do not blame themselves.
- **B**: Suggest patience and reassure them their turn will come if they keep doing good work.
- **C**: Help them clarify what advancement means to them and check whether their self-perception matches how others see them.
- **D**: Recommend they explore opportunities at other organisations where they will be more appreciated.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Frustration about advancement usually has multiple drivers, including unclear goals or perception gaps. (C) opens the conversation that surfaces them. (A) validates without helping. (B) is patronising and changes nothing. (D) jumps to exit before the mentee has explored what is actually happening.
