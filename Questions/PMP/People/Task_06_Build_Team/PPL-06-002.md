# Question

In Tuckman's model of team development, which behaviours best characterise the Storming stage?

## Options

- **A**: Members are polite, cautious, and focused on getting to know each other.
- **B**: Conflict and tension emerge as members push against roles, approaches, and informal leadership.
- **C**: The team has settled into shared norms and operates smoothly with little friction.
- **D**: The team is highly autonomous and consistently delivers at peak performance.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Tuckman's stages are Forming, Storming, Norming, Performing and Adjourning, each with distinct behavioural markers. (B) describes Storming. (A) is Forming. (C) is Norming. (D) is Performing.
