# Question

You are forming the team for a project that will significantly change how the finance organisation operates. Beyond technical skills, what composition choice is MOST likely to support adoption?

## Options

- **A**: Include respected change champions and influencers from the affected business areas alongside the delivery specialists.
- **B**: Staff the team entirely with external consultants to ensure objectivity and avoid internal politics.
- **C**: Keep the delivery team lean and technical; let the change manager handle all stakeholder concerns.
- **D**: Select only senior leaders from affected areas, so the team carries authority.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PMI links change adoption to stakeholder involvement and credible advocates inside the affected areas. (A) embeds that credibility into the team. (B) removes the insider knowledge and credibility the change actually needs. (C) separates delivery from change in a way that is known to underperform. (D) concentrates status without building grassroots trust.
