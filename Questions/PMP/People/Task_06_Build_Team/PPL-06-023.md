# Question

What is the primary purpose of a team charter or working agreement?

## Options

- **A**: To assign formal performance ratings to each team member.
- **B**: To document scope, deliverables, and the project budget at a high level.
- **C**: To capture how the team will work together, including values, communication norms, and decision-making approach.
- **D**: To define escalation paths for the steering committee.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

PMI defines a team charter as the team's own agreement on how it will operate, distinct from project scope or governance documents. (C) is the standard definition. (A) belongs to performance management. (B) belongs to the project charter or scope statement. (D) is part of the communications or governance plan.
