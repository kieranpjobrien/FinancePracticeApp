# Question

What is a RACI matrix used for?

## Options

- **A**: Tracking team velocity and throughput across delivery iterations.
- **B**: Documenting the project schedule, milestones, and cross-team dependencies.
- **C**: Recording who is Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, and Informed for each task.
- **D**: Logging risks together with their probability and overall impact ratings.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

A RACI is a responsibility assignment matrix that clarifies role expectations across a list of tasks or decisions. (C) is the standard definition. (A) is an agile metric. (B) is the schedule baseline. (D) is the risk register.
