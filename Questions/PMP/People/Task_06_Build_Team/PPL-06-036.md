# Question

According to the Scrum Guide, what is the recommended size for a Scrum team?

## Options

- **A**: Typically 10 or fewer members in total, including the Product Owner and Scrum Master.
- **B**: Exactly seven delivery members plus the Product Owner and Scrum Master.
- **C**: Between 15 and 20 members for sufficient capacity on a serious product.
- **D**: Any size, as long as the team's work in progress is bounded.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The Scrum Guide gives a rule of thumb of 10 or fewer members, inclusive of the PO and SM, because smaller teams communicate better and stay nimble. (A) is the stated guidance. (B) is more prescriptive than the guide. (C) exceeds the recommended range. (D) ignores the recommendation entirely.
