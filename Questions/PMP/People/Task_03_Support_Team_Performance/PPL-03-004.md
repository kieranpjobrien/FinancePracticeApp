# Question

A team member consistently delivers high-quality work but works in isolation, rarely participating in team discussions or helping others. The behaviour is creating resentment among colleagues who value collaboration. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Have a private conversation about the impact on the team and explore ways to collaborate.
- **B**: Accept their individual working style since their personal output is consistently excellent.
- **C**: Praise their technical excellence publicly so the rest of the team sees what good looks like.
- **D**: Pair them with the struggling team members to force more team interaction.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

(A) is the PMI feedback pattern: private, curious about the person's perspective, explicit about impact, collaborative on solutions. (B) ignores the team dynamic damaging overall performance. (C) deepens resentment by praising someone the team sees as non-collaborative. (D) forces interaction without buy-in and doesn't help either party.
