# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of the sprint retrospective?

## Options

- **A**: Inspect how the team worked together and agree on improvements for the next sprint.
- **B**: Demonstrate the increment of work completed during the sprint to stakeholders.
- **C**: Plan the user stories the team will commit to in the upcoming sprint.
- **D**: Update the project status report and burndown chart for the steering committee.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The retrospective is the Scrum event dedicated to inspecting the team's process, relationships, and tools, and committing to improvements for the next sprint. (B) describes the sprint review, where the increment is demonstrated to stakeholders. (C) describes sprint planning. (D) is a reporting activity and is not a Scrum event at all.
