# Question

A team's burndown shows steady completion but every sprint they leave one or two committed stories unfinished. What should the Scrum Master investigate FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Whether individual team members are underperforming on their assigned stories.
- **B**: Whether the product owner is adding unplanned work mid-sprint to the backlog.
- **C**: Whether team members simply aren't working hard enough during the sprint.
- **D**: Whether sprint planning is producing accurate estimates and appropriate commitment.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The pattern (consistently 1-2 stories over) points to systematic overcommitment, not individual or random issues — so look at the planning process and the velocity used to size the sprint. (A) and (C) blame individuals when the pattern is systemic. (B) would show as scope additions on the burndown, not steady completion with overflow.
