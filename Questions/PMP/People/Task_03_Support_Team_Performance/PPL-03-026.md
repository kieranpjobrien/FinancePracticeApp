# Question

A team member has missed three deliverable dates in the last two sprints. The PM had a brief informal conversation about it last sprint but the pattern is continuing. What should the PM do NEXT?

## Options

- **A**: Reassign the at-risk work to other team members so the sprint is not jeopardised.
- **B**: Hold a structured one-on-one to explore root causes, agree clear expectations and support, and document the conversation.
- **C**: Raise the missed dates with HR so a formal performance improvement process can begin.
- **D**: Wait one more sprint before acting, because performance issues need a longer pattern to be fair.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Performance management is progressive. The first informal conversation has not produced change, so the next step is a structured, documented coaching conversation that explores cause, agrees expectations, and offers support. (A) protects the sprint but takes no action on the underlying performance issue. (C) skips the PM's own coaching responsibility and jumps to HR before formal coaching has been attempted. (D) ignores a clear pattern and lets the impact on the team continue.
