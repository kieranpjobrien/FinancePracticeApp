# Question

Your agile team is pushing back on the compliance department's request for formal upfront design documents, arguing they conflict with the team's iterative way of working. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Defend the team's agile practice and ask compliance to accept running artifacts as evidence.
- **B**: Produce the requested documents in parallel even if the team does not use them operationally.
- **C**: Meet with compliance to understand the underlying need and agree artifacts that satisfy it within the agile cadence.
- **D**: Escalate to the PMO leadership to secure an exemption from the compliance requirement.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Most apparent agile-versus-compliance conflicts dissolve once the underlying compliance need is clear (C): traceability, approvals, or audit trail can usually be met with tailored artifacts. (A) dismisses a legitimate stakeholder. (B) creates documentation theatre. (D) escalates before genuine dialogue has happened.
