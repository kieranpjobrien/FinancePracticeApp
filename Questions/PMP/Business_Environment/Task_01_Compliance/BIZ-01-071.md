# Question

Public sector UAT reveals that the digital service fails six WCAG 2.1 AA criteria. The go-live is contractually required in three weeks and is mandated to be accessibility-compliant. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Go live on the contracted date and add the accessibility fixes as a prioritised post-launch workstream.
- **B**: Notify the accountable sponsor and contracting authority, size remediation, and propose a compliant launch path.
- **C**: Launch with an accessibility statement disclosing the six failing criteria and a public remediation timeline.
- **D**: Request a contractual variation that relaxes the accessibility criteria to the level currently achievable.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A mandated accessibility bar cannot be met by disclosure or relaxation; the PM surfaces the gap and proposes a compliant path (B). (A) breaches a contractual and regulatory obligation. (C) disclosure does not cure non-compliance in a mandated contract. (D) asks to lower the bar instead of meeting it.
