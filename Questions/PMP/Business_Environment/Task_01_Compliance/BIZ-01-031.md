# Question

What is the primary purpose of a compliance register on a project?

## Options

- **A**: To list every law and regulation that exists in the project's operating jurisdiction.
- **B**: To track applicable requirements, the controls that satisfy them, and evidence of conformance.
- **C**: To document fines and penalties that have been levied against similar projects in the past.
- **D**: To capture stakeholder concerns about ethical issues raised during project execution.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A compliance register is a working artefact that ties applicable obligations to the controls implemented and the evidence collected, supporting audits and decisions (B). (A) is too broad and not actionable; only applicable requirements matter. (C) is historical context, not project compliance management. (D) describes an issue log or stakeholder register, not compliance.
