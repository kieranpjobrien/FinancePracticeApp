# Question

You discover that a senior team member has been skipping the mandated security review checkpoint to keep up with sprint goals. No incidents have surfaced yet. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Acknowledge the speed gain and quietly tighten supervision going forward.
- **B**: Capture the shortcut as a process improvement candidate for the next retrospective.
- **C**: Continue current practice while the security team formally reassesses the checkpoint.
- **D**: Stop the practice, audit the affected work, and remediate any compliance gaps.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A bypassed control means compliance status of past work is unknown (D): stop the bypass, assess what was missed, remediate. (A) implicitly endorses the shortcut. (B) treats a control violation as innovation. (C) lets the violation persist indefinitely while waiting on someone else.
