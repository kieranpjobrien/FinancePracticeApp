# Question

A contractor team will join the project for a three-month engagement. Their work will touch regulated systems. What should the PM include in the onboarding plan?

## Options

- **A**: A brief written acknowledgement that they will follow company policies.
- **B**: The same compliance training and access controls applied to internal staff.
- **C**: A condensed version of compliance training tailored to a short engagement.
- **D**: Verbal briefings from the team lead on the most relevant policies.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Anyone touching regulated systems must meet the same standard regardless of employment status (B). (A) is signature without substance. (C) lowers the bar based on tenure rather than risk. (D) is informal and undocumented.
