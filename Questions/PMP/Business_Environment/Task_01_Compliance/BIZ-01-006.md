# Question

What does project compliance management primarily ensure?

## Options

- **A**: That the project finishes within the agreed schedule baseline.
- **B**: That deliverables conform to applicable laws, regulations, standards, and policies.
- **C**: That all stakeholders rate the project highly in the satisfaction survey.
- **D**: That total spend stays within the approved cost baseline.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Compliance management is about meeting external rules (laws, regulations, standards) and internal policies. (A) is schedule management. (C) is stakeholder management. (D) is cost management. All four matter, but only (B) is the definition of compliance.
