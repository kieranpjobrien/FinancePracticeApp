# Question

Who typically has primary responsibility for defining the compliance requirements that apply to a project?

## Options

- **A**: The project sponsor, since they own benefits realisation.
- **B**: The legal, compliance, and regulatory functions together with relevant SMEs.
- **C**: The delivery team, because they understand the work in detail.
- **D**: The customer, through clauses in the master services agreement.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Compliance requirements come from those who specialise in the rules and the risks (B). (A) the sponsor is accountable but rarely has the technical compliance expertise. (C) the team implements compliance but does not define it. (D) customer contracts add obligations but are only one input among many.
