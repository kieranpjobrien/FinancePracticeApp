# Question

According to the PMI Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct, what should a PM do when asked to act in a way that violates a regulation?

## Options

- **A**: Comply if instructed by a senior leader and document the instruction.
- **B**: Comply but raise the matter at the next governance meeting.
- **C**: Refuse to act and report the request through appropriate channels.
- **D**: Negotiate a partial action that minimises the violation.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The Code of Ethics requires honesty, responsibility, and respect; the PM refuses unlawful instructions and reports them through appropriate channels (C). (A), (B), and (D) all involve some level of compliance with the unlawful instruction, which the Code does not permit.
