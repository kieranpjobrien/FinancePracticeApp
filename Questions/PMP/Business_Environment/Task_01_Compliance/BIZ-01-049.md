# Question

Who is ultimately accountable for compliance on a project?

## Options

- **A**: The compliance officer, because they have specialist authority over compliance matters.
- **B**: The project sponsor, as the owner of the project and its outcomes.
- **C**: The project manager, who manages day-to-day delivery of the work.
- **D**: The auditor, who certifies whether the project complied with requirements.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Accountability for the project, including compliance with applicable obligations, sits with the sponsor (B). The compliance officer (A) advises and assesses but does not own delivery. The PM (C) is responsible for managing compliance as part of execution but accountability flows up. The auditor (D) is independent and provides assurance.
