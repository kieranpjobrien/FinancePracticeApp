# Question

Which sequence correctly orders compliance sources from most to least binding for a typical commercial project?

## Options

- **A**: Industry standards, internal policies, regulations, contracts.
- **B**: Internal policies, contracts, regulations, industry standards.
- **C**: Regulations, contracts, internal policies, industry standards.
- **D**: Contracts, regulations, industry standards, internal policies.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Regulations carry legal force, contracts bind the parties, internal policies bind employees, and industry standards are voluntary unless adopted (C). (A), (B), and (D) place voluntary or organisational obligations above legal ones, which is incorrect.
