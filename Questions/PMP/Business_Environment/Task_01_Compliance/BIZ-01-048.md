# Question

The records retention policy requires project documents to be archived for seven years. Your project will close in three months and the team is using a shared drive scheduled for decommissioning next year. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Print key documents and store them in the project sponsor's office.
- **B**: Identify the records management system, transfer documents before close, and confirm retention is configured.
- **C**: Compress the shared drive into a single archive and email it to the team.
- **D**: Extend the shared drive lifetime by negotiating with IT.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Project documents need to live in the sanctioned records system with the retention configured (B). (A) is informal and unrecoverable. (C) creates a fragile copy in inboxes that violates records governance. (D) postpones the problem by one year without solving it.
