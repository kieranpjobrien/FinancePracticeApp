# Question

Which statement best describes the consequences of shipping a deliverable that fails mandatory compliance requirements?

## Options

- **A**: Minor inconvenience that can be fixed quietly after deployment.
- **B**: Regulatory penalties, legal exposure, reputational harm, and potentially unusable deliverables.
- **C**: No material consequence provided all functional requirements have been met.
- **D**: Impact confined to the project manager's performance review and bonus.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Non-compliance routinely produces several of the outcomes in (B) at once, including loss of the right to operate or sell. (A) downplays outcomes that can be irreversible. (C) incorrectly separates function from the right to use. (D) limits consequences to one person when they hit the whole organisation.
