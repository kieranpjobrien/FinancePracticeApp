# Question

Which of the following items is most clearly a compliance requirement rather than a constraint or preference?

## Options

- **A**: The sponsor wants the new platform live by the end of the financial year.
- **B**: The development team prefers to work in two-week Scrum sprints.
- **C**: Financial transaction records must be retained for seven years per the regulator.
- **D**: Stakeholders have asked for weekly status reports in a particular template.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

A statutory retention period (C) is a non-negotiable rule imposed by an external authority — the definition of compliance. (A) is a business deadline, (B) a methodology preference, and (D) a communication preference. All can shape the plan, but only (C) carries legal force.
