# Question

An external regulator issues a surprise audit notice for your fintech project with a 10-day response window. The compliance evidence repository is 60% complete and scattered across tools. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Stand up a rapid triage team with compliance to locate, validate, and consolidate artefacts by priority.
- **B**: Request a regulator extension citing the team's current competing priorities and complex multi-tool evidence landscape.
- **C**: Recreate any missing evidence retroactively so the repository appears complete and well-organised before submission.
- **D**: Submit whatever is currently available and note the gaps in a covering letter to the regulator.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The PM mobilises a triaged response to meet the deadline with authentic evidence (A). (B) seeks an extension before making genuine effort, which signals poor control. (C) is evidence fabrication, a serious compliance and ethical breach. (D) under-delivers when the PM has not yet tried to consolidate what exists.
