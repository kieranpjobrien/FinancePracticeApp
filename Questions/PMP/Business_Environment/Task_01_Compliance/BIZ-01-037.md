# Question

A new team member joins a project subject to strict export control rules. The other team members completed compliance training during onboarding six months ago. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Direct the new member to the team's shared drive folder containing the training materials.
- **B**: Wait until the next scheduled team training cycle to enrol the new member.
- **C**: Pair the new member with an existing team member who can teach the rules informally.
- **D**: Schedule formal export control training and verify completion before granting access to controlled data.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Regulated training must be completed and documented before controlled access is granted (D). (A) provides materials without verification of completion. (C) is informal knowledge transfer with no audit trail. (B) creates a window of non-compliance during which the new member could access controlled data.
