# Question

A digital product launched four months ago is meeting all its KPIs, but the operations team has not been resourced to maintain it. Decay is likely without intervention. What should the PM raise?

## Options

- **A**: That the project should be relaunched as a new initiative to fund operations.
- **B**: That benefits sustainment requires explicit funding and ownership in operations.
- **C**: That marketing should drive more usage to justify the investment.
- **D**: That the team should accept that benefits will fade over time.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Benefits sustainment is part of value delivery and must be funded in operations (B). (A) creates an artificial new project. (C) addresses demand, not the operational decay. (D) accepts loss of value that planning could prevent.
