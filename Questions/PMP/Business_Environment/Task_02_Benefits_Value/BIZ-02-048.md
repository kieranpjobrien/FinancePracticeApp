# Question

The business case promises "improved customer experience" without quantifying it. The sponsor asks the PM to start delivery. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Begin delivery immediately and define the benefit measures during execution.
- **B**: Decline the engagement outright until the sponsor rewrites the business case.
- **C**: Work with the sponsor to agree one or two measurable proxies before starting.
- **D**: Adopt an industry-standard customer experience metric without consulting the sponsor first.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

A measurable definition has to exist before delivery commits resources (C); the PM facilitates that definition with the sponsor. (A) starts work that cannot be assessed against a benefit. (B) is overreach. (D) imposes a measure the sponsor has not agreed to.
