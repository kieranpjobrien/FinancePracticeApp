# Question

At what point should the benefits register first be created?

## Options

- **A**: At project closure as part of handover documentation.
- **B**: During execution once benefits start to be observable.
- **C**: After the first major deliverable is released to production.
- **D**: During initiation, derived from the business case, and refined throughout.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Benefits are identified at initiation and refined as the project progresses, with the register acting as the living artefact (D). (A) is far too late to influence anything. (B) and (C) start tracking after the project has already shaped the design that will or will not deliver value.
