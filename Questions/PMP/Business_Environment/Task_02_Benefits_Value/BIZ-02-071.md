# Question

A public sector digital service's primary benefit is citizen wait-time reduction, not financial return. Treasury is pressuring you to restate the benefit in dollars to compete with other budget proposals. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Restate the entire benefit in dollars using any reasonable conversion so the project competes for funding.
- **B**: Refuse to restate and submit the proposal with wait-time reduction as the sole benefit described.
- **C**: Present both metrics: wait-time as the primary benefit and a defensible dollar equivalence for comparability.
- **D**: Withdraw the proposal and resubmit only when treasury agrees to assess non-financial benefits fairly.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Public sector benefits often need both the true outcome and a defensible monetisation for comparability (C). (A) loses the real benefit in a dollar number that hides what is actually delivered. (B) wins principle but may lose funding that delivers the benefit. (D) withdraws rather than adapts to the funding process.
