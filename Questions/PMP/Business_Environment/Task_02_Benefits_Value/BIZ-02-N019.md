# Question

On a programme managing benefits realisation across delivery and operations, responsibilities must be assigned clearly so nothing falls between roles. Match each responsibility to the role that most appropriately owns it.

## Items

- **1**: Deciding the order of backlog items to maximise value within an increment
- **2**: Tracking and reporting whether projected benefits are realised after handover
- **3**: Confirming the programme's benefits remain aligned to organisational strategy and authorising continued funding

## Matches

- **A**: Benefits owner
- **B**: Product owner
- **C**: Steering committee / sponsor

## Answer

1-B, 2-A, 3-C

## Explanation

The product owner orders the backlog to maximise value within delivery (1-B). The benefits owner tracks and reports realisation after handover, when the work has moved to operations (2-A). The steering committee or sponsor holds strategic alignment and the funding decision (3-C). Each pairing reflects the level at which the decision genuinely sits: delivery sequencing, realisation tracking, and strategic authorisation respectively.
