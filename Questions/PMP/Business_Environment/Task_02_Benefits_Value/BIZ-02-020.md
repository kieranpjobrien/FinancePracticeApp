# Question

At 50% complete, two new competitors have entered the market and revenue projections in the original business case look optimistic. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Continue executing because the project was already approved.
- **B**: Reduce scope unilaterally to bring projected ROI back into line.
- **C**: Refresh the business case with current market data and present continue/modify/cancel options to stakeholders.
- **D**: Accelerate delivery to outpace the new competitors before further erosion occurs.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

A material change to the business environment requires a fresh decision based on a refreshed case (C). (A) ignores the new context. (B) cuts scope without understanding which scope creates the value. (D) treats speed as the answer without checking whether the value is still there.
