# Question

What does the term "payback period" describe?

## Options

- **A**: The total profit a project generates over its lifetime.
- **B**: The interest rate at which the project's NPV is zero.
- **C**: The time required to recover the initial investment from project cash flows.
- **D**: The percentage return on each pound invested in the project.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Payback period is the time to recoup the initial outlay (C). (A) is total profit. (B) is internal rate of return (IRR). (D) is ROI.
