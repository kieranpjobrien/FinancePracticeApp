# Question

What is the difference between a project output and a project outcome?

## Options

- **A**: An output is delivered by the team; an outcome is delivered by the sponsor.
- **B**: An output is the deliverable; an outcome is the change or benefit it enables.
- **C**: An output is intangible; an outcome is tangible.
- **D**: An output is measured in cost; an outcome is measured in time.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Outputs are what the project produces; outcomes are the resulting change in the world (B). (A), (C), and (D) describe other distinctions that are not the output/outcome relationship.
