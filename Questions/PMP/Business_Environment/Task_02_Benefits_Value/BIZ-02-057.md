# Question

Which of these is the best definition of "value" in PMI terms?

## Options

- **A**: The amount of money a project saves the organisation.
- **B**: The output of the project measured against the budget baseline.
- **C**: The total scope delivered against the original plan.
- **D**: The worth, importance, or usefulness of something to a stakeholder.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PMI defines value broadly: worth or usefulness to stakeholders, which may be financial, social, or strategic (D). (A) reduces value to cost savings. (B) and (C) are delivery measures, not value.
