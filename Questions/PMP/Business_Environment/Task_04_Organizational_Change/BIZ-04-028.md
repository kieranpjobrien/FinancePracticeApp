# Question

What does "change readiness assessment" evaluate?

## Options

- **A**: Whether the organisation and its people are prepared to adopt and sustain the change.
- **B**: Whether the technical system has completed pre-production testing and is ready to deploy.
- **C**: Whether the project's approved budget is sufficient for remaining work.
- **D**: Whether the schedule baseline is still achievable given current progress.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Change readiness focuses on people, leadership, culture, capability, and communication (A). (B) is technical readiness. (C) is a budget review. (D) is a schedule review.
