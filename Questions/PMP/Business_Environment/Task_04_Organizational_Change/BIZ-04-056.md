# Question

Lewin's three-stage change model is best summarised as:

## Options

- **A**: Plan, Do, Check, Act.
- **B**: Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, Ability, Reinforcement.
- **C**: Form, Storm, Norm, Perform.
- **D**: Unfreeze, Change, Refreeze.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Lewin's model is Unfreeze, Change, Refreeze (D). (A) is Deming's PDCA cycle. (B) is ADKAR. (C) is Tuckman's group development.
