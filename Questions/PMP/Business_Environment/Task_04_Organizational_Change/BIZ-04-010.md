# Question

The department head supports a workflow change you are introducing, but middle managers look sceptical in joint meetings. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Leave the situation to the department head to manage with their direct reports.
- **B**: Communicate directly with line staff to ensure they get the message clearly.
- **C**: Engage the middle managers individually, surface their concerns, and bring them into implementation planning.
- **D**: Ask the department head to replace the most sceptical managers with change champions.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Middle managers carry the change every day; their concerns must be understood and addressed (C). (A) abdicates an active OCM role. (B) creates conflict by going around the managers. (D) is heavy-handed and treats symptoms not causes.
