# Question

The first step in Kotter's eight-step change model is:

## Options

- **A**: Form a powerful guiding coalition.
- **B**: Communicate the change vision.
- **C**: Establish a sense of urgency.
- **D**: Anchor changes in the culture.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Kotter's model begins by establishing urgency (C). (A) is the second step. (B) is later. (D) is the final step.
