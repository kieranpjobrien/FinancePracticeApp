# Question

Six weeks before go-live, a survey shows 70% of affected staff still cannot articulate why the change is happening. Training is already scheduled. ADKAR's first stage is Awareness. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Re-run targeted awareness communications from credible leaders before proceeding into the planned training schedule.
- **B**: Proceed with training on the current schedule because awareness tends to build naturally through training participation.
- **C**: Delay the entire go-live by a quarter so the awareness deficit is fully addressed.
- **D**: Replace survey-based awareness measurement with training attendance rates as a simpler proxy for readiness.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Training without Awareness is typically ineffective; the PM addresses the root stage first (A). (B) assumes training will backfill awareness, which ADKAR says it will not. (C) is disproportionate when targeted awareness work could recover readiness. (D) replaces a meaningful measure with a misleading one.
