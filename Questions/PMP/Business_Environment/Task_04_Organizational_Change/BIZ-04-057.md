# Question

A single training session is planned for all staff regardless of role. Some staff have indicated the content will not be relevant to their work. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Keep the single session for cost reasons and let staff filter what they need.
- **B**: Cancel training and rely on documentation.
- **C**: Move training online so staff can skip irrelevant sections.
- **D**: Restructure training into role-based modules and confirm relevance with each role.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Training that is relevant to the role is far more effective than one-size-fits-all (D). (A) wastes time and reduces engagement. (B) removes a critical adoption activity. (C) makes a poor design more skippable but still poor.
