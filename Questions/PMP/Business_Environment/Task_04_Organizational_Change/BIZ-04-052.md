# Question

A change is "transformational" rather than "incremental" when it:

## Options

- **A**: Fundamentally alters how the organisation operates or delivers value.
- **B**: Affects more than 100 staff members.
- **C**: Has been approved at board level.
- **D**: Is delivered using agile rather than predictive methods.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Transformational change reshapes how the organisation works (A). (B), (C), and (D) describe size, governance, or method, not the nature of the change.
