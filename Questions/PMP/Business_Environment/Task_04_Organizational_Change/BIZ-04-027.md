# Question

Your project is shifting a department from individual-contributor culture to team-based work. Systems and processes have changed but behaviours are still individualistic. Bonuses remain tied to individual KPIs. What should the project manager recommend?

## Options

- **A**: Roll out more collaboration training and team-based workshops.
- **B**: Mandate collaborative behaviours via a new departmental policy.
- **C**: Align the reward system with the desired behaviours by adjusting performance metrics and bonuses.
- **D**: Allow the cultural shift to develop organically as the team adjusts.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Incentives almost always beat exhortation; aligning rewards with the desired behaviour removes the systemic barrier (C). (A) builds capability but doesn't change motivation. (B) creates policy-reward conflict that breeds cynicism. (D) ignores a structural blocker.
