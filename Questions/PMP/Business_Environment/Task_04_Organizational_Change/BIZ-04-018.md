# Question

A new customer service system saw strong initial adoption, but three months on, usage is declining and users are reverting to old habits. Which ADKAR element is most likely failing?

## Options

- **A**: Awareness — communication about the change was poor.
- **B**: Knowledge — training on the system was inadequate.
- **C**: Reinforcement — mechanisms to sustain the change are missing.
- **D**: Ability — the system itself is not properly tested.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The pattern of strong adoption followed by drift is the classic signature of weak reinforcement (C): no rewards, no embedded process, no follow-through. (A) and (B) would have shown weak initial adoption. (D) is a quality issue, not a change management one.
