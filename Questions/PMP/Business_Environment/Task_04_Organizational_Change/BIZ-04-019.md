# Question

Two departments received the same communications and training for a single change. Department A has embraced it; Department B is actively resistant. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Investigate what's different — leadership, culture, specific concerns, or impact — and tailor the approach for Department B.
- **B**: Apply more management pressure on Department B until adoption matches Department A.
- **C**: Use Department A as proof that the change works and demand Department B fall into line.
- **D**: Defer Department B's go-live until staff there demonstrate readiness on their own.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Different responses to the same intervention point to context, not to a process gap (A). (B) and (C) ignore the actual difference and pour pressure into resistance. (D) waits for an outcome that rarely emerges without active intervention.
