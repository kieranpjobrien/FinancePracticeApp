# Question

The PM wants to measure whether the change is working. Which of the following best indicates real adoption?

## Options

- **A**: Number of users logged into the new system at least once.
- **B**: Percentage of intended users completing key workflows in the new system regularly.
- **C**: Number of training sessions delivered.
- **D**: Volume of communication issued about the change.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Adoption is real when intended users perform real work in the new system regularly (B). (A) is presence, not adoption. (C) and (D) are change activities, not change outcomes.
