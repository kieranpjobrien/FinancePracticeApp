# Question

Your change communications rely on an internal intranet that most field workers never open. Field adoption is lagging. Office adoption is strong. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Require all field workers to log into the intranet weekly so the existing channel can work as originally intended.
- **B**: Replace the intranet as the primary change channel for everyone, including office workers, despite their strong adoption signals.
- **C**: Publish the change communications more frequently on the intranet in case field workers log in occasionally after all.
- **D**: Add channels that fit field work, such as toolbox talks, supervisor cascades, and SMS, and measure reach by cohort.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Communication design follows the audience; different cohorts need different channels and measurement (D). (A) forces the audience to fit the channel. (B) discards a working channel for one cohort to fix a different cohort's problem. (C) increases volume on a channel that is not reaching the audience.
