# Question

A Scrum team is delivering a new internal tool incrementally. Users are seeing the tool change every two weeks and complaining about "constant disruption." What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Stop releasing changes and bundle them into a big-bang release every quarter.
- **B**: Batch user-facing changes into release "themes" with previews and brief enablement, while continuing technical iteration.
- **C**: Tell users to adapt because agile delivery is the chosen approach.
- **D**: Delay the rest of the project until users are comfortable.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The team can preserve iterative delivery while smoothing the user-facing experience (B). (A) abandons the agile cadence. (C) ignores user experience. (D) stops value delivery instead of changing how change is communicated.
