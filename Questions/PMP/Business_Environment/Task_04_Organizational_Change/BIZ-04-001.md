# Question

Technical implementation of a new claims platform is on track, but training attendance is poor, hallway sentiment is negative, and pilot users barely engage. What should the project manager prioritise?

## Options

- **A**: Push to finish the technical build because the deliverable is the project's core commitment.
- **B**: Engage change sponsors, communicate the why, involve resisters, and address concerns.
- **C**: Mandate training attendance and system use through formal line management enforcement.
- **D**: Delay the rollout until the user community appears more receptive to the change.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Adoption signals are flashing red, and PMI's response is targeted change management with sponsors and champions (B). (A) ignores that delivery without adoption returns no value. (C) forces compliance and reaps passive resistance. (D) defers without addressing root causes.
