# Question

Resistance from one user group is centred on a specific concern that the PM believes is a misunderstanding. What is the best response?

## Options

- **A**: Issue a written correction through internal communications to clarify the misunderstanding to everyone.
- **B**: Ask the group to host a session where they explain the concern and walk through the facts.
- **C**: Bypass the group and work directly with their manager to get the message down.
- **D**: Wait for the misunderstanding to fade on its own as the project progresses further.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Engagement creates shared understanding; a one-way correction rarely changes minds (B). (A) is broadcast and dismissive. (C) bypasses the people whose buy-in matters. (D) assumes time will resolve it.
