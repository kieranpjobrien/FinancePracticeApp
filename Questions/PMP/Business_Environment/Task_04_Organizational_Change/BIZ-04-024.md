# Question

What is the role of a change sponsor in organisational change?

## Options

- **A**: Running the day-to-day execution of the project plan.
- **B**: Delivering classroom training to end users on the new system.
- **C**: Providing leadership, resources, and authority to support the change and clear barriers.
- **D**: Maintaining the project's change request log and CR statuses.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Sponsors lead, fund, and unblock — visibly and at the right level (C). (A) is the PM. (B) is the training function. (D) is a project administrative task.
