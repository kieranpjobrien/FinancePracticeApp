# Question

The change sponsor leaves the organisation midway through a major transformation. A new executive has been appointed but is still learning the project. What is the PM's first priority?

## Options

- **A**: Pause all change activities completely until the new sponsor is fully briefed and up to speed.
- **B**: Continue change activities at the current pace and let the new sponsor catch up on their own.
- **C**: Retain the previous sponsor as an unofficial advisor in parallel with the new appointment.
- **D**: Brief the new sponsor on commitments and visible support needed, then re-establish sponsorship quickly.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A sponsor vacuum quickly erodes adoption; the PM closes the gap by briefing and re-establishing visible sponsorship (D). (A) pauses momentum. (B) ignores the sponsor's role in change. (C) creates ambiguous accountability.
