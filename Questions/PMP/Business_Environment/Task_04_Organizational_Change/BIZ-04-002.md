# Question

What is the primary purpose of organisational change management on a project?

## Options

- **A**: To control changes to project scope, schedule, and baseline documents.
- **B**: To help individuals and the organisation transition to and adopt new ways of working.
- **C**: To coordinate technical implementation activities across delivery teams.
- **D**: To track and report on milestone delivery to the steering committee.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Organisational change management is the people side of change (B): awareness, desire, knowledge, ability, and reinforcement. (A) is project change control. (C) is technical delivery coordination. (D) is performance reporting.
