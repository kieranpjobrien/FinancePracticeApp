# Question

The project is closing. Change activities have been delivered. What is the most important sustainment hand-off to operations?

## Options

- **A**: A list of all training sessions delivered to date.
- **B**: The project's communication archive.
- **C**: A copy of the change impact assessment from project initiation.
- **D**: Owners and a plan for ongoing reinforcement, refresher training, and adoption monitoring.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Sustainment lives or dies on owners and an ongoing plan (D). (A), (B), and (C) are useful artefacts but do not constitute an operating model for ongoing adoption.
