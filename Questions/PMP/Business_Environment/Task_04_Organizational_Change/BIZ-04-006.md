# Question

A digital transformation reduces middle managers' span of control. They are now the most resistant stakeholder group. What is most likely to win them over?

## Options

- **A**: Have the executive sponsor mandate the change to remove the option to opt out.
- **B**: Bypass the managers and roll out training and communications directly to staff.
- **C**: Involve the managers in shaping the new operating model and show how it strengthens their effectiveness.
- **D**: Wait for organic turnover to refresh the manager population over the next year.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

People support what they help build (C); engaging managers in design converts the threat into an opportunity. (A) buys compliance, often with quiet sabotage. (B) creates an opponent who controls daily team behaviour. (D) is passive and unreliable.
