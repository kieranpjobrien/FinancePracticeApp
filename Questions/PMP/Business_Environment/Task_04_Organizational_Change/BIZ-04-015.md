# Question

Your project is the third attempt to land the same business process change. The previous two failed on user resistance. What approach should the project manager take?

## Options

- **A**: Re-run the same plan but with stronger executive mandates to push the change through.
- **B**: Analyse why previous attempts failed, engage sceptics early, and build around the lessons.
- **C**: Roll the change out gradually without naming it in order to slip under the resistance radar.
- **D**: Bring in external consultants to drive the change because internal efforts have twice failed.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A third attempt without diagnosing the first two will likely fail the same way (B): root causes, sceptic engagement, and visible learning. (A) doubles down on what didn't work. (C) is deceptive and breeds new resistance when discovered. (D) imports talent without importing understanding.
