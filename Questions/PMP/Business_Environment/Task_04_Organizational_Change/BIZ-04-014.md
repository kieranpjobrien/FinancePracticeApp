# Question

Your transformation project is 70% complete when its primary executive sponsor leaves the organisation. What should the project manager do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Continue executing the current plan because the project is already approved and on track.
- **B**: Pause delivery until a permanent new sponsor is formally appointed by the leadership team.
- **C**: Accelerate towards completion quickly before anyone senior questions the project's direction.
- **D**: Identify and engage an interim sponsor, assess project support, and communicate with stakeholders.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Sponsor loss creates an authority gap that must be filled fast and transparently (D). (A) ignores the risk to decisions and political backing. (B) is sometimes needed but is not the first move. (C) buries an issue rather than handling it.
