# Question

A "super user" network has been recruited to support adoption. Two months in, super users say they feel unsupported and are losing motivation. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Replace the current super users with a fresh cohort of new volunteers from other teams.
- **B**: Reduce the super user role to a name on a list and remove the expectations attached.
- **C**: Engage super users on what would help, then secure recognition, time, and access.
- **D**: Disband the super user network entirely and route all user support through central IT.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Sustaining a super user network requires explicit support structures, recognition, and protected time (C). (A) churns the network without fixing the structural issue. (B) hollows out the role. (D) eliminates a distributed adoption asset.
