# Question

In the ADKAR change model, what does the "A" stand for?

## Options

- **A**: Awareness of the need for change.
- **B**: Action plan for delivery.
- **C**: Approval from the steering committee.
- **D**: Acceptance of the new way of working.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

ADKAR begins with Awareness — understanding why the change is happening (A). (B), (C), and (D) are not the model's first step.
