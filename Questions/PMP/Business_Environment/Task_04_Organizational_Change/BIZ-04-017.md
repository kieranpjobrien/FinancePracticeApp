# Question

After completing training, employees clearly understand what they should do differently but they aren't doing it. Using ADKAR, which element is most likely the barrier?

## Options

- **A**: Awareness — they don't understand why the change is needed at all.
- **B**: Knowledge — they don't yet know how to perform the new behaviour.
- **C**: Desire — they are unwilling to change their day-to-day behaviour.
- **D**: Reinforcement — there is no incentive to maintain the change once adopted.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

If knowledge exists but action doesn't follow, the gap sits at desire (C). (A) is contradicted by the training they completed. (B) is also contradicted by completed training. (D) applies to sustaining change, not adopting it in the first place.
