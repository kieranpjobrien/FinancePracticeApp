# Question

A union representative challenges a workforce change planned by the project, citing inadequate consultation. The change is on the critical path. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Proceed with the change because senior leadership has already formally approved the plan.
- **B**: Drop the workforce element from the project scope entirely to avoid the consultation dispute.
- **C**: Negotiate directly with the union representative without involving HR or employee relations.
- **D**: Engage HR and employee relations to facilitate proper consultation and address the concerns.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Workforce consultation is a legal and relational obligation; the PM works through HR and employee relations to do it properly (D). (A) ignores the obligation. (B) sacrifices project scope rather than addressing the issue. (C) bypasses functions whose involvement is essential.
