# Question

In risk management, an external change that creates a beneficial opportunity is BEST treated as:

## Options

- **A**: A positive risk to be exploited, shared, enhanced, or accepted.
- **B**: An issue to be resolved through the issue management process.
- **C**: A scope change to be added to the WBS automatically.
- **D**: A communications matter to be reported but not acted on.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Positive risks have their own response strategies (A). (B) misclassifies a future event as a present problem. (C) bypasses analysis and change control. (D) treats opportunity as a passive observation.
