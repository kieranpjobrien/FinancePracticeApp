# Question

What is the purpose of environmental scanning on a project?

## Options

- **A**: Continuously monitoring external factors that could affect project viability or approach.
- **B**: Assessing the physical site for construction-related risks.
- **C**: Evaluating the environmental footprint of the deliverable.
- **D**: Reviewing the team's working environment for collaboration issues.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Environmental scanning watches the external world — markets, regulators, technology, competitors — so the project can adapt early (A). (B) is site assessment. (C) is environmental impact assessment. (D) is team-climate review.
