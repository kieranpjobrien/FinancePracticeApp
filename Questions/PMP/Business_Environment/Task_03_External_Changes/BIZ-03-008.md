# Question

A new consumer product is 40% complete when economic forecasts now point to a recession within 12 months that would compress consumer spending. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Re-test the business case under recession scenarios and present continue/pause/pivot/cancel options.
- **B**: Continue as planned on the grounds that economic forecasts are often wrong.
- **C**: Cancel the project immediately to protect organisational cash during uncertainty.
- **D**: Accelerate the launch so the product hits the market before the downturn arrives.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

A material change in market conditions warrants a refreshed business case and a structured set of options (A). (B) ignores a credible signal. (C) makes a major call without analysis. (D) treats speed as the answer without testing whether demand will be there at all.
