# Question

A Scrum team operates in a fast-changing market. The product owner is constantly absorbing external signals into the backlog. What is the PM's most useful contribution?

## Options

- **A**: Lock the backlog at the start of each sprint to protect the team from change.
- **B**: Encourage the product owner to shift backlog priorities only at quarterly reviews.
- **C**: Insist on freezing the roadmap for the next 12 months for predictability.
- **D**: Help the product owner triage signals against value and capacity, protecting the sprint commitment.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Sprints are protected, but the backlog is fluid; the PM helps the product owner triage and protects the sprint (D). (A) protects too much and ignores reality. (B) and (C) impose predictive cadence on an inherently adaptive context.
