# Question

A market research study shows customer demand has shifted away from the segment your in-flight project targets. The sponsor still wants to launch as planned. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Launch as planned because the sponsor has decided and the direction is clear.
- **B**: Document the shift and present its impact on the business case to the sponsor.
- **C**: Cancel the project unilaterally to prevent further value loss to the organisation.
- **D**: Pivot the project scope to a different customer segment without consulting the sponsor.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The PM's job is to surface the change and its consequences so the sponsor decides with full information (B). (A) is silent execution. (C) and (D) make decisions outside the PM's authority.
