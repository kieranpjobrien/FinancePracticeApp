# Question

What does the PESTLE acronym help a project manager analyse?

## Options

- **A**: Project schedule, equipment, scope, time, labour, equity.
- **B**: Political, economic, social, technological, legal, and environmental factors.
- **C**: Procurement, engineering, supply, transport, logistics, equipment.
- **D**: Performance, evaluation, status, technology, leadership, execution.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PESTLE captures the external macro-environment categories a project should monitor (B). (A), (C), and (D) are made up of unrelated terms and do not match the framework.
