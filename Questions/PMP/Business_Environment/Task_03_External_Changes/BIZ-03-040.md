# Question

A change driven by a new privacy regulation is best classified as which type of change?

## Options

- **A**: Internal policy change.
- **B**: Technical change.
- **C**: External regulatory change.
- **D**: Stakeholder change.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

A new regulation is external to the organisation and regulatory in nature (C). (A) is an internally driven response. (B) and (D) describe other change categories.
