# Question

Your construction project's site is now classified as high flood-risk under a revised climate risk model published by the insurer. Insurance premiums will rise significantly at renewal in four months. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Switch to a different insurer at any cost before the renewal window arrives to maintain existing premium levels.
- **B**: Accept the premium increase quietly as a pass-through cost and continue the build without further investigation.
- **C**: Re-baseline the budget to absorb the projected premium hike and proceed as planned at the current site without review.
- **D**: Quantify the premium impact, assess site mitigation and design options, and present trade-offs to the sponsor for decision.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Physical climate risk demands quantification and a sponsor-level trade-off across mitigation, design, and cost (D). (A) chases premium alone without addressing underlying risk. (B) absorbs material cost without examining alternatives. (C) re-baselines without governance or analysis.
