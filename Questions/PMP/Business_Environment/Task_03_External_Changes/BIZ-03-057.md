# Question

Demand for the specialist skills your project requires has spiked in the wider market, and two team members have given notice. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Backfill the two roles externally at current market rates as fast as possible.
- **B**: Talk with the leavers to understand reasons and assess retention options with HR.
- **C**: Offer immediate retention bonuses to the rest of the team to stop further departures.
- **D**: Reassign the work across remaining staff and hope the team absorbs the loss.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Understanding the leavers and broader retention risk informs every other action (B). (A) starts hiring before knowing whether more departures are imminent. (C) is reactive spending without diagnosis. (D) ignores the risk and overloads the team.
