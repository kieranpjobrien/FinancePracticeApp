# Question

While building a country-specific mobile payments product, the central bank announces plans for a CBDC that would compete directly with your offering. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Cancel the project on the assumption that government competition cannot be beaten.
- **B**: Disregard the announcement because government projects routinely slip.
- **C**: Accelerate launch to capture the market before the CBDC is live.
- **D**: Assess CBDC scope and timeline, evaluate differentiation, and bring strategic options to stakeholders.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A CBDC may be substitutable, complementary, or distant — the answer depends on analysis (D). (A) decides before knowing. (B) dismisses a real signal. (C) confuses speed with strategy.
