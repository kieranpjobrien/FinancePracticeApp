# Question

Six months into a market expansion project, a major competitor announces a similar product launching in three months. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Continue with the existing project plan and disregard the competitor announcement entirely.
- **B**: Mirror the competitor's design choices closely to ensure feature parity at launch.
- **C**: Recommend immediate project cancellation because the competitor will dominate the market.
- **D**: Analyse the threat, generate options, and present them to stakeholders for decision.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A deliberate, evidence-based response is the PMI default (D). (A) pretends the change has not happened. (B) cedes initiative without understanding fit. (C) decides without analysis when viable paths usually still exist.
