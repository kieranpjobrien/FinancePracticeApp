# Question

Your training-content project began before generative AI tools matured. New tools could now produce comparable content faster and at lower cost. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Assess quality, cost, IP, and risk implications and bring options to stakeholders.
- **B**: Quietly add AI tools to the content workflow within the existing project scope and budget.
- **C**: Switch the production approach to fully AI-generated content immediately across the programme.
- **D**: Continue with the original manual approach because the plan was formally approved already.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

A disruptive enabler should be evaluated against the project's quality and risk bar with stakeholders deciding the trade-offs (A). (B) hides scope changes inside execution. (C) adopts new tooling without testing fit. (D) ignores a potentially material opportunity.
