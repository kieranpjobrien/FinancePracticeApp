# Question

A competitor announces a product launch that will land 90 days before your project's planned release, addressing a similar market need. What should the project manager do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Analyse the competitive impact, evaluate differentiation options, and present alternatives to stakeholders.
- **B**: Compress the schedule aggressively to beat the competitor to market.
- **C**: Continue to plan because the original business case has been approved.
- **D**: Recommend cancellation because the market opportunity has effectively passed.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

External signals demand analysis before action (A): how comparable are the products, what differentiation is feasible, and what is the cost of being second? (B) reacts without weighing trade-offs and risks quality. (C) ignores a relevant change. (D) assumes second-to-market means failure, which is often untrue.
