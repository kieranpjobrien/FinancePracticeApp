# Question

A primary supplier of components for your hardware project has been hit by a port strike that will delay shipments by an estimated six weeks. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Cancel the supplier contract outright and find a replacement supplier immediately.
- **B**: Wait for the strike to resolve and adjust the schedule once the actual delay is known.
- **C**: Assess the impact on the critical path and identify mitigation options with procurement.
- **D**: Notify the sponsor that the overall project will now be six weeks late to delivery.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Impact assessment with relevant parties precedes any decision (C); the actual schedule effect depends on float, alternates, and inventory. (A) is a heavy action without analysis. (B) is passive. (D) reports an outcome the PM has not yet validated.
