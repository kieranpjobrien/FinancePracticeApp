# Question

Which tool is most useful for analysing external macro factors that could affect a project?

## Options

- **A**: Work breakdown structure.
- **B**: Gantt chart.
- **C**: RACI matrix.
- **D**: PESTLE analysis.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PESTLE explicitly covers political, economic, social, technological, legal, and environmental forces (D). (A) decomposes scope. (B) visualises the schedule. (C) maps responsibilities. None of those examines the external environment.
