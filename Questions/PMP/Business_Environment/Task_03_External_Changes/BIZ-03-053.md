# Question

A competitor's product has been publicly recalled, opening a window for your project to capture market share if released earlier. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Compress the schedule by reducing testing to release as early as possible.
- **B**: Continue with the original plan and ignore the competitor situation.
- **C**: Recommend cancellation because the competitor's troubles are temporary.
- **D**: Assess realistic acceleration options without compromising quality and present the trade-offs to the sponsor.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The PM treats the opportunity as analysable, not as a green light to cut quality (D). (A) accelerates by sacrificing quality, which often destroys the market opportunity. (B) ignores a real opportunity. (C) misreads the situation entirely.
