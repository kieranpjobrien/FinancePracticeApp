# Question

A "VUCA" environment refers to conditions characterised by:

## Options

- **A**: Vendor, user, customer, and analyst interactions.
- **B**: Validated, useful, complete, and accurate data.
- **C**: Volatility, uncertainty, complexity, and ambiguity.
- **D**: Vision, unity, capability, and accountability.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

VUCA stands for Volatility, Uncertainty, Complexity, and Ambiguity (C). (A), (B), and (D) are not the established definition.
