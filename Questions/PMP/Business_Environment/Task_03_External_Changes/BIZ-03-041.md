# Question

A major customer announces a strategic pivot that changes the integration requirements they expect from your product. Your project is in beta. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Pause the beta and rescope the product to fit the customer's pivot.
- **B**: Continue the beta unchanged because the customer's pivot is their problem.
- **C**: Refuse the change because the contract was signed before the pivot.
- **D**: Engage the customer to clarify their new requirements and assess impact with the team.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The PM understands what has actually changed before deciding to act on it (D). (A) reacts before the requirement is defined. (B) and (C) close the door without listening to a major customer.
