# Question

A new generative AI capability released by a hyperscaler could automate 60% of the manual workflow your project is building custom UI to support. Your team is 40% through the custom build. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Finish the custom UI as planned because any pivot would waste sunk effort and demoralise the delivery team.
- **B**: Replace the in-flight build with the new AI capability immediately regardless of remaining effort and contractual obligations.
- **C**: Pause the build indefinitely while waiting to see whether the AI capability matures sufficiently to be fully trusted.
- **D**: Run a structured options analysis comparing continue, pivot, and hybrid paths, and present to the sponsor with recommendations.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A credible disruptive capability calls for a structured options analysis, not reflex (D). (A) is sunk-cost thinking. (B) pivots without accounting for contractual and transition cost. (C) pauses without decision criteria for when to resume.
