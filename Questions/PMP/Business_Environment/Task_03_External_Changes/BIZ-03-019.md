# Question

What does the acronym PESTLE stand for in environmental analysis?

## Options

- **A**: Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Legal, Environmental.
- **B**: Project, Environment, Schedule, Timeline, Leadership, Evaluation.
- **C**: Planning, Execution, Strategy, Tracking, Learning, Evaluation.
- **D**: Process, Efficiency, Standards, Technology, Labour, Expenses.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PESTLE = Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Legal, Environmental (A). The other three are invented and are not recognised PMI or strategic-analysis frameworks.
