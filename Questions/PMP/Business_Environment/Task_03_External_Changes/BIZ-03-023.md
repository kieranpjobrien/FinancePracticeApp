# Question

How often should the external business environment be monitored on a project?

## Options

- **A**: Once at initiation, then again at closure for the lessons-learned review.
- **B**: Whenever a major news story prompts the sponsor to ask about it.
- **C**: Continuously across the project life cycle.
- **D**: At the annual portfolio planning cycle only.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

External conditions shift constantly; sustained scanning enables early detection (C). (A) misses everything between the bookends. (B) is reactive. (D) is far too coarse for project-level decisions.
