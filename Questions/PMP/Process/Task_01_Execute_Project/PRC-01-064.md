# Question

Two weeks before go-live on a banking platform, the PM learns the compliance team has not completed their mandatory pre-release controls review. The sponsor, under regulatory visibility, suggests proceeding and completing the review after release. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Proceed with go-live and have compliance expedite the review on a best-efforts basis immediately after the release.
- **B**: Hold the release, engage the compliance lead jointly with the sponsor, and reset the date against compliance completion.
- **C**: Seek a formal written waiver from the compliance lead accepting post-release review so the release can proceed on plan.
- **D**: Ship a restricted pilot to a small user group while compliance completes the review on the remaining broader release.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Compliance is non-negotiable in PMI's code of ethics and professional conduct; urgency never overrides mandatory controls. (B) protects the organisation and handles the sponsor conversation directly. (A) proceeds in breach of a mandatory control. (C) a waiver doesn't convert a required review into an optional one for regulated work. (D) a restricted pilot still ships unreviewed production functionality and is still in breach.
