# Question

What is the primary purpose of the sprint review in Scrum?

## Options

- **A**: To assess individual team-member performance against sprint commitments.
- **B**: To plan the work for the upcoming sprint with the development team.
- **C**: To identify process improvements the team will adopt next sprint.
- **D**: To inspect the increment with stakeholders and adapt the product backlog based on feedback.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The sprint review is about the product, the increment, and stakeholder feedback that updates the backlog. (D) captures all three. (A) describes performance management, which is outside Scrum ceremonies. (B) describes sprint planning. (C) describes the sprint retrospective — the review is product-focused, the retro is process-focused.
