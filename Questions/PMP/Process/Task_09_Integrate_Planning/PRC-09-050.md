# Question

Six weeks after kickoff on a public sector programme, the PM realises the communications plan assumes weekly stakeholder updates but the stakeholder engagement plan promises fortnightly. The team has been doing weekly. The PM is about to hold the quarterly governance review. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Reconcile the two plans to a single cadence, update artifacts, and confirm at the governance review.
- **B**: Keep the weekly cadence in practice but leave both plans in their current state, as practice is what matters.
- **C**: Ask the governance review to direct which cadence should prevail and update the plans afterwards.
- **D**: Switch immediately to the fortnightly cadence promised in the stakeholder engagement plan document.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Subsidiary plans must be internally consistent; the PM reconciles them and confirms the integrated position with governance. (B) leaves conflicting artifacts in place, undermining plan integrity. (C) offloads an integration decision the PM should bring already reconciled. (D) reverses a working cadence without consultation to chase artifact consistency alone.
