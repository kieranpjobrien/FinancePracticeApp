# Question

A hybrid programme has agile feature teams running two-week sprints alongside predictive infrastructure teams running phase-gated quarterly milestones. How should the PM integrate planning across both?

## Options

- **A**: Standardise everyone on a single methodology to remove planning friction across the programme.
- **B**: Let each stream plan independently and reconcile only at programme-level quarterly milestones.
- **C**: Build a master plan mapping sprint cadence to milestones, naming dependencies and alignment events.
- **D**: Restructure agile teams to follow infrastructure phase gates so the cadences match up cleanly.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Hybrid integration respects both methodologies and makes the seams explicit. The master plan plus regular alignment events keeps cadence differences from becoming surprises. (A) optimises uniformity over fitness for purpose. (B) under-integrates and lets dependencies surface as defects. (D) imposes one cadence on the other rather than coordinating both.
