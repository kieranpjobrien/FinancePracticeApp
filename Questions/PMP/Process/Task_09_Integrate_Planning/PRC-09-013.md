# Question

The risk management plan calls for weekly risk reviews and rapid stakeholder notification on amber/red issues. The communications plan only schedules a monthly stakeholder update. What integration issue does this signal?

## Options

- **A**: The risk plan's weekly cadence is excessive and should be reduced to monthly to align with comms.
- **B**: Risk owners should communicate directly with stakeholders independently of the communications plan as needed.
- **C**: Monthly stakeholder updates are sufficient because risk reviews are an internal team activity only.
- **D**: The communications plan is misaligned with risk needs and must add trigger-based and rapid escalation channels.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Subsidiary plans are supposed to fit together. A weekly risk cadence with no corresponding rapid-communication channel is a real misalignment that will surface as crisis communication later. (A) cuts a legitimate risk practice to fit a weak comms plan. (B) creates ad-hoc, ungoverned communication. (C) ignores the stated risk requirement.
