# Question

A PM is writing the project management plan for a hybrid programme: a predictive infrastructure stream and an agile application stream that depend on each other. What is the most appropriate integrated planning approach?

## Options

- **A**: Convert the entire programme to predictive so the integration model is uniform across both streams.
- **B**: Convert the entire programme to agile so cadence is shared across both streams and teams.
- **C**: Maintain two independent plans and resolve coordination issues as they arise during the execution phase.
- **D**: Build one integrated plan naming handoff points, synchronisation milestones, and coordination mechanisms.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Hybrid integration is intentional, not emergent. The integrated plan is what makes the dependencies between the two streams legible and managed. (A) and (B) override appropriate methodology choices. (C) defers integration to execution-time firefighting, which is exactly what the integration plan is meant to prevent.
