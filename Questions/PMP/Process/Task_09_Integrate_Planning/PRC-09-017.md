# Question

A new team member asks the PM whether release planning, sprint planning, or daily planning is the most important agile planning event. What is the best answer?

## Options

- **A**: They form an integrated system at three horizons (release, sprint, daily), each depending on the others.
- **B**: Release planning, because it sets the strategic direction the rest of the team and sprints must follow.
- **C**: Sprint planning, because it determines what the team actually commits to and builds within that iteration.
- **D**: Daily planning, because the day's coordination is what unblocks the next incremental deliverable for the team.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Agile planning is multi-horizon by design; treating any one level as supreme distorts the others. (B), (C), and (D) each elevate one event over the integrated system. The PMI/agile answer recognises that strategy, tactics, and execution all depend on each other.
