# Question

A risk has been assessed as 40% probability with a $200,000 cost impact if it materialises. What is the expected monetary value (EMV) of the risk?

## Options

- **A**: $80,000
- **B**: $120,000
- **C**: $200,000
- **D**: $500,000

## Answer

A

## Explanation

EMV = probability × impact = 0.40 × $200,000 = $80,000. (A) is correct. (B) is (1 - probability) × impact, which doesn't represent EMV. (C) is the raw impact, ignoring probability. (D) is impact ÷ probability, which is not a defined risk metric.
