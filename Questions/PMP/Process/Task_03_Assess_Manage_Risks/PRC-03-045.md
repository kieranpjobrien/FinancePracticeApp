# Question

Which of the following is a negative risk response strategy recognised by PMI?

## Options

- **A**: Exploit.
- **B**: Mitigate.
- **C**: Enhance.
- **D**: Share.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

For negative risks (threats), PMI recognises avoid, transfer, mitigate, accept, and escalate. Mitigate is one of these. (A) exploit, (C) enhance, and (D) share are all positive-risk (opportunity) strategies. Accept is the only strategy that applies to both threats and opportunities.
