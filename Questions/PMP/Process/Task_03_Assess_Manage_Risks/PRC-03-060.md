# Question

A PM estimates three outcomes for a critical activity: 20% chance of $200K gain, 50% chance of $0 impact, 30% chance of $150K loss. What is the expected monetary value of the activity?

## Options

- **A**: EMV $0 because the upside and downside roughly cancel each other out across the three weighted outcomes for this activity.
- **B**: EMV −$5,000, computed as 0.20×200,000 plus 0.30×(−150,000) plus 0.50×0 across all three identified outcomes for the activity.
- **C**: EMV $25,000, reflecting the weighted upside exceeding weighted downside across the three identified outcomes the PM has estimated.
- **D**: EMV −$25,000, reflecting the weighted downside exceeding weighted upside across the three identified outcomes the PM has estimated.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

EMV = (0.20 × 200,000) + (0.50 × 0) + (0.30 × −150,000) = 40,000 + 0 − 45,000 = −$5,000. (B) applies EMV correctly. (A) is an intuition-only response that ignores the arithmetic. (C) inverts the sign on the loss probability. (D) doubles the magnitude error on the downside.
