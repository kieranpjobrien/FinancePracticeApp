# Question

A project manager notices that the risk response for a technical risk is being implemented but the risk status hasn't been updated in six weeks. What should the PM do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Meet with the risk owner to review current status and update the risk register.
- **B**: Remove the risk from the register since active response means it's being handled.
- **C**: Reassign the risk to a different owner who is more diligent with updates.
- **D**: Raise the issue at the next steering committee meeting.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Active response doesn't mean monitoring can lapse — PMI treats the risk register as a living document. The first action is to re-engage the risk owner and refresh the status. (B) removing a risk under active response loses visibility of residual exposure. (C) reassigning punishes before the PM has understood why updates lapsed. (D) escalates before a direct conversation with the owner.
