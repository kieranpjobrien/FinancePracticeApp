# Question

A risk owner has been assigned to a critical risk but keeps missing the agreed review cadence. The PM has spoken with them once informally. What is the BEST next step?

## Options

- **A**: Meet with the risk owner to understand what's blocking their engagement and jointly agree on a workable cadence.
- **B**: Escalate to the risk owner's functional manager and ask for reassignment.
- **C**: Take over risk ownership temporarily and manage it from the PM role.
- **D**: Remove the owner from the risk register and document the risk as unassigned until resolution.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Servant leadership: find out what's in the way before escalating or reassigning. There may be legitimate constraints (competing work, unclear expectations) that a direct conversation resolves. (B) escalates after only one informal touch — the PM hasn't genuinely tried to help. (C) taking over defeats the point of ownership and masks the underlying issue. (D) removing the owner without replacement creates accountability gaps.
