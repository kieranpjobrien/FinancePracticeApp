# Question

A project team of 5 members averages 40 productive hours per week each. A planned feature is estimated at 600 person-hours. How many weeks will the feature take assuming 100% focus?

## Options

- **A**: 2 weeks
- **B**: 2.5 weeks
- **C**: 3 weeks
- **D**: 4 weeks

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Team capacity per week = 5 x 40 = 200 person-hours. Weeks needed = 600 / 200 = 3 weeks. (A) 2 weeks would require 300 hours/week capacity. (B) 2.5 weeks = 240/week. (D) 4 weeks = 150/week, below stated team capacity.
