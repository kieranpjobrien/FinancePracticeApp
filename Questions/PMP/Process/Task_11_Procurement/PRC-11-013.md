# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of a procurement statement of work (SOW)?

## Options

- **A**: To list every vendor that may receive the bid package and qualify for the competitive procurement round.
- **B**: To describe the work and deliverables in enough detail for sellers to decide whether to bid and price appropriately.
- **C**: To define how the buyer organisation will manage the procurement process internally and govern the contract award decision.
- **D**: To document the legal terms and conditions of the resulting signed contract between the buyer and the seller parties.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The procurement SOW describes the work, deliverables, and quality requirements so sellers can evaluate whether to bid and how to price. (A) describes the qualified seller list. (C) describes the procurement management plan. (D) describes the contract itself, of which the SOW is one input.
