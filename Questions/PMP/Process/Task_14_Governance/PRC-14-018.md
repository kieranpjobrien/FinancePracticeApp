# Question

Who typically holds ultimate decision authority over a project?

## Options

- **A**: Project sponsor
- **B**: Project manager
- **C**: Functional manager of the largest contributing team
- **D**: Scrum master or agile coach

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The sponsor provides executive backing, owns the funding decision, and has ultimate authority over the project. (A) is correct. (B) operates within authority delegated from the sponsor. (C) has functional authority, not project authority. (D) is a facilitative role and does not hold decision authority.
