# Question

What is the role of the project charter in governance?

## Options

- **A**: It records the lessons learned from the previous similar project for the new team's reference.
- **B**: It tracks the open issues and decisions outstanding throughout the project lifecycle.
- **C**: It formally authorises the project and documents the project manager's authority.
- **D**: It captures the detailed task list and the work breakdown structure.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The charter is the foundational governance artifact: it authorises the project, names the PM, and sets the bounds of their authority. (C) is correct. (A) describes the lessons learned register. (B) describes the issue log. (D) describes the WBS and schedule.
