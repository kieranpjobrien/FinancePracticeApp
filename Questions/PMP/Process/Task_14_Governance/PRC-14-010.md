# Question

What does RACI stand for in a responsibility assignment matrix?

## Options

- **A**: Review, Approve, Communicate, Implement
- **B**: Risk, Analysis, Control, Inspection
- **C**: Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed
- **D**: Requirements, Activities, Constraints, Issues

## Answer

C

## Explanation

RACI assigns each task to people across four roles: Responsible (does the work), Accountable (single point of ownership), Consulted (provides input), Informed (kept up to date). (C) is the standard expansion. (A), (B), and (D) are not standard project management acronyms in this context.
