# Question

Which of the following BEST describes a schedule baseline?

## Options

- **A**: The earliest realistic schedule that the project could deliver if every activity finishes at its optimistic estimate.
- **B**: The most current working schedule, updated continuously as activities start, finish, or change duration.
- **C**: A standard schedule template used by the PMO for projects of similar type, scale, or industry.
- **D**: The approved version of the schedule model used as the basis for measuring performance, modifiable only through formal change control.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

(D) is the PMI definition: a schedule baseline is the formally approved schedule model and is changed only through change control. (A) describes an optimistic schedule scenario. (B) describes the current schedule, which differs from the baseline once execution begins. (C) describes a PMO template, not a project-specific baseline.
