# Question

Activity A has a duration of 5 days and must finish before Activity B (duration 3 days) can start, with a 2-day lag between them. If Activity A starts at the beginning of Day 1, on which day does Activity B finish?

## Options

- **A**: Day 8
- **B**: Day 10
- **C**: Day 5
- **D**: Day 7

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A starts Day 1 and runs 5 days, finishing end of Day 5. The 2-day lag covers Days 6-7. B then runs Days 8-10 (3 days), finishing end of Day 10. (B) is correct. (A) ignores the 2-day lag entirely. (C) is just A's finish date. (D) is the day B starts, not the day it finishes.
