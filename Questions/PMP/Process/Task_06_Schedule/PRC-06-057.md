# Question

Activity X has an early start of day 10, early finish of day 15, late start of day 13, late finish of day 18. Its successor Y has an early start of day 17. What are Activity X's total float and free float?

## Options

- **A**: Total float 3 days and free float 2 days, because free float measures slip without delaying successors on this path.
- **B**: Total float 2 days and free float 3 days, with free float being the larger of the two whenever paths share resources.
- **C**: Total float 3 days and free float 3 days, because both are calculated from the late-date information available here.
- **D**: Total float 2 days and free float 2 days, because the two values are always identical on any project network diagram.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Total float = LS - ES = 13 - 10 = 3 days (or LF - EF = 18 - 15 = 3). Free float = successor ES - current EF = 17 - 15 = 2 days. Total float is always greater than or equal to free float. (B) swaps them. (C) confuses the two measures. (D) states a false rule.
