# Question

What are the four logical relationship types used in the Precedence Diagramming Method (PDM)?

## Options

- **A**: Finish-to-Start, Start-to-Start, Finish-to-Finish, and Start-to-Finish.
- **B**: Hard, Soft, External, and Internal.
- **C**: Mandatory, Discretionary, External, and Internal.
- **D**: Critical, Near-Critical, Float, and Dependency.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

(A) lists the four PMI relationship types in PDM. (B) lists dependency categories — hard vs soft and external vs internal — which describe the nature of the dependency rather than the logical relationship. (C) is another categorisation set used in dependency determination, also not the relationship types. (D) mixes schedule analysis terms with dependency terms and is not a recognised set.
