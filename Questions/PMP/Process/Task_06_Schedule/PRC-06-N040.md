# Question

A project manager has converted a chronically late project to critical chain scheduling. The team is used to committing to a date for every activity and reporting green until the day it slips. As the critical chain method beds in, which actions are consistent with managing the project this way? (Select all that apply.)

## Options

- **A**: Size each activity estimate at roughly a 50% confidence (aggressive but achievable) duration and pool the removed safety into a project buffer at the end of the critical chain.
- **B**: Keep reporting each activity's percent complete against its planned finish date, and raise a variance whenever an activity is forecast to miss its own date.
- **C**: Add feeding buffers where non-critical chains merge into the critical chain, sized to the uncertainty of those feeding paths.
- **D**: Sequence the critical chain to resolve resource contention first, so the chain reflects both task dependencies and the availability of the constraining resources.
- **E**: Stagger the start of parallel activities that share a constrained resource rather than starting them together, so owners are not forced to multitask between them.

## Answer

A, C, D, E

## Explanation

Critical chain sets activity durations at aggressive 50% estimates, pools the stripped safety into a project buffer, protects the chain with feeding buffers, builds the chain around resource contention as well as logic, and avoids multitasking by staggering resource-sharing work; control is by buffer consumption, not by individual activity dates. (A) pooling at 50% confidence is the core estimating move. (C) feeding buffers shield the chain from late feeders. (D) the critical chain is the resource-levelled longest path, so resolving resource contention defines it. (E) staggering shared-resource work removes the multitasking that inflates durations. (B) is the trap: tracking each activity to its own finish date and flagging individual variances is exactly the date-driven control critical chain replaces with buffer monitoring, and it recreates the padding-and-green-until-slip behaviour the method is meant to cure.
