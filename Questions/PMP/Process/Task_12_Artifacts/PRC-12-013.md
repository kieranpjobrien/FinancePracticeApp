# Question

Which artifact best helps a PM confirm that every requirement has been delivered and tested?

## Options

- **A**: Stakeholder register
- **B**: Risk register
- **C**: Requirements traceability matrix
- **D**: Issue log

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The requirements traceability matrix links each requirement to its source, design element, deliverable, and test case, which is exactly what you need to confirm completeness. (A) tracks stakeholder interests and engagement, not delivery. (B) tracks threats and opportunities, not requirement coverage. (D) tracks open issues and their resolution, which is a symptom view rather than a coverage view.
