# Question

A project manager is coaching a new team member on which artifact to consult for different information needs. Match each information need on a predictive project to the artifact that is the authoritative source for it.

## Items

- **1**: The list of identified threats and opportunities, with their owners and response plans.
- **2**: The record of problems that have already occurred and are being actively worked to resolution.
- **3**: The link between each requirement and the design, build, and test elements that satisfy it.

## Matches

- **A**: Issue log
- **B**: Risk register
- **C**: Requirements traceability matrix

## Answer

1-B, 2-A, 3-C

## Explanation

The risk register holds identified threats and opportunities with owners and planned responses, so need 1 maps to it. The issue log records problems that have already materialised and are being actively resolved, matching need 2. The requirements traceability matrix links each requirement through to the design, build, and test artifacts that satisfy it, matching need 3. Confusing the risk register with the issue log is a common error: risks are uncertain future events, whereas issues are present, realised problems.
