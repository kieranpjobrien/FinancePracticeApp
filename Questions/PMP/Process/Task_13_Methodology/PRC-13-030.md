# Question

What sprint length does the Scrum Guide recommend?

## Options

- **A**: One month or less, with two weeks being a common choice.
- **B**: Two to three months for complex enterprise work.
- **C**: As long as needed to complete all committed stories.
- **D**: Exactly four weeks for every team in the organisation.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The Scrum Guide caps sprints at one month, and most teams choose two weeks to balance focus and feedback. (A) is correct. (B) exceeds the cap and undermines feedback frequency. (C) abandons the timebox, which is the defining feature of Scrum. (D) over-prescribes; sprint length should be chosen by the team within the cap.
