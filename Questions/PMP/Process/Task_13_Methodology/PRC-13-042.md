# Question

Which Agile Manifesto value most directly addresses adapting when circumstances change?

## Options

- **A**: Working software over comprehensive documentation
- **B**: Customer collaboration over contract negotiation
- **C**: Individuals and interactions over processes and tools
- **D**: Responding to change over following a plan

## Answer

D

## Explanation

"Responding to change over following a plan" is the manifesto value about adaptability. (D) is the direct answer. (A) is about deliverables. (B) is about how the team works with the customer. (C) is about the importance of people and conversations over tooling.
