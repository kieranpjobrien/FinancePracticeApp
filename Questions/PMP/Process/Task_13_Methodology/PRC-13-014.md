# Question

Which factor is LEAST appropriate as a primary driver when selecting a delivery approach?

## Options

- **A**: How stable and well-understood the requirements are.
- **B**: The methodology the project manager personally prefers.
- **C**: The frequency of stakeholder interaction the project will need.
- **D**: The organisation's existing culture and capability.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PMI says fitness for purpose, not personal comfort, drives the decision. (B) is the wrong driver. (A) is a core selection input — uncertainty calls for adaptive, stability favours predictive. (C) shapes feedback cadence and ceremony design. (D) determines what the team can realistically execute. The PM's preferences are secondary to all three.
