# Question

What is a stakeholder communication matrix used for?

## Options

- **A**: Mapping each stakeholder to the information they need, the format, the cadence, and the communicator.
- **B**: Recording the dates each stakeholder was last contacted by the project team for audit purposes.
- **C**: Calculating how many communication channels exist among the project's stakeholders.
- **D**: Tracking the engagement state (resistant, neutral, supportive, leading) of each stakeholder over time.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The communication matrix is a planning artefact that pairs each stakeholder with the what, when, how, and who of their communication. (A) is correct. (B) describes a contact log. (C) describes the channel count formula, not the matrix. (D) describes the stakeholder engagement assessment matrix — related but a different artefact focused on engagement state, not communication mechanics.
