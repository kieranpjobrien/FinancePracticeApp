# Question

During the commissioning phase of a predictive water-treatment upgrade, a sensor calibration fault is found at 18:00. Until it is verified fixed in the morning, the chlorination pumps must stay off. The night shift, a supervisor and two operators whose names the PM knows, comes on at 22:00, and starting those pumps is one of their first tasks. The PM will not be on site overnight. How should the PM handle communicating the hold?

## Options

- **A**: Message the supervisor and the two operators directly with the hold and the instruction not to start the pumps, and require each to confirm they have read it before shift start.
- **B**: Telephone the supervisor to brief the hold verbally and rely on the supervisor to pass it to the two operators at the shift handover.
- **C**: Post the hold prominently on the commissioning log and at the pump control station, where the incoming shift reviews entries before starting work.
- **D**: Record the fault and the agreed hold in the issue log and notify the commissioning lead, so the hold is governed through the project's issue process.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The message is safety-critical, time-boxed, and must be acted on by three specific people at the start of a shift the PM will not attend, so the PM needs assurance it reached each of them, not just that it was made available. (A) targets the named recipients and closes the loop with individual confirmation, so the PM knows before 22:00 that every person who could start the pumps has the hold. (B) reaches the supervisor reliably but leaves the two operators dependent on a verbal relay the PM cannot confirm landed, and a single missed handover defeats it. (C) places the notice where the shift normally looks, but a posted notice is retrieved only if read and gives the absent PM no confirmation it was, a thin margin for a safety hold. (D) governs the issue correctly for the record and the daytime team but is a logging-and-escalation step, not a means of alerting tonight's operators in time. The discriminator is which method both reaches every actor and proves it did before they act.
