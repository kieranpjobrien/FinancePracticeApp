# Question

A project has 12 stakeholders who all need to communicate with each other. How many potential communication channels exist?

## Options

- **A**: 24
- **B**: 66
- **C**: 78
- **D**: 144

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Use the channels formula n(n-1)/2 with n = 12: 12 × 11 / 2 = 132 / 2 = 66 channels. (B) is correct. (A) is 2n, a common shortcut error. (C) is the sum of integers from 1 to 12 (n(n+1)/2), close to the right formula but off by one term. (D) is n^2, which counts ordered pairs and self-pairs.
