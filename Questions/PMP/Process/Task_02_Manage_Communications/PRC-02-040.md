# Question

What is the primary purpose of a project kickoff meeting?

## Options

- **A**: To complete the project charter and gain formal sponsor authorisation for the work.
- **B**: To celebrate the conclusion of project planning and the start of execution.
- **C**: To review the project's risk register with all stakeholders and assign risk owners.
- **D**: To align the team and stakeholders on objectives, scope, roles, and ways of working.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The kickoff is fundamentally about alignment — shared understanding of the why, what, who, when, and how. (D) captures this. (A) describes charter approval, which usually precedes the kickoff. (B) confuses the kickoff with a milestone celebration. (C) is one possible agenda item but not the meeting's primary purpose.
