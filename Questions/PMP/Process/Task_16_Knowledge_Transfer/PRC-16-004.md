# Question

What is the primary purpose of a lessons learned register?

## Options

- **A**: To capture knowledge from the project so current and future projects can benefit from what worked and what did not.
- **B**: To document individual team-member performance and inform formal performance reviews conducted after the project has formally closed down.
- **C**: To record the project's financial transactions and vendor payments accurately for audit and reconciliation purposes at project close.
- **D**: To list all project deliverables along with their final acceptance status and formal sign-off for the project closure pack.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

(A) is the textbook definition of the lessons learned register. (B) confuses it with performance management; lessons learned focus on practices, not individuals. (C) is the role of project accounting records. (D) is the deliverables register or scope baseline, not lessons learned.
