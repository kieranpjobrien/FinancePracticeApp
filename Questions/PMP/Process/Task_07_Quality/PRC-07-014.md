# Question

Which quality tool is MOST appropriate for identifying the vital few causes that account for the majority of observed defects?

## Options

- **A**: Control chart
- **B**: Pareto chart
- **C**: Cause-and-effect (fishbone) diagram
- **D**: Scatter diagram

## Answer

B

## Explanation

(B) is the Pareto chart, which sorts cause categories by frequency to highlight the vital few. (A) monitors process stability over time but does not rank causes. (C) helps brainstorm potential causes by category but does not quantify their relative contribution. (D) shows the relationship between two variables, not cause prioritisation.
