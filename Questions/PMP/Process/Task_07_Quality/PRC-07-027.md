# Question

What is the PRIMARY purpose of the project's Quality Management Plan?

## Options

- **A**: To list every test case that will be executed against the product during system and acceptance testing phases.
- **B**: To describe how the organisation's quality policies, standards, and requirements will be implemented and met on this project.
- **C**: To document the professional certifications required for project team members assigned to quality-critical work packages.
- **D**: To track each defect found during execution along with severity, owner, and resolution status for status reporting.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

(B) is the PMBOK definition: the QMP describes how quality policies will be implemented, standards applied, and requirements met. (A) describes a test plan, which is a downstream artefact. (C) describes resource and procurement matters, not the QMP's purpose. (D) describes a defect log, an output of QC.
