# Question

A project's quality costs are: Prevention = $50,000, Appraisal = $30,000, Internal Failure = $40,000, External Failure = $80,000. What is the Cost of Poor Quality (COPQ)?

## Options

- **A**: $120,000
- **B**: $150,000
- **C**: $80,000
- **D**: $200,000

## Answer

A

## Explanation

COPQ is the sum of failure costs only: Internal Failure + External Failure = $40,000 + $80,000 = $120,000. (A) is correct. (C) lists only external failure. (B) wrongly adds appraisal (a conformance cost) to the failure costs. (D) is the total Cost of Quality (all four categories), not COPQ.
