# Question

Which is an example of a push communication method?

## Options

- **A**: A collaborative workshop.
- **B**: A weekly status email distributed to a list.
- **C**: A project portal stakeholders log into when they choose.
- **D**: A video conference with real-time dialogue.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Push communication sends information to recipients without confirming receipt or engagement — email to a list is the classic example. (A) collaborative workshop is interactive. (C) a portal stakeholders access on demand is pull communication. (D) video conference with dialogue is interactive communication.
