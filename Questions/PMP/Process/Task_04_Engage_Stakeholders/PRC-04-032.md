# Question

The PM notices that stakeholders are receiving the same information through multiple channels — email, portal, Teams messages, and print reports. Several have complained about overload. What is the BEST response?

## Options

- **A**: Continue all channels since some stakeholders prefer each one for different reasons.
- **B**: Discontinue all but email since it's the most universal and accessible channel.
- **C**: Review the communications plan, consolidate channels by audience preference, and remove duplicates.
- **D**: Let each stakeholder subscribe or unsubscribe to individual channels as they prefer.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Duplicated communication is wasteful and erodes the value of each channel. (C) takes the analytical path through the plan, matching channels to audience. (A) ignores the stated complaint. (B) unilaterally dropping to a single channel may not fit all stakeholders' working styles. (D) opt-in/out fragments the comms plan and makes it hard to ensure key messages reach everyone.
