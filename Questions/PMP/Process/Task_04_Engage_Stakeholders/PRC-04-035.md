# Question

Which attribute is NOT typically recorded in the stakeholder register?

## Options

- **A**: Stakeholder name and role.
- **B**: Hourly billing rate.
- **C**: Assessment classification (power, interest, influence).
- **D**: Communication requirements.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The stakeholder register captures identifying information (A), assessment classification (C), communication requirements (D), and engagement expectations. (B) hourly billing rate belongs in procurement or resource management records, not the stakeholder register.
