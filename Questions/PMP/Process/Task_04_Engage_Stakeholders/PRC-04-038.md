# Question

An active stakeholder has been transferred to another division and no longer has a formal role on the project. What should the PM do with the stakeholder register?

## Options

- **A**: Update the register to reflect the stakeholder's changed role and reassess whether they remain relevant to engage.
- **B**: Keep the entry unchanged — the stakeholder was valuable and may still want updates.
- **C**: Delete the entry entirely to keep the register current.
- **D**: Leave the entry and add the replacement person as a duplicate entry without resolving the first.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Stakeholder registers track current relationships. (A) updates the entry and decides on continued engagement based on current relevance — the stakeholder may still care but in a different capacity. (B) leaving the entry unchanged misrepresents the relationship. (C) deletion loses institutional memory; retire or mark inactive instead. (D) duplicate entries create confusion about who is the current counterpart.
