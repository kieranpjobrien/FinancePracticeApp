# Question

Which process produces the stakeholder register as its primary output?

## Options

- **A**: Identify Stakeholders.
- **B**: Plan Stakeholder Engagement.
- **C**: Manage Stakeholder Engagement.
- **D**: Monitor Stakeholder Engagement.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Identify Stakeholders produces the stakeholder register — the foundational document listing stakeholders, classification, and key details. (B) Plan Stakeholder Engagement uses the register as an input and produces the stakeholder engagement plan. (C) Manage Stakeholder Engagement executes engagement actions. (D) Monitor Stakeholder Engagement evaluates effectiveness of engagement.
