# Question

What is the primary purpose of the stakeholder engagement plan?

## Options

- **A**: To list all stakeholders with their contact details and roles.
- **B**: To define strategies and actions for productively involving stakeholders throughout the project.
- **C**: To record the current level of engagement of each stakeholder.
- **D**: To log stakeholder issues and complaints as they are raised.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The stakeholder engagement plan documents strategies and actions to promote productive stakeholder involvement — how each stakeholder will be engaged, by whom, and when. (A) describes the stakeholder register. (C) describes the stakeholder engagement assessment matrix, an input to the plan. (D) describes an issue log.
