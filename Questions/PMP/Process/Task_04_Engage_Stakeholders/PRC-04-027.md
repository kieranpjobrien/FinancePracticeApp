# Question

What is a key output of the Monitor Stakeholder Engagement process?

## Options

- **A**: Stakeholder register produced during identification.
- **B**: Work performance information about stakeholder engagement effectiveness.
- **C**: Stakeholder engagement plan defining the engagement strategies.
- **D**: Project charter formally authorising the project and PM.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Monitor Stakeholder Engagement produces work performance information (among other outputs) — data on how well engagement strategies are working. (A) the stakeholder register is an output of Identify Stakeholders and can be updated here, but it's not the primary output. (C) the engagement plan is an output of Plan Stakeholder Engagement. (D) the charter is produced during initiation.
