# Question

Which engagement level indicates a stakeholder is aware of the project but may resist change?

## Options

- **A**: Unaware.
- **B**: Resistant.
- **C**: Neutral.
- **D**: Supportive.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Resistant stakeholders are aware of the project and its potential outcomes but oppose change. (A) unaware means they don't know about the project at all. (C) neutral means aware but neither supportive nor opposed. (D) supportive means aware and in favour.
