# Question

During Plan Stakeholder Engagement, the PM is deciding how detailed the engagement plan should be for a small, co-located team of eight. What is the BEST approach?

## Options

- **A**: Keep the plan lightweight — agreed working practices, a brief comms approach, and decision-making norms.
- **B**: Produce a full formal engagement plan identical to what a 100-person enterprise project would have.
- **C**: Skip the engagement plan entirely since the team is small, co-located, and talks every day.
- **D**: Use the template from a prior large project and fill in the same sections with lighter content.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Tailoring: small, co-located teams benefit from lightweight artefacts that capture the essentials without bureaucracy. (A) matches the principle. (B) heavyweight docs on small projects waste time and don't get read. (C) skipping entirely leaves gaps in how engagement will work. (D) copying a large-project template produces a document that doesn't fit the context.
