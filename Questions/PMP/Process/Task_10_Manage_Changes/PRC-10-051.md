# Question

On a hybrid banking programme, an approved change affects scope, schedule, and cost. The PM has updated scope and schedule baselines but has not yet updated the cost baseline. A steering report is due in two days. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Issue the steering report now with updated scope and schedule and note cost will follow in the next cycle.
- **B**: Update the cost baseline now and then issue the steering report with all three baselines fully aligned.
- **C**: Pause the steering report until a full plan review has been performed across all subsidiary plans.
- **D**: Issue the report using the old cost baseline and treat cost impact as a variance in future reports.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Integrated change control updates all affected baselines together so reports remain internally consistent and decision-ready. (A) reports partially updated baselines, causing confusion and conflicting numbers. (C) over-scopes the update and delays reporting unnecessarily. (D) reports known-stale numbers as baseline, which is misleading to governance.
