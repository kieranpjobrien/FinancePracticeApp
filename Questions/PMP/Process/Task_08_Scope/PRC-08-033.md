# Question

In an agile delivery, what is the primary artefact used to manage product scope?

## Options

- **A**: The project scope statement.
- **B**: The work breakdown structure.
- **C**: The product backlog.
- **D**: The requirements traceability matrix.

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The product backlog is the single, ordered source of work for the product, owned by the PO and continuously refined. (A) is a predictive scope artefact. (B) is a hierarchical decomposition used in predictive planning. (D) is a traceability artefact, not the primary scope tool.
