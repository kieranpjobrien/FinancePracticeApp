# Question

Which technique is most directly described as "building a simplified working model of the product to elicit requirements feedback"?

## Options

- **A**: Benchmarking against comparable products from other organisations.
- **B**: Document analysis of existing artefacts and reference materials.
- **C**: Focus groups with qualified stakeholders.
- **D**: Prototyping.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A prototype is a simplified, often disposable, working model that lets stakeholders react to something concrete and surface tacit requirements. (A) is comparative analysis, no model is built. (B) examines existing documents. (C) is a structured discussion technique with no model.
