# Question

On a hybrid data platform programme, the team pushes back on finalising detailed scope for phase three, arguing too much will change between now and then. The sponsor wants a fully detailed scope document for all phases this quarter. What should the PM recommend?

## Options

- **A**: Draft the detailed phase three scope now to meet the sponsor's quarterly documentation request.
- **B**: Plan phase one and two in detail now, and elaborate phase three closer to when it begins.
- **C**: Reduce phase three to a single high-level paragraph so sponsor documentation is satisfied.
- **D**: Ask the team to provide estimates for phase three despite their concerns about stability.

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Progressive elaboration and rolling wave planning are the PMI-aligned response to legitimate uncertainty; detail grows as clarity grows. (A) invests in detail that will be rework. (C) satisfies paperwork with a token that provides no planning value. (D) forces estimates on unstable content, producing numbers no one trusts.
