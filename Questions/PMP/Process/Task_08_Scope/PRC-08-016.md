# Question

Which of the following is most appropriately documented in the project scope statement?

## Options

- **A**: Project deliverables, acceptance criteria, and explicit exclusions.
- **B**: Daily task assignments for individual team members.
- **C**: A complete time-phased cost baseline.
- **D**: The risk register with owners and response strategies.

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The scope statement defines what is in and out of the project: deliverables, acceptance criteria, exclusions, constraints, and assumptions. (B) belongs in the schedule and resource assignments. (C) belongs in the cost baseline. (D) belongs in the risk register. Each artefact has a defined home in the project management plan.
