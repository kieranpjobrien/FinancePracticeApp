# Question

A stakeholder asks the PM why their change request was rejected at the most recent CCB meeting, which the PM did not attend. What should the PM do?

## Options

- **A**: Tell the stakeholder the decision is final and not subject to debate.
- **B**: Resubmit the same change request on the stakeholder's behalf to keep them engaged.
- **C**: Request an emergency CCB meeting to revisit the rejection.
- **D**: Pull the meeting record, understand the documented rationale, and brief the stakeholder transparently.

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Stakeholders deserve a clear, accurate explanation; the PM owes that as part of communications and stakeholder engagement. (A) provides nothing and damages trust. (B) wastes governance time without new information and likely produces the same answer. (C) escalates before understanding the decision; the PM should first know why before challenging it.
