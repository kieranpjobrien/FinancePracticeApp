# Question

According to PRINCE2 7, a high-performing project culture is BEST characterised by which of the following?

## Options

- **A**: Strict hierarchy where team members act only on explicit instructions
- **B**: Individuals competing so that only the strongest ideas survive
- **C**: Minimal interaction so people can focus solely on their own deliverables
- **D**: Shared purpose, mutual trust, open communication and collaboration among the project team

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7 describes a high-performing project culture as one built on a shared sense of purpose, trust, openness and collaboration, enabling people to perform at their best. (A) rigid command-and-control suppresses the engagement such a culture depends on. (B) internal competition undermines the collaboration and trust being sought. (C) isolation prevents the open communication central to high performance.
