# Question

In PRINCE2 7, which statement BEST distinguishes leadership from management?

## Options

- **A**: Leadership is about setting direction and inspiring people, while management is about organising resources and processes to deliver
- **B**: Leadership and management are interchangeable terms for the same activity
- **C**: Leadership applies only to the project board, while management applies only to the project manager
- **D**: Management sets the vision, while leadership administers the plans and budgets

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7 treats leadership as setting direction, aligning and motivating people, whereas management is the organising of resources, processes and systems to deliver. (B) the two are related but distinct disciplines, not synonyms. (C) both leadership and management can be exercised at any level, not confined to specific roles. (D) reverses the definitions: leadership provides vision, management administers plans and budgets.
