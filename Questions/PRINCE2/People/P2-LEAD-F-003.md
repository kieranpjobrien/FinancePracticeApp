# Question

What does 'situational leadership' mean in the context of PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: Always using the same authoritative style so the team knows what to expect
- **B**: Allowing the project board to dictate the leadership style for every team
- **C**: Leading only when a situation has escalated into a formal exception
- **D**: Adapting leadership style to the people, their competence and commitment, and the demands of the situation

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Situational leadership in PRINCE2 7 means flexing one's style to suit the individuals involved, their development level, and the circumstances, rather than applying one fixed approach. (A) a single fixed style contradicts the very idea of situational adaptation. (B) style is exercised by the leader reading the situation, not imposed top-down by the board. (C) leadership is exercised continuously, not reserved for exceptions.
