# Question

Which activity is the FIRST step in engaging stakeholders effectively under PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: Drafting the project's highlight reports
- **B**: Closing the project and capturing follow-on actions
- **C**: Identifying the stakeholders who have an interest in the project
- **D**: Appointing the team managers for each work package

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Effective engagement begins with identifying who the stakeholders are before their interests, influence and information needs can be analysed. (A) is a reporting output that depends on stakeholders already being known. (B) occurs at the very end of the project lifecycle. (D) concerns delivery resourcing, not the starting point of stakeholder engagement.
