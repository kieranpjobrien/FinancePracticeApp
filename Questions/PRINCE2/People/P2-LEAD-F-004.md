# Question

In PRINCE2 7, what is meant by 'influencing without formal authority'?

## Options

- **A**: Issuing instructions that team members are contractually obliged to follow
- **B**: Escalating to the project board whenever a team member declines a request
- **C**: Gaining cooperation and commitment from people the leader does not directly manage, through trust, credibility and relationships
- **D**: Delegating all decisions to whoever holds the most senior job title

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Project managers frequently rely on people they do not line-manage, so PRINCE2 7 stresses influencing through credibility, trust and relationships rather than positional power. (A) describes formal authority, which the project manager often lacks over matrixed staff. (B) escalation is a last resort, not a substitute for influence. (D) deferring to seniority abandons the leader's own influencing responsibility.
