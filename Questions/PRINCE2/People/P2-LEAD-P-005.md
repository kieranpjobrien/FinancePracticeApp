# Question

A renewable energy project has slipped behind schedule and the team is dispirited after a difficult stage. The work remains within stage tolerance. What leadership action should the project manager prioritise?

## Options

- **A**: Reset direction, acknowledge the difficulty, and re-motivate the team around achievable next goals
- **B**: Submit an exception report to the project board because morale is low
- **C**: Increase detailed monitoring and impose tighter individual deadlines without explanation
- **D**: Say nothing about the setback to avoid drawing attention to it

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Leadership in PRINCE2 7 is about setting direction and inspiring people, so re-motivating a dispirited team around clear, achievable goals is the priority, especially as the work is still within tolerance. (B) an exception report is for forecast tolerance breaches, not morale, and would be premature. (C) tightening control without explanation deepens disengagement. (D) silence forfeits the chance to lead the team through the setback.
