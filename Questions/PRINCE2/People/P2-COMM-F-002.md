# Question

What is the purpose of the communication management approach in PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: To describe how and when information will be sent to and received from stakeholders
- **B**: To record the lessons learned from communication failures on previous projects
- **C**: To list every email address and phone number for the project team
- **D**: To define the escalation route for issues raised by team managers

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The communication management approach describes the means and frequency of communication to and from stakeholders, both internal and external to the project. (B) belongs in a lessons log or lessons report. (C) is mere contact detail, not the approach itself. (D) is governed by tolerances and the reporting arrangements, not the defining purpose of this product.
