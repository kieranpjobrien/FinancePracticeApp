# Question

Which set of behaviours by a project manager would MOST help build a high-performing project culture according to PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: Establishing a clear shared purpose, encouraging open dialogue, and recognising contributions
- **B**: Centralising every decision and discouraging questions to maintain pace
- **C**: Rewarding only individual output and keeping team goals private
- **D**: Avoiding feedback so that people are not discouraged

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7 associates a high-performing culture with shared purpose, openness, trust and recognition, all of which (A) embodies. (B) centralisation and suppressing questions erode trust and engagement. (C) prizing individual output over shared goals undermines collaboration. (D) withholding feedback prevents the learning and improvement that high performance depends on.
