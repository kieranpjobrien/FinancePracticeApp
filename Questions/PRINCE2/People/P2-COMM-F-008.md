# Question

PRINCE2 7 stresses transparency in stakeholder communication. What does transparency primarily require of the project manager?

## Options

- **A**: Sharing accurate, honest and timely information, including bad news, with relevant stakeholders
- **B**: Restricting project information to the project board to avoid confusion
- **C**: Communicating only positive progress to maintain stakeholder confidence
- **D**: Publishing all information to every stakeholder regardless of their needs

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Transparency means being open and honest, providing accurate and timely information so stakeholders can make sound decisions, even when the news is unwelcome. (B) withholding information from stakeholders is the opposite of transparency. (C) filtering out bad news undermines trust and informed decision-making. (D) flooding everyone ignores the tailored information needs set out in the communication management approach.
