# Question

In PRINCE2 7, what is a stakeholder?

## Options

- **A**: Any individual, group or organisation that can affect, be affected by, or perceive itself to be affected by the project
- **B**: Only the people who provide the funding and resources for the project
- **C**: The members of the project board who authorise each stage
- **D**: The end users who will operate the project's products after handover

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7 defines a stakeholder as any individual, group or organisation that can affect, be affected by, or perceive itself to be affected by an initiative. (B) describes only the business and supplier funders, a narrow subset. (C) names a specific governance body, not the full stakeholder population. (D) covers users alone, ignoring suppliers, regulators and other affected parties.
