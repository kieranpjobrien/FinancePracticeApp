# Question

A newly formed project team is openly disagreeing about priorities and challenging how decisions are made. According to the common stages-of-development model, which stage does this BEST illustrate?

## Options

- **A**: Forming
- **B**: Storming
- **C**: Norming
- **D**: Performing

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The storming stage is characterised by conflict, jostling for position and challenges to authority and approach, which the described behaviour matches. (A) forming is the polite, tentative getting-to-know-you stage; (C) norming is when the team settles into agreed ways of working; (D) performing is when the team operates smoothly at high effectiveness, none of which fit open disagreement.
