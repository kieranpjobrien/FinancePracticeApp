# Question

A project manager groups stakeholders by their level of power over the project and their level of interest in it, then tailors engagement to each group. This technique is best described as which part of stakeholder engagement?

## Options

- **A**: Stakeholder analysis
- **B**: Quality control
- **C**: Configuration management
- **D**: Benefits management

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Assessing stakeholders by power and interest so that engagement effort can be prioritised is the essence of stakeholder analysis. (B) quality control verifies products against criteria, not people. (C) configuration management tracks product versions and status. (D) benefits management tracks expected outcomes, not stakeholder positioning.
