# Question

When building and developing a project team, why does PRINCE2 7 encourage attention to the mix of skills, experience and perspectives among members?

## Options

- **A**: Because a diverse and complementary mix of capabilities helps the team solve problems and deliver more effectively
- **B**: Because team members must all hold the same professional qualification to collaborate
- **C**: Because diversity removes the need to define individual roles
- **D**: Because a larger team is always more effective than a smaller one

## Answer

A

## Explanation

A team that combines complementary skills, experience and viewpoints is better equipped to address the varied challenges a project throws up and to deliver effectively. (B) is false — uniform qualifications are not required, and complementary differences are valued; (C) is wrong because roles still need clear definition; (D) is false, since effectiveness depends on the right mix, not sheer size.
