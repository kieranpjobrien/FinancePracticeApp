# Question

In PRINCE2 7, what BEST describes a project team?

## Options

- **A**: A temporary group of individuals brought together with the shared purpose of delivering the project's outputs and outcomes
- **B**: The permanent operational department that owns the products after the project closes
- **C**: The members of the project board who direct the project
- **D**: The external suppliers contracted to build the project's specialist products

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7 treats the project team as a temporary structure of people assembled to work towards a shared goal of delivering the project's outputs and outcomes, then disbanded at closure. (B) describes operational line teams, not the project team; (C) names a specific governance body within the wider team, not the team itself; (D) covers only the supplier element and ignores business and user representatives.
