# Question

Which statement about the relationship between leadership and management is correct in PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: A successful project manager needs only management skills, as leadership is the executive's concern
- **B**: Leadership and management cannot be exercised by the same individual
- **C**: Management should be applied at the start of a project and leadership only at the end
- **D**: Effective project delivery requires both leadership and management, and a good project manager blends the two as the situation demands

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7 holds that delivery needs both disciplines and that the project manager must combine them appropriately to circumstances. (A) wrongly strips leadership from the project manager. (B) the same person can and routinely does exercise both. (C) imposes an artificial sequencing; both are required throughout the project.
