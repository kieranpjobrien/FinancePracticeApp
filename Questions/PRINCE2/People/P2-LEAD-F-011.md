# Question

When leading the adoption of a new way of working, which action by the project manager is MOST consistent with PRINCE2 7 guidance on change?

## Options

- **A**: Announcing the change once by email and assuming compliance will follow
- **B**: Delaying all communication until the new system is fully built to avoid confusion
- **C**: Engaging those affected early, explaining the reasons for the change, and supporting them through the transition
- **D**: Leaving communication entirely to the change authority and focusing only on the schedule

## Answer

C

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7 emphasises engaging affected people early, communicating the rationale, and supporting them so the change is genuinely adopted. (A) a single announcement neglects the engagement and support that adoption requires. (B) withholding communication until completion breeds resistance rather than reducing it. (D) abdicating communication ignores the project manager's leadership role in adoption; note the change authority handles authorisation of changes, not behavioural transition.
