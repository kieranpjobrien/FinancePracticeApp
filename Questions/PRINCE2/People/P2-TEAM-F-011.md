# Question

Which responsibility BEST belongs to the Senior Supplier within the project team?

## Options

- **A**: Specifying the needs of those who will use the project's products
- **B**: Owning the business case and securing the project's funding
- **C**: Representing those designing, developing and delivering the products, and being accountable for their quality
- **D**: Managing the production of products within a single Work Package

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The Senior Supplier represents the supplier interest, speaking for those who design, develop and deliver the products and being accountable for the quality of what is produced. (A) is the Senior User's responsibility; (B) is the Executive's responsibility; (D) describes the Team Manager, who operates below the project board.
