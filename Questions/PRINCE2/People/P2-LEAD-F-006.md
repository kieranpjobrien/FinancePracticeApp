# Question

A project manager spends a morning agreeing the team's shared vision and motivating members through a period of uncertainty, then spends the afternoon updating the schedule and reallocating resources. Which PRINCE2 7 distinction does this BEST illustrate?

## Options

- **A**: The morning was management and the afternoon was leadership
- **B**: The morning was leadership and the afternoon was management
- **C**: Both activities are purely management because they occur within one project
- **D**: Both activities are purely leadership because the same person performs them

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7 frames leadership as setting direction and inspiring people (the morning's vision and motivation) and management as organising resources and processes (the afternoon's scheduling and allocation). (A) reverses the two disciplines. (C) and (D) wrongly collapse the distinction; a project manager routinely exercises both, and the nature of the activity, not the location or the person, determines which is which.
