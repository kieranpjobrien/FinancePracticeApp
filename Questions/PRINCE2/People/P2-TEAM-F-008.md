# Question

Which statement BEST describes a characteristic difference between co-located and virtual project teams in PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: Co-located teams cannot include members from different organisations, whereas virtual teams always can
- **B**: Virtual teams remove the need for defined roles and responsibilities
- **C**: Co-located teams are unsuitable for projects that use any digital collaboration tools
- **D**: Virtual teams typically need more deliberate effort to build trust and maintain communication than co-located teams

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Because virtual team members lack the informal face-to-face contact of a shared workplace, building trust and keeping communication flowing usually demands more conscious effort and structure. (A) is false — co-located teams can mix organisations as long as people share a location; (B) is wrong because clear roles and responsibilities remain essential regardless of location; (C) is false, as co-located teams routinely also use digital tools.
