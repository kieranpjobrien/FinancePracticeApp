# Question

Which of the following would you expect to find documented in the communication management approach?

## Options

- **A**: The tolerances delegated to each management level
- **B**: The detailed product breakdown structure for the project
- **C**: The information needs of stakeholders and the methods used to communicate with them
- **D**: The risk responses for each identified threat and opportunity

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The communication management approach captures stakeholders' information needs, the formats and channels to be used, and the timing and frequency of communications. (A) tolerances are recorded in the project initiation documentation and stage plans. (B) the product breakdown structure belongs to planning. (D) risk responses live in the risk register, not the communication approach.
