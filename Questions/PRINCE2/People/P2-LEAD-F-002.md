# Question

Why does PRINCE2 7 place 'people' as a central element connecting the principles, practices and processes?

## Options

- **A**: Because only people can buy the software licences a project needs
- **B**: Because the project board prefers the method to focus on human resources administration
- **C**: Because projects are delivered by and for people, so leading and engaging them is essential to success
- **D**: Because people are listed as a performance target alongside time and cost

## Answer

C

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7 introduces 'people' as a central, integrating element precisely because projects are designed, delivered and used by people, making their leadership and engagement fundamental to delivering benefits. (A) trivialises the element to a procurement function. (C) it is a deliberate methodological emphasis, not a board preference. (D) the seven performance targets are time, cost, quality, scope, benefits, risk and sustainability; people is not one of them.
