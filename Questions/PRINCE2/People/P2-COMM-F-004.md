# Question

In PRINCE2 7, communication and stakeholder engagement are most closely associated with which part of the method?

## Options

- **A**: The seven principles
- **B**: The seven processes
- **C**: The project's quality management approach
- **D**: The central "People" element

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7 places People at the centre of the method, and leading, communicating and engaging stakeholders sits within this element. (A) the principles guide the whole method but are not where communication is detailed. (B) the processes describe the lifecycle activities rather than the People dimension. (C) the quality approach concerns product acceptance criteria, not stakeholder communication.
