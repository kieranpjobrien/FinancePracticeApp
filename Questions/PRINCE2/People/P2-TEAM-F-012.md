# Question

In PRINCE2 7, how should the project manager generally approach interpersonal conflict that arises within the team?

## Options

- **A**: Ignore it, since conflict always resolves itself once delivery pressure eases
- **B**: Address it constructively and early, treating well-managed differences as a potential source of better outcomes
- **C**: Immediately escalate every disagreement to the project board for a decision
- **D**: Remove anyone involved in a disagreement from the team without discussion

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7 expects the project manager to deal with interpersonal issues constructively and promptly, recognising that differences, when handled well, can sharpen thinking and improve results. (A) ignoring conflict typically lets it worsen; (C) routine escalation of every disagreement undermines the manager's own role and the board's time; (D) summarily removing people is disproportionate and damages the team.
