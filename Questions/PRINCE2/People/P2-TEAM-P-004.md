# Question

During a banking systems upgrade, the supplier's technical lead and the business representative repeatedly clash because each pushes only their own priorities, and design decisions are stalling. What is the BEST response from the project manager?

## Options

- **A**: Side with the business representative, since the business funds the project
- **B**: Let the supplier's technical lead decide alone, as they understand the technology best
- **C**: Bring the business, user and supplier interests together to find a solution that balances value, usability and feasibility
- **D**: Halt the project until the two individuals resolve the matter privately

## Answer

C

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7 stresses collaboration across the business, user and supplier interests so decisions balance value, fitness for use and what is feasible to deliver; the project manager should facilitate that balance. (A) and (B) each let one interest dominate, which PRINCE2 warns against; (D) abdicates the project manager's facilitation role and needlessly stops progress.
