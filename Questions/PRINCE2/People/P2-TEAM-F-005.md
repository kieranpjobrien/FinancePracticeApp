# Question

A widely used model of team development describes teams moving through which sequence of stages?

## Options

- **A**: Plan, do, check, act
- **B**: Initiating, directing, controlling, closing
- **C**: Forming, storming, norming, performing
- **D**: Define, design, deliver, deploy

## Answer

C

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7 draws on the well-established forming, storming, norming and performing model to explain how a group becomes an effective team over time. (A) is a continuous-improvement cycle, not a team-development model; (B) lists project lifecycle-style activities; (D) is a generic delivery sequence, none of which describe how team relationships mature.
