# Question

In PRINCE2 7, what is a co-located project team?

## Options

- **A**: A team whose members are dispersed across different locations and collaborate mainly through digital tools
- **B**: A team made up only of contractors from a single supplier
- **C**: A team that reports to two project boards at the same time
- **D**: A team whose members work together in the same physical location

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A co-located team is one whose members share the same physical workplace, which tends to support spontaneous communication and faster relationship-building. (A) describes a virtual team, the opposite arrangement; (B) describes a sourcing characteristic, not location; (C) describes a governance structure unrelated to where the team physically sits.
