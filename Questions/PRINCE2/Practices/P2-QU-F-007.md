# Question

Which of the following would you expect to find in a product description?

## Options

- **A**: The category weights and tolerances for every product in the project
- **B**: A summary of all quality activities planned and completed for the project
- **C**: The project board's delegated tolerances for time, cost and risk
- **D**: The product's purpose, composition, quality criteria, quality tolerances, quality methods and quality responsibilities

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A product description defines a single product, setting out its purpose, composition, derivation, format, quality criteria, quality tolerances, quality method and the quality responsibilities for producing and checking it. (A) is invented; (B) describes the quality register; (C) describes information held in the project initiation documentation and stage plans, not a product description.
