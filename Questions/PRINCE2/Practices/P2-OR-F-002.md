# Question

Which statement about the Executive role is correct?

## Options

- **A**: There is a single Executive who is ultimately accountable for the project's success
- **B**: The Executive may be shared between two people to balance workload
- **C**: The Executive is appointed by the Project Manager at the start of initiation
- **D**: The Executive represents the interests of the users on the project board

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PRINCE2 requires a single individual to act as Executive, ultimately accountable for the project and owner of the business case, ensuring a clear line of accountability. (B) is wrong because the Executive role must not be shared; (C) reverses the hierarchy — corporate, programme management or the customer appoints the Executive, who in turn appoints the Project Manager; (D) describes the Senior User, not the Executive.
