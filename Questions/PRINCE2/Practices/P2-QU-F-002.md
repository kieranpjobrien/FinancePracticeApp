# Question

Which statement BEST describes the quality management approach?

## Options

- **A**: It is a register that records every planned and completed quality activity during the project
- **B**: It lists the customer's quality expectations and the acceptance criteria for the final product
- **C**: It describes how quality will be managed on the project, including the procedures, techniques, standards and responsibilities
- **D**: It is the detailed specification of a single product, including its quality criteria and tolerances

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The quality management approach defines how quality will be managed across the project, covering the procedures, standards, tools, techniques and roles and responsibilities to be applied. (A) describes the quality register; (B) describes elements captured in the project product description; (D) describes an individual product description.
