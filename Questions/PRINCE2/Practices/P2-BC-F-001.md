# Question

What is the purpose of the Business Case practice in PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: To define the quality standards the project's products must meet
- **B**: To plan how the project's work will be broken into management stages
- **C**: To assign roles and responsibilities to members of the project management team
- **D**: To establish mechanisms to judge whether the project is, and remains, desirable, viable and achievable

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The Business Case practice provides the means to judge whether the project is desirable, viable and achievable, and so worth the continuing investment. (A) is the Quality practice; (B) is the Plans practice; (C) is part of the Organizing practice. The Business Case is the running justification that supports continued business justification throughout the project.
