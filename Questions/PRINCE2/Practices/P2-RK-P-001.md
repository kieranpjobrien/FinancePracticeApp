# Question

During a stage of a hospital IT rollout, a team member tells the project manager about a credible threat that a key software licence may not be renewed in time, which could delay go-live. The threat is not yet in the risk register. What should the project manager do FIRST?

## Options

- **A**: Immediately escalate the situation to the project board as an exception
- **B**: Capture the threat in the risk register so it can be assessed and a response planned
- **C**: Wait until the next stage boundary to record the threat with any others
- **D**: Ask the team member to resolve the licensing issue without recording it

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The PRINCE2 risk procedure begins with identifying and recording the risk in the risk register so it can then be assessed and responses planned. (A) escalation as an exception is premature before the risk has even been assessed against tolerance. (C) delaying recording risks losing the information and breaches continual risk management, and (D) bypasses the risk register entirely and assigns work informally.
