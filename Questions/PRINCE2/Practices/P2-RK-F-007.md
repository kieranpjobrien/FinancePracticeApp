# Question

Which response to an opportunity seeks to make the opportunity definitely happen so that its benefit is realised?

## Options

- **A**: Explore
- **B**: Enhance
- **C**: Exploit
- **D**: Reject

## Answer

C

## Explanation

"Exploit" seizes an opportunity to ensure that it occurs and the benefit is realised in full. (A) "explore" investigates whether an apparent opportunity is real and how it might be captured, (B) "enhance" increases the probability or impact of an opportunity that is already possible, and (D) "reject" is the conscious decision not to pursue it.
