# Question

PRINCE2 7 describes four things that are done to the business case across a project. Which set correctly lists them?

## Options

- **A**: Draft, approve, archive and audit
- **B**: Identify, assess, plan and review
- **C**: Define, design, deliver and decommission
- **D**: Develop, verify, maintain and confirm

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The business case is developed (made into a suitable framework), verified (assessed as worthwhile), maintained (kept up to date), and confirmed (benefits are assessed) across the project life cycle. (A), (B) and (C) borrow plausible verbs from records management, risk and delivery but are not the PRINCE2 business case activities. Together these four describe a living justification, not a one-off document.
