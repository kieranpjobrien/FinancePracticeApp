# Question

Risk is one of the seven performance targets that PRINCE2 7 projects must keep in tolerance. Which statement correctly reflects how PRINCE2 treats risk across the project?

## Options

- **A**: Risk is identified once during initiation and then fixed for the life of the project
- **B**: Risks are managed informally and need not be recorded unless they become issues
- **C**: Only the project board may add entries to the risk register
- **D**: Risk management is a continual activity, with the risk register kept up to date throughout the project

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7 treats risk management as a continual cycle of identifying, assessing, planning, implementing and communicating, with the risk register maintained throughout the project. (A) is wrong because new risks arise continually and existing ones change. (C) is incorrect because anyone may raise a risk, subject to the approach, and (B) contradicts the requirement to record and actively manage risks rather than wait for them to become issues.
