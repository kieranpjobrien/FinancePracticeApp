# Question

A project team assesses a threat after planned response actions have been taken into account. What term describes the level of risk that remains?

## Options

- **A**: Residual risk
- **B**: Inherent risk
- **C**: Secondary risk
- **D**: Aggregated risk

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Residual risk is the risk remaining after responses have been applied. (B) inherent risk is the exposure before any response is taken. "Secondary risk" (C) refers to a new risk arising directly from a response, and "aggregated risk" (D) refers to the combined effect of several individual risks, neither of which describes the post-response level of the original threat.
