# Question

A project takes out an insurance policy so that a third party will pay for losses if a particular threat occurs. Which threat response does this BEST illustrate?

## Options

- **A**: Reduce
- **B**: Prepare contingent plans
- **C**: Share
- **D**: Transfer

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Insurance is the classic example of "transfer", where a third party bears some or all of the financial impact of a threat, although the threat itself still exists. (C) "share" involves both parties carrying a proportion of the risk and any gain or loss under an agreed formula, not a one-way payout. (A) "reduce" would lower probability or impact directly, and (B) "prepare contingent plans" sets out actions to take only if the threat occurs.
