# Question

Midway through a community solar project, the project manager learns that a new government grant might become available that could fund extra battery storage and increase the project's benefits. It is not yet certain the grant will apply. What is the BEST initial response?

## Options

- **A**: Reject the opportunity because the grant is uncertain
- **B**: Exploit the opportunity by committing now to install the extra storage
- **C**: Explore the opportunity to establish whether the grant is real and how it could be captured
- **D**: Record it only as a threat to the current budget

## Answer

C

## Explanation

When an opportunity is identified but its existence or extent is uncertain, the appropriate first response is "explore" — investigating whether the opportunity is genuine and how it might be captured before committing resources. (A) "reject" prematurely discards a potentially valuable benefit, (B) "exploit" commits resources before the opportunity is confirmed, and (D) mischaracterises a positive opportunity as a threat.
