# Question

What is the purpose of a Change Authority in PRINCE2?

## Options

- **A**: To deliver products to the agreed quality within a work package
- **B**: To approve every project initiation document before work begins
- **C**: To review and approve or reject requests for change and off-specifications within delegated limits set by the project board
- **D**: To audit the project board's decisions on behalf of corporate management

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The project board may delegate consideration of changes to a Change Authority, which approves or rejects requests for change and off-specifications within limits the board has set, avoiding overloading the board with minor issues. (A) describes the Team Manager's delivery role; (B) is not the Change Authority's purpose — initiation is authorised by the board; (D) wrongly casts the role as an external auditor rather than a delegated change decision-maker.
