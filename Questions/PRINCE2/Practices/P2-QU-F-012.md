# Question

In a PRINCE2 quality review technique, what is the role of the reviewers?

## Options

- **A**: To chair the review and ensure the procedure is followed correctly
- **B**: To assess the product against its quality criteria and identify any errors or questions
- **C**: To own the product and decide whether it has passed
- **D**: To record the actions agreed and the overall result of the review

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Reviewers assess the product against its agreed quality criteria and raise any errors, omissions or questions they find. (A) is the role of the chair; (C) describes the presenter or producer who owns the product; (D) is performed by the administrator who documents the review's outcome and actions.
