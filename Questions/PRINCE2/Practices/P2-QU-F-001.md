# Question

What is the purpose of the Quality practice in PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: To define the project's approach to identifying and controlling threats and opportunities
- **B**: To establish the structures that delegate authority and responsibility within the project
- **C**: To divide the project into management stages for control by the project board
- **D**: To ensure the project's products meet expectations and are fit for use, enabling the intended benefits

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The Quality practice exists to ensure the project's products meet business expectations and are fit for purpose, so the intended benefits can be realised. (A) describes the Risk practice; (B) describes the Organizing practice; (C) describes the use of management stages within the Plans and Progress practices.
