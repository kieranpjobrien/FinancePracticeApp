# Question

On a wind-farm construction project, a specialist subcontractor will deliver the turbine installation but has no understanding of PRINCE2 reporting. The Project Manager wants clear control over their output. What is the BEST organisational response?

## Options

- **A**: Promote the subcontractor's lead to the project board as a Senior Supplier
- **B**: Remove formal reporting for this team to avoid confusing the subcontractor
- **C**: Have the Project Manager personally supervise every installation task on site
- **D**: Appoint a Team Manager to take on the work package and report progress to the Project Manager through agreed checkpoints

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The Team Manager role exists precisely to manage the production of products in a work package and to report progress to the Project Manager, providing control over a specialist team. (A) confuses delivery-level management with board-level direction; (C) is impractical and bypasses the proper delivery layer; (B) removes the controls PRINCE2 relies on, leaving progress unmanaged.
