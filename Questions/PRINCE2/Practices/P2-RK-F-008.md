# Question

In PRINCE2 7, what distinguishes a risk owner from a risk actionee?

## Options

- **A**: The risk owner is responsible for managing and monitoring the risk, while the actionee carries out the assigned response action(s)
- **B**: The risk owner carries out the response actions while the actionee monitors the risk
- **C**: The risk owner and the risk actionee are always the same person
- **D**: The risk owner approves the risk budget while the actionee approves the response

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The risk owner is the named individual responsible for the management, monitoring and control of an assigned risk, whereas the risk actionee carries out a specific response action on the owner's behalf. (B) reverses the two roles. (C) is wrong because they may be the same person but need not be, and (D) invents budget-approval duties that belong to the project board, not these roles.
