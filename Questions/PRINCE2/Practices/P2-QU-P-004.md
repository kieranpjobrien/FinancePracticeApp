# Question

A renewable-energy project is about to start building a substation control panel. The assigned engineer asks the project manager how the panel will be checked and who is responsible for approving it. Where should this information already be defined?

## Options

- **A**: In the panel's product description, under its quality method and quality responsibilities
- **B**: In the quality register, which lists the checks once they are completed
- **C**: In the supplier's contract, as PRINCE2 does not address quality responsibilities
- **D**: In the highlight report the project manager sends to the project board

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The quality method and the quality responsibilities for producing, reviewing and approving a product are defined in that product's product description during quality planning. (B) the quality register records activities and their results but does not originate the responsibilities; (C) PRINCE2 explicitly places quality responsibilities in the product description; (D) the highlight report communicates progress, not product-level quality definitions.
