# Question

A risk actionee on an airport baggage-system project completes the response action he was assigned and notices the threat's likelihood has now fallen sharply. The risk owner is a different person. What should the actionee do?

## Options

- **A**: Update the risk register entry and re-assess the risk himself, since he carried out the action
- **B**: Inform the risk owner so the owner can review the risk and update its status as appropriate
- **C**: Close the risk immediately, as the response action is complete
- **D**: Escalate to the project manager that the risk owner is no longer needed

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The risk actionee carries out the assigned action but accountability for managing, monitoring and updating the risk stays with the risk owner, so the actionee should report progress and findings to the owner. (A) usurps the owner's responsibility for re-assessing and maintaining the entry. (C) closing the risk is the owner's judgement, not an automatic result of completing one action, and (D) wrongly assumes a single completed action removes the need for an owner.
