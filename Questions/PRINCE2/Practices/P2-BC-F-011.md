# Question

The benefits performance target is one of the seven PRINCE2 7 performance targets. What does setting tolerance against this target allow the project board to do?

## Options

- **A**: Define the permissible variation in expected benefits before the justification must be re-examined
- **B**: Define how much the project's products may deviate from their quality criteria
- **C**: Define the contingency held against identified risks
- **D**: Define the acceptable slippage in the project schedule

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Benefits tolerance states the permissible deviation in expected benefits, and breaching it signals that the business justification may no longer hold and must be reviewed. (B) is quality tolerance; (C) relates to the risk target and budget; (D) is time tolerance. Treating benefits as a controllable performance target keeps the focus on value, not only on delivery.
