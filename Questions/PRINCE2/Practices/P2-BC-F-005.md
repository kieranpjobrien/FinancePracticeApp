# Question

What is the primary purpose of the benefits management approach?

## Options

- **A**: To record the actions required to ensure the project's benefits are measured and confirmed
- **B**: To list every product the project will create and their quality criteria
- **C**: To document the project's stage boundaries and tolerances
- **D**: To capture the lessons learned from previous projects

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The benefits management approach defines how and when the project's benefits will be measured, including responsibilities and the baseline against which improvement is judged. (B) is the project product description and product descriptions; (C) relates to plans and tolerances; (D) is the lessons log or lessons report. It often outlives the project itself, since many benefits are realised only after closure.
