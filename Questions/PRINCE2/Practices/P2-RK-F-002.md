# Question

What is the purpose of the risk register in PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: To record and maintain information about all identified threats and opportunities relating to the project
- **B**: To define the project's overall approach to managing risk
- **C**: To record all issues, including requests for change and off-specifications
- **D**: To capture lessons that may be useful for future projects

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The risk register is the management product that records and maintains information about all the risks (threats and opportunities) identified for the project. (B) describes the risk management approach, which sets out the procedure and tools rather than the individual records. (C) describes the issue register, and (D) describes the lessons log.
