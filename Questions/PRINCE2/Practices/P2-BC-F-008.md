# Question

Which PRINCE2 principle is most directly supported by maintaining the business case throughout the project?

## Options

- **A**: Manage by stages
- **B**: Focus on products
- **C**: Ensure continued business justification
- **D**: Tailor to suit the project

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Maintaining the business case is the mechanism that delivers the principle of continued business justification, ensuring the project still makes sense at each decision point. (A) manage by stages provides the review points but is not the justification itself; (B) focus on products concerns deliverables and quality; (D) tailoring adapts the method to context. If justification disappears, PRINCE2 requires the project to be stopped.
