# Question

PRINCE2 describes four levels of management within and around a project. Which option lists them correctly from highest to lowest?

## Options

- **A**: Corporate/programme management/the customer; directing; managing; delivering
- **B**: Directing; managing; assuring; supporting
- **C**: Executive; Senior User; Senior Supplier; Project Manager
- **D**: Strategic; tactical; operational; administrative

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The four levels of management are corporate, programme management or the customer (which sits outside the project), then directing (project board), managing (Project Manager) and delivering (Team Manager). (B) and (D) invent labels that PRINCE2 does not use for the levels; (C) lists individual roles rather than the management levels.
