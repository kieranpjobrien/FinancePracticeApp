# Question

What is the purpose of the communication management approach?

## Options

- **A**: To describe how and when issues and risks will be escalated to corporate management
- **B**: To define how the project will quality-control its products
- **C**: To describe the means and frequency of communication with the project's stakeholders, in both directions
- **D**: To record the roles and responsibilities of the project management team

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The communication management approach describes the means, format and frequency of communication to and from stakeholders, ensuring information flows in both directions throughout the project. (A) describes elements of issue and risk management; (B) is the quality management approach; (D) is captured in role descriptions within the project management team structure, not the communication approach.
