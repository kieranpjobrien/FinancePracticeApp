# Question

What is the primary purpose of a change budget in PRINCE2?

## Options

- **A**: To fund the cost of correcting defects found during quality reviews only
- **B**: To provide a contingency reserve for responding to risks that materialise
- **C**: To pay for the management products required to run the project
- **D**: To set aside money the change authority can use to fund approved requests for change

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A change budget is a sum set aside to fund the cost of requests for change, allowing the change authority to approve changes without repeatedly returning to the project board for extra funding. (A) is too narrow and conflates defects with the broader change budget. (B) describes a risk budget, a distinct provision. (C) is simply part of normal project cost, not the change budget.
