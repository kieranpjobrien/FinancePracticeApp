# Question

The Executive owns the business case, but which role is accountable for specifying and subsequently realising the project's benefits?

## Options

- **A**: The Senior Supplier
- **B**: The Team Manager
- **C**: The Project Manager
- **D**: The Senior User

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The Senior User specifies the benefits and is accountable for ensuring they are realised once the project's products are in use. (A) the Senior Supplier is accountable for the quality of what is delivered, not the benefits gained from using it; (B) the Team Manager produces specialist products; (C) the Project Manager delivers products but does not realise benefits. Separating ownership of the case (Executive) from benefits realisation (Senior User) keeps accountabilities clear.
