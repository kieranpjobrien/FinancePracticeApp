# Question

Which statement best distinguishes a register from a log in PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: A register is informal and maintained by the team manager, whereas a log is formal and owned by the project board
- **B**: A register is a formal repository managed by the project manager, whereas a log is a more informal record often maintained by the project manager for their own use
- **C**: A register records only completed actions, whereas a log records only forecasts
- **D**: A register is time-driven and a log is event-driven

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Registers (such as the risk register, issue register and quality register) are formal repositories used for control, while logs (such as the daily log and lessons log) are more informal records, typically kept by the project manager. (A) reverses the formality and ownership; (C) and (D) invent distinctions that do not apply to registers and logs.
