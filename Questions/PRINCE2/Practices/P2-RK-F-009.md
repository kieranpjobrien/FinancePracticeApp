# Question

What is a risk budget in PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: The total cost of the project, including all stage budgets
- **B**: The maximum cost overrun the project board will tolerate before closing the project
- **C**: A reserve held by the project manager to pay for approved changes
- **D**: A sum of money set aside to fund specific management responses to the project's threats and opportunities

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A risk budget is money set aside specifically to fund risk responses, including contingent actions, for the project's threats and opportunities. (A) describes the overall project budget. (C) describes a change budget, which funds requests for change, and (B) describes cost tolerance rather than a dedicated risk fund.
