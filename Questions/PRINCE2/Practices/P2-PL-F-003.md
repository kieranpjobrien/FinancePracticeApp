# Question

In the product-based planning technique, what does the project product description define?

## Options

- **A**: The breakdown of all products into a hierarchy
- **B**: The sequence in which products must be developed
- **C**: The overall product the project must deliver, including its purpose, composition and acceptance criteria
- **D**: The detailed quality checks for each individual specialist product

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The project product description describes the overall output of the project, including its purpose, composition, derivation and the customer's quality expectations and acceptance criteria. (A) describes the product breakdown structure. (B) describes the product flow diagram. (D) describes the individual product descriptions, not the project product description.
