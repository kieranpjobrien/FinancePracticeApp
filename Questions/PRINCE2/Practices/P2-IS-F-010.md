# Question

When carrying out impact analysis on an issue, PRINCE2 advises that the impact should be considered from which three perspectives?

## Options

- **A**: Time, cost and quality
- **B**: Strengths, weaknesses and opportunities
- **C**: Business, user and supplier
- **D**: Probability, proximity and impact

## Answer

C

## Explanation

PRINCE2 recommends assessing the impact of an issue from the business, user and supplier perspectives, mirroring the three project interests so that no stakeholder group's concerns are missed. (A) lists performance targets, which form part of the assessment but are not the three perspectives. (B) is the basis of a SWOT analysis, unrelated here. (D) lists attributes used to assess risks, not issue impact.
