# Question

Which of the following best describes the role of the Senior Supplier on the project board?

## Options

- **A**: Represents the interests of those designing, developing and delivering the project's products
- **B**: Represents those who will use the project's products day to day
- **C**: Holds the budget and is accountable for value for money
- **D**: Provides administrative support to the Project Manager

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The Senior Supplier represents the supplier interest, accountable for the quality of the products delivered and for providing the supplier resources and expertise required. (B) is the Senior User; (C) is the Executive, who owns value for money and the business case; (D) is Project Support.
