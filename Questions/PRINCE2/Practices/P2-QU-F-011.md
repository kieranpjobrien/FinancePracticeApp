# Question

What does quality control refer to in PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: The activities of checking products against their quality criteria and managing any products that do not meet them
- **B**: The planning of how quality will be achieved across the whole project
- **C**: The board's responsibility for assuring the ongoing viability of the project
- **D**: The independent function that confirms the project follows organisational standards

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Quality control covers the activities used to inspect and test products against their defined quality criteria and to deal with products that fail to meet them. (B) describes quality planning; (C) describes project assurance; (D) describes quality assurance, which sits outside the project.
