# Question

A customer and supplier agree that any cost saving or cost overrun arising from an opportunity will be split between them according to an agreed formula. Which opportunity response does this represent?

## Options

- **A**: Enhance
- **B**: Exploit
- **C**: Share
- **D**: Explore

## Answer

C

## Explanation

"Share" applies to both threats and opportunities and involves the parties carrying a proportion of the risk, with gains or losses divided under an agreed formula such as a pain/gain arrangement. (A) "enhance" increases the probability or impact of an opportunity, (B) "exploit" makes the opportunity certain to occur, and (D) "explore" investigates whether the opportunity is genuine before deciding how to respond.
