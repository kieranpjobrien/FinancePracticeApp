# Question

A completed product fails to meet one of its agreed quality criteria. Which type of issue does this represent?

## Options

- **A**: A request for change
- **B**: A problem or concern only
- **C**: A risk
- **D**: An off-specification

## Answer

D

## Explanation

An off-specification is a product that does not, or is forecast not to, meet its agreed specification or quality criteria. (A) a request for change is a proposal to alter an agreed baseline; (B) a problem or concern is a general issue that is neither a change nor an off-specification; (C) a risk is an uncertain future event, not a confirmed failure to meet criteria.
