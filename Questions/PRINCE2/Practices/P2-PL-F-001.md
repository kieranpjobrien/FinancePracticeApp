# Question

What is the purpose of the project plan in PRINCE2?

## Options

- **A**: To provide a high-level plan covering the whole project, used by the project board as a baseline to measure actual progress
- **B**: To provide a detailed day-to-day plan for the work of a single team during one management stage
- **C**: To set out the activities required to recover from a forecast breach of stage tolerance
- **D**: To document only the products to be created during the initiation stage

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The project plan is a high-level plan for the whole project, providing the business case with planned costs and timescales and giving the project board a baseline against which to monitor progress stage by stage. (B) describes a team plan, which covers one team's work within a stage. (C) describes an exception plan. (D) is far too narrow; the project plan spans the entire project, not just initiation.
