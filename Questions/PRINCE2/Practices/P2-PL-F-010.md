# Question

Which statement about team plans in PRINCE2 is correct?

## Options

- **A**: A team plan is mandatory for every work package
- **B**: A team plan is created by the project board to authorise a stage
- **C**: A team plan replaces the stage plan once a stage begins
- **D**: A team plan is optional and is created by a team manager to plan the execution of one or more work packages

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Team plans are optional in PRINCE2 and are produced by a team manager to plan the work needed to deliver one or more work packages. (A) team plans are not mandatory; their use depends on project size and complexity. (B) the project board authorises stages but does not create team plans. (C) a team plan supplements, and does not replace, the stage plan.
