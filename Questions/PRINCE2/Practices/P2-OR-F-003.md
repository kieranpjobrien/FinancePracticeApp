# Question

What is the primary responsibility of the Senior User?

## Options

- **A**: To secure the funding and own the business case
- **B**: To provide the resources that will build the project's products
- **C**: To specify the needs of those who will use the products and to confirm the products meet those needs
- **D**: To manage the day-to-day work of the delivery teams

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The Senior User represents the user interest, specifying user needs, ensuring the solution meets them, and monitoring that the expected benefits are realised. (A) describes the Executive who owns the business case; (B) describes the Senior Supplier who provides delivery resources; (D) describes the Team Manager.
