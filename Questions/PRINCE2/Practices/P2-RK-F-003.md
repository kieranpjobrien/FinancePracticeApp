# Question

Which management product defines the procedures, techniques and roles to be used for managing risk on a project?

## Options

- **A**: The risk register
- **B**: The project initiation documentation summary
- **C**: The business case
- **D**: The risk management approach

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The risk management approach describes how risk will be managed on the project, including the procedure, techniques, tools, roles and responsibilities, risk tolerances and reporting. (A) the risk register holds the individual risk records, not the procedure. (C) the business case justifies the project, and (B) is not a defined PRINCE2 management product.
