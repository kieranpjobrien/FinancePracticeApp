# Question

When the business case is verified in PRINCE2 7, what is actually being assessed?

## Options

- **A**: Whether every product description has been signed off by the relevant authority
- **B**: Whether the project team has the right skills to deliver the work
- **C**: Whether the project is still worthwhile, balancing benefits, costs, time and risks
- **D**: Whether the quality management approach complies with corporate standards

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Verifying the business case means assessing whether the project remains worthwhile by weighing benefits, dis-benefits, costs, timescales and risks together. (A) concerns the Quality practice; (B) relates to Organizing and resourcing; (D) is governed by the quality management approach. Verification happens repeatedly, notably at end-stage assessments, not just once at the outset.
