# Question

What is the purpose of the Progress practice in PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: To establish mechanisms to monitor and compare actual achievements against those planned, provide a forecast for the project objectives, and control any unacceptable deviations
- **B**: To identify, assess and control uncertainty and so improve the ability of the project to succeed
- **C**: To define and deliver the project's products, ensuring they meet the agreed quality criteria
- **D**: To establish mechanisms to judge whether the project is, and remains, desirable, viable and achievable

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The Progress practice provides mechanisms to monitor and compare actual achievements against the plan, forecast the project objectives, and control unacceptable deviations. (B) describes the Risk practice; (C) describes the Quality practice; (D) describes the purpose of the business case and is assessed through the continued business justification principle.
