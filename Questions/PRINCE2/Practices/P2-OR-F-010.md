# Question

In the Organizing practice, which activity directly informs the content of the communication management approach?

## Options

- **A**: Defining the project's stage boundaries
- **B**: Identifying and analysing the project's stakeholders and their information needs
- **C**: Producing the end project report
- **D**: Estimating the cost of each work package

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Stakeholder identification and analysis determines who needs to be communicated with, what information they need and how, which feeds directly into the communication management approach. (A) relates to controlling stages and managing boundaries; (C) is a closing-process product; (D) belongs to planning rather than to stakeholder communication.
