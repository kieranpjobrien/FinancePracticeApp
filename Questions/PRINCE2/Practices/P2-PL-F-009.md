# Question

In PRINCE2 planning, what is the difference between estimating and scheduling?

## Options

- **A**: Estimating determines how long activities will take and what resources are needed; scheduling arranges those activities against a timeline considering dependencies and resource availability
- **B**: Estimating arranges activities against a calendar; scheduling predicts the effort each activity requires
- **C**: Estimating and scheduling are interchangeable terms for setting tolerances
- **D**: Estimating is performed only by the project board; scheduling is performed only by team managers

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Estimating predicts the time, effort and resources needed for the identified activities, and scheduling then places those activities on a timeline taking account of dependencies and resource availability. (B) reverses the two definitions. (C) neither relates to setting tolerances. (D) both are planning activities led by whoever is producing the plan, not split rigidly between those roles.
