# Question

PRINCE2 classifies issues into how many types, and what are they?

## Options

- **A**: Two types: requests for change and off-specifications
- **B**: Three types: risk, issue, and lesson
- **C**: Four types: change, defect, query, and escalation
- **D**: Three types: request for change, off-specification, and problem/concern

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7 recognises exactly three issue types: a request for change, an off-specification, and a problem/concern. (A) omits the problem/concern category entirely. (B) confuses issue types with broader management concepts; a risk is not an issue. (C) invents categories that are not part of PRINCE2 terminology.
