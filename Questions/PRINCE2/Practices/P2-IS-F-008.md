# Question

What is the role of a change authority in PRINCE2?

## Options

- **A**: A person or group to whom the project board delegates authority to approve or reject requests for change and off-specifications
- **B**: The team manager responsible for implementing every approved change at work-package level
- **C**: The body that sets the corporate change-management policy that the project must follow
- **D**: The project assurance role that audits whether change control has been followed correctly

## Answer

A

## Explanation

A change authority is a person or group to whom the project board may delegate the decision to approve or reject requests for change and off-specifications, usually within a defined change budget. (B) confuses approving changes with implementing them. (C) describes organisational or programme-level governance, not the project change authority. (D) describes project assurance, which checks but does not authorise changes.
