# Question

What is the primary purpose of the quality register?

## Options

- **A**: To summarise, for audit and control purposes, all the quality activities that are planned or have taken place
- **B**: To record the customer's quality expectations agreed at the start of the project
- **C**: To capture lessons about quality for use on future projects
- **D**: To list the acceptance criteria against which the final product will be approved

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The quality register provides a summary of all planned and completed quality management activities, giving an audit trail and supporting control and reporting. (B) belongs in the project product description; (C) is the role of the lessons log and lessons report; (D) records acceptance criteria, which sit in the project product description, not the quality register.
