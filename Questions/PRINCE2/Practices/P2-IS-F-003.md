# Question

What is the purpose of the issue register in PRINCE2?

## Options

- **A**: To capture and maintain information on all the issues that are being managed formally
- **B**: To record the daily, informal notes the project manager keeps for personal use
- **C**: To list every risk and its planned response throughout the project
- **D**: To record the quality checks that have been carried out on each product

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The issue register is the management product used to capture and maintain information on all issues being handled formally, giving the project manager a complete record. (B) describes the daily log, used for informal issues and notes. (C) describes the risk register. (D) describes the quality register.
