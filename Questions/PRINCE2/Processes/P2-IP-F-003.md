# Question

Which four management approaches are created during the Initiating a Project process in PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: Risk, quality, change and communication management approaches
- **B**: Risk, quality, benefits and sustainability management approaches
- **C**: Quality, change, configuration and procurement management approaches
- **D**: Risk, change, communication and benefits management approaches

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Initiating a Project creates the four management approaches: the risk, quality, change and communication management approaches, each describing how that aspect of the project will be handled. (B) and (D) wrongly substitute the benefits or sustainability management approach for one of the four; the benefits management approach is created separately within initiation but is not one of the four. (C) introduces configuration and procurement approaches, which are not the named four.
