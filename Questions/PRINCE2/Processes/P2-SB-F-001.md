# Question

What is the primary purpose of the Managing a Stage Boundary process?

## Options

- **A**: To deliver the products of a single work package to the agreed quality
- **B**: To provide the project board with information to decide whether to continue with the project
- **C**: To define the project mandate and appoint the project management team
- **D**: To capture and assess lessons at the close of the project

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Managing a Stage Boundary provides the project board with the information it needs to review the success of the current stage, approve the next stage plan, and confirm continued business justification — supporting a go/no-go decision. (A) describes Managing Product Delivery; (C) describes Starting Up a Project; (D) relates to Closing a Project.
