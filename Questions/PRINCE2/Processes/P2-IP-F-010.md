# Question

Which statement about the quality management approach created in Initiating a Project is correct?

## Options

- **A**: It records the individual quality criteria for every product the project will deliver
- **B**: It describes how quality will be managed across the project, including the quality control activities and responsibilities
- **C**: It is the same document as the project product description
- **D**: It lists the products that have already passed their quality checks

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The quality management approach describes how quality will be managed throughout the project, covering the quality control and quality assurance activities, the techniques to be used, and the associated responsibilities. (A) Individual quality criteria belong in each product description, not the approach. (C) The project product description is a distinct product created earlier, defining the project's overall output. (D) A record of completed quality checks would be a quality register, not the approach.
