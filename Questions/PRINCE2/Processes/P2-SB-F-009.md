# Question

When Managing a Stage Boundary is triggered to produce an exception plan, what does that plan do?

## Options

- **A**: Plans the entire remaining project from scratch
- **B**: Replaces the plan that is forecast to breach its tolerance, covering the period from the present to the end of that plan
- **C**: Documents the lessons from the exception for the next project
- **D**: Authorises additional funds without project board approval

## Answer

B

## Explanation

An exception plan is produced to replace a plan that is forecast to exceed tolerance; it covers the time from the present moment to the end of the plan being replaced, and is submitted to the board for approval. (A) overstates its scope; (C) confuses it with a lessons report; (D) is wrong because the board, not the plan, authorises the change.
