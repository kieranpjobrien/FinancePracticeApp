# Question

A team reports that a delivered product fails to meet its agreed quality criteria. How should this issue be classified in Controlling a Stage?

## Options

- **A**: As a request for change
- **B**: As an off-specification
- **C**: As a problem or concern only
- **D**: As a risk

## Answer

B

## Explanation

An off-specification is something that should be provided but currently is not, or a product that does not meet its specification, such as failing its quality criteria. (A) A request for change is a proposal to alter an agreed baseline, not a failure to meet it. (C) Problem/concern is a third issue type for matters that are neither a change nor an off-specification. (D) A risk is an uncertain future event; this has already occurred, so it is an issue.
