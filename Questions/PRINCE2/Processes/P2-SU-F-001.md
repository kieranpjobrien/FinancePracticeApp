# Question

What is the purpose of the Starting Up a Project process in PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: To deliver the project's products in a controlled series of management stages
- **B**: To ensure the prerequisites for initiating a project are in place by answering whether the project is worthwhile and viable
- **C**: To authorise the project and commit the resources required to deliver it
- **D**: To produce a detailed Project Plan and refined Business Case ready for delivery

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Starting Up a Project is the pre-project process that establishes whether the project is worthwhile and viable before any significant investment is made, ensuring the prerequisites for initiation exist. (A) describes the delivery work managed through Controlling a Stage and Managing Product Delivery. (C) is the responsibility of the project board in Directing a Project, which authorises initiation and the project. (D) describes outputs of Initiating a Project, not Starting Up.
