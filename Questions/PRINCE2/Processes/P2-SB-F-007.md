# Question

Why is the risk register reviewed and updated as part of Managing a Stage Boundary?

## Options

- **A**: To close the project once all risks have occurred
- **B**: To ensure the board's go/no-go decision reflects the project's current risk exposure
- **C**: To transfer ownership of all risks to the team manager for the next stage
- **D**: To remove the need for a risk management approach

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Updating the risk register at the stage boundary ensures the project board's decision to proceed is informed by the latest threats, opportunities and exposure, consistent with the Risk practice. (A) confuses this with closure; (C) misassigns risk ownership; (D) is wrong because the risk management approach continues to govern how risks are handled.
