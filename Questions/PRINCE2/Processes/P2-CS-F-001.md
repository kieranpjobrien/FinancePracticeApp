# Question

What is the purpose of the Controlling a Stage process in PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: To authorise the project and commit the resources needed to deliver it
- **B**: To assign work to the project board and confirm the business justification remains valid
- **C**: To assign work to be done, monitor that work, deal with issues, report progress and take corrective action to keep the stage within its tolerances
- **D**: To establish solid foundations before committing to significant project spend

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Controlling a Stage is where the project manager performs day-to-day management of a stage: assigning and monitoring work, dealing with issues and risks, reporting progress and taking corrective action to keep the stage within agreed tolerances. (A) and (D) describe Directing a Project and Initiating a Project respectively. (B) misattributes work assignment to the project board, whose role is direction and decision-making, not day-to-day control.
