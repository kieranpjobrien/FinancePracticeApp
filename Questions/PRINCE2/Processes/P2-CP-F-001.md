# Question

What is the purpose of the Closing a Project process in PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: To deliver the project's specialist products to the agreed quality standards
- **B**: To provide a fixed point at which acceptance of the project product is confirmed and to recognise that objectives have been achieved or that the project has nothing further to contribute
- **C**: To decide whether the project remains viable at each stage boundary
- **D**: To establish whether the project is worthwhile before any delivery work begins

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Closing a Project provides a controlled, fixed point at which acceptance of the project product is confirmed and the project is recognised as having achieved its objectives (or as having nothing further to contribute). (A) Delivery of specialist products happens in Managing Product Delivery. (C) Reviewing continued viability at a boundary is Managing a Stage Boundary. (D) Confirming the project is worthwhile before delivery is the role of Starting Up a Project.
