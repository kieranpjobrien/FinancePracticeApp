# Question

When a new risk is identified during Controlling a Stage, where should the project manager record it?

## Options

- **A**: In the issue register
- **B**: In the daily log only
- **C**: In the highlight report
- **D**: In the risk register

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Identified risks are captured and assessed in the risk register, in line with the Risk practice, so they can be tracked and managed throughout the stage. (A) The issue register holds issues, not risks. (B) The daily log is for informal notes and minor actions, not the formal record of an assessed risk. (C) The highlight report communicates status but is not the register where risks are formally recorded.
