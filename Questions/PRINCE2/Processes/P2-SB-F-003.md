# Question

Which management product reports on the performance of the management stage that is ending?

## Options

- **A**: Checkpoint report
- **B**: Highlight report
- **C**: End stage report
- **D**: Lessons report

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The end stage report is produced during Managing a Stage Boundary to give the project board a summary of the stage's performance against its plan and to support the decision to authorise the next stage. (A) is produced by the team manager during Managing Product Delivery; (B) is the project manager's regular report to the board during a stage; (D) captures lessons and is not the stage performance summary itself.
