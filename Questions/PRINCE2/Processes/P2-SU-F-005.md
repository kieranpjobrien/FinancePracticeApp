# Question

What is prepared regarding the Business Case during Starting Up a Project?

## Options

- **A**: The outline Business Case
- **B**: The detailed Business Case, fully costed and approved
- **C**: The Benefits Management Approach
- **D**: The final benefits review confirming value delivered

## Answer

A

## Explanation

During Starting Up a Project the Executive prepares the outline Business Case, giving just enough justification to decide whether the project is worth initiating. (B) The detailed Business Case is developed later, during Initiating a Project. (C) The Benefits Management Approach is also created during initiation, not start-up. (D) A benefits review confirming value occurs during or after delivery, typically post-project, and is far beyond the scope of start-up.
