# Question

The Directing a Project process most directly supports which PRINCE2 principle when the board reconfirms justification at each stage boundary?

## Options

- **A**: Learn from experience
- **B**: Ensure continued business justification
- **C**: Tailor to suit the project
- **D**: Focus on products

## Answer

B

## Explanation

When the board authorises each stage it reviews the updated business case to confirm the project remains desirable, viable and achievable, directly upholding "ensure continued business justification". (A) Learn from experience is supported through lessons but is not what a stage-boundary justification check primarily serves. (C) Tailoring concerns adapting the method, not the go/no-go decision. (D) Focus on products concerns defining and agreeing products, not the ongoing justification review.
