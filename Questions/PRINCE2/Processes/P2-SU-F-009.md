# Question

Which key question does Starting Up a Project set out to answer?

## Options

- **A**: Have the project's products been accepted by the users?
- **B**: Is the current stage still within its agreed tolerances?
- **C**: Have all the planned benefits been realised?
- **D**: Do we have a worthwhile and viable project?

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Starting Up a Project exists to answer "do we have a worthwhile and viable project?" before committing to the larger effort of initiation. (A) Acceptance of products is confirmed during Closing a Project. (B) Whether a stage remains within tolerance is monitored during Controlling a Stage. (C) Benefits realisation is assessed during and after delivery, well beyond the pre-project phase.
