# Question

What does the communication management approach define in PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: How and when the project will engage with and inform its stakeholders
- **B**: How the project's products will be reviewed and approved for quality
- **C**: How issues and changes will be captured, assessed and decided
- **D**: How threats and opportunities will be identified and controlled

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The communication management approach describes how and when the project will send and receive information to and from stakeholders, supporting effective engagement. (B) is the quality management approach. (C) is the change management approach. (D) is the risk management approach. Each approach addresses a distinct aspect of how the project will be managed.
