# Question

Which of the following would you expect a work package to define so the team manager understands the boundaries of the work?

## Options

- **A**: The project board's appointment of the executive and the project manager
- **B**: The tolerances, constraints, reporting arrangements and the quality methods to be applied to the products
- **C**: The full business justification and the expected return on investment
- **D**: The detailed lessons gathered from previous projects

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A work package sets out the products required together with their tolerances, constraints, reporting (such as checkpoint frequency) and the quality methods and approvals needed, so the team manager knows precisely what is being agreed. (A) appointments are made in Starting up a Project and Directing a Project. (C) business justification sits in the business case. (D) lessons are captured separately in the lessons log and report, not within the work package itself.
