# Question

Who is primarily responsible for carrying out the activities of Managing a Stage Boundary?

## Options

- **A**: The project board
- **B**: The team manager
- **C**: The project manager
- **D**: Project assurance

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The project manager performs the Managing a Stage Boundary activities — preparing the end stage report, the next stage (or exception) plan, and updating the project plan, business case and risk register — then submits them to the board. (A) reviews and authorises rather than performs the work; (B) delivers products at the work-package level; (D) advises on assurance but does not own the process.
