# Question

During execute a work package, how does the team manager handle the quality of the products being developed?

## Options

- **A**: Quality checks are deferred until the work package is delivered
- **B**: The team manager carries out the quality activities specified in the work package and records the results, updating the quality register
- **C**: Quality is assured solely by the project board before delivery
- **D**: The project manager performs all quality checks on the team's behalf

## Answer

B

## Explanation

When executing a work package the team manager develops the products and ensures the quality methods specified are applied, with results captured and the quality register kept up to date. (A) quality checking happens during execution, not only at delivery. (C) the project board does not perform product-level quality checks. (D) the team, not the project manager, performs the work-package quality activities.
