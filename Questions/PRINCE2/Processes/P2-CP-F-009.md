# Question

What must be confirmed before the project's products are handed over to those who will operate, maintain and support them?

## Options

- **A**: That the operational and maintenance organisation is ready to receive the products and that acceptance has been obtained
- **B**: That all post-project benefits have already been measured and realised
- **C**: That the next project in the programme has been authorised
- **D**: That the project budget has been fully spent

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Handover requires confirmation that acceptance has been obtained and that the receiving operational and maintenance environment is ready to take on the products, including any support arrangements. (B) Post-project benefits are measured later under the benefits review plan and need not precede handover. (C) Authorising another project is unrelated to handing over these products. (D) Budget being fully spent is not a condition of acceptance or handover.
