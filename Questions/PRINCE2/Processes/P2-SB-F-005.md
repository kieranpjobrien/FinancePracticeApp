# Question

Which management product is updated during Managing a Stage Boundary to confirm the project remains worthwhile?

## Options

- **A**: The business case
- **B**: The work package
- **C**: The communication management approach
- **D**: The product description

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The business case is reviewed and updated at each stage boundary so the project board can confirm that continued business justification still exists before committing to the next stage. (B) governs delegated work, not the project's justification; (C) and (D) are baselined products that are not the vehicle for reconfirming viability at a stage boundary.
