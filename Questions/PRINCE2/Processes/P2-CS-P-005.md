# Question

On a wind-farm construction project, a team manager's checkpoint report warns that a key turbine supplier may enter administration. The project manager judges this could delay the stage but it has not yet happened. What should the project manager do?

## Options

- **A**: Record it as an off-specification in the issue register
- **B**: Ignore it until the supplier actually fails
- **C**: Immediately replan the entire project
- **D**: Capture it as a risk, assess it in the risk register and identify a suitable response

## Answer

D

## Explanation

An uncertain future event that could affect objectives is a risk, so it is captured and assessed in the risk register and an appropriate response is planned, in line with the Risk practice. (A) An off-specification is a current failure of a product, not a future uncertainty. (B) Ignoring a credible threat abandons proactive risk management. (C) A full replan is premature before the risk has been assessed and a proportionate response chosen.
