# Question

During start-up of a hospital records digitisation project, the Project Manager has drafted an outline justification estimating high benefits but is unsure who should own the business justification. Who is accountable for the outline Business Case?

## Options

- **A**: The Project Manager, as author of the start-up documents
- **B**: The Senior User, who will realise the benefits
- **C**: The Executive
- **D**: Corporate management, who commissioned the project

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The Executive is accountable for the Business Case throughout the project, including preparing the outline Business Case during Starting Up a Project, ensuring the project remains justified. (A) The Project Manager may help draft and assemble it but is not accountable for the justification. (B) The Senior User specifies and is accountable for benefits being realised, but not for the Business Case itself. (D) Corporate management provides the mandate and sets the context, yet ownership of the project's justification rests with the Executive.
