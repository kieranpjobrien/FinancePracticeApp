# Question

Who is primarily responsible for carrying out the activities of the Initiating a Project process?

## Options

- **A**: The project board, supported by project assurance
- **B**: The project manager, on behalf of the project board
- **C**: The team managers delivering the specialist products
- **D**: The Executive, acting alone as the project's sponsor

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Initiating a Project is led by the project manager, who creates the management approaches, project plan, refined business case and PID on behalf of the project board. (A) The project board authorises initiation and later approves the PID, but does not perform the initiation activities; assurance monitors rather than executes. (C) Team managers operate during delivery, not initiation. (D) The Executive owns the business case but does not single-handedly carry out initiation.
