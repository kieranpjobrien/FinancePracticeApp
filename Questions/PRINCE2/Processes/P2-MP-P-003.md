# Question

While building a mobile banking feature, a development team manager completes the coding and an internal test reveals two defects, which the team fixes and retests successfully. What should the team manager ensure happens next within execute a work package?

## Options

- **A**: The defects and their resolution are recorded and the quality register is updated to reflect the quality activities performed
- **B**: The product is delivered immediately without recording the test results, to save time
- **C**: A request for change is raised because defects were found
- **D**: The project board is asked to approve the corrected code

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Execute a work package requires quality checks to be carried out and their outcomes captured, with the quality register kept up to date as evidence the products meet their criteria. (B) skipping the records loses the audit trail of quality. (C) fixing defects against existing acceptance criteria is normal execution, not a change request. (D) product-level approval rests with those named in the work package, not the project board.
