# Question

In which TWO circumstances is the Managing a Stage Boundary process triggered?

## Options

- **A**: At the start of every work package and when a risk materialises
- **B**: Near the end of a management stage and when an exception plan is required
- **C**: When the project mandate is received and when the project is authorised
- **D**: At every daily stand-up and at every project board meeting

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Managing a Stage Boundary runs near the end of each management stage (except the final one) and is also invoked when the project board requests an exception plan in response to a stage or project being forecast to exceed tolerance. (A), (C) and (D) describe other processes or routine events that do not trigger this process.
