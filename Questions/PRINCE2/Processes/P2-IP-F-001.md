# Question

What is the purpose of the Initiating a Project process in PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: To establish whether the project is worthwhile and viable before any commitment is made
- **B**: To establish solid foundations for the project, enabling the organisation to understand the work needed before committing to significant spend
- **C**: To assign work to teams and ensure products are built to the agreed quality
- **D**: To authorise the project and commit the resources required to deliver it

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Initiating a Project establishes solid foundations so that everyone understands what the project must achieve, why, how, when and by whom before the organisation commits to significant expenditure on delivery. (A) describes Starting Up a Project, the pre-project process. (C) describes the delivery work of Controlling a Stage and Managing Product Delivery. (D) is the project board's role in Directing a Project.
