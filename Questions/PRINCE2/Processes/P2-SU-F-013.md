# Question

The project definition within the Project Brief sets out the project's objectives, scope, constraints and which of the following?

## Options

- **A**: The detailed work packages for the first delivery stage
- **B**: The exact budget tolerances for each management stage
- **C**: The project's interfaces and the people or parties with an interest in it
- **D**: The confirmed acceptance records signed by the Senior User

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The project definition explains what the project needs to achieve and includes objectives, desired outcomes, scope and exclusions, constraints and assumptions, the project's interfaces, and the parties or stakeholders with an interest. (A) Work packages are detailed during delivery, not in the Project Brief. (B) Stage budget tolerances are set during planning and recorded in stage plans, not the project definition. (D) Acceptance records are completed at the end of the project during closure, not during start-up.
