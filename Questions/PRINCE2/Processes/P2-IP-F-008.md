# Question

A project manager needs to define how requests for change and off-specifications will be captured, logged and decided. Which management approach addresses this?

## Options

- **A**: The quality management approach
- **B**: The risk management approach
- **C**: The change management approach
- **D**: The communication management approach

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The change management approach describes how issues — including requests for change and off-specifications — will be identified, assessed and controlled, and it defines any change authority and budget. (A) The quality management approach concerns how product quality is planned and checked. (B) The risk management approach deals with uncertainty. (D) The communication management approach governs stakeholder information flows, not issue and change control.
