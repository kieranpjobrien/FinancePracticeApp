# Question

PRINCE2 7 recognises two circumstances under which Closing a Project may be triggered. What are they?

## Options

- **A**: Planned closure and premature closure
- **B**: Scheduled closure and budget closure
- **C**: Voluntary closure and forced closure
- **D**: Stage closure and project closure

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Closing a Project can be invoked either as a planned closure, when the project reaches its natural end, or as a premature closure, when the project board decides to stop the project early. (B), (C) and (D) use labels that PRINCE2 7 does not define; in particular a stage end is handled by Managing a Stage Boundary, not by Closing a Project.
