# Question

What is the purpose of follow-on action recommendations prepared during Closing a Project?

## Options

- **A**: To record unfinished work, open risks and issues so they can be dealt with after the project ends
- **B**: To authorise the next management stage of the project
- **C**: To confirm that the business case still justifies continued investment
- **D**: To set the tolerances for the final delivery stage

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Follow-on action recommendations pass on any outstanding work, open issues and unaddressed risks to whoever will support or maintain the products after closure, so nothing is lost. (B) Authorising a stage is a Directing a Project decision. (C) Justifying continued investment is the role of the business case at a boundary, not at closure. (D) Setting stage tolerances belongs to stage planning, not project closure.
