# Question

What is the purpose of the Managing Product Delivery process in PRINCE2 7?

## Options

- **A**: To control the link between the project manager and the project board by authorising stages
- **B**: To control the link between the project manager and the team manager(s) by agreeing the requirements for acceptance, execution and delivery of work packages
- **C**: To plan the entire project before any specialist work begins
- **D**: To assess whether the project remains worthwhile and viable at the end of each stage

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Managing Product Delivery controls the link between the project manager and the team manager(s), placing formal requirements on the accepting, executing and delivering of work packages. (A) describes the role of Controlling a Stage and Directing a Project, not product delivery. (C) is closer to Initiating a Project. (D) describes Managing a Stage Boundary and the project board's authorisation activity.
