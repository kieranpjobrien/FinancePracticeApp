# Question

What does "design and appoint the project management team" achieve in Starting Up a Project?

## Options

- **A**: It assigns delivery work packages to the team members who will build the products
- **B**: It produces the organisation chart for the host company's permanent structure
- **C**: It appoints only the Project Support and Project Assurance roles, as the board is already in place
- **D**: It identifies the people for the project management team roles and confirms their availability and acceptance

## Answer

D

## Explanation

This activity identifies who should fill the project management team roles, considers the skills and authority needed, and confirms the candidates are available and willing to take the roles, reflecting the central People element of PRINCE2 7. (A) Assigning work packages happens during delivery, not start-up. (B) The project management team structure is project-specific and temporary, not the organisation's permanent hierarchy. (C) The whole team is designed, including the wider project board roles, not just Support and Assurance.
