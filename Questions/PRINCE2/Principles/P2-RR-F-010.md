# Question

How does PRINCE2 7 strengthen the treatment of relationships compared with simply allocating roles?

## Options

- **A**: It removes the concept of roles entirely and relies on relationships alone
- **B**: It requires that all relationships be documented in the risk register
- **C**: It mandates that every relationship be governed by a formal contract
- **D**: It emphasises that effective projects depend on people, their behaviours and the relationships between them, not just defined roles

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7, through its central People element, stresses that project success depends on people, their behaviours and the relationships between them, complementing the formal definition of roles and responsibilities. (A) Roles are retained; relationships add to them. (C) Relationships are not recorded in the risk register. (D) Formal contracts apply to some supplier arrangements, not to every working relationship.
