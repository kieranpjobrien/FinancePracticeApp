# Question

"Ensure continued business justification" is one of the seven PRINCE2 principles. Which statement about the PRINCE2 principles is correct?

## Options

- **A**: Principles are optional guidelines that a project may choose to adopt
- **B**: Principles are universal and apply to every PRINCE2 project without exception
- **C**: A project can omit some principles and still be called a PRINCE2 project
- **D**: Principles apply only to large or high-risk projects

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The PRINCE2 principles are universal, self-validating and empowering, and all seven must be applied for a project to be a genuine PRINCE2 project. (A) and (C) are wrong because principles are mandatory, not optional or selectable. (D) is incorrect — they apply regardless of project size, which is why "Ensure continued business justification" holds even for the smallest project.
