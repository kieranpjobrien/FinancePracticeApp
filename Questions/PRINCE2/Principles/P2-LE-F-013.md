# Question

During the running of a PRINCE2 project, how should the lessons log be used?

## Options

- **A**: It is locked after initiation and reopened only at project closure
- **B**: It is replaced by the daily log once delivery begins
- **C**: It is updated as new lessons emerge, and those lessons inform ongoing decisions
- **D**: It is maintained by the project board and hidden from the delivery team

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The lessons log is a living document: as the project runs, new lessons are added and acted upon to improve the remaining work. (A) the log stays open throughout the project, not just at the ends. (B) the daily log and lessons log serve different purposes and coexist. (D) lessons are shared, not concealed, because everyone is responsible for acting on them.
