# Question

A project board member is most concerned that the delivered products will be fit for purpose and will achieve the intended benefits when used. Which project interest does this member primarily represent?

## Options

- **A**: The business interest
- **B**: The supplier interest
- **C**: The corporate interest
- **D**: The user interest

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The user interest is concerned with those who will use the project's products, so its focus is that products are fit for purpose and deliver the intended benefits in use, represented by the senior user. (A) The business interest focuses on value for money and the investment case. (C) The supplier interest focuses on the resources and expertise needed to create the products. (D) Corporate, programme or customer management sits above the project and is not one of the three interests.
