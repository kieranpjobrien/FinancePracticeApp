# Question

The "define roles, responsibilities and relationships" principle stresses "engaging the right people". What does this primarily mean?

## Options

- **A**: Recruiting as many stakeholders as possible to maximise input
- **B**: Allowing the supplier to nominate all members of the project board
- **C**: Restricting involvement to those employed by the customer organisation
- **D**: Ensuring people with the appropriate authority, responsibilities and competence are involved in the right roles

## Answer

D

## Explanation

Engaging the right people means ensuring those with the necessary authority, responsibilities and competence are placed in the appropriate roles so the project can be directed and delivered effectively. (A) More people does not mean the right people; involvement must be purposeful. (C) Board membership must balance all three interests, not be set by one party. (D) Suppliers and other external parties are frequently and legitimately involved.
