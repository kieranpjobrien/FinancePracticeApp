# Question

What is the main purpose of the PRINCE2 principle "define roles, responsibilities and relationships"?

## Options

- **A**: To ensure everyone involved knows who is accountable and responsible, and how they relate to one another
- **B**: To produce a detailed organisation chart for the whole performing organisation
- **C**: To guarantee that the project is delivered on time and within budget
- **D**: To remove the need for a project board by sharing decisions across the team

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The principle exists so that people engaged in the project understand who is accountable and responsible and how the working relationships operate, which underpins effective decision-making and direction. (B) is too broad — PRINCE2 defines the project management team, not the entire organisation. (C) describes performance targets, an outcome the principle supports but does not directly state. (D) is wrong because PRINCE2 retains the project board for accountability.
