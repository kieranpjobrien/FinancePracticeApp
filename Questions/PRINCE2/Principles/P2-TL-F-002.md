# Question

What is a project allowed to tailor when applying the "tailor to suit the project" principle?

## Options

- **A**: The seven PRINCE2 principles
- **B**: The processes, practices, roles, management products and terminology
- **C**: Whether or not a business case is required
- **D**: Whether the project needs continued business justification

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Tailoring may be applied to the processes, practices (themes), roles, management products and terminology so that PRINCE2 fits the project context. (A) is wrong because the principles are universal and must always be applied in full; (C) and (D) are wrong because a justifiable business case and continued business justification are themselves principles and management essentials that cannot be tailored away.
