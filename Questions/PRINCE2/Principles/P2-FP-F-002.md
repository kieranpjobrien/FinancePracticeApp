# Question

What is the purpose of a product description in PRINCE2?

## Options

- **A**: To record the actual costs incurred in building a product
- **B**: To define what a product must look like, its purpose and its quality criteria
- **C**: To list the project team members responsible for sign-off
- **D**: To capture lessons learned from delivering the product

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A product description defines a product's purpose, composition, derivation, format, and the quality criteria and quality method against which it will be assessed. (A) Actual costs are tracked elsewhere, not in the product description. (C) Responsibilities such as the producer and approver are noted, but the core purpose is defining the product and its quality. (D) Lessons are captured in a lessons log or report, not a product description.
