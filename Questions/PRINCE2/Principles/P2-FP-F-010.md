# Question

A completed product fails to meet one of the quality criteria stated in its product description. How is this best classified in PRINCE2?

## Options

- **A**: A request for change
- **B**: A new product description
- **C**: A project mandate
- **D**: An off-specification

## Answer

D

## Explanation

An off-specification is a product that does not meet its agreed specification, such as a stated quality criterion, or is forecast not to meet it. (A) A request for change asks to alter an agreed baseline, which is different from failing to meet it. (B) and (C) are management products, not classifications of an issue.
