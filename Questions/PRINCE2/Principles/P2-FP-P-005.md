# Question

A project manager on a vaccine cold-chain logistics project has a complete list of the products to be delivered but the team keeps starting products in the wrong order, causing rework. Which product-based planning output should the project manager produce to fix this?

## Options

- **A**: A product breakdown structure
- **B**: A separate risk register for each product
- **C**: A communication management approach
- **D**: A product flow diagram showing the sequence and dependencies of the products

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A product flow diagram shows the order in which products must be created and their dependencies, which directly addresses products being started in the wrong sequence. (A) A product breakdown structure lists the products hierarchically but does not show sequence. (B) and (C) are useful management products but do not define creation order.
