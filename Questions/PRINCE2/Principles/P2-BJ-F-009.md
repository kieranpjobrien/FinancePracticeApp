# Question

In PRINCE2, who is accountable for ensuring that the project remains justified throughout its life?

## Options

- **A**: The project manager
- **B**: The senior supplier
- **C**: The project assurance function
- **D**: The executive

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The executive owns the Business Case and is accountable for the project's continued business justification, supporting the principle. (A) prepares and maintains the Business Case on the executive's behalf but is not accountable for the justification itself. (B) represents supplier interests, not the business viewpoint. (C) provides independent assurance but does not hold the accountability.
