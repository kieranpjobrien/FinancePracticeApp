# Question

Within the "desirable, viable and achievable" test, which question does "achievable" address?

## Options

- **A**: Will the outcome address a real business need or opportunity?
- **B**: Do the benefits outweigh the costs and risks of doing the project?
- **C**: Can the project actually deliver the products and the resulting outcomes?
- **D**: Has the senior user confirmed they will accept the products?

## Answer

C

## Explanation

"Achievable" asks whether the project is genuinely capable of delivering the required products and the outcomes that follow from them. (A) relates to "desirable" — whether there is a genuine need. (B) relates to "viable" — the balance of benefits, costs and risks. (D) describes a user acceptance responsibility, which supports but is not the definition of achievability.
