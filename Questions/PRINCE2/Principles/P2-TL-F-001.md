# Question

Which of the following BEST describes the PRINCE2 principle "tailor to suit the project"?

## Options

- **A**: PRINCE2 should be applied identically on every project to ensure consistency
- **B**: PRINCE2 principles may be removed when they do not fit the project context
- **C**: PRINCE2 should be adapted to suit the project's environment, size, complexity, importance, team capability and risk
- **D**: Only the documentation, not the method itself, may be tailored

## Answer

C

## Explanation

The "tailor to suit the project" principle ensures PRINCE2 is adapted to the project's environment, size, complexity, importance, team capability and risk so the method fits the project rather than the project being forced to fit the method. (A) is the opposite of tailoring; (B) is wrong because tailoring never means omitting principles; (D) is too narrow as tailoring extends to practices, processes, roles and terminology, not just documents.
