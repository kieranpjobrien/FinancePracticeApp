# Question

Which statement BEST explains why the seven principles cannot be tailored while the practices and processes can?

## Options

- **A**: The principles are optional guidance, so changing them has no effect
- **B**: The principles are too simple to be worth adjusting
- **C**: The practices and processes are more important than the principles
- **D**: The principles are the universal characteristics that define whether a project is genuinely being managed using PRINCE2

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The seven principles are the universal, always-true elements that define a PRINCE2 project; remove any of them and the project is no longer being managed with PRINCE2, which is why they are not subject to tailoring. (A) wrongly calls the principles optional; (B) trivialises them; (C) inverts the relationship, as the principles underpin the practices and processes rather than being subordinate to them.
