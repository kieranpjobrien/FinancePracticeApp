# Question

A project board agrees that the project must keep its operational carbon emissions within a stated band. Within manage by exception, this band is an example of a tolerance set against which performance target?

## Options

- **A**: Quality
- **B**: Scope
- **C**: Sustainability
- **D**: Benefits

## Answer

C

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7 adds sustainability as a performance target, so a permitted emissions band is a sustainability tolerance. (A) Quality tolerance concerns acceptable variation in a product's specification, not environmental impact. (B) Scope tolerance concerns the products to be delivered. (D) Benefits tolerance concerns the acceptable range of outcomes the project enables, not its environmental footprint.
