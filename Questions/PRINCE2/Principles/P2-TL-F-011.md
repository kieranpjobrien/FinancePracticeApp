# Question

Which of the following is a legitimate example of tailoring a PRINCE2 management product?

## Options

- **A**: Combining several management products into a single document for a small project
- **B**: Removing the requirement for any form of business justification
- **C**: Deciding the project no longer needs defined roles and responsibilities
- **D**: Allowing the project to proceed with no agreed tolerances

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Tailoring management products can include combining or splitting them, for example merging several products into one document where that suits a small project. (B) breaches the continued business justification principle; (C) breaches the defined roles, responsibilities and relationships principle; (D) breaches manage by exception, which depends on agreed tolerances, so all three are tailoring out essentials rather than legitimate tailoring.
