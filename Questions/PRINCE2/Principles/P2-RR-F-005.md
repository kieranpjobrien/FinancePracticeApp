# Question

PRINCE2 7 introduced a central integrating element that sits at the heart of the method and is closely linked to defining roles, responsibilities and relationships. What is it called?

## Options

- **A**: The People element
- **B**: The Governance theme
- **C**: The Stakeholder practice
- **D**: The Leadership principle

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PRINCE2 7 places the "People" element at the centre of the method, recognising that successful projects depend on engaging, leading and managing the right people and their relationships. (B) PRINCE2 7 uses "practices", not "themes", and has no Governance theme. (C) There is no standalone "Stakeholder practice" — stakeholder engagement is part of the method but not the central element. (D) "Leadership" is not one of the seven principles.
