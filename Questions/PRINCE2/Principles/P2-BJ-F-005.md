# Question

Which PRINCE2 management product is the primary place where the business justification is documented?

## Options

- **A**: The Project Product Description
- **B**: The Risk Register
- **C**: The Highlight Report
- **D**: The Business Case

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The "Ensure continued business justification" principle is realised through the Business Case practice, and the justification is documented in the Business Case management product. (A) defines the project's main product and its acceptance criteria, not the justification. (B) records threats and opportunities. (C) is a regular progress report from the project manager to the project board.
