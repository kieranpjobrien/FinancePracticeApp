# Question

Why does PRINCE2 include 'learn from experience' as one of its seven principles?

## Options

- **A**: Because regulators require a documented lessons process for every project
- **B**: Because organisations often repeat mistakes that earlier projects had already overcome
- **C**: Because it removes the need to manage risk separately on the project
- **D**: Because it guarantees the project will finish within its time and cost targets

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The principle exists because projects frequently repeat avoidable mistakes; deliberately seeking and acting on lessons reduces this. (A) PRINCE2 is not driven by a specific regulatory mandate. (C) learning complements but does not replace the manage-by-risk practice. (D) learning improves the chances of success but cannot guarantee performance targets.
