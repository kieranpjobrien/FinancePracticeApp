# Question

'Learn from experience' is one of how many PRINCE2 principles, and what is a defining feature of all PRINCE2 principles?

## Options

- **A**: One of seven principles, and they are universal, self-validating and empowering
- **B**: One of five principles, and they apply only to large, high-risk projects
- **C**: One of seven practices, and they describe management products to be produced
- **D**: One of four principles, and they may be tailored away if inconvenient

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PRINCE2 has seven principles, including 'learn from experience', and the principles are described as universal (apply to every project), self-validating (proven over time) and empowering. (B) there are seven, not five, and they apply to all projects. (C) confuses principles with the seven practices. (D) a project is only PRINCE2 if all principles are applied; they are not tailored away.
