# Question

Which statement best describes the PRINCE2 principle "Ensure continued business justification"?

## Options

- **A**: A project must have a justification that is documented and remains valid throughout the project's life
- **B**: A project must deliver its products within the agreed time and cost tolerances
- **C**: A project must define accountability and responsibility for everyone involved
- **D**: A project must be broken into manageable and controllable management stages

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The principle "Ensure continued business justification" requires that there is a justifiable reason to start the project, that this reason is documented and approved, and that it remains valid and is revalidated throughout. (B) describes managing by tolerances and the performance targets, not justification. (C) is the basis for defined roles and responsibilities. (D) describes managing by stages.
