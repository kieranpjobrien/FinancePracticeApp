# Question

Under the "Ensure continued business justification" principle, how often should the business justification be reviewed?

## Options

- **A**: Only once, when the project is first authorised
- **B**: Only when a request for change is raised
- **C**: Only at project closure when the benefits are confirmed
- **D**: Continually throughout the life of the project, and at least at every stage boundary

## Answer

D

## Explanation

PRINCE2 requires the justification to be confirmed continually and reviewed at key decision points, in particular at every stage boundary before the next stage is authorised. (A) is wrong because justification can change and must be revalidated. (B) and (C) are too narrow — a single trigger or only closure would allow an unviable project to continue undetected.
