# Question

The 'focus on products' principle most directly supports the management of which of the seven PRINCE2 performance targets?

## Options

- **A**: Scope
- **B**: Sustainability
- **C**: Risk
- **D**: Benefits

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Because a project's scope is defined by its products, agreeing those products clearly is the foundation for managing the scope performance target and controlling scope creep. (B) Sustainability, (C) risk and (D) benefits are also performance targets, but they are not the one defined by the set of products; the principle's clearest link is to scope.
