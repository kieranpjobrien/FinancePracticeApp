# Question

How does focusing on products help prevent disputes at the point of acceptance?

## Options

- **A**: It allows the supplier to define acceptance after delivery
- **B**: It removes the customer from quality decisions
- **C**: It records agreed quality criteria and acceptance criteria before work begins
- **D**: It defers all quality checks until the final stage

## Answer

C

## Explanation

By defining quality criteria in product descriptions and acceptance criteria in the project product description before work begins, both parties share an objective basis for accepting deliverables. (A) Defining acceptance after delivery is precisely what causes disputes. (B) The customer remains central to setting expectations. (D) Quality is checked throughout, not deferred to the end.
