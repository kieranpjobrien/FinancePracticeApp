# Question

What is the minimum number of management stages required in a PRINCE2 project?

## Options

- **A**: One
- **B**: Two
- **C**: Three
- **D**: Four

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PRINCE2 requires a minimum of two management stages: the initiation stage plus at least one further (delivery) stage. (A) A single stage would leave the project board with no go/no-go control point after initiation. (C) and (D) Three or four stages may be appropriate for larger projects, but they are not the mandated minimum.
