# Question

Which statement BEST describes the PRINCE2 principle 'learn from experience'?

## Options

- **A**: Project teams learn from experience, so lessons are sought, recorded and acted upon throughout the life of the project
- **B**: Lessons are reviewed only once, during the closing of the project, to inform future projects
- **C**: Only the project manager is responsible for capturing lessons learned on a project
- **D**: Experience is gathered exclusively from external benchmarking organisations, not from internal sources

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The 'learn from experience' principle states that PRINCE2 project teams learn from experience: lessons are sought, recorded and acted upon throughout the life of the project. (B) lessons must be sought at the start, during delivery and at close, not only once. (C) everyone involved is responsible for seeking and acting on lessons, not the project manager alone. (D) lessons come from previous projects, the current project and external sources alike.
