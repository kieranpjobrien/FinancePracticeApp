# Question

Why must all three project interests be represented on the project board, rather than just the business interest?

## Options

- **A**: To ensure decisions balance value for money, fitness for use and what can realistically be delivered
- **B**: To increase the size of the board so that meetings carry more weight
- **C**: Because the business interest is the least important of the three
- **D**: To allow the supplier to outvote the customer on commercial matters

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Representing business, user and supplier interests ensures decisions are balanced across value for money, fitness for use and feasibility of delivery, preventing one perspective from dominating. (B) Board membership is about balanced representation, not size for its own sake. (C) No interest is inherently more or less important; they are complementary. (D) The board makes consensus decisions under the executive; it is not a voting contest between parties.
