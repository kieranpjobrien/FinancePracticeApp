# Question

Product-based planning in PRINCE2 begins with which of the following activities?

## Options

- **A**: Identifying and defining the products required
- **B**: Estimating the effort for each activity
- **C**: Scheduling activities onto a Gantt chart
- **D**: Assigning team members to work packages

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Product-based planning starts by identifying and defining the required products before identifying the activities and dependencies needed to create them. (B), (C), and (D) all describe activity-level steps that come later in the planning sequence, once the products and their order of creation are understood.
