# Question

Which option correctly pairs a PRINCE2 level of management with its primary responsibility under manage by exception?

## Options

- **A**: The project board directs by setting stage tolerances and responding to exceptions; the project manager manages day-to-day within those tolerances
- **B**: Corporate, programme or customer management manages the project day to day; the project board delivers the products
- **C**: The team manager directs the project; the project board creates the products
- **D**: The project manager sets the project tolerances; corporate management writes the work packages

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The project board sits at the directing level — setting stage tolerances and deciding on exceptions — while the project manager operates at the managing level, controlling work day to day within tolerance. (B) reverses the directing and delivering roles. (C) inverts the team manager and board responsibilities. (D) Project tolerances come from corporate, programme or customer management, and work packages are produced by the project manager, not the reverse.
