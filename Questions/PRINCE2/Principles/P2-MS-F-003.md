# Question

In PRINCE2, what is the primary purpose of a stage boundary?

## Options

- **A**: To reset all project tolerances to zero
- **B**: To allow team members to take annual leave between phases
- **C**: To hand the project over permanently to operations
- **D**: To act as a go/no-go decision point where the project board decides whether to continue

## Answer

D

## Explanation

A stage boundary is a control point at which the project board reviews progress and the next stage plan, then makes a go/no-go decision on whether to continue, change or stop the project. (A) Tolerances are set afresh for the next stage but are not "reset to zero". (B) Stage boundaries are decision points, not scheduled breaks. (C) Permanent handover to operations occurs at project closure, not every boundary.
