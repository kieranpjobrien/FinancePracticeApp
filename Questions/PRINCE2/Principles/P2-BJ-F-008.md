# Question

How does the "Ensure continued business justification" principle relate to the Business Case practice?

## Options

- **A**: The principle replaces the need for the Business Case practice
- **B**: The principle is applied in practice through the Business Case, which develops and maintains the justification
- **C**: The Business Case practice applies only at project start, whereas the principle applies only at closure
- **D**: The principle and the Business Case practice are unrelated parts of the method

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The principle is the "why"; the Business Case practice is the "how", providing the activities and the Business Case product that develop, maintain and confirm the justification across the project. (A) is wrong — principles and practices work together, not as substitutes. (C) misstates the timing; both operate throughout the project. (D) is incorrect because they are directly linked.
