# Question

Which benefit is most directly associated with applying the 'focus on products' principle?

## Options

- **A**: It guarantees the project will finish ahead of schedule
- **B**: It removes the need for a project board
- **C**: It reduces scope creep and acceptance disputes
- **D**: It eliminates all project risk

## Answer

C

## Explanation

By agreeing clearly defined products and quality criteria up front, the 'focus on products' principle reduces scope creep and the likelihood of disputes over whether deliverables are acceptable. (A) and (D) are overstated; the principle does not guarantee early delivery or remove risk. (B) The project board remains essential regardless of the product focus.
