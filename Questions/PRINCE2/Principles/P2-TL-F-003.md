# Question

What is the primary purpose of tailoring PRINCE2 to suit the project?

## Options

- **A**: To reduce the amount of project documentation as much as possible
- **B**: To allow the project manager to skip the processes they find least useful
- **C**: To standardise every project in the organisation around one fixed approach
- **D**: To ensure the method relates to the project's environment and that controls are based on the project's scale, complexity, importance, capability and risk

## Answer

D

## Explanation

The purpose of tailoring is to ensure the method relates to the project's environment and that project controls are appropriate to its scale, complexity, importance, capability and risk. (A) confuses an occasional side effect with the purpose, since tailoring can increase as well as reduce rigour; (B) is wrong because tailoring is a deliberate decision, not licence to skip useful processes; (C) describes embedding a fixed corporate method rather than tailoring to a specific project.
