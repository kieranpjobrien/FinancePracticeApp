# Question

Midway through a stage of a solar-farm construction project, the project manager forecasts that cost overruns will erode the benefits so much that the project may no longer be worthwhile. The forecast breaches stage tolerance. What is the BEST response?

## Options

- **A**: Raise an Exception Report to the project board, highlighting the threat to the business justification
- **B**: Absorb the overrun by cutting product quality to stay within tolerance
- **C**: Wait until the end of the stage and note the issue in the End Stage Report
- **D**: Continue without escalation because cost is the supplier's problem, not the bank's

## Answer

A

## Explanation

A forecast breach of tolerance that threatens justification must be escalated promptly via an Exception Report so the project board can decide, consistent with manage by exception and continued business justification. (B) sacrifices quality and conceals the real problem. (C) delays a decision that is needed now and lets an unviable project run on. (D) ignores the project manager's duty to protect the justification regardless of who bears the cost.
