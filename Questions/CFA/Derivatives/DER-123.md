# Question

A payer swaption gives the holder the right to:

## Options

- **A**: Receive fixed and pay floating in an interest rate swap
- **B**: Pay fixed and receive floating in an interest rate swap
- **C**: Terminate an existing swap early

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Swaption basics:

**Payer swaption:**
- Right to PAY fixed rate
- Receive floating rate
- Benefits if rates rise
- Like a call on rates

**Receiver swaption:**
- Right to RECEIVE fixed rate
- Pay floating rate
- Benefits if rates fall
- Like a put on rates

**Exercise decision:**
- Exercise if swap is valuable
- Payer: Exercise when market rates > strike rate
- Receiver: Exercise when market rates < strike rate

**Uses:**
- Hedge future borrowing needs
- Protect against rate movements
- Corporate treasury applications

**Comparison:**
- Payer swaption ≈ Call on interest rates
- Receiver swaption ≈ Put on interest rates
- Or: Payer ≈ Put on bond, Receiver ≈ Call on bond

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: This describes receiver swaption
- Option C: This is swap termination, different product
