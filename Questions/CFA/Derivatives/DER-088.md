# Question

The option premium is paid:

## Options

- **A**: At the time of exercise
- **B**: At the time the option is purchased
- **C**: Only if the option expires in the money

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Option premium payment:

**Timing:**
- Paid upfront when option bought
- Non-refundable
- Regardless of outcome

**Premium represents:**
- Price of the option right
- Time value + Intrinsic value
- Maximum loss for buyer

**For buyer:**
- Pays premium immediately
- Receives rights
- No further obligation

**For seller (writer):**
- Receives premium immediately
- Takes on obligations
- Premium is profit if option expires worthless

**After purchase:**
- Premium paid is sunk cost
- Exercise decision based on intrinsic value
- Don't consider premium already paid

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Premium paid at purchase, not exercise
- Option C: Premium paid regardless of outcome
