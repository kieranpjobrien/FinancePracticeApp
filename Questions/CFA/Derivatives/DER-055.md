# Question

The delta of a call option is:

## Options

- **A**: Always negative
- **B**: Between 0 and 1
- **C**: Always greater than 1

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Call option delta:

**Delta range:**
- Minimum: 0 (deep OTM)
- Maximum: 1 (deep ITM)
- ATM: approximately 0.5

**Interpretation:**
- Change in option price per $1 change in stock
- Hedge ratio
- Approximate probability of finishing ITM

**Delta behavior:**

*Deep out-of-the-money:*
- Delta near 0
- Option barely responds to stock moves
- Low probability of exercise

*At-the-money:*
- Delta around 0.5
- Moderate sensitivity
- About 50% chance of exercise

*Deep in-the-money:*
- Delta near 1
- Moves dollar-for-dollar with stock
- High probability of exercise

**Put delta:**
- Ranges from -1 to 0
- Relationship: Put delta = Call delta - 1

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Calls have positive delta
- Option C: Maximum is 1 for calls
