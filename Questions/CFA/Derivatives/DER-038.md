# Question

A pay-fixed, receive-floating interest rate swap can be replicated by:

## Options

- **A**: Buying a fixed-rate bond and selling a floating-rate note
- **B**: Selling a fixed-rate bond and buying a floating-rate note
- **C**: Buying both a fixed-rate and floating-rate bond

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Swap replication:

**Pay-fixed, receive-floating swap:**
- Outflow: Fixed payments
- Inflow: Floating payments

**Bond replication:**

*Selling (shorting) fixed-rate bond:*
- Creates fixed payment obligation
- Like paying fixed in swap

*Buying floating-rate note:*
- Receives floating payments
- Like receiving floating in swap

**Combined effect:**
- Short fixed bond + Long FRN
- = Pay-fixed, receive-floating swap

**Reverse for receive-fixed:**
- Long fixed bond + Short FRN
- = Receive-fixed, pay-floating swap

**This relationship:**
- Helps price swaps
- Shows interest rate exposure
- Explains duration of swaps

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: This is receive-fixed, pay-floating
- Option C: Buying both creates different exposure
