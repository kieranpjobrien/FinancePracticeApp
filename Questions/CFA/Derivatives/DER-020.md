# Question

Derivatives contribute to price discovery by:

## Options

- **A**: Increasing the cost of trading in underlying markets
- **B**: Providing information about expected future prices
- **C**: Reducing the number of market participants

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Price discovery function:

**How derivatives aid price discovery:**
- Futures prices reflect expectations
- Options imply volatility expectations
- Information incorporated quickly
- Forward-looking indicators

**Examples:**
- Oil futures indicate expected prices
- Fed funds futures predict rate decisions
- VIX reflects expected stock volatility
- Currency forwards show interest rate differentials

**Benefits:**
- Faster information incorporation
- Lower transaction costs than cash markets
- Leveraged positions enable speculation
- Arbitrage links derivative and cash prices

**Market efficiency:**
- Prices reflect available information
- Derivatives speed this process
- Benefit underlying markets

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Derivatives often reduce trading costs
- Option C: Derivatives increase participation and liquidity
