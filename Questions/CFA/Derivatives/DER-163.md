# Question

Derivatives help create more complete markets by:

## Options

- **A**: Reducing the number of available investment choices
- **B**: Allowing investors to access specific risk-return profiles not otherwise available
- **C**: Eliminating all investment risk

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Market completeness:

**Complete markets concept:**
- Any payoff pattern achievable
- All risks can be hedged
- Efficient risk sharing possible

**How derivatives contribute:**
- Create custom exposures
- Access to specific risks
- Payoffs not in cash markets

**Examples:**

*Volatility exposure:*
- Can't buy "volatility" directly
- VIX options/futures enable it
- Specific risk factor exposure

*Interest rate exposure:*
- Pure rate bet without bond risk
- Interest rate swaps
- Rate options

*Leveraged positions:*
- Futures provide leverage
- Options with limited downside
- Efficient capital use

**Economic benefits:**
- Better risk allocation
- Improved price discovery
- More efficient markets

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Derivatives increase choices
- Option C: Risk is transferred, not eliminated
