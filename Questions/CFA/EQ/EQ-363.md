# Question

Continuous trading markets allow:

## Options

- **A**: Trading only at specific times during the day
- **B**: Orders to be executed throughout the trading session as matches occur
- **C**: All trades to occur at a single price

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Continuous trading: orders match and execute throughout trading day as buyers and sellers agree on price. Contrasts with call auction (periodic aggregated clearing). Most modern markets are primarily continuous with call auctions at open/close. Provides ongoing price discovery and liquidity.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes call auction markets
- Option C describes single-price auction clearing
