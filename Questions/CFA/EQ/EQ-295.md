# Question

The Nikkei 225 is:

## Options

- **A**: A US bond index
- **B**: A price-weighted index of 225 Japanese stocks
- **C**: A European broad market index

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Nikkei 225: Japan's most-watched equity index. Price-weighted (like DJIA), 225 large companies on Tokyo Stock Exchange. Longest history of Japanese indices. Criticized for price-weighting limitations. TOPIX (Tokyo Stock Price Index) is market-cap weighted alternative for Japanese equities.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes fixed income, wrong country
- Option C describes European indices like STOXX 600
