# Question

The time value of money concept in equity valuation means:

## Options

- **A**: Cash received today has less value than cash received in the future
- **B**: Cash received today is worth more than the same amount received in the future
- **C**: All cash flows have equal value regardless of timing

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Time value of money: fundamental valuation principle. Dollar today worth more than dollar tomorrow due to: investment opportunity (earning returns), inflation, risk of not receiving future payment. Basis for discounting future cash flows. Higher discount rate = lower present value of future cash.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A reverses the relationship
- Option C ignores timing differences in value
