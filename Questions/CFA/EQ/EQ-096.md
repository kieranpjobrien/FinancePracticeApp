# Question

Effective capital allocation by management includes:

## Options

- **A**: Investing in all available projects regardless of returns
- **B**: Deploying capital to highest-return opportunities and returning excess to shareholders
- **C**: Accumulating cash indefinitely

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Capital allocation: key management responsibility. Good allocators invest in projects earning returns above cost of capital, make value-creating acquisitions, and return excess cash (dividends, buybacks) rather than hoarding or wasting on poor investments. Track record reveals management quality.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores return requirements
- Option C may waste shareholder capital through suboptimal deployment
