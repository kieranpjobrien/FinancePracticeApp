# Question

A seasoned equity offering (SEO) is:

## Options

- **A**: An IPO for established companies
- **B**: An additional stock issuance by an already public company
- **C**: A private placement of debt securities

## Answer

B

## Explanation

SEO (also called secondary or follow-on offering): public company issues new shares after its IPO. Raises additional capital for expansion, acquisitions, or deleveraging. May dilute existing shareholders. Distinguished from IPO (first public offering) and secondary sale (existing shareholders selling).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses with IPO
- Option C describes a different type of financing
