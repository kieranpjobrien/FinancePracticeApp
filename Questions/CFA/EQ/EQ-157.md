# Question

A differentiation strategy is most sustainable when:

## Options

- **A**: Competitors can easily replicate the differentiating features
- **B**: The differentiating features are valued by customers and difficult to imitate
- **C**: The company competes primarily on lowest price

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Sustainable differentiation requires: features customers value enough to pay premium for, barriers to imitation (patents, brand, network effects, complexity), and continuous innovation to stay ahead. Easily copied differentiation erodes quickly. Price competition is cost leadership, not differentiation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes unsustainable differentiation
- Option C describes cost leadership strategy, not differentiation
