# Question

Under ASC 606 / IFRS 15 revenue recognition standards, revenue should be recognized when:

## Options

- **A**: Cash is received regardless of delivery
- **B**: Control of goods or services transfers to the customer
- **C**: The contract is signed

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Modern revenue recognition: five-step model—identify contract, identify performance obligations, determine transaction price, allocate to obligations, recognize when/as obligations satisfied. Control transfer is key criterion. Timing can be point-in-time or over time depending on nature of obligation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes cash-basis accounting
- Option C ignores performance requirement
