# Question

A differentiation strategy involves:

## Options

- **A**: Being the lowest-cost producer in the industry
- **B**: Creating unique value through product features, brand, or service that commands premium pricing
- **C**: Focusing only on a small market segment

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Differentiation: compete through uniqueness—product quality, innovation, brand, customer service. Enables premium pricing despite higher costs. Requires continuous investment in R&D, marketing, or service capabilities. Risk: premium erosion if competitors imitate or customers become price-sensitive.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes cost leadership
- Option C describes focus strategy
