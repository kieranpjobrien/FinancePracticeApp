# Question

A corporate spin-off occurs when:

## Options

- **A**: A company sells a division to another company for cash
- **B**: A company distributes shares of a subsidiary to existing shareholders as a separate entity
- **C**: A company acquires a competitor

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Spin-off: parent company distributes subsidiary shares pro-rata to existing shareholders. Creates two independent public companies. Shareholders receive shares in both. Reasons: unlock value, strategic focus, different investor bases. Tax-free if structured properly. No cash changes hands.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes a sale/divestiture, not spin-off
- Option C describes M&A acquisition
