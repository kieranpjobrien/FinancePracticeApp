# Question

A focus strategy involves:

## Options

- **A**: Competing across all market segments with the lowest costs
- **B**: Targeting a specific market segment with either low cost or differentiation
- **C**: Offering the same products as competitors at premium prices

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Focus strategy: concentrate on narrow market segment—geographic, customer type, or product line. Can pursue cost focus (lowest cost in segment) or differentiation focus (unique offering for segment). Avoids direct competition with broad competitors. Risk: segment may shrink or be invaded.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes broad cost leadership
- Option C lacks strategic logic—same products shouldn't command premiums
