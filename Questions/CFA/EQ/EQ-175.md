# Question

A market order instructs the broker to:

## Options

- **A**: Buy or sell at a specific price or better
- **B**: Execute immediately at the best available price
- **C**: Wait until the market closes to execute

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Market order: prioritizes execution speed over price. Executes immediately at current best bid (for sell) or ask (for buy). Advantages: certainty of execution. Disadvantages: uncertain price, may face slippage in volatile or illiquid markets. Used when immediate execution is priority.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes limit order
- Option C describes market-on-close order
