# Question

Geographic segment analysis helps analysts understand:

## Options

- **A**: Only the company's domestic operations
- **B**: Revenue and profit exposure to different regions with varying growth and risk profiles
- **C**: Stock price movements by location

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Geographic segments: reveal regional revenue concentration, currency exposure, growth from emerging vs. developed markets, regulatory exposure by jurisdiction. Important for multinationals. Emerging market exposure may offer growth but higher risk. Helps assess impact of regional economic conditions.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores international operations
- Option C—segment data is financial, not stock price based
