# Question

Antitrust regulations affect equity analysis because:

## Options

- **A**: They have no impact on corporate strategy or M&A
- **B**: They can block mergers, limit pricing power, and constrain dominant firms
- **C**: They apply only to small companies

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Antitrust impact: regulators can block anti-competitive mergers, break up monopolies, fine price-fixing, limit exclusionary practices. Large company M&A faces regulatory scrutiny. Industry analysis must consider regulatory constraints on consolidation and competitive behavior. Varies by jurisdiction.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores significant regulatory influence
- Option C—antitrust targets dominant firms, not small ones
