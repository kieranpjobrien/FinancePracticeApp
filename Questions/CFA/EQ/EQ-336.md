# Question

The MSCI ACWI (All Country World Index) differs from MSCI World by:

## Options

- **A**: Excluding all Asian markets
- **B**: Including both developed and emerging markets
- **C**: Only covering fixed income

## Answer

B

## Explanation

MSCI ACWI: combines MSCI World (developed) and MSCI EM (emerging markets). Comprehensive global equity benchmark. Covers large and mid-cap across 23 developed and 24 emerging market countries. Used by investors seeking total global equity exposure in single benchmark.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—ACWI includes developed and emerging Asian markets
- Option C—ACWI is equity, not fixed income
