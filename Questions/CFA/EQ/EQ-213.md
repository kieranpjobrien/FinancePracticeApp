# Question

Network effects create competitive advantage because:

## Options

- **A**: Physical networks require large capital investment
- **B**: The value of a product or service increases as more people use it
- **C**: Networking events help companies find new customers

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Network effects: utility increases with user base. Examples: social networks, payment systems, marketplaces, operating systems. Creates winner-take-most dynamics, high switching costs. Difficult to replicate once established. Direct effects (same users) and indirect effects (complements) both matter.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes capital-intensive infrastructure, different concept
- Option C refers to business networking, not network effects
