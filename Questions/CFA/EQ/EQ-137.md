# Question

A company has beginning book value of $50 per share, net income of $8 per share, and required return on equity of 12%. The residual income per share is:

## Options

- **A**: $2.00
- **B**: $6.00
- **C**: $8.00

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Residual income = Net Income - (Required Return × Beginning Book Value) = $8 - (12% × $50) = $8 - $6 = $2. Positive residual income means company earns above its cost of equity. This $2 represents value creation beyond the required return on shareholders' capital.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B subtracts RI from NI instead of equity charge
- Option C ignores the equity charge entirely
