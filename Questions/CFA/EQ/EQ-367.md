# Question

An analyst's target price represents:

## Options

- **A**: The guaranteed future stock price
- **B**: The analyst's estimate of where the stock price should trade based on valuation
- **C**: The current market price

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Target price: analyst's estimate of intrinsic value or expected price at future date (typically 12 months). Based on valuation analysis. Compared to current price for buy/sell recommendations. Not a guarantee—represents opinion based on assumptions. Often accompanied by recommendation (buy, hold, sell).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—no price guarantee exists
- Option C—target differs from current price (that's the point)
