# Question

The momentum effect anomaly suggests that:

## Options

- **A**: Stocks revert to their average price quickly
- **B**: Stocks with strong recent performance tend to continue outperforming in the near term
- **C**: All stocks move in the same direction

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Momentum: tendency for winners to keep winning and losers to keep losing over intermediate horizons (3-12 months). Contradicts weak-form efficiency (which says past returns don't predict future). Possible explanations: underreaction to information, behavioral biases, delayed information processing.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes mean reversion, opposite of momentum
- Option C mischaracterizes stock behavior
