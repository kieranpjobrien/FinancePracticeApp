# Question

A broker acts as:

## Options

- **A**: A principal trading from their own inventory
- **B**: An agent executing trades on behalf of clients
- **C**: The issuer of securities

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Broker: agent that matches buyers and sellers, earns commission. Does not take position or inventory risk. Fiduciary duty to client for best execution. Contrast with dealer who trades as principal from own inventory, earning bid-ask spread. Some firms act as both (broker-dealer).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes a dealer
- Option C describes an issuing company
