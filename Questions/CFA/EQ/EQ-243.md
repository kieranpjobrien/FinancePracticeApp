# Question

Capital expenditure (capex) analysis is important because it indicates:

## Options

- **A**: Only the amount spent on marketing
- **B**: Investment in future capacity, growth, and maintenance of productive assets
- **C**: Current period operating expenses

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Capex analysis: distinguishes maintenance capex (sustaining current operations) from growth capex (expanding capacity). High capex relative to depreciation suggests growth investment. Capex drives future revenue and cash flows. Compare to industry peers and assess return on invested capital.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes operating expense, not capex
- Option C—capex is capitalized, not expensed in current period
