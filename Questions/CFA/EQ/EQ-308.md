# Question

Price improvement occurs when:

## Options

- **A**: A trade executes at a price worse than the quoted bid or ask
- **B**: A trade executes at a price better than the current best bid or ask
- **C**: The stock price increases after the trade

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Price improvement: buyer pays less than ask, or seller receives more than bid. Occurs when market maker offers better price than national best bid/offer, or when orders interact within the spread. Part of best execution evaluation. Measures execution quality beyond just avoiding the spread.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes slippage, the opposite
- Option C describes post-trade price movement, not execution quality
