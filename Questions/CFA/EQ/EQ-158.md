# Question

A cost leadership strategy is best suited for industries characterized by:

## Options

- **A**: Highly differentiated products where customers value unique features
- **B**: Commodity-like products where price is the primary purchase criterion
- **C**: Rapid technological change requiring constant innovation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Cost leadership: competing by having lowest costs, enabling competitive pricing or higher margins. Works best with standardized products, price-sensitive customers, limited differentiation opportunities. Requires scale economies, efficient operations, tight cost control. Less effective when customers seek unique features.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A favors differentiation strategy
- Option C may require innovation investment that conflicts with cost focus
