# Question

In SWOT analysis, competitive threats and market opportunities are classified as:

## Options

- **A**: Internal factors under management's direct control
- **B**: External factors that affect all industry participants
- **C**: Historical performance metrics

## Answer

B

## Explanation

SWOT framework: Strengths and Weaknesses are internal (controllable). Opportunities and Threats are external (market, competitive, regulatory environment). Threats and opportunities affect all industry participants differently based on their internal strengths/weaknesses. Strategic planning links internal capabilities to external factors.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes strengths and weaknesses (internal)
- Option C—SWOT is forward-looking strategic analysis
