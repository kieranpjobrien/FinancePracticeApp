# Question

Which industry would be considered most cyclical?

## Options

- **A**: Utilities
- **B**: Consumer discretionary (luxury goods, automobiles)
- **C**: Healthcare

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Cyclical industries: demand tied to economic cycle—consumer discretionary (autos, luxury, travel), industrials, materials, financials. In recessions, consumers defer purchases. High beta, earnings volatility. Utilities and healthcare are defensive—demand persists regardless of economy.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—utilities are classic defensive sector
- Option C—healthcare demand is relatively inelastic
