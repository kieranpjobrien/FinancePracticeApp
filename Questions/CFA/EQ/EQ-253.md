# Question

The Altman Z-Score is used to predict:

## Options

- **A**: Future stock price appreciation
- **B**: Probability of corporate bankruptcy
- **C**: Expected dividend growth

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Altman Z-Score: multivariate model predicting bankruptcy probability. Variables: working capital/assets, retained earnings/assets, EBIT/assets, market equity/total liabilities, sales/assets. Z > 2.99 = safe zone, Z < 1.81 = distress zone, between = gray zone. Developed 1968, still widely used.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—Z-Score predicts distress, not price appreciation
- Option C—unrelated to dividend forecasting
