# Question

High-frequency trading (HFT) strategies typically:

## Options

- **A**: Hold positions for weeks or months
- **B**: Use algorithmic systems to execute many trades at extremely high speeds
- **C**: Avoid using technology in trading decisions

## Answer

B

## Explanation

HFT: algorithm-driven trading executing thousands of trades per second. Strategies include market making, arbitrage, statistical patterns. Requires co-location near exchanges, low latency systems. Controversial: may improve liquidity and efficiency, but concerns about fairness and market stability.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes long-term investing, not HFT
- Option C is opposite—HFT relies heavily on technology
