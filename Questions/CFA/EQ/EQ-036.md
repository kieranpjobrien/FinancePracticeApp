# Question

Common stock ownership provides:

## Options

- **A**: Guaranteed dividends
- **B**: Voting rights and residual claim on assets after all other claims are satisfied
- **C**: Priority over bondholders in bankruptcy

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Common stockholders: voting rights (board elections, major decisions), residual claim (after debt, preferred). Dividends discretionary, not guaranteed. Last in line during liquidation—receive remaining assets after all other claims. Upside potential unlimited; downside limited to investment.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is incorrect—dividends are discretionary
- Option C reverses the priority—bondholders come before equity
