# Question

Defensive industries are characterized by:

## Options

- **A**: High sensitivity to economic cycles with volatile earnings
- **B**: Stable demand regardless of economic conditions
- **C**: Only investing in military and defense contractors

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Defensive industries: products/services with inelastic demand—utilities, healthcare, consumer staples, tobacco. Earnings less volatile through economic cycles. Lower beta stocks. Outperform in recessions, underperform in expansions. "Defensive" refers to portfolio protection, not military sector.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes cyclical industries
- Option C confuses "defensive" strategy with defense sector
