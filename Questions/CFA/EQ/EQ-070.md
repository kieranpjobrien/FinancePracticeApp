# Question

In Porter's framework, the threat of substitutes is highest when:

## Options

- **A**: Alternative products are expensive and inferior
- **B**: Substitute products offer attractive price-performance trade-offs and switching costs are low
- **C**: The industry has strong patent protection

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Substitute threat: products from other industries meeting same need. High threat when substitutes offer comparable benefits at lower cost, switching is easy. Caps pricing power and profitability. Technology often creates new substitutes (streaming vs. cable, email vs. postal mail).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes low substitute threat
- Option C describes barrier to substitutes
