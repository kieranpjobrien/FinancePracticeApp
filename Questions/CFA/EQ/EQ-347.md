# Question

Capital-intensive industries typically:

## Options

- **A**: Require minimal investment in physical assets
- **B**: Require large investments in property, plant, and equipment relative to revenue
- **C**: Have very low barriers to entry

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Capital intensity: high PP&E relative to sales—utilities, telecom, manufacturing, airlines. High fixed costs create operating leverage. Barriers to entry from capital requirements. ROIC often lower than asset-light businesses. Depreciation significant expense. Asset turnover typically low.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes asset-light industries
- Option C—high capital needs create barriers
