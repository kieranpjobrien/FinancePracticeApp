# Question

Intense rivalry among existing competitors is most likely when:

## Options

- **A**: Industry growth is rapid and products are differentiated
- **B**: Industry growth is slow, fixed costs are high, and products are commoditized
- **C**: Few competitors exist with high barriers to exit

## Answer

B

## Explanation

High rivalry: slow growth (fight for market share), high fixed costs (pressure to fill capacity), commodity products (compete on price), low switching costs, high exit barriers (excess capacity persists). Intense rivalry erodes profitability through price wars, advertising battles, and service competition.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes conditions with less intense rivalry
- Option C has fewer competitors reducing rivalry intensity
