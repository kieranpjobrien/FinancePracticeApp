# Question

High industry capacity utilization typically leads to:

## Options

- **A**: Intense price competition and margin compression
- **B**: Pricing power and improved profitability for producers
- **C**: Lower barriers to entry

## Answer

B

## Explanation

High capacity utilization: demand approaching or exceeding supply. Producers have pricing power, can be selective about customers, earn higher margins. Low utilization (overcapacity) creates opposite—desperate competition for volumes, price cutting. Capacity cycle important for cyclical industries.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes overcapacity situation
- Option C—capacity utilization doesn't directly affect entry barriers
