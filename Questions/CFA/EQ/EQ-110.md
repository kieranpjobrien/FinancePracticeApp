# Question

Normalized earnings are used in valuation to:

## Options

- **A**: Report the highest possible earnings figure
- **B**: Estimate earnings adjusted for cyclical or non-recurring factors to reflect sustainable earning power
- **C**: Ignore all accounting adjustments

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Normalized earnings: adjust reported earnings for cyclical peaks/troughs, non-recurring items, and unusual charges to estimate mid-cycle sustainable earnings. Methods: historical average, ROE × book value. Important for cyclical industries and when recent results don't reflect normal operations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes aggressive earnings presentation
- Option C ignores the purpose of normalization
