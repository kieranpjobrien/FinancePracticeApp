# Question

If markets are fully efficient, the most appropriate investment strategy would be:

## Options

- **A**: Active stock picking to beat the market
- **B**: Passive investing in index funds to minimize costs
- **C**: Technical analysis based on price patterns

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Efficient market implications: if prices reflect all information, active management cannot consistently beat market after costs. Passive indexing minimizes expenses, matches market return. Active management justified only if markets have exploitable inefficiencies. Debate continues on degree of market efficiency.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A assumes ability to outperform, contradicting efficiency
- Option C relies on past prices, ineffective in efficient markets
