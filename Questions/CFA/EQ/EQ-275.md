# Question

Emerging market equity indices typically include:

## Options

- **A**: Only US and European stocks
- **B**: Stocks from developing economies like China, India, Brazil, and others
- **C**: Exclusively small-cap stocks from any country

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Emerging markets: developing economies with less mature financial markets but higher growth potential. MSCI EM includes China, India, Brazil, Taiwan, Korea, South Africa, Mexico, etc. Higher risk/volatility, currency exposure, political risk, but diversification and growth benefits. Distinct from developed and frontier markets.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes developed markets
- Option C—EM includes all cap sizes, defined by country development
