# Question

In Porter's Five Forces framework, high barriers to entry:

## Options

- **A**: Decrease industry profitability by attracting new competitors
- **B**: Protect existing firms' profitability by limiting new competition
- **C**: Have no effect on industry dynamics

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Barriers to entry: high capital requirements, economies of scale, regulatory barriers, brand loyalty, switching costs. High barriers limit new entrants, protecting incumbents' market share and pricing power. Low barriers invite competition, eroding margins. Key factor in assessing industry attractiveness.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A reverses the relationship
- Option C ignores competitive dynamics
