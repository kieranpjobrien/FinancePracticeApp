# Question

In the mature stage of the industry lifecycle:

## Options

- **A**: Rapid revenue growth continues
- **B**: Growth matches overall economy, competition is stable, and cash flow is strong
- **C**: Companies are exiting the industry

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Mature stage: growth slows to economic growth rate, market saturated, competition stabilized among survivors. Companies generate strong cash flows with limited reinvestment needs. Focus on efficiency, cost control, returning cash to shareholders. May seek growth through acquisition.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes growth stage
- Option C describes decline stage
