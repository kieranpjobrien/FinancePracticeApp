# Question

The S&P 500 Index:

## Options

- **A**: Includes the 500 largest global companies
- **B**: Is a float-adjusted market-cap weighted index of 500 large US companies
- **C**: Uses price weighting like the DJIA

## Answer

B

## Explanation

S&P 500: 500 large-cap US stocks, float-adjusted market-cap weighted (excludes closely held shares). Primary US equity benchmark. Selection committee chooses constituents based on size, liquidity, sector representation. Represents approximately 80% of US equity market value.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—S&P 500 is US-focused, not global
- Option C—S&P 500 uses market-cap weighting, unlike DJIA
