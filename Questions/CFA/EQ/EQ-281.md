# Question

Market-wide circuit breakers are designed to:

## Options

- **A**: Increase trading volume during volatile periods
- **B**: Halt trading temporarily during extreme price moves to prevent panic selling
- **C**: Guarantee that stock prices never decline

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Circuit breakers: automatic trading halts triggered by significant index declines (e.g., S&P 500 down 7%, 13%, 20%). Purpose: allow information dissemination, cool emotions, prevent cascade selling. Implemented after 1987 crash. Balance between allowing price discovery and preventing disorderly markets.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—circuit breakers pause, not increase trading
- Option C—can't prevent declines, only pause during extreme moves
