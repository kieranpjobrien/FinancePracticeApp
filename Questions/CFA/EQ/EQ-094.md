# Question

The extended DuPont analysis decomposes ROE into:

## Options

- **A**: Two components: profit margin and asset turnover
- **B**: Five components including tax burden, interest burden, operating margin, asset turnover, and leverage
- **C**: Only financial leverage

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Extended DuPont: ROE = (Net income/EBT) × (EBT/EBIT) × (EBIT/Revenue) × (Revenue/Assets) × (Assets/Equity). Components: tax burden, interest burden, operating profit margin, asset turnover, financial leverage. Identifies specific drivers of profitability and areas for improvement.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is the basic two-factor decomposition
- Option C is only one of five factors
