# Question

Market liquidity is characterized by:

## Options

- **A**: Large price changes from small trades
- **B**: The ability to trade quickly at prices close to previous transaction prices
- **C**: High trading costs and wide bid-ask spreads

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Liquidity: ability to buy/sell quickly without significant price impact. Characteristics: narrow bid-ask spreads, market depth (large orders don't move prices), trading activity. Liquid markets: lower transaction costs, faster execution, prices closer to fair value. Illiquidity requires compensation (liquidity premium).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes illiquidity
- Option C also describes illiquidity
