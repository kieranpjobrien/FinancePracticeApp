# Question

The primary function of a stock exchange is to:

## Options

- **A**: Set the prices at which securities should trade
- **B**: Provide a venue for buyers and sellers to trade securities efficiently
- **C**: Guarantee that all investors make profits

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Exchange functions: facilitate trading, provide price transparency, enforce rules, ensure fair treatment of participants, maintain orderly markets. Prices determined by buyer/seller interaction, not set by exchange. Exchanges reduce search costs and counterparty risk through standardization and clearing.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—exchanges facilitate price discovery but don't set prices
- Option C—exchanges can't guarantee profits, only fair trading conditions
