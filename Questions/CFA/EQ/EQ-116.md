# Question

Asset turnover ratio indicates:

## Options

- **A**: How much profit is generated per dollar of assets
- **B**: How efficiently a company uses its assets to generate revenue
- **C**: The percentage of assets financed by debt

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Asset turnover = Revenue / Average total assets. Measures revenue generation efficiency from asset base. Higher turnover indicates more efficient asset utilization. Varies significantly by industry—retailers have high turnover; utilities have low turnover. Part of DuPont decomposition.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes return on assets
- Option C describes debt ratio or leverage
