# Question

First-mover advantage can benefit early industry entrants through:

## Options

- **A**: Always guaranteeing market leadership
- **B**: Brand recognition, customer lock-in, and learning curve benefits
- **C**: Avoiding all competitive pressures

## Answer

B

## Explanation

First-mover benefits: brand establishment, customer acquisition before competition, experience curve cost advantages, setting industry standards, network effects if applicable. Doesn't guarantee success—later entrants can learn from pioneer mistakes. Balance of first-mover advantages vs. fast-follower strategies varies by industry.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A overstates—first movers sometimes fail
- Option C—first movers still face subsequent competition
