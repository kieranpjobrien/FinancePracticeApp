# Question

Ordinary least squares (OLS) regression finds the line that:

## Options

- **A**: Maximizes the sum of squared residuals
- **B**: Minimizes the sum of squared residuals
- **C**: Passes through the maximum and minimum observations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

OLS regression determines the coefficient estimates by minimizing the sum of squared residuals (SSR)—the sum of the squared vertical distances between each observed Y value and the fitted regression line. This minimization ensures the best fit in the least squares sense, producing unbiased and efficient estimates under classical assumptions.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is the opposite; minimization, not maximization, is the objective
- Option C describes a different approach that does not produce optimal estimates
