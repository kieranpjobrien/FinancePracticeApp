# Question

An investment returns +20% in Year 1 and -10% in Year 2. The geometric mean annual return is closest to:

## Options

- **A**: 3.9%
- **B**: 5.0%
- **C**: 5.5%

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Geometric Mean = [(1 + R₁) × (1 + R₂)]^(1/n) - 1

Geometric Mean = [(1.20) × (0.90)]^(1/2) - 1
Geometric Mean = [1.08]^0.5 - 1
Geometric Mean = 1.0392 - 1 = 0.0392 = 3.9%

The geometric mean is always less than or equal to the arithmetic mean and better represents compound growth.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B (5%) is the arithmetic mean: (20% + -10%) / 2 = 5%
- Option C may result from calculation error
