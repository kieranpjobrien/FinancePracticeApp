# Question

Natural resource investments include exposure to:

## Options

- **A**: Only precious metals like gold and silver
- **B**: Energy, agriculture, metals, and timber
- **C**: Exclusively renewable energy sources

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Natural resources categories:

**Energy:**
- Oil and gas
- Natural gas
- Coal
- Renewable sources

**Agriculture:**
- Grains (corn, wheat, soybeans)
- Soft commodities (coffee, cocoa)
- Livestock
- Farmland

**Metals:**
- Precious (gold, silver, platinum)
- Base metals (copper, aluminum, zinc)
- Industrial metals

**Timber:**
- Timberland ownership
- Wood products
- Forestry operations

**Ways to invest:**
- Direct commodity exposure
- Commodity futures
- Natural resource equities
- Physical asset ownership

**Investment rationale:**
- Inflation protection
- Diversification
- Global growth exposure
- Supply constraints

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Includes many categories beyond precious metals
- Option C: Includes traditional energy sources
