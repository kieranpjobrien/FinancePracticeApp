# Question

Farmland investment returns are generated from:

## Options

- **A**: Only capital appreciation of land values
- **B**: Both crop income and land appreciation
- **C**: Government subsidies exclusively

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Farmland return components:

**Income component:**
- Crop sales revenue
- Rental income (if leased)
- Annual cash flow
- Typically 2-5% yield

**Appreciation component:**
- Land value increases
- Follows commodity prices
- Scarcity of quality land
- Population growth demand

**Return drivers:**
- Commodity prices
- Agricultural productivity
- Land supply constraints
- Inflation (food prices)

**Investment approaches:**
- Direct ownership
- Farm operating companies
- Farmland REITs
- Agricultural funds

**Characteristics:**
- Low correlation with stocks
- Inflation protection
- Long investment horizon
- Illiquid (direct ownership)

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Income also significant component
- Option C: Subsidies may help but not primary source
