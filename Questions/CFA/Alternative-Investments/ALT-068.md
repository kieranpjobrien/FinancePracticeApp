# Question

Infrastructure investments typically have:

## Options

- **A**: Short investment horizons of 1-3 years
- **B**: Very long investment horizons, often 20-30+ years
- **C**: No defined investment period

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Infrastructure investment horizons:

**Why long duration:**
- Assets last 30-50+ years
- Concessions: 20-99 years
- Regulatory frameworks: Ongoing
- Capital intensity

**Typical timelines:**
- Utility investment: Perpetual
- Toll road concession: 30-50 years
- Airport: 20-40 years
- Power plant: 20-30 years

**Implications for investors:**
- Patient capital required
- Match with long liabilities
- Limited liquidity
- Illiquidity premium

**Suitable investors:**
- Pension funds
- Insurance companies
- Sovereign wealth funds
- Endowments

**Cash flow pattern:**
- Initial development/construction
- Long operational phase
- Stable income generation
- Asset transfer (some models)

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Much longer than 1-3 years
- Option C: Defined by asset life or concession
