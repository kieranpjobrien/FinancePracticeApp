# Question

An independent fund administrator for hedge funds primarily provides:

## Options

- **A**: Investment advice and trading decisions
- **B**: NAV calculation, investor servicing, and accounting functions
- **C**: Marketing and investor relations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Fund administrator role:

**Core functions:**
- NAV calculation
- Investor recordkeeping
- Financial statements
- Capital activity processing

**Why important:**
- Independent verification
- Investor protection
- Operational infrastructure
- Regulatory requirement (often)

**Services provided:**
- Monthly/quarterly NAV
- Investor statements
- Subscription/redemption processing
- Tax reporting (K-1s)

**Independence value:**
- Separate from manager
- Check on valuations
- Fraud prevention
- Investor confidence

**Major administrators:**
- State Street
- SS&C
- Citco
- SEI

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Managers make investment decisions
- Option C: Separate from marketing
