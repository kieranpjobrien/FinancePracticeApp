# Question

Net absorption in real estate markets measures:

## Options

- **A**: Total existing space in the market
- **B**: The change in occupied space over a period
- **C**: The construction cost per square foot

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Absorption metrics:

**Net absorption:**
- Occupied space end of period
- Minus occupied space beginning
- Change in occupancy
- Demand indicator

**Positive absorption:**
- More space occupied
- Demand exceeds supply
- Healthy market sign
- Vacancy declining

**Negative absorption:**
- Less space occupied
- Tenants vacating
- Weak demand
- Vacancy rising

**Gross absorption:**
- Total leasing activity
- Regardless of vacating
- Activity measure
- Higher than net

**Market analysis use:**
- Supply/demand balance
- Rental rate direction
- Investment timing
- Development decisions

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: This is inventory
- Option C: This is construction cost
