# Question

The vintage year of a private equity fund is important for:

## Options

- **A**: Determining the fund's legal domicile
- **B**: Comparing performance with funds that invested in similar market conditions
- **C**: Calculating the management fee structure

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Vintage year importance:

**Definition:**
- Year fund begins investing
- First capital call or closing date
- Identifies market entry point

**Why it matters for comparison:**
- Different market conditions each year
- Entry valuations vary significantly
- Exit environments differ
- Fair comparison requires similar vintage

**Examples:**
- 2007 vintage: Pre-crisis investments
- 2009 vintage: Post-crisis bargains
- Very different performance expected

**Benchmarking approach:**
- Compare to same vintage peers
- Industry benchmarks by vintage
- Quartile rankings within vintage

**Performance drivers:**
- Entry valuations
- Economic cycle position
- Exit market conditions
- Manager skill (within vintage)

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Domicile unrelated to vintage
- Option C: Fees not determined by vintage
