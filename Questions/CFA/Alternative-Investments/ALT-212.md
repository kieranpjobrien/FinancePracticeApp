# Question

Form 13F filings for hedge funds disclose:

## Options

- **A**: Complete fund strategy and methodology
- **B**: Quarterly US equity long positions above certain thresholds
- **C**: Fund performance and investor information

## Answer

B

## Explanation

13F reporting requirements:

**What's disclosed:**
- US equity holdings
- Long positions over $100M AUM
- Quarterly snapshots
- Certain options positions

**What's NOT disclosed:**
- Short positions
- Non-US holdings
- Fixed income
- Derivatives (mostly)
- Cash positions

**Filing requirements:**
- Managers with $100M+ in 13F securities
- Filed within 45 days of quarter end
- Publicly available
- SEC database

**Limitations:**
- Partial picture only
- Stale data (45-day delay)
- No short exposure
- Incomplete portfolio view

**Uses:**
- Track manager positions
- Idea generation
- Academic research
- Market analysis

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Holdings only, not strategy
- Option C: No performance disclosed
