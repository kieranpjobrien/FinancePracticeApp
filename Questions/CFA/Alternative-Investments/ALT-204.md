# Question

Silver differs from gold as an investment because silver:

## Options

- **A**: Has no industrial demand
- **B**: Has significant industrial demand in addition to investment demand
- **C**: Is more stable in price than gold

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Silver characteristics:

**Dual nature:**
- Investment/monetary demand
- Industrial demand (~50%)
- More volatile than gold
- Smaller market

**Industrial uses:**
- Electronics
- Solar panels
- Medical applications
- Photography (declining)

**Investment demand:**
- Store of value
- Monetary history
- Safe haven (less than gold)
- Portfolio diversification

**Comparison to gold:**
- More volatile
- Stronger industrial correlation
- Lower price per ounce
- More accessible

**Gold-silver ratio:**
- Historical comparison
- Currently ~80:1 range
- Trading signal for some
- Varies over time

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Significant industrial demand
- Option C: More volatile than gold
