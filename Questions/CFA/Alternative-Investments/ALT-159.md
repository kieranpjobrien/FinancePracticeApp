# Question

A "platform company" in private equity refers to:

## Options

- **A**: A software technology company only
- **B**: The initial acquisition that serves as the foundation for add-on investments
- **C**: The limited partner in the fund

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Platform company concept:

**Definition:**
- Initial/anchor acquisition
- Foundation for strategy
- Base for bolt-on acquisitions
- Core investment thesis

**Characteristics sought:**
- Strong management team
- Scalable operations
- Consolidation opportunity
- Defensible market position

**Buy-and-build strategy:**
1. Acquire platform
2. Execute operational improvements
3. Add bolt-on acquisitions
4. Achieve scale benefits
5. Exit at higher multiple

**Value creation:**
- Organic growth at platform
- Add-on synergies
- Multiple expansion from scale
- Operational improvements

**Example:**
- Platform: Regional HVAC company
- Bolt-ons: Smaller local competitors
- Result: National player

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Any industry, not just software
- Option C: Platform is portfolio company, not investor
