# Question

Cryptocurrency airdrops involve:

## Options

- **A**: Physical delivery of hardware wallets
- **B**: Free distribution of tokens to wallet addresses, often to promote a project
- **C**: Government-mandated token redistribution

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Airdrops explained:

**Definition:**
- Free token distribution
- To existing wallet holders
- Promotional mechanism
- Community building

**Types:**
- Standard: Free to all
- Holder: Based on existing tokens
- Exclusive: Community participants
- Hard fork: Chain split tokens

**Purposes:**
- Marketing/awareness
- Decentralize ownership
- Reward early users
- Bootstrap network effects

**How to qualify:**
- Hold certain tokens
- Use specific protocols
- Complete tasks
- Be active community member

**Tax implications:**
- May be taxable income
- Fair market value at receipt
- Varies by jurisdiction
- Record keeping important

**Risks:**
- Scam airdrops exist
- Phishing attempts
- Wallet security
- Spam tokens

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Digital distribution, not physical
- Option C: Private project decisions
