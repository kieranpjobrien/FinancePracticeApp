# Question

Water as an investment theme focuses on:

## Options

- **A**: Only bottled water companies
- **B**: Companies involved in water treatment, distribution, and conservation
- **C**: Ocean shipping and maritime industries

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Water investment opportunities:

**Water value chain:**
- Treatment and purification
- Distribution infrastructure
- Conservation technology
- Desalination
- Testing and monitoring

**Investment thesis:**
- Growing global demand
- Finite freshwater supply
- Climate change impact
- Aging infrastructure

**Ways to invest:**
- Water utility stocks
- Water technology companies
- Water-focused ETFs
- Infrastructure funds

**Sectors included:**
- Utilities
- Industrial equipment
- Environmental services
- Technology/analytics

**Growth drivers:**
- Population growth
- Urbanization
- Regulatory requirements
- ESG focus

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Much broader than bottled water
- Option C: Maritime is separate sector
