# Question

Institutional adoption of digital assets has accelerated due to:

## Options

- **A**: Complete elimination of regulatory uncertainty
- **B**: Development of institutional-grade custody, ETFs, and regulatory frameworks
- **C**: Guaranteed returns from cryptocurrency investments

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Institutional adoption drivers:

**Infrastructure development:**
- Regulated custodians (Fidelity, Coinbase)
- Prime brokerage services
- Trading platforms
- Insurance solutions

**Regulatory progress:**
- Bitcoin ETF approvals
- Clearer guidance
- Licensing frameworks
- AML/KYC standards

**Investment products:**
- Spot Bitcoin ETFs (2024)
- Futures ETFs
- Institutional funds
- Managed accounts

**Investor motivations:**
- Portfolio diversification
- Inflation hedge thesis
- Technology exposure
- Client demand

**Remaining challenges:**
- Regulatory uncertainty remains
- Volatility concerns
- Custody complexity
- Accounting treatment

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Uncertainty reduced, not eliminated
- Option C: No guaranteed returns
