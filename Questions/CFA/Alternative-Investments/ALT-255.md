# Question

When considering digital assets in a diversified portfolio, most institutional recommendations suggest:

## Options

- **A**: Allocating 50% or more of the portfolio
- **B**: Small allocations (1-5%) given high volatility and uncertainty
- **C**: Avoiding any allocation under all circumstances

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Digital asset allocation:

**Common guidance:**
- 1-5% typical recommendation
- Based on risk tolerance
- Volatility consideration
- Uncertain asset class

**Rationale for small allocation:**
- High volatility (50-100%+)
- Regulatory uncertainty
- Asset class maturity
- Correlation uncertainty

**Potential benefits:**
- Diversification (potentially)
- High return potential
- Portfolio completion
- Technology exposure

**Sizing considerations:**
- Risk budget allocation
- Maximum acceptable loss
- Rebalancing approach
- Investment horizon

**Growing institutional interest:**
- Increasing allocations
- Infrastructure improving
- Regulatory clarity growing
- Education increasing

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Far too large given risks
- Option C: Some allocation being considered
