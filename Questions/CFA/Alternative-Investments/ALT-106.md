# Question

Decentralized Finance (DeFi) refers to:

## Options

- **A**: Traditional banking services provided by fintech companies
- **B**: Financial services built on blockchain without traditional intermediaries
- **C**: Government-regulated cryptocurrency exchanges

## Answer

B

## Explanation

DeFi explained:

**Core concept:**
- Financial services on blockchain
- No banks or brokers
- Smart contract automation
- Permissionless access

**DeFi applications:**
- Decentralized exchanges (DEX)
- Lending/borrowing protocols
- Yield farming
- Derivatives
- Insurance

**Key characteristics:**
- Open and permissionless
- Transparent (on-chain)
- Composable ("money legos")
- Non-custodial

**Benefits:**
- Global access
- 24/7 operation
- Lower costs (potentially)
- Innovation speed

**Risks:**
- Smart contract bugs
- Protocol hacks
- Regulatory uncertainty
- Complexity

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: DeFi eliminates traditional intermediaries
- Option C: Unregulated/decentralized by design
