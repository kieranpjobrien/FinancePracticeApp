# Question

A private equity fund-of-funds offers investors:

## Options

- **A**: Lower total fees than direct fund investment
- **B**: Diversification across multiple PE managers and strategies
- **C**: Direct control over individual investment decisions

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PE fund-of-funds:

**Benefits:**
- Diversification across managers
- Access to top-tier funds
- Strategy diversification
- Vintage year diversification

**Access advantage:**
- Top funds often oversubscribed
- Minimum investments very high
- FoF provides pooled access
- Relationship benefits

**Diversification value:**
- Multiple GPs
- Different strategies (buyout, VC, growth)
- Various geographies
- Spread across vintage years

**Drawbacks:**
- Double layer of fees
- FoF fee + underlying fund fees
- Lower net returns
- Less control

**Suitable for:**
- Smaller institutional investors
- Those new to PE
- Seeking diversification
- Limited internal resources

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: FoF adds fees, doesn't reduce them
- Option C: FoF manager selects funds, not investor
