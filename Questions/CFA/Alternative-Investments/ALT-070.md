# Question

Digital infrastructure, such as data centers and cell towers, is classified as infrastructure because:

## Options

- **A**: It has the same risk-return profile as technology stocks
- **B**: It provides essential services with long-lived assets and contracted revenues
- **C**: It requires no capital investment

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Digital infrastructure characteristics:

**Essential service nature:**
- Critical for modern economy
- Growing data/connectivity demand
- Mission-critical for users
- High switching costs

**Infrastructure-like features:**
- Long-lived physical assets
- High barriers to entry
- Contracted revenues
- Predictable cash flows

**Data centers:**
- Housing critical IT systems
- Long-term leases
- Power-intensive operations
- Growing demand (cloud, AI)

**Cell towers:**
- Multi-tenant structures
- Long-term carrier contracts
- Escalators in contracts
- 5G driving growth

**Why growing sector:**
- Digitalization trends
- 5G buildout
- Cloud computing growth
- Work-from-home acceleration

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: More stable than tech stocks
- Option C: Highly capital intensive
