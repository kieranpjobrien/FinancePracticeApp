# Question

Construction risk in infrastructure projects includes:

## Options

- **A**: Only the risk of minor delays
- **B**: Cost overruns, schedule delays, and technical/quality issues
- **C**: Risk that is always borne by the government

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Construction risk components:

**Cost risk:**
- Budget overruns
- Material price increases
- Design changes
- Unforeseen conditions

**Schedule risk:**
- Delays in completion
- Weather impacts
- Permitting delays
- Labor issues

**Technical risk:**
- Design defects
- Technology issues
- Performance shortfalls
- Quality problems

**Mitigation approaches:**
- Fixed-price contracts
- Performance bonds
- Experienced contractors
- Contingency reserves

**Risk allocation:**
- Often private sector bears
- Contractors may share
- Completion guarantees
- Lender requirements

**Consequences:**
- Delayed revenue
- Additional financing costs
- Penalty payments
- Reduced returns

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Can be major, not just minor
- Option C: Usually private sector risk
