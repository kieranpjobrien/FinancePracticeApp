# Question

A Private Placement Memorandum (PPM) in alternative investments:

## Options

- **A**: Is a public SEC filing available to everyone
- **B**: Provides detailed fund information and risk disclosures to prospective investors
- **C**: Is optional and rarely provided

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PPM contents:

**Key sections:**
- Investment strategy
- Risk factors
- Management team
- Fee structure
- Terms and conditions

**Risk disclosures:**
- Investment risks
- Market risks
- Manager risks
- Liquidity risks
- Conflicts of interest

**Legal importance:**
- Disclosure document
- Liability protection
- Investor decision basis
- Regulatory compliance

**Who receives:**
- Prospective investors
- During marketing
- Before commitment
- Confidential document

**Related documents:**
- Limited Partnership Agreement
- Subscription documents
- Side letters
- Due diligence materials

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Private, not public document
- Option C: Standard and required practice
