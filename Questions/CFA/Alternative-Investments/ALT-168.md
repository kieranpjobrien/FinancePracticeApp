# Question

Lifecycle costs in infrastructure investment include:

## Options

- **A**: Only initial construction costs
- **B**: Construction, maintenance, operation, and eventual decommissioning costs
- **C**: Just the annual operating budget

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Infrastructure lifecycle costs:

**Construction phase:**
- Initial capital investment
- Development costs
- Financing during construction
- Major upfront expenditure

**Operating phase:**
- Annual maintenance (O&M)
- Staffing costs
- Insurance and fees
- Ongoing expenses

**Major maintenance:**
- Periodic refurbishment
- Equipment replacement
- Capacity upgrades
- Reserve funding

**End of life:**
- Decommissioning costs
- Asset transfer preparation
- Environmental remediation
- Handback requirements

**Why lifecycle matters:**
- Total cost of ownership
- Long-term commitments
- Reserve planning
- Bid/valuation accuracy

**Common underestimations:**
- Maintenance escalation
- Rehabilitation timing
- End-of-life costs

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Must consider full lifecycle
- Option C: Ignores capital and decommissioning
