# Question

Refinancing risk in infrastructure debt relates to:

## Options

- **A**: The risk of interest rates falling
- **B**: The risk of inability to refinance maturing debt on acceptable terms
- **C**: The risk of prepaying debt early

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Refinancing risk:

**The concern:**
- Debt matures before asset life ends
- Must refinance to continue
- Market conditions may be adverse
- Terms may worsen

**Why relevant for infrastructure:**
- Very long asset lives
- Debt typically shorter term
- Multiple refinancings needed
- Market access required

**Risk factors:**
- Interest rate changes
- Credit market conditions
- Asset performance
- Lender appetite

**Mitigation strategies:**
- Longer-dated debt
- Amortizing structures
- Cash reserves
- Backup facilities
- Rating maintenance

**Consequences if problems:**
- Higher interest costs
- Covenant tightening
- Reduced distributions
- Potential distress

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Rising rates are the concern
- Option C: Prepayment is different risk
