# Question

Bitcoin's "store of value" thesis is based on:

## Options

- **A**: Government backing and deposit insurance
- **B**: Fixed supply cap of 21 million coins and decentralization
- **C**: Stable daily prices with no volatility

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Bitcoin store of value thesis:

**Fixed supply:**
- 21 million maximum coins
- Decreasing issuance (halvings)
- Scarcity by design
- Unlike fiat currencies

**Decentralization:**
- No central authority control
- Censorship resistant
- Distributed network
- Trustless system

**"Digital gold" comparison:**
- Scarcity like gold
- Not easily inflated
- Portable and divisible
- Store wealth long-term

**Supporting arguments:**
- Inflation hedge
- Currency debasement protection
- Institutional adoption
- Network effect

**Counterarguments:**
- High volatility
- Short track record
- No intrinsic value
- Regulatory risk

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: No government backing
- Option C: Extremely volatile
