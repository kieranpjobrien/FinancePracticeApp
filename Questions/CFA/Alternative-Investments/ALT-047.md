# Question

Direct lending funds primarily provide financing to:

## Options

- **A**: Large investment-grade corporations
- **B**: Middle-market companies that may not access public debt markets
- **C**: Government entities seeking infrastructure funding

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Direct lending explained:

**Target borrowers:**
- Middle-market companies
- $10M-$500M revenue typical
- Too small for public markets
- Often PE-backed companies

**Why they need direct lenders:**
- Banks retreated post-2008
- Regulatory capital requirements
- Relationship lending declined
- Speed and certainty valued

**Loan characteristics:**
- Senior secured typically
- Floating rate
- Covenants for protection
- Higher spreads than syndicated

**Lender benefits:**
- Higher yields (8-12%+)
- Illiquidity premium
- Floating rate protection
- Covenant protections

**Risks:**
- Credit risk
- Concentration risk
- Illiquidity
- Limited secondary market

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Large companies access public markets
- Option C: Infrastructure has separate financing
