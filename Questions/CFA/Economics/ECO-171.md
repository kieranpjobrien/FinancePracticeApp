# Question

Safe-haven currencies typically appreciate during:

## Options

- **A**: Periods of global economic optimism
- **B**: Times of financial stress and uncertainty
- **C**: Commodity price booms

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Safe-haven currencies (USD, JPY, CHF) tend to strengthen during periods of global uncertainty, financial stress, or risk aversion. Investors flee riskier assets and currencies, seeking perceived safety. This "flight to quality" reflects these currencies' association with stable economies, deep capital markets, and historical reliability during crises.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes risk-on conditions when safe havens often weaken
- Option C typically benefits commodity currencies, not safe havens
