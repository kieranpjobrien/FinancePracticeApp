# Question

The monetary policy transmission mechanism describes how:

## Options

- **A**: Fiscal policy affects interest rates
- **B**: Central bank actions affect the broader economy through various channels
- **C**: Banks set their reserve requirements

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The monetary policy transmission mechanism explains how central bank policy decisions (like rate changes) flow through to affect the real economy. Key channels include: the interest rate channel (affecting borrowing costs), the exchange rate channel (affecting exports/imports), the asset price channel (affecting wealth and collateral values), and the credit channel (affecting bank lending). Understanding these channels helps assess policy effectiveness.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses fiscal and monetary policy
- Option C misunderstands the role of reserve requirements
