# Question

Infrastructure investment promotes economic growth by:

## Options

- **A**: Reducing the need for private sector investment
- **B**: Lowering transportation and communication costs
- **C**: Increasing the government budget deficit

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Infrastructure—roads, ports, telecommunications, power grids—reduces transaction costs and improves efficiency. Better infrastructure allows businesses to access markets, reduces logistics costs, and enables more productive resource allocation. While infrastructure spending may increase deficits, the growth benefit comes from improved connectivity and reduced costs, not from the spending itself.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A misunderstands—infrastructure complements rather than substitutes for private investment
- Option C confuses the method of financing with the source of growth
