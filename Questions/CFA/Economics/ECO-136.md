# Question

"Helicopter money" as a policy concept involves:

## Options

- **A**: Raising interest rates to control inflation
- **B**: Direct money transfers to citizens financed by money creation
- **C**: Reducing government spending to balance the budget

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Helicopter money, a term from Milton Friedman, describes direct transfers to households financed by newly created money rather than borrowing. Unlike QE (which increases bank reserves but may not reach consumers), helicopter money puts purchasing power directly in consumers' hands. It blurs the line between monetary and fiscal policy and is considered a last-resort stimulus when conventional policies fail.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes contractionary policy
- Option C describes fiscal austerity
