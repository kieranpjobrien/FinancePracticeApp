# Question

Challenges to US dollar dominance as the primary reserve currency include:

## Options

- **A**: The dollar's complete stability and universal acceptance
- **B**: Geopolitical tensions, sanctions use, and efforts by rivals to develop alternatives
- **C**: The elimination of all other major currencies

## Answer

B

## Explanation

While the dollar remains dominant (~60% of global reserves), its hegemony faces challenges: frequent use of dollar-based sanctions has motivated countries to seek alternatives; China promotes RMB internationalization; some countries explore de-dollarization in trade. However, network effects, deep US capital markets, and lack of clear alternatives maintain dollar dominance for now.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes factors supporting dominance, not challenges
- Option C is unrealistic and would actually reinforce dollar use
