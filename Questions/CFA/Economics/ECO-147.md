# Question

The spot EUR/USD is 1.1000 and the 3-month forward is 1.1050. The annualized forward premium on the euro is closest to:

## Options

- **A**: 0.45%
- **B**: 1.82%
- **C**: 4.55%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Forward premium = (Forward - Spot) / Spot × (12 / months) × 100%
= (1.1050 - 1.1000) / 1.1000 × (12/3) × 100%
= 0.00455 × 4 × 100%
= 1.82%

The euro is at a 1.82% annualized forward premium, suggesting eurozone interest rates are lower than US rates.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A doesn't annualize (uses 3-month rate)
- Option C uses incorrect annualization factor
