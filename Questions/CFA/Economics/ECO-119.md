# Question

If USD/EUR spot is 1.1000 and the 1-year forward is 1.1150, the forward points are:

## Options

- **A**: 150 points (euro at forward premium)
- **B**: -150 points (euro at forward discount)
- **C**: 15 points

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Forward points = Forward rate - Spot rate = 1.1150 - 1.1000 = 0.0150 = 150 points

Since the forward rate is higher than spot (more dollars per euro forward), the euro is at a forward premium. This typically reflects lower eurozone interest rates than US rates per covered interest rate parity.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B reverses the calculation or premium/discount interpretation
- Option C miscounts the decimal places
