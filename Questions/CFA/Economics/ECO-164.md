# Question

Energy security concerns have led many countries to:

## Options

- **A**: Increase dependence on single energy suppliers
- **B**: Diversify energy sources and develop domestic or allied supply
- **C**: Eliminate all energy imports immediately

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Energy security involves ensuring reliable, affordable energy supply. Geopolitical events (Russia-Ukraine conflict, Middle East tensions) have highlighted risks of dependence on hostile or unstable suppliers. Countries have responded by diversifying suppliers, investing in renewables, developing domestic resources (like US shale), and building strategic reserves. Complete energy independence is impractical for most.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A increases rather than reduces vulnerability
- Option C is infeasible and economically harmful
