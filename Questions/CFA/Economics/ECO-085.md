# Question

If USD/EUR moves from 1.1050 to 1.1075, the change expressed in pips is:

## Options

- **A**: 2.5 pips
- **B**: 25 pips
- **C**: 250 pips

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A pip (percentage in point) is the smallest common price movement in forex, typically the fourth decimal place for most currency pairs.

Change = 1.1075 - 1.1050 = 0.0025 = 25 pips

For pairs quoted to four decimal places, one pip equals 0.0001. The exception is yen pairs, where a pip is 0.01 due to lower currency unit value.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A miscounts the decimal movement
- Option C multiplies by 10 extra
