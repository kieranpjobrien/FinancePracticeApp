# Question

Bond equivalent yield (BEY) converts yields to:

## Options

- **A**: An effective annual yield basis
- **B**: A semiannual-pay, 365-day year basis for comparison
- **C**: A continuously compounded basis

## Answer

B

## Explanation

BEY: standardizes yields to semiannual bond basis for comparison. Converts money market (360-day, discount) yields to 365-day, semiannual-pay equivalent. Allows comparing T-bills to coupon bonds. BEY = 2 × semiannual yield.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes effective annual yield
- Option C describes continuous compounding
