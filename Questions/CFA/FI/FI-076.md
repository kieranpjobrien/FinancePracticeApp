# Question

A 90-day T-bill with a bank discount yield of 4% has a money market yield (CD equivalent) of approximately:

## Options

- **A**: 4.00%
- **B**: 4.04%
- **C**: 3.96%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Money market yield = (360 × discount yield)/(360 - days × discount yield) = (360 × 0.04)/(360 - 90 × 0.04) = 14.4/356.4 = 4.04%. Money market yield higher because based on purchase price, not face value.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A assumes yields are identical
- Option C inverts the relationship
