# Question

The CDS-bond basis is:

## Options

- **A**: The difference between CDS spread and bond credit spread
- **B**: The notional amount of the CDS contract
- **C**: The recovery rate assumption in CDS pricing

## Answer

A

## Explanation

CDS-bond basis = CDS spread - bond spread (typically ASW or Z-spread). Positive basis: CDS > bond spread. Negative basis: CDS < bond spread. Basis trades attempt to profit from convergence. Basis affected by funding costs, liquidity, delivery options.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B describes contract size
- Option C describes pricing input, not basis
