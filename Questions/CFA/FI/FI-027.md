# Question

Spot rates are:

## Options

- **A**: Rates for forward-starting loans
- **B**: Yields on zero-coupon bonds for specific maturities
- **C**: Average yields across all maturities

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Spot rates: yields on zero-coupon instruments for specific maturities. Also called zero rates. Spot curve plots rates vs. maturity. Used to discount individual cash flows. Par yields and spot rates related but different—par assumes coupon reinvestment.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes forward rates
- Option C doesn't describe spot rates specifically
