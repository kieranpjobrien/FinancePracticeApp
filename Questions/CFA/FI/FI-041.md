# Question

When a bond is sold between coupon dates, the buyer pays:

## Options

- **A**: Only the clean price
- **B**: The clean price plus accrued interest (dirty price)
- **C**: Less than the quoted price

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Bond pricing: quoted (clean) price + accrued interest = full (dirty/invoice) price. Buyer compensates seller for interest earned but not yet paid. Accrued interest accumulates daily from last coupon. Seller receives accrued; buyer receives full next coupon.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores accrued interest component
- Option C misunderstands the full price relationship
