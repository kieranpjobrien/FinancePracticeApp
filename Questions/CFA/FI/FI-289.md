# Question

Arbitrage-free bond valuation requires that:

## Options

- **A**: All bonds yield the same regardless of maturity
- **B**: Each cash flow is discounted at the spot rate for its specific maturity
- **C**: Only the final payment matters for valuation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Arbitrage-free: discount each cash flow at appropriate spot rate. Ensures no arbitrage between bonds and zero-coupon securities. Using single YTM ignores term structure shape. Proper valuation uses spot curve. Prevents riskless profit opportunities.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—different maturities have different rates
- Option C—ignores interim coupon payments
