# Question

If a bond has a 40% recovery rate, its loss given default (LGD) is:

## Options

- **A**: 40%
- **B**: 60%
- **C**: 140%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

LGD = 1 - Recovery rate = 1 - 0.40 = 60%. Recovery rate is amount recovered as percentage of par. Secured bonds have higher recovery (40-60%); unsecured lower (30-40%); subordinated lowest (20-30%). Seniority determines recovery in bankruptcy.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses recovery with loss
- Option C adds rather than subtracting from 100%
