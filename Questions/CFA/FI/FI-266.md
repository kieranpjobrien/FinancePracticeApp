# Question

Credit card ABS differ from auto loan ABS because credit card pools:

## Options

- **A**: Have no prepayment risk
- **B**: Are revolving—new receivables added and principal paid reinvested during initial period
- **C**: Always have government guarantees

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Credit card ABS: revolving pool. During revolving period, principal payments buy new receivables (maintain pool size). Amortization period begins later. Auto loans are non-revolving—pool pays down immediately. Different cash flow dynamics.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—credit card ABS have different prepayment characteristics
- Option C—no government guarantee on credit card ABS
