# Question

The preferred habitat theory differs from segmented markets theory in that:

## Options

- **A**: Investors have no maturity preference
- **B**: Investors will leave preferred maturity if premium is sufficient
- **C**: Supply and demand don't affect yields

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Preferred habitat: investors have maturity preferences but will deviate for sufficient premium. Combines segmented markets (preferences exist) with flexibility (can be induced to move). More realistic than strict segmentation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes pure expectations theory
- Option C contradicts all term structure theories
