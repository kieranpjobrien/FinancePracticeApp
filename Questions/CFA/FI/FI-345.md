# Question

A money market instrument with a 360-day bank discount yield of 4% has a bond-equivalent yield closest to:

## Options

- **A**: 4.00%
- **B**: 4.17%
- **C**: 4.25%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

BEY converts discount yield to bond-equivalent basis (365-day, add-on yield). BEY = (365 × Discount yield) / (360 - Discount yield × Days). For 1-year: approximately BEY = 4% × (365/360) / (1 - 0.04) = 4.167% ≈ 4.17%. Allows comparison with bond yields.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—doesn't convert from discount basis
- Option C—calculation error
