# Question

Most corporate bond trading occurs in:

## Options

- **A**: Organized exchanges like the NYSE
- **B**: Over-the-counter (OTC) dealer markets
- **C**: Electronic order books with central limit order matching

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Bond markets: predominantly OTC, dealer-based. Dealers quote bid/ask, trade from inventory. Less transparent than equity markets. Corporate bond market decentralized. Electronic platforms growing but dealer model persists. Government bond markets also OTC but more liquid.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes equity trading model
- Option C describes exchange-style trading less common in bonds
