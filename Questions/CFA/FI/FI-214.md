# Question

For a callable bond trading at a premium, yield to worst (YTW) is typically:

## Options

- **A**: Equal to yield to maturity
- **B**: Equal to the lowest yield among all call dates and maturity
- **C**: Higher than yield to maturity

## Answer

B

## Explanation

YTW: minimum yield assuming issuer acts optimally. For premium bonds, likely called—YTC < YTM, so YTW = YTC. For discount bonds, call unlikely—YTW = YTM. Conservative measure for investor planning.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores potential call
- Option C—YTW is lowest, not highest yield
