# Question

Government-sponsored enterprise (GSE) bonds are:

## Options

- **A**: Direct obligations of the federal government
- **B**: Issued by entities created by government but not explicitly guaranteed
- **C**: Municipal bonds with federal tax exemption

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GSEs: created by Congress (Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac, Federal Home Loan Banks). Implied but not explicit government backing. Trade at small spread to Treasuries. After 2008 crisis, Fannie/Freddie placed in conservatorship—implicit guarantee became more explicit.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes direct government debt (Treasuries)
- Option C describes municipal bonds
