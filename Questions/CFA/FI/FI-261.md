# Question

A liquidity premium in bond yields compensates investors for:

## Options

- **A**: Credit risk of the issuer
- **B**: Difficulty in selling the bond quickly at fair value
- **C**: Interest rate volatility

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Liquidity premium: extra yield for less liquid bonds. Harder to sell quickly without price impact = higher required yield. Off-the-run Treasuries, small corporate issues, private placements less liquid. Liquidity premium widens in market stress.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes credit spread
- Option C describes term/volatility premium
