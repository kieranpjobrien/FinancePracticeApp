# Question

Country risk for corporate bonds includes:

## Options

- **A**: Only the company's domestic operations risk
- **B**: Political, legal, and economic risks that could affect the issuer
- **C**: Currency exchange rates only

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Country risk: political stability, legal system (contract enforcement, bankruptcy), economic conditions, transfer/convertibility risk (ability to move funds). Affects corporate issuers in that country. Even strong company can be impaired by weak sovereign.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A too narrow—includes all country-level risks
- Option C is one component but not the full picture
