# Question

Commercial mortgage-backed securities (CMBS) differ from residential MBS in that CMBS:

## Options

- **A**: Have lower prepayment risk due to lockout periods and prepayment penalties
- **B**: Have higher prepayment risk than residential MBS
- **C**: Are backed by government guarantees

## Answer

A

## Explanation

CMBS: backed by commercial property loans. Lower prepayment risk—loans have lockout periods, yield maintenance, defeasance requirements. Credit risk more significant. Analyzed on property-by-property basis. No agency guarantee typically.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B—residential MBS have more prepayment risk
- Option C—CMBS typically non-agency (private label)
