# Question

Prepayment risk in mortgage-backed securities:

## Options

- **A**: Is the risk that borrowers will pay late
- **B**: Is the risk that borrowers will pay off mortgages early, typically when rates fall
- **C**: Only affects commercial mortgages

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Prepayment risk: homeowners refinance when rates fall, paying off existing mortgages early. MBS investors receive principal back sooner than expected, must reinvest at lower rates. Contraction risk (fast prepayment in falling rates) vs. extension risk (slow prepayment in rising rates).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes delinquency risk
- Option C—prepayment risk affects residential MBS significantly
