# Question

A bond with a 6% annual coupon (paid semiannually) and $1,000 par has 60 days since the last coupon. Using 30/360 day count, accrued interest is:

## Options

- **A**: $10.00
- **B**: $5.00
- **C**: $6.00

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Accrued interest = (coupon × days since last payment) / days in period. Semiannual coupon = $30. Days in period (30/360) = 180. Accrued = 30 × (60/180) = $10.00. Buyer pays seller accrued interest at settlement.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B uses full annual coupon in denominator
- Option C miscalculates day fraction
