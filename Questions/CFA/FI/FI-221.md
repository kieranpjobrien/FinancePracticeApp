# Question

If the CPR (conditional prepayment rate) is 12%, the SMM (single monthly mortality) is approximately:

## Options

- **A**: 1.00%
- **B**: 1.06%
- **C**: 12.00%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

SMM = 1 - (1 - CPR)^(1/12) = 1 - (1 - 0.12)^(1/12) = 1 - (0.88)^0.0833 = 1 - 0.9894 = 1.06%. SMM is monthly equivalent of annual CPR. Can't simply divide by 12 due to compounding.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A divides CPR by 12 (ignores compounding)
- Option C uses annual rate for monthly
