# Question

Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities (TIPS) protect investors from inflation by:

## Options

- **A**: Paying higher fixed coupons than regular Treasury bonds
- **B**: Adjusting principal value based on changes in CPI
- **C**: Having shorter maturities than regular Treasuries

## Answer

B

## Explanation

TIPS: principal adjusts with CPI-U (inflation index). Coupon rate fixed but applied to inflation-adjusted principal. Provides real return protection. At maturity, receive greater of adjusted principal or original par. Deflation floor protects principal.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—TIPS typically have lower nominal coupons
- Option C—TIPS come in similar maturities to regular Treasuries
