# Question

The primary bond market is where:

## Options

- **A**: Previously issued bonds are traded between investors
- **B**: New bonds are first sold to investors by the issuer
- **C**: Central banks conduct monetary policy operations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Primary market: initial sale of new securities from issuer to investors. Underwriters facilitate. Proceeds go to issuer. Secondary market: subsequent trading between investors. Primary issuance sets initial price/yield.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes secondary market
- Option C describes open market operations, separate function
