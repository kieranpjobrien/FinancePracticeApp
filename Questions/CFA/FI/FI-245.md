# Question

A "negative outlook" from a rating agency indicates:

## Options

- **A**: An immediate downgrade has occurred
- **B**: Increased probability of a downgrade in the medium term
- **C**: The rating is stable and unlikely to change

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Negative outlook: rating agency believes conditions may lead to downgrade over 6-24 months. Not immediate action. Credit watch is shorter-term (90 days). Positive outlook = possible upgrade. Stable = rating likely unchanged.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes actual rating action, not outlook
- Option C describes stable outlook
