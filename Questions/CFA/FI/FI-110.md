# Question

A global bond is:

## Options

- **A**: A bond issued only in emerging markets
- **B**: A bond issued simultaneously in multiple markets, both domestic and international
- **C**: A bond backed by global commodity prices

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Global bond: issued in multiple markets at same time (e.g., US domestic and Eurobond market). Larger issue size, broader investor base, better liquidity. First issued by World Bank 1989. Combines domestic and international distribution.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes emerging market bonds
- Option C describes commodity-linked bonds
