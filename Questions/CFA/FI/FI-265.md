# Question

A Z-tranche (accrual tranche) in a CMO:

## Options

- **A**: Receives all principal payments first
- **B**: Receives no cash until prior tranches are retired, with interest accruing
- **C**: Has the shortest average life

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Z-tranche: receives no cash initially. Interest accrues and adds to principal balance. After other tranches retired, Z-tranche receives cash. Longest average life, highest duration. Sometimes called accrual tranche. Volatile due to prepayment timing.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes first-pay sequential tranche
- Option C—Z-tranche has longest average life
