# Question

A laddered bond portfolio:

## Options

- **A**: Concentrates holdings at one target maturity
- **B**: Distributes holdings evenly across multiple maturities
- **C**: Only holds the shortest maturity bonds

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Ladder: equal amounts maturing at regular intervals (e.g., annually over 10 years). Provides regular reinvestment opportunities, diversifies rate exposure across time. Simple to manage. As bonds mature, reinvest at longest rung.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes bullet portfolio
- Option C describes money market strategy
