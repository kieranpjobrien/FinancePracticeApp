# Question

A negative covenant restricting the issuer's debt-to-EBITDA ratio is classified as:

## Options

- **A**: An affirmative covenant
- **B**: A financial (maintenance) covenant
- **C**: A change of control provision

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Financial covenants: specific ratio requirements (debt/EBITDA, interest coverage, etc.). Subset of negative covenants—restrict issuer from exceeding thresholds. Maintenance covenants tested periodically; incurrence covenants tested only when taking action.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—financial covenants are negative, not affirmative
- Option C—change of control is separate provision type
