# Question

Commercial paper is typically:

## Options

- **A**: A long-term secured bond
- **B**: Short-term unsecured debt issued at a discount by corporations
- **C**: A government-guaranteed instrument

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Commercial paper: short-term (typically < 270 days to avoid SEC registration). Unsecured promise to pay. Issued at discount, matures at par. Used for working capital. Investment-grade issuers. Rollover risk if cannot refinance at maturity.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—CP is short-term and unsecured
- Option C—no government guarantee on CP
