# Question

Social bonds differ from green bonds in that social bonds fund:

## Options

- **A**: Any general corporate purpose
- **B**: Projects with positive social outcomes like affordable housing or healthcare
- **C**: Environmental conservation exclusively

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Social bonds: proceeds fund socially beneficial projects (affordable housing, healthcare access, education, employment). Sustainability bonds combine green and social. ESG-labeled bond market growing rapidly. Same credit fundamentals as conventional bonds.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes conventional bonds
- Option C describes green bonds
