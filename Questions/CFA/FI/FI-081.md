# Question

A banker's acceptance is primarily used for:

## Options

- **A**: Financing consumer purchases
- **B**: Financing international trade transactions
- **C**: Government deficit financing

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Banker's acceptance: time draft drawn on and accepted by bank. Used in international trade—importer's bank guarantees payment. Trades at discount in money market. Bank substitutes its credit for importer's, facilitating trade.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes consumer credit, not trade finance
- Option C describes Treasury securities
