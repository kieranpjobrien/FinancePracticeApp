# Question

A bond's maturity date is:

## Options

- **A**: When the issuer makes the first coupon payment
- **B**: When the issuer repays the principal amount
- **C**: When the bond was originally issued

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Maturity date: when principal (face value) is repaid to bondholder. Defines bond's term. Short-term: < 5 years. Intermediate: 5-12 years. Long-term: > 12 years. Affects duration, interest rate sensitivity, and required yield.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—describes first coupon date
- Option C—describes issue/settlement date
