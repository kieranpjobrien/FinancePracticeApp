# Question

Event risk in credit analysis refers to:

## Options

- **A**: Gradual deterioration of operating performance
- **B**: Sudden credit quality changes from corporate events like LBOs, mergers, or spin-offs
- **C**: Normal business cycle fluctuations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Event risk: sudden, hard-to-predict events affecting credit. LBOs add massive debt. Spin-offs may remove valuable assets. Share buybacks increase leverage. Stock splits neutral. Covenants (change of control, debt limitation) provide some protection.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—describes gradual credit deterioration
- Option C—describes cyclical, not event risk
