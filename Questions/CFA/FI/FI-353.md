# Question

Given 1-year spot rate of 3% and a 2-year 5% annual coupon bond priced at par, the 2-year spot rate is closest to:

## Options

- **A**: 4.0%
- **B**: 5.0%
- **C**: 5.03%

## Answer

C

## Explanation

100 = 5/(1.03) + 105/(1+S₂)². 100 = 4.854 + 105/(1+S₂)². 95.146 = 105/(1+S₂)². (1+S₂)² = 105/95.146 = 1.1035. S₂ = √1.1035 - 1 = 5.03%. Bootstrapping: sequentially derive spot rates from bond prices.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—calculation error
- Option B—confuses coupon rate with spot rate
