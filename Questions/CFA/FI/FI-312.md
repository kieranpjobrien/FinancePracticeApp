# Question

A 10-year corporate bond yields 5.5% and the 10-year Treasury yields 4.0%. The yield spread is:

## Options

- **A**: 100 basis points
- **B**: 150 basis points
- **C**: 200 basis points

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Yield spread = Corporate yield - Treasury yield = 5.5% - 4.0% = 1.5% = 150 basis points. Spread compensates for credit risk, liquidity risk, and other factors versus risk-free benchmark. Widely used metric for relative value analysis.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—arithmetic error
- Option C—arithmetic error
