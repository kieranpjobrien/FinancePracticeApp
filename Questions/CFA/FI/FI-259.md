# Question

Financial leverage increases credit risk by:

## Options

- **A**: Reducing the company's debt obligations
- **B**: Creating fixed interest obligations that must be paid regardless of earnings
- **C**: Increasing equity relative to debt

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Financial leverage: using debt creates fixed obligations (interest, principal). Must be paid regardless of business performance. High leverage means less cushion to absorb earnings declines. Combined with high operating leverage = very high risk.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—leverage means more debt, not less
- Option C describes deleveraging
