# Question

A cap on a floating-rate note:

## Options

- **A**: Benefits the issuer by limiting the maximum coupon rate
- **B**: Benefits the bondholder by guaranteeing a minimum return
- **C**: Has no effect on either party

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Cap: maximum rate issuer pays regardless of reference rate. Benefits issuer, disadvantages holder if rates rise above cap. Floor: minimum rate holder receives. Collar: both cap and floor. FRN with cap trades at lower price than uncapped.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B describes a floor, not a cap
- Option C ignores caps' impact on cash flows
