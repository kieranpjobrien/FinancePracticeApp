# Question

The relationship between clean price and dirty price is:

## Options

- **A**: Clean price = Dirty price + Accrued interest
- **B**: Dirty price = Clean price + Accrued interest
- **C**: Clean price and dirty price are always equal

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Dirty price (full price): total amount buyer pays = clean price + accrued interest. Clean price (flat price): quoted price excluding accrued interest. Bonds quoted clean but settle dirty. Accrued interest compensates seller for interest earned.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A reverses the relationship
- Option C ignores accrued interest accumulation
