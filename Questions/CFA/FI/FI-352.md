# Question

In bond underwriting, "book building" refers to:

## Options

- **A**: Recording trades after execution
- **B**: Collecting investor indications of interest to determine offering price and allocation
- **C**: Creating the bond indenture

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Book building: underwriters gather orders from investors before pricing. "Build the book" of demand. Helps determine appropriate spread/price. Larger orders may get better allocations. Process reveals demand curve. Common for corporate bond new issues.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—describes trade recording/settlement
- Option C—describes legal documentation
