# Question

U.S. Treasury securities are typically issued through:

## Options

- **A**: Negotiated underwriting with investment banks
- **B**: Competitive auctions
- **C**: Private placement with institutional investors

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Treasury auctions: competitive bidding by primary dealers and others. Single-price (Dutch) auction—all winners pay same price. Regular schedule: bills weekly, notes/bonds monthly. Most transparent, liquid government bond market.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes corporate bond issuance
- Option C describes private placements, different market
