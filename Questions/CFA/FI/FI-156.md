# Question

The "Four Cs" of credit analysis include all of the following EXCEPT:

## Options

- **A**: Capacity, Collateral, Covenants
- **B**: Character (management quality)
- **C**: Currency (denomination of debt)

## Answer

C

## Explanation

Four Cs: Capacity (ability to repay from cash flows), Collateral (asset backing), Covenants (legal protections), Character (management quality/integrity). Currency denomination is a risk factor but not one of the traditional Four Cs framework.

**Common mistakes:**
- Options A and B are correct components of Four Cs
- Currency risk is separate consideration
