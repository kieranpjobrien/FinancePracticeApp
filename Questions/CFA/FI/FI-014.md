# Question

Positive convexity benefits bondholders because:

## Options

- **A**: It causes equal price changes for rate increases and decreases
- **B**: Price gains from rate decreases exceed price losses from equal rate increases
- **C**: It eliminates all interest rate risk

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Positive convexity: price-yield relationship is curved, not linear. Gains from falling rates exceed losses from rising rates of same magnitude. Duration understates gains, overstates losses. Convexity adjustment improves price change estimates. Investors prefer higher convexity.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes linear relationship (no convexity)
- Option C overstates—convexity improves but doesn't eliminate rate risk
