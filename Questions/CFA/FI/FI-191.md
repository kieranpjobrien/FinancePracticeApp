# Question

DV01 (dollar value of a basis point) measures:

## Options

- **A**: The percentage change in bond price for a 1% yield change
- **B**: The dollar change in bond price for a 1 basis point yield change
- **C**: The bond's yield in basis points

## Answer

B

## Explanation

DV01 = Dollar value of 01 (one basis point). Price change in dollars for 1 bp yield move. DV01 = (Modified duration × Price × 0.0001). Useful for hedging—match DV01 to neutralize rate exposure. Also called PVBP (price value of basis point).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes modified duration
- Option C describes yield level, not sensitivity
