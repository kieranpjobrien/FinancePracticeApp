# Question

Sovereign bonds are issued by:

## Options

- **A**: National governments
- **B**: Corporations with government contracts
- **C**: Municipal authorities

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Sovereign bonds: debt issued by national governments. Risk varies—developed countries (low risk) vs. emerging markets (higher risk). May be local or foreign currency denominated. Credit risk depends on fiscal position, currency, political stability.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B describes government contractors, not sovereign issuers
- Option C describes municipal bonds (sub-sovereign)
