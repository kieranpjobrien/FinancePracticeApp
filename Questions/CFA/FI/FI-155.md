# Question

Free cash flow to the firm (FCFF) is important in credit analysis because it represents:

## Options

- **A**: Cash available to pay all capital providers (debt and equity)
- **B**: Cash available only to equity holders
- **C**: Accounting profits after depreciation

## Answer

A

## Explanation

FCFF = Operating cash flow - capex. Cash available to pay interest, repay debt, and distribute to equity. Credit analysts focus on ability to service debt from operating cash flow. FCFE (free cash flow to equity) deducts debt payments.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B describes FCFE
- Option C describes accounting income, not cash flow
