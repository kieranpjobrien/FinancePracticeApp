# Question

The yield to maturity calculation assumes:

## Options

- **A**: The bond is sold before maturity at current price
- **B**: Coupons are reinvested at the YTM and the bond is held to maturity
- **C**: Interest rates remain at current levels forever

## Answer

B

## Explanation

YTM assumptions: hold to maturity, reinvest all coupons at YTM, no default. Rarely all true—reinvestment rate varies. Realized return may differ from YTM. Total return analysis accounts for different reinvestment assumptions.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A contradicts hold-to-maturity assumption
- Option C—YTM doesn't assume flat rates, just reinvestment at YTM
