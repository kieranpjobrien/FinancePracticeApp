# Question

In a firm commitment underwriting, the underwriter:

## Options

- **A**: Acts only as agent, taking no risk
- **B**: Purchases the entire issue and resells to investors, bearing inventory risk
- **C**: Guarantees the issue will trade above par

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Firm commitment: underwriter buys entire issue from issuer at agreed price, resells to investors. Bears inventory risk if can't place bonds. Higher fees than best efforts. Most common for investment-grade corporate bonds.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes best efforts underwriting
- Option C—no guarantee of secondary market performance
