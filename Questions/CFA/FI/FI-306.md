# Question

Most US corporate bonds pay coupons:

## Options

- **A**: Annually
- **B**: Semiannually
- **C**: Monthly

## Answer

B

## Explanation

US corporate bonds: semiannual coupon payments (every 6 months). Convention differs by market—Eurobonds typically annual. Affects yield calculations (semiannual compounding vs annual). Payment frequency impacts duration and reinvestment considerations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—annual is Eurobond convention
- Option C—monthly is mortgage payment convention
