# Question

A 200 PSA prepayment assumption means:

## Options

- **A**: 200% of the PSA standard prepayment model benchmark
- **B**: Prepayments are exactly 200 basis points annually
- **C**: 200 months until all loans prepay

## Answer

A

## Explanation

PSA benchmark: prepayment increases from 0.2% CPR to 6% CPR over first 30 months, then constant 6%. 200 PSA = 2× that speed (ramps to 12% CPR). Higher PSA = faster prepayments. Used for MBS valuation assumptions.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B confuses PSA with CPR level
- Option C describes timing incorrectly
