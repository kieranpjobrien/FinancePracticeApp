# Question

Treasury STRIPS are created by:

## Options

- **A**: Issuing new zero-coupon securities directly
- **B**: Separating a Treasury bond's coupon and principal payments into individual zero-coupon securities
- **C**: Combining multiple Treasury bills into a single security

## Answer

B

## Explanation

STRIPS: Separate Trading of Registered Interest and Principal Securities. Dealers strip coupon bonds into zeros—each coupon and principal becomes separate tradable security. Can also reconstitute—combine strips back into coupon bond. Arbitrage keeps prices aligned.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—STRIPS are derived from coupon bonds, not issued directly
- Option C describes combination, not stripping
