# Question

The before-tax cost of debt is best estimated using:

## Options

- **A**: The coupon rate on existing debt
- **B**: The yield to maturity on the company's existing bonds
- **C**: The prime rate

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The cost of debt is the yield investors require on new borrowing—approximated by YTM on existing traded bonds (market rate). Coupon rates reflect historical issuance, not current costs. Prime rate is a benchmark for some loans but doesn't reflect company-specific credit risk.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A uses historical, not current market rate
- Option C ignores company-specific credit spreads
