# Question

A stock dividend differs from a cash dividend in that a stock dividend:

## Options

- **A**: Reduces shareholders' wealth
- **B**: Increases the number of shares without changing total equity value
- **C**: Increases the company's cash balance

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Stock dividend: distribution of additional shares to existing shareholders. Total equity unchanged; each shareholder's proportional ownership unchanged. Share price adjusts downward proportionally. Similar to stock split but different accounting treatment (transfers from retained earnings to capital).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is incorrect—shareholder wealth unchanged
- Option C is opposite—no cash involved in stock dividends
