# Question

When NPV and IRR give conflicting rankings for mutually exclusive projects, the analyst should:

## Options

- **A**: Always choose the higher IRR project
- **B**: Prefer the NPV ranking
- **C**: Calculate payback period as a tiebreaker

## Answer

B

## Explanation

NPV directly measures value creation and should be preferred. Conflicts arise from different scale or timing of cash flows. IRR assumes reinvestment at IRR (often unrealistic); NPV assumes reinvestment at cost of capital (more realistic). NPV maximizes wealth; IRR maximizes percentage return.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A may select a smaller project with less total value creation
- Option C doesn't resolve the economic comparison
