# Question

The compensation committee is responsible for:

## Options

- **A**: Setting prices for the company's products
- **B**: Designing and overseeing executive pay packages
- **C**: Approving the annual budget

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The compensation committee (typically independent directors) designs executive compensation to attract/retain talent and align incentives with shareholders. It sets base salary, bonuses, equity awards, and benefits. Compensation design aims to balance short-term and long-term performance incentives.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is a management/marketing function
- Option C is typically a full board or finance committee function
