# Question

Operating leverage refers to:

## Options

- **A**: The use of debt in the capital structure
- **B**: The proportion of fixed operating costs in the cost structure
- **C**: The ratio of current assets to current liabilities

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Operating leverage: sensitivity of operating income to changes in revenue, driven by fixed costs. High fixed costs = high operating leverage = greater earnings volatility. Degree of operating leverage (DOL) = % change in EBIT / % change in sales. Different from financial leverage (debt use).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes financial leverage, not operating leverage
- Option C describes liquidity ratios
