# Question

Hard capital rationing occurs when:

## Options

- **A**: Management voluntarily limits investment spending
- **B**: External factors prevent the company from raising needed capital
- **C**: The company has unlimited access to capital at its WACC

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Hard capital rationing: external constraints prevent raising capital—tight credit markets, company credit rating issues, market conditions, regulatory restrictions. Company cannot fund all positive NPV projects. Contrasts with soft rationing (internal/voluntary budget limits) and no rationing (unlimited capital access).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes soft capital rationing
- Option C describes the assumption of unconstrained capital markets
