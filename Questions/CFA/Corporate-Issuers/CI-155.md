# Question

A supplier offers terms of "2/10, net 30." The cost of not taking the discount (annualized) is approximately:

## Options

- **A**: 2%
- **B**: 36.7%
- **C**: 24%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Cost of trade credit = [Discount / (1 - Discount)] × [365 / (Full period - Discount period)] = [0.02 / 0.98] × [365 / 20] = 0.0204 × 18.25 = 37.2% (approximately 36.7%). Foregoing a small discount for a short deferral is very expensive on an annualized basis.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A only considers the nominal discount rate
- Option C doesn't properly annualize the cost
