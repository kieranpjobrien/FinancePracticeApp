# Question

A break point in the marginal cost of capital schedule occurs when:

## Options

- **A**: The company stops investing entirely
- **B**: A source of capital is exhausted, requiring more expensive financing
- **C**: All projects become unprofitable

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Break points occur when: retained earnings are exhausted (requiring new equity issuance), debt capacity at current rates is reached (requiring higher-cost debt), or target capital structure limits are hit. Beyond break points, marginal cost of capital increases.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes stopping investment, not a break point
- Option C describes project exhaustion, not financing exhaustion
