# Question

A "poison pill" (shareholder rights plan) is designed to:

## Options

- **A**: Facilitate hostile takeovers
- **B**: Make hostile takeovers more expensive or difficult
- **C**: Increase dividend payments

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Poison pills give existing shareholders rights to buy additional shares at a discount if an acquirer accumulates a threshold stake—diluting the hostile bidder's position and making the takeover more expensive. They're controversial: may protect management entrenchment but also give boards negotiating leverage.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is the opposite effect
- Option C is unrelated to takeover defenses
