# Question

Accelerated depreciation methods affect project NPV by:

## Options

- **A**: Changing total depreciation over the project life
- **B**: Shifting tax savings to earlier years, increasing their present value
- **C**: Eliminating the need for salvage value estimates

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Accelerated depreciation: same total depreciation, but higher amounts in early years. Tax shield (depreciation × tax rate) comes sooner. Due to time value of money, earlier tax savings have higher present value. Increases NPV compared to straight-line depreciation, all else equal.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is incorrect—total depreciation unchanged
- Option C doesn't address accelerated depreciation's impact
