# Question

Credit terms of "2/10, net 30" mean a 2% discount if paid within 10 days, otherwise full payment in 30 days. The annualized cost of not taking the discount is closest to:

## Options

- **A**: 24%
- **B**: 37%
- **C**: 44%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Cost of not taking discount = (Discount / (1 - Discount)) × (365 / (Full period - Discount period)) = (0.02 / 0.98) × (365 / 20) = 0.0204 × 18.25 = 37.2%. Forgoing the 2% discount for 20 extra days is expensive annualized.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A underestimates the annualization
- Option C uses incorrect formula
