# Question

Under a residual dividend policy, dividends are:

## Options

- **A**: Kept constant regardless of earnings
- **B**: Paid from earnings remaining after funding positive NPV projects
- **C**: Always equal to a fixed percentage of earnings

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Residual dividend policy: fund all positive NPV investments first using retained earnings, then distribute remaining earnings as dividends. Prioritizes investment opportunities. Results in variable dividends based on available projects. Consistent with value maximization but may create dividend volatility.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes a stable dividend policy
- Option C describes a constant payout ratio policy
