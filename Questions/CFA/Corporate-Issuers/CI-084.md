# Question

The build-up method for estimating cost of equity:

## Options

- **A**: Uses only the risk-free rate
- **B**: Adds risk premiums for equity risk, size, and company-specific factors
- **C**: Is identical to CAPM

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Build-up method: starts with risk-free rate, adds equity risk premium, size premium (small stocks riskier), industry premium, and company-specific risk premium. Used for private companies where beta isn't available. More subjective than CAPM but captures additional risk factors.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores all risk premiums
- Option C—build-up doesn't use beta, unlike CAPM
