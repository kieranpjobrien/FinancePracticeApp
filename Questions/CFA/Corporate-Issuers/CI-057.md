# Question

Clawback provisions in executive compensation:

## Options

- **A**: Increase guaranteed compensation
- **B**: Allow companies to recover compensation if financial results are restated
- **C**: Guarantee minimum bonus payments

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Clawbacks allow recovery of previously paid bonuses or incentive compensation if financial statements are later restated (reducing reported performance) or misconduct is discovered. They align incentives over time and discourage short-term manipulation. Many regulations now require clawback policies.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes the opposite effect
- Option C describes guaranteed pay, not recovery mechanisms
