# Question

Share repurchases are often viewed more favorably than dividends because repurchases:

## Options

- **A**: Are required to be made annually
- **B**: Offer flexibility and may signal undervaluation without committing to ongoing payments
- **C**: Always receive preferential tax treatment

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Repurchase advantages: flexibility (no ongoing commitment), signal that management believes shares are undervalued, return cash without creating dividend expectations, may be tax-advantaged in some jurisdictions. Can be reduced or eliminated in tough times without the negative signal of a dividend cut.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is incorrect—repurchases are discretionary
- Option C overstates—tax treatment varies by jurisdiction and investor situation
