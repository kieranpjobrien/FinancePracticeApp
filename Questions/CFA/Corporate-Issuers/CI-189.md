# Question

From an agency cost perspective, dividends can be beneficial because they:

## Options

- **A**: Allow management to retain maximum cash for projects
- **B**: Reduce free cash flow available for wasteful investment
- **C**: Eliminate the need for external monitoring

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Agency costs and dividends: excess cash can lead to empire building, perks, or poor investments. Dividends reduce cash available for such waste, forcing managers to seek external financing (and scrutiny) for new investments. Dividends act as disciplining mechanism on management behavior.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A increases agency problems by giving management more discretion
- Option C is too strong—monitoring remains important
