# Question

A lead independent director's role includes:

## Options

- **A**: Running day-to-day company operations
- **B**: Chairing executive sessions and serving as liaison between independent directors and management
- **C**: Approving all financial transactions

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Lead independent director: when CEO also chairs the board, the lead director provides independent leadership. Responsibilities: chairing meetings of independent directors, leading CEO evaluation, serving as shareholder contact, and ensuring board independence. Balances combined CEO/chair structure.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes management's role
- Option C describes operational/finance functions
