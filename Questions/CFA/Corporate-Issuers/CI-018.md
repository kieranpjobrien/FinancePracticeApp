# Question

The trade-off theory of capital structure suggests that optimal leverage balances:

## Options

- **A**: Debt and equity issuance costs only
- **B**: Tax benefits of debt against costs of financial distress
- **C**: Shareholder and bondholder interests

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Trade-off theory: optimal capital structure balances the marginal benefit of debt (tax shield) against marginal costs (financial distress, bankruptcy costs, agency costs). Firms with stable cash flows and tangible assets can support more debt. The optimal point maximizes firm value.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the primary trade-offs
- Option C describes a different consideration (agency conflicts)
