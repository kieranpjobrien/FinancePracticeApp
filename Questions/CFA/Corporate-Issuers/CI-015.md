# Question

A company's before-tax cost of debt is 8% and its marginal tax rate is 25%. The after-tax cost of debt is:

## Options

- **A**: 2%
- **B**: 6%
- **C**: 10%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

After-tax cost of debt = Pre-tax cost × (1 - Tax rate) = 8% × (1 - 0.25) = 8% × 0.75 = 6%. Interest is tax-deductible, creating a tax shield that reduces the effective cost. The after-tax cost is used in WACC calculations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A multiplies by tax rate instead of (1 - tax rate)
- Option C adds instead of multiplying
