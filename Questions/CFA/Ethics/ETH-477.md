# Question

An analyst learns that trading in a stock will be halted for a major announcement. Before the halt takes effect:

## Options

- **A**: The analyst may trade since halts are publicly announced
- **B**: Trading on advance knowledge of the halt would likely violate Standard II(A)
- **C**: Only short selling would be prohibited

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Advance knowledge of an impending trading halt is typically material nonpublic information under Standard II(A). Trading before the halt becomes public exploits information asymmetry, regardless of trade direction.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores that advance knowledge differs from public announcement
- Option C incorrectly limits the prohibition to short selling
