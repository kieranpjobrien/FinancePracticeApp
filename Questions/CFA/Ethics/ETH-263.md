# Question

Stakeholders in the investment management profession include:

## Options

- **A**: Only direct clients paying fees
- **B**: Clients, employers, colleagues, regulators, and the broader capital markets
- **C**: Only shareholders of publicly traded companies

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The investment profession affects many stakeholders: clients whose assets are managed, employers who rely on ethical employees, colleagues in the profession, regulators overseeing markets, and the broader public that depends on capital market integrity.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A too narrowly defines affected parties
- Option C focuses only on one type of market participant
