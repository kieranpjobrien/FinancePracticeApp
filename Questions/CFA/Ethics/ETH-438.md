# Question

Two advisors agree to refer clients to each other without any monetary exchange. Under Standard VI(C):

## Options

- **A**: No disclosure is required since no money changes hands
- **B**: The referral arrangement must be disclosed as the benefit of reciprocal referrals has value
- **C**: Only one-way referrals require disclosure

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(C) requires disclosure of referral arrangements regardless of form of compensation. Reciprocal referral agreements provide non-monetary benefits that could influence recommendations and must be disclosed.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A defines compensation too narrowly
- Option C creates an arbitrary distinction between referral types
