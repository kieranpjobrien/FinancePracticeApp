# Question

An analyst communicates investment rationale through instant messages with colleagues. Under Standard V(C):

## Options

- **A**: Instant messages are too informal to require retention
- **B**: These communications should be retained as they document the basis for recommendations
- **C**: Only formal written reports require retention

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(C) requires retention of records supporting investment analysis. Modern communications including instant messages and emails can contain material analysis and should be preserved regardless of format.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A exempts communications based on format
- Option C too narrowly defines what constitutes supporting records
