# Question

GIPS-compliant presentations must include:

## Options

- **A**: Performance for the most recent one-year period only
- **B**: At least five years of performance history, or since inception if shorter
- **C**: Performance since the firm's founding, regardless of data availability

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS requires firms to present at least five years of annual performance history, or since inception if the composite has existed for less than five years. After presenting five years, the firm must add annual returns each year until reaching a minimum of ten years of performance data.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A presents too short a history for meaningful evaluation
- Option C may require data that doesn't exist; GIPS sets practical minimums
