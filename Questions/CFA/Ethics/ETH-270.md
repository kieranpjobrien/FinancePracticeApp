# Question

Market manipulation harms capital markets by:

## Options

- **A**: Increasing trading volume
- **B**: Distorting price discovery and undermining investor confidence
- **C**: Reducing regulatory oversight

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Market manipulation distorts the natural price discovery process, causing prices to reflect artificial rather than genuine supply and demand. This undermines investor confidence and the efficient allocation of capital.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses volume with harm
- Option C describes a consequence rather than the mechanism of harm
