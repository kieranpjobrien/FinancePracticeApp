# Question

GIPS requires disclosure of composite dispersion because:

## Options

- **A**: It is required by securities regulators
- **B**: It shows how consistently the strategy was applied across portfolios
- **C**: It demonstrates the manager's skill level

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Dispersion measures variability of returns among portfolios in a composite. High dispersion may indicate inconsistent strategy application or significant client-specific constraints. This helps prospects evaluate consistency of management.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A misidentifies the requirement source
- Option C confuses consistency measurement with skill demonstration
