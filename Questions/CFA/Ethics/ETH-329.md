# Question

A reasonable basis for recommendations under Standard V(A) must be:

## Options

- **A**: Based solely on the analyst's intuition
- **B**: Supported by appropriate research and investigation
- **C**: Approved by a committee

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(A) requires that recommendations have a reasonable and adequate basis, supported by appropriate research and investigation. Intuition alone or committee approval without substance is insufficient.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A relies on unsupported judgment
- Option C substitutes process for substance
