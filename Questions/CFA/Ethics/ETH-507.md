# Question

A firm uses model portfolios and some client accounts deviate from the model due to tax considerations. Standard III(B):

## Options

- **A**: Requires all accounts to hold identical positions
- **B**: Permits deviation when serving client-specific needs like tax efficiency
- **C**: Is violated by any account-level customization

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(B) requires fair treatment, not identical treatment. Customizing for client-specific needs such as tax considerations is appropriate and does not violate fair dealing, as long as the approach serves client interests.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses fair with identical
- Option C prohibits beneficial customization
