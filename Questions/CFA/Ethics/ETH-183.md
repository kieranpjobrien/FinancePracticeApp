# Question

An analyst's compensation is partially based on investment banking revenue generated from companies she covers. Under Standard VI(A):

## Options

- **A**: This compensation structure is prohibited
- **B**: The arrangement must be disclosed in research reports
- **C**: Disclosure is only required to regulators

## Answer

B

## Explanation

While such compensation arrangements create conflicts, they are permitted if disclosed. Standard VI(A) requires disclosure of compensation arrangements that could affect objectivity so readers can evaluate potential bias.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is too restrictive; disclosure manages the conflict
- Option C ignores the primary duty to disclose to clients and readers
