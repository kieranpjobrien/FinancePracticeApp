# Question

A portfolio manager learns of a new buy recommendation and immediately purchases the stock for her largest institutional clients before informing smaller retail clients. This action:

## Options

- **A**: Is acceptable because institutional clients generate more revenue
- **B**: Violates Standard III(B) Fair Dealing
- **C**: Is required by the duty of loyalty to major clients

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(B) requires fair treatment in disseminating recommendations. All clients should receive investment recommendations fairly, regardless of account size. Discriminating based on revenue generation violates the fair dealing requirement.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly prioritizes revenue over fairness
- Option C misapplies loyalty to justify preferential treatment
