# Question

While employed, a member may engage in independent practice:

## Options

- **A**: Without any restrictions
- **B**: Only with written consent from the employer after full disclosure
- **C**: As long as it occurs outside business hours

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(A) requires disclosure and employer consent before engaging in independent practice that could compete with or affect the employer. The timing of the activity is not the determining factor.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores disclosure and consent requirements
- Option C focuses on timing rather than the nature of the conflict
