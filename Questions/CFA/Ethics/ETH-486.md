# Question

A firm allows employees to work remotely. Under Standard IV(C), supervisors must:

## Options

- **A**: Require all employees to work onsite for proper supervision
- **B**: Adapt supervisory procedures to effectively monitor remote work
- **C**: Reduce supervisory expectations for remote employees

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(C) requires reasonable supervision regardless of work location. Remote work requires adapted procedures—technology tools, communication protocols, and monitoring systems—to maintain effective oversight.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A fails to adapt to legitimate work arrangements
- Option C inappropriately reduces supervisory standards
