# Question

Conduct that reflects adversely on a member's professional reputation includes:

## Options

- **A**: Only violations that occur during working hours
- **B**: Any conduct involving dishonesty, fraud, or deceit
- **C**: Only conduct directly related to investment activities

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Professional standards extend to all conduct that involves dishonesty, fraud, deceit, or misrepresentation, regardless of when or where it occurs. Such conduct reflects on a professional's integrity and trustworthiness, which are essential qualities for investment professionals. The timing or context of the conduct is less important than its nature.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly limits the scope to working hours
- Option C too narrowly defines what conduct can harm professional reputation
