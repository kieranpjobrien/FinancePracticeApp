# Question

A "pump and dump" scheme violates Standard II(B) because it:

## Options

- **A**: Uses social media for promotion
- **B**: Artificially inflates prices through misleading promotion followed by selling
- **C**: Involves penny stocks

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard II(B) prohibits market manipulation. Pump and dump schemes manipulate prices through false or misleading promotion, then profit by selling into artificially inflated demand. The violation lies in the deception, not the medium or stock type.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A focuses on the platform rather than the deceptive conduct
- Option C incorrectly links manipulation to specific security types
