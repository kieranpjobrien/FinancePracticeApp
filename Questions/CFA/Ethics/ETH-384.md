# Question

A financial planner will receive a referral fee from a mortgage broker for each client referred. Under Standard VI(C), the planner must:

## Options

- **A**: Disclose the arrangement to clients before making any referral
- **B**: Only disclose if specifically asked by the client
- **C**: Keep the fee arrangement confidential to maintain broker relationships

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Standard VI(C) requires disclosure of referral fee arrangements before making recommendations. Clients need this information to evaluate whether the referral is made in their interest or is motivated by the compensation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B fails to provide proactive disclosure
- Option C prioritizes business relationships over client transparency
