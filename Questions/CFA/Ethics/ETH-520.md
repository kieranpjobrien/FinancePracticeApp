# Question

Standard VII(A) applies to conduct that:

## Options

- **A**: Only occurs during CFA exam administration
- **B**: Compromises the reputation or integrity of CFA Institute or the CFA designation
- **C**: Only affects other CFA candidates

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(A) broadly protects the integrity and reputation of CFA Institute and the designation. This extends beyond exam conduct to any actions that could damage the credibility of the CFA program.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A too narrowly limits to exam conduct
- Option C ignores broader reputational concerns
