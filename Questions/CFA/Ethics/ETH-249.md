# Question

A prospective client asks to see the firm's best-performing composite. Under Standard III(D):

## Options

- **A**: The firm should present only the highest-returning composite
- **B**: The firm should present performance of composites relevant to the prospect's investment needs
- **C**: The firm may choose any composite to show

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(D) requires fair and complete presentation. Showing only top performers without regard to the prospect's needs misrepresents likely outcomes. Relevant performance matching the prospect's objectives provides fair representation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A encourages cherry-picking
- Option C ignores the duty to present relevant information
