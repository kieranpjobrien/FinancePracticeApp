# Question

Under Standard I(A), members should encourage their employers to:

## Options

- **A**: Minimize regulatory compliance costs
- **B**: Establish compliance procedures
- **C**: Limit employee access to the Code and Standards

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(A) encourages members to support the establishment of compliance procedures by their employers. This helps create an environment where legal and ethical obligations can be met systematically.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A prioritizes cost over compliance
- Option C limits awareness of ethical obligations
