# Question

An investment professional operates in a jurisdiction where local securities laws are less restrictive than the professional standards she has committed to follow. In this situation, the professional should:

## Options

- **A**: Follow local law since it takes precedence in that jurisdiction
- **B**: Follow the stricter requirements of her professional standards
- **C**: Follow whichever set of rules her employer prefers

## Answer

B

## Explanation

When there is a conflict between local law and professional standards, members should follow whichever is stricter. Since professional standards in this case are more restrictive than local law, the member should adhere to those higher standards. This approach ensures consistent ethical behavior regardless of jurisdiction.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly suggests that legal compliance alone is sufficient when professional standards require more
- Option C inappropriately delegates ethical decisions to the employer; the professional must follow applicable standards
