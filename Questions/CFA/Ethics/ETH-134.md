# Question

A portfolio manager directs brokerage to a firm that provides research services in exchange for client commission dollars. This practice:

## Options

- **A**: Always violates fiduciary duty to clients
- **B**: Is permissible if the research benefits clients and is properly disclosed
- **C**: Is prohibited regardless of disclosure

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Soft dollar arrangements are permissible under Standard III(A) if the research services benefit the client accounts generating the commissions, best execution is maintained, and the arrangement is disclosed. The key is ensuring client benefit, not manager convenience.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is too absolute; soft dollars can benefit clients when properly managed
- Option C ignores that these arrangements are widely accepted when properly disclosed
