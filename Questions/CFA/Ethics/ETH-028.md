# Question

Professional codes of ethics for investment professionals typically emphasize promoting the integrity and viability of capital markets because:

## Options

- **A**: Well-functioning markets benefit all participants and support economic growth
- **B**: Market manipulation provides short-term profits that harm long-term returns
- **C**: Regulatory penalties for market manipulation are increasingly severe

## Answer

A

## Explanation

The emphasis on capital market integrity reflects the understanding that well-functioning, fair markets benefit all participants—investors, issuers, and intermediaries—and support broader economic growth and prosperity. When markets are perceived as fair and efficient, they attract more capital, provide better price discovery, and allocate resources more effectively.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B focuses on a narrow aspect (manipulation) rather than the broader principle of market integrity
- Option C incorrectly suggests the motivation is avoiding penalties rather than the fundamental value of market integrity
