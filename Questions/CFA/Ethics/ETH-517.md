# Question

Conflict disclosures under Standard VI(A) should be made:

## Options

- **A**: Only when clients request them
- **B**: Before or at the time of the recommendation
- **C**: After the investment decision is made

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(A) requires timely disclosure. Conflicts should be disclosed before or at the time of recommendations so clients can evaluate the information when making decisions. Post-decision disclosure is too late.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A waits for client inquiry
- Option C delays disclosure past the decision point
