# Question

A member is offered a position requiring relocation to a country with substantially different securities laws. Under Standard I(A):

## Options

- **A**: The member can rely solely on knowledge of home country laws
- **B**: The member must become familiar with applicable laws in the new jurisdiction
- **C**: Local law knowledge is only needed after working there for one year

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(A) requires understanding applicable laws wherever the member works. Moving to a new jurisdiction creates an obligation to learn relevant local laws before conducting regulated activities there.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A fails to recognize jurisdiction-specific requirements
- Option C delays necessary compliance knowledge
