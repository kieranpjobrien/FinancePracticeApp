# Question

After taking the CFA exam, a candidate posts on social media: "That question about derivatives hedging was tricky!" Under Standard VII(A):

## Options

- **A**: This is acceptable as it doesn't reveal specific answers
- **B**: This may violate the Standard by revealing exam content
- **C**: Only discussing questions with other candidates is prohibited

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(A) prohibits disclosing any specific exam content, including topics or types of questions that appeared. Even without revealing answers, discussing specific question topics compromises exam integrity.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A underestimates what constitutes impermissible disclosure
- Option C incorrectly limits the prohibition to conversations with candidates
