# Question

When initially claiming GIPS compliance, a firm must present a minimum of:

## Options

- **A**: Ten years of performance history
- **B**: Five years of GIPS-compliant performance, or since inception if shorter
- **C**: Only the most recent year

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS requires presenting at least five years of annual performance that is GIPS-compliant, or performance since the firm's inception or composite's inception if shorter than five years. The firm then builds toward a minimum ten-year track record.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A states the ultimate goal, not the initial requirement
- Option C falls short of minimum requirements
