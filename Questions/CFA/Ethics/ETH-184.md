# Question

A trust managed by a CFA charterholder holds securities for the benefit of the charterholder's children. Under Standard VI(B), this account should be treated as:

## Options

- **A**: A client account with normal priority
- **B**: A personal account subject to trading restrictions
- **C**: An account exempt from all restrictions

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Accounts in which the member has a beneficial interest, including trusts for family members, are treated as personal accounts under Standard VI(B). Trading restrictions and priority rules apply to prevent conflicts.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the personal beneficial interest
- Option C incorrectly exempts family accounts from conflict rules
