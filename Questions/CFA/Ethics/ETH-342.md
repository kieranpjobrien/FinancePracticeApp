# Question

A member serves as an unpaid advisor to a charity's investment committee. Under Standard I(A):

## Options

- **A**: Unpaid roles are exempt from the Standards
- **B**: The member must still follow applicable laws and Standards in this advisory capacity
- **C**: Only paid professional activities are covered

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(A) applies to all professional activities, regardless of compensation. Serving on an investment committee, even for a charity without pay, involves professional conduct subject to the Standards.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly exempts unpaid roles
- Option C incorrectly limits coverage to paid activities
