# Question

Which activity does NOT violate Standard II(B)?

## Options

- **A**: Spreading false information to move stock prices
- **B**: Conducting large trades as part of a legitimate investment strategy
- **C**: Creating artificial trading volume to mislead other investors

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard II(B) targets manipulative intent. Large trades for legitimate investment purposes, even if they affect prices, are not manipulation. The distinction lies in the purpose—genuine investing versus misleading others.

**Common mistakes:**
- Options A and C are clear manipulation violations
- Large legitimate trades should not be confused with manipulation
