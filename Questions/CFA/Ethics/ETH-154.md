# Question

Standard VI(A) Disclosure of Conflicts requires members to disclose conflicts of interest:

## Options

- **A**: Only when asked by clients
- **B**: In a full and fair manner to all affected parties
- **C**: Only to regulators

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(A) requires full and fair disclosure of all matters that could reasonably be expected to impair objectivity or interfere with duties to clients, prospective clients, and employers. Disclosure must be proactive, not just responsive to questions.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A fails the proactive disclosure requirement
- Option C ignores the primary duty to disclose to clients and employers
