# Question

The duty of loyalty to employers under Standard IV(A) is:

## Options

- **A**: Absolute and unlimited
- **B**: Limited by higher duties to clients and the law
- **C**: Only applicable during business hours

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(A) requires loyalty to employers, but this duty is subordinate to obligations to clients and legal requirements. Members cannot violate client duties or laws to serve employer interests.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the hierarchy of obligations
- Option C incorrectly limits when the duty applies
