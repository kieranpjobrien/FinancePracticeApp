# Question

Standard V(B) Communication with Clients requires members to distinguish between:

## Options

- **A**: Short-term and long-term recommendations
- **B**: Facts and opinions in investment analysis
- **C**: Domestic and international investments

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(B) requires members to clearly distinguish between statements of fact and opinions in their communications. This helps clients understand the basis for recommendations and make informed decisions.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A relates to investment horizon, not communication clarity
- Option C relates to investment geography, not communication requirements
