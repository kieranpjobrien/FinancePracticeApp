# Question

After a client relationship ends, the duty of confidentiality:

## Options

- **A**: Immediately terminates when the relationship ends
- **B**: Continues to apply to information obtained during the relationship
- **C**: Only applies if specified in the termination agreement

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(E) confidentiality obligations do not expire with the client relationship. Information learned during the relationship remains confidential. Former clients are entitled to continued protection of their private information.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly terminates obligations with the relationship
- Option C makes continued confidentiality contingent on documentation
