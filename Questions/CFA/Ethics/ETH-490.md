# Question

A firm's research department covers a company for which the firm is seeking investment banking business. Standard VI(A) requires:

## Options

- **A**: No special disclosure if the banking relationship is not yet established
- **B**: Disclosure of the potential conflict in research reports
- **C**: Suspension of research coverage

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(A) requires disclosure of matters that could reasonably affect objectivity. The pursuit of banking business creates a potential conflict that should be disclosed to allow clients to evaluate research independence.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A delays disclosure until conflict materializes
- Option C is overly restrictive when disclosure can address the concern
