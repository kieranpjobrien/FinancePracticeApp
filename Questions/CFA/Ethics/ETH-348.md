# Question

A firm participates in a secondary offering where demand exceeds supply. Under Standard III(B):

## Options

- **A**: The firm may allocate entirely to its best clients
- **B**: Allocation should be fair among interested clients, potentially on a pro-rata basis
- **C**: Secondary offerings are exempt from fair dealing requirements

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(B) applies to all investment opportunities including secondary offerings. When supply is limited, fair allocation methods such as pro-rata distribution help ensure no clients are systematically disadvantaged.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A allows favoritism based on client value
- Option C incorrectly exempts secondary offerings
