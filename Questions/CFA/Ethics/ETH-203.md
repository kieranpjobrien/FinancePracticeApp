# Question

An analyst is offered an equity stake in a start-up hedge fund that will compete with her current employer once launched. Under Standard IV(B):

## Options

- **A**: She may accept since the fund is not yet operational
- **B**: She must disclose to her employer and obtain consent before accepting
- **C**: Accepting any equity compensation requires resignation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

An equity stake that could create conflicts with current employment must be disclosed and approved by the employer. The fact that the competing business hasn't launched doesn't eliminate the conflict—it creates incentives that could affect current duties.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the prospective conflict of interest
- Option C is too restrictive; disclosure and consent may permit acceptance
