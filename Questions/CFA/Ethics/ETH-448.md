# Question

A statement that "This investment will double in value" violates Standard I(C) because it:

## Options

- **A**: Uses technical language
- **B**: Guarantees future performance that cannot be assured
- **C**: Expresses too much confidence

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(C) prohibits misrepresentation, including guaranteeing future investment performance. No investment outcome can be guaranteed, and such statements mislead clients about the nature of investment risk.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A focuses on language style rather than substance
- Option C understates the problem as mere overconfidence
