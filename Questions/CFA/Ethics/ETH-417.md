# Question

A member manages assets for both institutional and retail clients. The fiduciary duty:

## Options

- **A**: Applies more strictly to institutional clients due to larger asset sizes
- **B**: Applies equally to all clients regardless of asset size or sophistication
- **C**: Only applies to clients who have signed fiduciary agreements

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The duty of loyalty, prudence, and care applies equally to all clients. Asset size and client sophistication do not alter the fundamental fiduciary obligations. All clients deserve the same standard of ethical treatment.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A inappropriately varies the standard by client size
- Option C incorrectly requires formal agreements for fiduciary duties to apply
