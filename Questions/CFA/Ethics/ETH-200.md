# Question

After a client relationship ends, the duty of confidentiality under Standard III(E):

## Options

- **A**: Ends immediately upon termination of the relationship
- **B**: Continues indefinitely regarding information obtained during the relationship
- **C**: Lasts for exactly seven years

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The duty of confidentiality under Standard III(E) extends beyond the term of the professional relationship. Information obtained while serving a client remains confidential even after the relationship ends.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly terminates the duty with the relationship
- Option C invents an arbitrary time limit not specified in the Standards
