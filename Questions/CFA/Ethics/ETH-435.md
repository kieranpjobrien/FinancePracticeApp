# Question

An analyst leaving a firm wants to take copies of research reports she authored. Under Standard V(C):

## Options

- **A**: She may take copies since she created the work
- **B**: The records belong to the employer and should not be taken without permission
- **C**: Only final published reports may be taken

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(C) recognizes that research records typically belong to the employer. While members should maintain records for the retention period, they cannot take employer property when departing without explicit permission.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses authorship with ownership
- Option C creates an artificial distinction without basis
