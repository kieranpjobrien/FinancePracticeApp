# Question

GIPS compliance is:

## Options

- **A**: Required for all investment firms globally
- **B**: Voluntary but must be applied on a firm-wide basis if adopted
- **C**: Required only for firms managing pension assets

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS compliance is voluntary, but firms that choose to claim compliance must do so on a firm-wide basis. Partial compliance or compliance for only certain products is not permitted.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A overstates the requirement; GIPS is voluntary
- Option C incorrectly limits the scope of GIPS applicability
