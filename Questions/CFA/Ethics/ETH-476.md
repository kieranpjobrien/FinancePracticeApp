# Question

A CFA member posts derogatory comments about colleagues on social media. This conduct:

## Options

- **A**: Is protected personal expression
- **B**: May violate Standard I(D) if it reflects adversely on professional integrity
- **C**: Only matters if it mentions the member's CFA designation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(D) covers conduct reflecting adversely on professional reputation and integrity. Unprofessional behavior on social media can damage the member's reputation and that of the profession, regardless of whether the CFA designation is mentioned.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly applies free speech concepts to professional standards
- Option C ties misconduct only to designation use
