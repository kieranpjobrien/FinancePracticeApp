# Question

A manager charges clients different fees for similar services without disclosure. This practice:

## Options

- **A**: Is normal business practice
- **B**: May violate Standard III(A) by failing to treat clients fairly
- **C**: Is only problematic if clients ask about fees

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(A) requires fair dealing with clients. While some fee differentiation may be appropriate, undisclosed fee disparities for similar services can indicate unfair treatment and should be transparent.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores fair dealing requirements
- Option C shifts disclosure burden to clients
