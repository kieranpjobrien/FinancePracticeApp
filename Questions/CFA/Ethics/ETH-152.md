# Question

Standard V(C) Record Retention requires members to:

## Options

- **A**: Retain records indefinitely
- **B**: Develop and maintain records that support their investment analysis and actions
- **C**: Keep only records that show positive outcomes

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(C) requires members to maintain records that support their recommendations, decisions, and actions. This includes documentation of research, analysis, and the basis for investment decisions. Records should be kept according to applicable regulatory requirements.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A overstates the retention period requirement
- Option C ignores the need for complete documentation regardless of outcome
