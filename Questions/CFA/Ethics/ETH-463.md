# Question

A financial advisor's compensation is based on sales commissions. Standard VI(A) requires:

## Options

- **A**: Hiding this information to avoid client concerns
- **B**: Disclosure of the compensation structure to clients
- **C**: Conversion to a fee-only model

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(A) requires disclosure of compensation arrangements that could affect objectivity. Commission-based compensation creates potential conflicts that clients should understand when evaluating recommendations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A hides material conflicts
- Option C requires a business model change rather than disclosure
