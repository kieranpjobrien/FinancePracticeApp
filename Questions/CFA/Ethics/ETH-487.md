# Question

Standard V(A) requires that investment recommendations:

## Options

- **A**: Remain valid indefinitely once established
- **B**: Be reviewed and updated as conditions change
- **C**: Only reflect conditions at the time of original analysis

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(A) requires ongoing diligence. Recommendations should be reviewed and updated as market conditions, company circumstances, or other relevant factors change. Static recommendations become stale and potentially unsuitable.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A treats recommendations as permanent
- Option C ignores the ongoing nature of analytical responsibility
