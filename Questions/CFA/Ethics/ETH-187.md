# Question

GIPS requires the use of time-weighted returns primarily because:

## Options

- **A**: They are easier to calculate
- **B**: They eliminate the impact of external cash flows, allowing fair comparison of manager skill
- **C**: They always show higher performance than money-weighted returns

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Time-weighted returns remove the impact of cash flows that are typically outside the manager's control. This allows fair comparison of investment skill across managers regardless of client deposit and withdrawal timing.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A misunderstands calculation complexity
- Option C is factually incorrect; time-weighted returns are not systematically higher
