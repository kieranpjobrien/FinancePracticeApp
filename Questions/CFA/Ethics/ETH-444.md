# Question

An analyst believes market conditions justify an exception to normal ethical procedures. The appropriate response is:

## Options

- **A**: Proceed since exceptional circumstances warrant flexibility
- **B**: Maintain ethical standards as they apply regardless of market conditions
- **C**: Seek approval from clients before making the exception

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Ethical standards apply consistently regardless of market conditions. The Code and Standards do not provide exceptions for difficult markets. Members must maintain ethical conduct in all circumstances.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A allows situational ethics to undermine principles
- Option C incorrectly suggests client approval can authorize ethical violations
