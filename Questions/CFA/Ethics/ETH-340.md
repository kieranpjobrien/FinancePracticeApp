# Question

Ethical violations in the investment profession can result in:

## Options

- **A**: Only reputational damage to the individual
- **B**: Harm to clients, markets, and the profession's reputation
- **C**: No meaningful consequences if not detected

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Ethical violations can harm clients who suffer losses, damage market integrity affecting all participants, and undermine public trust in the investment profession. The impact extends well beyond the individual violator.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A underestimates the broader impact
- Option C ignores the real harm caused regardless of detection
