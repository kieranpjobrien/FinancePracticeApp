# Question

An analyst's firm is underwriting an IPO. The analyst wants to purchase shares for her personal account. Standard VI(B) requires:

## Options

- **A**: Client orders must be filled before any personal purchases
- **B**: The analyst may purchase simultaneously with clients
- **C**: Personal participation is unrestricted in underwritten offerings

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Standard VI(B) requires client and employer transactions to take priority over personal trades. In IPO situations where demand exceeds supply, clients must receive allocation priority before employees purchase shares.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B ignores the priority requirement
- Option C exempts IPOs from transaction priority rules
