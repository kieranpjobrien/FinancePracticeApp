# Question

Promoting the integrity of the investment profession includes:

## Options

- **A**: Maximizing personal compensation as a sign of professional success
- **B**: Encouraging ethical behavior among colleagues and subordinates
- **C**: Criticizing competing firms to enhance one's own reputation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Investment professionals should actively promote ethical behavior within their organizations and the broader profession. This includes leading by example, providing ethics training to subordinates, and creating a culture where ethical conduct is expected and rewarded. The goal is to elevate standards across the profession.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses financial success with professional integrity
- Option C involves conduct that may itself be unethical and harms the profession
