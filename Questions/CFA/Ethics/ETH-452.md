# Question

A client directs the manager to use a specific broker who charges higher commissions. Under Standard III(A):

## Options

- **A**: The manager must refuse and use lower-cost brokers
- **B**: The manager may follow the direction after disclosing cost implications
- **C**: Client directions override all fiduciary considerations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(A) allows following client-directed brokerage instructions when the client understands the cost implications. The manager should disclose that higher costs may affect performance, then may implement the client's informed decision.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores client autonomy over their own accounts
- Option C removes the disclosure and education requirement
