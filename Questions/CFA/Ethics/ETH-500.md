# Question

A member advises clients across multiple countries with varying regulatory requirements. Standard I(A) requires:

## Options

- **A**: Following only home country regulations
- **B**: Understanding and complying with applicable laws in each jurisdiction
- **C**: Applying the least restrictive regulations to all clients

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(A) requires knowledge of and compliance with applicable laws. For cross-border activities, members must understand and follow regulations in each relevant jurisdiction, applying the stricter of local law or the Code and Standards.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores foreign regulatory requirements
- Option C risks violating stricter local requirements
