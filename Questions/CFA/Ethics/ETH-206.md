# Question

An analyst's firm has no formal record retention policy. Under Standard V(C), the analyst:

## Options

- **A**: Has no obligation since the firm sets retention policy
- **B**: Should retain records in the absence of firm or regulatory requirements
- **C**: May destroy records immediately after publishing research

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(C) creates a personal obligation to maintain appropriate records regardless of firm policy. When employers or regulators don't specify retention periods, members should maintain records that support their recommendations and actions.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly limits responsibility to firm requirements
- Option C ignores the need to support recommendations with documentation
