# Question

Standard VI(C) referral fee disclosure applies to:

## Options

- **A**: Only fees paid by the member for receiving referrals
- **B**: Both fees paid for receiving referrals and fees received for giving referrals
- **C**: Only fees received for giving referrals

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(C) requires disclosure of referral fee arrangements in both directions—whether the member pays for clients received or receives payment for clients referred. Both create potential conflicts.

**Common mistakes:**
- Options A and C each capture only one direction of referral fee arrangements
