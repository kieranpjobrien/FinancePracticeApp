# Question

A new client account must be added to the appropriate composite:

## Options

- **A**: At the end of the first full year under management
- **B**: On a timely and consistent basis according to firm policy
- **C**: Only after achieving positive performance

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS requires new portfolios to be included in composites on a timely and consistent basis. Firms must have documented policies for when portfolios enter composites. Delaying entry to wait for performance is prohibited.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A delays inclusion too long
- Option C allows cherry-picking based on performance
