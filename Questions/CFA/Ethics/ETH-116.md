# Question

Under the CFA Institute Code of Ethics, members must practice and encourage others to practice in a manner that will:

## Options

- **A**: Maximize returns for clients
- **B**: Reflect credit on themselves and the profession
- **C**: Minimize regulatory scrutiny

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The Code of Ethics requires members to practice and encourage others to practice in a professional and ethical manner that reflects credit on themselves, the profession, and the CFA designation. This emphasizes professional reputation and integrity.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A focuses only on investment outcomes, not ethical conduct
- Option C suggests avoiding oversight, which contradicts ethical principles
