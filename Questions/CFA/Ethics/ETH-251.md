# Question

Standard IV(B) requires disclosure of additional compensation arrangements:

## Options

- **A**: After the arrangement concludes
- **B**: Before entering into the arrangement
- **C**: Only if the employer specifically asks

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(B) requires obtaining written consent before accepting additional compensation that could create conflicts. Disclosure must be made in advance, not after the fact.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A delays disclosure until after potential conflicts arise
- Option C fails the proactive disclosure requirement
