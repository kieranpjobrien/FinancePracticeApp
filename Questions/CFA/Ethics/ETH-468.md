# Question

A GIPS-compliant firm discovers a calculation error that overstated past performance by 0.02%. The firm should:

## Options

- **A**: Immediately notify all clients and prospects who received the incorrect information
- **B**: Evaluate materiality and correct prospectively if the error is immaterial
- **C**: Ignore the error since it is small

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS requires error correction but allows consideration of materiality. Small errors may be corrected prospectively without retroactive notification. Firms should have policies defining material errors and correction procedures.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A treats all errors the same regardless of materiality
- Option C ignores the obligation to correct errors
