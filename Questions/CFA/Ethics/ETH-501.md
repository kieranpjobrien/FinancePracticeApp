# Question

A buy-side analyst's portfolio manager pressures her to maintain a favorable rating on a stock the manager owns in large quantities. Under Standard I(B):

## Options

- **A**: Internal pressure is exempt from independence requirements
- **B**: The analyst must maintain objectivity regardless of internal pressure
- **C**: The analyst should follow the portfolio manager's guidance

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(B) requires independence from all pressure sources, including internal. Analysts must base recommendations on objective analysis regardless of who applies pressure or their position within the organization.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly exempts internal sources
- Option C subordinates analyst judgment to manager preference
