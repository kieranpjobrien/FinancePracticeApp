# Question

An analyst issues a buy recommendation based solely on a tip from a trusted colleague without conducting independent research. This:

## Options

- **A**: Is acceptable if the colleague has a strong track record
- **B**: Violates Standard V(A) because recommendations require a reasonable basis from the analyst's own analysis
- **C**: Is permissible if the tip is disclosed to clients

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(A) requires a reasonable and adequate basis for recommendations. Analysts must conduct their own diligent research and cannot rely solely on tips, regardless of the source's reputation or disclosure to clients.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A accepts colleague reputation as a substitute for analysis
- Option C incorrectly suggests disclosure cures lack of analysis
