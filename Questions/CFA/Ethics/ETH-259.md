# Question

A firm's advertisement states "Our team includes 10 CFAs." Under Standard VII(B):

## Options

- **A**: This is proper use of the designation
- **B**: This improperly uses CFA as a noun rather than adjective
- **C**: Numbers should not be disclosed in advertising

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(B) requires proper use of the designation. "CFA charterholder" should be used, not "a CFA" as a noun. Correct phrasing would be "10 CFA charterholders" rather than "10 CFAs."

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores proper usage requirements
- Option C invents a prohibition on numbers
