# Question

A CFA charterholder is arrested for drunk driving on a weekend. Under Standard I(D):

## Options

- **A**: Personal legal matters have no bearing on professional standing
- **B**: Conduct that reflects poorly on professional integrity may be relevant
- **C**: Only financial crimes trigger Standard I(D)

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(D) covers conduct that reflects adversely on professional reputation, integrity, or competence. While a single DUI might not automatically violate the Standard, patterns of behavior or serious offenses can affect professional standing.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly separates personal and professional conduct entirely
- Option C too narrowly limits the types of relevant misconduct
