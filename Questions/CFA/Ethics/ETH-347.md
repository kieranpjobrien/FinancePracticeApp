# Question

The duty of loyalty under Standard III(A) requires:

## Options

- **A**: Blind obedience to client instructions
- **B**: Acting in clients' best interests while using professional judgment
- **C**: Maximizing short-term returns regardless of risk

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(A) requires members to act in clients' best interests using professional judgment. This is not blind obedience; members should advise against unsuitable actions and consider long-term interests.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the role of professional judgment
- Option C conflates best interest with maximum short-term return
