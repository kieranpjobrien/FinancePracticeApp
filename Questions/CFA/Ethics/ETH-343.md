# Question

An analyst receives excessive entertainment from a company she covers. Under Standard I(B), this may:

## Options

- **A**: Enhance the relationship and improve information access
- **B**: Compromise independence and objectivity requiring disclosure or refusal
- **C**: Be acceptable if it is industry standard practice

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Excessive entertainment can create obligations that compromise objectivity. Standard I(B) requires members to decline excessive benefits or, at minimum, disclose them and manage the resulting conflict.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A focuses on benefits while ignoring objectivity concerns
- Option C uses industry practice to justify potential compromises
