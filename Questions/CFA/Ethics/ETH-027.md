# Question

A code of ethics requirement to "practice and encourage others to practice in a professional and ethical manner" most directly promotes:

## Options

- **A**: A culture of integrity throughout the investment profession
- **B**: Whistleblowing on colleagues who violate regulations
- **C**: Increased regulatory oversight of industry participants

## Answer

A

## Explanation

This requirement promotes a culture of integrity by making ethical conduct a shared responsibility. Members are expected not only to behave ethically themselves but also to encourage ethical behavior in others, creating a professional environment where high standards are the norm rather than the exception.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B is too narrow; while reporting violations may sometimes be necessary, the broader goal is encouraging positive ethical behavior
- Option C misunderstands the purpose; the goal is industry self-governance through shared values, not increased external regulation
