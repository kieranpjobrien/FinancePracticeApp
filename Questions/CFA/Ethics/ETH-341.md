# Question

CFA candidates are required to:

## Options

- **A**: Follow only the exam rules, not the full Code and Standards
- **B**: Comply with the Code of Ethics and Standards of Professional Conduct
- **C**: Wait until becoming charterholders to follow ethical standards

## Answer

B

## Explanation

CFA candidates are bound by the Code of Ethics and Standards of Professional Conduct from the time they register for the CFA Program, not just after becoming charterholders.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly limits candidate obligations
- Option C delays ethical obligations inappropriately
