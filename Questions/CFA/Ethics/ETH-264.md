# Question

The difference between a compliance-based culture and an ethics-based culture is that:

## Options

- **A**: Compliance cultures produce better ethical outcomes
- **B**: Ethics-based cultures focus on doing what is right, not just what is required
- **C**: Compliance-based cultures are voluntary

## Answer

B

## Explanation

A compliance-based culture focuses on meeting minimum legal requirements, while an ethics-based culture emphasizes doing what is right. Ethics-based cultures often lead to better outcomes because they address the spirit, not just the letter, of appropriate conduct.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A assumes compliance cultures are superior
- Option C misunderstands that compliance is typically mandatory
