# Question

An analyst is under pressure from senior management to issue a favorable research report on a company that is also a major investment banking client. This situation primarily illustrates:

## Options

- **A**: A situational influence that may challenge ethical behavior
- **B**: An acceptable business practice in integrated financial firms
- **C**: A legal violation requiring immediate reporting

## Answer

A

## Explanation

This scenario illustrates a situational influence—pressure from authority figures combined with conflicts of interest—that can challenge ethical decision-making. Recognizing such influences is the first step in maintaining ethical behavior. The situation itself is not necessarily illegal but creates ethical risks.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B ignores the ethical concerns inherent in this conflict of interest
- Option C overstates the situation; pressure alone is not a legal violation, though acting on it unethically could be
