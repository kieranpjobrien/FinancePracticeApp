# Question

Information-based manipulation under Standard II(B) includes:

## Options

- **A**: Publishing accurate research that differs from consensus
- **B**: Spreading false rumors to influence security prices
- **C**: Legitimate marketing of investment products

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard II(B) prohibits information-based manipulation such as spreading false or misleading information to affect prices. Accurate research and legitimate marketing do not constitute manipulation.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses legitimate analysis with manipulation
- Option C mischaracterizes normal business activities
