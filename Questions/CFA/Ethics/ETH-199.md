# Question

When managing assets for an institutional client with an investment policy statement, Standard III(C) suitability applies:

## Options

- **A**: Only to retail clients, not institutional clients
- **B**: At the total portfolio level, ensuring investments align with the stated IPS
- **C**: To each security individually, regardless of portfolio context

## Answer

B

## Explanation

For institutional clients, suitability under Standard III(C) is evaluated at the portfolio level against the investment policy statement. Individual securities should be suitable in the context of the overall portfolio and stated investment objectives.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly exempts institutional relationships
- Option C ignores portfolio context in evaluating individual holdings
