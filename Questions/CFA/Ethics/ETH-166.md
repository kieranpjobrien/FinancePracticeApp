# Question

Under GIPS, a composite must include:

## Options

- **A**: Only the firm's best-performing portfolios
- **B**: All fee-paying discretionary portfolios managed according to a similar strategy
- **C**: Both discretionary and non-discretionary accounts

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS requires composites to include all actual fee-paying discretionary portfolios managed according to a similar investment strategy or objective. Cherry-picking best performers is prohibited, and non-discretionary accounts are excluded.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A violates the requirement for representative inclusion
- Option C incorrectly includes non-discretionary accounts
