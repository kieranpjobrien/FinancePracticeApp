# Question

Standard I(C) Misrepresentation prohibits members from guaranteeing:

## Options

- **A**: The quality of their research process
- **B**: Specific investment returns or the preservation of capital
- **C**: Their professional qualifications

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(C) prohibits members from making any statements that guarantee specific investment returns or the preservation of capital. Investments inherently involve risk, and guaranteeing outcomes misrepresents the nature of investing.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A relates to describing methodology, which is allowed
- Option C relates to accurately stating credentials, which is required
