# Question

If a charterholder allows CFA Institute membership to lapse, they:

## Options

- **A**: May continue using the CFA designation indefinitely
- **B**: May not use the CFA designation until membership is reinstated
- **C**: Permanently lose the right to use the designation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The right to use the CFA designation requires maintaining CFA Institute membership. If membership lapses, use of the designation must cease until membership is reinstated. The right is not permanently lost but is suspended.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the membership requirement
- Option C overstates the consequences of lapsed membership
