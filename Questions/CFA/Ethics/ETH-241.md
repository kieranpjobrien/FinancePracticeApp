# Question

Under Standard I(A), claiming ignorance of a law:

## Options

- **A**: Is a valid defense if the law is complex
- **B**: Does not excuse violations
- **C**: Only applies to new regulations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(A) requires members to understand and comply with applicable laws. Claiming ignorance is not a defense—members have a duty to know the laws that govern their professional activities.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A accepts ignorance as an excuse
- Option C incorrectly limits when knowledge is expected
