# Question

GIPS verification:

## Options

- **A**: Is required for all firms claiming compliance
- **B**: Is encouraged but not required, and tests firm-wide compliance processes
- **C**: Certifies the accuracy of individual composite returns

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS verification is recommended but not required. Verification assesses whether the firm has complied with all composite construction requirements and whether processes are designed to calculate and present performance in compliance with GIPS. It does not certify individual composite returns.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A overstates the verification requirement
- Option C confuses verification scope with performance certification
