# Question

An investment bank's research department covers a company for which the bank is also providing investment banking services. The potential conflict should be addressed by:

## Options

- **A**: Having the investment bankers review research for accuracy before publication
- **B**: Disclosing the investment banking relationship in research reports
- **C**: Only publishing positive research during the engagement period

## Answer

B

## Explanation

The investment banking relationship creates a potential conflict that should be disclosed to research readers. Clients can then consider whether the relationship might influence the research conclusions. Information barriers should also exist between departments, but disclosure is essential for transparency.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A would worsen the conflict by allowing bankers to influence research
- Option C would be a clear violation—allowing the conflict to actually bias the output
