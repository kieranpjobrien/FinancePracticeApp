# Question

A firm's largest client requests advance notice of rating changes before other clients. Under Standard III(B):

## Options

- **A**: This is acceptable since the client generates the most revenue
- **B**: Providing advance notice would violate fair dealing requirements
- **C**: This is permitted if the client pays a premium for the service

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(B) requires fair dissemination of recommendations to all clients. Providing advance notice to favored clients—regardless of their revenue contribution or willingness to pay—creates unfair advantages and violates fair dealing.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly prioritizes revenue over fair treatment
- Option C suggests that payment justifies unfair treatment
