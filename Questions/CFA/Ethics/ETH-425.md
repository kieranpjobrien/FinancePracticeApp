# Question

A manager has discretion over client portfolios including proxy voting authority. Standard III(A) requires:

## Options

- **A**: Voting with management on all proxies for simplicity
- **B**: Voting proxies in clients' best interests with appropriate policies and disclosure
- **C**: Abstaining from all proxy votes to avoid conflicts

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(A) requires proxy votes to be cast in clients' best interests. Managers with voting authority must establish policies, disclose their approach, and vote thoughtfully rather than reflexively with management or abstaining.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A doesn't ensure votes serve client interests
- Option C abdicates the voting responsibility
