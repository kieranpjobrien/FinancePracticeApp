# Question

When regulations change, Standard I(A) expects members to:

## Options

- **A**: Wait for employer training before complying
- **B**: Proactively stay informed and update their practices accordingly
- **C**: Continue following previous rules until formally notified

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(A) requires members to understand and comply with current laws and regulations. This includes proactively staying informed about changes and updating practices, not passively waiting for notification.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A delays necessary compliance
- Option C creates gaps between regulatory changes and compliance
