# Question

A portfolio manager is invited to speak at a conference for a substantial fee. Under Standard IV(B):

## Options

- **A**: The fee may be accepted without disclosure if it doesn't interfere with work
- **B**: Disclosure to and consent from the employer is required before accepting
- **C**: Speaking fees are exempt from compensation disclosure requirements

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(B) requires disclosure and employer consent for compensation from outside parties. Speaking fees represent additional compensation that could create conflicts and must be disclosed before acceptance.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the disclosure requirement
- Option C incorrectly exempts a category of compensation
