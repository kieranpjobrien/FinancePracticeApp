# Question

A manager uses aggregated, anonymized client data for marketing purposes. Standard III(E):

## Options

- **A**: Prohibits any use of client data for marketing
- **B**: May permit this if the data is truly anonymized and does not identify individual clients
- **C**: Requires individual client consent for any data use

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(E) protects client confidentiality. Properly anonymized and aggregated data that cannot identify individual clients may be used. The key is ensuring no individual client information is disclosed or can be inferred.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A prohibits potentially acceptable practices
- Option C may be impractical for truly anonymized data
