# Question

A research department releases a new "buy" recommendation. Standard III(B) Fair Dealing requires:

## Options

- **A**: Releasing the recommendation to all clients simultaneously
- **B**: Disseminating to clients in a fair manner, though not necessarily simultaneously
- **C**: Giving priority to the firm's largest clients

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard III(B) requires fair dissemination of recommendations, meaning all clients should have a fair opportunity to act. While simultaneous release is ideal, it may not always be practical. The key is that no clients are systematically advantaged over others.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is the ideal but the Standard requires fairness, not strict simultaneity
- Option C violates fair dealing by prioritizing based on account size
