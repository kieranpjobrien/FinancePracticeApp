# Question

A marketing presentation overstates the firm's assets under management by including committed but uninvested capital. Under Standard I(C):

## Options

- **A**: Including committed capital is standard industry practice
- **B**: This may misrepresent the firm's actual operating scale
- **C**: Only investment performance can be misrepresented

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(C) prohibits misrepresentation of qualifications, services, or performance. Overstating assets under management by including uninvested commitments misrepresents the firm's actual operating scale and capabilities.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A justifies misrepresentation with industry practice
- Option C too narrowly limits what can be misrepresented
