# Question

A portfolio manager serves on the board of directors of a company held in client portfolios. Under Standard VI(A):

## Options

- **A**: This is prohibited and the manager must resign from the board
- **B**: The manager must disclose this relationship to clients and manage any conflicts
- **C**: No disclosure is needed if the board position is unpaid

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Board membership creates potential conflicts that must be disclosed to affected clients. The manager may continue serving if conflicts are managed appropriately through disclosure and, where necessary, recusal from certain decisions. Payment status does not determine disclosure requirements.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is too restrictive; board service is allowed with proper disclosure
- Option C incorrectly links disclosure to compensation
