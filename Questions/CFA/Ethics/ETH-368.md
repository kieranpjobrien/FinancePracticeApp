# Question

Standard I(D) Misconduct applies to:

## Options

- **A**: Only investment-related misconduct
- **B**: Conduct that reflects adversely on professional integrity, whether or not investment-related
- **C**: Only conduct during business hours

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard I(D) covers any conduct reflecting adversely on professional reputation, integrity, or competence—not limited to investment activities or business hours. Personal misconduct can also violate this Standard.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A too narrowly limits coverage
- Option C incorrectly restricts by time
