# Question

"Everyone does it" is an example of what type of challenge to ethical behavior?

## Options

- **A**: A legitimate justification for questionable conduct
- **B**: A rationalization that can lead to ethical lapses
- **C**: An accurate assessment of industry norms

## Answer

B

## Explanation

"Everyone does it" is a common rationalization that allows individuals to justify unethical behavior by suggesting it is normal or acceptable. This thinking pattern can erode ethical standards and lead to violations, regardless of what others actually do.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A accepts rationalization as valid reasoning
- Option C treats the rationalization as factual assessment
