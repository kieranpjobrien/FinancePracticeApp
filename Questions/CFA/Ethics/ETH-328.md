# Question

A supervisor may delegate compliance monitoring to subordinates:

## Options

- **A**: And thereby eliminate personal responsibility
- **B**: But must ensure the subordinates are qualified and monitor their effectiveness
- **C**: Only with explicit regulatory approval

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard IV(C) permits delegation of monitoring activities, but supervisors retain responsibility for ensuring delegates are qualified and that monitoring is effective. Delegation transfers tasks, not responsibility.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A incorrectly suggests delegation eliminates responsibility
- Option C invents a regulatory approval requirement
