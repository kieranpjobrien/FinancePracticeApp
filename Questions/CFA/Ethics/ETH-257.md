# Question

Under Standard VI(C), referral fees that must be disclosed include:

## Options

- **A**: Only cash payments
- **B**: All forms of compensation including cash, gifts, and services
- **C**: Only fees exceeding certain thresholds

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VI(C) requires disclosure of all compensation for referrals, regardless of form. This includes cash payments, gifts, entertainment, services, or any other benefit received for making referrals.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores non-cash compensation
- Option C creates a threshold that doesn't exist in the Standard
