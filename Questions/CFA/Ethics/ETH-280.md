# Question

An analyst relies on third-party research for a recommendation. Under Standard V(C), the analyst should retain:

## Options

- **A**: Only the final recommendation
- **B**: The third-party research and any analysis performed on it
- **C**: No records since the third party holds the original research

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(C) requires maintaining records supporting investment analysis and recommendations. When relying on third-party research, the analyst should retain copies of that research plus any independent analysis performed.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A fails to document the analytical basis
- Option C incorrectly shifts record-keeping to third parties
