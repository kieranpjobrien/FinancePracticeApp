# Question

The primary purpose of maintaining records under Standard V(C) is to:

## Options

- **A**: Satisfy only regulatory requirements
- **B**: Support the basis for investment actions and recommendations
- **C**: Create a historical archive

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard V(C) emphasizes maintaining records that support and document the basis for investment decisions and recommendations. This serves transparency, accountability, and defense of actions taken.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A focuses only on regulatory compliance
- Option C emphasizes preservation without purpose
