# Question

GIPS requires presentation of internal dispersion of composite returns to show:

## Options

- **A**: The range of fees charged
- **B**: The variability of returns across portfolios within the composite
- **C**: Comparison to competitor performance

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Internal dispersion measures the spread of individual portfolio returns within a composite. This helps prospective clients understand how consistently the strategy is applied and the range of outcomes they might experience.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A addresses fees, not return dispersion
- Option C confuses internal dispersion with external benchmarking
