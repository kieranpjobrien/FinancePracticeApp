# Question

After taking the CFA exam, a candidate may:

## Options

- **A**: Discuss specific exam questions on social media
- **B**: Discuss general topics covered without revealing specific questions
- **C**: Share questions with candidates taking the exam in other locations

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Standard VII(A) protects exam confidentiality. Candidates may discuss general topic coverage but cannot share specific questions. This applies regardless of platform or audience.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A directly violates exam confidentiality
- Option C constitutes sharing confidential information
