# Question

Under GIPS, the definition of "firm" is important because:

## Options

- **A**: It determines which regulator has jurisdiction
- **B**: Compliance claims apply to the entire defined firm, not just portions
- **C**: Smaller firms are exempt from GIPS

## Answer

B

## Explanation

GIPS compliance applies to the entire defined firm. The firm definition determines the scope of compliance obligations. Partial compliance or excluding certain divisions from the firm definition for convenience is not permitted.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses GIPS with regulatory jurisdiction
- Option C incorrectly suggests size-based exemptions
