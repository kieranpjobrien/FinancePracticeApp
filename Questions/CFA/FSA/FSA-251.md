# Question

Inventory has a cost of $50,000, a selling price of $55,000, and estimated selling costs of $8,000. Under IFRS, the inventory should be reported at:

## Options

- **A**: $47,000
- **B**: $50,000
- **C**: $55,000

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Net realizable value (NRV) = Selling price - Costs to complete and sell = $55,000 - $8,000 = $47,000. Since NRV ($47,000) < Cost ($50,000), inventory is written down to NRV. The $3,000 loss is recognized in cost of goods sold. IFRS uses lower of cost or NRV.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B fails to recognize the impairment
- Option C uses selling price instead of NRV
