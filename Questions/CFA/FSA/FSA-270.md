# Question

The fixed charge coverage ratio adds to the numerator and denominator:

## Options

- **A**: Dividend payments
- **B**: Lease payments
- **C**: Principal repayments

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Fixed charge coverage = (EBIT + Lease payments) / (Interest + Lease payments). It expands on interest coverage by including lease obligations, which are effectively fixed financing costs. This ratio is particularly useful when companies have significant operating leases. Higher ratios indicate better ability to meet fixed obligations.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A—dividends are discretionary, not fixed charges
- Option C—principal is already excluded from interest coverage; this ratio focuses on periodic charges
