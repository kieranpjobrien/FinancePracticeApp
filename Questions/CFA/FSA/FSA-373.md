# Question

PPE note disclosures typically include:

## Options

- **A**: Future market values
- **B**: Gross carrying amounts, accumulated depreciation, and depreciation methods
- **C**: Names of equipment suppliers

## Answer

B

## Explanation

PPE disclosures include: gross amounts by category, accumulated depreciation, depreciation methods and useful lives, impairment losses, reconciliation of opening/closing balances (additions, disposals, depreciation). This enables ratio analysis and assessment of asset age and reinvestment patterns.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is forward-looking and uncertain—not disclosed
- Option C is supplier information, not relevant for financial analysis
