# Question

Segment reporting requires disclosure of:

## Options

- **A**: Only the most profitable operating segment
- **B**: Operating segments that meet quantitative thresholds
- **C**: All geographic regions regardless of size

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Companies must report segments meeting any of: revenue ≥ 10% of combined segment revenue, profit/loss ≥ 10% of combined profit/loss, or assets ≥ 10% of combined assets. Segments are based on internal management reporting (management approach). This helps analysts understand business composition and risks.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the comprehensive disclosure requirement
- Option C overstates requirements—only material segments are reported
