# Question

Under IFRS, research costs are:

## Options

- **A**: Capitalized as an intangible asset
- **B**: Expensed as incurred
- **C**: Capitalized if certain criteria are met

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Under IFRS, research costs must be expensed as incurred because future economic benefits are too uncertain at the research stage. Development costs can be capitalized if specific criteria are met (technical feasibility, intent to complete, ability to use or sell, etc.). US GAAP generally expenses both R&D, with limited exceptions for software development.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes development costs under IFRS (if criteria met)
- Option C describes development costs, not research costs
