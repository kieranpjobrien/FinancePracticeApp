# Question

A company has cost of goods sold of $500 million and average inventory of $100 million. Its days inventory outstanding (DIO) is closest to:

## Options

- **A**: 50 days
- **B**: 73 days
- **C**: 100 days

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Inventory turnover = COGS / Average inventory = $500M / $100M = 5.0 times. DIO = 365 / Inventory turnover = 365 / 5.0 = 73 days. This means inventory is held for approximately 73 days before being sold. Lower DIO generally indicates better inventory management, though optimal levels vary by industry.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A may use incorrect divisor
- Option C uses inventory/daily COGS calculation incorrectly
