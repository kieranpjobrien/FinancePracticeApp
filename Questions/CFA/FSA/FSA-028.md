# Question

Which of the following is classified as a financing activity?

## Options

- **A**: Interest received on investments
- **B**: Issuance of common stock
- **C**: Collection of accounts receivable

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Financing activities involve transactions with owners and creditors that affect the company's capital structure. Stock issuance, stock repurchases, bond issuance, debt repayment, and dividend payments are financing activities. Interest received is typically an operating activity; receivable collection is an operating activity.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is generally an operating activity (investing under IFRS option)
- Option C is an operating activity
