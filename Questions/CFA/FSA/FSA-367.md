# Question

The effect of exchange rate changes on cash is reported in the cash flow statement:

## Options

- **A**: As part of operating activities
- **B**: As a separate reconciling item
- **C**: Only in the notes

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Exchange rate effects on cash held in foreign currencies are shown separately to explain the change in cash not attributable to operating, investing, or financing activities. This reconciles beginning and ending cash when foreign currency cash balances exist. It's a non-cash adjustment to the cash rollforward.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A classifies it with operations, which is incorrect
- Option C understates disclosure requirements
