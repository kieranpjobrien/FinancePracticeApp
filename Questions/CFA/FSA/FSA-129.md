# Question

A company had 10 million shares outstanding for the first 6 months of the year, then issued 2 million shares. The weighted average shares for the year is:

## Options

- **A**: 10 million
- **B**: 11 million
- **C**: 12 million

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Weighted average shares = (10M × 6/12) + (12M × 6/12) = 5M + 6M = 11 million. Shares are weighted by the portion of the year they were outstanding. The 2 million new shares are only weighted for the 6 months they existed.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the new share issuance
- Option C counts all shares for the full year
