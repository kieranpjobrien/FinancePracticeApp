# Question

Deferred charges (long-term prepaid expenses) should be scrutinized because:

## Options

- **A**: They always represent high-quality assets
- **B**: They may represent aggressive capitalization of costs that should be expensed
- **C**: They are prohibited under GAAP

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Deferred charges (organization costs, debt issuance costs, software) represent capitalized expenditures amortized over time. Aggressive capitalization overstates assets and understates current expenses. Analysts should evaluate whether capitalization is appropriate and if amortization periods are reasonable. Growing deferred charges relative to revenue warrant scrutiny.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores potential for manipulation
- Option C is incorrect—many deferred charges are GAAP-compliant
