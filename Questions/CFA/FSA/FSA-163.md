# Question

A company has revenue of $500,000 and cost of goods sold of $325,000. The gross profit margin is:

## Options

- **A**: 35%
- **B**: 65%
- **C**: 54%

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Gross profit = Revenue - COGS = $500,000 - $325,000 = $175,000. Gross profit margin = Gross profit / Revenue = $175,000 / $500,000 = 35%. This margin indicates how much of each revenue dollar remains after covering direct production costs.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B is COGS/Revenue (65%), not gross margin
- Option C results from calculation error
