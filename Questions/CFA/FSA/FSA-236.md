# Question

Total income tax expense reported on the income statement equals:

## Options

- **A**: Current taxes payable only
- **B**: Current tax expense plus/minus change in deferred taxes
- **C**: Statutory rate multiplied by pretax income

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Income tax expense = Current tax expense ± Change in deferred tax assets/liabilities. Current tax is based on taxable income; deferred tax reflects temporary differences. The total represents the tax cost of pretax accounting income, even though cash payment timing differs.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores deferred tax effects
- Option C ignores permanent differences and rate reconciliation items
