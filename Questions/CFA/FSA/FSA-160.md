# Question

Cost of goods sold for a manufacturer includes:

## Options

- **A**: Marketing and distribution costs
- **B**: Direct materials, direct labor, and manufacturing overhead
- **C**: Research and development expenses

## Answer

B

## Explanation

COGS includes all costs directly attributable to producing goods sold: raw materials, direct labor (production workers), and manufacturing overhead (factory rent, utilities, depreciation of production equipment). Marketing, distribution, and R&D are period costs expensed as incurred, not inventoried.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes selling expenses (SG&A)
- Option C is expensed as incurred (except certain development costs under IFRS)
