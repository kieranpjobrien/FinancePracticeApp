# Question

Which statement about IFRS and US GAAP is most accurate?

## Options

- **A**: IFRS allows LIFO inventory costing; US GAAP prohibits it
- **B**: US GAAP allows LIFO inventory costing; IFRS prohibits it
- **C**: Both standards prohibit LIFO inventory costing

## Answer

B

## Explanation

LIFO (Last-In, First-Out) is permitted under US GAAP but prohibited under IFRS. This is a significant difference affecting multinational companies' financial statements. LIFO can reduce taxable income in rising price environments, which is why it remains popular in the US despite global convergence efforts.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A reverses the treatment between standards
- Option C incorrectly states both prohibit LIFO
