# Question

Discontinued operations are reported on the income statement:

## Options

- **A**: As part of operating income
- **B**: Net of tax, below income from continuing operations
- **C**: As an extraordinary item

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Discontinued operations (components disposed of or held for sale representing a strategic shift) are reported separately, net of tax, after income from continuing operations. This presentation helps users assess sustainable earnings. Extraordinary items are no longer permitted under IFRS or US GAAP.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A would obscure ongoing business performance
- Option C references an obsolete classification
