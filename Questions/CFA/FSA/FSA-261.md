# Question

Timeliness is an enhancing qualitative characteristic because:

## Options

- **A**: Information should be audited before release
- **B**: Information must be available in time to influence decisions
- **C**: Information should predict future outcomes

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Timeliness means providing information early enough to be useful for decision-making. Delayed information loses relevance. However, timeliness must be balanced with reliability—rushing to publish inaccurate information isn't beneficial. Timeliness enhances (but doesn't create) relevance of faithfully represented information.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A relates to reliability, not timeliness
- Option C describes predictive value, a component of relevance
