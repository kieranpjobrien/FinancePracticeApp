# Question

Under IFRS, development costs:

## Options

- **A**: Are always expensed as incurred
- **B**: Must be capitalized if certain criteria are met
- **C**: Are capitalized at management's discretion

## Answer

B

## Explanation

IFRS requires capitalization of development costs when all criteria are met: technical feasibility, intention to complete, ability to use or sell, probable future economic benefits, resources available, and costs reliably measurable. Research costs are always expensed. US GAAP generally expenses both (except software development).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes US GAAP treatment
- Option C ignores the mandatory nature when criteria are met
