# Question

Under US GAAP, inventories are reported at:

## Options

- **A**: Historical cost only
- **B**: Lower of cost or net realizable value
- **C**: Lower of cost or market

## Answer

C

## Explanation

US GAAP uses lower of cost or market (LCM) where market is replacement cost bounded by NRV ceiling and NRV minus normal profit floor. IFRS uses lower of cost or NRV (simpler approach). Both require inventory write-downs when values decline, but US GAAP's "market" definition differs from pure NRV.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A ignores the requirement for write-downs
- Option B is the IFRS treatment, not US GAAP
