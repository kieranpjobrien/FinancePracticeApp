# Question

Comparability, timeliness, verifiability, and understandability are:

## Options

- **A**: The fundamental qualitative characteristics
- **B**: The enhancing qualitative characteristics
- **C**: The recognition criteria for assets

## Answer

B

## Explanation

These are enhancing characteristics that improve the usefulness of financial information that is relevant and faithfully represented (the fundamental characteristics). Comparability allows comparison across entities and time periods. Timeliness ensures information is available when needed. Verifiability means independent observers can reach consensus.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A confuses enhancing with fundamental (relevance and faithful representation)
- Option C describes asset recognition criteria
