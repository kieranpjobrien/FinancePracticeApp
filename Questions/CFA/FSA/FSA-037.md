# Question

A company has revenue of $730 million and average accounts receivable of $100 million. Its days sales outstanding (DSO) is closest to:

## Options

- **A**: 37 days
- **B**: 50 days
- **C**: 73 days

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Receivables turnover = Revenue / Average receivables = $730M / $100M = 7.3 times. DSO = 365 / Receivables turnover = 365 / 7.3 = 50 days. This means it takes approximately 50 days to collect receivables. Lower DSO indicates faster collection and better working capital management.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A may use 365/10 calculation
- Option C incorrectly calculates receivables/daily revenue
