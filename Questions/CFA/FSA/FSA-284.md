# Question

Restructuring charges typically include:

## Options

- **A**: Regular maintenance and repairs
- **B**: Employee severance, asset write-downs, and contract termination costs
- **C**: Depreciation on newly acquired equipment

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Restructuring charges arise from significant organizational changes: employee termination benefits, asset impairments from facility closures, lease termination penalties, and relocation costs. These should be separately disclosed. Analysts evaluate whether charges are truly one-time or recurring (serial restructurers raise quality concerns).

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes normal operating expenses
- Option C is regular depreciation, not restructuring
