# Question

Using the extended DuPont formula, if net profit margin is 8%, asset turnover is 1.5, interest burden is 0.9, tax burden is 0.75, and financial leverage is 2.0, ROE is closest to:

## Options

- **A**: 16.2%
- **B**: 18.0%
- **C**: 24.0%

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Extended DuPont: ROE = Net profit margin × Asset turnover × Financial leverage × Interest burden × Tax burden. ROE = 8% × 1.5 × 2.0 × 0.9 × 0.75 = 0.08 × 1.5 × 2.0 × 0.9 × 0.75 = 16.2%. This decomposition shows how operating efficiency, asset use, leverage, and tax efficiency drive returns.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B ignores interest and tax burden effects
- Option C only uses basic three-factor DuPont
