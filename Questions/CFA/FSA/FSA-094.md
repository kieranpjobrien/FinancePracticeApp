# Question

Selling, general, and administrative (SG&A) expenses typically include:

## Options

- **A**: Cost of raw materials and direct labor
- **B**: Marketing, rent, and executive salaries
- **C**: Interest expense and income taxes

## Answer

B

## Explanation

SG&A includes operating costs not directly tied to production: marketing and advertising, administrative salaries, office rent, legal fees, and depreciation of non-manufacturing assets. Raw materials and direct labor are part of COGS. Interest and taxes are non-operating items reported below operating income.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes product costs included in COGS
- Option C describes non-operating expenses
