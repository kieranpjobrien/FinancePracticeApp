# Question

A company has pretax income of $200,000 and income tax expense of $52,000. The effective tax rate is:

## Options

- **A**: 21%
- **B**: 26%
- **C**: 35%

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Effective tax rate = Income tax expense / Pretax income = $52,000 / $200,000 = 26%. This differs from the statutory rate due to permanent differences (non-deductible expenses, tax credits), state/local taxes, and international rate differences. Analyzing the rate reconciliation reveals tax planning effectiveness.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A is a common statutory rate, not the calculated effective rate
- Option C assumes a higher statutory rate without calculation
