# Question

A company has revenue of $2,400,000 and average accounts receivable of $200,000. Days sales outstanding (DSO) is closest to:

## Options

- **A**: 30 days
- **B**: 45 days
- **C**: 60 days

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Receivables turnover = Revenue / Average A/R = $2,400,000 / $200,000 = 12 times. DSO = 365 / Turnover = 365 / 12 = 30.4 ≈ 30 days. This indicates how quickly the company collects receivables—lower DSO means faster collection and better liquidity.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B and C result from calculation errors or using different formulas
