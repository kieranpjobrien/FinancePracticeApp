# Question

Prepaid expenses are classified as:

## Options

- **A**: Current assets
- **B**: Non-current assets
- **C**: Operating expenses

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Prepaid expenses (rent, insurance, supplies paid in advance) are current assets because they provide economic benefits within one year. As time passes, they're expensed—reducing the asset and recognizing expense. Long-term prepayments (rare) would be non-current. They represent cash converted to future expense reduction.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B applies only if benefits extend beyond one year
- Option C describes the eventual treatment, not balance sheet classification
