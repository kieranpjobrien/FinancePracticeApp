# Question

Under IFRS 9, an equity investment designated at fair value through OCI (FVOCI):

## Options

- **A**: Has gains and losses recycled to profit or loss on sale
- **B**: Has gains and losses remain in equity and are never recycled
- **C**: Is accounted for using the equity method

## Answer

B

## Explanation

IFRS 9 allows irrevocable election for non-trading equity investments to be measured at FVOCI. Fair value changes go to OCI but are never recycled to P&L (even on disposal). Only dividends affect profit or loss. This differs from FVOCI debt instruments where gains/losses are recycled. US GAAP has no equivalent.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A describes FVOCI debt instruments, not equity
- Option C applies to significant influence (20-50%), not passive investments
