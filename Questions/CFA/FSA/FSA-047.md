# Question

Equipment costs $100,000, has a salvage value of $10,000, and a useful life of 9 years. Annual straight-line depreciation is:

## Options

- **A**: $10,000
- **B**: $11,111
- **C**: $12,222

## Answer

A

## Explanation

Straight-line depreciation = (Cost - Salvage value) / Useful life = ($100,000 - $10,000) / 9 years = $90,000 / 9 = $10,000 per year. This method allocates an equal expense each year, representing the systematic consumption of the asset's value.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option B ignores salvage value: $100,000 / 9 = $11,111
- Option C results from calculation error
