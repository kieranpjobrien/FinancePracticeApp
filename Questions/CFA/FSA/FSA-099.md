# Question

A company has purchases of $730,000 and average accounts payable of $100,000. Its days payable outstanding (DPO) is closest to:

## Options

- **A**: 37 days
- **B**: 50 days
- **C**: 73 days

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Payables turnover = Purchases / Average payables = $730,000 / $100,000 = 7.3 times. DPO = 365 / Payables turnover = 365 / 7.3 = 50 days. This means the company takes approximately 50 days to pay suppliers. Longer DPO preserves cash but may strain supplier relationships.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A uses incorrect calculation
- Option C confuses with DSO or DIO formulas
