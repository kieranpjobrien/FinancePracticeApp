# Question

Assets are grouped into asset classes based on:

## Options

- **A**: Identical returns in all market conditions
- **B**: Similar risk, return, and correlation characteristics
- **C**: Being issued by the same company

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Asset class definition:

**Criteria for asset class:**
- Homogeneous risk-return characteristics
- Similar correlations with other classes
- Distinct from other asset classes
- Capacity to absorb meaningful allocation

**Major asset classes:**
- Equities (stocks)
- Fixed income (bonds)
- Cash equivalents
- Real estate
- Commodities
- Alternative investments

**Sub-asset classes:**
- Large-cap vs. small-cap stocks
- Domestic vs. international
- Investment-grade vs. high-yield bonds

**Purpose:**
- Simplify allocation decisions
- Enable diversification analysis
- Strategic asset allocation

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Returns vary; similar characteristics, not identical
- Option C: Same issuer doesn't define asset class
