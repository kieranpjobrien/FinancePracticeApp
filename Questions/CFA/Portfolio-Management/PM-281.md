# Question

Tax-efficient asset location suggests placing high-yield bonds in:

## Options

- **A**: Taxable accounts for liquidity
- **B**: Tax-deferred accounts to shelter interest income
- **C**: Municipal bond funds for tax exemption

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Asset location strategy:

**Tax-deferred accounts (IRA, 401k):**
- Place tax-inefficient assets
- High-yield bonds (ordinary income)
- REITs (ordinary income)
- High-turnover strategies

**Taxable accounts:**
- Place tax-efficient assets
- Index funds (low turnover)
- Growth stocks (defer gains)
- Municipal bonds (tax-exempt)

**High-yield bond taxation:**
- Interest taxed as ordinary income
- Highest marginal rates apply
- Shelter in tax-deferred accounts

**Benefit:**
- Reduce current tax liability
- Maximize after-tax returns
- Efficient wealth accumulation

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Taxable accounts expose interest to high tax rates
- Option C: This addresses different bonds, not high-yield placement
