# Question

Regulatory risk refers to:

## Options

- **A**: The risk of market price fluctuations
- **B**: The risk that regulatory changes will adversely affect investments
- **C**: The risk of currency exchange rate movements

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Regulatory risk:

**Definition:**
- Risk from changes in laws, regulations, or enforcement
- Can affect specific securities or entire markets
- May be sudden or gradual

**Examples:**
- New environmental regulations
- Changes in tax law
- Financial industry reform
- Trade policy changes
- Antitrust actions

**Affected areas:**
- Banking and finance
- Healthcare/pharmaceuticals
- Energy and utilities
- Technology/telecom

**Management:**
- Monitor regulatory environment
- Diversify across regulatory regimes
- Engage in policy analysis
- Consider regulatory risk in valuation

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: This is market risk
- Option C: This is currency risk
