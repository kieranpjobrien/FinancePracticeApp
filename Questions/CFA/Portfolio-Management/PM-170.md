# Question

The correlation coefficient between two assets ranges from:

## Options

- **A**: 0 to +1.0
- **B**: -1.0 to +1.0
- **C**: -∞ to +∞

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Correlation coefficient properties:

**Range:**
- Minimum: -1.0 (perfect negative correlation)
- Maximum: +1.0 (perfect positive correlation)
- Zero: No linear relationship

**Interpretation:**
- +1.0: Assets move perfectly together
- 0: No linear relationship (may have nonlinear)
- -1.0: Assets move perfectly opposite

**Diversification benefits:**
- Lower correlation → greater benefit
- Negative correlation → potential risk elimination

**Formula:**
ρ = Cov(A,B) / (σA × σB)

**Important notes:**
- Measures linear relationship only
- Doesn't imply causation
- Can change over time

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Correlation can be negative
- Option C: Correlation is bounded by -1 and +1
