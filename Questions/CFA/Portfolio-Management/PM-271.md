# Question

When correlation between two assets decreases, portfolio risk:

## Options

- **A**: Increases
- **B**: Decreases
- **C**: Remains unchanged

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Correlation and diversification:

**Lower correlation effect:**
- Greater diversification benefit
- More risk reduction possible
- Portfolio variance decreases

**Mathematical basis:**
- Portfolio variance includes covariance term
- Lower correlation = lower covariance
- Reduces overall portfolio risk

**Extreme cases:**
- Correlation = +1: No diversification benefit
- Correlation = -1: Maximum diversification
- Correlation = 0: Significant benefit

**Practical implication:**
- Seek low-correlation assets
- International diversification
- Alternative assets may help

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Lower correlation reduces, not increases risk
- Option C: Correlation directly impacts portfolio risk
