# Question

A spending policy determines:

## Options

- **A**: Which securities to buy
- **B**: How much can be withdrawn from the portfolio for spending
- **C**: The portfolio's asset allocation

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Spending policy:

**Purpose:**
- Rules for withdrawals
- Balance current spending vs. preservation
- Provide predictable income

**Common approaches:**
- Fixed percentage of assets
- Fixed dollar amount (inflation-adjusted)
- Moving average methods
- Hybrid approaches

**Considerations:**
- Sustainability over time
- Volatility of income
- Preservation of capital
- Flexibility in adverse markets

**For institutions:**
- Endowments often use 4-5% spending rate
- Universities, foundations have perpetual horizons

**For individuals:**
- 4% rule as starting point
- Adjust for circumstances

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Security selection is separate
- Option C: Asset allocation is separate policy
