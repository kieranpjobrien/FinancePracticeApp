# Question

Assets commonly considered inflation hedges include:

## Options

- **A**: Long-term fixed-rate bonds
- **B**: Real estate, commodities, and TIPS
- **C**: Money market funds only

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Inflation hedging assets:

**Good inflation hedges:**
- **Real estate:** Rents and values tend to rise with inflation
- **Commodities:** Prices often drive inflation
- **TIPS:** Principal adjusts with CPI
- **Equities:** Long-term (earnings grow with prices)
- **Gold:** Traditional inflation hedge

**Poor inflation hedges:**
- **Fixed-rate bonds:** Fixed coupons lose purchasing power
- **Cash:** Purchasing power erodes
- **Nominal annuities:** Fixed payments

**Considerations:**
- Short-term vs. long-term inflation protection
- Expected vs. unexpected inflation
- Asset-specific factors

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Fixed-rate bonds suffer from inflation
- Option C: Money market provides minimal inflation protection
