# Question

Explicit transaction costs include:

## Options

- **A**: Market impact and opportunity costs
- **B**: Brokerage commissions and exchange fees
- **C**: Slippage from delayed execution

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Transaction costs classification:

**Explicit costs:**
- Brokerage commissions
- Exchange fees
- Taxes (stamp duty, transaction tax)
- Regulatory fees
- Easily measurable and documented

**Implicit costs:**
- Bid-ask spread
- Market impact (price moves from trading)
- Opportunity cost (delay, non-execution)
- Slippage
- Harder to measure

**Total trading costs:**
= Explicit + Implicit

**Importance:**
- Can significantly erode returns
- Particularly relevant for active strategies
- Must be considered in strategy evaluation

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: These are implicit, not explicit costs
- Option C: Slippage is an implicit cost
