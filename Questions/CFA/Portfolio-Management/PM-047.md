# Question

Which of the following is NOT an assumption of the Capital Asset Pricing Model (CAPM)?

## Options

- **A**: Investors can borrow and lend at the risk-free rate
- **B**: All investors have the same one-period time horizon
- **C**: Investors have different expectations about asset returns and risks

## Answer

C

## Explanation

CAPM assumptions include:

1. **Homogeneous expectations** - All investors have identical expectations about returns, variances, and correlations
2. Investors can borrow/lend unlimited amounts at risk-free rate
3. Single-period investment horizon (same for all)
4. No taxes or transaction costs
5. All assets are marketable and divisible
6. Investors are rational mean-variance optimizers
7. All information is freely available

Different expectations would violate homogeneous expectations assumption, leading to different optimal risky portfolios for different investors.

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: This IS an assumption of CAPM
- Option B: This IS an assumption of CAPM (homogeneous time horizons)
