# Question

Interest rate risk is a component of:

## Options

- **A**: Credit risk
- **B**: Market risk
- **C**: Operational risk

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Market risk categories:

**Market risk** includes:
- Interest rate risk: Changes in bond prices due to rate changes
- Equity risk: Changes in stock prices
- Currency risk: Changes in exchange rates
- Commodity risk: Changes in commodity prices

Interest rate risk is a key market risk for:
- Bond portfolios (prices move inversely to rates)
- Loans and mortgages
- Interest rate derivatives
- Any cash flow sensitive to rates

**Other risk categories:**
- Credit risk: Counterparty default
- Operational risk: Process/system failures
- Liquidity risk: Inability to trade quickly

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Credit risk relates to default, not rate movements
- Option C: Operational risk relates to processes and systems
