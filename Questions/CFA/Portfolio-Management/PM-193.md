# Question

An index fund's primary objective is to:

## Options

- **A**: Outperform its benchmark index through active stock selection
- **B**: Match the performance of a specific market index
- **C**: Minimize all investment risk

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Index fund characteristics:

**Primary goal:**
- Replicate benchmark index returns
- Minimize tracking error
- Passive management approach

**How achieved:**
- Full replication: Hold all index constituents
- Sampling: Hold representative subset
- Optimization: Mathematical approximation

**Advantages:**
- Low costs (no active research)
- Diversification
- Tax efficiency (low turnover)
- Transparency

**Tracking error sources:**
- Transaction costs
- Cash drag
- Sampling error
- Rebalancing timing

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: This describes active management, not indexing
- Option C: Index funds have market risk; not designed to minimize all risk
