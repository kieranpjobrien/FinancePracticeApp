# Question

The major components of market risk include:

## Options

- **A**: Only credit risk
- **B**: Equity, interest rate, currency, and commodity risks
- **C**: Only operational risk

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Market risk components:

**Equity risk:**
- Changes in stock prices
- Systematic market movements
- Individual stock volatility

**Interest rate risk:**
- Changes in yield curve
- Bond price sensitivity
- Duration risk

**Currency (FX) risk:**
- Exchange rate movements
- International investment exposure
- Translation and transaction risk

**Commodity risk:**
- Price changes in commodities
- Supply/demand factors
- Energy, metals, agriculture

**Measurement:**
- VaR across market factors
- Stress testing
- Factor sensitivities

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Credit risk is separate from market risk
- Option C: Operational risk is separate category
