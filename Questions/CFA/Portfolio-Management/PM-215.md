# Question

Risk tolerance questionnaires are used to:

## Options

- **A**: Guarantee specific investment outcomes
- **B**: Assess an investor's willingness and ability to take risk
- **C**: Predict exact future portfolio returns

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Risk profiling:

**Purpose:**
- Measure risk tolerance (willingness)
- Assess risk capacity (ability)
- Guide appropriate asset allocation

**Question types:**
- Hypothetical scenarios
- Past experiences
- Time horizon
- Financial situation
- Emotional responses

**Limitations:**
- Self-reported (may not reflect actual behavior)
- Context-dependent answers
- May not predict behavior in real crises
- Cultural differences in responses

**Best practices:**
- Combine with financial analysis
- Discuss results with client
- Regular reassessment
- Observe actual behavior

**Common mistakes:**
- Option A: Questionnaires don't guarantee outcomes
- Option C: Risk profiling doesn't predict returns
